About Stamford Plaza Sydney Airport Hotel & Conference Centre

Convenient Comfort at Stamford Plaza Sydney Airport

Stamford Plaza Sydney Airport offers an ideal location just 15 minutes from central Sydney, providing easy access for travelers. This hotel is perfect for those seeking convenience and modern amenities, situated only 3 minutes from the domestic terminal and 10 minutes from the international terminal.

  • Soundproofed rooms ensuring a peaceful stay.
  • On-site fitness center to maintain workout routines.
  • Air-conditioned rooms with views over Botany Bay, the city, and the airport.
  • 11 function rooms available for a variety of events, from large conferences to intimate gatherings.
  • 24-hour front desk offering airport transfers and dry cleaning services.

Each room at Stamford Plaza Sydney Airport is equipped with a mini-bar, flat-screen TV, and tea and coffee making facilities. Guests can enjoy the comfort of soundproofed accommodations with stunning views of the surrounding areas.

The hotel's strategic location allows easy access to iconic attractions like the Sydney Opera House, just a 20-minute drive away. Although the bar is currently closed, it will be reopening soon to offer additional on-site dining options. Whether for business or leisure, Stamford Plaza Sydney Airport ensures a comfortable and convenient stay with its range of amenities and services.
