About Hilton Anaheim

Hilton Anaheim

Perfectly situated adjacent to the Convention Center and within a leisurely 15-minute walk to Disneyland and Disney California Adventure Park, Hilton Anaheim is the quintessential destination for both leisure and business travelers. This hotel not only boasts spacious and modern accommodations but also offers an array of amenities designed to enhance your stay, including a sprawling outdoor pool, diverse dining options, and comprehensive spa services.

Key Features:

  • Strategically located within walking distance to Disneyland, providing the perfect base for family fun and adventure.
  • Expansive outdoor pool area complemented by a play area, making it an ideal spot for relaxation and family time.
  • A variety of dining experiences on-site, including Mix Lounge for cocktails, a Food Court with five restaurants, Starbucks, and seasonal outdoor dining.
  • Exclusive health club and spa offering fitness classes, a basketball court, whirlpools, and a sauna, along with an adults-only swimming pool for tranquility and relaxation.
  • 140,000 square feet of versatile indoor and outdoor meeting spaces, catering to events of all sizes and natures.

Whether you're visiting for the theme parks, attending a convention, or seeking a relaxing getaway, Hilton Anaheim offers a perfect blend of convenience, comfort, and entertainment. Experience the magic of Anaheim with Hilton, where every stay is memorable.
