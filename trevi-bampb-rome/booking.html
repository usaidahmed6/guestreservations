About Trevi Beau Boutique Hotel

Located 164 feet from the Trevi Fountain, Trevi Beau Boutique Hotel offers free WiFi throughout and stylish air-conditioned rooms. Barberini Metro Stop is a 5-minute walk away. Trevi Beau Boutique Hotel's reception is located on the first floor, while rooms are on the first, second and third floors. Rooms have wooden ceilings and modern décor, creating a contemporary atmosphere. A buffet-style continental breakfast is available at the hotel. The property’s ground floor is also home to 2 restaurants, Il Chianti and Le Tamerici, where you can try characteristic Tuscan, Roman and classic Italian cuisine, accompanied by fine wines. Staff at the hotel are available to book airport transfers and can recommend the best places to visit during your stay in Rome.
