About Hotel Metropolitan Marunouchi

Discover the Vibrant Stay at Hotel Metropolitan Marunouchi

Nestled in the heart of Tokyo, Hotel Metropolitan Marunouchi offers an unparalleled blend of modern comfort and urban convenience. Perfectly situated just a minute from Otemachi Station and directly connected to Tokyo Station, this stylish 4-star hotel serves as an ideal base for both business and leisure travelers. Enjoy seamless access to iconic attractions, with the Imperial Palace a short walk away and Akihabara's vibrant electronics district just two train stops on the Yamanote Line.

Guests are welcomed into air-conditioned rooms featuring contemporary interiors, each equipped with a fridge and flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Free WiFi ensures connectivity throughout your stay, while the 24-hour front desk provides luggage storage and laundry services for added convenience.

Located on the 27th floor, the TENQOO restaurant and bar offers stunning views of Tokyo's cityscape, serving a delightful fusion of French and Japanese cuisine. For those seeking relaxation, the hotel provides massage services upon request, making it easy to unwind after a day of exploration.

  • Prime location directly connected to Tokyo Station
  • Stunning city views from the 27th-floor TENQOO restaurant
  • Easy access to major attractions like the Imperial Palace and Akihabara
  • 24-hour business center and front desk services
  • In-room massage services for ultimate relaxation

Whether visiting for business or pleasure, Hotel Metropolitan Marunouchi ensures a memorable stay with its exceptional location, modern amenities, and attentive service.
