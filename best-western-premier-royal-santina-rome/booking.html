About Best Western Premier Hotel Royal Santina

This property offers a Special Protection Program, a strict program of precise safeguards dedicated to our guests and our staff. Offering modern rooms with design furniture and free high-speed WiFi, Hotel Royal Santina is set 656 feet from Rome Termini Station. Via Nazionale shopping street is 1804 feet away. The stylish rooms with chromotherapy include a mini-bar and an electric kettle. The private bathroom comes with bathrobes and a hairdryer. At breakfast, you can enjoy a varied buffet. The on-site restaurant offers a varied cuisine with vegetarian and gluten-free options. Terme di Diocleziano baths are 820 feet from the property, while the Coliseum is 2 metro stops away.
