About Sofitel Sydney Darling Harbour

Discover Elegance at Sofitel Sydney Darling Harbour

Embrace unparalleled luxury and breathtaking cityscapes at Sofitel Sydney Darling Harbour. Located a mere stone's throw from the International Convention Center and within easy reach of Sydney's vibrant CBD, this exquisite hotel is your gateway to the best of the city, from Circular Quay's iconic landmarks to Darling Harbour's bustling waterfront.

  • Experience the epitome of luxury in rooms with flat-screen Smart LED TVs, free WiFi, and elegant bathrooms, all boasting stunning views of Sydney's skyline.
  • Indulge in the serenity of our rooftop infinity pool, offering a mesmerizing urban oasis with panoramic views.
  • Savor gourmet cuisine at our fine dining restaurant or unwind at one of our three chic bars, including a rooftop pool bar.
  • Host unforgettable events in our versatile meeting spaces, including the grand Magnifique Ballroom, accommodating up to 450 guests.
  • Enhance your well-being at Sofitel SPA Darling Harbor, a sanctuary of wellness featuring a range of treatments, an infrared sauna, and a relaxation lounge with views over Darling Harbour.

From the moment you step into Sofitel Sydney Darling Harbour, you are enveloped in a world where French elegance meets contemporary design. Whether you're visiting for business or leisure, our dedicated team ensures your stay is nothing short of magnifique. Discover a place where luxury is a standard, and every detail is crafted for your pleasure and relaxation.
