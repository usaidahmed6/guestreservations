About Monastero dei Santi

Located in Rome, Monastero dei Santi is a 5-star hotel featuring a restaurant, free parking, a bar and a terrace. A concierge service, 24-hour front desk. The hotel is a 5-minute drive from Stadio Olimpico and 1640 feet from the Zodiaco panoramic viewpoint. At the Monastero dei Santi hotel, all rooms feature a balcony and city views. Each also includes a wardrobe, flat-screen TV and private bathroom. The property is 1.6 mi from the Vatican. Both Ciampino Airport and Fiumicino Airport can be reached by car in about 30 minutes.
