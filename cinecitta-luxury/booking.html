About Cinecittà Luxury

Cinecittà Luxury has air-conditioned guest accommodations in Rome, 1.1 miles from Anagnina Metro Station, 3.2 miles from Università Tor Vergata and 5.3 miles from Ponte Lungo Metro Station. Free Wifi is offered throughout the property and private parking is available on site. The bed and breakfast offers a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with bathrobes, a hair dryer and bidet. Guests can also relax in the shared lounge area. San Giovanni Metro Station is 5.8 miles from Cinecittà Luxury, while Porta Maggiore is 6.1 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino, 5 miles from the accommodation, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
