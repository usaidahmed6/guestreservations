About Exhibition Court Hotel 4

Centrally located in the Royal Borough of Kensington and Chelsea, the Exhibition Court 4 Hotel is less than 5 minutes’ walk from Earl's Court Underground Station and easily accessible from Gatwick, Stansted, Luton and Heathrow International airports. Bedrooms at the Exhibition Court 4 Hotel offer a mix of old-world charm, combining period architecture with modern amenities. In-room controlled heating as well as air-conditioning are available. Guests can enjoy large Smart TV and complimentary WiFi. Each room has a private bathroom with a hair dryer. Guests also have access to a large refreshment lounge with a microwave and tea & coffee making facilities. The hotel features a lobby decorated in an eclectic avant-garde style with historic artifacts from a 500 year old church and modern artworks. The 24-hour front desk is also available there. Located in one of Kensington's historic white stucco buildings, the hotel is only a short walk from London highlights such as the Natural History Museum, the Victoria and Albert Museum, Buckingham Palace, Hyde Park, Oxford Street and Chelsea Football Club.
