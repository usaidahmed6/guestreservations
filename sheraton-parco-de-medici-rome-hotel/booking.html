About Sheraton Rome Parco de' Medici

Surrounded by landscaped gardens, Sheraton Rome Parco de Medici features a 27-hole golf course and an outdoor pool. It is 6.2 mi from Fiumicino Airport and 5 minutes' drive from Rome's A90 ring road. Rooms come with modern or classic furnishings, some of them handcrafted. All air conditioned, they offer satellite TV and views of the gardens, the pool or the golf course. Free WiFi is available throughout the entire property. Sheraton Rome Parco de Medici consists of a number of buildings across the green grounds. There are 8 individually decorated restaurants and bars serving international cuisine and cocktails. Wi-Fi is free in the lobby and shared areas. The hotel is located just outside Rome’s E.U.R. district,8.1 mi from the historic center. Friendly staff at the 24-hour reception can provide tourist information, and organize shuttle services to the airport and the city center.
