About Prior Castle Inn

Experience the grandeur of Edwardian-era Victoria at Prior Castle Inn, located just 1640 feet from the historic Craigdarroch Castle. Our charming bed and breakfast offers a unique and luxurious experience with individually decorated rooms featuring thoughtful touches such as a cozy fireplace and stunning garden views. Guests can enjoy modern amenities such as a flat-screen SmartTV and cable channels, as well as free WiFi throughout the property. Select units offer kitchen or kitchenette facilities for added convenience. In the morning, guests can indulge in a delicious homemade breakfast served in the elegant Victorian dining room or on the picturesque garden patio. Our property is conveniently located near Victoria Harbor ferry terminal and Waterfront Tours, while Butchart Gardens are just a 30-minute drive away. Craigdarroch Castle and the Governors Mansion and Gardens are also part of the neighborhood. We invite you to experience the magic of Prior Castle Inn and make your stay in Victoria unforgettable.
