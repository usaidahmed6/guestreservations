About Hotel degli Arcimboldi

Welcome to Hotel degli Arcimboldi: Modern Comfort in the Heart of Milan

Discover the charm of Milan with a stay at Hotel degli Arcimboldi, nestled in the vibrant Bicocca â€“ Zara District. Offering modern accommodations just 820 feet from the Bignami Metro Stop, this hotel is a gateway to the beauty and excitement of Milan. Perfect for both leisure and business travelers, guests can enjoy the convenience of complimentary WiFi throughout the property and rooms boasting stunning city views.

Each air-conditioned room is equipped with a flat-screen TV for your entertainment and features a private bathroom with a shower, ensuring a comfortable stay. Start your day with a delightful sweet and savory breakfast, promising a taste of the local cuisine.

Strategically located, Hotel degli Arcimboldi is just 0.9 mi from the University of Milan Bicocca and the Arcimboldi Theater, making it an ideal choice for those visiting the university or seeking cultural experiences. Moreover, with Milan Lintate Airport only a 25-minute drive away, your arrival and departure will be as smooth as your stay.

  • Strategic location near Bignami Metro Stop for easy city access
  • Stunning city views from every room
  • Complimentary sweet and savory breakfast daily
  • Close proximity to University of Milan Bicocca and Arcimboldi Theater
  • Convenient access to Milan Lintate Airport, just 25 minutes away

Hotel degli Arcimboldi is more than just a place to stay; it's your home base for exploring all that Milan has to offer. From the bustling city life to the serene comfort of your room, experience the best of both worlds. Book your stay today and immerse yourself in the Milanese lifestyle.
