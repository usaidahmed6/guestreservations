About Hotel White

With a strong focus on comfort and service, Hotel White enjoys a superb location in the midst of Rome’s most famous sights, from the Trevi fountains to the Spanish Steps. This beautifully renovated 19th century building stands in a characteristic street in the heart of Rome’s ancient historic center and provides luxurious rooms equipped with the utmost in modern conveniences and comfort. The hotel also includes an exclusive spa and gym, together with a relaxation area and sensory shower. Guests can enjoy a drink from the bar, and benefit from a shared computer and printer.

Amenities

  • Room Service
  • 24 hour front desk
  • Fitness Center
