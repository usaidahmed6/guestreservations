About Hotel Locarno

Experience the Charm of Hotel Locarno in Rome

Located just off Piazza del Popolo, Hotel Locarno stands as a beacon of art and history within the heart of Rome. Housed in twin buildings from the early 20th century, this hotel offers a unique stay with its Art Nouveau style rooms and a rooftop terrace that boasts stunning views of Rome's historic center.

Rooms at Hotel Locarno are a blend of retro-classic style and urban hipness, featuring Art Deco furniture and modern amenities in a meticulously restored setting. Each air-conditioned room provides guests with luxurious fabrics, a safe, TV, and mini-bar, ensuring comfort throughout your stay.

  • Rooftop terrace offering panoramic views of the city
  • Complimentary bikes for exploring Rome’s historical sites
  • Continental breakfast available al fresco on the patio in fine weather
  • On-site bar, popular with guests and locals alike
  • Proximity to Flaminio Metro Station and iconic landmarks such as the Spanish Steps

Hotel Locarno is not only steeped in history but also deeply connected with the world of art and culture, making it an ideal choice for those who appreciate both. Enjoy a generous continental breakfast served outdoors on the beautifully furnished patio, or unwind in the evening with a drink at the hotel’s own bar.

With its close proximity to Flaminio Metro Station, guests have direct connections to Vatican City and Termini Train Station, making it easy to explore all that Rome has to offer. The Spanish Steps, a mere stroll away, are just one of the many cultural treasures nearby.

Whether visiting for business or leisure, Hotel Locarno in Rome offers a unique and enchanting experience, combining the elegance of historical architecture with modern comforts and a vibrant cultural atmosphere.
