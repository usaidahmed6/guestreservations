About The Beach by Glitter House

Stop at The Beach by Glitter House to discover the wonders of Phuket. The hotel has everything you need for a comfortable stay. Facilities like free Wi-Fi in all rooms, car park, airport transfer, car hire, tours are readily available for you to enjoy. Guestrooms are fitted with all the amenities you need for a good night's sleep. In some of the rooms, guests can find television LCD/plasma screen, internet access – wireless, internet access – wireless (complimentary), non smoking rooms, air conditioning. The hotel offers various recreational opportunities. Friendly staff, great facilities and close proximity to all that Phuket has to offer are three great reasons you should stay at The Beach by Glitter House.
