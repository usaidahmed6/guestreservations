About Inn San Francisco

Serving a daily breakfast buffet and 24-hour refreshments, Inn San Francisco offers guest rooms with authentic Victorian décor and free WiFi. This B&B is 1.5 mi from San Francisco City Hall. A flat-screen cable TV is provided in each plush room at this B&B. Guest rooms also include a refrigerator and an private bathroom with free toiletries and guest bathrobes. Guests of Inn San Francisco can relax in the hot tub. A garden and a games room are located on site. Other facilities offered at the property include a shared lounge and laundry facilities. This B&B is 2 mi from Moscone Center and 2.1 mi from Union Square. San Francisco International Airport is 9.9 mi away.
