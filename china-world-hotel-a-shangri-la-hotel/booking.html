About Shangri-la's China World Hotel, Beijing

Shangri-la's China World Hotel, Beijing is located a 3-minute walk from Guomao Subway Station. It offers stylish accommodations with an indoor pool, meeting and banquet space. Free Wi-Fi is available in all areas. China World Hotel serves as an ideal starting point for exploring famous historical sites, with convenient access to landmarks such as the Forbidden City, Universal Studios, Wangfujing, and Qianmen. The hotel is a popular City Walk destination in Beijing‘s XiSi Street. The China Hotel is 17 miles from Beijing Capital International Airport and 34 miles from Beijing Daxing International Airport.Nearby the sixth-floor outdoor terrace of the China World Trade Center Mall which offers a panoramic view of the CBD urban landscape, allowing guests to relax and enjoy the Guomao's beauty after shopping. With a vibrant mix of fashionable cuisine, shopping, entertainment, and numerous recreational activities, the hotel provides a diverse and enriching experience. China World Hotel, Beijing offers 584 beautifully appointed guestrooms, as well as 69 modern and comfortable Residences. And 5 restaurants uphold the Shangri-la’s creative culinary. The Michelin Guide-listed Summer Palace restaurant offers delicious Cantonese and Huaiyang cuisine, while the Scene a Café buffet restaurant presents a diverse array of Asian and Western delicacies. Guests staying on the Horizon Club floors will have exclusive access to the new Horizon Lounge on the 3 floor. The hotel Health Club has an indoor heated swimming pool, excellent facilities for squash courts and indoor tennis courts, providing a professional experience for the CBD area fitness professionals. Moreover, China World Hotel Beijing offers impeccable service and facilities to meet every aspect of your stay. The hotel provides guests and travelers an all-new leisure and business travel experience with the outstanding Asian hospitality Shangri-La is renowned for. 50% off parking privilege per stay at China World Hotel parking lot.
