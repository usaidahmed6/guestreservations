About Hotel American Palace Eur

Hotel American Palace Eur is a historical property, just 164 feet from the Laurentina Metro Station. Drinks are available in the green courtyard with fruit trees. There is a free 24-hour gym. Air-conditioned rooms at the American Palace have wooden furnishings and satellite TV. The private bathrooms are complete with a shower and hairdryer. The American Palace offers quick metro links to Rome's Termini Train Station. Both Fiumicino and Ciampino Airports can be reached in 20 minutes by car.
