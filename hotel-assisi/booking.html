About Hotel Assisi

Hotel Assisi is 220 yards from Termini Train Station and just around the corner from the airport shuttle stop. A delicious Italian buffet breakfast is included in your rate. The hotel provides public areas including a bar, a breakfast room and a lounge. Reception is always open and the Assisi's staff can book you car rental, taxis, tours and more. Wi-Fi internet access is available here. Hotel Assisi's rooms are well-maintained and quiet. All come with TVs and safes.

Amenities

  • Room Service
  • 24 hour front desk
