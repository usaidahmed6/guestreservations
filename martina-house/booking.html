About Martina House

Martina House is a small and romantic B&B in Rome, just 164 feet from Villa Borghese park. It is set in a historical building, with original decorated doors and parquet. All rooms at the Martina offer a private bathroom, satellite TV, a fridge and air conditioning. Accommodations is also soundproofed and furnished with antiques. A sweet, Italian-style breakfast is offered daily in a nearby bakery. It is possible to choose an international breakfast at the same bakery with an additional cost. B&B Martina House is on Via Veneto, famous since the 1950s for being the set of Federico Fellini's La Dolce Vita masterpiece.
