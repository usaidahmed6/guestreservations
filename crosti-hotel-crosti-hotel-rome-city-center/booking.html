About Crosti Hotel & Residence

A 10-minute walk from Rome's Termini Station you'll find Hotel Crosti, which guarantees you comfortable accommodations and a great service from its dedicated team of staff. Wi-Fi is free in the rooms. Each room is elegantly furnished and comes with a flat-screen satellite TV and high-speed internet access. The room rate includes a continental buffet breakfast. Staying at the Crosti Hotel means you have easy access, on foot or by public transport, to all of Rome's attractions. Set just off the prestigious Via XX Settembre, the area is full of the city's most important banks and embassies. The beautiful Church of Santa Maria della Vittoria is just around the corner.
