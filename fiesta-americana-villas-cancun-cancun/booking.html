About Fiesta Americana Cancun Villas

This hotel has an amazing beachfront location in Cancn's Island. The hotel offers a spa and on-site dining at the Palapa Restaurant, Restaurante Mexicano and La Bodega. The guest suites include a kitchenette and a balcony. Fiesta Americana Cancun Villas features an outdoor pool and a fitness center. A spa is located at the hotel and offers body treatments and massages. The hotel also offers an on-site gift shop and supervised childrens activities. Cancun city center is a short drive from the Fiesta Americana Cancun Villas. Cancun International Airport is a 25-minute drive away.
