About Hilton Miami Airport Blue Lagoon

Hilton Miami Airport Blue Lagoon: A Tranquil Waterfront Escape

Discover the Hilton Miami Airport Blue Lagoon, a serene oasis nestled on a picturesque peninsula overlooking the Blue Lagoon. Conveniently located with complimentary shuttle service to Miami International Airport, this hotel serves as the ideal gateway for travelers. Guests can enjoy spacious accommodations, a variety of on-site dining options, and a wealth of recreational facilities, all while being just a short drive from Miami's vibrant attractions, including South Beach, Zoo Miami, and Dolphin Mall.

Key Features:

  • Complimentary airport shuttle service ensures seamless travel to and from Miami International Airport.
  • Spacious guest rooms with scenic views, equipped with mini-bars and essential work desks.
  • Recreational amenities including a large outdoor pool, fitness center, jogging trail, and sports courts.
  • Diverse dining options from casual breakfasts at Coral Café to poolside drinks and sandwiches at Blue Lagoon Saloon.
  • Convenient location, offering easy access to South Beach, Zoo Miami, and shopping at Dolphin Mall.

Whether visiting for business or leisure, the Hilton Miami Airport Blue Lagoon offers a peaceful retreat with its waterfront views and comprehensive amenities, making it an excellent choice for your Miami stay.
