About Best Western Plus Sands

Featuring an on-site restaurants and 2 bars, the Best Western Plus Sands is in the heart of Vancouver, only a 4-minute walk from English Bay beach. Free Wi-Fi is available in all rooms. A flat-screen cable TV is featured in each air-conditioned guest room at this hotel. A coffee machine is provided. A work desk is available. The Park restaurant specializes in fresh seafood and pasta and is open for breakfast, lunch and dinner. The Bayside Lounge offers views of English Bay with occasional live music. An on-site fitness center and relaxing sauna feature in the Vancouver Best Western Plus Sands. The front desk is available 24 hours a day. Guests can take advantage of the coin laundry facilities. Stanley Park, Coal Harbour, and Robson Street shopping area are all just over just over half a mile away. Third Beach is an 11-minute and Granville Island is a 10-minute drive away.
