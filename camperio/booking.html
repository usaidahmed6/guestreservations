About numa l Camperio Rooms & Apartments

Set 984 feet from The Last Supper, numa l Camperio Rooms & Apartments is an ancient noble residence, restored to provide luxurious rooms and apartment-style suites in the center of Milan, near the Sforzesco Castle and La Scala Theater. numa l Camperio Rooms & Apartments offers stylish rooms and self-catering suites for long or short stays. In-room amenities include a TV with satellite channels and free high-speed Wi-Fi access. Suites feature fully equipped modern kitchens and high-quality elegant furnishings. Guests at the aparthotel can enjoy a continental breakfast. Popular points of interest near the accommodations include San Maurizio al Monastero Maggiore, Sforzesco Castle and La Scala. The nearest airport is Milan Linate, 5 mi from numa l Camperio Rooms & Apartments.
