About Castletown House Apartments

Just 2 minutes walk from West Kensington Tube Station, these self-catering apartments and studios offer free WiFi and flat-screen TVs. Local supermarkets, restaurants, bars and cafe can be reached within 328 feet from the property. Living areas all feature a DVD player and sound system. Castletown House Apartments apartments also each have a bath or shower room, brightly decorated bedrooms and a flat-screen TV. Castletown House Apartmentss kitchens are equipped with a microwave, cooker, kettle and a range of utensils. A washing machine and ironing facilities are also provided (apart from in the compact studio). The Westfield shopping center, Europes largest, is less than 2 miles away. Heathrow Airport is 45 minutes away on the Tube, while the night life of the West End is a 20-minute Tube journey.
