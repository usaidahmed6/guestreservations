About Hilton Vienna Waterfront

Step into the Riverside Charm at Hilton Vienna Waterfront

Nestled on the serene banks of the Danube River, the Hilton Vienna Waterfront stands as a distinguished 4-star superior hotel, perfectly positioned just seven minutes from Vienna's vibrant city center by underground. Enjoy a unique blend of comfort and convenience where professional and leisure pursuits meet an invigorating riverside backdrop.

The Hilton Vienna Waterfront enhances each guest's stay with sophisticated amenities and a commitment to quality service. The spacious guest rooms, crafted for both relaxation and productivity, feature 37-inch LCD flat-screen TVs, air conditioning, and large work desks, boasting stunning views over the city skyline or the flowing Danube.

Indulge in authentic, seasonal farm-to-fork dishes at OXBO Dining, or relax at the laid-back OXBO Bar, where the atmosphere is casual yet refreshing. Enhance a visit with exclusive access to the Executive Lounge when booking an Executive room or suite, and enjoy additional perks like complimentary breakfast and refreshments throughout the day.

Strategically located for both business and pleasure, the Hilton Vienna Waterfront offers easy access to major attractions such as the Messe Wien Exhibition Center, the Prater amusement park, and much more.

  • Open-air riverside pool overlooking the Danube
  • Exclusive Executive Lounge with special privileges
  • On-site saunas, a 24/7 gym, and a volleyball court
  • Proximity to Stadion Underground Station and Vienna exhibition center
  • Direct access to jogging and bicycle paths along the Danube

Whether in town for an important meeting or a leisurely getaway, the Hilton Vienna Waterfront promises a visit that is as productive as it is pleasurable. Step into the best seat in Vienna by the beautiful Danube.
