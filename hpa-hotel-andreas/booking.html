About Hotel Pension Andreas

HPA Hotel Andreas is a traditional bed and breakfast in a quiet and very central location right behind the Town Hall, in Vienna’s 8th district. The Rathaus Underground Station (line U2) is just 656 feet away and the city center is a 5 to 10-minute walk away. The newly renovated rooms offer a shower, toilet, TV, and safe. WiFi is available in the rooms free of charge. Hotel Andreas is located close to the Vienna University and the major museums, only a few minutes’ walk from the Ringstraße. Tickets for the nearby parking garage at Otto-Wagner-Platz are available at the reception of HPA Hotel Andreas.
