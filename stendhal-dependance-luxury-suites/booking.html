About Stendhal Luxury Suites

Centrally located in Rome, Hotel Stendhal Luxury Suite is 295 feet from Barberini metro Station. This property offers free WiFi throughout and air-conditioned rooms. Elegant rooms will provide you with a satellite flat-screen TV and a mini-bar with free soft drinks and beer. Some feature a four-poster bed. The private bathroom includes a hairdryer. A sweet and savory breakfast is served each morning. Guests can also enjoy it in their rooms. At Hotel Stendhal Luxury Suite you will find a 24-hour front desk. Other facilities offered at the property include a shared lounge and luggage storage. Trevi Fountain is a 7-minute walk from the property. Termini Train Station is 2 metro stops away.
