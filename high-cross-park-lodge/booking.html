About High Cross Randwick by Sydney Lodges

High Cross Randwick by Sydney Lodges is a small family-owned boutique property, just a short walk from UNSW (University of NSW), Prince of Wales Hospital and all the Randwick Hospitals. Walk to the beach, shop in the city or try some of the amazing food in and around the buzzing streets of eastern Sydney. The property is just 20 minutes by light rail or bus to get to the exciting Sydney CBD and stunning Sydney Harbor. The property offers modern comforts such as wireless internet access and air-conditioning. The property also has an outdoor barbecue area for guests to enjoy. All rooms feature air-conditioning, free WiFi up to 1GB per day, flat-screen TV's, a small refrigerator and tea and coffee-making facilities. Each room also includes a private private bathroom with free toiletries. Premium linen and towels are included. High Cross Randwick by Sydney Lodges offers guests the use of a shared basic communal kitchen and guest laundry. With fabulous facilities and a great location, High Cross Randwick by Sydney Lodges is the perfect place to stay to experience the real Sydney. There are a number of restaurants and cafs in The Spot area, just a 5-minute walk from High Cross Randwick by Sydney Lodges.
