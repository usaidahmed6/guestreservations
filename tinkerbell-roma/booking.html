About TinkerBell

Located in Rome, 1640 feet from Vatican Museums, TinkerBell features air-conditioned rooms. WiFi is free throughout at this property set a 5-minute walk from Ottaviano Metro Station. Each with a memory foam mattress, rooms here include a flat-screen TV and an electric kettle in the room. The private bathroom has chromotherapy features, free toiletries and a hairdryer. You will find a shared kitchen at the property. St. Peter's Basilica is 0.6 mi from TinkerBell, while Via Cola di Rienzo shopping street is 10 minutes' walk away. Rome Ciampino Airport is 11 mi from the property.

Amenities

  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • Non-Smoking Facility
