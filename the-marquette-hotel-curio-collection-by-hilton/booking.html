About The Marquette Hotel, Curio Collection by Hilton

Welcome to The Marquette Hotel, Curio Collection by Hilton

Located in the heart of Minneapolis, within the iconic IDS Center, The Marquette Hotel, Curio Collection by Hilton, presents a unique blend of contemporary comfort and convenient amenities. Set against the cityscape, this hotel stands as a beacon of hospitality, offering guests a uniquely tailored experience with each stay.

Each room at The Marquette boasts a 42-inch flat-screen TV, alongside a fridge and safety deposit box, ensuring that convenience and security are at the forefront of every visit. The hotel's dedication to comfort is complemented by its strategic location, providing easy access to the Minneapolis Skyway system. This network of heated above-ground tunnels offers an array of shopping and dining options, ensuring that entertainment and exploration are just steps away.

Dining at The Marquette Hotel is a delight, with two distinctive options. The Jolliet House invites guests to enjoy classic American dishes while taking in serene views of an adjacent waterfall, creating a perfect dining ambiance. Alternatively, Jacques Restaurant & Bar offers a robust menu for lunch and dinner, along with a lively happy hour scene, ideal for unwinding after a busy day.

Just an 18-minute drive from the Minneapolis-Saint Paul International Airport, The Marquette Hotel offers unparalleled access to the city's vibrant heart, making it an ideal choice for both business travelers and leisure explorers alike.

  • Centrally located in Minneapolis' tallest building, providing spectacular city views.
  • Direct access to the Minneapolis Skyway, enhancing guest convenience in urban exploration.
  • Two on-site dining options featuring distinct culinary experiences and atmospheres.
  • Proximity to major transport links, including an easy commute to the international airport.
  • Comprehensive in-room amenities designed for comfort and security.
