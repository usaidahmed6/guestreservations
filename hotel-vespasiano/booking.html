About Hotel Vespasiano

Hotel Vespasiano is located in Rome, just 984 feet from the Vatican Museums. It offers elegant modern rooms and free WiFi throughout. Each elegant room comes with air conditioning, a TV, an electric kettle and a mini-bar. All rooms are fitted with a private bathroom. Extras include slippers, free toiletries and a hair dryer. There is a 24-hour front desk at the property. Ottaviano Metro Station is just 984 feet from the Vespasiano Hotel, while Via Cola di Rienzo shopping street is a 10-minute walk away.

Amenities
