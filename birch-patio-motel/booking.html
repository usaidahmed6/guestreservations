About Birch Patio Motel

This Fort Lauderdale, Florida hotel is a 5-minute walk to Fort Lauderdale Beach on the Atlantic Ocean. The hotel features an outdoor swimming pool and 2 barbecue facilities. The guests at Birch Patio Motel can play in an outdoor shuffle board court near tropical gardens. The hotel offers laundry facilities for guests. Rooms at the Birch Patio Motel Fort Lauderdale feature cable TV and free Wi-Fi. Rooms are equipped with a coffee maker and microwave. Birch Patio Motel is 2 miles from Las Olas Boulevard. The hotel is an 11-minute drive to downtown Fort Lauderdale.
