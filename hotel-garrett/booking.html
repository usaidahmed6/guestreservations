About Hotel Garrett

Our Stylish boutique hotel has recently undergone a complete makeover, now boasting stylish yet relaxed interiors with cozy designs. Whether you're a seasoned traveler or visiting for the first time, we offer all the essentials for a comfortable stay without breaking the bank. 

Relaxation awaits you as you step into our newly renovated haven, where a warm and inviting ambiance welcomes you. We take pride in providing a laid-back atmosphere that lets you unwind and recharge after a day of travel, meetings, or exploring San Francisco. 

As an added treat, guests of Hotel Garrett enjoy exclusive access to the heated rooftop pool at SoMa House, located just across the street. Take a dip, soak up the California sunshine, and enjoy breathtaking views of the San Francisco skyline. 

Come discover the exciting new look of Hotel Garrett – the absolute best value for budget-minded guests seeking the authentic San Francisco experience.
