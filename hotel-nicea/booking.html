About Hôtel Byakko Nice

Hôtel Byakko Nice is located in the center of Nice, a 5-minute walk from the SNCF station and within 20 minutes' walk of the Promenade des Anglais, the beach, the Old Town and Place Massena. Free WiFi access is available. The air-conditioned rooms feature satellite TV and a private bathroom. Guests are invited to relax on the sun terrace on the last floor with panoramic views of the city. Hôtel Byakko Nice include a 24-hour reception. Nice Côte d'Azur Airport is 5 mi away.
