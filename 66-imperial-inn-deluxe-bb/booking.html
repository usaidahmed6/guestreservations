About 66 Imperial Inn Guest House

Set in Rome's popular theater and shopping district, 66 Imperial Inn Deluxe B&B offers spacious rooms with Sky TV, air conditioning and a hydromassage shower. Free Wi-Fi is available at reception. You can walk to Termini Train Station in less than 10 minutes. 66 Imperial Inn is recommended by prestigious guide books. Start your day with a delicious continental breakfast and in the evening enjoy special rates at characteristic Italian restaurants nearby. You are just across the road from the Teatro dellOpera and a 10-minute walk from the Spanish Steps and the Coliseum.
