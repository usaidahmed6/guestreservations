About HOTEL 11, MOD a Sonesta Collection

Hotel 11 by Sonesta offers views of the Rocky Mountains, swimming pool, fitness center, and hot tub. Free WiFi is available throughout the property. Calgary International Airport is 1.2 mi away. Each air conditioned room at this hotel features a 50-inch flat-screen TV. Certain units have a seating area for your convenience. The rooms are fitted with a private bathroom including bath robes. Select rooms offer steam showers. Freeport Food & Drink restaurant and lounge is located on site for guests' enjoyment. Hotel 11 by Sonesta features a contemporary meeting space totaling 343 square yards. You will find free 24-hour shuttle service at the property. Free private parking is available on site. Calgary University is 6.2 mi from Hotel 11 by Sonesta, while Crossiron Mills Shopping Center is only 6.8 mi away.
