About Hotel Paseo del Arte, a member of Radisson Individuals

Located in Madrid's Art Triangle, Hotel Radisson RED Madrid is just 984 feet from Atocha AVE Train Station. This design hotel offers a restaurant and rooms with flat-screen satellite TV. Rooms at Radisson RED Madrid feature minimalist design and plenty of natural light. They all include air conditioning and a modern bathroom with a hairdryer and toiletries. This hotel offers a business center and 24-hour front desk. The hotel’s café overlooks Reina Sofia Museum. Hotel Radisson RED Madrid is within a 10-minute walk of the Thyssen-Bornemisza and Prado Museums. The popular tapas bars of Santa Ana Square are 2625 feet away, and the Retiro Park is 1969 feet away.
