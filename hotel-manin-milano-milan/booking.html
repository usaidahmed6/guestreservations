About Hotel Manin

With lovely views over Indro Montanelli Park, Hotel Manin is a 10-minute walk from La Scala Opera House and Milan Cathedral. Via Montenapoleone's exclusive shopping district is less than 820 feet away. Owned and run by the Colombo family since 1904, Hotel Manin is set in one of the oldest and more famous buildings in Milan. It features a glamorous outdoor terrace with a colorful garden. Rooms are elegant and come with satellite TV and a marble bathroom. Each has air conditioning, and Wi-Fi is free throughout the hotel. Restaurant Manin offers characteristic regional, Italian and international cuisine. Meals are served out in the garden during warm weather. Guests can also enjoy free access to the fitness room. The Rho Fiera Exhibition Center can be reached with a direct metro ride from Palestro Station, which is 2461 feet away. Linate Airport is a 5 mi away.
