About Hilton San Francisco Financial District

Experience the Vibrant Heart of San Francisco at Hilton San Francisco Financial District

Located at the crossroads of the Financial District, Chinatown, and North Beach, Hilton San Francisco Financial District offers a central and convenient stay in downtown San Francisco. With a focus on guest comfort and service, this hotel is ideal for both business and leisure travelers.

Each air-conditioned room features modern amenities such as a 55-inch flat-screen smart TV, coffee machine, large work desk, and ergonomic desk chair. The on-site 750 Restaurant and Bar serves diverse cuisine with a California influence, providing guests with a delicious dining experience right at the hotel.

Stay active at the 24-hour fitness center or take care of business with ease at the 24-hour business center. The award-winning concierge service is available to help plan your San Francisco adventures, ensuring a memorable stay.

  • Located at the intersection of the Financial District, Chinatown, and North Beach
  • On-site 750 Restaurant and Bar with California-inspired cuisine
  • Rooms equipped with 55-inch flat-screen smart TVs and ergonomic workspaces
  • 24-hour fitness and business centers
  • Close proximity to Fisherman’s Wharf and Alcatraz Island ferry

Discover the vibrant culture and iconic sights of San Francisco from the Hilton San Francisco Financial District. With its central location and exceptional amenities, this hotel provides the perfect base for exploring the city's diverse attractions.
