About No1 The Mansions By Mansley

No1 The Mansions is a self-catering accommodations located within a 10-minute walk of Earls Court Exhibition Center and Tube station.. Free WiFi access is available throughout. The apartments are tastefully decorated and all feature a comfortable king size bed, a living space in which to entertain or unwind and watch TV, and a modern kitchen with cooking utensils and high spec utilities. The luxury self-catering apartments are fully loaded with all the essentials and mod cons you would expect to make this your perfect home away from home, whether you are traveling for business or leisure. High Street Kensington is just over a 10-minute walk away and features a wide variety of shops, places to eat and nightlife. London Heathrow Airport can be reached in less than 40 minutes via Tube from Earls Court station.
