About Hilton Garden Inn Calgary Downtown

Hilton Garden Inn Calgary Downtown is set in the Downtown Calgary district in Calgary, 1969 feet from Calgary Telus Convention Center. Private parking is available on site for a cost of 25 CAD per day. Scotiabank Saddledome is a 4 minutes' drive away. Studio Bell is a 2 minutes' walk away. Every room at this hotel is air conditioned and is fitted with a flat-screen TV. Hilton Garden Inn Calgary Downtown features free WiFi. You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property. BMO Center is 0.6 mi from the Hilton Garden Inn Calgary Downtown. TELUS Convention Center is 1969 feet from Hilton Garden Inn Calgary Downtown, while Calgary Tower is 2297 feet away. Calgary International Airport is 6.2 mi from the property.
