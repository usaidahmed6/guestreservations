About Centara Villas Phuket Hotel

Enjoying beautiful views of the ocean, Centara Villas Phuket - SHA PLUS Certificate is located in Karon Beach. It also features a two-tiered outdoor pool with a waterfall. The villas at Centara Phuket face the sea and are surrounded by greenery. Fitted with wooden flooring and furnishings, each villa has satellite TV, a safety deposit box, and a mini-bar fridge. Bathrooms have a bathtub and shower. Guests can have a relaxing massage at Centara Spa, or join a Thai cooking class. The resort offers the convenience of a 24-hour reception, a tour desk, and a business center. A drop-off shuttle to Karon and Patong are also available for an additional charge. Buffet breakfast is served at the resort. Overlooking the Andaman Sea, The Cliff Restaurant features Asian and Mediterranean dishes. Thai and international dishes are offered at The Bayview Restaurant & Bar. The open-air Cliff Bar is great for sipping cocktails while enjoying sunset views.
