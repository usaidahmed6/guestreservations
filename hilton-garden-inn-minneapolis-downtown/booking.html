About Hilton Garden Inn Minneapolis Downtown

Steps from the Minneapolis Convention Center and the U.S. Bank Stadium, this hotel offers comfortable guestrooms with free wireless internet access and an on-site restaurant and is a perfect venue for concerts at the Armory. Hilton Garden Inn Minneapolis Downtown features an indoor pool and state-of-the-art fitness center. Guests can work in the 24-hour business center, then enjoy a meal in the hotel's Garden Grille & Bar or a snack from the 24-hour convenience store. Guestrooms at the Downtown Minneapolis Hilton Garden Inn boast 37-inch flat-screen TVs. Guests will also appreciate in-room microwaves and refrigerators.
