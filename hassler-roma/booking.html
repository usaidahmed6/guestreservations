About Hassler Roma

Experience Timeless Elegance at Hassler Roma

Perched atop the Spanish Steps, Hassler Roma stands as a landmark of timeless elegance in the heart of Rome. Known for its sublime service and exquisite accommodations, the hotel offers a unique vantage point with panoramic views over the eternal city, making it a preferred choice for travelers seeking the quintessence of luxury.

Each room and suite at Hassler Roma is a blend of classic decor and modern amenities, ensuring a stay that is both luxurious and comfortable. The interiors are adorned with satellite TV, air conditioning, and some feature spacious seating areas with sumptuous sofas.

  • Elevated dining experiences including the Michelin-starred Im√†go, and the vibrant Salone Eva and Palm Court restaurants
  • Complimentary high-speed WiFi throughout the property
  • Free wellness facilities, including a sauna, Turkish bath, and a fully equipped gym with rooftop city views
  • Prime location at the top of the Spanish Steps, close to Via dei Condotti and Spagna Metro station
  • Exclusive electric shuttle service for convenient access to nearby shopping areas

Hassler Roma not only offers a gateway to Rome's rich history and culture but also provides an oasis of tranquility with its well-appointed wellness facilities. Guests can unwind in the Turkish bath, rejuvenate in the sauna, or maintain their fitness regime in the state-of-the-art gym, all while enjoying sweeping views of Rome.

Just steps away from chic shopping streets and key cultural sites, Hassler Roma invites guests to indulge in a world of culinary delights, relax in palatial accommodations, and explore the vibrant surroundings of one of the world's most enchanting cities. Discover the pinnacle of Roman hospitality and sophisticated charm at Hassler Roma.
