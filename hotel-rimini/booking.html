About Hotel Rimini

Hotel Rimini is in Rome's central Via Marghera, 100 yards from the entrance of Termini Train Station, on the airport shuttles' side. There is free Wi-Fi throughout the property. All rooms at the Rimini have air conditioning and a private bathroom with bathtub/shower. Some rooms also have a balcony with city views. A buffet of hot and cold food is served in Rimini Hotel's panoramic breakfast room on the 5th floor, with unique views of the Eternal City. A few steps away, Termini metro station offers connections to the Spanish Steps, Coliseum and the Vatican Museums. The staff is happy to arrange city tours or book taxies.
