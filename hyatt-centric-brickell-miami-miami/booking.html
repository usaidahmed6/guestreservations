About Hyatt Centric Brickell Miami

Located in Miami, a 17-minute walk from Bayfront Park Station, Hyatt Centric Brickell Miami provides accommodations with an outdoor swimming pool, private parking, a fitness center and a shared lounge. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, room service and a 24-hour front desk, along with free WiFi throughout the property. The property is 0.9 miles from the city center and 1.4 miles from Bayside Market Place. Guest rooms at the hotel come with air conditioning, a seating area, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a safety deposit box and a private bathroom with a shower, free toiletries and a hairdryer. At Hyatt Centric Brickell Miami each room comes with bed linen and towels. The accommodation offers an la carte or American breakfast. Hyatt Centric Brickell Miami has a terrace. There's an on-site bar and guests can also use the business area. Bayfront Park is 1.5 miles from the hotel, while American Airlines Arena is 1.9 miles away. The nearest airport is Miami International Airport, 7.5 miles from Hyatt Centric Brickell Miami.
