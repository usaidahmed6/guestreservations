About Twinpalms Phuket

This resort is where minimalist style luxury meets traditional Thai design in a perfect location. The resort features the most fashionable and exclusive beach club of Phuket, located only 175 m away from the resort on the white sandy Surin Beach. Phuket International Airport is about 35 minutes by car from the hotel. Rooms are luxury and comfort, carefully designed to meet the needs of all guests. Guests will love the marvellous tropical water garden, a landscaped garden and pool area of more than 1,600 m2 with an Olympic-sized pool and a Lagoon-style pool surrounded by exotic plants in a romantic ambience. The hotel also features a gym for guests to maintain fitness and spa centre to relax and relieve stress. A sophisticated luxury experience that is second to none.
