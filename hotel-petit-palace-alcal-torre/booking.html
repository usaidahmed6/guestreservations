About Petit Palace Alcal

Set inside a 1930s skyscraper and one of Madrid's most emblematic buildings, Petit Palace Alcala is located just 820 feet from Puerta del Sol. Rooms are equipped with mini bar, air-conditioning, flat-screen TV, free WiFi, and a private bathroom. Bikes are available for free at the reception, subject to availability. The hotel offers buffet breakfast. Free hot drinks are available in the hotel's reception. Staff at the 24-hour reception can provide information about what to see and do in Madrid. Casino de Madrid is just 328 feet away, and Madrid's Círculo de Bellas Artes, a multidisciplinary center with one of the most dynamic cultural programs in the world, is just a 3-minute walk away. The Prado, Reina Sofía and Thyssen Museums are all within 15 minutes’ walk. Sevilla Metro Station is right outside the hotel, and Atocha Train Station is a 15-minute walk away.
