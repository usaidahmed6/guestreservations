About Earls Court Garden Hotel

Located 3 minutes’ walk from Earl’s Court Underground Station, Earl’s Court Gardens Hotel offers budget accommodations and free WiFi in public areas. Hyde Park, The Natural History Museum and The Victoria & Albert Museum are just a 15-minute walk away. Each room has an private bathroom, a TV and tea and coffee making facilities. Earls Court Exhibition Center is 5 minutes away on foot, while Kensington and Chelsea can be reached in a 15-minute walk. The Royal Albert Hall is 20 minutes’ walk away, while Harrods and the shops of Knightsbridge are 30 minutes away on foot. West Brompton Rail Station is a 10-minute walk away.
