About Mornington Hotel London Victoria

Mornington Hotel London Victoria is set in London, 0.8 mi from Buckingham Palace, and provides free WiFi. Open since 1900, the property is within 0.9 mi of Westminster Abbey, St James's Park and Houses of Parliament. Local points of interest like Big Ben and Harrods are reachable within a 19-minute walk and 1.2 mi, respectively. Guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Every room is fitted with a private bathroom with free toiletries. Banqueting House is 1.2 mi from Mornington Hotel London Victoria, while Battersea is 1.2 mi away. London City Airport is 8.1 mi from the property.
