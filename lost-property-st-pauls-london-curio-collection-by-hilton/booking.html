About Lost Property St Pauls London, Curio Collection By Hilton

Well situated in the center of London, Lost Property St Pauls London, Curio Collection By Hilton has air-conditioned rooms, a fitness center, free WiFi and a restaurant. This 4-star hotel offers room service and luggage storage space. Guests can use a bar. All rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV and free toiletries. Guests at Lost Property St Pauls London, Curio Collection By Hilton can enjoy a buffet breakfast. Speaking Czech, English, Spanish and Estonian, staff at the 24-hour front desk can help you plan your stay. Popular points of interest near the accommodation include St Paul's Cathedral, Sky Garden and Somerset House. The nearest airport is London City Airport, 7.5 miles from Lost Property St Pauls London, Curio Collection By Hilton.
