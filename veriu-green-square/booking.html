About Veriu Green Square

Modern Elegance Meets Industrial Chic at Veriu Green Square

Steeped in the rich history of a luxury European auto-trader from the 1970s, Veriu Green Square emerges as a beacon of contemporary style and sophistication in Sydney. Opened in 2019, this hotel marries polished chrome accents and high gloss finishes with modern amenities, creating a unique accommodation experience. Conveniently located south of the Sydney CBD and merely a 4-minute train ride from Kingsford Smith Airport, Veriu Green Square offers unparalleled access to the city's bustling heart.

  • Guest rooms equipped with free WiFi and a flat-screen Smart TV, enabling guests to log into Netflix for personalized entertainment.
  • Striking design elements that pay homage to the building's automotive history, blending industrial chic with luxury.
  • Prime location just 328 feet from Green Square station, offering effortless access to Sydney's attractions.
  • Complimentary bike hire, encouraging guests to explore the vibrant surroundings in an eco-friendly manner.
  • Proximity to Allianz Stadium and Darling Harbor, making it easy for guests to experience Sydney's premier sporting events and waterfront attractions.

Veriu Green Square is not just a place to stay; it's a destination that captures the essence of Sydney's dynamic culture and the spirit of its past. Whether you're in town for business or leisure, Veriu Green Square provides a stylish, comfortable base from which to explore all that Sydney has to offer.

Discover a new way to experience Sydney at Veriu Green Square, where every stay is an opportunity to immerse yourself in a world where contemporary luxury and industrial heritage converge. Welcome to your Sydney home, where every detail is crafted for your enjoyment and comfort.
