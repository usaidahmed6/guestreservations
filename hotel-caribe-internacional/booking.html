About Hotel Caribe Internacional Cancun

Set in Cancún, 1312 feet from Cancun Bus Station, Hotel Caribe Internacional Cancun offers accommodations with a seasonal outdoor swimming pool, free private parking, a bar and a shared lounge. This 3-star hotel features free WiFi in common areas and a garden. The accommodations features a 24-hour front desk, a concierge service and currency exchange for guests. At the hotel every room includes air conditioning, a desk, a patio with pool view, a private bathroom, a flat-screen TV, bed linen and towels. At Hotel Caribe Internacional Cancun, the rooms are equipped with a seating area. The accommodations offers a continental or à la carte breakfast. Popular points of interest near Hotel Caribe Internacional Cancun include Cancun Government Palace, Beto Avila Stadium and Cristo Rey Church. The nearest airport is Cancún International Airport, 8.7 mi from the hotel.
