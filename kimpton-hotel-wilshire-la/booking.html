About Kimpton Hotel Wilshire

Discover the Modern Charm of Kimpton Hotel Wilshire

Experience the vibrant energy of Los Angeles at Kimpton Hotel Wilshire, a contemporary oasis nestled in the heart of the city. Just a short walk from iconic attractions like the Los Angeles County Museum of Art, La Brea Tar Pits, and the Petersen Automotive Museum, this hotel offers an ideal blend of convenience and comfort. Guests can unwind by the rooftop pool, soak up the sun on the furnished terrace, or explore the local sights with ease.

The rooms and suites at Kimpton Hotel Wilshire are designed with modern travelers in mind, featuring flat-screen cable TVs and iPod docking stations. Private bathrooms are equipped with slippers and plush bathrobes, ensuring a restful stay. Select accommodations provide stunning area views, perfect for taking in the city's dynamic landscape.

  • Rooftop pool with furnished sun terrace
  • Concierge service for personalized recommendations
  • Business center with laptop stations
  • Convenient location near top cultural attractions
  • 24-hour reception for around-the-clock assistance

Whether visiting for business or leisure, Kimpton Hotel Wilshire offers a seamless experience with its thoughtful amenities and prime location. Enjoy a blend of modern conveniences and the vibrant spirit of Los Angeles, making every stay memorable and productive.
