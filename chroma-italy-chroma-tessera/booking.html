About Chroma Italy - Chroma Tessera

Offering modern design rooms with chromotherapy and aromatherapy, Chroma Tessera is 656 feet from Castro Pretorio Metro Station in Rome's University district. Wi-Fi is free. Set on Via dell'Universit√†, Chroma Tessera is next to La Sapienza University campus and 5 minutes' walk from Termini Train Station. There is a bus stop right in front of the building. Each room has a different color scheme, complimented by the chromotherapy bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries. All rooms are air conditioned and feature an LCD TV, mini-bar and kettle with a selection of teas. Some overlook the city. Rooms are located on the third floor of a building with elevator. A luggage storage area is available, as well as a fax and photocopying service.
