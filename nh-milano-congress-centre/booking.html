About NH Milano Congress Centre

NH Milano Congress Center is located inside the Milanofiori Shopping Center, 820 feet from Assago Milanofiori Forum Metro Station. It offers free Wi-Fi and free parking. Rooms are air conditioned and feature a TV, mini-bar and private bathroom at NH Milano Congress Center. This NH hotel has 2 restaurants, serving Italian cuisine and dishes from the Lombardy region. Breakfast is buffet style and meals are served out in the garden during summer. 32 meeting rooms with various capacity are available at the property. The business center, located nearby the bar, has a free tablet and a working area. The NH Milano Congress Center is located right next to the popular sports and music venue of Mediolanum Forum. It is also within easy reach of Milan's ring road and the A7 Motorway.
