About Hotel Exe Moncloa

Explore Madrid from Hotel Exe Moncloa

Hotel Exe Moncloa, situated next to Intercambiador de Moncloa Station, stands as a prime choice for travelers seeking a stylish and convenient base in Madrid. This hotel offers excellent connectivity to the city's key attractions and business districts, ensuring a stay that is both comfortable and convenient.

Each room in Hotel Exe Moncloa is a testament to modern elegance, featuring bright dcor, smart wooden floors, flat-screen satellite TV, and a mini-bar. The well-appointed bathrooms come equipped with hairdryers and complimentary toiletries, adding to the comfort of your stay. Guests can enjoy free Wi-Fi throughout their stay, ensuring connection beyond the hotel.

The hotel is not just about stylish rooms; it also offers exceptional facilities that cater to the needs of both leisure and business travelers. A small outdoor pool provides a perfect escape from the summer heat, while the roof terrace offers panoramic views of the city, making it an ideal spot for relaxation or casual meetings.

  • Small outdoor pool to unwind and relax
  • Gym with sauna for health and fitness
  • Rooftop terrace with stunning city views
  • Direct access to Intercambiador de Moncloa Station
  • Proximity to Casa de Campo Park and Plaza Espaa Square

Located just off the A-6 Motorway and a short drive from Madrid Barajas Airport, Hotel Exe Moncloa is perfectly positioned for exploring Madrids rich culture, entertainment, and business sectors. Whether you are visiting for leisure or work, Hotel Exe Moncloa offers a refined and accessible experience in one of Europes most vibrant capitals.
