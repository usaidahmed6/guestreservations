About Khaosan Tokyo Samurai

Located within a 10-minute walk from both Asakusa Subway Station and the famed Sensoji Temple, Khaosan Tokyo Samurai Capsule offers cozy dormitory accommodations in Tokyo. The dormitory rooms are fitted with multiple bunk beds and a large window. Each bed comes with a locker, a coat hook, a shelf, and a reading light. Bathroom facilities are shared with other guests. Guests can relax at the Japanese-style shared lounge area, use the kitchen to prepare their own meals, or socialize at the rooftop terrace. Samurai Capsule Khaosan Tokyo is just a 3-minute walk away from the nearest supermarket. From Asakusa Subway Station, Tokyo SkyTree is just a 3-minute train ride away.
