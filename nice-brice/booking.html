About Best Western Plus Hotel Brice Garden

Best Western Plus Htel Brice Garden Nice is located in central Nice, just a 5-minute walk from the Promenade des Anglais and the beach. It offers a garden with a fountain, where you can enjoy breakfast weather permitting. Each guest room at Best Western Plus Htel Brice Garden Nice is equipped with satellite TV, a mini fridge, a desk and a private bathroom. A buffet breakfast is served in the breakfast room every morning. Guests can enjoy a drink from the hotel bar on the terrace under the orange and lemon trees. The hotel is just a 10-minute walk from the tram line and Nices central train station. It is ideally located for exploring the city and nearby towns such as Monte-Carlo and Cannes.
