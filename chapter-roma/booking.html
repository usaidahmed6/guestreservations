About Chapter Roma

Set in a 19th-century building in Romeâs center, Chapter Roma is a 5-minute walk from Campo de' Fiori square, and just 2953 feet from the Pantheon and Piazza Navona square. It offers free Wi-Fi throughout and air-conditioned accommodations. Each room includes a Smart TV and a mini-bar. The bathroom comes with bathrobes and slippers, free toiletries and a hairdryer. Breakfast is a continental buffet, complete with Italian pastries, cold cuts and hot drinks. The hotel is less than a 5-minute walk from the lively Trastevere district.
