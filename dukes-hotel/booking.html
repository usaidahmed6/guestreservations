About Dukes London

Timeless British Charm Awaits at Dukes London

Nestled discreetly off Piccadilly in the heart of Mayfair, Dukes London offers a quintessential British experience that is both historic and sophisticated. Ideally situated for exploring iconic landmarks such as Green Park, Buckingham Palace, and Regent Street, this hotel is perfect for those seeking a blend of tradition and modern convenience.

Dukes London boasts rooms with classic British style, featuring fine details such as 24-hour room service, plush bathrobes, and free Wi-Fi. The hotel is renowned for its inviting atmosphere and exceptional service, making it an ideal choice for discerning travelers.

  • Indulge in a signature martini at the famous Dukes Bar, known for its cool, sophisticated ambiance.
  • Savor classic British cuisine crafted by chef Nigel Mendham at the GBR, Great British Restaurant.
  • Relax in the hotel's cigar garden, offering a selection of fine cigars and premium cognacs.
  • Enjoy a serene afternoon tea in the intimate setting of the Drawing Room.
  • Take advantage of the hotel's prime location, within walking distance to top theaters and exquisite shops.

With its rich history and impeccable service, Dukes London provides a unique and memorable stay for every guest. Whether exploring the vibrant local area or unwinding in the elegant surroundings, an unforgettable experience awaits at Dukes London.
