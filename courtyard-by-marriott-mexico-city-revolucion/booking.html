About Courtyard by Marriott Mexico City Revolucion

This Mexico City hotel is located less than 0.6 mi away from the World Trade Center. The hotel offers free WiFi and a night club at the weekend. Courtyard by Marriott Mexico City Revolucion features a 32-inch flat-screen TV, in-room movies, a coffee maker and ironing facilities in each guest room. Some rooms include a buffet breakfast, DVD player and bathrobes. Guests can use the fitness center or the business center at the Courtyard by Marriott Mexico City Revolucion. A game room are also available. The San Pedro de los Pinos subway station is about 3 blocks away.
