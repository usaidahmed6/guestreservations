About Altitude at Krystal Grand Cancun - All Inclusive

Set in Cancn, a 4-minute walk from Coco Bongo, Altitude at Krystal Grand Cancun - All Inclusive offers an outdoor swimming pool and fitness center. The property has a bar, as well as a restaurant serving Mexican cuisine. Free WiFi is available. At the hotel, each room includes a wardrobe and a flat-screen TV. Rooms are equipped with a coffee machine and a private bathroom, while certain rooms come with a balcony. A continental breakfast can be enjoyed at the property. Altitude at Krystal Grand Cancun - All Inclusive offers a terrace. Speaking English and Spanish at the reception, staff are ready to help around the clock. La Isla Shopping Mall is 2.1 mi from the accommodations. The nearest airport is Cancn International Airport, 11 mi from the property.
