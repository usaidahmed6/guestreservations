About Hotel Mirage, Sure Hotel Collection by Best Western

With a stay at Hotel Mirage, Sure Hotel Collection by Best Western in Milan (Certosa), you'll be a 4-minute drive from San Siro Race Course and 6 minutes from Castello Sforzesco. This 4-star hotel is 1.3 mi (2.1 km) from Milan Convention Centre and 2.3 mi (3.6 km) from Sempione Park. Make yourself at home in one of the 86 guestrooms featuring minibars and Smart televisions. Your bed comes with down comforters and premium bedding. Complimentary wired and wireless Internet access keeps you connected, and satellite programming provides entertainment. Private bathrooms with showers feature complimentary toiletries and bidets.
