About Best Western Blu Hotel Roma

This property offers a Special Protection Program, a strict program of precise safeguards dedicated to our guests and our staff. Set in the lively residential neighborhood of Portonaccio, Best Western Blu Hotel Roma is 0.6 mi from Tiburtina Train and Metro Station. This 4-star property offers a restaurant, a bar, and free WiFi throughout. Air conditioned and soundproofed, rooms here feature a classic or contemporary décor, satellite flat-screen TV and a mini-bar. Each has a private bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries. A sweet and savory buffet-style breakfast is served daily and includes eggs, cold meats and cakes, as well as hot and cold drinks. The Grano Duro Restaurant specializes in local and classic Italian cuisine. Staff at Blu Hotel Roma can help you with ticket services, car rentals and airport transfers. Tours to the center of Rome and surrounding areas like Tivoli, can also be booked on site.
