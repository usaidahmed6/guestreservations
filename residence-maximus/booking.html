About HT Residence Maximus

Residence Maximus is in Rome's central Celio hill, right above the Colosseum. It has guest rooms as well as apartments with cooking facilities, and all units feature free Wi-Fi and a flat-screen TV. The rooms are cleaned daily and each has a private bathroom. The apartments included a kitchenette and a washing machine, which are available for longer stays. All units are furnished with modern furniture. The area surrounding the Maximus Residence is also famous for the clubs and restaurants of the pedestrian area in Via di San Giovanni in Laterano. Colosseo Metro Station, line B, is just just over a quarter mile away and is 2 stops from Termini Train Station. Trams and public buses stop nearby Via Labicana.
