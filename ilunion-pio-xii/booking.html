About Ilunion Pio XII

This design hotel offers free Wi-Fi and a pillow menu. Direct buses to Puerta del Sol stop outside, and Madrids IFEMA Exhibition Center is a 10-minute drive away. The contemporary, air-conditioned rooms at the Ilunion Pio XII feature satellite TV, and have a private bathroom complete with a hairdryer. This hotel is officially adapted for guests with reduced mobility, accessible rooms are available on request, also for people with auditory and visual impairment. The Pio XIIs restaurant offers a daily breakfast, as well as la carte and set menus. There is also a special menu for celiacs and food intolerant guests. Guest also can enjoy of their international cuisine for lunch and dinner and finishing with a drink in the hotels caf-bar. Ilunion Pio XII has internet points with free internet access for guests. The hotel also offers information on the surrounding area and a ticket service. Chamartin Station and Pio XII Metro Station are a 15-minute walk from the hotel. Madrid-Barajas Airport is a 15-minute drive from the hotel. You can hire a car from the 24-hour reception, and on-site underground parking is available for an extra charge.
