About Hotel Vienna

Hotel Vienna is only 656 feet from Lambrate Metro and Train Stations, which provide quick connection with Milan's center. It offers functional rooms with flat-screen TV and classic wooden furniture. Rooms have simple dcor a private bathroom with toiletries and hairdryer. Drinks can be enjoyed at the bar, open until late. The family-run Vienna Hotel is 10 minutes' walk from Politecnico di Milano, and 3.1 mi from University of Milan Bicocca. The Tangenziale Est bypass is 1.2 mi away and paid public parking is available in the property's surrounding area.
