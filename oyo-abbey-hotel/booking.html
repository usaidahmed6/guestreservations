About Abbey Hotel

Less than a 5-minute walk from the O2 Shepherds Bush Empire, a music venue, the Abbey is located opposite the Westfield London Shopping Center. Free WiFi. Bars and restaurants are in the immediate area and Shepherds Bush Market Underground Station is just 984 feet away. Hyde Park, Marble Arch and Oxford Streets shops are all within a 20-minute tube journey. Queens Park Rangers Football Club and the Olympia National Exhibition Center are both a 5-minute taxi journey away. A TV is included in each basic room at the Abbey Hotel. All rooms also have a private bathroom.
