About Hotel 55 Fifty-Five - Maison d'Art Collection

Featuring a bar, Hotel 55 Fifty-Five - Maison d'Art Collection is located in the center of Rome, a 6-minute walk from Spanish Steps. This 4-star hotel offers a concierge service and a tour desk. The property provides a 24-hour front desk, airport transportation, room service and free WiFi throughout the property. The hotel will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms offering a closet, an electric tea pot, a minibar, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a bidet. Selected rooms come with a kitchen with a fridge and a microwave. A buffet, continental or Italian breakfast is served at the property. Popular points of interest near Hotel 55 Fifty-Five - Maison d'Art Collection include Trevi Fountain, Barberini Metro Station and Piazza Barberini. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino Airport, 9.3 miles from the accommodation.
