About Meliá Avenida América

Located just off the A2 Motorway, this hotel is 15 minutes’ drive from central Madrid. The air-conditioned rooms at the Meliá Avenida América feature modern décor. Each one has satellite TV and free Wi-Fi. The hotel offers some superior rooms and the exclusive service The Level with premium amenities. In The Level lounge guests can have a private buffet breakfast and taste tapas and snacks. There is also a working area with internet access, fax and printer. The hotel features a convention center with 32 rooms for up to 1000 people, available for an extra cost. Madrid’s Barajas Airport is a 10-minute drive from the Avenida América and the IFEMA Convention Center is just over 1.2 mi away. Buses to Plaza Mayor leave from a stop just outside the hotel.
