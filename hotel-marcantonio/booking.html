About Hotel Marcantonio

Just 656 feet from Roma Termini Train Station, Hotel Marcantonio is set in a 19th-century building. Rooms have an LCD TV, and WiFi is free throughout. Staff serve freshly brewed Italian coffee in the morning. Decorated in a modern style, rooms at the Marcantonio all have air conditioning, double-glazing windows and wooden furniture. The private bathroom has a shower. A simple sweet Italian breakfast is served daily in your room. The surrounding area has many cafés and restaurants serving Italian, North African and Asian cuisine. Santa Maria Maggiore Basilica is 656 feet from the hotel. The Opera House is a 5-minute walk away. Termini Metro is 328 feet from the property.

Amenities

  • Room Service
  • 24 hour front desk
  • Concierge
  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
