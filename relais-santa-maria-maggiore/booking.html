About Relais Santa Maria Maggiore

Located in Rome center, Relais Santa Maria Maggiore features air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi. The property is a few steps from Santa Maria Maggiore. The property is 2953 feet from Coliseum. Each room in this bed and breakfast is equipped with a kettle and includes a private bathroom with free toiletries. You will also find a flat-screen TV and a hairdryer. Relais Santa Maria Maggiore is a 10-minute walk from Termini Train Station with links to Rome Fiumicino Airport, 19 mi away.

Amenities

  • Room Service
  • 24 hour front desk
  • Concierge
  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • Non-Smoking Facility
