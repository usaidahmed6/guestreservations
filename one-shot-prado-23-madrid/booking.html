About One Shot Prado 23

Centrally located, One Shot Prado 23 offers design-rooms with TV, and free Wi-Fi. Just 492 feet from Plaza Santa Ana Square, the hotel is a 7-minute walk from Puerta del Sol. Fitted with wooden flooring, each soundproof room features modern, functional décor. The mirror TV has a HDMI connection. The bathroom has a large rain-effect shower, and there is also a safe, an à la carte mini-bar and a dressing area. You can find Latina District nearby, it's popular for its traditional tapas bars in a 10-minute walk. The famous Reina Sofía Museum is 0.6 mi away, while the Thyssen Museum is only 656 feet away. The Prado Museum is 1640 feet away, and charming Retiro Park can be reached on foot in 20 minutes. Airport can be reached via public transport in 40 minutes. The closest metro stations are Anton Martin and Sevilla.
