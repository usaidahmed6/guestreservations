About Atrium Hotel Vancouver

Only 3.7 mi from Vancouver city center. Atrium Inn Vancouver offers a complimentary hot and cold buffet breakfast, free WiFi, and free underground parking. A shuttle service to the city center, Canada Place, and cruise terminal is available free of charge. Guests at the Vancouver Atrium Inn can take advantage of in-room coffeemakers and weekday newspapers. Guests can enjoy the on-site fitness center. The hotel's restaurant, Pressbox Pub, is open for dinner. This property is less than 10 minutes' walk from Playland Amusement Park. Mount Seymour is 10 minutes' drive away.
