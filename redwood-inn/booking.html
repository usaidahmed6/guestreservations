About Redwood Inn

This hotel is located on Lombard Street in San Francisco and is one mile from Fishermans Wharf. The hotel features free parking and rooms with free Wi-Fi. Redwood Inn rooms include a refrigerator and cable TV. The rooms also feature private bathrooms. Inn Redwood San Francisco is 5 blocks from Ghirardelli Square, which features shopping. The Maritime Museum San Francisco is within a 15-minute walk of the hotel.

Amenities

  • 24 hour front desk
  • Non-Smoking Facility
