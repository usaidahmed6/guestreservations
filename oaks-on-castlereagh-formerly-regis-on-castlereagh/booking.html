About Oaks Sydney Castlereagh Suites

Centrally located in Haymarket in Sydney city, Oaks Sydney Castlereagh Suites is just 5 minutes' walk from Central Station and a 10-minute walk from Darling Harbor. Offering various self-contained apartment options with free WiFi, the property also features an on-site fitness center, swimming pool and room service via online food delivery. The apartments offer air conditioning and a large open-plan living area with a flat-screen TV with streaming capabilities. Most apartments include a private balcony with panoramic city views. Surrounded by restaurants, bars and cafes, these modern apartments are just 656 feet from Sydneys Capitol Theater and only a 10-minute walk from some of Sydneys best shopping along Pitt Street Mall. Oaks Sydney Castlereagh Suites offers business and secretarial services, barbecue facilities and guest laundry facilities. Secure covered car parking and broadband internet access are available.
