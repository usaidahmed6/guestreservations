About DoubleTree By Hilton Hotel Minneapolis Park Place

Welcome to DoubleTree By Hilton Hotel Minneapolis Park Place

Discover the charm of Minneapolis at DoubleTree By Hilton Hotel Minneapolis Park Place, perfectly positioned on the fringe of downtown. This welcoming haven stands just 3.1 miles from the buzzing Minneapolis Convention Center, inviting guests to enjoy a blend of convenience and serenity. With proximity to notable landmarks such as the Target Center, Target Field, and U.S. Bank Stadium, home of the Minnesota Vikings, explorers and sports enthusiasts alike will find themselves in the heart of the action. Moreover, the iconic Mall of America is merely a short drive away, offering a retail and entertainment experience like no other.

Begin each morning with a delightful full continental breakfast buffet at Dover Restaurant and Bar, an on-site culinary gem. Dover doesn't just stop at breakfast; it offers a delectable array of savory dishes for lunch and dinner, ensuring your dining needs are splendidly met throughout your stay. In the evening, unwind in the bar with a glass of wine in hand, captivated by the latest sporting event on one of the five flat-screen TVs.

  • Strategic location near downtown Minneapolis, offering easy access to major venues and attractions
  • Start the day right with a full continental breakfast buffet at the on-site Dover Restaurant and Bar
  • Dover Restaurant and Bar serves savory lunch and dinner options
  • Unwind with a glass of wine while watching sports on one of the five flat-screen TVs in the bar
  • Short drive to the world-renowned Mall of America for an unmatched shopping and entertainment experience

DoubleTree By Hilton Hotel Minneapolis Park Place extends a warm welcome, inviting guests to discover the vibrant city of Minneapolis from a location that combines convenience with comfort. Whether here for business or leisure, this hotel promises a stay filled with memorable moments and unparalleled ease.
