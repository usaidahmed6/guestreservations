About Sb Park Mansion

Just a 7-minute drive to Phuket city center, SB PARK MANSION offers free Wi-Fi throughout the property. Boasting an outdoor pool, the hotel provides free private parking on site. The hotel is a 2-minute drive to Central Festival Phuket Mall. Patong Beach is a 15-minute ride away, while Promthep Cape view point is a 30-minute drive from the hotel. It takes 40 minutes to drive to Phuket International Airport. Fitted with a balcony, each air-conditioned room features satellite TV, fridge, electric kettle and sofa. Shower facilities are included in an private bathroom. Staff at the 24-hour front desk can offer assistance with shuttle arrangements. Free luggage storage is available. Laundry services are provided at a fee. Local restaurants are located within walking distance.
