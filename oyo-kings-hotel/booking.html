About OYO Kings Hotel

With a stay at OYO Kings Hotel in London (London City Centre), you'll be within a 5-minute drive of Russell Square and Theatreland. This hotel is 1.3 mi (2.1 km) from The British Museum and 1.5 mi (2.3 km) from Oxford Street. Stay in one of 45 guestrooms featuring flat-screen televisions. Complimentary wireless Internet access is available to keep you connected. Bathrooms with showers are provided. Conveniences include coffee/tea makers, and housekeeping is provided daily. A complimentary continental breakfast is served daily from 7:00 AM to 9:00 AM.
