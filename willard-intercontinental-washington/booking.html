About Willard InterContinental Washington

Discover the Willard InterContinental Washington: A Monumental Experience

Step into the heart of history at the Willard InterContinental Washington, where sophistication meets convenience just steps from the White House. This iconic hotel, nestled on Pennsylvania Avenue, offers guests an unparalleled stay amidst the vibrancy of the nation's capital. With a storied past and a commitment to exceptional service, the Willard InterContinental is more than just a place to stay; it's a part of American history.

  • Historic luxury hotel with a prime location 1581 feet from the White House, offering a unique blend of heritage and modernity.
  • Exclusive on-site dining at Café du Parc, featuring traditional French fare in a chic setting, with a dedicated wine bar for evening relaxation.
  • 24-hour fitness center and sauna, ensuring wellness and relaxation are always within reach.
  • Comprehensive concierge services, a 24-hour lounge for seamless comfort, and a florist to add a touch of elegance to your stay.
  • Proximity to the International Spy Museum and Metro Center subway station, placing the best of Washington, D.C., at your doorstep.

Each guest room in the Willard InterContinental Washington exudes charm and sophistication, equipped with a flat-screen TV, mini-bar, and coffee maker. A work desk and stereo complement the space, making it perfect for both leisure and business travelers. The hotel's commitment to convenience is evident in its array of services, including shops, a private limo service, and the allure of pastries available between meals. Discover a landmark of luxury and a gateway to the past at the Willard InterContinental Washington, where every stay is a journey through history.
