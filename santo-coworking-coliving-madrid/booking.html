About Santo - Coworking - Coliving - Madrid

Santo - Coworking - Coliving - Madrid in Madrid provides adults only accommodations with a shared lounge, a garden and a terrace. Among the facilities at this property are a shared kitchen and a business center, along with free WiFi throughout the property. All rooms are equipped with a balcony. The rooms is fitted with air conditioning, a microwave, a fridge, an electric tea pot, a shower, a hairdryer and a desk. The hostel has certain accommodations that have a patio and city view, and rooms include a shared bathroom and a closet. All rooms feature bed linen. You can play pool at Santo - Coworking - Coliving - Madrid. Popular points of interest near the accommodation include Gran Via, Gran Via Station Metro Station and Plaza de Espaa Metro Station. The nearest airport is Adolfo Suarez Madrid-Barajas Airport, 8.1 miles from Santo - Coworking - Coliving - Madrid.
