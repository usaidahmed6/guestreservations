About Ibis Budget Sydney East

Located in the heart of Kings Cross, ibis budget Sydney East offers budget accommodations just 10 minutes walk from Hyde Park, Oxford Street and the Australian Museum. The famous Bondi Beach is a 15-minute drive. The air-conditioned rooms feature an private bathroom and a TV. Some rooms offer Sydney Harbor views. Continental breakfast is served every morning. There are also a number of restaurants and cafs within a 2-minute walk. Surrounded by Sydneys popular pubs, clubs and nightlife, ibis budget Sydney East is just 30 minutes' walk from the iconic Sydney Opera House & Sydney Cricket Grounds (SCG).
