About Gran Hotel Sercotel Conde Duque

Sercotel Gran Hotel Conde Duque is located in the center of Madrid. The elegant, air-conditioned rooms at Sercotel Gran Hotel Conde Duque feature a private bathroom with a shower and amenities. They also include a free laptop-sized safe, pillow menu and a flat-screen TV. San Bernardo Metro Station is conveniently located 656 feet away. The famous Centro Cultural Galileo Galilei and Clamores concert halls are in the surrounding area. The hotel offers a charming Tea Room with more than 70 types of tea. Guests have free access to an onsite gym. The buffet breakfast includes a corner with healthy, organic food. Staff can also take guests on a tapas route.

Amenities

  • Room Service
  • 24 hour front desk
