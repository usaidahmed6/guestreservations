About Brewsters Mountain Lodge

Brewster Mountain Lodge is centrally located in downtown Banff. Log furnishings, western dcor, and historic photos from the Brewster Family define the interior of the lodge. Free WiFi and a daily continental breakfast are included. Each room is equipped with a flat-screen cable TV, fan, ironing facilities, hair dryer, kettle and coffee machine. The private bathroom comes with a shower, bath and free toiletries. At Brewster Mountain Lodge you will find a 24-hour front desk. Other facilities offered at the property include a tour desk, ski storage, and shops on site. The hotel is 656 feet from The Whyte Museum of the Canadian Rockies, 984 feet from Banff Park Museum and 0.7 mi from The Banff Center.
