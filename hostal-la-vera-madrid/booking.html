About Hostal La Vera

This pleasant guest house is set in a 19th century building formerly owned by the Dukes of Santoña. It offers a free Wi-Fi zone and comfortable rooms, 328 feet from Anton Martin Metro Station. Rooms at Hostal La Vera are bright and feature modest décor, central heating and a TV. Bathrooms are shared or private. You can enjoy breakfast at the hotel’s cafeteria, 2 minutes’ walk away. Various bars, restaurants and shops are within 5 minutes’ walk and Puerta del Sol and Plaza Mayor are 8 minutes’ walk away. La Vera is well located, 10 minutes’ walk from the famous Art Triangle and within 15 minutes’ walk of the Royal Palace and Madrid Cathedral. Atocha AVE Train and Metro Station is 10 minutes away by foot.
