About OYO Hotel Miami OK I-44

OYO Hotel Miami OK I-44 hotel is less than one-half mile away from the birthplace of historic Route 66, The Main Street of America. This hotel is minutes from local points of interest like Stables Casino and the Dobson Museum. The Miami BMX track is adjacent to the hotel.

Sports enthusiasts will enjoy a visit to nearby Mickey Mantle Museum. For those craving outdoor adventure, Grand Lake is 16.1 km away and provides the perfect backdrop for boating, fishing, swimming, hiking or picnicking. A number of parks, recreation areas, and golf courses like Miami Golf & Country Club and Peoria Ridge are in the vicinity. Several entertainment venues, specialty stores, antique shops and shopping malls are close by. Enjoy a variety of restaurants, unique eateries and cocktail lounges near the hotel.

This hotel offers amenities and features, including: free wireless high-speed Internet access, free local calls, seasonal outdoor pool.The hotel is pet-friendly (fees apply). Business travelers to this hotel will appreciate having access to copy and fax services.

All of our well-appointed guest rooms are equipped with microwaves, refrigerators, coffee makers, curved shower rods, hair dryers, irons, ironing boards and cable television.

Coin-operated laundry facilities are provided for guest use. Ample parking is available and can accommodate most cars, trucks or buses.
