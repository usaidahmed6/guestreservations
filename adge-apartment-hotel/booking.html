About Adge Apartment Hotel

Discover a dynamic oasis nestled in the heart of Sydney's vibrant neighborhood village where fashion, music, art, food, and culture converge to create an extraordinary experience at the ADGE. Just a 10-minute walk from Hyde Park, Chinatown, The Botanic Gardens, and Sydney CBD and a 7-minute drive from the iconic Darling Harbor, Opera House, Circular Quay, and The Art Gallery of New South Wales, and the closest premium boutique hotel to the Darlinghurst Theater Company and the Oxford Street nightlife, the ADGE Hotel and Residences is a destination that beckons adventurers and experience-seekers. ADGE mirrors the energy and creativity of the community we proudly call home with spacious guestrooms equipped with free Wi-Fi, a desk, plush seating, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, an iPhone/Android charging station, a SMEG fridge, a coffee machine with herbal teas, an open wardrobe, and private bathroom toiletries from APOTHICALS by Damana. Offering modern hotel rooms with charm and character and all the necessities, our 1-bedroom studios include an entertainment and dining area, microwave, cutlery, and crockery for 2 with a private balcony with views of the city or Eastside. Up style with our ADGE Residences that feature 2-bedroom apartments that include a fully equipped kitchen with an oven, a stove and a microwave, entertainment, dining area, laundry facilities and a private balcony with views of the city or Eastside. Onsite parking is available at cost. Relish the sunset and the end of the day with our selection of alcoholic and nonalcoholic beverages available on demand and delivered to your room, or enjoy a wine, beer, or pre-made cocktails in the lobby. As the sun rises, awaken to a sumptuous breakfast at our on-premise restaurant, which offers a delectable array of culinary delights. With unrivaled hospitality, we extend a warm invitation for you to indulge in an extraordinary stay at the ADGE Hotel + Residences. Go on "dare to be different" and immerse yourself in Surry Hills and Darlinghurst's vibrant and eclectic world. Only 3.1 mi or 20 mins drive from Sydney Kingsford Smith Airport.
