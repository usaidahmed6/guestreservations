About Grand Master Suites

Grand Master Suites is centrally located in Rome, right on the popular Via del Corso shopping street. It features modern air-conditioned rooms, a great location, a shared terrace, and free WiFi throughout. The individually furnished rooms feature a mini-bar, flat-screen TV and an private bathroom with rainfall shower head, hairdryer and toiletries. A kettle and tea/coffee ingredients are provided. The Spanish Steps are just 1312 feet from the property, while the Trevi Fountain is a 10-minute walk away.
