About Aiden by Best Western Darling Harbour

Well situated in the center of Sydney, Aiden by Best Western Darling Harbour has free WiFi throughout the property and a bar. This 4-star hotel offers a 24-hour front desk and a tour desk. Hyde Park Barracks Museum is a 18-minute walk from the hotel and Sydney Central Station is 1.2 miles away. The hotel will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms offering a closet, a coffee machine, a fridge, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a shower. All rooms come with an electric tea pot, while some rooms also offer a balcony and others also have city views. At Aiden by Best Western Darling Harbour all rooms have bed linen and towels. An la carte, Full English/Irish or vegetarian breakfast is served at the property. At the accommodation you'll find a restaurant serving Australian cuisine. Vegetarian, vegan and gluten-free options can also be requested. Popular points of interest near Aiden by Best Western Darling Harbour include Australian National Maritime Museum, The Star Event Centre and Sydney International Convention Centre. The nearest airport is Sydney Kingsford Smith Airport, 5.6 miles from the hotel.
