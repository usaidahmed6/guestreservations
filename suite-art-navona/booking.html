About Suite Art Navona

With a stay at Suite Art Navona, you'll be centrally located in Rome, steps from Stadium of Domitian and Palazzo Altemps. This guesthouse is 0.1 mi (0.1 km) from Piazza Navona and 0.1 mi (0.2 km) from Museo Napoleonico. Make yourself at home in one of the 16 individually furnished guestrooms, featuring iPod docking stations and LCD televisions. Your memory foam bed comes with down comforters and Frette Italian sheets. Complimentary wireless Internet access keeps you connected, and digital programming is available for your entertainment. Private bathrooms with showers feature rainfall showerheads and designer toiletries. Make use of convenient amenities, which include complimentary wireless Internet access and concierge services. Featured amenities include dry cleaning/laundry services, a 24-hour front desk, and multilingual staff. A shuttle from the hotel to the airport is provided for a surcharge (available 24 hours).
