About Capricci Romani

Just a 5-minute walk from St Peter’s Basilica, Capricci Romani is in Rome’s center, 2625 feet from Ottaviano Metro Station. It offers air-conditioned rooms with free WI-Fi. Accommodations at Capricci Romani features a 32" Smart TV with Netflix connection and marble floors. The private bathrooms come with a hairdryer and a courtesy set. Capricci Romani also features a little library and staff can provide free guides and maps. The property is just 1640 feet from Castel Sant’Angelo. The Spanish Steps and the Trevi Fountain are 3 metro stops away. A shuttle service for Ciampino and Fiumicino Airports is available on request and at an additional cost.

Amenities

  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • Non-Smoking Facility
