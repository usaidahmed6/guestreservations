About Hotel Arya, BW Premier Collection

Discover Elegance at Hotel Arya, BW Premier Collection

Nestled just 6 miles from Miami International Airport, Hotel Arya, BW Premier Collection, offers a serene retreat with breathtaking bay or city views. Immerse yourself in a world of comfort and convenience, perfectly paired with modern amenities and exceptional service. Located within a stone's throw of the vibrant CocoWalk and an array of dining options, our hotel promises an unforgettable stay in Miami.

  • Strategically located with easy access to Miami International Airport and a short walk to CocoWalk.
  • Modern guest rooms featuring wall-mounted 42-inch flat-screen TVs, coffee makers, and safes, with panoramic bay or city views.
  • Luxurious outdoor pool, hot tub, and sauna for ultimate relaxation.
  • Access to a well-equipped gym and squash courts for fitness enthusiasts.
  • Proximity to major attractions, including Vizcaya Museum and Gardens, Dolphin Harbor, Miami Seaquarium, Jungle Island, and Miami Metro Zoo.

Hotel Arya, BW Premier Collection, not only offers a prime location and splendid accommodations but also houses over 8,000 works of art, adding a unique cultural dimension to your stay. Whether you're in Miami for business or leisure, our hotel provides an upscale experience with the warmth and hospitality the city is known for. Enjoy the blend of comfort and elegance that makes Hotel Arya the perfect choice for your Miami adventure.
