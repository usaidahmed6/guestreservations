About Kimpton EPIC Hotel

Welcome to Kimpton EPIC Hotel: A Glimpse of Miami's Heart

Nestled in the vibrant core of Miami's financial district, the Kimpton EPIC Hotel stands as a beacon of comfort and convenience, offering breathtaking views of the Miami River and Biscayne Bay. With a commitment to eco-friendly practices, this distinguished hotel not only offers a luxurious stay but also ensures a positive impact on the environment. Guests are invited to indulge in the unique experiences provided, from water view dining at the acclaimed Area 31 restaurant to the serenity of its full-service marina.

  • Unique full-service marina access, setting the stage for unforgettable aquatic adventures.
  • Floor-to-ceiling windows in every guest room, offering unmatched views of Miami's skyline and waters.
  • Private balconies in each room, creating the perfect backdrop for a morning coffee or evening relaxation.
  • Exclusively designed spa and well-being sanctuary, offering an oasis of tranquility.
  • Rooftop pool with panoramic city and bay views, offering a sublime escape from the hustle and bustle.

Each guest room is a haven of comfort, equipped with state-of-the-art technology for seamless connectivity to personal devices, ensuring that every stay is as connected as desired. Beyond the walls of Kimpton EPIC Hotel, the Miami-Dade Arena and the Port of Miami beckon with their vibrant energy, both just a 5-minute drive away. Miami International Airport, situated 8.6 miles from the hotel, provides convenient access for travelers from around the globe. Experience the heart of Miami at Kimpton EPIC Hotel, where every stay is an opportunity to explore, relax, and create lasting memories.

Whether seeking an urban adventure or a tranquil sanctuary, Kimpton EPIC Hotel caters to every desire, making it the perfect choice for your next Miami escape.
