About Ocean Dream Cancun by GuruHotel

With 2 outdoor pools, an on-site dove center and à la carte, international restaurant, Ocean Dream BPR is located in Cancún, right on a beautiful white-sand beach. It is just 328 feet from restaurants and nightlife. Each spacious, modern room here includes a cable TV, safety-deposit box and free WiFi. They also boast a private bathroom with free toiletries and a shower. At Ocean Dream BPR Condo Hotel you will find a private beach area. Cancun Convention Center, plus small stores, banks and bars, is just a 5-minute walk away. Plaza Caracol shopping center is just 2789 feet from the hotel, while Cancun International Airport can be reached within 25 minutes by car. The property offers free parking.
