About Ramada Limited Calgary

This hotel is 3 blocks from the Foothills Athletic Park and 5 miles from the Calgary Zoo. The hotel offers an indoor pool, hot tub and free parking. Southern Alberta Jubilee Auditorium is 1.4 mi away. Ramada Limited Calgary rooms include a coffee maker and cable TV. The Limited Ramada provides guests with a continental breakfast. The hotel also offers laundry facilities. The Ramada Limited is within a 15-minute drive to the University of Calgary. The Canada Olympic Park is 8 miles from the hotel. Scotiabank Saddledome is 4.8 mi away. Alberta Children's Hospital 1.8 mi 7 minutes away.
