About Wyndham Garden Newark/Fremont Silicon Valley

The Comfort Inn & Suites Newark Fremont / Silicon Valley is located in the San Francisco Bay area of California. This Newark, CA hotel is just minutes from downtown San Jose and business, research and development firms headquartered in the Silicon Valley like Adobe Systems, IBM Corporation and Sun Microsystems. Kaiser Permanente health insurance company and the LAM Research corporate offices are also nearby. A number of entertainment venues, outdoor recreation areas and golf courses can be found in the surrounding area. Several retailers, specialty shops, restaurants and cocktail lounges are close to the hotel; some are within walking distance. Guests of this Newark, CA hotel can enjoy features and amenities like free wireless high-speed Internet access, free daily newspaper, outdoor heated pool, exercise room. Enjoy our free hot breakfast featuring eggs, meat, yogurt, fresh fruit, cereal and more, including your choice of hot waffle flavors. The on-site business center provides access to conveniences like copy and fax service. This California hotel has two banquet and meeting rooms that can accommodate up to 163 people for most business or social functions. All of our well-appointed guest rooms feature coffee makers with free coffee, spacious work desks, curved shower rods, hair dryers, irons, ironing boards, Nintendo game systems, voice mail, cable television with free HBO and include free local calls. Ask about our rooms that have refrigerators, microwaves, mini-bars and sofa sleepers. Be sure to join us in our spacious Mediterranean-style lobby and atrium for a nightly manager's reception. Valet cleaning service is offered. On-site parking is available and can accommodate most cars, trucks or buses.
