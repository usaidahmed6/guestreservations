About Hostal Alicante

Located 492 feet from Madrids Puerta del Sol Square, Hostal Alicante offers functional rooms with a private bathroom and free Wi-Fi. This guest house has a 24-hour reception. Each heated room features a flat-screen TV, wardrobe and telephone. Some rooms have a private balcony. The property has a vending machine serving hot and cold drinks. Luggage storage is also available. Staff at Hostal Alicante can offer tourist information on the surrounding area. There is a wide variety of tapas bars, restaurants and shops within a 5-minute walk of the property. You can catch both the train and metro from Puerta del Sol Station, 656 feet from Hostal Alicante. Gran Via is 1312 feet from the guest house and Plaza Mayor Square is 820 feet away.
