About Ih Hotels Milano Lorenteggio

Just a 30-minute tram ride to Milan center, IH Hotel Milano Lorenteggio features a lounge bar, free WiFi throughout and air-conditioned rooms. Rooms at the Lorenteggio Hotel are modern with simple, functional furnishings. Each room has a mini-bar, a safe and a private bathroom with slippers and free toiletries. The hotel's restaurant, iH Gusto Restaurant Lorenteggio, serves international cuisine as well as traditional dishes from Milan. The daily buffet breakfast includes homemade cakes and a variety of savory foods. Public busses stop right in front of the IH Hotel Milano Lorenteggio and go to the historic center and the Bisceglie Metro station. Malpensa Airport is a 40-minute drive from the property.
