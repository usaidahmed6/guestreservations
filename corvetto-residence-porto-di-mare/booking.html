About Corvetto Residence Porto Di Mare

Corvetto Residence Porto Di Mare is a recently restored 4-story building offering free WI-FI, parking and fitness area available the property is 492 feet from Porto di Mare Metro Station, with direct links to Milan Cathedral and Centrale Train Station. Studios and apartments offer an elegant dcor and are complete with a fully equipped kitchenette. The residence is located in the business district in eastern Milan. It is 3.1 mi from Linate Airport, and 5 mi from the Fieramilanocity exhibition center.

Amenities

  • 24 hour front desk
  • Fitness Center
  • Concierge
  • Wheelchair Access
  • Swimming Pool - Outdoor
  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • Non-Smoking Facility
