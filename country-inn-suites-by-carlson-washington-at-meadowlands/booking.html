About Country Inn & Suites by Radisson, Washington at Meadowlands, PA

Located off of Interstate 79, this hotel is a 6-minute drive from Interstate 70 and is 6 miles from Washington and Jefferson College. A breakfast with eggs, toast and more is served daily. Guests staying at the Country Inn and Suites can enjoy swimming in the indoor pool, exercising in the fitness center or using the business center. Coffee is always available in the lobby. The rooms at the Washington Country Inn and Suites have a microwave, refrigerator and flat-screen TV with satellite channels. The suites feature a separate sitting area with a sofa bed. The Meadows Racetrack and Casino is less than 1 mile away. Tanger Outlet Center is a 3-minute drive from the hotel.
