About Corso Grand Suite

Corso Grand Suite is centrally located in Rome, and is set along Via del Corso main shopping street. This B&B offers free WiFi and elegant rooms with a flat-screen TV. All air-conditioned rooms feature cable channels and a safe. The private bathroom comes with a shower and hairdryer. The bed and breakfast lies 492 feet from the exclusive Via Condotti. The world-famous Spanish Steps are a 5-minute drive from the property. Spagna Underground lies 984 feet away, and has direct links to Termini Central Station.

Amenities

  • Concierge
  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • Non-Smoking Facility
