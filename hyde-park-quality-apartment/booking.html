About Imperial Court Studios

Imperial Court Studios provides accommodations within 2.3 miles of the center of London, with free Wifi and a kitchenette with a microwave, a toaster, and a fridge. This 3-star apartment offers a 24-hour front desk. Royal Albert Hall is 1.1 miles away, and Madame Tussaud's is 2 miles from the apartment. At the apartment complex, every unit includes a wardrobe, a flat-screen TV, a private bathroom, bed linen, and towels. There's also a stovetop, kitchenware, and a kettle. The rooms are equipped with heating facilities. Popular points of interest near the apartment include Paddington Station, Portobello Road Market, and The Serpentine. The nearest airport is London City Airport, 12 miles from Imperial Court Studios.

Amenities

  • 24 hour front desk
  • Masks are available to guests
  • Social distancing measures are in place
