About Ovolo Woolloomooloo

Ovolo Woolloomooloo is a 5-star hotel located in Sydney. All accommodations offers Google Chromecast and unique historic fittings. Guests enjoy an indoor pool and a fitness center. The hotel is located in Woolloomooloo, next to the Royal Botanical Gardens. It is a 15-minute walk from Sydney Opera House. Accommodations options include private rooms and loft suites. All rooms and suites include free WiFi, access to a fitness center and pool and free self-service laundry throughout the stay. Each features ultra-modern furnishings, a LCD Smart TV and luxurious bedding. Guest facilities include a personalized concierge service, in-room massages and a valet laundry and dry cleaning service.
