About Holiday Inn Express Miami Airport-Blue Lagoon Area, an IHG Hotel

Holiday Inn Express Miami Airport - Blue Lagoon, a spacious all-suites hotel located in Miami's prestigious Blue Lagoon district. The hotel is located close to the Miami International Airport, Port of Miami, South Beach, shopping, professional sports venues, internationally-acclaimed medical facilities, and colleges. Guests of the nonsmoking Holiday Inn Express Miami Airport - Blue Lagoon are invited to relax in the outdoor heated pool, grab a meal or a drink at Lobby Bar, work out in the fitness center, and utilize the business center. There are 3 meeting rooms, able to accommodate up to 150 people for banquets and conferences. Additional amenities include free wireless high-speed internet access and free coffee. All spacious guest rooms offer a flat-screen cable TV, iPod docking station, desk, coffee machine, microwave, sofa bed, hair dryer, alarm clock, and ironing facilities.
