About ibis Nice Centre Gare

The ibis Nice Center Gare is located in the center of Nice, just 591 feet from Nice-Ville Train Station and 0.6 mi from Promenade des Anglais. The hotel offers and outdoor swimming pool and free Wi-Fi internet access. The air-conditioned guest rooms at ibis Nice Center Gare include a flat-screen TV with cable and satellite channels. They each feature a work desk and a private bathroom. A buffet breakfast composed of sweet and savory dishes such as eggs, fruit salad, yogurts and juices is served every day. Pastries baked on site and fresh French Madeleine cakes are also on offer, as well as a hot beverage and a piece of fruit to take away. Outside of regular breakfast hours guests can also enjoy a lighter option, available from 04:00. Drinks are served in the hotel’s bar, and a 24-hour reception is provided. Nice Old Town is 1.2 mi from the hotel and the Musée Marc Chagall is 0.7 mi away. The property is 3.7 mi from Nice Côte d’Azur Airport and 12 mi from Monaco.
