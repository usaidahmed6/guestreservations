About Grand Fiesta Americana Chapultepec

Overlooking Chapultepec Park in one of the best neighborhoods in Mexico City, this hotel offers relaxing spa services and gourmet dining within minutes of the city's main business, commercial and entertainment districts. Grand Fiesta Americana Chapultepec features the sophisticated Spa 19th. Specialty services include massages, body scrubs, hydrotherapy and aromatherapy. Feel pampered with the spa's use of unique ingredients such as gold, wine, chocolate, clay, milk and coffee. There is also a gym. The spacious, air-conditioned rooms at Grand Fiesta Americana Chapultepec have modern dcor and offer views of Chapultepec Castle. Enjoy international cuisine for lunch and dinner in a contemporary atmosphere at Salammb Restaurant. You can enjoy a drink in the Deco Bar.
