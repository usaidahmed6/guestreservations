About Buonanotte Colosseo

Buonanotte Colosseo offers accommodations in Rome, a 12-minute walk from Piazza Venezia and 0.6 miles from Palazzo Venezia. Popular points of interest nearby include Santa Maria Maggiore, Palatine Hill, and Quirinale Palace. The property is a 3-minute walk from Cavour Metro Station, and within 0.6 miles of the city center. All units feature air conditioning and a flat-screen TV. Each room includes a coffee machine, a private bathroom, and free Wifi, while certain rooms are equipped with a terrace and some have city views. At the bed and breakfast, each unit includes bed linen and towels. Popular points of interest near the bed and breakfast include Colosseo Metro Station, Domus Aurea, and The Colosseum. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino Airport, 8.7 miles from Buonanotte Colosseo.
