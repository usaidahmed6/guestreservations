About Hotel Della Conciliazione

Hotel Della Conciliazione is just 328 feet from Saint Peter's Basilica, and a 2-minute walk from the terminus of bus 40 Express, providing direct links with Roma Termini Train Station. The rooms offer a flat-screen TV. Each room is air conditioned, and features handmade Italian furniture and tiled floors. The private bathroom is complete with bath or shower, toiletries, and hairdryer. Della Conciliazione Hotel is a 4-story building with a characteristic Roman façade. The large reception hall is classically decorated, and leads to a TV lounge. Discounts apply at a restaurant next door. The hotel is a 10-minute walk from Ottaviano Metro Station, on line A, and also from San Pietro Train Station, with trains to/from Fiumicino Airport.
