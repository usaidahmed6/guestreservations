About B&B Drago D'Oro - Metro piazza Vittorio Emanuele II

Less than 328 feet from Vittorio Emanuele Metro Station, B&B Drago D'Oro - Metro piazza Vittorio Emanuele II is only a 10-minute walk from Termini Train Station. It offers air-conditioned rooms with city views. Romes Colosseum is 0.6 mi away. With free Wi-Fi, classic-style rooms at the Drago D'Oro feature a mini-bar, safe and TV with satellite channels. The private bathroom is complete with a hairdryer, toiletries and slippers. A sweet Italian breakfast, including a pastry, cappuccino and juice, is served at the bar nearby or at the adjacent partner property. All Romes landmarks such as the Spanish Steps, Piazza Navona or Vatican City can be easily reached by metro. Fiumicino Airport is a 25-minute drive away.
