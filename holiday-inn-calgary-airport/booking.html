About Holiday Inn Calgary Airport

Offering an indoor pool, the Holiday Inn Calgary Airport is located only 5.6 mi from Stampede Park. Free shuttles to the Calgary International Airport are available. Free WiFi is included. Each air-conditioned guest room at the Calgary Airport Holiday Inn features a flat-screen smart TV. Guests have access to a microwave, refrigerator, and coffee maker. A work desk is provided. An on-site fitness center and hot tub are provided. A full service restaurant is available on site and serves children under 12 for free. The front desk is open 24 hours, and dry cleaning and laundry services are provided. A business center is available. Telus Spark Center is 1.9 mi away. The Calgary Zoo is only 6 minutes' drive away.
