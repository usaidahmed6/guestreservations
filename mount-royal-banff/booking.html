About Mount Royal Hotel

Offering a restaurant, Mount Royal Hotel is located in Banff. Free WiFi and a flat-screen TV. A sauna and a hot tub are available on site. Banff Upper Hot Springs is 2.5 mi away. Air-conditioned rooms will provide you with a coffee machine, a desk and a seating area. Featuring a hairdryer, private bathrooms also come with free toiletries. Extras include a safety deposit box and ironing facilities. At Mount Royal Hotel you will find a 24-hour front desk, luggage storage, ski storage and dry cleaning. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including skiing. Lake Minnewanka is 8.7 mi from Mount Royal Banff. Banff Gondola is 5 minutes drive away.
