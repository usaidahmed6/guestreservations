About Hotel Area

Garden Area Roma Eur is 2625 feet from Roma Tre University and a 5-minute walk from Marconi Underground on line B, with direct links to the Coliseum and Termini Train Station. This design hotel offers free parking, spacious rooms, and free Wi-Fi throughout. The ultra-modern rooms of the Area are air-conditioned and decorated in a minimalist style. Each features a satellite LCD TV and a mini-bar, and some have views over the hotels gardens. Every morning, guests can enjoy the buffet breakfast in the bright dining room. There is also a cocktail bar and a huge lounge area. Dinner is also available. Within 10 minutes by car, you can reach to the main landmarks of the EUR business district, including the congress center and the Palalottomatica concert venue.
