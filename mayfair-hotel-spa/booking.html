About Mayfair House Hotel & Garden

Escape to Luxury at Mayfair House Hotel & Garden in Coconut Grove

Immerse yourself in the elegance and charm of Mayfair House Hotel & Garden, a premier boutique hotel nestled in the vibrant heart of Coconut Grove. Just a short distance from the historical Vizcaya Museum and Gardens, this hotel offers a sanctuary of luxury for travelers seeking an intimate getaway. Experience the height of sophistication with meticulously designed accommodations, rooftop amenities, and state-of-the-art fitness facilities. At Mayfair House Hotel & Garden, every detail is crafted to create an unforgettable stay.

  • Spacious accommodations equipped with 65-inch flat-screen TVs, in-room bars, and coffee and tea making machines for your comfort and convenience
  • Rooftop pool, bar, and lounge offering panoramic views of Coconut Grove, perfect for relaxation or enjoying a cocktail under the stars
  • On-site gourmet dining, where guests can savor exquisite culinary creations in a sophisticated setting
  • Select rooms featuring private outdoor showers, adding a unique and refreshing touch to your luxury experience
  • 24-hour state-of-the-art fitness center, allowing guests to maintain their workout routines with cardio equipment and free weights

Located just miles from the iconic South Beach and a short drive from the University of Miami, Mayfair House Hotel & Garden positions guests perfectly to explore the best of Miami. Whether you're here to soak up the sun, dive into the rich cultural scene, or simply unwind in luxury, this hotel offers an unparalleled experience. Discover the allure of Coconut Grove at Mayfair House Hotel & Garden, where luxury meets sophistication in an enchanting setting.
