About Olympia Lodge

Located on the Trans-Canada Highway next to Shouldice Park, this Calgary motel features free Wi-Fi in every room. Canada Olympic Park is 3.1 mi away. Cable TV is found in each decorative, wood-furnished room at Olympia Lodge. A microwave and a small refrigerator are also available. The reception desk of the Calgary Lodge Olympia is open 24-hours to serve guests. An on-site launderette is also open. Calgary city center is 6.2 mi away. Confederation Park Golf Course is less than 15 minutes’ drive away.
