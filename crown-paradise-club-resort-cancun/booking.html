About Crown Paradise Club - All Inclusive

Located along the Caribbean Sea, this Cancun hotel is 2.2 mi from Aqua World Marina. It features a water park with 8 water slides and The Spa offers hair and body treatments. Every spacious room offers balcony with beautiful ocean views and air conditioning. All rooms are brightly decorated and include a satellite TV, refrigerator and coffee maker. Guests can enjoy a poolside massage or relax in the hot tubs. A gym and 2 tennis courts are available for active guests. Crown Paradise Cancun offers free on-site activities, such as a mini-golf course and game room with billiards. A variety of cuisine is available at Club Cancun’s 7 restaurants, including Japanese specialties at Fujiyama and Italian dishes at La Piazza. Beer and cocktails are served at 6 bars, such as Club Caribe Pool Bar and Beach Bar.
