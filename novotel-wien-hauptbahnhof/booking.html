About Novotel Wien Hauptbahnhof

Located in Vienna, 2625 feet from Belvedere Palace, Novotel Wien Hauptbahnhof features a sauna and fitness center. Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant. The rooms are fitted with a flat-screen TV. Certain rooms include a seating area to relax in after a busy day. The rooms include a private bathroom equipped with a bath or shower. Vienna State Opera is 1.2 mi from Novotel Wien Hauptbahnhof, while Stadtpark is 1.3 mi away. Vienna International Airport is 9.3 mi from the property.
