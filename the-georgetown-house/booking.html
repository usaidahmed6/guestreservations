About The Georgetown House Boutique Inn

Located along the C&O canal in the retail and cultural epicenter of the nations capital, The Georgetown House is 322 metres from Georgetown Waterfront and Georgetowns restaurants and boutiques. Every room at this inn is air conditioned and is fitted with a flat-screen TV. Some rooms have a sitting area to relax. The rooms are equipped with a private bathroom, toiletries and hairdryers. There's shared kitchen at the property where guests have access to tea, coffee and orange juice. Il Canale, an authentic Italian restaurant with an outdoor terrace is 5 metres from The Georgetown House. Sea-Catch Seafood is 26 metres, Sushi to Go is 5.5 metres and Martins Tavern is 482 metres away from this property. The White House is 2 km and the Washington Monument is 3 km from The Georgetown House. The John F. Kennedy Center for Performing Arts is 1.5 km while the Smithsonian Museums are 4.6 km away.
