About Nerva Accomodation Cavour

Nerva Accomodation Cavour is a guest house featuring rooms with free Wifi and air conditioning in the center of Rome. Popular points of interest nearby include Domus Aurea, Rome Termini Metro Station, and Roma Termini. The accommodation provides an elevator, a shared kitchen, and luggage storage for guests. At the guest house, each unit is equipped with a desk, a flat-screen TV, a private bathroom, bed linen, and towels. There's also a well-equipped kitchenette in some units equipped with a microwave, a fridge, and a stovetop. At the guest house, all units are allergy-free and soundproof. There is an on-site coffee shop. Popular points of interest near the guest house include Cavour Metro Station, Santa Maria Maggiore, and Colosseo Metro Station. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino, 8.7 miles from Nerva Accomodation Cavour, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
