About Do Step Inn Central - Self-Service-Hostel

Conveniently set in the 04 Wieden district of Vienna, Do Step Inn Central - Self-Service-Hostel is located a 3-minute walk from Vienna Main Train Station, 0.7 miles from Belvedere Palace and a 18-minute walk from Karlskirche. Among the facilities at this property are a shared kitchen and a shared lounge, along with free WiFi throughout the property. Albertina Museum is 1.5 miles away and House of Music is 1.5 miles from the hostel. At the hostel, every room comes with a desk. All guest rooms include a shared bathroom, a hairdryer and bed linen. Vienna State Opera is 1.3 miles from Do Step Inn Central - Self-Service-Hostel, while Musikverein is 1.2 miles away. The nearest airport is Vienna International Airport, 11 miles from the accommodation.
