About PJ Patong Resortel

PJ Patong Resortel offers affordable rooms with free Wi-Fi and modern furnishings. Well located a 15-minute walk from lively Patong Beach, it is a 5-minute walk from Jungceylon Shopping Mall. Featuring private balconies overlooking Patong’s streets, air-conditioned rooms at PJ Patong Resortel are fitted with modern dark wood furnishings and cable TV. A fridge and seating area are available. Travel around Phuket easily with the tour and shuttle services provided at the property. Valuables can be deposited in the safe at the 24-hour front desk. The residence is within a 15-minute walk from numerous shopping and eating options surrounding Patong Beach. It is a 45-minute drive from Phuket International Airport.
