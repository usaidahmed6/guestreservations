About W Fort Lauderdale

Seaside Chic at W Fort Lauderdale

Set against the backdrop of the Atlantic Ocean, W Fort Lauderdale offers a sophisticated escape just minutes from the heart of the city. This stylish hotel combines stunning ocean views, luxurious amenities, and exceptional service, providing an ideal destination for both leisure and business travelers.

The guest rooms and suites at W Fort Lauderdale feature contemporary decor with floor-to-ceiling windows that highlight the breathtaking ocean views. Each room is equipped with modern conveniences like iPod docking stations, flat-screen TVs, and plush bedding, ensuring a comfortable and luxurious stay. The private bathrooms are adorned with exclusive Away Spa® amenities.

Guests can enjoy the hotel’s rooftop pool, which offers panoramic views of both the city and the intracoastal waterways. The on-site Away Spa® caters to those seeking relaxation and rejuvenation with a variety of treatments, including massages and manicures. For dining, STEAK 954 presents a modern twist on steakhouse classics, available for breakfast, lunch, and dinner, while WET® Deck Bites offers a casual menu complemented by artisanal cocktails.

  • Rooftop pool with stunning ocean and city views
  • Luxurious rooms with modern amenities and ocean vistas
  • Away Spa® offering a range of relaxing treatments
  • On-site dining at STEAK 954 and WET® Deck Bites
  • Conveniently located near Las Olas Boulevard and Port Everglades

Located just a short drive from Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport, W Fort Lauderdale is the perfect place to experience the vibrancy of Fort Lauderdale. Whether you're looking for a tranquil beach retreat or a dynamic city adventure, W Fort Lauderdale encapsulates a truly memorable stay.
