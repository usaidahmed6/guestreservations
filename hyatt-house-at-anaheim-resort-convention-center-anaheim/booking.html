About Hyatt House at Anaheim Resort/Convention Center

Hyatt House at Anaheim Resort/Convention Center has an outdoor swimming pool, fitness center, a restaurant and bar in Anaheim. This 3-star hotel offers a 24-hour front desk, a business center and free WiFi. The property is allergy-free throughout and is located a 9-minute walk from Anaheim Convention Center. The hotel will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms offering a desk, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a bath or shower. A seating area is accessible to all guests, while certain rooms also offer a DVD player. All rooms will provide guests with a fridge. Hyatt House at Anaheim Resort/Convention Center offers a buffet or American breakfast. The accommodation offers a hot tub. Disney California Adventure is 1.2 miles from Hyatt House at Anaheim Resort/Convention Center, while Knotts Berry Farm is 6.8 miles from the property. The nearest airport is John Wayne Airport, 11 miles from the hotel.
