About Hotel Canada

Hotel Canada is 1640 feet from Crocetta Metro Station and 984 feet from Missori Metro Station, just 1 metro stop from Milan's Duomo. It offers rooms with modern decor and an LCD TV with Sky channels. With light wood furniture and large windows, the bright rooms include a mini-bar and air conditioning. They have a private bathroom with hairdryer and toiletries. Guests enjoy a free Wi-Fi access, and a reception open 24 hours a day. Every morning, a varied breakfast is served in the hotel’s dining room. Surrounded by good transport links by metro and tram, Canada Hotel is a 20-minute walk from La Scala Theater. Milano Linate Airport is 4.3 mi away. The lively Navigli area is a 15-minute walk away. The Rho Fiera Milano and Area Expo exhibition centers are both within 9.9 mi of the property.
