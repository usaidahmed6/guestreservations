About Grey&White

Just 328 feet from Romes Santa Maria Maggiore Basilica, Gray&White offers modern rooms with free WiFi. Termini Station is reachable on foot in 5 minutes. Rooms at the Gray&White come with a flat-screen TV and a mini-bar. The private bathroom includes a hairdryer and free toiletries. The surrounding area is filled with restaurants and cafs. Vittorio Emanuele Metro is just 984 feet from the property.
