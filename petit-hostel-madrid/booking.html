About Petit Hostel La Latina

Located within a 6-minute walk of Puerta de Toledo and 0.9 miles of Mercado San Miguel, Petit Hostel La Latina provides rooms with air conditioning and a shared bathroom in Madrid. Among the facilities at this property are a shared lounge and a 24-hour front desk, along with free WiFi throughout the property. Guests can have a drink at the snack bar. All guest rooms at the hostel are equipped with a shared bathroom and bed linen. Popular points of interest near Petit Hostel La Latina include Plaza Mayor, Royal Palace of Madrid and Casa de Campo. The nearest airport is Adolfo Suarez Madrid-Barajas Airport, 10 miles from the accommodation.

Amenities

  • 24 hour front desk
  • Wheelchair Access
  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
