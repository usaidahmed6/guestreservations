About Grand Hyatt San Francisco

Discover the Heart of San Francisco at Grand Hyatt San Francisco

Located in the vibrant Union Square, Grand Hyatt San Francisco offers modern accommodations just steps away from Chinatown. With convenient on-site dining and a 24-hour gym, this hotel provides everything needed for a comfortable and memorable stay.

Each spacious room features a flat-screen cable TV, Uno-Bitell Media phone with MP3/iPod docking capabilities, a small refrigerator, and a coffee machine. Bathrooms are equipped with designer bath amenities, ensuring a relaxing experience.

Guests can savor American cuisine and cocktails at the on-site OneUP Restaurant and Lounge. The hotel's prime location places guests within an 11-minute walk from Montgomery Street BART Station and the San Francisco Museum of Modern Art. Popular attractions like Fisherman's Wharf and Alcatraz Island are just one mile away, offering plenty to explore.

  • Prime location in Union Square, steps from Chinatown
  • On-site dining at OneUP Restaurant and Lounge
  • 24-hour gym for fitness enthusiasts
  • Modern rooms with flat-screen TVs and docking stations
  • Close to Montgomery Street BART Station and major attractions

Experience the best of San Francisco with a stay at Grand Hyatt San Francisco, where comfort, convenience, and a central location come together seamlessly.
