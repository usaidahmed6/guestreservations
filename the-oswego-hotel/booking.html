About Oswego Hotel

The Oswego Hotel is located on a quiet street a few minutes' walk from Victoria’s parliament buildings and historic harbor. Our facilities include secure underground parking, an on-site restaurant, fitness facilities, and a quiet outdoor zen garden. Rooms and suites at Oswego all feature full kitchens. The hotel is family-friendly with dog-friendly suites available upon request. EV charging stations are also available on a first-come first-serve basis in our underground parking area. We are proud to welcome guests from both Canada and around the world to the Oswego Hotel. Our guest-care team is available to help make your stay comfortable and to help recommend things to do in Victoria.
