About Park Villa Boutique Hostel

Located in London, a 15-minute walk from Victoria Park, Park Villa Boutique Hostel has accommodations with a garden, private parking, a shared lounge and a terrace. The property is around 1.9 miles from Olympic Stadium, 2 miles from Canary Wharf Tube Station and 2.1 miles from Brick Lane. The property provides a shared kitchen and free WiFi throughout the property. The rooms in the hostel are equipped with a coffee machine. Guest rooms have bed linen. Stratford Tube Station is 2.1 miles from Park Villa Boutique Hostel, while Stratford City Westfield is 2.4 miles from the property. The nearest airport is London City Airport, 5 miles from the accommodation.
