About NOX HOTELS- Paddington

Ideally located just 820 feet from central London's Paddington Railway Station, NOX Lancaster Gate features modern rooms with free WiFi. Leafy Hyde Park can be reached after a 5-minute walk. Featuring stylish decor, each of the air-conditioned room features tea and coffee making facilities and colorful furniture. The modern bathrooms have a hairdryer and free toiletries. There are numerous bars and restaurants within a 5-minute stroll of the property. The NOX Lancaster Gate Hotel is a 25-minute walk from popular shops on London's famous Oxford Street. Madame Tussaud’s is also just over 1 mile away. The Natural History Museum is a 30-minute walk through Hyde Park and Bond Street can be reached after a 30-minute stroll.
