About Studios Floreal

Located a 15-minute walk from the Promenade des Anglais in Nice, Studios Floreal offers modern self-catering accommodations, 0.9 mi from Nice Old Town. Each studio offers an equipped kitchenette with electric hotplates, a fridge and microwave. A dining area is provided and there is a flat-screen TV. A shower and hairdryer are offered in the studio’s bathroom. All studios are serviced by an elevator and bed linen and towels are provided. Studios Floreal is 4.3 mi from Nice Côte d’Azur Airport. Gare Thiers Tram Stop is 164 feet away and Nice-Ville Train Station is a 5-minute walk from the property. The A8 motorway is just 2.2 mi away.
