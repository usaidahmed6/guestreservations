About Canopy by Hilton Madrid Castellana

Canopy by Hilton Madrid Castellana has a privileged location in the city's financial and business center, surrounded by the most famous stores and restaurants. The air-conditioned rooms at Canopy by Hilton Madrid Castellana feature a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries. Located in front of the Real Madrid Football Stadium - the Santiago Bernabeu stadium and very close to the historical center, with its monuments, old tascas and picturesque spots. Perfect for business and leisure stay, Canopy by Hilton Madrid Castellana includes 11 meeting rooms available for a surcharge. Located only 20 minutes away by car from the airport, with direct access from the metro station Nuevos Ministerios.
