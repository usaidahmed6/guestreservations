About BreakFree on Clarence

Located in the heart of Sydney CBD (Central Business District), the refurbished BreakFree on Clarence hotel offers air-conditioned rooms with a flat-screen TV. Public transport options, including a train station and bus stop, are available out the front of the hotel. Located in a beautiful heritage building, BreakFree is a 5-minute walk from King Street Wharf and Cockle Bay. Guests can enjoy shops, restaurants, cafes and bars right at their doorstep. The Rocks, the citys historic old town, is only 15 minutes walk away. Each room features a work desk, wardrobe, and in-room safe. There is a refrigerator, tea/coffee making facilities, and a private bathroom with shower. Luggage storage facilities and a safety deposit box are available. Guests can relax in the communal lounge/seating area. Laundry facilities, airport shuttle services and WiFi are also available for an additional charge.
