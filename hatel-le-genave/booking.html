About Hotel Le G (ex Le Geneve)

Located in the heart of Nice by Garibaldi Square, Htel Le G (ex Le Genve) is located just 1640 feet from the Port, Palais des Congrs, Nice's Old Town and the Acropolis Exhibition Center. Free WiFi is available throughout. The air-conditioned rooms feature modern decoration, a flat-screen TV and a mini-bar. The hotel's restaurant serves breakfast 7 days a week and lunch and dinner 6 days a week. It also provides snacks. A range of restaurants and bars can be found in the hotels surroundings and on Place Garibaldi. From Garibaldi Tram Station 558 feet away, guests have good connections to the whole town. They can also drive 19 mi to visit Monaco. Nice Ville Train Station is 1.2 mi away and Nice Cte-dAzur Airport is 6.2 mi away, directly accessible on tram in 35 minutes.
