About The Line Hotel

Urban Escape at The LINE Hotel LA

Nestled in a striking mid-century building in the heart of Los Angeles, The LINE Hotel LA presents a unique blend of cultural vibrancy and urban sophistication less than four miles from Hollywood. With its prominent location in Koreatown, the hotel offers an immersive experience into the city’s dynamic rhythm.

Each room at The LINE Hotel LA is a testament to contemporary design, featuring floor-to-ceiling windows with expansive views of Los Angeles, custom furnishings, modern artwork, and a range of high-tech amenities including a flat-screen TV and iPod docking station. Rooms are also equipped with a mini-bar stocked with an eclectic mix of American and Korean snacks and beverages, ensuring a taste of local flavors.

  • Outdoor pool and furnished sun terrace for relaxation
  • Openaire Restaurant offering poolside dining with a seasonal menu
  • On-site retail store by art and design collective Poketo
  • Complimentary bicycle hire and free yoga classes on Saturdays
  • Proximity to Staples Center, Los Angeles Convention Center, and iconic LA attractions

Dine al fresco at Openaire Restaurant, where a shareable format menu connects guests with both land and sea. The hotel’s central location also places you alongside Koreatown's historic colonial revival buildings and within easy reach of the city’s most sought-after destinations including Beverly Hills and Universal Studios.

The LINE Hotel LA is more than just a place to stay; it’s a hub of creativity and comfort. Whether you're here for the vibrant nightlife, the cultural touchstones, or a business rendezvous, The LINE Hotel LA encapsulates the spirit of Los Angeles. Discover a space where design, culinary expertise, and location converge to create a memorable stay in the City of Angels.
