About Palazzo Cardinal Cesi

Palazzo Cardinal Cesi is set in a 15th-century building, and is just 459 feet from Saint Peters Square. This property has a private courtyard garden. The property is set on Via della Conciliazione between Castel SantAngelo and Saint Peters Basilica. Ottaviano Metro is a 10-minute walk away. You can enjoy drinks in the Library lounge and have your American buffet breakfast in the old Refectory, where monks once ate. Restaurants, cafs, shops and bars are located close to Palazzo Cardinal Cesi.
