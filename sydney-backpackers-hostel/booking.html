About Sydney Backpackers

Sydney Backpackers is a trendy hostel located in the heart of Sydney CBD (Central Business District), a 30-minute drive from Sydney Airport. It offers a rooftop terrace with barbecue facilities, free WiFi access, and a fully equipped common kitchen. Backpackers Sydney is within a 20-minute walk of the Hyde Park, Darling Harbor and the central station. The hostel offers a variety of private and dormitory rooms. Each comes with a free sanitized linen pack, and free use of an individual locker. A padlock can be purchased at reception. The property has a coin-operated laundry, and soap powder can be purchased at the reception at a small price. The staff at the tour desk can provide information about Sydney and various parts of Australia. The common room is equipped with a Smart TV and Netflix. Guests can prepare simple meals at the shared kitchen, which comes with crockery, cutlery and cooking utensils. It also has a microwave, an oven and cook-tops. There are communal fridges in the shared dining room.
