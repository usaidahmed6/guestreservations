About Wedgewood Hotel And Spa

Discover the Charm of Wedgewood Hotel And Spa

Welcome to Wedgewood Hotel And Spa, nestled in the heart of Vancouver. Just a stone's throw from the bustling city center and a mere 5 minutes' walk from the SkyTrain station, this unique destination ensures a memorable stay with a blend of comfort and convenience. Each room promises a serene view from private balconies where guests can revel in the urban panorama or enjoy a quiet moment away from the city's hustle.

At the Wedgewood, experience the art of relaxation at the full-service spa, or maintain your fitness regime at the well-equipped gym, followed by a refreshing session in the sauna. Dine at Bacchus, the sophisticated lounge and restaurant, where every meal is an occasion. For those who seek more than just a stay, the 24-hour reception and concierge service are at your disposal to assist with transportation to various city points including the airport and cruise ship terminals.

Whether in town for business or leisure, the Wedgewood Hotel And Spa is more than just a place to stay; it's a space to experience the vibrancy of Vancouver. Every detail is crafted to enhance your visit, ensuring a comfortable and seamless experience from arrival to departure.

  • Private balconies with stunning views
  • Full-service spa for ultimate relaxation
  • On-site restaurant Bacchus, offering a unique dining experience
  • Convenient access to Vancouver city center and SkyTrain
  • Comprehensive concierge services for personalized travel and transportation
