About Astors Belgravia

In the heart of lively Victoria, Astors Belgravia is just 10 minutes’ walk from Victoria Train station and a 4 minute walk from Victoria Coach Station. The traditional hotel has a 24-hour front desk and air-conditioned rooms with free Wi-Fi. The bright and airy bedrooms each have a luxurious modern bathroom with free toiletries and a hairdryer. All rooms feature a work desk and a TV with cable channels. Breakfast to go bag is available with our Bed and Breakfast rates which can be taken in the Breakfast area if desired or taken back to your room. Breakfast to go bag includes items such as a selection of pastries, fruit, yogurt, juices and hot beverages. Astors Belgravia is just a 10 minute walk from Knightsbridge. Westminster Abbey and the Houses of Parliament are within a 20 minute walk.
