About Chollada Inn Kamala

Chollada Inn Kamala is offering accommodations in Kamala Beach. The property is located a 9-minute walk from Kamala Beach, 1.7 miles from Nakalay Beach and 5.3 miles from Patong Boxing Stadium. The inn provides a terrace, a 24-hour front desk, and free WiFi is available. The inn will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms with a desk, an electric tea pot, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a shower. Jungceylon Shopping Center is 5.5 miles from Chollada Inn Kamala, while Phuket Simon Cabaret is 6.3 miles away. The nearest airport is Phuket International Airport, 17 miles from the accommodation.
