About St. James' Court, A Taj Hotel, London

Elegant Retreat at St. James' Court, A Taj Hotel, London

Discover the blend of regal heritage and contemporary luxury at St. James' Court, A Taj Hotel, London. Perfectly situated within walking distance of Buckingham Palace, Hyde Park, and Mayfair, this 4-star deluxe hotel offers a prestigious stay in the heart of London.

St. James' Court boasts spacious and elegantly appointed rooms, combining old-world charm with modern design. Each room features state-of-the-art amenities including air-conditioning, flat-screen TVs, hypo-allergenic sheets, Nespresso coffee machines, and luxurious bathrooms equipped with fluffy bathrobes and slippers.

Indulge in exceptional dining experiences within the hotel. Enjoy Michelin-starred South-West coastal Indian cuisine at Quilon, or savor award-winning afternoon teas at Kona. TH@51 offers contemporary dining options, while the Victorian Courtyard provides a stunning setting for cocktails and al fresco dining during the summer months. The House Of Ming presents authentic Chinese cuisine, enriching your stay with cultural and gastronomic delights.

  • Two exquisite restaurants and a contemporary dining venue
  • Michelin-starred and award-winning culinary experiences
  • Luxurious rooms with modern amenities and old-world charm
  • Full-service spa offering treatments based on Indian wellness philosophies
  • Prime location near iconic landmarks and convenient transport links

Whether visiting for business or pleasure, St. James' Court, A Taj Hotel, London ensures a memorable experience with its sophisticated ambiance and impeccable service. Positioned just a 10-minute walk from Victoria Coach and Rail Stations, the hotel is easily accessible from Gatwick and Heathrow International airports. Experience the perfect blend of luxury and comfort at this distinguished London hotel.
