About Hotel Seven Rooms

Set in Milan, 2.5 mi from Milan Cathedral, Hotel Seven Rooms features free WiFi access in some rooms and private parking. Milan Linate Airport is a 10-minute drive away. Every room is equipped with a flat-screen TV. Each room is equipped with a private bathroom fitted with a bidet. For your comfort, you will find slippers and free toiletries. You can play mini-golf at this hotel, and the area is popular for golfing. Milan Fashion District is 3.1 mi from Hotel Seven Rooms, while Duomo Milan is 3.1 mi from the property. The nearest airport is Milan Linate Airport, 1.9 mi from the property.
