About Bed & Breakfast A San Siro 75

Bed & Breakfast A San Siro 75 is set in Milan, 1312 feet from San Siro Stadium and 984 feet from Lampugnano Bus Station. A free shuttle service to and from Lampugnano is available for guests. Free WiFi is available. Some rooms have a seating area for your convenience. All rooms are fitted with a private or shared bathroom. Extras include bath robes and free toiletries. There is a 24-hour front desk at the property. Coffee and water are offered on site. Ippodromo Metro Station is 820 feet from the property. Arena Civica and Sforzesco Castle are 3.1 mi from Bed & Breakfast A San Siro 75. The nearest airport is Linate Airport, 8.1 mi from A San Siro.
