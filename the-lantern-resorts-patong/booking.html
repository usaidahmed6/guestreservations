About The Lantern Resorts Patong - SHA Extra Plus

A 20-minute drive from Phuket Town, The Lantern Resort Patong is located in the heart of Patong Beach. It offers 3 swimming pools, a spa and a restaurant. Free WiFi is available throughout the property. The classy rooms at Patong Lantern Resort come with modern décor, and are fitted with a flat-screen TV. Toiletries and hot showers are in each private bathroom. The resort offers an airport shuttle and free parking. Laundry and ironing services are offered upon request. A variety of local and international dishes are served at the on-site restaurant. All-day dining options are available. Resort Lantern Patong is a 5-minute walk from Soi Bangla and a 30-minute drive from Phuket International Airport.
