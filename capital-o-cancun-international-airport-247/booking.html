About Capital O Airport 24/7

Capital O Cancun International Airport 24 p 7 has accommodations in Cancún. The property is around 8.4 miles from Beto Avila Stadium, 8.8 miles from Cancun Government Palace and 9 miles from Cancun Bus Station. The property is non-smoking throughout and is located 3.7 miles from Cancun Underwater Museum. With a private bathroom equipped with a shower and free toiletries, rooms at the hotel also feature free WiFi. At Capital O Cancun International Airport 24 p 7 the rooms have bed linen and towels. La Isla Shopping Mall is 12 miles from the accommodation, while Anahuac University Cancun is 1.7 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Cancún International Airport, 0.6 miles from Capital O Cancun International Airport 24 p 7.
