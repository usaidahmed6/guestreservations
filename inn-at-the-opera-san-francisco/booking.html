About Inn at the Opera

Ideally located in the heart of San Francisco's Performing Arts District, near vibrant Hayes Valley neighborhood. This contemporary hotel offers luxurious accommodations just 4 minutes' walk from the San Francisco War Memorial Opera House and Civic Center. Civic Center BART Station is 10 minutes' walk away. The Inn features European inspired accommodations with classic furnishings and elegant decor. Downtown San Francisco and Union Square are just 1.9 mi away from Inn at the Opera. AT&T Park, home of the San Francisco Giants professional baseball team, is 2.8 mi away.

Amenities

  • 24 hour front desk
  • Internet Access - Free Public Access
  • ATM Machine
  • Parking - Valet
  • Non-Smoking Facility
  • Business Center
