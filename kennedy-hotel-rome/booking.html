About Hotel Kennedy

Overlooking Rome's Casa dell'Architettura, The Kennedy Hotel is between the Basilica of Santa Maria Maggiore and Termini Train Station. It features soundproof rooms with private bathrooms, 2 lounges, and a small international library. The Kennedy is in an 18th-centruy building. The rooms have period architectural features modern bathrooms, LED TVs, and air conditioning. Some of the rooms are in the annex building 195 ft away. The hotel features a bar and a luggage storage room. Free Wi-Fi access is available throughout the property. A breakfast buffet is available daily between 7:15 am and 9:45 am.
