About The Pyramid Cancun by Oasis - All Inclusive

Located in the heart of Cancuns hotel area, The Pyramid offers its guests restaurants and an outdoor pool. The bright and spacious rooms are air conditioned. The amenities available include a flat-screen TV with cable channels, safe and outdoor furniture. The full bathroom includes free toiletries and may either have a bath or shower. The Pyramid also features tennis courts and a fitness center as part of its facilities. Night entertainment with a show, nightclub and a shared lounge are also some of the features that guests will find here. The surroundings are good for practicing snorkeling, diving and windsurfing. La Isla Shopping Village, an elegant shopping mall featuring designer and brand shops, restaurants and bars, is 0.7 mi from the resort. Cancun International Airport can be reached in a 30-minute drive.
