About Hotel Gambara

Hotel Gambara provides affordable rooms with free Wi-Fi only a 20 minute walk from Milan's San Siro Stadium. The Gambara Metro Station is just 55 yards away. The Gambara Hotel is in a quiet location near stores and with good bus services. The nearby metro station, on the red line, provides connection to the cathedral in approximately 10 minutes. Hotel Gambara is family run and the front desk is always open. There is a refreshment area with soft drinks and coffee.

Amenities

  • Room Service
  • 24 hour front desk
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
