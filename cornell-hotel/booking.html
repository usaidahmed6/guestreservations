About Cornell Hotel De France

Discover the charm and elegance of France in the heart of San Francisco at this family-owned and operated hotel. Originally built in 1910, Cornell Hotel de France combines old-world charm with modern amenities such free WiFi. Admire prints of masterpieces from famous artists throughout the hotel. Union Square is just 5 minutes walk away. Individually decorated, each charming room is equipped with a cable TV and a telephone with private voice mail. Cornell Hotel de France features an exceptional location close to many of the city's main points of interest such as the Theater District. San Franciscos legendary Chinatown is 10 minutes walk from the hotel. The city's historic cable car system is also easily accessible and the Powell Street cable car stop is 7 minutes walk away.
