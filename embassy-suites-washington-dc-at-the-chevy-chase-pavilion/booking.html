About Embassy Suites by Hilton Washington DC Chevy Chase Pavilion

Discover the Heart of Washington DC from Embassy Suites Chevy Chase Pavilion

Embassy Suites by Hilton Washington DC Chevy Chase Pavilion welcomes you to experience unparalleled convenience and comfort in the nation's capital. Nestled inside the vibrant Chevy Chase Pavilion with direct access to the Friendship Heights Metro Station, our hotel places the entire DC Metro area at your doorstep. Enjoy the blend of spacious accommodations and warm hospitality that defines our hotel.

  • Spacious two-room suites featuring separate living areas, flat-screen cable TVs, refrigerators, microwaves, and iPod docking stations.
  • Complimentary cooked-to-order breakfast served daily, ensuring a perfect start to your day.
  • Exclusive indoor swimming pool for relaxation and recreation, regardless of the weather outside.
  • Enjoy on-site dining at Atrio Caf and unwind with a drink at Willy's Bar, offering a selection of local and national beers, cocktails, and small plates.
  • Benefit from our evening manager's reception, offering a warm and inviting atmosphere for socializing each night.

Located merely 4.6 miles from Dupont Circle and 7 miles from the White House, our hotel is an ideal choice for travelers seeking to explore the rich history and vibrant culture of Washington DC. Whether you're visiting for business or leisure, Embassy Suites by Hilton Washington DC Chevy Chase Pavilion promises a memorable stay with easy access to all major attractions. Ronald Reagan Washington National Airport is just 11 miles away, making your travel plans effortless. Join us for an exceptional stay in the heart of everything.
