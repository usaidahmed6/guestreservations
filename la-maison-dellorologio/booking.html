About La Maison Dell'Orologio

La Maison Dell'Orologio is in the historic center of Rome, near Piazza Navona. If offers bed and breakfast service in a renovated 17th-century building. Public buses are a 5 minute walk away. At La Maison you will stay in large rooms with private bathrooms, plasma-screen TV, satellite channels, DVD player, and air conditioning. The staff at the Maison Dell'Orologio can organize private shuttle service to and from Rome's main airports. Baggage storage is available on request. The bed and breakfast is located on the third floor of a building with an elevator.
