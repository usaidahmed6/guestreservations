About Splash Beach Resort, Mai Khao Phuket

Set in the peaceful Mai Khao Beach, Splash Beach Resort, Maikhao Phuket features functional and comfortable accommodations on the northwest coast of Phuket, stretching along 6.8 miles. Surrounded by spacious landscaped gardens, a range of on-site restaurants and bars and a large conference center. Featuring a variety of accommodations including rooms, suites, penthouses and villas, each modern unit offers free WiFi and views of tropical gardens, the pool or Andaman Sea. Flat-screen TVs with cable channels are provided. Selected rooms are fitted with a balcony and a kitchen. Splash Jungle Water Park is open Daily from Sunday to Monday ( 10.00-17.45 hrs). At the water park, guests can enjoy exciting rides such as Boomerango, a six-level wave pool and an aqua play pool for children. For those looking for a relaxing water park tour, a 335-yard lazy river is where they can float around and enjoy the place. The resort also has a sandy pool with direct access to the beach. Eight dining options serving authentic Thai and international cuisine await guests at the resort. Drinks and light snacks are also available. The nearest airport is Phuket International Airport, approximately a 15-minute drive away.
