About The Residence Resort & Spa Retreat

Luxurious villas with private pools, full kitchen facilities and spacious lounge areas await at The Residence Resort. A 2-minute drive from Bangtao Beach, it features a spa and fitness facilities. Free Wi-Fi is available inside the property. Featuring a rooftop area for outdoor dining and barbecues, these large villas offer warm color schemes and laundry facilities. There is a TV, DVD player and spacious bathrooms. The Residence Resort & Spa Retreat provides a free shuttle service to Tesco Lotus and Bangtao Beach (Catch Beach Club). It is a 25-minute drive from Patong’s nightlife and a 30-minute drive from Phuket International Airport. Guests can enjoy meals throughout the day from 06:30 - 21:30 hrs in the privacy of their own villas. For dinner, a personal chef can also prepare private barbecues in villas.
