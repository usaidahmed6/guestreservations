About Hotel Bellclassic Tokyo

Right next to Otsuka Train Station on the Yamanote Line, the elegant Hotel Bellclassic features French and Japanese restaurants. Free Wi-Fi is available in the entire property, and rooms come with a 40-inch flat-screen TV and an private bathroom. Guests at Hotel Bellclassic Tokyo stay in classically decorated rooms with a wood desk and a fridge. All are air-conditioned and have an electric kettle. The private bathroom is fitted with a bathtub. Raconter Restaurant serves French-inspired cuisine amid traditional Western elegance. Mimatsu offers authentic Japanese dishes with seasonal ingredients. A Western or Japanese-style buffet is available for breakfast and guests can enjoy a reduced rate if they reserve the day before. The Ikebukuro area is just 1 train stop away. Shinjuku, Shibuya and Ueno can all be reached within 20 minutes via the Yamanote Train Line.
