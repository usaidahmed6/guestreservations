About Pullman at Sydney Olympic Park Hotel

The Pullman at Sydney Olympic Park offers 5-star accommodations next to Accor Stadium, Qudos Bank Arena, Sydney Olympic Park Aquatic Center and Sydney Showground. Facilities include a restaurant, bar and 24-hour-fitness center. Each modern room at Pullman Sydney Olympic Park features sweeping views, large bathrooms with separate shower and bath, and cable TV. Suites with spa bath are available. Bacar Restaurant features an open kitchen, private dining room and modern international cuisine. Bacar Bar offers casual dining with light snacks and a selection of drinks, including wines and cocktails. Olympic Park Station is only 328 feet from The Pullman at Sydney Olympic Park. The business districts of Parramatta and North Ryde are 15 minutes’ drive away.
