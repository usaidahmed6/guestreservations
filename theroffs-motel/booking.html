About Theroff's Motel

This Washington, Indiana motel features free Wi-Fi throughout the property. The Waterworld of Washington Waterpark is 2 miles away. Each guest room includes cable TV. A microwave and refrigerator are provided in every room at Theroffs Motel. Guests can make use of the work desk or relax in the seating area. Free parking is available on site at Theroffs Motel Washington. A vending machine with drinks is offered at this pet-friendly accommodations. The Daviess County Museum is 5 minutes drive from this property. Outdoor fun at East Side Park is just 1 mile away.
