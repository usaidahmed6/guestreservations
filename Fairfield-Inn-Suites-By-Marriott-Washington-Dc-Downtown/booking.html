About Fairfield Inn & Suites by Marriott Washington, DC/Downtown

Fairfield Inn & Suites by Marriott Washington Downtown is in Washington, D.C.’s Chinatown neighborhood features free Wi-Fi access. A flat-screen cable TV and air conditioning are included in each room, extras include a desk and ironing facilities. Each bathroom is equipped with a hairdryer. Fairfield Inn & Suites by Marriott Washington Downtown offers a 24-hour front desk, complimentary breakfast and a snack bar. Other facilities offered include dry cleaning and a vending machine. Lunch and dinner are available on-site. Additional dining options are just a short walk from the hotel. The Smithsonian Institution is 5 minutes drive from Fairfield Inn & Suites by Marriott Washington Downtown. Capital One Arena, home of the Washington Capitals hockey team, is just 3 minutes walk.
