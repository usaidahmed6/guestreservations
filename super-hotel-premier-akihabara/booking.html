About Super Hotel Premier Akihabara

Located in the center of Tokyo, a few steps from Yanagimori Shrine, Super Hotel Premier Akihabara provides air-conditioned rooms and free WiFi. The property is close to Koobu Inari Shrine, Kusawakeinari Shrine and Akihabara Convention Hall. The property is close to popular attractions like Sakuma Park, Izumi Park and TKP Garden City Premium Akihabara. At the hotel, all rooms have a desk, a TV, a private bathroom, bed linen and towels. Super Hotel Premier Akihabara offers 3-star accommodations with a hot spring bath. Popular points of interest near the accommodation include Akihabara Park, Old Manseibashi Station and Fujisoft Akiba Plaza. The nearest airport is Tokyo Haneda Airport, 12 miles from Super Hotel Premier Akihabara.
