About NH Nice

Featuring a seasonal rooftop swimming pool, sauna and terrace, NH Nice is a hotel located a 10-minute walk from Nice's Old Town and the Port. It offers free WiFi and is next to Nice Acropolis. Rooms at NH Nice are contemporary in style and offer a flat-screen TV and a mini-bar. Each is air-conditioned and has a private bathroom. All rooms are serviced by the hotel’s elevator. A breakfast is offered every morning in L'Oliveraie restaurant and during summer the NH Nice rooftop 360° restaurant serves Mediterranean cuisine for lunch and dinner. Open for fine weather in summer months, the hotel bar prepares cocktails which guests can enjoy on the rooftop terrace, overlooking Nice’s rooftops." Additional facilities include a fitness center. The hotel also has a sauna and solarium. NH Nice is a 5-minute drive from the A8 and private off-site parking is available near the hotel for 20€/24h. Monte-Carlo is 20 minutes away by car.
