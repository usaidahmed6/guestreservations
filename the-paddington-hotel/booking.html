About The Crescent Hyde Park

This hotel is located a short walk from Hyde Park, in the quiet surroundings of Bayswater. Lancaster Gate Underground Station is a 5-minute walk away, with access to London’s attractions via the Central line. The Crescent Hyde Park offers rooms that are equipped with a TV and tea-and-coffee-making facilities. Kensington Gardens and the shops of Knightsbridge can be reached in around 20 minutes’ walk.
