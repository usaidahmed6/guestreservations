About Hilton Washington DC Capitol Hill

Located in the city center of Washington, D.C, this hotel is within 2789 feet of the United States Capitol Building and Union Metro Station. The rooms at Hilton Washington DC Capitol Hill Hotel offer city and U.S. Capitol Building views. A flat-screen cable TV, a desk and coffee maker are included in each room. The marble private bathrooms provide a hairdryer. Hilton Washington DC Capitol Hill offers an on-site restaurant, and a fitness center. The National Gallery of Art is a 0.7 mi away while Ronald Reagan Washington National Airport is 5 mi away.
