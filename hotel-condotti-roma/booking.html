About Hotel Condotti

Stay in the Heart of Rome at Hotel Condotti

Immerse yourself in the beauty of Rome at Hotel Condotti, a charming boutique hotel nestled on a serene, picturesque street just moments from the iconic Spanish Steps. This intimate haven is ideally situated for exploring Rome's historic splendors, with the Spagna Metro Station mere steps away, providing fast access to the Vatican, Trevi Fountain, and Termini Central Station. Stroll to the nearby Via Condotti, a world-renowned fashion hub.

At Hotel Condotti, every detail is carefully curated to provide a warm and welcoming atmosphere. Guests are greeted by a cozy lobby that boasts marble floors, fine antiques, tapestries, and an exquisite Venetian-glass chandelier. The attention to detail extends to the guest rooms, some of which are conveniently located in an adjacent building, ensuring peace and privacy. A delightful continental buffet breakfast awaits every morning, offering the perfect start to a day of exploration.

To enhance your stay, Hotel Condotti is pleased to offer a bespoke airport pick-up service, available upon pre-booking. Experience the perfect blend of comfort and convenience, all set against the backdrop of one of the world's most enchanting cities.

  • Steps from the Spagna Metro Station for easy city navigation
  • Near Via Condotti for top-tier shopping experiences
  • Marble-floored lobby with antique and tapestry decorations
  • Venetian-glass chandelier enhancing the ambient elegance
  • Customized airport pick-up service available with prior arrangement

Whether visiting for leisure or business, Hotel Condotti promises a stay filled with comfort and convenience, right in the center of Rome's cultural and fashion epicenter. Book your stay today and discover all that Rome has to offer.
