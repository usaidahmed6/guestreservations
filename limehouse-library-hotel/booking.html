About Limehouse Library Hotel

Limehouse Library Hotel features a fitness center, terrace, a restaurant and bar in London. The property is around 1.1 miles from Canary Wharf Tube Station, 1.9 miles from Canada Water Tube Station and 2 miles from Tower of London. The hotel has family rooms. The units are equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a minibar, a coffee machine, a shower, free toiletries and a desk. Featuring a private bathroom with a hairdryer, rooms at the hotel also have free WiFi, while some have a city view. At Limehouse Library Hotel all rooms are equipped with bed linen and towels. A buffet, continental or Full English/Irish breakfast is available every morning at the property. Speaking English and Turkish at the 24-hour front desk, staff are always on hand to help. Brick Lane is 2.1 miles from the accommodation, while Tower Bridge is 2.2 miles away. The nearest airport is London City Airport, 4.3 miles from Limehouse Library Hotel.
