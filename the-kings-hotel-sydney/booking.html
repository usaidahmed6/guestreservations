About The King's Hotel Sydney

The King's Hotel Sydney is set in Sydney, 1.5 mi from Capitol Theater and a 30-minute walk from the Sydney Opera House. Each room at this motel has sound proofing, heating, is air conditioned and features a flat-screen TV. You will find a kettle in the room. Rooms are fitted with a private bathroom. Parking is available off-site, a 4-minute walk from the property. Kings Cross train station is a 3-minute walk from the property, while Allianz Stadium is 1.1 mi from The King's Hotel Sydney. Circular Quay is 1.2 mi from the property. The nearest airport is Kingsford Smith Airport, 6.2 mi from The King's Hotel Sydney. Guests can take a 30-minute (approx.) taxi ride from the airport to the property, or catch the train from the airport, which takes approximately 40 minutes to The King's Hotel Sydney.
