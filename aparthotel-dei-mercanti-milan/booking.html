About Aparthotel Dei Mercanti Milano

Set 328 feet from Milan Cathedral, Aparthotel Dei Mercanti offers air-conditioned accommodations with free WiFi in Milan. La Scala is a 5-minute walk from the property. Apartments feature views of the city, a fully equipped kitchenette and seating area with a sofa bed. One unit offers a spa bath. Duomo Metro Station is a few steps from Aparthotel Dei Mercanti, with direct links to Milan Centrale Metro and Train Station. Milan Fashion District is 1.2 mi away.

Amenities

  • Room Service
  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • Non-Smoking Facility
