About The Baray Villa by Sawasdee Village

Featuring an outdoor swimming pool, garden and views of pool, The Baray Villa by Sawasdee Village is located in Kata Beach, a 14-minute walk from Karon Beach. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, a 24-hour front desk and room service, along with free WiFi throughout the property. The property has a concierge service, luggage storage space and currency exchange for guests. At the resort the rooms include air conditioning, a seating area, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a safety deposit box and a private bathroom with a shower, bathrobes and a hairdryer. The Baray Villa by Sawasdee Village provides some accommodations with garden views, and the rooms come with a terrace. All rooms will provide guests with a desk and an electric tea pot. Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a buffet or an American breakfast. Kata Beach is a 15-minute walk from The Baray Villa by Sawasdee Village, while Kata Noi Beach is one mile from the property. The nearest airport is Phuket International, 28 miles from the resort, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
