About Capital Rooms

Providing city views, Capital Rooms in Rome features accommodations and a shared lounge. Among the facilities at this property are a 24-hour front desk and an elevator, along with free Wifi throughout the property. There's private parking and the property provides an electric vehicle charging station. The bed and breakfast will provide guests with air-conditioned units with a desk, a kettle, a minibar, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom with a bidet. Additional in-room amenities include chocolates or cookies. At the bed and breakfast, all units have bed linen and towels. There is a snack bar, and a mini-market is also available. Both a bicycle rental service and a car rental service are available at the bed and breakfast. Popular points of interest near Capital Rooms include Ottaviano Metro Station, Lepanto Metro Station, and Vatican Museums. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino, 12 miles from the accommodation, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
