About Houspitality Nero Bed & Breakfast

Featuring free Wi-Fi access throughout, Houspitality Nero B&B offers rooms in Rome, a 3-minute walk from Vittorio Emanuele Metro Station. Santa Maria Maggiore Church is 2297 feet away. With air conditioning, each room has modern furnishings and a safety deposit box. The private bathroom comes with a shower and hairdryer. Roma Termini Train Station is a 12-minute walk from B&B Nero Houspitality.

Amenities

  • Room Service
  • Masks are available to guests
  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
