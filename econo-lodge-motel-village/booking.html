About Econo Lodge Motel Village

Located in Calgary, Econo Lodge Motel Village is less than 1.2 mi from the University of Calgary. The motel offers an exercise room and laundry facilities. Each air-conditioned room is equipped with free WiFi, cable TV and free movie channels. A microwave and a fridge are offered in all rooms. Guests can enjoy free coffee and newspapers and the 24-hour front desk. Vending machines are on site. Motel Village Econo Lodge is 12 mi from the Calgary International Airport. The Foothills Hospital is 1.2 mi away from the motel.
