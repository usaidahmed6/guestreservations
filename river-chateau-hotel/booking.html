About River Chateau Hotel

Just 984 feet from the banks of the Tiber River, the 4-star River Chateau Hotel is a 5-minute walk from the Ponte Milvio bridge. It offers a free gym and elegant rooms decorated with precious fabrics, marble, and mosaics. These air-conditioned rooms feature a mini-bar, free wired internet, and a flat-screen TV with satellite and pay-per-view channels. Each has a private bathroom with a hairdryer, bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries. A sweet and savory buffet-style breakfast is served daily in the dining hall. The River Chateau is complete with a bar. Just 0.9 mi from the Olympic Stadium, this hotel is a 5-minute drive from the Auditorium Parco della Musica. Saint Peters Square is 3.1 mi away.
