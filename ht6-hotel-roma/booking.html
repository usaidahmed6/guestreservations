About HT6 Hotel Roma

Set in a historical building in the Roman Ghetto area, HT6 Hotel Roma offers stylish rooms with parquet floors and free WiFi throughout. The Great Synagogue of Rome is 164 feet from the property. Featuring a satellite smart LED TV and Art Nouveau décor, rooms here have air conditioning, a mini-bar, and an electric kettle. Some rooms include an in-room spa bath and chromotherapy shower with steam function. Every morning you can start your day with an extensive buffet-style breakfast including cakes and homemade croissants, as well as cold cuts and cheese. Scrambled eggs and bacon are also included. Original works of art are displayed at HT6 Hotel Roma's lounge bar where you can have a drink or an apéritif. The Pantheon is 2297 feet away on foot.
