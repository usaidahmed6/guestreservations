About AC Hotel by Marriott Washington DC Downtown

Attractively located in Washington, D.C., AC Hotel by Marriott Washington DC Downtown has air-conditioned rooms, a fitness center, free WiFi and a shared lounge. This 4-star hotel offers a 24-hour front desk and a business center. Guests can enjoy American and Spanish dishes at the restaurant or have a drink at the bar. At the hotel, rooms come with a desk, a flat-screen TV, a private bathroom, bed linen and towels. The rooms will provide guests with a fridge. An American breakfast is available each morning at AC Hotel by Marriott Washington DC Downtown. Popular points of interest near the accommodation include The White House, Phillips Collection and Vietnam Veterans Memorial. The nearest airport is Ronald Reagan Washington National Airport, 6.8 miles from AC Hotel by Marriott Washington DC Downtown.
