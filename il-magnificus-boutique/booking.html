About Il Magnificus Boutique

Il Magnificus Boutique offers accommodations in Rome, 1.5 miles from Universit La Sapienza and 1.9 miles from Roma Termini. Among the facilities at this property are a 24-hour front desk and an elevator, along with free Wifi throughout the property. Each room has a flat-screen TV with streaming services. Accessible via private entrance, all rooms have air conditioning, soundproofing, desk, and a private bathroom with a walk-in shower. Rooms have a coffee machine, while some rooms also boast a fully equipped kitchen with a toaster, a stovetop, and kitchenware. At the guest house, units have bed linen and towels. There is an on-site snack bar. Popular points of interest near the guest house include Tiburtina Metro Station, Roma Tiburtina Train Station, and Bologna Metro Station. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino Airport, 15 miles from Il Magnificus Boutique.
