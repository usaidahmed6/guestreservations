About Bahia Mar Fort Lauderdale Beach- A DoubleTree By Hilton

Welcome to Bahia Mar Fort Lauderdale Beach - DoubleTree by Hilton

Experience the essence of coastal luxury at Bahia Mar Fort Lauderdale Beach - DoubleTree by Hilton, where sun-drenched shores and ocean breezes await. Nestled in the heart of Fort Lauderdale, our resort offers direct access to pristine beaches through a private walkway, ensuring a perfect getaway.

Key Features:

  • Direct beach access via a private walkway, making it easy for guests to enjoy Fort Lauderdale Beach's golden sands and crystal-clear waters.
  • Outdoor pool and lighted tennis courts, providing leisure and sport activities for all ages and skill levels.
  • Water sports facility on-site offering Jet Ski hires, snorkeling equipment, and boats, perfect for adventure seekers.
  • Spacious guest rooms equipped with a 40-inch flat-screen TV, microwave, refrigerator, and coffee-making facilities, accented with warm, inviting decor.
  • Diverse dining options including Breezes Bar & Grille for all-day dining, Waves Poolbar & Grille for poolside snacks, and All About Food Market Deli for quick meals.

Bahia Mar Fort Lauderdale Beach - DoubleTree by Hilton is not just a destination but a retreat for those seeking a blend of relaxation and adventure. With our 250 slip marina, state-of-the-art fitness center, and close proximity to the International Swimming Hall of Fame and Jungle Queen Riverboat, guests are assured an unforgettable stay filled with comfort and convenience.
