About Hilton Fort Lauderdale Marina

Discover the Charm of Hilton Fort Lauderdale Marina

Immerse in the scenic beauty of Fort Lauderdale at the Hilton Fort Lauderdale Marina, perfectly situated to offer captivating views of the Intracoastal Waterway and a stone's throw away from the pristine beaches that define this vibrant city. This distinguished hotel is not only notable for its prime location but also for its dedication to providing an enriching stay with a plethora of thoughtful amenities and well-appointed accommodations.

Guests are welcomed into a world where convenience meets sophistication, with spacious guest rooms adorned with floor-to-ceiling windows for an unobstructed view of the waterway's serene beauty, complemented by flat-screen TVs for in-room entertainment. The on-site marina adds a unique flair to the stay, allowing maritime enthusiasts direct access to the water. The hotel's waterfront restaurant offers a dining experience that combines delicious cuisine with breathtaking views, ensuring every meal is memorable.

Proximity to Fort Lauderdale's key attractions like the Broward County Convention Center and the bustling streets of Las Olas Boulevard places guests at the heart of the action, with endless opportunities for exploration and enjoyment. Whether it's indulging in the local nightlife, shopping for unique finds, or engaging in a variety of recreational activities, everything is within easy reach.

Key Features:

  • Prime location with views of the Intracoastal Waterway and easy beach access
  • On-site marina for boating enthusiasts
  • Waterfront dining experience at the hotel's restaurant
  • Outdoor pool for relaxation and leisure
  • Modern fitness center for health and wellness pursuits

Embark on a journey of discovery and comfort at the Hilton Fort Lauderdale Marina, where every stay is designed to be unforgettable, nestled between the allure of natural beauty and the excitement of city life.
