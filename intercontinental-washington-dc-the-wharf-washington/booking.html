About InterContinental - Washington D.C. - The Wharf

This hotel is located along the mile-long pier of The Wharf, featuring restaurants, retail and fun activities on DC’s Potomac River. The guestroom amenities include a Nespresso machine®, 50 inch Smart TV's with Bluetooth, in-room safe and a mini bar. The property is home to three dining and bar options, a state-of-the-art fitness center, a business center and a rooftop pool to take in the DC skyline. The property is located just 12 minutes from Ronald Reagan Washington National Airport and 45 minutes from Washington Dulles International Airport. It is also just 15 minutes from Union Station, The White House, the Capitol Hill and many other iconic destinations in the District.
