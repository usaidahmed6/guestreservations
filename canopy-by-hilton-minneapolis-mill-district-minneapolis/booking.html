About Canopy by Hilton Minneapolis Mill District

Well set in the center of Minneapolis, Canopy by Hilton Minneapolis Mill District provides air-conditioned rooms, free bikes, free WiFi and a fitness center. This 4-star hotel offers a 24-hour front desk and an ATM. Guests can enjoy American and French dishes at the restaurant or have a drink at the bar. At the hotel, every room includes a desk. All guest rooms at Canopy by Hilton Minneapolis Mill District are equipped with a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a safety deposit box. There's an on-site snack bar and guests can also use the business area. Popular points of interest near the accommodation include U.S. Bank Stadium, Target Field and Minnesota Orchestra. The nearest airport is Minneapolis-Saint Paul International Airport, 10 miles from Canopy by Hilton Minneapolis Mill District.
