About The Adrian

Set 1640 feet from San Francisco City Hall, the Adrian Hotel features free WiFi access. Guests can enjoy surrounding restaurants in the middle of downtown San Francisco. All rooms are equipped with a TV. Guest rooms offer a city view of San Francisco’s Tenderloin neighborhood. The front desk is open 24 hours. Union square is 2953 feet from Adrian Hotel, while Moscone Center is 0.9 mi from the property. The nearest airport is San Francisco International Airport, which is 8.7 mi from the property.

Amenities

  • Room Service
  • 24 hour front desk
  • Non-Smoking Facility
