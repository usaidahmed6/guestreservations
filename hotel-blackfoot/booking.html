About Hotel Blackfoot

Welcome to Hotel Blackfoot

Discover the charm of Calgary at Hotel Blackfoot, perfectly situated just a short distance from the bustling Chinook Center Mall and the vibrant heart of the city. This unique establishment not only offers comfortable accommodations with stunning views of the city and mountains but also hosts an exclusive on-site comedy club for entertainment right at your doorstep.

Each room at Hotel Blackfoot is designed with guest comfort in mind, featuring high-speed internet access and convenient coffee-making facilities. Guests can choose rooms that overlook the bustling cityscape, the serene mountains, or the hotel's inviting outdoor pool. Whether in town for business or leisure, the amenities and services here cater to every need.

For those seeking relaxation, in-room spa services provide a private sanctuary of wellness. The culinary delights at Green's Restaurant present a perfect blend of local flavors and innovative cuisine, promising a memorable dining experience every time.

Active guests will appreciate the 24-hour fitness room and the seasonal heated outdoor pool, offering a refreshing way to unwind or kick-start the day. With Calgary Stampede Park just a short drive away and Calgary Zoo also easily accessible, Hotel Blackfoot serves as your gateway to explore the best of Calgary.

  • Exclusive on-site comedy club
  • In-room spa services
  • On-site dining at Green's Restaurant
  • 24-hour fitness room
  • Seasonal heated outdoor pool

Whether planning a business trip or a leisure getaway, Hotel Blackfoot invites guests to experience a comfortable and vibrant stay in the heart of Calgary.
