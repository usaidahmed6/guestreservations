About Sydney Central YHA

Sydney Central YHA: Experience Sydney's Vibrant Heart

Housed in a heritage-listed building directly opposite Central Station, Sydney Central YHA offers award-winning accommodations in the bustling Central Business District. Enjoy panoramic views of the city from the rooftop outdoor area or take a dip in the indoor pool. With a variety of activities and facilities, this hostel is an ideal choice for travelers looking to immerse themselves in the dynamic energy of Sydney.

  • Rooftop area with panoramic city views
  • Indoor pool for year-round swimming
  • On-site cinema showing the latest films
  • Travel agency, café, restaurant, and BBQ facilities on-site
  • Convenient access to Central Station and Haymarket light rail stop

Sydney Central YHA provides a range of accommodations, from shared dormitories to private rooms, all with access to free WiFi. Guests can make use of two kitchens, spacious dining and lounge areas, and a guest laundry. The hostel also features Schirrmo's Bar in the basement, open most evenings for drinks. Enjoy organized activities such as pizza nights, walking tours, movie nights with free popcorn, and rooftop BBQs, making it easy to meet fellow travelers and explore the city.

Located just 6.2 miles from Sydney International Airport and easily accessible via Central Station, Sydney Central YHA is the perfect base for exploring all that Sydney has to offer. From the vibrant atmosphere of the hostel to its prime location, a stay at Sydney Central YHA ensures an unforgettable Sydney experience.
