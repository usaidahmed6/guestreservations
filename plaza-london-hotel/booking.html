About Plaza London Hotel

Just a 5-minute walk from Bethnal Green Tube Station, the Plaza London Hotel is a 5-minute tube journey from the Olympic Stadium in Stratford. Located in the East End of London, this hotel provides a fully-equipped and modern kitchen for communal use. Victoria Park, in which regular festivals and music events are held, is less than 10 minutes’ walk away. Olympic zone Stratford City is only 10 minutes; King’s Cross St. Pancras international is within a 20-minute, Canary Wharf is within 20 minutes and London West End is 15 minutes by London Underground. Each room comes with a smart TV with cable channels, an private bathroom and air-conditioning. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel. A flat-screen TV and sofas feature in the Plaza’s spacious lounge. English breakfast is served in the hotel’s dining room.
