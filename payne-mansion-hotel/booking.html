About Mansion on Sutter

Mansion on Sutter is located in San Francisco, a 15-minute walk from San Francisco City Hall and one mile from Union Square. The property is around 1.8 miles from Moscone Center, 1.9 miles from Ghirardelli Square and 2.2 miles from University of San Francisco. Free WiFi is available and private parking can be arranged at an extra charge. The rooms at the hotel come with air conditioning, a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a safety deposit box and a private bathroom with a shower, free toiletries and a hairdryer. At Mansion on Sutter rooms contain bed linen and towels. A gym, business center and ironing facilities are available at the accommodation. Staff speak English and Spanish at the reception. Coit Tower is 2.5 miles from Mansion on Sutter, while Alcatraz is 2.6 miles from the property. The nearest airport is San Francisco International Airport, 12 miles from the hotel.
