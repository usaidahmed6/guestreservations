About Hotel Victor Hugo Nice

Hotel Victor Hugo Nice is located on the ground-floor of a 20th-century house with marble staircase, just a 5-minute walk from the Promenade des Anglais and the beach, as well as a 10-minute walk from shops and Nice's Old Town. It offers 7 rooms and suites with a private bathroom and a kitchenette. Guests can choose between rooms and studio, all equipped with air-conditioning, a flat screen TV and a clock radio. Rooms feature a balcony and the private bathrooms have a shower and a hairdryer. A buffet breakfast is served daily and many restaurants are located in the area. Hotel Victor Hugo also provides daily newspapers and free WiFi access. The Victor Hugo is just 2461 feet from Nice-Ville Station and 3.7 mi from Nice-Cte dAzur Airport.
