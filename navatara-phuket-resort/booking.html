About Navatara Phuket Resort - SHA Extra Plus

Located in Rawai Beach area at south of Phuket Island, Navatara Phuket Resort offers an accommodations with an outdoor pool and free WiFi in all rooms. Free parking is available on site. The resort offers modern air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi. Rooms are fitted with a balcony, TV and refrigerator. A safety deposit box and private bathroom are included. Some rooms have a pool access. The beautiful swimming beach Nai Harn with its white sand is a short ride away with the resort's shuttle service. For scenic view points, Phuket's Promthep Cape and the local Yanui Beach are just under 1.2 mi from the resort. Guests can find local seafood restaurants along the beachfront or use the Rawai Beach as a starting point to do some island hopping. Staff at the 24-hour front desk can assist guests with laundry and shuttle service. Other services include luggage storage and fax and photocopying. Phuket International Airport is about an hour drive from the property.
