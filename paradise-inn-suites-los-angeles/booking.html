About Paradise Inn & Suites

Paradise Inn & Suites is perfectly located for both business and leisure guests in Los Angeles (CA). The hotel offers guests a range of services and amenities designed to provide comfort and convenience. Free Wi-Fi in all rooms, daily housekeeping, laundromat, facilities for disabled guests, Wi-Fi in public areas are just some of the facilities on offer. Comfortable guestrooms ensure a good night's sleep with some rooms featuring facilities such as television LCD/plasma screen, internet access wireless, internet access wireless (complimentary), non smoking rooms, air conditioning. The hotel offers various recreational opportunities. Discover all Los Angeles (CA) has to offer by making Paradise Inn & Suites your base.
