About Ice Kamala Beach Hotel

Ice Kamala Beach Hotel is located 25-yards from Kamala Beach. It offers spacious rooms with a balcony and flat-screen cable TV. Guests also enjoy free Wi-Fi and parking. The air-conditioned rooms at Beach Kamala Ice Hotel are fitted with hardwood furnishings. Each room is equipped with a flat-screen TV and refrigerator. Hot shower facilities are provided in the private bathroom. Guests of the hotel can enjoy a relaxing body massage. Kamala Ice Beach Hotel offers bicycle rental and airport shuttle services. A tour desk can assist with travel and sightseeing arrangements. Meals are served at the on-site restaurant. A wide variety of local dishes and beverages are available. Hotel Ice Kamala Beach is a 5-minute walk from Phuket Fantasea Show. Phuket International Airport is a 45-minute drive from the hotel.
