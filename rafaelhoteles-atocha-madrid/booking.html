About Rafaelhoteles Atocha

This conveniently located hotel is set near Madrids transport hubs of lvaro Mendez and Atocha terminal for the AVE high-speed lines. The contemporary-style Rafaelhoteles Atocha is filled with impressive artwork, putting you in the mood to visit the nearby galleries of Reina Sofia, Prado and Thyssen-Bornemisza. After a days sightseeing you can relax with a refreshing cocktail and a meal beneath the canopy in the pleasant terrace garden. There is also a comfortable lounge where you can meet up with friends and family before enjoying the citys vibrant nightlife. recommended for 2 adults + 1 child
