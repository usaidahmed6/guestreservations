About Sandman Hotel & Suites Calgary South

This Calgary hotel is 6 miles from downtown Calgary and The Military Museums. Sandman Hotel features a 24-hour restaurant and contemporary rooms with free Wi-Fi. Guest rooms at Sandman Hotel and Suites Calgary South are designed with modern styles and have flat-screen TVs. All rooms are furnished with a work desk and coffee maker. American dishes are served at Denny’s restaurant, open 24 hours at Calgary South Sandman Hotel. Shark Club Sports Bar & Grill Restaurant has additional dining options. Guests are provided free parking and use of the business center. Deerfoot Meadows Shopping Center, with a variety of shopping, dining and entertainment options, is a 5-minute drive from Sandman Calgary South. The hotel is just over one mile from RCGA Golf Center.
