About 4c Bravo Murillo

The 4C Bravo Murillo hotel is located near Cuatro Caminos, in the commercial area of Madrid. It is a short walk from the famous Bernabeu soccer stadium. The hotel offers comfortable, good-value accommodations. The rooms have extra-long beds and 26-inch plasma TV. The private bathrooms have hairdryers and heated towel racks. The Bravo Murillo has excellent access to public transportation to the center of the city. Estrecho metro station is right next to the hotel. The AZCA business area is also less than half a mile away.

Amenities

  • 24 hour front desk
  • Masks are available to guests
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • Non-Smoking Facility
