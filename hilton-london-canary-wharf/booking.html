About Hilton London Canary Wharf

Ideally located just a short 6-minute walk from Canary Wharf Underground Station, our hotel offers effortless access to London's iconic landmarks. The nearby Elizabeth Line, a mere 10-minute walk away, ensures swift connections to the city's treasures. From the towering skyscrapers of Canary Wharf to the historic charm of the West End, our guests enjoy easy access to London's most iconic sights. Discover the vibrant energy of the West End, with landmarks like London Bridge just a 7-minute tube ride away, or immerse yourself in the heart of the city with a 12-minute journey to Westminster. Additionally, the O2 Arena is a mere 5-minute journey by Jubilee Line, making it convenient for guests attending events or concerts. Indulge in contemporary comfort with our air-conditioned rooms, equipped with modern amenities including free Wi-Fi and flat-screen TVs. Unwind after a day of exploration in our well-equipped gym, sauna and steam room. Savor international cuisine at our stylish Cinnamon Bar & Restaurant, or enjoy a cocktail at TwoRuba bar, where lively ambiance meets exquisite drinks. With 24-hour room service, your every need is effortlessly catered to. For those arriving by car, take advantage of our convenient on-site parking facilities, ensuring a stress-free experience from the moment you arrive. Whether you're here for business or pleasure, Hilton London Canary Wharf promises a memorable stay where every moment is crafted with comfort and convenience in mind.
