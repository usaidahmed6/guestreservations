About The Westin Washington, DC Downtown

This hotel is located opposite Washington Convention Center, between Capitol Hill and the White House. Guests of The Westin Washington, DC Downtown have access to the on-site fitness center. All guest rooms have cable TV, pay-per-view movies and high-speed internet access. All rooms are air-conditioned and feature a work desk and coffee maker. The Westin Washington, DC Downtown has several restaurants on site, including theRoot and Vine offering breakfast. The hotel also features the market at Root and Vine, a Starbucks caf and a Root and Vine offering light fare and drinks. The Westin Washington, DC Downtown is 1 mi from the Smithsonian Institute and the Air & Space Museum. There are 2 metro stations within 5 minutes walk of the hotel. The Westin Washington, DC Downtown is 25 mi from Washington Dulles Airport and 5 mi from Ronald Reagan Washington National. On-site car parking is available
