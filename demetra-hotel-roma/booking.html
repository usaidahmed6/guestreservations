About Demetra Hotel

Opened in 2012, the Demetra Hotel is opposite the Rome Opera House and 5 minutes' walk from Termini Station. This hotel offers free WiFi throughout, and modern-style rooms with air conditioning and wood floors. Hotel Demetra occupies 3 floors of a 6-story building. Rooms are soundproofed and include an LCD TV with Sky channels, and an private bathroom with free toiletries and a hairdryer. A continental buffet-style breakfast is available each morning. Reception is open 24-hours a day and there are comfortable lounge areas with contemporary furnishings. The many shops of Via Nazionale are just 656 feet away. The metro stations of Termini and Repubblica are 5 minutes away on foot and you have lots of good bus links around the city.
