About Annex Copenhagen

In the trendy Vesterbro district, the Annex Copenhagen provides good-value accommodations only a quarter mile from Tivoli Gardens and Copenhagen Central Train Station. Restaurants, shops and popular nightclubs are just steps away. The colorful rooms of Annex Copenhagen include Fatboy lounge chairs and a flat-screen TV. Bathroom facilities are shared. Nearby attractions include the City Hall Square and Strget, the longest pedestrian shopping street in Europe, both of which are about a 10-minute walk away. The staff at the Annex Copenhagen will gladly suggest further places for guests to visit.
