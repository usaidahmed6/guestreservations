About Orchid Garden Hotel

Orchid Garden Hotel is located in Phuket, a 5-minute walk from Patong Beach. This nonsmoking hotel features an outdoor pool, a restaurant and rooms with a private balcony. Wi-Fi and parking are free. Garden Orchid Hotel is a 10-minute drive from Karon Beach and a 15-minute drive from Kata Beach. Phuket International Airport is a 45-minute drive away. Air-conditioned rooms come with modern Thai interiors, neutral wall colors and warm lighting. Each is equipped with a cable TV, mini-bar and private bathroom with a hot shower. A hairdryer and toiletries are provided. Guests can laze in the sauna, or relax on the terrace. Staff at the 24-hour front desk can assist with car rental. Orchid restaurant serves a variety of Thai and Scandinavian dishes. Room service is also available upon request.
