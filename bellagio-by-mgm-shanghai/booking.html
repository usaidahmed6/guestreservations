About Bellagio by MGM Shanghai - on the bund

Bellagio Shanghai is the first Bellagio Hotel in China and the second in the world succeeding its sister hotel, Bellagio Las Vegas. Located in Shanghai in the Shanghai Area Region, 0.8 mi from Oriental Pearl Tower, Bellagio Shanghai features a spa center and sauna. The hotel has a fitness center and indoor pool, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant. Guests can enjoy welcome fruits upon arrival. Bellagio Shanghai is 5 minutes' drive to Waibaidu Bridge and 8 minutes' drive to Nanjing Road Pedestrian Street. It takes 15 minutes driving to Oriental Pearl Tower, and Jing'an Temple can be reached within 17 minutes by car. Shanghai Hongqiao International Airport is 50 minutes' drive away, while Pudong International Airport can be reached within 48 minutes by car. Each room at this hotel is air conditioned and is equipped with a flat-screen TV. Certain rooms have a seating area where you can relax. You will find a coffee machine and a kettle in the room. The rooms are equipped with a private bathroom equipped with a bath. For your comfort, you will find bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries. Bellagio Shanghai features free WiFi throughout the property. You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property.
