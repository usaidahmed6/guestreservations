About Novotel Brighton Beach Hotel

Located opposite the beach, this hotel enjoys sweeping views over beautiful Botany Bay, 9.3 mi from Sydney city center. Novotel Sydney Brighton Beach is currently under partial refurbishment including temporary closure of some facilities. This includes the laundry room and some accommodations rooms. The refurbishment does include noisy works during the day. Enjoy 24/7 In-Room Dining in the comfort of your room. We anticipate the disruption to be minimal, and we apologize for any inconvenience. The hotel's guest rooms are modern in design, spacious and well furnished. Each has a private balcony with lovely views over the bay or the city. The surrounding Brighton Beach area has numerous cosmopolitan cafes and restaurants. Sydney Airport is just a 10 minute drive away. Our brand new gym, indoor and outdoor pools are all open. Hotel Bar is called Sands Bar and it offers all day dining from 11am till late 7 days a week.
