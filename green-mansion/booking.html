About The Green Mansion Hotel - Patong Beach

The Green Mansion Hotel - Patong Beach is located in Patong, just 5 minutes' walk from the famous Night Bazaar and Patong Beach. The property is surrounded by local restaurants and bars Decorated in calming shades of green, all rooms come equipped with a cable TV and a fridge. Private bathrooms come with hot and cold water showers. The Green Mansion Hotel - Patong Beach is a 30-minute drive from Phuket Town and a 45-minute drive from Phuket International Airport. For guests' convenience, the hotel provides laundry and ironing services.
