About Campo de' Fiori 34

Campo de' Fiori 34 features a hot tub and air-conditioned accommodations with free Wifi in the center of Rome, a 7-minute walk from Torre Argentina. The property is around a 4-minute walk from Piazza Navona, a few steps from Campo de' Fiori, and a 9-minute walk from Great Synagogue of Rome. The accommodation offers a shared kitchen, full-day security, and organizing tours for guests. At the guest house, every unit is fitted with a desk, a flat-screen TV, a private bathroom, bed linen, and towels. Every room is fitted with a coffee machine, while certain rooms have a fully equipped kitchen with a dishwasher, a stovetop, and kitchenware. At the guest house, all units are allergy-free. Buffet and continental breakfast options with fresh pastries, fruit, and juice are available each morning at the guest house. There is a coffee shop, bar, and lounge. Popular points of interest near Campo de' Fiori 34 include Pantheon, Palazzo Venezia, and Piazza Venezia. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino, 10 miles from the accommodation, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
