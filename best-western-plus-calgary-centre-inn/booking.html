About Best Western Plus Calgary Centre Inn

Featuring an indoor pool, hot tub, and on-site gym, this Calgary hotel is located 5 minutes from the city center and less than 1.9 miles from Calgary Tower. Free WiFi is available. The rooms at the Calgary Center Inn Best Western Plus are equipped with cable TV. A coffee maker and hairdryer are provided. A continental breakfast is served daily and the front desk is open 24 hours a day. The Calgary Zoo, Botanical Garden, and Prehistoric Park are less than 10 minutes from Best Western Plus Calgary Center Inn. The Talisman Center is just 5 minutes away from the hotel.
