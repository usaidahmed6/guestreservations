About Cavour Hotel

Experience Refined Elegance at Cavour Hotel Milan

Located in the heart of Milan, Cavour Hotel presents a blend of comfort and style, situated just steps from Montenapoleone Metro Station and a short stroll from the iconic Milan Cathedral. Ideal for both leisure and business travelers, this hotel combines classic Italian charm with modern amenities.

Key Features:

  • Prime Location Just 984 feet from Montenapoleone Metro Station and close to Milan's famed attractions.
  • Luxurious Accommodations Spacious rooms featuring LCD TVs, soft slippers, and the option of private Turkish baths in select rooms.
  • Dining Excellence Enjoy Conte Camillo restaurants classic Italian cuisine and a varied breakfast buffet with gluten-free options.
  • Comprehensive Connectivity High-speed wired and wireless internet access available in all rooms.
  • Special Amenities Hypoallergenic rooms with parquet floors available for guests with allergies.

Each guest room at Cavour Hotel offers air conditioning and hypoallergenic options, ensuring comfort and convenience. The hotels restaurant, Conte Camillo, provides a perfect setting for savoring traditional Italian dishes, while the extensive breakfast selection caters to all dietary needs.

Guests can also take advantage of a complimentary internet point with a printer in the lobby, adding to the convenience for business travelers. With its proximity to cultural landmarks such as La Scala Theater and the luxury shopping district, Cavour Hotel is a premier choice for those wishing to explore Milan in style. Linate International Airport is just 4.7 miles away, making arrivals and departures effortless.
