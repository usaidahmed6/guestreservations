About Flower Garden Hotel

Just around the corner from the Opera Theater, Flower Garden Hotel is 984 feet from Termini Station, offering excellent transport links. The generous breakfast buffet includes Italian cappuccino and cakes. All featuring free WiFi and wooden floors, rooms at Flower Garden have air conditioning, satellite TV and a mini-bar, while some include a sofa. Bathrooms are complete with a hairdryer and free toiletries. Staff are available 24 hours a day and can organize city tours or transfers to Fiumicino and Ciampino Airports. You can walk to the Coliseum in 15 minutes. Repubblica Metro Station and the shopping street Via Nazionale are 820 feet away.
