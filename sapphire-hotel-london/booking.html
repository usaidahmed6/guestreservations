About Sapphire Hotel London

The O2 Shepherds Bush Empire music venue is just 330 yards from this modern hotel. Private Italian marble bathrooms feature in each soundproof room. Europe’s largest shopping center, Westfield, is a 10 minute walk away. The Sapphire has a 24-hour front desk and an on-site tour desk. Hammersmith and Shepherd's Bush subway stations, shops, restaurants and bars are all within a 5 minute walk of Sapphire Hotel London. Harrods, Harvey Nichols, Big Ben, Parliament Square and the museum district of South Kensington are all reachable within 15 minutes via tube. A flat-screen TV with DVD player, a fridge and tea and coffee-making facilities are provided in each highly furnished, neutrally decorated room. The bathrooms include a hairdryer and a selection of toiletries. Continental breakfasts are served in the partner hotel's dining area a short walk down the street. Free Wi-Fi can be accessed throughout the hotel.
