About Omni Berkshire Place

Experience the Heart of New York City at Omni Berkshire Place

Omni Berkshire Place stands as a beacon of sophistication and convenience in New York City's prestigious Madison Avenue. Just steps from St. Patrick's Cathedral and the famed shops and art galleries of 5th Avenue, this exclusive hotel offers a quintessential New York experience. Its prime location ensures that guests are merely moments away from iconic attractions such as Rockefeller Center, Radio City Music Hall, the Broadway Theater District, Times Square, and Central Park. With LaGuardia Airport less than 9 kilometers away, Omni Berkshire Place is a perfect gateway for exploring the vibrancy of the city.

Guests are welcomed into rooms that epitomize comfort and convenience, each furnished thoughtfully and equipped with practical appliances. The hotel's dedication to guest satisfaction is evident in the attention to detail and the provision of high-quality amenities.

  • Prime location on Madison Avenue, adjacent to St. Patrick's Cathedral and moments from 5th Avenue
  • Close proximity to New York City's major attractions, including Central Park and Times Square
  • State-of-the-art fitness center with views of St. Patrick's Cathedral and advanced exercise equipment
  • Rooms designed for ultimate comfort with elegant furniture and practical amenities
  • Easy access to and from LaGuardia Airport, ensuring a seamless travel experience

Whether you're visiting for business or leisure, Omni Berkshire Place invites you to immerse yourself in the energy of New York City while enjoying the peace and comfort. Discover the perfect blend of location, luxury, and service at Omni Berkshire Place, where every stay is crafted to be memorable.
