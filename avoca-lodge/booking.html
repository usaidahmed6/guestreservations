About Avoca Lodge

Avoca Randwick by Sydney Lodges is a boutique property located 10 minutes' walk from Coogee Beach. You can spend the day at the beach, shop in the city or try some of the amazing food in the local streets of Randwick and the eastern suburbs of Sydney. Located just yards from the Prince of Wales Hospital and UNSW Kensington campus, Avoca Randwick by Sydney Lodges is well serviced by public transport. Reach the Sydney CBD and Sydney Harbor in just 20 minutes by light rail or bus. Avoca Randwick by Sydney Lodges offers modern comforts in all rooms: air-conditioning, free WiFi up to 1GB per day, flat-screen TV's, a small refrigerator and tea and coffee-making facilities. Each room also includes a private private bathroom with free toiletries. Premium linen and towels are included. Avoca Randwick by Sydney Lodges offers guests the use of a shared communal kitchen and guest laundry. There are a number of restaurants and cafés in The Spot area, just a 5-minute walk from Avoca Randwick by Sydney Lodges.
