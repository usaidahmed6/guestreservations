About Enterprise Hotel Design & Boutique

With a 7th-floor spa with panoramic views, the Enterprise Hotel is a luxurious choice in Milan. It is near the Mico Convention Center, less than a 10-minute walk from Domodossola metro stop and 2789 feet from Fiera Milano City. Rooms at the 4-star hotel have a contemporary and elegant design with customized furniture, parquet floors, and an LCD TV. You will also have free WiFi and tea and coffee making facilities. Le Terme di Kyoto features sensory showers, a Turkish bath, hot tub and a variety of beauty treatments. Breakfast is an extensive American buffet that includes a natural and wellbeing section, with gluten and sugar free products. A quick breakfast is available before 07:00 for early risers. Sophia's Restaurant offers top-quality dishes, while the Garden Bar is ideal for relaxing to live music or a DJ set. Enterprise Hotel Design & Boutique features a garage, and during major trade fairs it provides a free shuttle to Rho Fiera Milano Exhibition Center. The tram stops 262 feet away and takes you to Milan Cathedral. San Siro Stadium is 2.5 mi from the hotel.
