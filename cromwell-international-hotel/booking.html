About Cromwell International Hotel

The Cromwell International Hotel is in close to the vibrant 'Theatreland', in London, with a 24-hour front desk and multilingual staff. Free WiFi is available in public areas. Professionally managed, the Cromwell International Hotel is located a short walk from famous shopping in Knightsbridge, close to all the major attractions in London. It is within 20 minutes walk of the Science Museum, Natural History Museum, Royal Albert Hall, Hyde Park, and the Olympia and Earl's Court exhibition halls. Visitors from all over the world are made to feel at home. There is a elevator available (though not to the 5th floor) and a meeting room.

Amenities

  • 24 hour front desk
  • Internet Access - Free Public Access
  • Non-Smoking Facility
