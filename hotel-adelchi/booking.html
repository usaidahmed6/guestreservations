About Hotel Adelchi

Hotel Adelchi is located in Lambrate district, just a 6-minute Metro journey from Milan Central Station. It offers simple rooms with TV and a shared or private bathroom. WiFi access is available throughout and the Adelchis guests can use the left luggage facility. The Adelchi Hotel is 5 minutes walk from Lambrate Metro Station and 10 minutes from the university area. Milan Rho-Pero Exhibition Center can be easily reached by metro.

Amenities

  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • Non-Smoking Facility
