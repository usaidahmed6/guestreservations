About acom-Hotel Vienna

acom-Hotel Vienna offers air-conditioned rooms next to the Hauptbahnhof - Main Train Station, and it is 1640 feet away from Belvedere Palace. The hotel is officially certified as being eco-friendly. Rooms in this nonsmoking hotel are fitted with boxspring beds, satellite TV, a work desk, and a bathrooms with hairdryer. Each floor features shared facilities to prepare coffee and tea. Guests of the acom-Hotel Vienna can enjoy a rich breakfast buffet in the breakfast room, containing organic products and fresh local delicacies. A bar offers guests some characteristic Austrian coffee specialties, such as Melange or Einspänner, which can be enjoyed in the cafe, or a Spritzer (wine mixed with sparkling water) and other drinks on the Schanigarten terrace. The center of Vienna, with St. Stephen's Cathedral, is only 3 underground stops away from acom-Hotel Vienna.
