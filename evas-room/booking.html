About Spagna Ave

Set in Rome’s historic center, between the Spanish Steps and Trevi Fountain, Spagna Ave offers a personalized service. There is free WiFi, and the elegant rooms are air-conditioned. Each of the rooms at this family-run guest house has a frescoed ceiling and is equipped with a TV and a safe. On request, Spagna Ave can provide a shuttle service to Rome Fiumicino and Ciampino airports.

Amenities

  • Masks are available to guests
  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • Non-Smoking Facility
