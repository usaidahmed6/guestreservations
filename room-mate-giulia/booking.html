About Room Mate Giulia

Explore Milan in Style at Room Mate Giulia

Experience the vibrancy of Milan with a stay at Room Mate Giulia, ideally located just steps away from the majestic Milan Cathedral and a short stroll to the heart of the Milan Fashion District. This boutique hotel merges a prime location with striking modern design and top-tier amenities, ensuring a memorable visit to Italy's fashion capital.

Key features include:

  • Strategic location near the Duomo Metro Stop, offering effortless access to Milan Centrale Train Station and a direct route to Linate Airport, just 4.7 miles away.
  • Chic, air-conditioned accommodations with designer furnishings, a mini-bar, and a flat-screen TV, providing a comfortable urban retreat.
  • Exclusive fitness center equipped with a sauna, perfect for relaxation and maintaining your workout routine while traveling.
  • Selected suites boasting furnished terraces, offering private outdoor space to enjoy the bustling city atmosphere.
  • Complimentary high-speed WiFi throughout the hotel, ensuring connectivity and convenience for all guests.

Room Mate Giulia not only places guests in the lap of Milanese culture and shopping but also provides a peaceful and stylish sanctuary with its thoughtfully designed spaces. Whether visiting for business or leisure, Room Mate Giulia caters to those seeking an immersive and chic Milan experience. Discover the charm of Milan from a hotel that combines local flavor with global design trends, making every stay unique.
