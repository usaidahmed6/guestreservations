About voco Madrid Retiro, an IHG Hotel

voco Madrid Retiro, an IHG Hotel is a design hotel 2297 feet from Retiro Park. It offers air-conditioned rooms with free Wi-Fi and flat-screen satellite TV. The hotel has its own gardens. The hotel's restaurant serves international buffet meals. There is also a bar and cafe where you can enjoy a snack or drink. The historic city center and famous Prado, Thyssen and Reina Sofa museums are a 30-minute walk away across the park from the Voco Madrid Retiro, an IHG hotel. The Sainz de Baranda metro station is 656 feet away and offers excellent links to the IFEMA center, Barajas Airport and the city center.
