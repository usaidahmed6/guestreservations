About Novotel Bern Expo

This hotel is located 984 feet from Bern Expo. Novotel Bern Expo offers a restaurant, bar, fitness room and a 24-hour reception. All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a kettle, a shower, a hairdryer and a desk. At the hotel every room is fitted with a wardrobe and a private bathroom. Guests at Novotel Bern Expo can enjoy a continental breakfast. Popular points of interest near Novotel Bern Expo include the Wankdorfstadion, PostFinance Arena and Bern Cathedral. The nearest airport is Belp Airport, 3.7 mi from the hotel.
