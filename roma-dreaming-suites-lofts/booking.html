About Roma Dreaming ApartHotel

Roma Dreaming guest house offers modern rooms and studios with design furniture and free internet access. It is located around a 15-minute walk from St. Peter’s Basilica and Castel Sant’Angelo. Featuring particular wall décor and different color schemes, accommodations is air conditioned and comes with an LCD TV and a private bathroom. Studios have a fully equipped kitchenette. Rooms also share a common kitchen which can be used for free. Roma Dreaming is located opposite a bus stop linking with the city center. Rome’s San Pietro Train Station is 820 feet away and links with Fiumicino Airport.
