About Hotel Carlo Goldoni

Located in Milan, 1.1 miles from Villa Necchi Campiglio, Carlo Goldoni Hotel provides accommodations with a terrace, private parking and a bar. Featuring a shared lounge, the 2-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom. The property has room service, and luggage storage for guests. Guest rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, an electric tea pot, a bidet, free toiletries and a desk. All rooms include a safety deposit box. Breakfast is available daily, and includes buffet, continental and Italian options. With staff speaking English, Spanish, French and Italian, guidance is available at the reception. San Babila Station is 1.5 miles from the hotel, while GAM Milano is 1.2 miles away. The nearest airport is Milan Linate Airport, 3.1 miles from Carlo Goldoni Hotel.
