About Courtyard by Marriott Fort Lauderdale Weston

Located just 1.1 miles from the Cleveland Clinic, the newly renovated Courtyard by Marriott Fort Lauderdale Weston offers air-conditioned rooms with a 42" inch flat-screen TV. Facilities include an outdoor heated pool. The recently renovated rooms of Courtyard by Marriott Fort Lauderdale Weston feature a modern interior. All rooms are equipped with a mini-fridge, tea and coffee making facilities, and a living area. Guests can enjoy an American breakfast, fresh coffee, dinner, and full-service bar in The Bistro. The Courtyard Market is open 24 hours and offers a variety of sandwiches and salads. As well as an outdoor swimming pool, the Courtyard also offers a fitness center, a hot tub, and laundry facilities. An enhanced business center with the latest news and travel information is also on site. The Weston Fort Lauderdale Courtyard is 12.4 miles from Dolphin Stadium and 5.7 miles from Regional Park of Weston.
