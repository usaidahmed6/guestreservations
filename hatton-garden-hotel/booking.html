About Hatton Garden Hotel

Ideally located in the Camden district of London, Hatton Garden Hotel is located a 19-minute walk from Royal Opera House, 1.1 miles from British Museum and 1.2 miles from King's Cross St Pancras Tube Station. This 4-star hotel offers room service, a 24-hour front desk and free WiFi. The property is 1.4 miles from the city center and a 14-minute walk from St Paul's Cathedral. The hotel will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms offering a desk, an electric tea pot, a fridge, a safety deposit box, a TV and a private bathroom with a shower. At Hatton Garden Hotel the rooms have bed linen and towels. Popular points of interest near the accommodation include Lyceum Theatre, Theatre Royal Drury Lane and Dominion Theatre. The nearest airport is London City Airport, 8.1 miles from Hatton Garden Hotel.
