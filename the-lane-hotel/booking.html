About The Lane Hotel

Located in Edinburgh, 1.6 miles from EICC, The Lane Hotel has accommodations with a garden, free private parking, a terrace and a restaurant. With free WiFi, this 4-star hotel offers a 24-hour front desk and luggage storage space. Guests can use a bar. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with a shower, while certain accommodations at the hotel also feature a seating area. A buffet, à la carte or Full English/Irish breakfast is available each morning at the property. National Museum of Scotland is 2 miles from The Lane Hotel, while The Real Mary King's Close is 2.1 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Edinburgh Airport, 6.2 miles from the accommodation.
