About Villa Bougainville by Happyculture

Villa Bougainville is a boutique hotel renovated in April 2015 and located in the heart of Nice, in the Musician's district. The property was originally constructed in 1928 and provides a warm cozy environment. All rooms at the Villa Bougainville are equipped with a cable TV and a telephone. The private bathrooms feature a shower and hairdryer and all rooms are serviced by an elevator. A fresh breakfast buffet is available daily and can be enjoyed onsite. Many shops and restaurants can be found within a 10-minute walk. The reception desk is open 24 hours a day and car and bicycle rental can be organized at the hotels tour desk. Ideally located in the center of the city, the hotel is 984 feet from Nice train station, a 5-minute walk from the main street and all the shops, and a 20-minute walk from the Promenade des Anglais and the beaches.
