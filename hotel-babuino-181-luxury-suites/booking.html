About Babuino 181 - Small Luxury Hotels of the World

Babuino 181 is set in 2 historical buildings and offers elegant accommodations in the heart of Rome, between Piazza Del Popolo and the Spanish Steps. Flaminio Metro is 984 feet away. The Babuino 181 offers comfortable rooms and luxurious suites, all with free Wi-Fi, a Nespresso coffee machine and a marble and mosaic bathroom. A sweet and savory breakfast buffet is available daily, including scrambled eggs. The property is set along Via del Babuino, a charming Roman street full of boutiques, jewelers and antique dealers.

Amenities
