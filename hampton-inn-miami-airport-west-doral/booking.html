About Hampton Inn By Hilton Miami-Airport West

This Doral, Florida hotel has free shuttle services to Miami International Airport. The hotel features an outdoor pool and guest rooms include free Wi-Fi and cable TV with pay-per-view movies. Every room at the Hampton Inn Miami Airport West provides a coffee machine. Other amenities available in the rooms are a hairdryer, ironing facilities and a work desk. Miami-Airport West Hampton Inn includes a fitness center and business center. Guests can use the on-site laundromat and barbecue facilities. Miami Beach is 14 miles from the Hampton Inn Doral. The hotel is nearby water skiing, parasailing, boating and many other activities.
