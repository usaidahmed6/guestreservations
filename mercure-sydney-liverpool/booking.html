About Mercure Sydney Liverpool Hotel

This 4-star property offers rooms with modern décor, including LCD TV, cable TV and a work space with internet access. Guests can choose from 3 on-site dining options. Mercure Sydney Liverpool provides free parking. Guests can enjoy the outdoor swimming pool, al fresco dining areas, a fully equipped professional fitness center and an ice rink. Located next to the Liverpool Catholic Club, Mercure Hotel Liverpool is located 5 minutes’ drive from Liverpool town center and 30 minutes’ drive from Bankstown Airport. Each room has a luxurious bathroom. Air conditioning is provided, along with a media hub for USB, AV or laptop input.
