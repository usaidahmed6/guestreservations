About Sutton Place Hotel Ueno

Located in Tokyo, a 4-minute walk from Ryukoku-ji Temple, Sutton Place Hotel Ueno features views of the city. With free WiFi, this 3-star hotel offers a shared kitchen and a shared lounge. The property is a 6-minute walk from Front gate of Honobo In Kanei-ji Temple, and within 3.8 miles of the city center. The hotel will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms offering a desk, an electric tea pot, a fridge, a microwave, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a bidet. At Sutton Place Hotel Ueno rooms include bed linen and towels. Popular points of interest near the accommodation include Kissho-in Temple, Gate of Former Residence of Koda Rohan and The Shitaya Shrine. The nearest airport is Tokyo Haneda Airport, 14 miles from Sutton Place Hotel Ueno.
