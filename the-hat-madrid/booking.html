About The Hat Madrid

Discover Vibrant Urban Living at The Hat Madrid

Steps away from the iconic Plaza Mayor Square, The Hat Madrid redefines the hostel experience with its stylish design, air conditioning, complimentary Wi-Fi, and a rooftop bar that boasts breathtaking city views and stunning sunsets. This unique hostel, situated in the heart of Madrid, offers a modern and comfortable stay for travelers of all types, from solo adventurers to families and groups of friends.

  • Variety of accommodation options including private rooms, mixed and female dormitories, catering to the needs of every traveler.
  • Convenient location with La Latina District's medieval charm and vibrant nightlife just a short walk away, alongside easy access to Puerta del Sol and La Latina Metro Stations.
  • A rooftop bar where guests can enjoy panoramic views of Madrid and witness the city's beautiful sunsets.
  • On-site restaurant and proximity to a diverse array of cafés and bars within a 5-minute walk, offering guests a taste of Madrid's culinary delights.
  • Comprehensive guest services including a 24-hour front desk, free activities, a nightlife guide, and bicycle hire to explore the city.

Each bright room at The Hat Madrid, whether private or shared, is designed with comfort in mind, with select rooms featuring a charming small balcony. Located directly opposite a supermarket and within easy reach of Madrid's most beloved sights and metro stations, guests at The Hat Madrid enjoy both convenience and culture right at their doorstep.

Embrace the spirit of Madrid with a stay at The Hat Madrid, where modern amenities meet the historical allure of the city. Whether you're exploring Madrid's rich heritage or enjoying its contemporary culture, The Hat Madrid offers a welcoming and dynamic base for your adventures.
