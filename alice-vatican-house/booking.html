About Alice Vatican House

Located 1476 feet from Saint Peters Square, Alice Vatican House offers contemporary rooms with free WiFi and city views. Ottaviano Metro Station is a 5-minute walk away. Each room comes with an electric kettle and selection of tea, coffee, and biscuits. They also include air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, and private bathroom with free toiletries. Tourist maps are provided on site. The Vatican Museums are 8 minutes walk from the B&B, and you can reach the Spanish Steps in 10 minutes from Ottaviano Metro Station.
