About Mary MacKillop Place

Experience the epitome of convenience at Mary MacKillop Place. Ideally located in the heart of North Sydney, a short train journey from Sydney’s city center. Indulge in our thoughtfully designed, nonsmoking, large comfortable rooms, complete with modern conveniences such as flat-screen TVs, private bathrooms, complimentary Wi-Fi, and in-room tea and coffee facilities. Whether basking in the peaceful ambiance of our garden or unwinding in our café with a coffee and a book, relaxation awaits you at every turn. Should you be traveling for business or pleasure, Mary MacKillop Place offers a haven of tranquility amid the city bustle. Your stay includes a continental buffet breakfast, including muesli, yogurt, toast, cereal, pancakes, fresh fruit and more. We cater to suit a range of dietary preferences. Venture out to explore Sydney's iconic landmarks, by train, bus, or ferry. Luna Park, Circular Quay, The Royal Botanic Gardens, and Sydney Opera House are just moments away. Discover the allure of Sydney from Mary MacKillop Place. Secure your reservation today and embark on an unforgettable urban retreat.
