About River Palace Hotel

Located just a few steps from Villa Borghese Park, River Palace Hotel offers elegant interiors and antique furnishings. Internet is free and rooms are equipped with air conditioning and satellite TV. The large buffet breakfast is served daily and includes homemade cake and pastries, fresh fruit, meats and cheeses. The lounge bar serves cocktails and 22 different types of whiskey. The River Palace is just a 5 minute walk from the famous Piazza del Popolo, and from Flaminio Metro Station. From here, there is direct access to the Spanish Steps and Vatican City.
