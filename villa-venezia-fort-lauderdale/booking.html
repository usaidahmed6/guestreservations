About Villa Venezia

Located in Fort Lauderdale, 1.6 miles from Fort Lauderdale Beach, Villa Venezia has accommodations with an outdoor swimming pool, free private parking, a garden and a terrace. This 3-star hotel offers room service, a concierge service and free WiFi. The hotel has family rooms. The hotel will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms offering a closet, a coffee machine, a fridge, a microwave, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a bath. Some rooms have a kitchen with a dishwasher, an oven and a stovetop. At Villa Venezia rooms are equipped with bed linen and towels. Las Olas Boulevard is 1.2 miles from the accommodation, while City of Fort Lauderdale Las Olas Marina is 1.4 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport, 7.5 miles from Villa Venezia.
