About Holiday Inn Express Hotel & Suites Miami

This hotel in Miami, Oklahoma offers a hot breakfast and wireless internet access throughout the property/ The Vintage Iron Motorcycle Museum is 5 minutes’ drive from the hotel. Rooms at the Holiday Inn Express Hotel & Suites Miami feature a work desk and tea and coffee-making facilities. Also featured is cable TV with in-room movies. A fitness center, a heated indoor pool and a whirlpool are all on-site at the Miami Holiday Inn Express. Other amenities include a business center and guest launderette. Historic Route 66 and the Coleman Theater are within 10 minutes of the hotel. Buffalo Run Casino is 2 miles away.
