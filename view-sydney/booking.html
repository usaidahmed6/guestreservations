About View Sydney

Overlooking beautiful Sydney Harbor and the citys iconic Sydney Harbor Bridge, View Sydney features a restaurant and bar. Many rooms feature Sydney Harbor Bridge. The air-conditioned rooms at View Sydney include a flat-screen TV, work desk and tea/coffee making facilities. Some rooms feature a bath in the private bathroom. The property also offers a pet friendly option should you book their Harbor View Queen Room for extra charge. LBs Restaurant serves bistro-style dishes for lunch and dinner and offers a selection of Australian wines. Luna Park and Milsons Point ferry stop is a 15-minute walk from the hotel. View Sydney is just 164 feet from North Sydney train station, providing easy access to Sydneys key attractions, including Sydney Opera House and the Botanical Gardens.
