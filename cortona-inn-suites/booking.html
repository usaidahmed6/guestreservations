About Cortona Inn And Suites Anaheim Resort

Disneyland is 1.5-miles from this Anaheim resort. It features an outdoor heated pool with hot tub and warm-colored rooms with free WiFi. Each traditional room at the Cortona Inn & Suites Anaheim Resort is furnished with a microwave, a refrigerator and coffee facilities. Cable TV and a work desk are included. Guests can find a gift and snack shop at the resort along with a business center. Tickets for the ART shuttle service to the Disneyland Resort are available for purchase. Knott’s Berry Farm, an adventure park, is 9-miles away. The resort is 2-miles from the Angel Stadium, home to the Los Angeles Angels of Anaheim baseball team. Property offers breakfast and Wi-Fi
