About Daiwa Roynet Hotel Shimbashi

Ideally located in the Minato Ward district of Tokyo, Daiwa Roynet Hotel Shimbashi is located a 3-minute walk from TKP Shinbashi Conference Center, 500 yards from Sakurada Park and a 6-minute walk from Shimbashi Station. With free WiFi, this 3-star hotel offers a 24-hour front desk and luggage storage space. The property is 1.3 miles from the city center and a 3-minute walk from Karasumori Shrine. Popular points of interest near the hotel include Space FS Shiodome, Advertising Museum and Japan Sake and Shochu Information Center. The nearest airport is Tokyo Haneda Airport, 10 miles from Daiwa Roynet Hotel Shimbashi.
