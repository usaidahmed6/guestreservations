About Hotel Sol Levante

Just a 3-minute walk from Roma Termini Station, Hotel Sol Levante offers classic accommodations in the center of Rome. The property is 1312 feet from Castro Pretorio Metro Station that takes you to the Colosseum. Rooms here feature a TV. The private bathroom includes a shower and free toiletries. WiFi is free in the lobby. An Italian breakfast with cappuccino and croissants is served daily in the rooms. Hotel Sol Levante is 0.6 mi from Termini Metro station that takes you to the Spanish Steps, Palazzo Barberini and St. Peters Square.
