About COVE Cannon Street

COVE Cannon Street is set in London, 0.7 mi from Tower of London. Tower Bridge is 0.9 mi from the property. All units include a dining area and a flat-screen TV. There is also a kitchen, fitted with an oven. A microwave and kettle are also offered. Every unit features a private bathroom with free toiletries and a hair dryer. Towels are provided. Somerset House is 1.1 mi from COVE Cannon Street, while Lyceum Theater is 1.1 mi away. The nearest airport is London City Airport, 6.2 mi from COVE Cannon Street.
