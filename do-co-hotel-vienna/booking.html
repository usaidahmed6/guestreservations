About Do&Co Hotel Vienna

Directly opposite Saint Stephens Cathedral in the heart of Vienna, this elegant 5-star hotel in the unique Haas-Haus building includes the japanese ONYX restaurant and the DO&CO gourmet restaurant on the top floor. Free WiFi and wired internet access are available. The very spacious and stylish rooms at DO&CO Hotel Vienna feature modern furnishings, wooden floors, and air conditioning. Every room is equipped with a seating area, a large mini-bar with an exclusive selection of wines and spirits, and a large B&O flat-screen TV with a DVD player. The elegant bathrooms feature exclusive ETRO toiletries and a rainforest shower. British and American power plugs are also available. Rooms offer views of the cathedral, the streets, or the courtyard. an extensive room service menu is also available around the clock. The Stephansplatz Underground Station (lines U1 and U3) is right outside the hotel. Guests enjoy free admission to an exclusive fitness center a 10-minute walk away. The Hofburg Congress and Event Center is a 10-minute walk away.
