About Torino Hotel

Boasting a terrace overlooking Romes roof tops, Hotel Torino is only 656 feet from Termini Train Station and Repubblica Metro . Free WiFi is available throughout. Housed in a restored building in Umbertine style, rooms at the Torino are classically furnished. Each offers a flat-screen TV, air conditioning and a safety deposit box, while the private bathroom comes with a hairdryer. The daily buffet breakfast is served on the outdoor terrace and includes ham, eggs and cereal, together with pastries and cakes. Many traditional restaurants and cafs are scattered around the neighboring Monti area. Piazza Venezia and the Coliseum are a gentle downhill stroll from the hotel. The staff can suggest walking routes or organize tours, and a shuttle is available from/to Rome Fiumicino Airport, a 40-minute drive away.
