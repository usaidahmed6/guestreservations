About Mercure Roma Corso Trieste

Located in a modern residential area 5 minutes' walk from Sant'Agnese Annibaliano Metro, Hotel Mercure Roma Corso Trieste offers air-conditioned rooms with design furnishings and free WiFi throughout. All soundproofed and with parquet floors, rooms at the Roma Corso Trieste include modern Dorelan mattresses and beds, a 49-inch smart TV, a mini-bar with 1 free small bottle of water, and a tea/coffee maker. The private bathroom has free toiletries and a hairdryer. Some rooms feature a private balcony or terrace and some come with a spa bath. A sweet and savory breakfast buffet is available daily. The hotel is in a lively area, with plenty of restaurants serving local cuisine and Italian classics. Staff is happy to offer their recommendations. On-site facilities include a lobby bar offering all-day refreshments and local specialties, and garage parking. The Colosseum and Termini Central Metro and Train Station are respectively 6 and 4 metro stops from the property. Hosting many summer events, Villa Ada Park is only 0.6 mi away.
