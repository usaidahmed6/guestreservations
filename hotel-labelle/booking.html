About Hotel Labelle

Hotel Labelle is in the historical center of Rome, a 5-minute walk from the Coliseum. This hotel is set right in the trendy Monti district. Labelle Hotel is on the 3rd floor of a historic building, and offers spacious rooms with air-conditioning. The breakfast will be served in a nearby bar. The Labelle is 1640 feet from Colosseo Metro Station on line B. Public buses run on Via Cavour and on nearby Via Dei Fori Imperiali.

Amenities

  • Room Service
  • 24 hour front desk
  • Concierge
  • Babysitting Services
  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • Non-Smoking Facility
