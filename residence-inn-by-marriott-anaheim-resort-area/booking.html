About Residence Inn by Marriott Anaheim Resort Area/Garden Grove

Only 4 blocks from Disneyland, this hotel offers an outdoor pool and a palm tree-rimmed courtyard. Free daily breakfast is provided in a full American buffet style. There is also an on-site bar. Offering free WiFi and fully equipped kitchens, all rooms at the Residence Inn Garden Grove come with 27 TVs featuring HBO and Disney Channel. Each has a work desk and a coffee maker. Guests can enjoy the fitness center on-site. An outdoor wading pool is also available for children. Tickets for Disneyland are also sold at the hotel. Residence Inn Anaheim Resort Area/Garden Grove is located 4 miles from the River View Golf. The Knott's Soak City USA Water Park is 7.9 mi away.
