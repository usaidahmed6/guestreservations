About Ramada by Wyndham Washington

Perched atop a scenic hillside at the junction of I-70 and I-79, our Ramada by Wyndham Washington Hotel is a sanctuary from stress, away from highway noise and traffic. Stream your favorite shows using FREE WiFi and easily get around thanks to free parking with space for large vehicles. Each room in our pet-friendly hotel (fee applies) comes with a Flat Screen TV, Mini Refrigerator, Microwave, Coffee/Tea maker, Hairdryer & more. Both Smoking & Nonsmoking rooms are available. We offer Banquet/Meeting space and Guest Valet services for your convenience. Nearby attractions include Washington Wild Things Baseball at Wild Things Park while those who like to try their luck on Slot Machines, Table Games, Standardbred Harness Racing, and more will have a blast at Hollywood Casino at the Meadows. You can also browse the stores at Tanger® Outlets, attend a concert at the Pavilion at Star Lake or take a step back in time at the Pennsylvania Trolley Museum & take a ride on authentic restored Trolley Cars. Pittsburgh is just 30 miles away. Whether you're our guest for just one night or an extended stay, we offer comfortable accommodations at a great price.
