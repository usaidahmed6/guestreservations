About High Country Inn

Offering an indoor pool, a dry cedar sauna and a hot tub, High Country Inn is located in Banff. Free W-Fi and a mountain view are enjoyed from all rooms. Mount Norquay is 13 minutes drive away. A flat-screen cable T, patio, and a seating area are offered in each air-conditioned room. A fridge and tea and coffee-making facilities are included. Featuring a shower, private bathrooms also come with a hairdryer. At High Country Inn you will find a restaurant. Other facilities offered include meeting facilities and luggage storage. An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including skiing. The property offers free parking. Whyte Museum of the Canadian Rockies is just 10 minutes walk away. - Not all rooms have patios or balconies. - Free parking is subject to availability.
