About Peacock Suites

Conveniently located off Interstate 5, this all-suite Anaheim hotel features an outdoor pool and 2 hot tubs. All air-conditioned suites offer free WiFi and. Shuttle service to Disneyland is also available. A satellite TV, an iPod docking station, a CD and DVD player, a dining table and a hairdryer are included in all of the comfortable suites at Peacock Suites. Suites also offer kitchenettes with a microwave, a fridge and tea and coffee-making facilities. Peacock Suites Anaheim offers a reception desk where guests can get information and pickup tickets for local attractions. For convenience a gift shop, a guest launderette and a business center with fax and photocopying services are also located on-site. Anaheim Convention Center is 1 mile from the hotel. Knott’s Berry Farm is 12 minutes’ drive away. Long Beach Airport is 20 miles away.
