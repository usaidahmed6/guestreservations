About The Millennials Shibuya / Vacation STAY 80776

Located in the center of Tokyo, a few steps from Ancient Egyptian Art Museum, The Millennials Shibuya / Vacation STAY 80776 has air-conditioned rooms and free WiFi. Popular points of interest nearby include Shibuya Center Town, Hachiko Statue and Moyai Statue. The property is close to popular attractions like Shibuya Station, Shikisai Museum and Shibuya Mark City. At the hotel, rooms come with a desk. At The Millennials Shibuya / Vacation STAY 80776 the rooms are equipped with a shared bathroom with free toiletries and a hairdryer. Popular points of interest near the accommodation include Shidax Culture Hall, Shibuya Scramble Crossing and Shibuya Center-gai Shopping Street. The nearest airport is Tokyo Haneda Airport, 12 miles from The Millennials Shibuya / Vacation STAY 80776.
