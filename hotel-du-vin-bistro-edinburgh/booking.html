About Hotel Du Vin Edinburgh

Step Into the Unique Charm of Hotel Du Vin Edinburgh

Nestled in the heart of Edinburghâ€™s historic Old Town, Hotel Du Vin Edinburgh stands out with its rich heritage and unique character. Housed in a beautifully restored former city asylum, this hotel offers a distinctive escape into a world where modern luxury meets historical elegance. Each bedroom in the hotel is uniquely styled, featuring high-quality Egyptian linens, plasma TVs, and luxurious private bathrooms that often include signature roll top baths and retain original architectural features.

The centerpiece of Hotel Du Vin Edinburgh is the French Bistro Du Vin, where guests can savor exquisite dishes prepared with the finest produce sourced from the Lothian region. The dining experience is enhanced by the bistroâ€™s relaxed, French-inspired atmosphere, making every meal a memorable event.

For relaxation and socializing, the hotel boasts a chic mezzanine bar and the Laroche tasting room, where connoisseurs and casual drinkers alike can explore an impressive selection of fine wines from across the globe. Additionally, the walk-in humidor offers an extensive collection of luxury cigars, providing a sophisticated touch to the end of an evening.

  • Unique bedrooms with historical elegance and modern comforts
  • French Bistro Du Vin showcasing local Scottish produce
  • Mezzanine bar and Laroche wine tasting room for exquisite evenings
  • Extensive luxury cigar collection in the walk-in humidor
  • Prime location within walking distance to Edinburgh Castle and Princes Street

Hotel Du Vin Edinburgh invites guests to experience a stay that blends Scottish heritage with the quintessential French bistro spirit, all within the iconic and historic ambiance of Edinburghâ€™s Old Town.
