About Crowne Plaza Coogee Beach

Crowne Plaza Sydney Coogee Beach offers an iconic beachside retreat in Sydney. Boasting panoramic sea views, the hotel has been refurbished to reflect its coastal surroundings and is just steps from Coogee Bay. It features an outdoor pool, on-site fitness center and a choice of restaurants and bars. Free WiFi is offered throughout the hotel. The coastal suburb of Coogee offers clear blue waters at Coogee Beach, a variety of cafes, indigenous roots and sea baths, making it an ideal location for a seaside trip. You can start your day with a stroll along the Bondi to Coogee Coastal Walk. Or, if you're feeling active, enjoy stand up paddle boarding, snorkeling or swimming at Wylie's Bath which is located just 1969 feet from Crowne Plaza Sydney Coogee Beach. The hotel is designed with blue hues to compliment the subtle color palette that has been featured throughout the accommodations. The light-filled guest rooms feature a mini-bar, tea/coffee making facilities and a work desk. Modern bathrooms and high quality bed linen are also included in the contemporary rooms. Some rooms include a private balcony and floor to ceiling windows to showcase the panoramic ocean views. The hotel features restaurants and bars within the hotel precinct. Just before sunset, you can visit Shutters to witness stunning views of Coogee Beach. At Shutters, a fusion of French-Italian cuisine is offered, with locally-sourced ingredients and traditional preparation methods. The concierge team at Crowne Plaza Sydney Coogee Beach can help you plan your stay in Sydney. Popular landmarks and the city are all within easy reach from the hotel. Westfield Bondi Junction shopping center is a 10-minute drive away and the Sydney Opera House is a 20-minute drive from the property. The nearest airport is Sydney Airport, 6.8 mi from Crowne Plaza Sydney Coogee Beach.
