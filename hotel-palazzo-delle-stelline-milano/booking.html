About Hotel Palazzo Delle Stelline

Palazzo Delle Stelline is set in a former convent in the historic center, beside Santa Maria delle Grazie Church with Leonardo's famous fresco. Rooms overlook the peaceful inner courtyard. Hotel Palazzo Delle Stelline features a cloister of the 15th century and a large garden. The bar is open until 19:00. Wi-Fi is free throughout. The TVs in the rooms have Mediaset Premium channels. Breakfast includes fresh fruit. Palazzo Delle Stelline Hotel is 1640 feet from Milano Cadorna Train Station, with direct links to Rho Fiera Milano and Expo 2015 Exhibition Centers. Milan's Duomo Cathedral is a 15-minute walk or a short tram ride away.
