About Grand Hotel De La Minerve

Set in a 17th-century building 2-minutes' walk from the Pantheon, Grand Hotel De La Minerve features a rooftop terrace with city views, air-conditioned rooms, and free WiFi throughout. Piazza Navona is 1476 feet away. Rooms at De La Minerve feature elegant interiors, a satellite flat-screen TV, and a mini-bar. The private bathroom includes luxury toiletries. Breakfast is an extensive sweet and savory buffet including scrambled eggs and bacon. The Minerva Roof Garden restaurant is open throughout the year, serving both lunch and dinner. It offers a fine mix of Italian, Mediterranean, and international cuisine. The Spanish Steps can be reached on foot in 15 minutes. The concierge can assist guests and organize transfers and guided tours of the city.
