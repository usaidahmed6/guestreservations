About B&B Villa sensorielle

Villa sensorielle is a Bed & Breakfast set in Nice, 4 km from the city centre. The property is situated 5 km from MAMAC, 5 km from Allianz Riviera Stadium and 3.8 km from Russian Orthodox Cathedral. The property is located 5 km from Massena Square. At the bed and breakfast, the rooms include a private hot tub, a terrace and a private bathroom with free toiletries and wardrobe. They also provide sea views and a coffee machine. One room features a private heated indoor pool and a sauna. Massage treatments can be arranged and gastronomic cuisine can be delivered to your room if you reserve 72 hours in advance. Albert 1st Gardens is 5 km from Villa sensorielle, while Promenade du Paillon is 5 km away. The nearest airport is Cte d'Azur Airport, 9 km from the property.
