About Chateau Monfort - Relais & Chateaux

Step into a Fairy Tale at Chateau Monfort - Relais & Chateaux, Milan

Immerse yourself in the enchanting atmosphere of Chateau Monfort - Relais & Chateaux, a 5-star boutique hotel nestled in the historic heart of Milan. Just an 8-minute walk from Piazza San Babila and the prestigious fashion district of Via Montenapoleone, this hotel offers a luxurious escape with a unique twist on classical elegance.

Each room at Chateau Monfort is inspired by different fairy tales and nursery rhymes, creating a whimsical yet sophisticated setting. The rooms feature elegant parquet floors, modern LCD TVs, and complimentary Wi-Fi, perfectly blending antique charm with contemporary amenities. The themed decor provides an unforgettable backdrop for your stay in Milan.

Dining at Chateau Monfort is a culinary adventure. The Rubacuori restaurant offers classic Italian cuisine, while La Cella Di Bacco wine bar boasts an extensive wine list. For those looking to unwind, the Mezzanotte Lounge Bar remains open until late, providing a chic space to relax with a drink.

  • Fairy-tale themed rooms for a unique, glamorous experience
  • Prime location near Milan’s fashion district and Piazza San Babila
  • Restaurant Rubacuori and La Cella Di Bacco wine bar for exquisite dining and wine selection
  • Luxurious breakfast buffet featuring cakes, fresh fruit, and traditional Italian fare
  • Close proximity to Linate Airport and Porta Venezia Station

Chateau Monfort - Relais & Chateaux invites you to a world of fantasy and luxury, set within the vibrant backdrop of Milan. Whether you are visiting for fashion, culture, or simply relaxation, the hotel promises a memorable and magical stay.
