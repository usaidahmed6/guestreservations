About Signor Suite Colosseo

Just 656 feet from Cavour Metro Station and a 10-minute walk from the Coliseum, Signor Suite Colosseo is located in central Rome. It offers modern rooms and apartments with free Wi-Fi. All accommodations is uniquely decorated and features a satellite flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries. Apartments are air conditioned and come with a living area with kitchenette. Guests of Signor Suite can enjoy a sweet and savory breakfast daily. The property is surrounded by a variety of traditional restaurants and cafés. The property is set in the popular Monti area, full of artisan shops, bars and restaurants. Piazza Venezia square and the Vittoriano monument, as well as Termini Train Station can be reached in 15 minutes on foot.
