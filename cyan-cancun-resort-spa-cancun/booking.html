About Cyan Cancun Resort & Spa

This property is 1 minute walk from the beach. Set in Cancn, 2297 feet from Playa Punta Nizuc, Cyan Cancun Resort & Spa offers accommodations with an outdoor swimming pool, free private parking, a bar and a private beach area. The property is located 0.7 mi from Playa Delfines, 1.7 mi from San Miguelito Beach and 4.3 mi from La Isla Shopping Mall. The hotel provides a terrace, a 24-hour front desk, and free WiFi is available throughout the property. The hotel will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms with a desk, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a shower. The units feature a wardrobe. Cyan Cancun Resort & Spa offers a hot tub. Coco Bongo is 6.2 mi from the accommodations, while Beto Avila Stadium is 8.1 mi away. The nearest airport is Cancn International Airport, 6.2 mi from Cyan Cancun Resort & Spa.
