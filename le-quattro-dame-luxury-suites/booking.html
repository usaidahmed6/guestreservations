About Le Quattro Dame Luxury Suites

Featuring free WiFi throughout the property, Le Quattro Dame is centrally set in Rome, 656 feet from Palazzo Venezia. The rooms feature an elegant and contemporary décor and are equipped with a flat-screen TV and a kitchenette with Nespresso coffee machine. All rooms are fitted with a private bathroom with a bath or shower and bidet, with bathrobes provided. Extras include slippers, free toiletries and a hairdryer. There is a shared lounge at the property. Torre Argentina is 656 feet from Le Quattro Dame, while Piazza Venezia is 984 feet away. Rome Ciampino Airport is 8.7 mi from the property.

Amenities

  • Concierge
