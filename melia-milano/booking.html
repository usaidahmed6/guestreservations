Experience Comfort and Convenience at Melia Milano

Located just a 2-minute walk from Lotto Metro Stop, Melia Milano offers spacious accommodations with modern amenities and stylish dÃ©cor. Ideal for both business and leisure travelers, the hotel is within walking distance of the CityLife Shopping District and MiCo Milano Congressi exhibition center.

  • Free 24-hour gym for fitness enthusiasts
  • Mami restaurant serving a delicious buffet breakfast
  • Elyxr bar offering signature cocktails and finger food
  • Proximity to Milan's major attractions including the Cathedral and San Siro Stadium
  • Spacious rooms with flat-screen TV, mini-bar, and safe

Each air-conditioned room at Melia Milano features luxurious bed linens and elegant furniture, providing a comfortable and stylish stay. Guests can enjoy modern amenities such as a flat-screen TV with satellite and pay-per-view channels, a mini-bar, and a safe.

The Mami restaurant serves a delightful buffet breakfast each morning, while the Elyxr bar is perfect for relaxing with signature cocktails and finger food. Guests can also take advantage of the free 24-hour gym, ensuring they can maintain their fitness routine during their stay.

Melia Milano's convenient location makes it easy to explore the city's top attractions. The hotel is just 0.4 miles from MiCo Milano Congressi exhibition center, 8 metro stops from both Milan Cathedral and Rho Fiera Milano, and less than 1.2 miles from San Siro Football Stadium.

Book your stay at Melia Milano and enjoy a blend of comfort, convenience, and modern amenities in the heart of Milan.
