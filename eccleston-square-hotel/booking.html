About Eccleston Square Hotel

Offering beautiful interior design and state-of-the-art facilities throughout, the grade II listed Eccleston Square Hotel is also ideally located in central London, bordering Belgravia and Victoria. Hstens beds, known as the most comfortable beds in the world, are included in every room, as well as curtains, sound and the large 49-inch flat-screen TVs with Chromecast. It will also provide the guest with a full concierge service. Guests can also benefit from a Nespresso machine with complimentary Nespresso capsules and free Evian water. Free phone calls can be made inside of London landlines. The luxury marble bathroom includes a hidden TV in the bathroom mirror, massage showers and a 'smart glass' wall that turns opaque instantly at the touch of a button. This impressive hotel is set in a classic London square in Pimlico, with access to the gated square garden. Victoria Train and Underground Station can be reached in 3 minutes by taxi, or a 10-minute walk. A bar menu is available to guests throughout the day and evening, afternoon tea is offered free of charge from 1pm to 6pm.
