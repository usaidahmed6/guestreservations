About Hm Villas Paraiso Del Mar

Set on the beachfront on Holbox Island, north of the Yucatan Peninsula, this secluded complex offers spacious air-conditioned suites with a TV and minibar. HM Villas Paraiso del Mar features tropical gardens and an outdoor pool with a poolside bar. Guests can sip on a refreshing cocktail or go for a swim at the nearby beach. Guests can relax with a massage, available upon request. The restaurant at the HM Villas Paraiso serves fresh seafood as well as delicious Mexican cuisine. It is open for breakfast, lunch and dinner. After dinner, guests can enjoy a typical Mexican or international drink at the lobby bar. Guests at the Paraiso del Mar HM Villas can visit the front desk for bike and kayak hires and information on the island. Located within the Yum Balan Nature Reserve, Holbox is an ideal base for bird watching, snorkeling or swimming with whale sharks.
