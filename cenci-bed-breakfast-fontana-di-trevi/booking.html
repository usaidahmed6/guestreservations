About Cenci Bed & Breakfast Fontana di Trevi

Cenci Bed & Breakfast is just 164 feet from Trevi Fountain and a 5-minute walk from Barberini Metro Station. Its design rooms come with free WiFi and a private bathroom with chromo-therapy. The soundproofed rooms offer flat-screen TV and air conditioning. They feature minimalist décor, earth tones and elegant tiled floors. A mini-bar is also available in each room. An Italian breakfast is available daily at a nearby cafè. Bed & Breakfast Cenci has a great location in Rome's historic center, a 5-minute walk from the Spanish Steps. The friendly owner can recommend restaurants and provide tourist information.
