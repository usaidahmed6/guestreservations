About The Bedford Regency Hotel

Located in the center of Victorias shopping and business district, this hotel is 5 minutes' walk from the Inner Harbor. It offers 2 on-site restaurants. Free WiFi is offered in all rooms. The elegantly decorated rooms at the Bedford Regency Hotel are furnished with work desks and refrigerators. They also include a flat-screen cable TV. The on-site Garrickss Head Pub, open since 1867, serves drinks and pub-style food and is open for lunch and dinner. The Victoria Conference Center is only 2297 feet from the Bedford Regency. The Victoria Golf Club is 3.7 miles away.
