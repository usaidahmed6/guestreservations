About Hotel Aniene

With free Wi-Fi throughout, Hotel Aniene is located in a quiet, green area of Rome, 10 minutes from Termini Train Station by Metro. All rooms come with parquet floors and a 32" LCD TV with satellite channels. A varied continental buffet-style breakfast is served daily in the dedicated room. The multilingual reception staff are always on hand and can provide tourist information and recommendations. The Aniene is a modern-style hotel located in the north of Rome, and takes its name from nearby River Aniene, Rome's second river. The new B1 Metro stop Conca dOro is 328 feet away. Two busses stop right outside the hotel and connect you to the main station and central Piazza Venezia.
