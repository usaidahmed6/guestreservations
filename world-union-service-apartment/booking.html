About Shanghai Cosmo World Union Service Apartment

Located in Jing An district, World Union Service Apartment is a 5-minute walk from Jing An Temple Metro Station. It features free internet and fully furnished apartments with a kitchen. Modern décor and floor-to-ceiling windows feature throughout the spacious apartments at World Union. Each apartment has a separate living area, equipped with a DVD player and TV with cable channels. To relax, guests can rent a bike or car to explore the city. The non-smoking hotel provides currency exchange and ticketing services. The staff at the 24-hour front desk can assist with guests’ requests. Union World Apartment is a 15-minute drive from The Bund and Lujiazui Financial District. Shanghai Hong Qiao Airport is a 30-minute drive from the hotel.
