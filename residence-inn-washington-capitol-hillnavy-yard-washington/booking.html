About Residence Inn by Marriott Washington Capitol Hill/Navy Yard

Featuring free WiFi and a fitness center, Residence Inn Washington Capitol Hill/Navy Yard offers pet-friendly accommodations in Washington, 1 mi from Supreme Court. A flat-screen TV with cable channels, as well as an iPod docking station are available. Some rooms feature a seating area to relax in after a busy day. Every room has a private bathroom fitted with a bath or shower. For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer. You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property. Smithsonian National Air and Space Museum is 1.1 mi from Residence Inn Washington Capitol Hill/Navy Yard, while National Gallery of Art is 1.3 mi from the property. The nearest airport is Ronald Reagan Washington National Airport, 2.5 mi from the property.
