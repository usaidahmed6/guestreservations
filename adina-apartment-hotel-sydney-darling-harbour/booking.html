About Adina Apartment Hotel Sydney, Darling Harbour

Featuring a swimming pool, fitness center and a sauna and overlooking beautiful Cockle Bay, Adina Apartment Hotel Sydney, Darling Harbor is located in Sydney CBD (Central Business District), and offers self-contained accommodations with flat-screen satellite TV. Most rooms offer a private balcony. Located in Sydneys entertainment hub of King Street Wharf, Adina Apartment Hotel is less than 3 minutes walk from Sydney Aquarium and Wildlife World. Chinatown is a 5-minute walk away and Sydneys city center is just 10 minutes' walk away. All air-conditioned studios and apartments have elegant Italian furniture and a kitchenette or kitchen. Some rooms offer beautiful harbor views. Guests receive free WiFi at a speed of 512kb/ps for up to 2 devices per room, per 24 hours. High speed WiFi is available for an additional charge of $9.95 per 24 hours for up to 2 devices. Guests can relax in the garden or enjoy a meal in the barbecue area. The hotel also offers a 24-hour front desk, in-room massage services and dry cleaning facilities. There are several other Darling Harbor cafs, restaurants and bars within a 5-minute walk.
