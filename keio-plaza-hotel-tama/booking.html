About Keio Plaza Hotel Tama

Keio Plaza Hotel Tama is conveniently located a 3-minute walk from Tama Center Station. Guest rooms are decorated with chic interior and come with free wired/wireless internet connection. All rooms feature a flat-screen TV, a fridge and a seating area. The private bathroom comes with a hairdryer and free toiletries. Wi-Fi is available in all guest rooms. Sanrio Puroland, a Hello Kitty-themed amusement park, is a 5-minute walk from Tama Keio Plaza Hotel. Tama Zoo is a 15-minute ride away on the monorail. The front desk is open 24 hours, and free luggage storage service is available. Guests can use a safety deposit box or laundry services at an additional cost. The hotel features 3 restaurants and 1 caf. Jurin restaurant provides a breakfast/lunch buffet, offering a selection of international dishes.
