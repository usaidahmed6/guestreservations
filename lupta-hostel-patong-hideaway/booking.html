About Lupta Hostel Patong Hideaway

Occupying a prime position in Patong, a short walk from the beach and the nightlife, Lupta Hostel Patong Hideaway offers cozy accommodations and free WiFi access. Dormitory-style rooms and private rooms feature air conditioning and an electrical socket. Shower facilities can be found in the shared bathroom. Towels, bed linen and slippers are provided. At Lupta Hostel Patong Hideaway you will find a comfortable shared lounge and kitchen area. Lockers and luggage storage are available. The hostel is 1640 feet from Jungceylon Shopping Center and 0.6 mi from Patong Boxing Stadium. Phuket International Airport is 15 mi away.
