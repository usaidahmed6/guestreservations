About Hotel Xbalamque & Spa Cancun Centro

Located in central Cancun, the Hotel Xbalamqu Resort & Spa features Mayan-style dcor, an outdoor pool, and spa. Every air-conditioned room at the Hotel Xbalamqu Resort & Spa provides cable TV. Hotel Xbalamqu includes an on-site bar "4 Elementos" which offers drinks and snacks with available room service. Tour information and car rentals are also available at the hotel, while safes and WiFi are available at reception. Cancuns ADO Bus Station is just 1640 feet from the Xbalamqu Resort & Spa. The Hotel Zone is a 5-minute drive away, while Cancn International Airport is a 20-minute drive away.
