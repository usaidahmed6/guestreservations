About Maitrise Hotel Maida Vale - London

Maitrise Hotel London Maida Vale is set in a townhouse, located in North Maida Vale, just a 3-minute walk from Kilburn Park Station on the Bakerloo Line. There is a bus stop opposite to the property, offers direct services to Hyde Park, Marble Arch, Oxford Circus, Piccadilly Circus, Wembley Stadium and Arena, and Paddington station. The bedrooms are all nonsmoking and have a TV, tea/coffee-making facilities, a hairdryer, and a bathroom with a shower. Local attractions include Lord’s Cricket Ground, Little Venice area, the famous Abbey Road Studios, The Regents Park, London Zoo and bustling Camden Market. The hotel is close to Wembley Stadium and Wembley Arena, with transport links via the nearby London Overground line or Kilburn Park tube station which is just a 3-minute walk. Guests are welcome to use the 24-hour fitness center next door. Chaiiwala Kilburn offers food from an all-day breakfast menu.
