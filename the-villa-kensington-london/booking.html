About The Villa Kensington

Located in stylish Kensington, just off the famous Cromwell Road, The Villa Kensington is 984 feet from Gloucester Road Underground Station. The hotel offers a 24-hour front desk. The traditional rooms at The Villa Kensington each have a private modern bathroom with a hairdryer. All rooms feature Freeview TV, tea/coffee facilities and Wi-Fi access. Just a 10-minute walk from the Natural History Museum, the hotel is only a mile from Harrods. Hyde Park and Kensington High Street are a 15-minute walk away, and Earls Court is a 10-minute walk.
