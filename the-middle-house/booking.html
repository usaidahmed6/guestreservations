About The Middle House

Located in Shanghai, a 17-minute walk from People's Square, The Middle House provides accommodations with free bikes, private parking, a fitness center and a terrace. Among the facilities at this property are a 24-hour front desk and a concierge service, along with free WiFi throughout the property. The hotel has an indoor pool, sauna and room service. Certain rooms here will provide you with a kitchen with an oven and a minibar. All guest rooms feature a safety deposit box. Guests at the hotel can enjoy a continental or an Asian breakfast. At The Middle House you'll find a restaurant serving Chinese, Italian and Local cuisine. Vegetarian, dairy-free and halal options can also be requested. The area is popular for cycling, and car rental is available at the accommodation. Pedestrian Street of East Nanjing Rd. Is a 15-minute walk from The Middle House, while Jing'an Temple is 1.5 miles away. The nearest airport is Shanghai Hongqiao International Airport, 8.7 miles from the hotel.
