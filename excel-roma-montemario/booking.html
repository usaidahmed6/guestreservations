About Excel Roma Montemario

Live an exclusive experience at the first four-stars sport hotel in Rome Montemario Thanks to the variety of sports activities that can be practiced, LH Hotel LH Hotel Excel Roma Montemario is the favorite destination for training tennis and padel players both in winter, thanks to the presence of covered courts, and in summer, in the outdoor courts where they can experience the pleasure of sport outside. With its complete sport center, the first in the area, LH Hotel LH Hotel Excel Roma Montemario is equipped with a crossfit room, a large fully equipped gym, an outdoor swimming pool open from June to September with umbrellas and sunbeds, tennis and padel courts. Sports guests will benefit from discounted rates for both the seasonal summer pool and open entry to the gyms and all fields, including sports equipment. LH Hotel LH Hotel Excel Roma Montemario is located a few minutes' walk from the Gemelli Polyclinic, 2.5 mi from the Olympic Stadium and the Vatican City and 3.7 mi from the Flaminio Stadium; the historic center of Rome is only 6.2 mi away. Immersed in a centuries-old pine forest, it offers free parking and comfortable air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi and flat-screen TV. The restaurant serves Mediterranean dishes for dinner and an abundant buffet breakfast every morning, which during the summer season can be consumed in the relaxing, pleasantly furnished outdoor garden
