About Hôtel Du Centre

Located next to Jean-Médecin, the main shopping street in Nice, Hôtel Du Center, un hôtel AMMI is just a 5-minute walk from Nice-Ville Train Station, and a 15-minute walk from both the Promenade des Anglais and the beach. The guest rooms feature a flat-screen TV, telephone, refrigerator and wardrobe. All rooms have a private bathroom including a bath or shower, free toiletries and hairdryer. A healthy continental breakfast (served on a tray) is served every morning in our café, the "Hug Café". You can enjoy it inside ,on the nice terrace outside, or in the room. In the café you can take lunch, too. The hotel is located in front of Noter-Dame Basilica and is a 10-minute walk from Place Massena. Paid public parking is available 328 feet away, which as a guest can be enjoyed at a discounted rate.
