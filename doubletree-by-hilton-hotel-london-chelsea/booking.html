About DoubleTree By Hilton London - Chelsea

Welcome to DoubleTree by Hilton London - Chelsea: Your London Home

Step into the heart of London's vibrant landscape with a stay at DoubleTree by Hilton London - Chelsea. Situated in the prestigious Imperial Wharf, near the chic Kings Road, this hotel is a gateway to the best the city has to offer. Following a comprehensive renovation, each bedroom now showcases a blend of comfort and contemporary design, promising guests a restful and stylish retreat.

From the iconic welcome of a chocolate chip cookie at check-in to the convenience of free WiFi throughout the hotel, every aspect of your stay is imbued with the thoughtful hospitality DoubleTree by Hilton is known for. The on-site restaurant and bar cater to a range of tastes, serving a full English breakfast, an international dinner menu, and a selection of coffee and snacks throughout the day.

  • Renovated bedrooms blending comfort with modern style
  • On-site dining options offering full English breakfast and international cuisine
  • Conveniently located opposite Imperial Wharf Train Station
  • Just 15 minutes' walk from Chelsea Football Club
  • Public parking available close to the hotel

DoubleTree by Hilton London - Chelsea not only provides a comfortable and stylish base for exploring London but also offers easy access to the city's transportation network. Opposite Imperial Wharf Train Station, guests can reach Shepherd's Bush and the famed Westfield Shopping Center in just 8 minutes. Whether visiting for business or leisure, guests can expect impeccable service, including dry cleaning, laundry, and luggage storage, ensuring a seamless and hassle-free stay. Discover the charm and convenience of DoubleTree by Hilton London - Chelsea, where every visit is a memorable experience.
