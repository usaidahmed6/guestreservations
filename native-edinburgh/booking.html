About Native Edinburgh

Located in Edinburgh city center, Native Edinburgh offers complimentary WiFi. The accommodations comes with a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with shower while the kitchen features a dishwasher, a microwave and a toaster. A fridge and stovetop are also featured, as well as a Dualit coffee maker with a small supply of pods and Marshall speaker. The property has 24-hour front desk and a laundry room. We offer weekly housekeeping. Popular points of interest near the aparthotel include Edinburgh Castle, Edinburgh Military Tattoo and Royal Mile. Edinburgh Airport is 6.2 mi away.
