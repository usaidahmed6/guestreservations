About A&O Wien Stadthalle

The a&o Wien Stadthalle offers hotel and hostel accommodations only 1 underground stop from the Western Train Station (Westbahnhof). Free WiFi is available in all areas. The Stadthalle event center is a 5-minute walk away. The hotel and hostel rooms are located on different levels. The rooms are bright and modern, while the hostel area mostly consists of multi-bed rooms, suitable for larger groups. a&o Wien Stadthalle offers an optional rich breakfast buffet. Tickets and ideas for walking and sightseeing tours are provided at the reception. Guests can enjoy satellite TV broadcasting various sport channels in the lobby and bar. The Thalistraße Metro Stop is a 3-minute walk away, and the Burggasse-Stadthalle Metro Stop (both line U6) is a 5-minute walk away.
