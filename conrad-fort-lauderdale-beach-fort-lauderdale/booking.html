About Conrad By Hilton Fort Lauderdale Beach

Conrad Fort Lauderdale Beach has free bikes, a fitness center and a sun terrace with swimming pool and American breakfast in Fort Lauderdale. Offering a restaurant, the property also features a garden, as well as a hot tub. Private parking is available on site. All rooms include air conditioning, a desk and a flat-screen TV, and some rooms at the resort have a balcony. All rooms will provide guests with a microwave. Guests at Conrad Fort Lauderdale Beach will be able to enjoy activities in and around Fort Lauderdale, like cycling. Staff at the accommodation are available to give information at the 24-hour front desk. Popular points of interest near Conrad Fort Lauderdale Beach include Fort Lauderdale Beach, Bonnet House Museum and Gardens and City of Fort Lauderdale Las Olas Marina. The nearest airport is Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport, 9.3 miles from the resort.
