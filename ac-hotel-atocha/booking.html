About AC Hotel by Marriott Atocha

This hotel is set in the heart of Madrid, just 656 feet from Puerta de Atocha Station. It offers a fitness room with free mineral water. Free WiFi is available in all areas. Each stylish room at AC Hotel Atocha is decorated with modern furnishings. They feature air conditioning, a mini-bar and a safe. The hotel has an elegant bar and the AC Lounge, serving creative versions of traditional dishes. The AC Hotel Atocha is a 5-minute walk from The Reina Sofia and Botanical Gardens. It is also just 10 minutes’ walk from the Prado Museum.
