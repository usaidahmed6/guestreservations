About Moxy London Excel

Moxy London Excel is located in London, 1.8 mi from O2 Arena and features a shared lounge. This 4-star pet-friendly hotel offers a bar. Free WIFI is available on site. Private parking is available for a fee. All rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Guests can enjoy a complimentary welcome drink on arrival. A continental breakfast is served each morning at the property. A business center and ironing facilities are available at Moxy London Excel. The accommodations can conveniently provide information at the reception to help guests to get around the area. Greenwich is 2.7 mi from Moxy London Excel. London City Airport is 0.6 mi away.
