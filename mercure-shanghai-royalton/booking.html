About Mercure Shanghai Royalton

Featuring free WiFi, Mercure Shanghai Royalton offers accommodations in Shanghai, only a 10-minute walk from Zhongshan Park Subway Station (line 2,3&4) and the Cloud Nine Shopping Mall. It provides modern comfortable guestrooms. A buffet breakfast is served daily. The hotel is only a 10-minute drive from Jing'an Temple, a 15-minute drive from People's Square and a 20-minute drive from Xintiandi. The Hongqiao International Airport and Hongqiao Railway Station are 5 mi away from Mercure Shanghai Royalton while Pudong International Airport is 32 mi away. Fitted with airconditioning, all soundproofing rooms here will offer a flat-screen cable TV, mini-bar and electric kettle. Featuring a shower, private bathroom also provides a hairdryer, slippers and free toiletries. There is a fitness center at this property. Currency exchange and luggage storage service are possible at the 24-hour front desk, while there is also a tour desk offering travel arrangements and ticketing service.
