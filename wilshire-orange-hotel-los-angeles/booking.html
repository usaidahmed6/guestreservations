About Wilshire Orange Hotel

Situated less than 1 mile from the Miracle Mile business district, this Los Angeles hotel offers extended stay rooms and has vending machines with snacks and drinks on site. The University of California Los Angeles is 6 miles away. Each individually decorated room at Wilshire Orange Hotel offers a TV, microwave and refrigerator. Select guest rooms offer private bathrooms. The Grove Shopping Center is one mile from Wilshire Orange Hotel. The Broad Contemporary Art Museum is a 5-minute walk away. Live music venue Largo at the Coronet is 2 miles from this hotel.
