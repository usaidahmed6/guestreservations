About Maison Durante

Maison Durante is located in the center of Nice, Maison Durante offers 4-star accommodations close to Plage Lido and Plage du Ruhl. Featuring a garden, the 4-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom. Private parking is available on site. At the hotel, rooms are equipped with a desk and a flat-screen TV. At Maison Durante every room includes bed linen and towels. The accommodations offers a buffet or continental breakfast. Maison Durante offers a sun terrace. Speaking English, French, Italian and Dutch, staff are willing to help at any time of the day at the reception. Popular points of interest near the hotel include Plage Sporting, Avenue Jean Medecin and Russian Orthodox Cathedral. The nearest airport is Nice Cte d'Azur, 5.6 mi from Maison Durante and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service. Monaco is accessible by train within 20 minutes. There is also a bus stop just 492 feet from the property with a direct line to Nice Cte d'Azur Airport. Nice-Ville Train Station is just 558 feet from the hotel. Promenade des Anglais is a 15-minute walk away.
