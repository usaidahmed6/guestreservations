About PuSu Zen Collection Hotel Xi'an

Ideally located in the center of Xi'an, PuSu Zen Collection Hotel Xi'an has air-conditioned rooms, a garden, free WiFi and a shared lounge. The property is located 1.2 miles from Drum Tower, 1.3 miles from Bell Tower of Xi'an and 3.1 miles from Xi'an City Wall. The property provides room service, a 24-hour front desk and organizing tours for guests. All rooms at the hotel come with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a shower. The rooms come with an electric tea pot, while some rooms also feature a terrace and others also feature city views. The rooms feature a closet. Big Wild Goose Pagoda is 4 miles from PuSu Zen Collection Hotel Xi'an, while Daming Palace is 5.3 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Xi'an Xianyang International, 26 miles from the accommodation, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
