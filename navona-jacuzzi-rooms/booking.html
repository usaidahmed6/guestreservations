About Navona Jacuzzi Rooms

A 2-minute walk from Piazza Navona in the center of Rome, Navona Jacuzzi Rooms provides accommodations with free Wifi and access to a hot tub. The property is close to several well-known attractions a 6-minute walk from Pantheon, 0.6 miles from Palazzo Venezia, and a 13-minute walk from Piazza Venezia. On-site staff can arrange a shuttle service. The guest house will provide guests with air-conditioned units offering a wardrobe, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom with a shower. Some units also have a well-equipped kitchen with a dishwasher, a microwave, and a toaster. At the guest house, all units are fitted with bed linen and towels. There is an on-site snack bar. Popular points of interest near the guest house include Castel Sant'Angelo, Torre Argentina, and Campo de' Fiori. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino, 10 miles from Navona Jacuzzi Rooms, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
