About Winterset

This Fort Lauderdale Winterset Hotel hotel is 6 minutes' walk from the beach and 2 minutes' walk from the intracoastal. Features include 3 outdoor swimming pools, a hot tub, and tropical gardens. A garden or pool view is offered in each spacious apartment or studio. Extras include a flat-screen cable TV and small refrigerator as well as coffee-making facilities. Guests can use the 3 barbecue grills and shuffleboard. Laundry facilities are offered at Winterset Hotel. Bonnet House Museum & Gardens is 1 mi from this motel. Shopping at The Galleria Mall at Fort Lauderdale is just 4 minutes' drive away.
