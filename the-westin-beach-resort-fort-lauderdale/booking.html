About The Westin Fort Lauderdale Beach Resort

Discover Coastal Bliss at The Westin Fort Lauderdale Beach Resort

Located on the scenic shores of Fort Lauderdale, The Westin Fort Lauderdale Beach Resort offers an unparalleled experience with direct beach access via a convenient skywalk. Guests can unwind and enjoy breathtaking views of the Atlantic Ocean from the heated outdoor pool or maintain their fitness regimen at the 24-hour fitness center.

The well-appointed rooms are designed for relaxation and productivity, featuring a 50-inch flat-screen TV and a work station complete with a desk and chair. The expansive 6500-square-foot Heavenly Spa By Westin provides a sanctuary for rejuvenation, offering a variety of massages, body treatments, and skincare services.

  • Heated outdoor pool with ocean views
  • Direct beach access via a skywalk
  • On-site Mexican cuisine at Lona
  • Waves Bar & Grill for snacks and drinks
  • Proximity to Hugh Taylor Birch State Park and The Galleria at Fort Lauderdale

Culinary delights await at Lona, the on-site restaurant serving authentic Mexican cuisine, while Waves Bar & Grill offers a variety of snacks, light meals, and beverages. For coffee enthusiasts, a Starbucks Caf is also conveniently located on the premises.

The resort's prime location allows guests to explore nearby attractions such as Hugh Taylor Birch State Park, just 1.2 miles away. Shopping enthusiasts can visit The Galleria at Fort Lauderdale, a short 7-minute drive from the resort. Additionally, the Jimmy Evert Tennis Center and Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport are within easy reach, at 3.7 miles and 6 miles away respectively. InterMiami Soccer Stadium is located 11 miles from the resort.

The Westin Fort Lauderdale Beach Resort combines coastal charm with modern amenities, providing an ideal setting for both relaxation and adventure.
