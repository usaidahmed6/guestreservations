About Le Suite del Barone

Le Suite del Barone offers accommodations in Rome, 984 feet from Piazza Navona and a 5-minute walk from Campo de' Fiori. Free WiFi is available throughout. Each air-conditioned room has a flat-screen TV with Sky channels and parquet floors. You will find a Nespresso coffee machine and a kettle with a variety of teas in the room. Rooms are equipped with a private bathroom with a shower, free toiletries, slippers and a hairdryer. Some rooms have a balcony, while others have a hot tub. A continental breakfast is available daily and can be enjoyed directly in the guests' room. The Pantheon is 2625 feet from Le Suite del Barone, while Fontana di Trevi is a 20-minute walk away. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino Airport, 9.3 mi from the property.
