About St. Peter Guest House

St. Peter Guest House features stylish, air-conditioned rooms with satellite TV and a designer bathroom. Located just a short walk from Vatican City, the guesthouse has free Wi-Fi. With only 6 rooms, Guest House St. Peter offers a personalized service, with individually decorated rooms, tour and ticket booking service, and an airport shuttle. The guesthouse is just a few steps from Ottaviano Metro Station, which has direct services to Rome Termini Metro Station.

Amenities
