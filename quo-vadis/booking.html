About Quo Vadis

Quo Vadis bed and breakfast offers transfer services and rooms with attached bathroom with free Wi-Fi in Rome's city center. The Vatican City and Museum are less than 1640 feet away. The Quo Vadis features colorful air-conditioned rooms with private bathroom, mini-bar and cable TV. Quo Vadis staff can assist you with personalized tours and transfer services to Rome Fiumicino and Ciampino Airports. The Ottaviano-Musei Vaticani Metro Line A is just 328 feet from the Quo Vadis. Termini Railway Station is a 10-minute metro ride away.
