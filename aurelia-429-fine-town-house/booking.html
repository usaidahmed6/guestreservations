About Aurelia 429 Fine Town House

The Aurelia 429 Fine Town House has modern rooms with LCD TVs and free Wi-Fi access. It is just 500 ft from the Baldo degli Ubaldi Metro Station. The Aurelia 429 Fine Town House is in a quiet residential area of Rome in an elegant building with an internal courtyard. Guest rooms feature private bathrooms, ceiling fans and access to the shared kitchen. Some rooms feature a furnished balcony with table and chairs. When staying at the Fine Town House, you can enjoy a typical Italian breakfast in the shared kitchen. Local buses stop right outside and Vatican City is just 4 metro stops away. The large public park of Villa Doria Pamphili is a 15-minute walk away.
