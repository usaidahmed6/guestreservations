About Hotel Spiess & Spiess

Just a 5-minute metro ride from the Stephansplatz square right in the heart of Vienna, the eco-labeled Hotel Spiess & Spiess is located in a quiet backstreet. It offers elegant and allergy-free rooms and suites and a savory and premium quality breakfast including mainly organic and regional specialties and products. All rooms in this hotel garni are air-conditioned and come with underfloor heating, a flat-screen TV, and a luxurious bathroom. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the building. The hotel also features a sauna and relaxation area that can be used free of charge. The Rochusgasse stop of the U3 underground line can be found just 328 feet from Hotel Spiess & Spiess. The airport trains are just 1 metro stop away and the Erdberg multi-story car park is 3 stops away. Hotel Spiess & Spiess is certified by the European Center for Allergy Research Foundation (ECARF). Self-service tea, coffee, and filtered sparkling and non-sparkling water are available 24/7 free of charge in the lobby.
