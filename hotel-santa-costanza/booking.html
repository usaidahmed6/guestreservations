About Hotel Santa Costanza

Hotel Santa Costanza by OMNIA hotels is located in north-eastern Rome, a 10-minute walk from Bologna Metro Station. It enjoys good bus links with Termini Station and has free Wi-Fi. Rooms are simply decorated and feature either wood or light-colored furniture, artwork and mostly wood floors. Facilities include air conditioning, a flat-screen TV and a complete private bathroom. Staff are available 24 hours a day and can recommend sights in the surrounding and organize city tours. The Santa Costanza is next to a bus stop, providing quick links with Rome Termini Station in 10 minutes.
