About Trocadero

The Trocadero Hotel is located in the heart of Nice, a 2 minute-walk from Nice-Ville Train Station and Gare Thiers Tram Station. Just a 20-minute walk from the beach, it offers air-conditioned accommodations with a private bathroom. Each guest room at Hotel Trocadero is soundproofed and serviced by a elevator. They are also equipped with a TV and a telephone. Breakfast is served daily at the Trocadero. The hotel also provides a 24-hour reception with free Wi-Fi access. Guests of the Trocadero Hotel can visit the Promenade des Anglais and the old town district located a 20-minute walk away. Nice Cote dAzur Airport is just 4.3 mi away and there is a bus stop to the Airport just 492 feet from the property.
