About Hotel Pension Baronesse

The family-run 4-star Hotel Pension Baronesse is located in the Josefstadt district, only 2 tram stops from the Ringstraße boulevard and Vienna University. It offers free WiFi. All rooms at Hotel Pension Baronesse offer cable TV and a bathroom with hairdryer. Most are furnished in a traditional Viennese style with chandeliers and carpets. Some rooms face the courtyard. The rooms are located between the ground floor and the third floor of a residential building and can all be reached by elevator. A 24-hour reception providing sightseeing information and a ticketing service is available as well. The Lange Gasse Tram Stop is only a few steps away, and the Schottentor Underground Station (line U2) is close by. Hotel Pension Baronesse is a member of Private Hotels.
