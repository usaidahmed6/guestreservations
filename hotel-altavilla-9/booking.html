About Hotel Altavilla 9

Conveniently set in the Central Station district of Rome, Hotel Altavilla 9 is located a 10-minute walk from Cavour Metro Station, 600 yards from Repubblica - Teatro dell'Opera Metro Station and a 4-minute walk from Rome Termini Metro Station. Featuring a bar, the 2-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom. The property is a 6-minute walk from Santa Maria Maggiore, and within 0.9 miles of the city center. At the hotel, each room includes a desk. Every room is equipped with a flat-screen TV, and certain units at Hotel Altavilla 9 have a city view. Guest rooms include a closet. Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a buffet breakfast. Speaking Catalan, English, Spanish and French, staff will be happy to provide guests with practical guidance on the area at the reception. Popular points of interest near Hotel Altavilla 9 include Roma Termini, Vittorio Emanuele Metro Station and Domus Aurea. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino Airport, 8.7 miles from the hotel.
