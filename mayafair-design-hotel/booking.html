About Mayafair Design Hotel

MayaFair Design Hotel is located in the heart of of Cancun's Hotel Zone. It is close to many bars, restaurants and attractions and offers contemporary suites with free Wi-Fi. Suites at the MayaFair Design Hotel feature minimalist design, decorated with artwork inspired by key figures of the fashion industry. All suites have satellite TV and air conditioning. MayaFair Design Hotel offers a daily breakfast buffet. Surrounded by entertainment venues and shops, the MayaFair is less than 6.2 mi from Cancun's city center. The helpful staff can provide tourist information to help you experience the best of Cancun.
