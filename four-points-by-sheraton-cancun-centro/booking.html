About Four Points By Sheraton Cancun Centro

Offering an outdoor pool and a restaurant, Four Points Cancun by Sheraton is located in Cancún. Free WiFi access is available. Each room offers air conditioning and a coffee machine. A cable TV is provided in each room at Four Points Cancun. The private bathroom comes with a shower. A work desk is also standard in the rooms. Four Points Cancun by Sheraton Hotel features a 24-hour front desk and a bar. Other facilities offered at the property include meeting facilities. The property has free on-site parking. The hotel is 2.5 mi from Cancun’s ADO Bus Station and 9.3 mi from La Isla Shopping Mall. Cancún International Airport is 15 minutes’ drive away.
