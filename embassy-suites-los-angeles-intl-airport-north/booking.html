About Embassy Suites By Hilton Hotel Los Angeles-International Airport North

Welcome to Embassy Suites By Hilton Hotel Los Angeles-International Airport North

Conveniently located just half a mile from Los Angeles International Airport, Embassy Suites By Hilton Hotel Los Angeles-International Airport North offers spacious suite-style accommodations and a range of amenities for a comfortable stay. Guests can enjoy easy access to nearby attractions such as the Los Angeles Air Force Base, Manhattan Beach Pier, and Hollywood Park.

Each room is equipped with a coffee machine, flat-screen cable TV, mini refrigerator, and microwave. Some rooms also offer stunning city views. Guests can start their day with a complimentary breakfast and enjoy the nightly manager's reception for a relaxing evening. Free WiFi is available throughout the hotel, ensuring guests stay connected during their visit.

  • Complimentary breakfast served daily
  • Nightly manager's reception
  • Convenient location near Los Angeles International Airport
  • Meeting rooms and business center available
  • Close proximity to Staples Center, Manhattan Beach Pier, and Hollywood Park

Embassy Suites By Hilton Hotel Los Angeles-International Airport North provides meeting rooms and a business center, making it an ideal choice for business travelers. Whether visiting for business or leisure, guests will appreciate the convenient location and comfortable accommodations.

Experience the best of Los Angeles with a stay at Embassy Suites By Hilton Hotel Los Angeles-International Airport North. Book your stay today and enjoy the convenience and comfort of this well-appointed hotel.
