About Comfort Inn & Suites Fort Lauderdale West Turnpike

The Comfort Inn & Suites hotel is conveniently located just six miles from Interstate 95 and two miles from the Florida Turnpike. The Fort Lauderdale Executive Airport is minutes away and the Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport is 20 minutes from the hotel. This hotel is in the heart of the corporate district and surrounded by shopping areas, restaurants, entertainment and many attractions including the BankAtlantic Center, Lockhart Stadium and Motorola. Additional area attractions include Isles Casino, Festival Flea Market, Sawgrass Mills mall, Butterfly World, golf courses and local beaches. Full-service amenities and features include: Free wireless high-speed Internet access, Free parking, Free local calls, Free newspaper. Your morning breakfast is full of hot and delicious items, making breakfast at the Comfort Inn & Suites the perfect way to start your day. Enjoy our free hot breakfast featuring eggs, meat, yogurt and fresh fruit, cereal and more, including your choice of hot waffle flavors! Guests of this hotel will also enjoy the fitness center, outdoor heated pool and hot tub. Business travelers will appreciate conveniences like a business center and access to copy and fax services. The meeting room can accommodate most events and business functions. The hotel offers spacious guest rooms that include coffee makers, hair dryers, safes, large work desks, microwaves and refrigerators. Executive suites and hot tub suites are available. Guest laundry facilities are located on the property. About 80 percent of the rooms are designated as nonsmoking.
