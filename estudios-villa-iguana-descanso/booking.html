About Estudios Villa Iguana descanso

Located in Cancún, 1.5 miles from Playa Las Perlas, Estudios Villa Iguana descanso has accommodations with a shared lounge, free private parking, a garden and barbecue facilities. An indoor swimming pool and a car rental service are available for guests. The property provides a 24-hour front desk, a shared kitchen and currency exchange for guests. At the inn, the rooms include a closet. At Estudios Villa Iguana descanso rooms are equipped with bed linen and towels. The accommodation has a sun terrace. Guests at Estudios Villa Iguana descanso will be able to enjoy activities in and around Cancún, like cycling. Popular points of interest near the inn include Cancun Government Palace, Cancun Bus Station and Beto Avila Stadium. The nearest airport is Cancún International, 9 miles from Estudios Villa Iguana descanso, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
