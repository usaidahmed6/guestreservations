About Amatara Wellness Resort - SHA Extra Plus

Amatara Welleisure Resort offers luxurious accommodations, equipped with a private balcony, and wellness offering. True to its name, Amatara, Thailands premium destination spa, provides you everlasting moments and an experience like no other. Located at the majestic Cape Panwa, Amatara offers guests Asias best coastal view in Phuket. Surround yourself with the calming tranquility of Andamans blue seas and endless skies with the 180 degree sea view available only at Amatara. Each of Amataras rooms & villas features the timeless designed spacious space for your utmost comfort with its private balcony facing the sea views. Amatara offers the guests with the spacious and stunningly-designed 22 pavilions and 48 suites that set a new standard in a destination worth a visit. Amatara Welleisure Resort offers all-inclusive wellness retreat programs. These combine accommodations, healthy and nutritional food, along with the carefully designed and personalized wellness and leisure activities.
