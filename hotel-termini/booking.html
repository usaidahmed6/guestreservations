About Hotel Termini

Offering a central location and great transport links, this 2-star hotel lies 328 feet from Roma Termini Train Station. All air-conditioned, rooms include an LCD TV and free wired internet. Hotel Termini is 820 feet from Santa Maria Maggiore Basilica and a 5-minute walk from the Via Nazionale shopping district. The Colosseum and St. Peter's Square are a 15-minute metro ride away. Rooms at the Termini have simple modern furnishings and are located on the 3rd floor of a town house with a elevator. Each room includes a telephone, and a private bathroom with hairdryer and toiletries.
