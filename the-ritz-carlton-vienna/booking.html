About The Ritz-Carlton Vienna

Experience Elegance at The Ritz-Carlton, Vienna

Set in four historic palaces in the heart of Vienna, The Ritz-Carlton, Vienna embodies a perfect blend of historical grandeur and contemporary sophistication. This distinguished location makes it an ideal starting point for exploring Viennas iconic sights, all just a short walk away.

The hotel's rooms and suites exude luxury, each air-conditioned and equipped with modern amenities including a flat-screen TV, mini-bar, Geneva sound system with an iPod docking station, and complimentary WiFi. Marble bathrooms feature a bath, shower, and high-end toiletries, enhancing the comfort of your stay.

For relaxation, The Ritz-Carlton Spa offers the largest indoor pool in central Vienna, complete with underwater music. The spa also provides separate saunas and steam baths, a state-of-the-art fitness room, and spa suites where guests can enjoy exclusive treatments using Susanne Kaufmann products.

  • Indoor pool with underwater music for a unique relaxation experience
  • Luxurious spa with comprehensive wellness facilities
  • Dstrikt Steakhouse Restaurant, offering premium Austrian Dry Aged Beef and a fine selection of wines
  • Atmosphere Rooftop Bar for cocktails with a panoramic view of Vienna
  • Exclusive Club Lounge offering private check-in and personalized concierge services

Whether dining at the Dstrikt Steakhouse for locally sourced steaks or enjoying innovative cocktails at the D-bar, culinary delights abound. During summer, the Atmosphere Rooftop Bar invites guests to sip exquisite cocktails while taking in sweeping views of the city skyline.

Conveniently located near key attractions like St. Stephen's Cathedral and the Vienna State Opera, and just minutes from major transit stops, The Ritz-Carlton, Vienna offers an unmatched experience of luxury and accessibility. Guests can explore the city's rich culture and history with ease and return to the comfort and elegance of a world-class hotel.
