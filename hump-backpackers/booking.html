About Mad Monkey East Side

Mad Monkey East Side is all about location - right in the midst of Kings Cross with bars, restaurants & cafes all around. The Opera House & Harbor Bridge is within walking distance & the hostel is only a short walk from Rushcutters Bay park, which is a great spot to relax & take in the harbor views. The chilled out vibes mixed with party atmosphere means it's the perfect place to meet & socialize with other adventurers. All guests can enjoy: Ultrafast WiFi, Large Security Lockers (BYO padlocks), Tea & Coffee, TV room, Courtyard - ALL INCLUDED IN THE ROOM RATE - No E-Bike batteries are allowed on the premises With our perfect location, right in the heart of Kings Cross & a 2 minute walk to the train station, guests are sure to make the most of this beautiful city. Please Note: Our reception is closed on 25th December. You will not be able to check in or out on that date. Payment for all reservations made between 24th December & 2nd of January will be taken on 1st of December and are NON REFUNDABLE. If credit card is invalid or inefficient funds you have 72 hours to update your details otherwise your booking will be canceled. We do not accept bookings of 8 or more people.
