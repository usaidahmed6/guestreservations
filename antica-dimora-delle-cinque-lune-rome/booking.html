About Antica Dimora Delle Cinque Lune

Antica Dimora Delle Cinque Lune is just around the corner from one of Rome's most beautiful squares, Piazza Navona. This property offers free WiFi throughout and elegant rooms with air conditioning. An Italian breakfast with locally produced cakes and croissants will be served between 8:00 and 10:00. Your room is decorated with luxurious fabrics and wallpaper. It includes a flat-screen TV, mini-bar and private bathroom with hairdryer and free toiletries. You are a 10-minute walk from Castel Sant'Angelo across the Tiber River and 15 minutes' walk from St. Peter's Square. Antica Dimora Delle Cinque Lune is located in a restricted traffic area so be sure to let staff know in advance if you are arriving by car.
