About The Clermont London, Charing Cross

Experience Historic Elegance at The Clermont London, Charing Cross

Located in the heart of London, The Clermont London, Charing Cross combines historical grandeur with contemporary comfort, just steps away from iconic landmarks like Trafalgar Square and Covent Garden. Set in a Grade II-listed building next to Charing Cross train station, this 4-star hotel offers guests an unparalleled gateway to the city’s vibrant cultural and entertainment scenes.

Key features of The Clermont London, Charing Cross:

  • Smart, spacious rooms equipped with free mini-bars, Nespresso coffee makers, and unlimited free WiFi.
  • On-site dining at The Clermont Restaurant and Bar, featuring all-day dining, classic afternoon teas, and expertly crafted cocktails.
  • Prime location adjacent to Charing Cross train station, providing easy access to London's extensive transport network.
  • Short walking distance to Theatreland, The National Gallery, The Mall, and the scenic banks of the River Thames.
  • Stunning views of the bustling Strand from the comfort of the hotel’s restaurant and bar.

The Clermont London, Charing Cross not only offers a refuge for leisure travelers but also serves as a sophisticated haven for business guests, with each room designed to provide a relaxing yet productive stay. Whether you're in town for business or pleasure, The Clermont London, Charing Cross ensures a memorable experience with its blend of historic architecture and modern luxuries. Discover the charm of London at one of its most esteemed addresses, where every stay is infused with a touch of prestige and sophistication.
