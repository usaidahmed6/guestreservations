About Corus Hyde Park Hotel

Set within a 20-minute walk from Royal Albert Hall and boasting a stylish, spacious piano lounge , Corus Hotel Hyde Park feaures modern rooms with free WiFi. The hotel is just a 10-minute walk from Paddington Station, offering a direct train link to Heathrow Airport. The cozy, air-conditioned bedrooms at Corus Hotel have private private bathrooms and satellite TVs with on-demand movies. Rooms also feature work desks, tea/coffee facilities, iron and ironing boards. The conservatory brasserie serves a modern European menu using seasonal produce. Olio Bar offers light snacks and beverages, and overlooks Hyde Park. Just a 15-minute walk from lively Oxford Street and Mayfair, Corus Hotel Hyde Park is 492 feet from Lancaster Gate Underground Station. The famous Museum Mile is a 20-minute walk away. Corus Hyde Park Hotel is located just a short distance from the shops of Oxford Street, Knightsbridge, Notting Hill, Shepherd’s Bush, the Kensington Gardens or the West End. Stansted Airport is 39 miles away, and Gatwick and Luton Airport are both 33 miles away.
