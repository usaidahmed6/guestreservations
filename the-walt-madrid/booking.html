About The Walt Madrid

Located in the heart of Madrid, just 492 feet from the world famous Gran Via, the Walt Madrid offers modern accommodations in Madrid. This hotel features an on-site bar Free WiFi is available. Each room and suite have parquet floors, and they include a flat-screen TV, mini-bar, and a safe. Air conditioning and free WiFi are available. Family Suites also offer a small kitchen. Some rooms include private terraces. The on-site bar serves fresh juices, a variety of teas, Mediterranean wines, and spirits, as well as a variety of tapas. Guests will find a wide range of restaurants, bars, and nightclubs within a 10-minute walk away. Private parking is available next to the property for an additional cost. The Puerta del Sol, Plaza Mayor, the Royal Palace, Opera and the Mercado de San Miguel are within walking distance. The Thyssen-Bornemisza Museum, Reina Sofia and Prado Museum are an easy stroll from The Walt Madrid.
