About NH Milano Touring

NH Collection Milano Touring is 3 minutes' walk from both Turati and Repubblica Metro, 0.6 mi from Milano Centrale Train Station, and a 15-minute walk from both the city center and Via della Spiga shopping street. This design hotel is consisted of of classically decorated or modern rooms. They have parquet or carpeted floors, free WiFi and air conditioning. Some rooms are complete with LCD TV. Milano Touring's Pianoterra restaurant serves a generous breakfast every morning. For lunch and dinner you can enjoy classic Italian dishes, as well as international cuisine. A traditional Milan aperitif is served before dinner. Milan Linate International Airport is 4.7 mi away.
