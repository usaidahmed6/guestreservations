About St Christopher's Vienna

Located in Vienna, St Christopher's Vienna features a terrace, restaurant, bar, and free WiFi throughout the property. The property is around 1.7 miles from Karlskirche, 1.7 miles from Vienna State Opera and 1.9 miles from Musikverein. Albertina Museum is 2 miles away and House of Music is 2 miles from the hostel. At the hostel each room has a private bathroom and bed linen. Buffet and continental breakfast options are available daily at St Christopher's Vienna. Speaking German, English, Spanish and Dutch, staff will be happy to provide guests with practical guidance on the area at the 24-hour front desk. Popular points of interest near the accommodation include Vienna Main Train Station, Belvedere Palace and Museum of Military History. The nearest airport is Vienna International Airport, 11 miles from St Christopher's Vienna.
