About Accent Inn Victoria

Located 8 minutes’ walk from Mayfair Shopping Center, this Victoria hotel offers free Wi-Fi. All rooms feature a coffee machine and a fridge. A cable TV and a work desk are provided in all guest rooms at Accent Inns Victoria. A kitchenette is offered in select rooms for an additional fee. Guests can take advantage of the well-equipped fitness room. A 24-hour reception, a business center and on-site vending machines are also available. Victoria Harbor and Victoria city center are 15 minutes' drive from the hotel.
