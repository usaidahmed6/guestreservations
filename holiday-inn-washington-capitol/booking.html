About Holiday Inn Washington-Capitol

Discover the Heart of D.C. at Holiday Inn Washington-Capitol

Steps away from the Smithsonian National Air and Space Museum and a brief walk to iconic attractions, Holiday Inn Washington-Capitol invites guests to immerse in the rich history and vibrant culture of Washington, D.C. Offering premier amenities and convenient access to the city's most famous landmarks, our hotel is the perfect base for exploring the nation's capital.

  • Prime location one block from the Smithsonian National Air and Space Museum and close to the Capitol Building, the White House, and the Washington Monument.
  • On-site dining options including the Capitol Bistro and a Starbucks outlet for your morning and throughout-the-day refreshments.
  • 24-hour business center and fitness facilities, catering to your schedule and keeping you connected and in shape.
  • Outdoor pool for a refreshing swim after a day of sightseeing or business meetings.
  • Comfortable rooms equipped with refrigerators and coffeemakers, enhancing your stay with convenience.

With its strategic location near the Holocaust Museum, the World War II Memorial, and other prestigious museums and monuments, Holiday Inn Washington-Capitol is your gateway to the wonders of America's capital. Whether you're here for business or leisure, our friendly staff and excellent facilities are ready to make your stay memorable. Dive into the essence of Washington, D.C., with us.
