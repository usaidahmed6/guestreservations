About The Front Hotel and Apartments

Featuring an outdoor pool facing the ocean, The Front hotel and apartments is just across the street from a peaceful spot on Patong Beach. It has a modern sea-facing room and free Wi-Fi. The spacious air-conditioned rooms and apartments feature cement finishes, a flat-screen cable TV and DVD player. Some rooms have a private balcony. They come with daily cleaning and bottled water. Steps from the Patong Beach beachfront, The Front Hotel and apartments is an easy walk from the nearest minimart. It s a 45-minute drive from Phuket International Airport. Guests can enjoy a relaxing poolside massage or explore Phuket with a day trip. The staff at the 24-hour front desk can provide concierge and childcare services. Light meals and refreshing cocktails are served at the pool bar. Room service is also available.
