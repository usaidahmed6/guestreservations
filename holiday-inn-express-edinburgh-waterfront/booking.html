About Holiday Inn Express Edinburgh - Leith Waterfront

Discover Comfort at Holiday Inn Express Edinburgh - Leith Waterfront

Experience the convenience and comfort of staying at the Holiday Inn Express Edinburgh - Leith Waterfront, strategically located next to the Ocean Terminal shopping centre and the iconic Royal Yacht Britannia. This hotel offers a refreshing blend of modern amenities and excellent services, making it an ideal choice for travelers looking to explore the historical and vibrant city of Edinburgh.

Set in the charming old port area of Leith, the hotel is mere steps away from local shops, bars, and restaurants. Enjoy easy access to the heart of Edinburgh, with Princes Street and Edinburgh Waverley Rail Station less than 2.5 miles away. For those flying in or out, Edinburgh Airport is conveniently located just 7.5 miles from the property.

Accommodations include air-conditioned rooms with essential comforts such as hairdryers, wall-mounted TVs with Freeview, work desks, power showers, and tea and coffee making facilities. Each room also features USB charging ports, and ironing facilities, ensuring guests have everything they need for a comfortable stay.

Guests can start the day with an Express breakfast, which includes continental choices and hot items like bacon, scrambled eggs, sausages, and beans. The hotel's dining options cater to all tastes and schedules, from 24-hour pizza service to a variety of snacks and meals such as paninis, soups, and burgers.

  • Adjacent to Ocean Terminal shopping and Royal Yacht Britannia
  • Convenient paid onsite parking
  • Modern lounge area perfect for relaxation or casual meetings
  • All day dining and stylish bar serving a selection of drinks and snacks
  • Efficient public transport links with regular buses to the city center

Whether visiting for business or leisure, Holiday Inn Express Edinburgh - Leith Waterfront ensures a stay filled with comfort and accessibility, placing the best of Edinburgh at your doorstep.
