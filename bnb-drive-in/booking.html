About Bnb Drive In

Set 4.3 mi from Porta Maggiore, Bnb Drive In offers accommodations with a bar. Free WiFi is featured throughout the property. Fitted with a balcony, the units offer air conditioning and feature a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with bidet and free toiletries. Breakfast is available at a partner caf. Bnb Drive In offers a terrace. A car rental service is available at the accommodations. Sapienza University of Rome is 5 mi from Bnb Drive In, while Coliseum is 5.6 mi away. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino, 5.6 mi from the bed and breakfast, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
