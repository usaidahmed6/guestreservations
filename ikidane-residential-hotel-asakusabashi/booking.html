About IKIDANE Residential Hotel Asakusabashi

Located in the center of Tokyo, a few steps from Jinnai Shrine, IKIDANE Residential Hotel Asakusabashi has air-conditioned rooms and free WiFi. Popular points of interest nearby include Kuramae Mizu no Yakata, Kanaami Inari Shrine and Japan Stationery Museum. The property is a 8-minute walk from Asakusa Mitsuke Memorial and 0.4 miles from Shubi no Matsu Monument. At the hotel, the rooms have a desk, a flat-screen TV, a private bathroom, bed linen and towels. Popular points of interest near IKIDANE Residential Hotel Asakusabashi include Choju-in Temple, Ichogaoka Hachiman Shrine and Hulic Hall and Hulic Conference. The nearest airport is Tokyo Haneda Airport, 16 miles from the accommodation.
