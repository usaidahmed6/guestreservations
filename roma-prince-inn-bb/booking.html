About Roma Prince Inn B&B

Roma Prince Inn B&B offers rooms with free WiFi throughout. It is located in the heart of Rome, 2625 feet from Termini Train Station and Metro stop. Each classic-style room comes with a fan. Some have a private bathroom. A characteristic Italian breakfast of hot drinks, croissants and pastries is served at a nearby caf. The Coliseum is 0.6 mi from B&B Roma Prince Inn. The Spanish Steps are a 30-minute walk away.
