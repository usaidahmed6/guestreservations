About The Juniper Hotel & Bistro

Featuring an on-site gourmet restaurant and lounge, this Banff hotel is located 10 minutes drive from Mount Norquay Ski Resort and features panoramic views of the Rocky Mountains. Free WiFi is included. Offering mountain views, each room at The Juniper Hotel & Bistro features a cable TV. Comfortably furnished, all rooms include tea and coffee-making facilities and a refrigerator. Breakfast and dinner is offered at Banff Juniper Hotel & Bistro, which features local produce and a varied menu including braised bison and salmon. An extensive cocktail list is available. Banff Center is 8 minutes drive from this hotel. Banff Ski Club as well as Banff Hot Springs are both within 6.2 mi away.
