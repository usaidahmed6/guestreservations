About Hotel Paba

The Paba is a small hotel set in a period building less than 5 minutes' walk from the Coliseum. All rooms are en suite and come with air conditioning. The rooms overlook the street, and some are complete with a balcony. The style is classic, with wooden furniture and wooden floors. All rooms include a kettle and an LCD TV. Mattress toppers can be requested. Cavour Metro Station on Rome's line B is 1148 feet away.

Amenities

  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • Non-Smoking Facility
