About Quo Vadis Roma 2

Overlooking the Vatican Walls, Quo Vadis Roma 2 offers rooms with free Wi-Fi, tea/coffee maker, and most with a balcony. Ottaviano Metro Station is 328 feet away. The Quo Vadis 2 B&B is family-run. There is a 12-hour front desk with luggage room, and fast check-in and check-out service. All rooms of the Quo Vadis feature air conditioning and a private bathroom with shower. Breakfast is served in the room.

Amenities

  • Room Service
  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • Non-Smoking Facility
