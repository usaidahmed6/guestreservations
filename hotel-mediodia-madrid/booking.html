Welcome to Hotel Mediodia: A Historical Gem in the Heart of Madrid

Located just 325 feet from Madridâ€™s Atocha Station and the renowned Reina Sofia Museum, Hotel Mediodia offers a perfect blend of historical charm and modern convenience. This centrally situated hotel, with its elegant 1914 faÃ§ade, provides an ideal base for exploring Madrid's vibrant culture and attractions. Guests can enjoy air-conditioned rooms equipped with a TV and free Wi-Fi, ensuring a comfortable stay.

  • Historical Architecture: Admire the hotel's original stained glass windows and grand staircase, reflecting its rich history since 1914.
  • 24-Hour Front Desk: Benefit from around-the-clock assistance, making your stay seamless and stress-free.
  • Prime Location: Situated within the famous Art Triangle, guests are just a short walk from the Prado and Thyssen-Bornemisza Museums, as well as El Retiro Park.
  • Continental Breakfast: Start your day with a delightful continental breakfast served in the hotel's dining room.
  • Excellent Transport Links: Proximity to Atocha Station offers easy access to Metro, local trains, and high-speed AVE trains.

Hotel Mediodia stands as a testament to Madrid's historical grandeur while providing modern amenities to meet the needs of contemporary travelers. Whether visiting for business or leisure, guests will find this hotel an exceptional choice for exploring the rich artistic and cultural offerings of Madrid.
