About Ram's Hotel

Ram's Hotel is located in the South of Market (SOMA) neighborhood in San Francisco, 1312 feet from San Francisco City Hall and 0.9 mi from Union Square. This property also offers free WiFi. A flat-screen cable TV is available in all rooms. All rooms offer city views. Moscone Center is 0.9 mi from Ram's Hotel, while Lombard Street is 1.8 mi away. The nearest airport is San Francisco International Airport, 11 mi from the property.

Amenities

  • 24 hour front desk
  • Masks are available to guests
  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • Non-Smoking Facility
