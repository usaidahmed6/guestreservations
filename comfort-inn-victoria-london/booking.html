About Comfort Inn Victoria

Located in the prestigious Royal Borough of Westminster, this renovated Georgian Town House hotel offers modern. Victoria Train and Coach Stations are just 5 minutes away by foot. Every air-conditioned room at the Comfort Inn Victoria provides stylish decor, a TV and private shower rooms. Victoria Comfort Inn serves a hearty breakfast and the front desk is open 24-hours-a-day. Excellently positioned to explore vibrant Central London, the boutique shops of Sloane Square and Belgravia are both within 15 minutes’ walk. Oxford Street and Westminster Stations are just 10 minutes by Underground.
