About Hotel Dei Fiori

Boasting free private parking, free Wi-Fi and soundproofed rooms with air conditioning, Hotel Dei Fiori is 492 feet from Famagosta Metro Stop in south Milan. Direct metro links get to Milano Centrale Train Station in 15 minutes. Rooms at the 3-star Dei Fiori come with double-glazed windows, a flat-screen satellite TV, small safe and a mini-bar. An extensive buffet breakfast is available daily until 10:00. It can be included in your rate or added at the time of booking. There are many restaurants and bars in the area. Great public transport links are available to take you to the rest of Milan and its Exhibition and Conference centers. Bike storage is also offered on site.

  • 24 hour front desk
  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
