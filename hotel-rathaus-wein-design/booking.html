About Hotel Rathaus - Wein & Design

Hotel Rathaus - Wein & Design is located in Vienna’s 8th district and has an extensive wine list with over 450 top Austrian wines. Each room is dedicated to a specific Austrian winemaker and offers some of its best-selling wines in the mini-bar. It offers free WiFi and a 24-hour reception. Units at Hotel Rathaus - Wein & Design are spacious and have modern furnishings and come with air conditioning and a private bathroom. Double rooms have a mini-bar stocked with Austria’s top wines, and WiFi is available free of charge in all units. The hotel's signature room, the Junior Suite, offers Scandinavian lifestyle, including a boxspring bed from Hästens. A breakfast buffet is available every morning including regional specialties, antipasti and a broad selection of cheese. Freshly prepared egg dishes can be requested. Also, guests can choose a small breakfast which offers coffee or tea and a croissant. Vienna’s city center and the Rathaus Metro Station are both a 5-minute walk away. A few of the city’s attractions can be reached on foot within 10 minutes, and include several theaters, the Museum of Modern Art and St. Stephen’s Cathedral. A multi-story car park is located opposite Hotel Rathaus - Wein & Design, and charges are applicable there.
