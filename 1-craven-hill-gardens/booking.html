About 1 Craven Hill Gardens

1 Craven Hill Gardens is a 4-star property set in London opening after complete renovation in March 2019. The property is located 0.7 mi from Hyde Park. The property is 0.8 mi from Royal Albert Hall. At the hotel, each room includes a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. Rooms are fitted with a coffee machine, while certain rooms have a kitchenette with a microwave and a fridge. All guest rooms at 1 Craven Hill Gardens have air conditioning and a wardrobe. With staff speaking English, guidance is available at the reception. The Serpentine is 0.8 mi from 1 Craven Hill Gardens. London City Airport is 9.9 mi away.
