About Anaheim Astoria Inn & Suites

Just 15 minutes' walk from the world famous Disneyland Resort, this motel offers bright, modern rooms including mini-fridges and coffee makers. All of the rooms at Anaheim Astoria Inn and Suites include TVs with film channels, microwaves, and free WiFi. Guests can also make use of hairdryers and work desks in the room. Guests at the Anaheim Astoria can make use of guest laundry facilities and arranged sightseeing and tours. This motel is located in the heart of Anaheim and provides free parking. Anaheim Convention Center is 10 minutes' drive away. Central Los Angeles is 35 minutes' drive away.
