About Interno 7 Luxury Rooms B&B

Just a 10-minute walk from the Trevi Fountain and Coliseum, Interno 7 Luxury Rooms is located in the lively Rione Monti neighborhood. This property offers free WiFi and air-conditioned rooms with a 32" satellite LED TV. Featuring modern dcor and parquet floors, these soundproofed rooms come with a Nespresso coffee machine and a mini-bar. Guests can enjoy complimentary coffee capsules, kettle, tea and herbal teas in the room. The Repubblica Metro Station is 2133 feet from the B&B. Termini Train Station is a 15-minute walk away.

Amenities

  • Room Service
  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • Non-Smoking Facility
