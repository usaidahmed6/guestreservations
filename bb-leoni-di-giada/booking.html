About B&B Leoni Di Giada

Offering air-conditioned rooms with free Wi-Fi, CARLO ALBERTO GUESTHOUSE - close to CENTRAL STATION is 1476 feet from Termini Station. A few steps away, Vittorio Emanuele Metro has direct links to the Spanish Steps and Vatican Museums. On the second floor of a central building in Rome, CARLO ALBERTO GUESTHOUSE features rooms with attached bathroom with an LCD TV, safe and mini-bar. A sweet breakfast is served in your room, or in the garden in fine weather. The property is 656 feet from the Santa Maria Maggiore Basilica and a 15 minutes' walk from the Coliseum. The surrounding area is full of shops, restaurants and cafs.
