About Four Points by Sheraton Newark Christiana Wilmington

Featuring fitness facilities and a spa and wellness center, Four Points by Sheraton Newark Wilmington is located in Newark. Free WiFi access is available. Interstate 95 is just 3 minutes’ drive from the hotel. A work desk and a flat-screen cable TV are provided in each guest room. There is also a safety deposit box. The private bathrooms are equipped with a hairdryer and free toiletries. Newark Wilmington Four Points by Sheraton offers on-site dining at the Farm-A-Sea Bistro and Crafthouse. Luggage storage and dry cleaning services are available. University of Delaware is 11 minutes’ drive from the hotel. The shops and restaurants at Christiana Mall are 2.7 mi away.
