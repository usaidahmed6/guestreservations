About Hostal Los Alpes

This good-value guest house is conveniently located in the city’s historic center, just off Gran Vía Boulevard. Set in a 19th-century house, the renovated Hostal Los Alpes offers free Wi-Fi throughout. Rooms at Los Alpes are tastefully decorated and include satellite TV. All rooms come with a private bathroom or shower. The guest house can arrange excursions and provide useful information on Madrid and the surrounding area. Front desk services are from 9:00 to 21:00 The lively Chueca district is just 5 minutes’ walk from Los Alpes Hostal. Gran Via Metro Station (L5 - Green Line) is just 328 feet away.

Amenities

  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • Non-Smoking Facility
