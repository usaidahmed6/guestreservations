About The Live Hotel

Immerse yourself in the vibrant energy of downtown Los Angeles, with world-class entertainment, cultural attractions, and trendy shopping districts right at your doorstep. From the iconic landmarks to the hidden gems, The Live Hotel provides the perfect starting point for your urban exploration located in between Koreatown and Downtown Los Angeles. This completely nonsmoking hotel is 5 minutes' drive from Los Angeles Convention Center and Downtown Los Angeles. It offers a seasonal outdoor pool and rooms with free WiFi. Parking is available for $10 per day. Amenities Provided - Secure Parking Lot and Garage - A flat-screen cable TV - Coffee machine - Refrigerator - Work desk - Upon request: microwave Nearby Destination and Amenities < 1 mile away - Crypto.com Arena (Home of the LA Kings and Lakers) - LA Live - LA Convention Center - Koreatown - Santee Alley Shopping District Destinations and Amenities <2.3 miles away - University of Southern California - Grand Central Market - Downtown Los Angeles - Walt Disney Concert Hall - Broad Museum - The Last Bookstore - Pershing Square - Destinations and Amenities ~ 3 miles - Smorgasburg - Dodgers Stadium - Banc of California (BMO) Stadium - Natural History Museum (~3.1 miles) - Little Tokyo - Chinatown - LA Historic Park - Silver Lake Destinations and Amenities <6 miles - Hollywood Walk of Fame - Griffith Observatory - Hollywood Sign
