About Kerry Hotel, Beijing

Shangri-la Kerry Hotel, Beijing is located around 656 feet from Beijing's Central Chinese Television Tower in the commercial district. Offering free internet, it features an aerobics studio, indoor tennis court and 5 dining options. Shangri-la Kerry Hotel, Beijing is within around 2.5 mi from Wangfujing shopping street and Panjiayuan Antique Market. Guomao Subway Station is approximately 2297 feet away. Beijing Capital International Airport is around 40 minutes' car journey away. The Palace Museum is around 19 minutes' drive away. Modern interiors are featured in each room, fitted with high ceilings and wooden furnishings. Offering city or garden views, amenities featured include ample workspace, automatic washlet and mini-bar. Guests get to choose from a pillow menu to aid in a good night’s rest. The hotel’s fitness center features a 24-hour gym, indoor swimming pool, multi-purpose court and a recreation room, plus an outdoor jogging track. The property is also suitable for large scale conferences and banquets with 6,000 square yards of event space. A children’s playground with drop slides and themed party rooms are offered to the little ones. Wine and dine facilities are presented through five establishments. The Horizon Chinese Restaurant serves wood-fired Beijing duck along with Cantonese signature dishes. Centro is open 24 hours a day and features live music, guests can relax with champagne, cocktails and exquisite canapés.
