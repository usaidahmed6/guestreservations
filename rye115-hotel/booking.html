About Rye115 Hotel

This charming hotel is located in a quiet area, 2 minutes walk from Parken Stadium. It offers free Wi-Fi and convenient access to Copenhagens city center, a 10-minute bus ride away. Rooms at Hotel Rye feature a work desk. A robe and slippers are also included for added comfort. The hotels breakfast buffet offers several choices, including homemade bread. Free tea/coffee is available until 15:00. It can be enjoyed on the terrace or in the cozy garden. Rye Hotel staff can recommend nearby cafs and restaurants. Serne (the Lakes), only 2 minutes walk away, is a popular spot for joggers.
