About NH Eurobuilding

Located in the heart of Madrid, this stylish hotel is near Real Madrids famous Bernabeu Stadium and the impressive Kio Towers. It has a modern spa and 24-hour room service. Each bright, spacious room at NH Collection Madrid Eurobuilding offers satellite TV, air conditioning, and a private bathroom with a rain-effect shower. Some rooms also include access to the hotel's luxurious VIP area. The hotel has a gym and a spa, including massage services. The sports hall includes paddle tennis courts. NH Collection Madrid Eurobuilding provides areas for conventions, conferences, meetings and other events. There is free WiFi. There is 1 restaurant on site, the 3 Michelin-starred DiverXO, run by David Muoz. There is also a 99 Sushi Bar & Restaurant. The hotel offers a healthy breakfast daily. The NH Collection Madrid Eurobuilding is in a quiet area. Cuzco and Colombia Metro Stations are within 1640 feet. They offer easy access to the city center. The hotel is close to Chamartn Station. The famous Paseo de la Castellana is within walking distance. It is only 6.8 mi from Barajas Airport. The following VIP Level services are also included for guests: - Parking - Free access to the gym and spa - Early check-in and late check-out, subject to availability - Free soft drinks in the mini-bar - An ironing service for 1 item of clothing - Premium WiFi - National and international press - Access to the business center - Welcome gift in the room.
