About The Harrington

In the heart of Kensington and Chelsea, The Harrington - Gloucester Road - 1 Harrington Gardens offers serviced apartments with air conditioning and free WiFi. Gloucester Road Underground Station is a 2-minute walk away and there are a number of restaurants and shops in the immediate area. The Harrington - Gloucester Road - 1 Harrington Gardens Residences is the inclusion of a fully equipped kitchen, complete with microwave, oven, hob and fridge, in every apartment. Each flat incorporates a lounge and dining area with flat-screen TVs throughout. There is also 24-hour reception. The Natural History Museum, Science Museum and the Victoria & Albert Museum are within a short 15-minute walk of The Harrington - Gloucester Road - 1 Harrington Gardens Residences. London Olympia, Harrods, Kensington Gardens and Hyde Park are 20 minutes away by foot.
