About Melita House Hotel

Just a 10-minute walk from Victoria Station, Complimentary Premium Teas and Coffees and Viennoiserie to Take Away available 07.30 - .09.00 DAILY in the lounge and 24-hour Front Desk for arrivals. The family-run hotel is located in elegant Pimlico, just a mile from Westminster Abbey. The traditional rooms each have a mini-fridge and a TV with satellite channels and on-demand movies. All rooms have WiFi access, a work desk and a private modern bathroom with a hairdryer. The Melita is just 1640 feet from Pimlico Underground Station, providing easy access to Oxford Circus and Kings Cross. The Houses of Parliament are just a 20-minute stroll away.
