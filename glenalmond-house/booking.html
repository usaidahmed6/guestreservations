About Glenalmond House

This Victorian guest house is 1.5 mi from Edinburgh city center, with easy access by bus. It has guaranteed free parking for each room, free Wi-Fi and traditionally decorated luxury rooms. Glenalmond House is a 4-star bed and breakfast in the quiet area of Newington. The Royal Mile, Princes Street and Edinburgh Waverley Rail Station are less than 2 mi away. There are buses every few minutes. The Victorian house mixes traditional charm and original features with modern luxuries. The rooms with attached bathroom have flat-screen TVs, showers and luxury toiletries. Some have four-poster beds. The extensive breakfast menu includes Scottish pancakes, waffles , traditional cooked Scottish breakfasts and vegetarian alternatives. Guests can enjoy golfing in the local area and the property offers discounts all year round.
