About Hotel De Petris

Just a 5-minute walk from the Trevi Fountain and the Spanish Steps, Hotel De Petris offers elegant rooms in the center, and a spacious terrace with views of Romes sights. Barberini Metro Station is 820 feet away. The spacious and air-conditioned rooms at De Petris have a cozy feeling, created by light and warm color schemes. In-room facilities include free WiFi, satellite TV, and a mini-bar. When the weather is warm, you can sit and unwind on the refurbished terrace. It offers views of the Quirinale presidential palace and its garden, as well as Saint Peters Basilica Dome. It is an ideal place to enjoy the sweet and savory buffet breakfast and enjoy an apritif. Staff are available 24 hours a day for useful recommendations on the surroundings. Roma Termini Train Station is just 2 metro stops away.
