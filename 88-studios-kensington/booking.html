About NOX HOTELS - Kensington

Located just 1312 feet from Olympia Exhibition Center, NOX Kensington offers accommodations in the London borough of Kensington. Free WiFi is featured throughout the property. The rooms offer an equipped kitchenette, air conditioning and flat-screen smart TV. The private bathroom features a rain shower and complimentary toiletries. There is a 24-hour front desk and daily maid service at the property. Kensington tube station is just 0.8 miles away. Westfield Shopping Center, The Kensington Gardens and The Kensington Palace can all be reached within 1 mile from NOX Kensington. The Royal Albert Hall is 1.4 miles away and Hyde Park is 1.6 miles away.

Amenities

  • 24 hour front desk
  • Concierge
  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
  • Non-Smoking Facility
