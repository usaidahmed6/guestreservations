About Sea Club Ocean Resort

Nestled directly across the street from the beach, this cozy hotel offers a relaxed atmosphere and convenient facilities within walking distance of attractions, restaurants and nightlife in Fort Lauderdale, Florida. Enjoy breakfast, dinner, and drinks with ocean views at Sea Club Ocean's on-site restaurant, The Deck. Stop by the gift shop for the necessary beach amenities, then spend the day swimming and lounging in the Florida sun. Guestrooms include a 42-inch flat-screen TV, a small refrigerator, and coffee-making facilities. Private bathrooms are also included. Sea Club Ocean Resort offers an on-site pool and fitness center as well as an on-site launderette for guests' use. The Fort Lauderdale Convention Center is 11 minutes' drive from the property while the Bonnet House Museum and Gardens is just 5 minutes' drive away. World-class shopping is available nearby at the Galleria (7 minutes' drive).
