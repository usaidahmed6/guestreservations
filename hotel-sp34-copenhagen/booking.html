Welcome to Hotel SP34 by Brøchner Hotels

Discover modern comfort and style at Hotel SP34 by Brøchner Hotels, a boutique hotel located just feet from City Hall Square and Copenhagen’s popular pedestrian street, Strøget. Guests can enjoy a range of amenities, including free WiFi, a rooftop terrace, and two on-site restaurants. Start the day with a breakfast buffet featuring organic food and ingredients, perfect for a healthy and delicious meal.

Each room at Hotel SP34 is equipped with tea/coffee facilities and a large, flat-screen TV with WiFi connectivity. Designer wooden furnishings and quality Ern Clean Skincare toiletries add a touch of sophistication, while suites offer additional perks like a free mini-bar stocked with soft drinks. Guests can also enjoy complimentary wine from 17:00 to 18:00 daily, adding a special touch to the evening.

  • Organic breakfast buffet with quality ingredients
  • Rooftop terrace with city views
  • Two on-site restaurants: Cock's and Cows and Restaurant Väkst
  • Designer wooden furnishings and Ern Clean Skincare toiletries
  • Complimentary wine hour every evening

Cock's and Cows serves upscale burgers made from homemade ingredients, while Restaurant Väkst offers Nordic-style dining in an urban garden setting. The hotel's prime location provides easy access to vintage clothing shops, trendy restaurants, and a wide selection of cafés and bars. Tivoli Gardens, one of Copenhagen's top attractions, is just an 8-minute walk away.

Experience the best of Copenhagen with a stay at Hotel SP34 by Brøchner Hotels, where modern amenities and a prime location create a memorable and comfortable experience.
