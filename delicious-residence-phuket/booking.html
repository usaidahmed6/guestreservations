About Hanuman Residence

Located in Patong, Hanuman Residence is 0.7 mi from Patong Beach. It offers an outdoor swimming pool, on-site restaurant and air-conditioned rooms with a cable TV. Guests enjoy free WiFi. Rooms are equipped with a fridge and a personal safe. Shower facilities and toiletries are in the private bathroom. Staff at the front desk can arrange for airport shuttle and motorbike rental services. Free parking is available near the property. Hanuman Residence is a 2-minute walk from local dining outlets and a 15-minute walk from Jungceylon Shopping Mall and the nightlife district.
