About The Select Inn Penrith

Offering a lovely outdoor swimming pool, The Select Inn Penrith is located just 2 minutes drive from Penrith town center. Accommodations is air-conditioned and includes a refrigerator and an electric kettle. There is also flat-screen, a desk and ironing facilities. The private bathroom has a shower and a hairdryer. There are 2 function rooms available catering for up to 40 people. The Select Inn Penrith is a 3-minute drive from Penrith Train Station and Penrith Panthers Leagues Club. Nepean Hospital is a 10-minute drive away, and Sydney CBD is a 55-minute drive away.
