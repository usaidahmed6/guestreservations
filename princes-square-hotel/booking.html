About Princes Square

Just 1640 feet from Hyde Park, Princes Square offers cozy rooms and a 24-hour front desk. The property is a 5-minute walk from Bayswater and Queensway Underground Stations. All rooms are comfortably furnished, with private bathrooms and satellite TVs. All rooms have tea/coffee facilities and a hairdryer, and some have flat-screen TVs. There is a great selection of international restaurants just a short walk away. Set in a Grade II Listed building, Princes Square is a 5-minute walk from elegant Notting Hill. Kensington High Street is a 15-minute walk away, and the Museum Mile is a 25-minute walk.

Amenities

  • 24 hour front desk
  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
  • Non-Smoking Facility
