About Belle Via Tokyo

Located in Tokyo, a 3-minute walk from Toshima Ward Minamiotsuka Park, Belle Via Tokyo provides air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi and express check-in and check-out. The property is around a 7-minute walk from Otsuka Park, 600 yards from Shintaro Suzuki Memorial Museum and a 7-minute walk from Tenso Shrine. The property is close to popular attractions like Atrevie Sugamo, Sogo Recreation Park and Sugamo Jizo-dori Shopping Street. At the inn, each room comes with a desk, a flat-screen TV, a shared bathroom, bed linen and towels. Belle Via Tokyo has certain rooms that include a balcony, and all rooms come with an electric tea pot. The accommodation has a sun terrace. Popular points of interest near Belle Via Tokyo include Koyasu Tenman-gu Sugawara Shrine, Sugamo Park and Otsuka Mosque. The nearest airport is Tokyo International Airport, 12 miles from the inn.
