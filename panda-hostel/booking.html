About Panda Hostel

A 5-minute walk from Loreto Metro Stop in Milan, Panda Hostel features a terrace and free WiFi throughout. All rooms have colorful interiors, some come with a shared bathroom. Panda Hostel is 15 minutes’ walk from both Politecnico di Milano university and from Milan Centrale Train and Metro Station. Milan Cathedral is 15 minutes away by metro.

Amenities

  • 24 hour front desk
  • Masks are available to guests
  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
