About Nobis Hotel Copenhagen, a Member of Design Hotels

Discover the Elegance of Nobis Hotel Copenhagen, A Beacon of Scandinavian Design

Step into the heart of Denmark's vibrant capital and experience the allure of Nobis Hotel Copenhagen, a Member of Design Hotels. Perfectly positioned just a 2-minute stroll from the enchanting Tivoli Gardens, this hotel, set in a historic 1903 building, marries the charm of yesteryear with cutting-edge Scandinavian design. The meticulously crafted interiors of the bright rooms, adorned with Scandinavian design furniture, ensure a stay that is both aesthetically pleasing and supremely comfortable.

The hotel extends an invitation to explore the realm of relaxation with complimentary access to a fitness center, sauna, hammam hot stone, and a cold plunge pool, promising a rejuvenating escape from the urban bustle. The on-site restaurant, NOI, tempts guests with a menu that celebrates the best of Scandinavian cuisine, focusing on seasonal and quality products.

  • Scandinavian designed rooms with marble bathrooms and flat-screen TVs
  • Complimentary access to wellness facilities including a sauna and cold plunge pool
  • NOI restaurant, offering exquisite Scandinavian cuisine
  • Prime location near Tivoli Gardens and central Copenhagen's cultural landmarks
  • Free WiFi throughout the hotel for all guests

Within walking distance of cultural treasures such as the Ny Carlsberg Glyptotek Art Museum, National Museum of Copenhagen, and the Danish Design Center, Nobis Hotel Copenhagen offers an unparalleled opportunity to delve into the rich tapestry of Danish history and design. Coupled with its proximity to Copenhagen Central Station, the hotel serves as an ideal hub for both business and leisure travelers seeking a distinguished and effortlessly stylish base. Nobis Hotel Copenhagen is not just a place to stay; it's a passage to the essence of Nordic charm and hospitality.
