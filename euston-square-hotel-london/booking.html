About Euston Square Hotel

Less than 1148 feet from London Euston Station, the hotel offers stylish, modern and air-conditioned rooms. The Euston Square Hotel has a superb location, just a short walk from the British Museum, Regent's Park, British Library, Oxford Street and the theaters of the West End. Camden, with its eclectic mix of bars, restaurants, shops and markets is 10 minutes away by bus. Euston Square London Underground station is just yards away and the hotel is also just over half a mile (0.6 mi) from King's Cross Railway Station and St Pancras International, London's Eurostar terminal. All of the stylish and contemporary rooms have digital flat-screen TVs with built-in entertainment systems. An online music library, games console, internet, and business services are available via the TV.
