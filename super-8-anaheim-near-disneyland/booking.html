About Kings Inn Anaheim at The Park & Convention Center

Located within a 13-minute walk of Anaheim Convention Center and 0.8 miles of Disneyland, Kings Inn Anaheim at The Park & Convention Center has rooms in Anaheim. Featuring an outdoor swimming pool, the 2-star motel has air-conditioned rooms with a private bathroom. The motel has a hot tub and a 24-hour front desk. At the motel, each room comes with a desk and a flat-screen TV. At Kings Inn Anaheim at The Park & Convention Center all rooms include bed linen and towels. The accommodation has a sun terrace. Disney California Adventure is 2 miles from Kings Inn Anaheim at The Park & Convention Center, while Knotts Berry Farm is 7.9 miles from the property. The nearest airport is John Wayne Airport, 12 miles from the motel.
