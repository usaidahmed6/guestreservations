About The Vic, Ascend Hotel Collection

Enjoy a beautiful getaway when you choose The Vic, Ascend Hotel Collection. The Garden City is yours to explore when you visit our boutique hotel in Victoria BC. With a central location in the heart of a scenic, historic community, we offer easy access to Victorias Inner Harbor, Victoria International Airport (YYJ), and Mayfair Shopping Center, featuring over 120 upscale shops. With a stunning boardwalk, the Inner Harbor district frequently hosts festivals, live shows, and visiting yachts and cruise ships. Chartered boat tours are also available, including whale watching tours where you can get up close and personal with herds of humpback whales. If youre traveling with children, theyll love a visit to the Royal British Columbia Museum, featuring indigenous art and natural history exhibits. Travelers looking for boutique hotels in Victoria or Victoria Inner Harbor accommodations can get the best price guaranteed when they book direct at The Vic.
