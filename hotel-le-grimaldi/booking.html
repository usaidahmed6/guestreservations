About Hotel Le Grimaldi by Happyculture

Located in the heart of Nice, Le Grimaldi is a 15-minute walk from Cours Saleya Flower Market and 1476 feet from the beach and the Promenade des Anglais. It offers air-conditioned and soundproofed rooms. Free WiFi access is available throughout. Each room is individually decorated and equipped with satellite TV and private bathroom. A fresh continental breakfast buffet is available daily and can be enjoyed onsite. In the afternoon Nespresso coffee and tea are available or you can enjoy a drink at the hotel’s bar. The lively area offers many restaurants and shops. Le Grimaldi is 2461 feet from the Nice-Ville Train Station and a 15-minute walk to Vieux Nice. Nice Côte d'Azur Airport is 4.3 mi away. Public parking is available just 328 feet from the hotel.
