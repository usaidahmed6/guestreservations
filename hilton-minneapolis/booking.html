About Hilton Minneapolis

Enter the Heart of Minneapolis at Hilton Minneapolis

Welcome to Hilton Minneapolis, a premier destination perfectly situated in the heart of the city and directly connected to the Minneapolis Convention Center via skyway. This central location ensures guests enjoy effortless access to Minneapolis' top attractions, making it an ideal choice for both business travelers and leisure seekers. With a focus on comfort and convenience, Hilton Minneapolis provides a host of modern amenities designed to enhance every stay.

  • Direct skyway connection to the Minneapolis Convention Center
  • Spacious rooms equipped with a 50-inch flat-screen cable TV and iPod docking station
  • On-site dining at Ten 01 Social Restaurant and Lounge for all meals
  • State-of-the-art fitness center and indoor pool
  • 24-hour business center and complimentary WiFi in the lobby

Each guest room at Hilton Minneapolis boasts ample space and is furnished with a fridge and coffee machine for your convenience. The hotel's Ten 01 Social Restaurant and Lounge serves an array of delicious meals throughout the day, catering to all tastes and dietary needs. Whether staying for business or leisure, guests can take advantage of the hotel's comprehensive facilities, including a cutting-edge fitness center and a serene indoor pool.

Located just a short distance from the US Bank Stadium, home of the Vikings, and the bustling Nicollet Mall, Hilton Minneapolis offers easy access to the city's vibrant life. The renowned Mall of America is also within reach, ensuring a memorable stay filled with shopping, dining, and entertainment options. Experience the best of Minneapolis with a stay at Hilton Minneapolis, where convenience meets comfort in the city center.
