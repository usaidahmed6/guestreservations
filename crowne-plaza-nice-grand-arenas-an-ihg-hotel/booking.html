About Crowne Plaza Nice - Grand Arenas

Crowne Plaza - Nice - Grand Arenas, an IHG Hotel features a shared lounge, terrace, a restaurant and bar in Nice. This 4-star hotel offers room service, a 24-hour front desk and free WiFi. Guests can use the sauna, or enjoy city views. The hotel will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms offering a desk, an electric tea pot, a minibar, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a shower. At Crowne Plaza - Nice - Grand Arenas, an IHG Hotel every room comes with bed linen and towels. Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a buffet or an American breakfast. Plage Landsberg is 1.3 miles from Crowne Plaza - Nice - Grand Arenas, an IHG Hotel, while Plage des Flots Bleus is 1.4 miles away. The nearest airport is Nice Cte d'Azur Airport, 0.6 miles from the hotel.
