About Hôtel Le Petit Palais

Located in Nice, Hotel Le Petit Palais welcomes you in a green and elegant historic district. Le Petit Palais features 25 air-conditioned rooms with a refined decor. Each is fitted with modern equipment and free Wi-Fi. Some rooms rooms offer views of the sea or of Nice Old Town. The hotel is 10 minutes' walk from the Chagall Museum and close to the Matisse Museum and the Roman archaeological site. During your stay, enjoy the many attractions of Nice. You can go to the beach and shop in the city center.

Amenities

  • Room Service
  • 24 hour front desk
  • Concierge
  • Babysitting Services
  • Masks are available to guests
  • Swimming Pool - Outdoor
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Balcony
  • Non-Smoking Facility
  • Lounge/Bar
