About Fairway Hotel

Located in the center of London, this traditional Georgian house has been family-run for over 50 years. Kings Cross Station and St Pancras International are just 3 minutes' walk away. Each room is centrally heated, with a TV and tea and coffee making facilities. Free WiFi is available throughout the building. Guests can take the London Underground to visit all the citys attractions, with Oxford Street, Madame Tussauds and Trafalgar Square only a short train ride away from Fairway Hotel.
