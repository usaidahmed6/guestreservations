About Rosa Grand Milano - Starhotels Collezione

Overlooking Piazza Fontana in central Milan, Rosa Grand Milano - Starhotels Collezione is a 2-minute walk from Milan Cathedral. It offers 2 elegant restaurants, a modern wellness center, free WiFi, and stylish rooms. All rooms at the Rosa Grand feature a Starhotels' Starbed, pillow selection, a flat-screen satellite TV, mini-bar and work desk. Including state-of-the-art equipment, the Rosa Grands wellness center comes with a fitness area, relaxation area and solarium. The hotel's restaurants all offer high quality products and serve a mix of Milanese specialties and international dishes. Eight meeting rooms complete the hotel's offer. The hotel is a 5-minute walk from La Scala Theater and Via Montenapoleone shopping street. Duomo Metro is 1640 feet away, giving you quick and easy access to the rest of Milan.
