About Dormi a Roma

Dormi a Roma provides free Wifi and rooms with air conditioning in Rome. The property is around a 17-minute walk from Roma Trastevere Train Station, 1.9 miles from Basilica San Paolo Metro Station, and 2.3 miles from Piazza di Santa Maria in Trastevere. On-site staff can arrange a shuttle service. At the bed and breakfast, each room comes with private entrance, desk, a TV, and a private bathroom. The rooms are equipped with heating facilities. Guests can enjoy fresh pastries during the buffet breakfast. Campo de' Fiori is 2.4 miles from the bed and breakfast, while Roman Forum is 2.5 miles away. The nearest airport is Fiumicino, 13 miles from Dormi a Roma, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
