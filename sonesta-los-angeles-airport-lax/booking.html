About Sonesta Los Angeles Airport LAX

Welcome to Sonesta Los Angeles Airport LAX: Your Gateway to the City

Discover the convenience and comfort of Sonesta Los Angeles Airport LAX, situated a mere 1312 feet from Los Angeles International Airport. This ideally located hotel offers a seamless connection to the heart of Los Angeles, making it the perfect base for both business and leisure travelers. With an array of dining options on-site and a refreshing outdoor pool, every stay is designed to cater to the diverse needs of global guests.

  • 24-hour airport shuttle service, ensuring travel ease around the clock.
  • On-site dining variety, including The Landing Restaurant, Century Taproom with live music, Boulevard Market Café, and Yokoso Sushi Bar.
  • An outdoor pool, offering a tranquil oasis amidst the bustling city.
  • Proximity to Venice Beach, Marina del Rey, and Santa Monica, with easy access via nearby I-405 and I-105 motorways.
  • Spacious, air-conditioned rooms equipped with flat-screen HD cable TV, ergonomic chairs, and movable work desks.

Guests can indulge in California cuisine at The Landing Restaurant for breakfast or savor the evening at Century Taproom, which boasts live music, sports broadcasts, a selection of wines, and an array of microbrews. For those on the go, Boulevard Market Café offers quick meals throughout the day, while Yokoso Sushi Bar presents exquisite sushi for dinner.

Whether it’s the allure of Hollywood, which lies just 12 miles away, or the scenic beaches of Venice, Marina del Rey, and Santa Monica, Sonesta Los Angeles Airport LAX serves as the perfect gateway to explore the vibrant city of Los Angeles. Experience the convenience of city access and the comfort of a well-appointed stay, all located within driving distance to the city’s most iconic attractions.
