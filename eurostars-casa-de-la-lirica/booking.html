About Eurostars Casa de la Lirica

The Eurostars Casa de la Lírica is 984 feet from the Puerta del Sol in central Madrid. Its lobby has a beautiful glass roof, and it offers rooms with shower. The air-conditioned rooms at the Casa de la Lírica are elegant and comfortable. They are equipped with a TV and the private bathrooms come with a hairdryer. Eurostars Casa de la Lírica is 10 minutes’ walk from Paseo del Prado, home to the Thyssen, Prado and Reina Sofía Museums. The hotel is well connected to the rest of Madrid, with 3 metro stations within 984 feet. Due to the Sustainable Mobility Ordinance in the city of Madrid, there are currently traffic restrictions in the area where the hotel is located. It is mandatory that the vehicles have the environmental label since, failing that, they can be penalized with a fine. Please consult the "Madrid 360" strategic plan page for the conditions that apply to each device.
