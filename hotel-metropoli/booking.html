About Hotel Metropoli

Conveniently set in the Central Station Area district of Milan, Hotel Metropoli is located a 6-minute walk from Centrale Metro Station, 0.9 miles from Bosco Verticale and 1.8 miles from GAM Milano. Each room at the 3-star hotel has city views, and guests can enjoy access to a shared lounge and to a bar. Free WiFi is available and private parking can be arranged at an extra charge. The hotel will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms offering a desk, an electric tea pot, a minibar, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a bidet. Guest rooms include a closet. Languages spoken at the reception include English, Spanish, Italian and Russian, and guests are invited to request guidance on the area when needed. Brera Art Gallery is 1.8 miles from Hotel Metropoli, while Arena Civica is 1.9 miles away. The nearest airport is Milan Linate Airport, 5.6 miles from the accommodation.
