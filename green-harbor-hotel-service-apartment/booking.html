About Green Harbor Hotel & Service Apartment

Located in a quiet area of Patong with easy access to all main attractions, Green Harbor Hotel & Service Apartment offers the choice between a comfortable hotel stay or self-careting with free WiFi throughout the property and free parking. It offers daily American breakfast, several water sports and tour services. Green Harbor Hotel & Service Apartment is a 5-minute drive from Patong Beach. It is a 45-minute drive from Phuket International Airport. The property offers airport drop-off service at an additional charge. Modern apartment rooms offer the comfort of light wood furnishings and flat-screen cable TVs. They also have well-equipped kitchenettes with a fridge, a microwave oven and hobs. Private balconies are featured in certain rooms. For leisure, guests can explore several attractions or enjoy a range of activities within the area including cabaret shows, clubs, Thai boxing, football and many eateries. They can also go on a diving trip arranged by the tour desk. Staff can provide ticketing and car hire services for guests convenience.
