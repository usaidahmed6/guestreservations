About Snoozebox Olympic Park

Stay Smart and Simple at Snoozebox Olympic Park

Snoozebox Olympic Park offers unique and convenient accommodations in the heart of London, perfect for travelers looking to explore the city. Located just 1.2 miles from the nearest Tube station and close to popular attractions like Olympic Stadium and Stratford City Westfield, this hotel provides easy access to the best of London.

  • Adults-Only Accommodation: Enjoy a peaceful and quiet stay designed specifically for adult guests.
  • Prime Location: Situated near major transport links and attractions such as Victoria Park and Canary Wharf.
  • 24-Hour Front Desk: Benefit from around-the-clock assistance to make your stay as smooth as possible.
  • On-Site Bar and Lounge: Relax and unwind with a drink at the hotel’s bar or enjoy the communal lounge area.
  • Complimentary Breakfast: Start your day with a buffet or continental breakfast offered daily.

Guest rooms at Snoozebox Olympic Park are equipped with modern amenities, including flat-screen TVs and private bathrooms with showers and free toiletries. Certain rooms also feature a safety deposit box. Fresh bed linen and towels are provided in every room, ensuring a comfortable stay.

The hotel's terrace offers a pleasant space to relax, while free WiFi is available throughout the property, keeping guests connected during their visit. With its close proximity to Stratford Tube Station and easy access to London City Airport, Snoozebox Olympic Park is an ideal choice for those looking to explore London effortlessly.

Discover the vibrant energy of London while enjoying the convenience and simplicity of Snoozebox Olympic Park, where comfort meets practicality in a prime location.
