About HM Park Life Pradox

Featuring a bar, terrace and views of city, HM Park Life Pradox is located in Mexico City, 6 miles from Six Flags Mexico. Located in the Santa Fe district, the property provides guests with an indoor pool, a fitness center and a restaurant. The property has a 24-hour front desk and room service for guests. At the hotel, rooms are equipped with a desk. Complete with a private bathroom equipped with a shower and a hairdryer, the rooms at HM Park Life Pradox have a flat-screen TV and air conditioning, and some rooms contain a seating area. Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a à la carte or an American breakfast. National Cinematheque is 7 miles from HM Park Life Pradox, while Chapultepec Forest is 7 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Benito Juarez International Airport, 14 miles from the hotel.
