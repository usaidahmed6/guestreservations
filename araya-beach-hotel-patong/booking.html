About Clarion Hotel Patong Phuket

Set a few steps away from the beach and the famous spots in Phuket, Clarian Hotel Beach Patong features an outdoor pool and free WiFi in all areas. All rooms are air-conditioned and have a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom fitted with a shower. A separate living area and a kitchenette is also available in some room. Guests can enjoy both Thai and international cuisines at the on-site restaurant. Refreshing drinks and a selection of wine is also available at the bar. Other restaurants, bars and cafes can also be found within a few steps from the property. The hotel is only 984 feet away from Bangla Road, Jungceylon Shopping Center and Bangla Boxing Stadium can both be found within walking distance. Phuket International Airport is 22 mi away. The property offers shuttle services with an additional charge.
