About De Guestibus

Conveniently located across from the Tuscolana - Giulio Agricola Busstop, De Guestibus offers rooms with air conditioning. Free Wi-Fi access is available in all areas. The Colosseum is 4.3 mi away. The modern rooms all offer city views and a flat-screen TV. Some rooms also have a private balcony. Rooms have a private en suite or external bathroom with a shower and hairdryer. All rooms are serviced daily. De Guestibus is located a 15-minute walk from Cinecitta and 15 minutes' drive from Termini Metro and Train Station. Ciampino – G.B. Pastine International Airport is 6.2 mi away.

Amenities

  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • Non-Smoking Facility
