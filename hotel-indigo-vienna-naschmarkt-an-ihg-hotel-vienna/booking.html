About Hotel Indigo - Vienna - Naschmarkt, an IHG Hotel

Located in Vienna, 1.1 miles from Vienna State Opera, Hotel Indigo Vienna - Naschmarkt, an IHG Hotel provides accommodations with a fitness center, private parking, a terrace and a restaurant. With free WiFi, this 4-star hotel offers room service and a 24-hour front desk. Some units at the property feature a balcony with a city view. The hotel will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms offering a desk, an electric tea pot, a minibar, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a shower. All guest rooms include a closet. A buffet breakfast is available every morning at Hotel Indigo Vienna - Naschmarkt, an IHG Hotel. Popular points of interest near the accommodation include Albertina Museum, Musikverein and Karlskirche. The nearest airport is Vienna International Airport, 12 miles from Hotel Indigo Vienna - Naschmarkt, an IHG Hotel.
