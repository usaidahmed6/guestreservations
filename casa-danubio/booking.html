About Casa Danubio Reforma by VH

Boasting a shared lounge, terrace and views of city, Casa Danubio Reforma by VH is set in Mexico City, 800 metres from United States Embassy. The property is located 2.8 km from Chapultepec Castle, 3.1 km from National Museum of Anthropology and 3.1 km from Museo de Memoria y Tolerancia. The property is non-smoking and is situated 1.1 km from The Angel of Independence. All guest rooms come with a fridge, microwave, a kettle, a shower, a hairdryer and a desk. The rooms at the hostel are fitted with a private bathroom and bed linen. Museo de Arte Popular is 3.2 km from Casa Danubio Reforma by VH, while The Museum of Fine Arts is 3.6 km from the property. The nearest airport is Benito Juarez International Airport, 13 km from the accommodation.
