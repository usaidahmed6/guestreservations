About Neutral Bay Lodge

Just 820 feet from Neutral Bay Wharf, Neutral Bay Lodge offers rooms with free Wi-Fi, TV, bar fridge, bed linen and toiletries. It features a lovely garden, a communal kitchen and a self-service laundry. Limited free parking is available on site, for 6 cars only, on the "first in, best dress basis". Neutral Bay Lodge Sydney is 1.2 mi from Luna Park and North Sydney Train Station. Sydney Opera House is a 10-minute drive, and Sydney International Airport is a 25-minute drive away. The Royal North Shore Hospital is a 8-minute drive from the property.
