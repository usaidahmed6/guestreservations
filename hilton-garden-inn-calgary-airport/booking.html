About Hilton Garden Inn Calgary Airport

Located 15 minutes from the Calgary International Airport, Hilton Garden Inn Calgary Airport offers an indoor saltwater pool and hot tub. A microwave, refrigerator, and free WiFi feature in each room. A cable TV, seating area, and a desk are provided in the air-conditioned guest rooms at the Calgary Airport Hilton. Suites offer separate living rooms with a pull-out sofa bed and an extra LCD TV. Guests can dine in the Garden Grille & Bar restaurant, enjoy drinks in the Pavilion Lounge, or grab a quick snack at the 24-hour pavilion pantry. The on-site fitness center is open 24 hours a day. A free airport shuttle service is available. Laundry and dry cleaning services are provided. A business center and meeting facilities are offered for traveling guests. Telus Spark Center and the Calgary Zoo are both 6.8 mi from the property. CrossIron Mills mall is only 14 minutes’ drive away.
