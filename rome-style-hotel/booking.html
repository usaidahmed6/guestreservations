About Rome Style Hotel

Just 1312 feet from the Spanish Steps and Via Condotti, The Style is set in the heart of Rome's historic center. The functional, rooms with attached bathroom come with air conditioning and a flat-screen TV. Featuring air conditioning, all rooms at the The Style come with modern furniture, a flat-screen TV and free WiFi. The private marble bathrooms are complete with large shower and hairdryer. The property is set in a historic building from the 19th century. A buffet breakfast is available daily in the lounge on the ground floor. It includes sweet and savory items, hot and cold drinks, yogurt. Lactose- and gluten-free products are available upon request. Located less than a 5-minute walk from Spagna Metro Station, The Style is 1312 feet from the Trevi Fountain. The property is 328 feet from a bus stop with links to Roma Termini Train Station.
