About Casa Garbata

Offering free WiFi, Casa Garbata is 1312 feet from Garbatella Metro Station. It features simply furnished rooms with a private bathroom. These rooms come with a cooling fan and a TV. A shared kitchen and lounge are also available. The Basilica of Saint Paul Outside the Walls is 0.9 mi from Casa Garbata. The Coliseum is 3 metro stops away.
