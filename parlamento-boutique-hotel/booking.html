About Parlamento Boutique Hotel

Parlamento Boutique Hotel is located in Rome's Spagna district, housed in a 17th-century building. It is 984 feet from Via Condotti and 984 feet from Trevi Fountain. Free WiFi is available throughout. Every room at this hotel is air conditioned and comes with a TV with Sky channels, and a kettle. Each room includes a private bathroom with a bath and a bidet, with free toiletries and a hairdryer provided. A continental buffet breakfast is served daily, and can be enjoyed in the guests' room on request. There is a 24-hour front desk at the property. Piazza di Spagna is 1312 feet from Parlamento Boutique Hotel, while Spanish Steps are 1312 feet away. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino Airport, 9.3 mi from the property.
