About Centara Karon Resort Phuket - SHA Extra Plus

Centara Karon Resort Phuket - SHA Extra Plus Certificate is a 3-minute walk to the beachfront. The resort has 4 residential zones and features 3 outdoor pools and 6 dining options. Free parking is provided. Each room has a satellite TV, fridge and tea/coffee maker. Enjoy a massage at SPA Cenvaree, or work out at the gym. Activities like bungy jumping and go-kart racing can be arranged. Other facilities include tennis courts and a children’s recreation center. Lotus Restaurant serves buffet breakfast and a la carte lunch and dinner. Azure Restaurant provides meals and you can have a cocktail at The Mix Lounge. Refreshments are available at Tropix, Lagoon and Terraces pool bars, located at each of the 3 pools. Centara Karon Resort Phuket - SHA Extra Plus Certificate is a 45-minute drive from Phuket International Airport. It is 9.3 mi from Phuket Town.
