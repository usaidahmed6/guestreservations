About NH Madrid Las Tablas

NH Madrid Las Tablas is a design hotel offers excellent access to Barajas Airport. Set to the north of Madrid city center. The modern rooms at Las Tablas are soundproofed and air conditioned. They include a 40-inch TV, desk, mini-bar and safe. The private bathroom includes a set of toiletries and a hairdryer. The hotel has a stylish restaurant and bar. Free WiFi is available throughout the building. The hotel has good access to major roads, including the A1 and M40. Mara Tudor Tram Station is just 1312 feet away. NH Madrid Las Tablas is located in the citys Fuencarral district, near Universidad Autnoma de Madrid. The business area of La Moraleja is nearby, and the IFEMA Trade Center is 15 minutes away by car.
