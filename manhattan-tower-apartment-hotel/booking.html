About Manhattan Tower Apartment Hotel

These apartments are located on the Intracoastal and 2297 feet from Fort Lauderdale Beach. A fully equipped kitchen, free WiFi access, and an outdoor pool are featured. The Galleria shopping center is 2297 feet away. Decorated with original art, these apartments also include a cable TV, DVD player, and separate living room. Extras include a washing machine and tumble dryer at this Fort Lauderdale Manhattan Tower. When not relaxing on the waterfront terrace, guests can stroll through the garden or enjoy the shared lounge. Free parking is provided to all Manhattan Tower guests throughout their stays. The property is 1640 feet from Bonnet House Museum and Gardens, 0.8 mi from City of Fort Lauderdale Las Olas Marina, 6 minutes' drive from Las Olas Boulevard shopping area, and 10 minutes' walk from the beach.
