About Native Hyde Park

Adjacent to Kensington Gardens just 2 minutes walk from Hyde Park, these stunning apartments boast sleek, minimalist interiors in the affluent area of Paddington. Oxford Street is only 15 minutes walk away, while Paddington Station is just 5 minutes on foot. Stylish furnishings and light, modern touches feature in every Native Hyde Park apartment, along with unique Victorian features. Most apartments offer a bright living area in which you can relax with cozy sofas, and all apartments provide a TV with cable channels, and free Wi-Fi access. Modern, fully equipped kitchens and kitchenettes each come with stainless steel appliances including an oven and microwave to cook meals with, and a dining area to enjoy them at. Guests can also make use of a dishwasher, a washer/dryer, and a refrigerator. The surrounding area is filled with bars and restaurants, while Lancaster Gate Tube Station is only 3 minutes walk away from the hotel. The boutique shops of Notting Hill are only a 15-minute walk away.
