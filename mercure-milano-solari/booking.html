About Hotel Mercure Milano Solari

Offering free Wi-Fi and minimalistic-style rooms, Hotel Mercure Milano Solari is just 164 feet from Don Giussani Park. The property is a 5-minute walk from Sant'Agostino Metro. With white furnishings and wooden floors, rooms at the Milano Solari have air conditioning, an LCD TV, an electric kettle, and a safe for laptops. The Club 1 is the hotel's elegant bar where fine wines, light lunches, and a variety of cocktails are served. Guests can enjoy discounted rates at a partner garage in Via Valparaiso 8. Rho Fiera Milano Exhibition Center is reachable in 25 minutes by car, while Milan Centrale Train Station is 7 metro stops away.

Amenities

  • Wheelchair Access
