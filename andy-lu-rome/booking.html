About B&B Andy&Lu

B&B Andy&LU is just a 5-minute walk from both the Diocletian Baths and Porta Pia. It offers free WiFi and air-conditioned rooms with LCD TV, and private bathroom. Rooms at the B&B Andy&LU are traditionally furnished and have large windows with courtyard garden or street views. An Italian breakfast is provided daily at the B&B Andy&LU, including cappuccino, tea, or hot chocolate with pastries. The B&B Andy&LU is 1969 feet from Castro Pretorio Metro on the B Line. Termini Station is a 10-minute walk away.
