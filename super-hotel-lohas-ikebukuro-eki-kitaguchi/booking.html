About Super Hotel Lohas Ikebukuro-Eki Kitaguchi

Located just a 5-minute walk from JR Ikebukuro Station, the Super Hotel Inn Ikebukuro-Eki Kitaguchi offers cozy guest rooms and paid Japanese-style breakfast. Free WiFi is available in all areas. The Sunshine 60 Observatory is a 16-minute walk from the hotel. Shinjuku and Shibuya areas are accessible within 15 minutes via the JR Yamanote Line. There are numerous dining and shopping options around the hotel. The rooms at Ikebukuro Super Hotel are all air conditioned and equipped with a private bathroom, a flat-screen TV and a fridge. Basic amenities are also included. Guests can enjoy breakfast featuring a daily Japanese dish and salads at the dining room. Coin-operated laundry machines are on site and a trouser press is available for use upon request. Free luggage storage is also provided at the front desk.
