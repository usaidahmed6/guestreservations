About Hotel Delle Nazioni

Experience the heart of Rome staying in an elegant palace, formerly the residence of a noble family. Hotel Delle Nazioni offers a luxurious approach to the Eternal City. Enjoy a superb location in the artistic, cultural and commercial center of Rome, close to the main landmarks of this magnificent city, including the beautiful Trevi Fountain. The nearby Barberini Metro Station (line A) offers quick access to a host of destinations. All rooms and suites are elegant and refined, with parquet flooring and en suite, marble bathrooms. Each comfortable, sophisticated room is well-equipped with modern comforts.
