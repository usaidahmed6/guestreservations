About B&B Suites Trastevere

This guesthouse is just 220 yards from Trastevere Train Station, where trains to and from Fiumicino Airport stop. Rooms feature hand-painted walls or ceilings with trompe-l'oeil images of historic Rome. Just 55 yards away, you will find trams and public buses. Tram 8 will bring you to Largo Argentina, just steps from the Pantheon and Piazza Navona. Room prices at Trastevere Suites include free use of a mobile phone with an Italian Sim card. All rooms are elegant and feature digital TV and free Wi-Fi. Breakfast includes homemade cake, and snacks and beverages are available all day. A computer with free internet is available in the common area. The staff can organize a shuttle or limousine service upon request to and from the airport or other destinations.
