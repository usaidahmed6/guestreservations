About Four Seasons Hotel London at Ten Trinity Square

Offering 2 restaurants and spa, Four Seasons Hotel London at Ten Trinity Square is located 656 feet from Tower of London. All of the elegant rooms offer 24-hour in-room dining, city views and are fitted with a private marble bathroom. The rooms include flat-screen TV and an iPad to access many hotel leisure and business services. The luxury spa includes 8 treatment rooms and a sauna. There is also a fully equipped fitness center. Four Seasons Hotel London Ten Trinity Square's dining options include a gourmet French restaurant with a 3 Michelin-star chef. There is also vibrant bar and lounge for guests to enjoy. The hotel has excellent transport connections: it is only 2 minute walk to Tower Hill London Underground Station and also 3 minutes to Fenchurch Street Station. Tower Bridge is 1640 feet from Four Seasons Hotel London at Ten Trinity Square, while St Pauls Cathedral is a 20 minute walk away. London City Airport is 6.3 mi from the hotel.
