About Il Campo Marzio

Featuring a bar, Il Campo Marzio is located in the center of Rome, a 8-minute walk from Trevi Fountain. This 4-star hotel offers a concierge service and a tour desk. The property has a 24-hour front desk, airport transportation, room service and free WiFi throughout the property. Rooms have a private bathroom, while certain rooms come with a terrace and others also feature a city view. Popular points of interest near the hotel include Piazza Navona, Spanish Steps and Via Condotti. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino Airport, 10 miles from Il Campo Marzio.
