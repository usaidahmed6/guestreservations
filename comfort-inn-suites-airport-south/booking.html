About Comfort Inn And Suites Airport

Offering an indoor pool, hot tub and waterslide, Comfort Inn & Suites Airport Calgary is located just 10 minutes from the Calgary International Airport and the city center. Free WiFi is available. A flat-screen cable TV is available in each nonsmoking guest room and suite. A desk and coffee making facilities are provided. A private bathroom includes free toiletries and a hairdryer. A daily hot breakfast is served to guests of the Calgary Airport Comfort Inn. A fitness center is provided on site. For business travelers, a business center and meeting rooms are available. Free parking is offered. McCall Lake Golf Course is less than 1.2 mi away. University of Calgary is 17 minutes drive from the property.
