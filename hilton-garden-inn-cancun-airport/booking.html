About Hilton Garden Inn Cancun Airport

Located in Cancún, 5.8 miles from Cancun Underwater Museum, Hilton Garden Inn Cancun Airport provides accommodations with an outdoor swimming pool, free private parking, a fitness center and a terrace. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, free shuttle service and room service, along with free WiFi throughout the property. The property has an ATM, and luggage storage for guests. Each room comes with air conditioning and a flat-screen TV, and certain rooms at the hotel have a safety deposit box. Guest rooms will provide guests with a minibar. Hilton Garden Inn Cancun Airport offers a buffet or American breakfast. Guests can use the business center or relax in the bar. Speaking English and Spanish, staff at the 24-hour front desk can help you plan your stay. Beto Avila Stadium is 10 miles from the accommodation, while Cancun Government Palace is 11 miles away. The nearest airport is Cancún International Airport, a few steps from Hilton Garden Inn Cancun Airport.
