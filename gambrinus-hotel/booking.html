About Hotel Gambrinus

Gambrinus Hotel is in a quiet, residential area near Porta Pia, just a 15 minute walk from the Spanish Steps and Via Veneto. The charming and elegant rooms offer free Wi-Fi. The service is personalized. The rooms are furnished in four different styles, and in four dominant colors: red, beige, green and yellow. Each offers a satellite LCD TV with pay-per-view channels, and a teakettle with a selection of tea and coffee. The beautiful breakfast room features original stone-works of the 19th century in the vaulted ceilings, and Roman-travertine floors. A generous buffet is served daily, and includes cereals, bread, pastries, fresh fruit, eggs, cheese, and cold cuts. The young, multi-language staff will help arrange the stay according to guests needs. Nearby public buses provide services to Termini Train Station, the Coliseum, and the Vatican.
