About Atwell Suites - Miami Brickell, an IHG Hotel

Located in Miami, 1.2 miles from Bayfront Park Station, Atwell Suites - Miami Brickell, an IHG Hotel has accommodations with an outdoor swimming pool, private parking, a fitness center and a shared lounge. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, room service and a 24-hour front desk, along with free WiFi. The property is 0.9 miles from the city center and 1.6 miles from Bayside Market Place. At the hotel all rooms include air conditioning, a desk, a balcony with a city view, a private bathroom, a flat-screen TV, bed linen and towels. All rooms will provide guests with a fridge. Atwell Suites - Miami Brickell, an IHG Hotel has a sun terrace. There's an in-house bar and guests can also use the business area. Bayfront Park is 1.7 miles from the accommodation, while American Airlines Arena is 1.9 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Miami International Airport, 7.5 miles from Atwell Suites - Miami Brickell, an IHG Hotel.
