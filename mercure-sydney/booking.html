About Mercure Sydney

Refurbished in 2021, Mercure Sydney offers contemporary, spacious and light-filled rooms with renovated bathrooms, bedside USB ports, rain showers and a dedicated work space. Some rooms feature city views or a balcony. Located just a 4-minute walk from Central Station in the CBD (Central Business District), Mercure Sydney offers a heated rooftop swimming pool with panoramic city views, a sauna and a fitness center. The hotel is centrally located with bus, train and light-rail connections all within a 3-minute walk away. Sydney's iconic locations are easily accessible from the hotel, with both Chinatown and Darling Harbor just a 10 minute walk from the property, ICC Sydney is a 15-minute walk away and the Sydney Opera House and Harbor Bridge just 15 minutes' by train or light rail. Sydney International Airport is 20 minutes’ drive from Mercure Sydney, or just 4 stops away on the train line. Platform 818 Restaurant and Bar serves Australian and international cuisine, with a wide selection of bespoke cocktails and local wines. Eve's Bar offers craft beers and classic pub food, or you can take advantage of the hotel's 24-hour room service. The Mercure concierge team can provide sightseeing advice and assist guests with travel arrangements. The 24-hour reception can arrange car rental and dry cleaning services.
