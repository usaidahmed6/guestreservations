About Richmond Hotel Premier Tokyo Oshiage - Vacation STAY 38423v

Located in Tokyo, within a 3-minute walk of Tokyo Skytree and 700 yards of Kawashima Knit Museum, Richmond Hotel Premier Tokyo Oshiage - Vacation STAY 38423v provides accommodations with free WiFi throughout the property. The property is around a 10-minute walk from Sumida Cultural Museum, half a mile from Mimeguri Shrine and a 10-minute walk from Ushijima Shrine. The property is an 11-minute walk from Hatonomachidori Shopping Street and 0.6 miles from Kofukuji Temple. All guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a TV. Guest rooms at Richmond Hotel Premier Tokyo Oshiage - Vacation STAY 38423v include air conditioning and a desk. A buffet breakfast is available daily at the accommodation. Popular points of interest near Richmond Hotel Premier Tokyo Oshiage - Vacation STAY 38423v include Kamejimashogakko Memorial Park, Tobacco & Salt Museum and Tsukada Kobo. The nearest airport is Tokyo International Airport, 11 miles from the hotel.
