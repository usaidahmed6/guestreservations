About London Marriott Hotel Maida Vale

Located in the leafy Maida Vale neighborhood, this modern hotel is 6 minutes’ walk from Kilburn Park Tube Station (on the Bakerloo Line), providing access to central London stops including Oxford Circus and Piccadilly Circus within 15 minutes. The bedrooms and suites feature Apple TV, additional power sockets and an upgraded entertainment system. The e-suites have walk-in showers and enhanced lighting. The onsite Carluccio's Restaurant serves a broad range of authentic Italian cuisine and is open for breakfast, lunch and dinner. Free WiFi is available in public areas. Local attractions include Lord’s Cricket Ground, Little Venice area, the famous Abbey Road Studios and bustling Camden Market. The property is also the closest Marriott hotel to Wembley Stadium and Arena, with great transport links via the nearby London Overground line, just 3 minutes’ walk. This hotel provides secure underground parking and is located outside the congestion charging zone. Guests can enjoy complimentary access to our partner leisure complex, Bannatyne’s, located next door to the hotel, with extensive fitness facilities, indoor pool, sauna, and steam room.
