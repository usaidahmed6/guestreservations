About ibis budget Hotel Edinburgh Park

Convenient Comfort at ibis Budget Hotel Edinburgh Park

Located just an 8-minute drive from Edinburgh Airport, ibis Budget Hotel Edinburgh Park is a prime choice for travelers seeking efficiency and comfort. Nestled near the City of Edinburgh Bypass and within easy reach of Edinburgh Park Train Station, this hotel offers a strategic base for exploring the historic and vibrant city of Edinburgh.

  • Direct tram access to Edinburgh Airport and the city center from Gyle Center Tram Stop, located opposite the hotel.
  • Complimentary high-speed WiFi throughout the hotel, ensuring guests stay connected.
  • Air-conditioned rooms equipped with flat-screen TVs and en suite showers for a comfortable and convenient stay.
  • Continental breakfast buffet available daily, providing a fresh start to your mornings.
  • Vending machines offering drinks and snacks 24/7 for quick and easy refreshment.

ibis Budget Hotel Edinburgh Park is more than just a place to stay; it's a gateway to experiencing Edinburgh's rich cultural tapestry. With direct rail services from Edinburgh Park, guests can find themselves in the city center in approximately 7 minutes, making it effortless to explore iconic attractions such as Edinburgh Zoo and Edinburgh Castle.

Whether in town for business, a quick layover, or a leisurely exploration of the Scottish capital, ibis Budget Hotel Edinburgh Park provides a no-frills, comfortable accommodation option. It combines convenience, comfort, and value, making it an ideal choice for budget-conscious travelers looking to maximize their stay in Edinburgh.
