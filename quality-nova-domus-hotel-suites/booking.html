About Quality Hotel Nova Domus

Nova Domus Hotel features a terrace and is just a 5 minute walk from the Vatican. Its Le Terrazze restaurant serves the best of Roman and Italian cuisine. All rooms at Hotel Nova Domus come with carpet floors, individually controlled air conditioning, and satellite TV. Some rooms have a private balcony with views of Rome's Prati district. The area is famous for its many shops and boutiques. Cipro - Musei Vaticani Metro station is a quarter mile away, and provides direct services to Roma Termini. Breakfast includes sweet and savory food. From spring to fall, all meals are served on the rooftop terrace of Quality Hotel Nova Domus.
