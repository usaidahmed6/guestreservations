About The Elser Hotel Miami

Located in Miami, a 6-minute walk from American Airlines Arena, The Elser Hotel Miami - An All-Suite Hotel provides accommodations with an outdoor swimming pool, private parking, a fitness center and a terrace. This 4-star hotel offers a 24-hour front desk, a concierge service and free WiFi. The property is 500 yards from the city center and a 3-minute walk from Bayside Market Place. At the hotel each room is equipped with air conditioning, a seating area, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a safety deposit box and a private bathroom with a shower, free toiletries and a hairdryer. The Elser Hotel Miami - An All-Suite Hotel has certain rooms with city views, and rooms contain a balcony. At the accommodation every room is equipped with bed linen and towels. At The Elser Hotel Miami - An All-Suite Hotel you'll find a restaurant serving American, Italian and Mediterranean cuisine. Vegetarian, dairy-free and halal options can also be requested. At the hotel guests are welcome to use a hot tub. Popular points of interest near The Elser Hotel Miami - An All-Suite Hotel include Bayfront Park, Bayfront Park Station and Adrienne Arsht Center for the Performing Arts. The nearest airport is Miami International Airport, 6.8 miles from the accommodation.
