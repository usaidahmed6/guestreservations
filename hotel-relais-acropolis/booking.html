About Hotel Relais Acropolis

Hôtel Relais Acropolis is set in the heart of Nice, a 10-minute walk from Nice's Old Town and 492 feet from the Acropolis Convention Center. Guest rooms at the Relais Acropolis all feature free WiFi access. The beach is a 20-minute walk away. The modern air-conditioned rooms include a flat-screen TV. The private bathrooms are equipped with a bathtub and all of the rooms are serviced by a elevator. A buffet breakfast is served daily in the dining area. The hotel offers a 24-hour reception. Acropolis Tram Stop is located next to the hotel. Le Relais Acropolis is a 15-minute drive from Nice International Airport and a 10-minute drive from A8 motorway.
