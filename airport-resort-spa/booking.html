About Airport Resort & Spa - SHA Extra Plus

Airport Resort & Spa - SHA Extra Plus is nestled within a tropical garden on the tranquil Nai Yang Beach. It offers free shuttle services to Phuket International Airport, a large pool and elegant accommodations with private balconies. Rooms are bright and airy with large windows that allow the warm tropical breeze in. They have cable TV, a mini-bar and a bathtub in the bathroom. Guests can have a relaxing day soaking in the outdoor spa pool, or enjoy strolls within the resort’s gardens. There is a tour desk on site for guests who wish to explore the area. The restaurant serves a varierty of Thai and international dishes. Room service is available. Airport Resort & Spa - SHA Extra Plus is a 5-minute drive from Phuket Airport and about 16 mi from Phuket Town.
