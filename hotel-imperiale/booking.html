About Hotel Imperiale

Hotel Imperiale by OMNIA hotels is set in a 19th-century building on Rome's Via Veneto, near Barberini Metro Station on line A. Rooms are luxurious and decorated with fine fabrics and the property's interiors are in marble. The Imperiale's rooms feature a shower or hydromassage bath, plus a mini-bar, flat-screen TV and internet access. They are all different sizes and designs, some with armchairs and a small entrance hall. A buffet breakfast is served daily. The Gazebo restaurant is set right on Via Veneto, where tourists and locals come to shop and admire this famous street. Massage services are also available. The Spanish Steps and Villa Borghese park are both a 10-minute walk from the Imperiale Hotel. Termini Station is just 2 Metro stops away.
