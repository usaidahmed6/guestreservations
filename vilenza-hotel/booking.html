About Vilenza Hotel

Located in the Tower Hamlets district in London, Vilenza Hotel offers 3-star rooms with free WiFi. The property is around 1.4 miles from Tower of London, 1.5 miles from Tower Bridge and 2 miles from London Bridge. St Paul's Cathedral is 2 miles from the hotel and London Bridge Tube Station is 2.2 miles away. The rooms at the hotel come with a TV with satellite channels and a kitchenette. At Vilenza Hotel rooms are equipped with bed linen and towels. Popular points of interest near the accommodation include Brick Lane, Sky Garden and Liverpool Street Tube Station. The nearest airport is London City Airport, 5.6 miles from Vilenza Hotel.
