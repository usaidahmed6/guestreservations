About Edinburgh's Dean Village River View Retreat

Located in Edinburgh, 0.9 mi from EICC, Edinburgh's Dean Village River View Retreat B&B provides accommodations with free WiFi. The property features river and garden views, and is 1.4 mi from Murrayfield Stadium. Each unit comes with a safe and a wardrobe. National Museum of Scotland is 1.4 mi from Edinburgh's Dean Village River View Retreat B&B, while University of Edinburgh is 1.5 mi away. The nearest airport is Edinburgh Airport, 7.5 mi from the accommodations.
