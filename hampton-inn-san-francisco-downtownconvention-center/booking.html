About Hampton Inn By Hilton San Francisco Downtown/Convention Center

Featuring a fitness center, Hampton Inn San Francisco Downtown/Convention Center offers rooms with free WiFi. The hotel is conveniently located 2297 feet from Union Square. A cable TV and seating area is provided in each guest room at this hotel. Private bathrooms come with a bath or shower. Free breakfast is served daily at Hampton Inn San Francisco Downtown/Convention Center. A 24-hour reception is available. Other facilities offered at the property include luggage storage. San Francisco City Hall is 0.6 mi away. San Francisco International Airport is 12 mi away.
