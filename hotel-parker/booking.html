About Hotel Parker

Hotel Parker - Gruppo BLAM HOTELS is located 0.6 mi from Roma Termini Railway Station. Rooms are air-conditioned and have a mini-bar and private bathrooms. Rome’s famous historic monuments such as the Coliseum and the Roman Forum are just a 15-minute walk away from Parker Hotel. The Parker is near Porta Maggiore, former city gate which is 656 feet away. From Termini you can catch both Metro lines and buses to anywhere in the city.

  • Non-Smoking Facility
