About art'otel London Battersea Power Station, Powered by Radisson Hotels

Artotel London Battersea Power Station features a restaurant, caf, sauna, Wi-Fi, treatment rooms, gym and an art gallery. The accommodations features a 24-hour front desk, a concierge service, room service and a luggage storage for guests. The air-conditioned rooms offer coffee and tea facilities, a mini-bar, a safety deposit box and a smart TV. The bathroom comes with a shower, hairdryer, bathrobes and slippers. Art'otel London Battersea Power Station, Powered by Radisson Hotels provides A la carte breakfast. The hotel is a 2-minute walk from the Battersea Power Station Zone 1 underground station and 10 minutes from Vauxhall Station. The hotel features a rooftop pool and bar, located on the 16th floor with 360-degree views Houses of Parliament is 1.9 mi from the hotel while Victoria Train Station is 2 mi from the property. The nearest airport is London City Airport, 11 mi from art'otel London Battersea Power Station, Powered by Radisson Hotels.
