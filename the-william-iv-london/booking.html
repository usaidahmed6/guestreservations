About The William IV

A 5-minute walk from Kensal Green Underground Station and less than 30 minutes walk from Notting Hill, this hotel offers free WiFi. The citys main attractions are within easy reach on the Tube. Hyde Park and Buckingham Palace are a 10-minute drive from William IV, and Wembley Stadium can be reached in 15 minutes by car. Elegant, boutique-style rooms all have a TV and an private bathroom. Kensal Green tube station is located nearby.
