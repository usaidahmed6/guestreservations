About Hotel Santa Prisca

Located on the Aventino, one of Rome's 7 hills, Santa Prisca is 10 minutes' walk from FAO headquarters and 1312 feet from Piramide Metro Station. Parking and internet are free. Hotel Santa Prisca is set in a central yet quiet area of Rome, next to a large park and 2297 feet from Giardino degli Aranci, a garden with breathtaking views across the historic center. All air conditioned, rooms here also feature satellite TV, free wired internet access and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and toiletries. At Hotel Santa Prisca guests can enjoy a continental breakfast and characteristic italian pastries. A selection of gluten free and lactose free products is also available. Breakfast service is open from 7am until 10am. The Santa Prisca Hotel is 20 minutes' walk from the Coliseum and just 5 minutes on foot from the lively Testaccio area, full of wine bars, restaurants and discos.
