About Quality Inn Miami South

Quality Inn Miami South at The Falls is conveniently located less than five miles from major attractions like Jungle Island, Fairchild Tropical Botanic Garden, the world-famous Coral Castle and the Miami Metrozoo. The hotel is close to area beaches, including Matheson Hammock Park and Marina, South Beach and only a short distance from two unique national parks: the Everglades National Park and Biscayne National Park. The Miami International Airport and Kendall-Tamiami Executive Airport are close to the hotel. Guests will enjoy the many specialty shops of The Falls, including Macy's and Bloomingdale's. There are several restaurants and theaters in the area. The hotel is within walking distance from several fast food places. All spacious guest rooms feature cable television with HBO and coffee makers with first-class coffee and condiments. In addition to standard amenities, some rooms have refrigerators. Nonsmoking rooms and guest laundry facilities are available. Full-service hotel features and amenities include free continental breakfast, free coffee in the lobby, free local calls and outdoor heated pool. Business travelers will appreciate modern conveniences like speaker phones, free high-speed Internet access, access to a fax machine and competitive corporate rates. The hotel offers a meeting room that accommodates up to 56 people. This is a pet-friendly hotel.
