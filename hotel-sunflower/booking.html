About Hotel Sunflower

Just 1.2 mi from FieraMilanoCity's Portello Exhibition Space, this 4-star hotel offers rooms with Wi-Fi access and flat-screen TV. Politecnico di Milano University is a 15-minute walk away. All modern rooms at the Sunflower have a simple wooden furnishing. Each has a private bathroom complete with hairdryer and slippers. A continental breakfast is served daily, and includes cold meats, cheeses and croissants. The hotel bar is open for coffee and drinks throughout the day. Trains to the city center stop at Bovisa Station, 2461 feet away. The A4 Milan-Turin Motorway is a 10-minute drive away. Transfers to and from Milan's main airports can be arranged on request. The Rho Fiera and Area Expo exhibition centers are both within 7.5 mi.
