About Executive Residency by Best Western Calgary City View North

Located in Calgary, 6.6 miles from CrossIron Mills, Executive Residency by Best Western Calgary City View North provides accommodations with a fitness center, free private parking, a restaurant and a bar. Featuring a concierge service, this property also provides guests with a sun terrace. The property has a 24-hour front desk, airport transportation, room service and free WiFi throughout the property. The hotel will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms with a desk, a coffee machine, a fridge, a dishwasher, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a shower. At Executive Residency by Best Western Calgary City View North all rooms come with bed linen and towels. Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a buffet breakfast. Executive Residency by Best Western Calgary City View North offers 3-star accommodations with an indoor pool and hot tub. A business center and vending machines with snacks and drinks are available on site at the hotel. Crowchild Twin Arena is 8.3 miles from Executive Residency by Best Western Calgary City View North, while McMahon Stadium is 9.2 miles away. The nearest airport is Calgary International Airport, 5 miles from the accommodation.
