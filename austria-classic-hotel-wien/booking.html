About Austria Classic Hotel Wien

Austria Classic Hotel Wien is only 656 feet away from an underground station with direct connections to the city center. The hotel is entirely air-conditioned and offers free WiFi access. Located close to the Prater amusement park, Hotel Wien has been owned by the same family for 200 years. The modern and spacious rooms all have flat-screen TVs. Most face the quiet courtyard. The sauna, the fitness center, and an internet terminal are available free of charge. The rich breakfast buffet is available from 06:30 to 10:30. Austria Classic Hotel Wien also has a roof terrace. The city center, the Austria Center (conference center), and the UN headquarters can be reached in just a few minutes from the nearby Praterstern underground station (lines U1 and U2). There are also direct trains to the airport. The Prater with the Riesenrad (ferris wheel) and the Wiener Messe (exhibition center) are only a few steps away from Austria Classic Hotel Wien.
