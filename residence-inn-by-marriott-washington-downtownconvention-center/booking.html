About Residence Inn by Marriott Washington Downtown/Convention Center

Located in Washington, D.C., a 3-minute walk from Walter E Washington Convention Center, Residence Inn by Marriott Washington Downtown/Convention Center has accommodations with free bikes, private parking, a fitness center and a shared lounge. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, room service and a 24-hour front desk, along with free WiFi. The property is 0.7 miles from the city center and 1.1 miles from The National Mall. At the hotel rooms include air conditioning, a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a safety deposit box and a private bathroom with a bath, free toiletries and a hairdryer. Rooms contain a coffee machine, while certain rooms have a kitchen with a fridge, a dishwasher and a microwave. At Residence Inn by Marriott Washington Downtown/Convention Center every room is equipped with bed linen and towels. Breakfast is available each morning, and includes buffet, continental and American options. The accommodation has a terrace. Guests can use the business center or relax in the bar. Popular points of interest near Residence Inn by Marriott Washington Downtown/Convention Center include National Gallery of Art, Washington Union Station and The White House. The nearest airport is Ronald Reagan Washington National Airport, 6.8 miles from the hotel.
