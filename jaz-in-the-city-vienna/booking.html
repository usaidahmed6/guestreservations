About Jaz in the City Vienna

Jaz in the City Vienna has a fitness center, shared lounge, a restaurant and bar in Vienna. Built in 2021, this 4-star hotel is within a 8-minute walk of Leopold Museum and 0.7 miles of Natural History Museum. Offering free WiFi throughout the property, the non-smoking hotel features a sauna and nightclub. All guest rooms at the hotel come with a seating area. Complete with a private bathroom equipped with a shower and free toiletries, the rooms at Jaz in the City Vienna have a TV and air conditioning, and some rooms contain a terrace. At the accommodation rooms are equipped with bed linen and towels. Jaz in the City Vienna offers a buffet or American breakfast. Round-the-clock assistance is available at the reception, where staff speak German and English. Popular points of interest near the hotel include Vienna State Opera, Kunsthistorisches Museum and Parliament of Austria. The nearest airport is Vienna International Airport, 12 miles from Jaz in the City Vienna.
