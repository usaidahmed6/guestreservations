About Hotel Tuscolana

In the friendly neighborhood of San Giovanni, Hotel Tuscolana is 260 yards from Tuscolana Train Station, with direct services to Fiumicino Airport. Ponte Lungo Metro Station is a 5-minute walk away. Air-conditioned rooms at the Tuscolana Hotel each come equipped with a TV and radio, as well as a fully equipped private bathroom. Wi-Fi is free. The hotel features a TV lounge with bar, and a reading room. The front desk is open 24 hours a day.
