About Cardilli Luxury Rooms

Cardilli Luxury Rooms is located on central Rome's Via Milano avenue, near the exclusive shopping on Via Nazionale and the Opera House. This stylish b&b is on the 4th floor of a historic noble building. The Cardilli offers spacious rooms with antique furniture and parquet floors. Each room comes with air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, and LCD TV, and some rooms feature a hot tub. Breakfast is served in a large dining room, with TV and radio. Guests receive the keys to the building and the room.

Amenities

  • Hot Tub or Spa
  • Concierge
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • Non-Smoking Facility
