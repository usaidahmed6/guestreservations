About Goldstar Suites

Goldstar Resort & Suites offers apartments and studios in the center of Nice, just 820 feet from the beach. Free high-speed WiFi access is available throughout the entire hotel. The air-conditioned units feature a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a kitchenette with a microwave and a dishwasher. Most units have a separate bedroom and a living area with a sofa bed. Household essentials are supplied and bed linen and towels are refreshed once a week during long stays. Nice's central train station is a 10-minute walk from Goldstar Resort & Suites. Alsace Lorraine Tram Stop is 5 minutes, walk away, which provides direct access to Nice Côte d’Azur Airport, 3.5 mi away. Private secure parking is available on site for an extra charge.

Amenities

  • 24 hour front desk
