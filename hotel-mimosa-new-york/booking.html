About Hotel Mimosa Pantheon

Just 492 feet from the Pantheon in the heart of Rome, Hotel Mimosa is a 5-minute walk from Piazza Navona. It offers modern air-conditioned rooms with a private bathroom and free WiFi throughout. A shared computer with internet connection is available in the lobby. Set on the second floor of a historical building, Hotel Mimosa Pantheon is 10 minutes on foot from the Trevi Fountain. A shuttle service is available to Fiumicino Airport and Termini Train Station.

Amenities

  • Room Service
  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • Non-Smoking Facility
