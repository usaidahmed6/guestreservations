About Hotel Nikko San Francisco

Discover the Heart of San Francisco at Hotel Nikko

Attractively located in the bustling center of San Francisco, Hotel Nikko San Francisco offers an exceptional stay just a 4-minute walk from Union Square and 0.7 miles from the Moscone Center. This 5-star hotel provides stunning city views from every room and features a range of amenities to enhance your visit. With convenient access to top attractions and modern comforts, Hotel Nikko San Francisco is the perfect base for exploring the vibrant city.

  • Spacious Terrace with Panoramic City Views
  • Bar Offering Signature Cocktails and Local Beverages
  • On-site Restaurant Serving American and Japanese Cuisine with Vegetarian, Dairy-Free, and Vegan Options
  • Multilingual Staff Available 24/7
  • Private Parking Facilities

Each room at Hotel Nikko San Francisco is designed with comfort and convenience in mind. Guests can enjoy air conditioning, a seating area, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a safety deposit box, and a private bathroom equipped with free toiletries and a hairdryer. Bed linen and towels are provided, ensuring a restful stay.

Hotel Nikko San Francisco's prime location makes it easy to visit nearby attractions such as San Francisco City Hall, just a 16-minute walk away, and Oracle Park, located 1.6 miles from the property. For travelers flying in, the hotel is situated 12 miles from San Francisco International Airport, offering an ideal starting point for both leisure and business visitors.

Book your stay at Hotel Nikko San Francisco and experience the dynamic energy and cultural richness of one of America's most iconic cities.
