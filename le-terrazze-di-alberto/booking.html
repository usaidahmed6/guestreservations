About Le terrazze di Alberto

Le terrazze di Alberto offers accommodations in Rome, 2.1 miles from Porta Maggiore and 2.4 miles from Vittorio Emanuele Metro Station. There's a private entrance at the bed and breakfast for the convenience of those who stay. Free Wifi is available, as well as a terrace and a restaurant. The property also offers accommodation for disabled guests. The bed and breakfast will provide guests with air-conditioned units with a desk, a kettle, a stovetop, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV, a balcony, and a private bathroom with a bidet. Some units also have a well-equipped kitchenette with a microwave, a fridge, and kitchenware. At the bed and breakfast, each unit is fitted with bed linen and towels. There is a coffee shop, and a mini-market is also available. A car rental service is available at the bed and breakfast. San Giovanni Metro Station is 2.5 miles from Le terrazze di Alberto, while Ponte Lungo Metro Station is 2.6 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino, 10 miles from the accommodation, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
