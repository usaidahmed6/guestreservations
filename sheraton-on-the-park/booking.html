About Sheraton Grand Sydney Hyde Park

Sheraton Grand Sydney Hyde Park: A Central Haven in the Heart of Sydney

Situated in the vibrant Sydney CBD, Sheraton Grand Sydney Hyde Park offers an unparalleled location just steps away from key transport hubs and the city's most iconic landmarks. A brief stroll from St. James Station and Town Hall Station, the hotel provides easy access to the bustling cityscape, including the Westfield shopping center, St. Marys Cathedral, Royal Botanic Garden, and the world-renowned Sydney Opera House.

The fully refurbished rooms and suites at Sheraton Grand Sydney Hyde Park present a perfect blend of comfort and modernity, featuring separate bathtubs and showers with stunning views of the Sydney CBD or Hyde Park. Select suites offer front-facing views of Hyde Park, complete with private balconies and spa baths, ideal for those seeking a bit of extra indulgence.

The Gallery at Sheraton Grand Sydney Hyde Park is designed as a versatile social space, perfect for casual meetings, enjoying a morning coffee, or savoring an afternoon tea. Guests can rejuvenate at the rooftop Health Club, which boasts an indoor heated pool, a 24-hour fitness center, and an award-winning day spa, ensuring a well-rounded experience of relaxation and wellness.

For an exclusive experience, visit the Sheraton Club on Level 21. Enjoy panoramic views of Hyde Park, alongside a delightful continental breakfast and evening drinks with canaps.

  • Prime location in Sydney CBD, steps from major attractions and transport hubs
  • Fully refurbished rooms with picturesque views of Sydney CBD and Hyde Park
  • The Gallery: a versatile space for dining and socializing
  • Rooftop Health Club with indoor heated pool, fitness center, and day spa
  • Exclusive Sheraton Club access with breathtaking views and complimentary refreshments

Discover a seamless blend of convenience and comfort at Sheraton Grand Sydney Hyde Park, where every stay is tailored to meet the needs of today's traveler.
