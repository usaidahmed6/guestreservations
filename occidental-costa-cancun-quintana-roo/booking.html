About Occidental Costa Cancun - All Inclusive

Experience Fun and Relaxation at Occidental Costa Cancun - All Inclusive

Located along the beautiful shores of the Caribbean, Occidental Costa Cancun - All Inclusive offers a perfect getaway for families and travelers seeking a blend of comfort and adventure. This resort provides spacious accommodations, a variety of on-site facilities, and friendly service, ensuring a memorable stay for all guests.

Occidental Costa Cancun features a large outdoor pool, a children's club, and a variety of daily activities, making it an ideal destination for families. Guests can stay active with state-of-the-art fitness facilities or browse the on-site boutiques and gift shop. Dining options abound, with international buffets, classic Mexican dishes, traditional Spanish meals, and quick snacks to satisfy every palate.

  • Large outdoor pool for relaxation and fun
  • Children's club with daily activities
  • Multiple dining options including buffets and specialty cuisines
  • On-site fitness facilities and boutiques
  • Convenient car rental services and travel agency for exploring area attractions

The resort's convenient location allows easy access to nearby attractions, including the ancient city of Chichen Itza and the Mayan ruins. With on-site car rental services and a travel agency, guests can effortlessly explore the rich history and culture of the region. A small environmental fee will be requested at check-in, ensuring the preservation of the beautiful surroundings. Enjoy a fun-filled and relaxing vacation at Occidental Costa Cancun - All Inclusive.
