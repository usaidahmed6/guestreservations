About Boutique Centrale Palace

Boutique Centrale Palace Hotel is nestled in the heart of Rome, 1969 feet from Trevi Fountain and a 5-minute walk from Spagna Metro Station. It offers free WiFi and modern rooms with attached bathroom. All rooms feature a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a coffee machine, mini-bar and parquet floors. Some rooms have a balcony and some include a spa bath. The property offers a shared lounge and a bar. Guests can enjoy the breakfast buffet every morning. The Spanish Steps are a 5-minute walk from Boutique Central Palace, which is around the corner from the lively Via del Corso shopping street. You can reach most of Rome's landmarks on foot.
