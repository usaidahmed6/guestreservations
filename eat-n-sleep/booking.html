About Eat n Sleep

Located in Phuket, 1.8 miles from Chinpracha House, Eat n Sleep has accommodations with a garden, free private parking, a shared lounge and a terrace. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, room service and valet parking, along with free WiFi throughout the property. Every room has a balcony with a city view. At the hotel rooms come with air conditioning, a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a safety deposit box and a private bathroom with a shower, free toiletries and a hairdryer. The rooms are equipped with an electric tea pot, while some rooms include a kitchen with a fridge, a microwave and a minibar. The rooms will provide guests with a toaster. You can play table tennis at this 2-star hotel. Thai Hua Museum is 1.9 miles from Eat n Sleep, while Prince of Songkla University is 2.2 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Phuket International, 20 miles from the accommodation, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
