About Hauptbahnhof Boutique Rooms - Virtual Reception & Self-Checkin

Situated within 700 metres of Vienna Main Railway Station and 1.6 km of Belvedere Palace, Hauptbahnhof Boutique Rooms - Virtual Reception & Self-Checkin features rooms with air conditioning and a private bathroom in Vienna. Located around 2.5 km from Karlskirche, the hotel with free WiFi is also 2.6 km away from Vienna State Opera. The property is non-smoking and is set 1.2 km from Museum of Military History. The rooms in the hotel are equipped with a coffee machine. The private bathroom is fitted with a shower, free toiletries and a hairdryer. At Hauptbahnhof Boutique Rooms - Virtual Reception & Self-Checkin rooms have bed linen and towels. Musikverein is 2.8 km from the accommodation, while Albertina Museum is 3 km from the property. The nearest airport is Vienna International Airport, 18 km from Hauptbahnhof Boutique Rooms - Virtual Reception & Self-Checkin.
