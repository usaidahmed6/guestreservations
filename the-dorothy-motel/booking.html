About The Dorothy Motel

Discover the Charm of The Dorothy Motel in Banff

Experience a delightful stay at The Dorothy Motel, nestled in the heart of Banff, just 1.2 miles from the Banff Park Museum and the Whyte Museum of the Canadian Rockies. This welcoming motel offers a blend of comfort and convenience, ideal for travelers looking to explore the stunning natural beauty of the Canadian Rockies.

The Dorothy Motel provides air-conditioned rooms equipped with a range of amenities to ensure a comfortable stay. Each room features a closet, coffee machine, fridge, microwave, safety deposit box, flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom with a shower. Selected accommodations also offer serene garden views and an electric tea pot for added convenience.

Guests can enjoy a variety of activities in and around Banff, including hiking, skiing, and cycling. After a day of adventure, relax on the motel's terrace and take in the fresh mountain air. With free WiFi available throughout the property, staying connected is easy. The Dorothy Motel is committed to offering a non-smoking environment for all guests.

  • Proximity to Banff Park Museum and Whyte Museum of the Canadian Rockies
  • Convenient access to hiking, skiing, and cycling trails
  • Garden-view rooms available
  • On-site terrace for relaxation
  • Non-smoking rooms for a pleasant stay

Located just 83 miles from Calgary International Airport, The Dorothy Motel is the perfect base for exploring Banff and its surrounding attractions. Whether visiting for a short getaway or an extended vacation, guests will find comfort and convenience at every turn.
