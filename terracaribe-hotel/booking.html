About Terracaribe Boutique Hotel

Just 2 minutes from the ferry port for Isla Mujeres, this downtown Cancn hotel features a large outdoor pool with sun deck. Each air conditioned room at the Terracaribe Hotel Boutique is decorated in a Mediterranean style. Cable TV is available in every room. Terracaribe Hotel has a cafeteria with hours from 07:00 am to 11:00 am

Amenities

  • 24 hour front desk
  • Hair Dryer
