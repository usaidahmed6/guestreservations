About Hilton London Tower Bridge

In the heart of London, Hilton London Tower Bridge has a striking modern exterior and is located just a 5-minute walk from Tower Bridge and opposite The Shard. There is free WiFi in public areas of the building, as well as the Executive Lounge. There are 2 public car parks within a 4-minute stroll from the property. London Bridge Train Station is a 3-minute walk away, and offers access to London’s attractions via the Northern and Jubilee Lines. Canary Wharf, the O2 Arena, and Westminster are all easily accessible. The hotel offers stylish rooms, each with a plasma-screen TV and private bathrooms. Executive rooms include use of the 9th floor Executive Lounge, with a terrace overlooking The Shard and Southbank River. The Executive Lounge serves a complimentary full English breakfast, coffee and tea, evening canapés, and alcoholic and soft beverages. You can also relax in the stylish destination bar, TwoRuba. King's Cross St Pancras Station can be reached in 15 minutes on the Northern Line. Shakespeare’s Globe is within a 10-minute walk.
