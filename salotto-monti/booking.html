About Salotto Monti

Set in the historical center of Rome, Salotto Monti features free WiFi throughout. Guests can enjoy a drink on the panoramic terrace. With air conditioning, rooms come with a TV and some have a balcony. A free smartphone per room is provided. Each has a private bathroom equipped with free toiletries and a hairdryer. Guests can taste local specialties and classic Italian food at the several cafes and restaurants in the immediate surroundings. The Roman Forum and the Coliseum are within a 10-minute walk from Salotto Monti. Cavour Metro Station is 2297 feet away.
