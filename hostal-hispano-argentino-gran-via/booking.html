About Hostal Hispano - Argentino

This hostel is on Madrids famous Gran Va, a short walk from the Puerta del Sol. There is free Wi-Fi in rooms and easy access to the Art Triangle. The Hostel Hispano-Argentino is on the 6th floor of a historic building in central Madrid. The building has a heated garden/terrace with views of Gran Va. Gran Va Metro is only 328 feet away. El Corte Ingls shopping center is also within yards and there are shops, cinemas and restaurants nearby. All rooms at the Hispano-Argentino have full air conditioning and heating. There is a mini-bar and a TV with international channels. The private bathroom has a hairdryer. Bicycle and car rental are available nearby. There is a 24-hour front desk and night security.
