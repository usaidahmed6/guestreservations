About Hotel Stefanie

Step into History at Hotel Stefanie: Vienna's Time-Honored Gem

Embrace the charm of Vienna at the historic Hotel Stefanie, the city's oldest hotel, boasting over 400 years of hospitality excellence. As a proud member of Historic Hotels of Europe, Hotel Stefanie merges timeless elegance with modern comforts, creating an unforgettable experience for guests. Located in the heart of Vienna, just a stone's throw from the iconic St. Stephen's Cathedral and the vibrant Schwedenplatz, the hotel serves as an ideal base for exploring the city's rich cultural tapestry.

Each room at Hotel Stefanie is a blend of tradition and contemporary amenities, offering guests individually adjustable air conditioning, private bathrooms, and Sky satellite TV. Select rooms overlook the serene courtyard garden, providing a peaceful retreat amidst the bustling city.

Key features include:

  • Proximity to Schwedenplatz Underground Station, ensuring easy access to Vienna's attractions.
  • A la carte breakfast and Austrian and international cuisine at Restaurant Stefanie.
  • A picturesque garden restaurant and the cozy hotel bar Stefanie for relaxing evenings.
  • Access to a private parking garage, offering convenience for guests traveling by car.
  • Complimentary WiFi access throughout the hotel, keeping you connected during your stay.

Hotel Stefanie is not just a place to stay; it's a passage through Vienna's history, offering a unique glimpse into the city's past while providing all the amenities needed for a comfortable and memorable visit. From the moment you step into the lobby, you'll be transported to a world where every detail tells a story of elegance and tradition. Discover the heart of Vienna at Hotel Stefanie - where history and hospitality meet.
