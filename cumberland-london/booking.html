About The Cumberland, London

Experience the Heartbeat of London at The Cumberland

Located at the iconic Marble Arch, where Oxford Street meets Park Lane, The Cumberland Hotel offers a vibrant and dynamic stay in the heart of central London. Just steps from Hyde Park, this hotel seamlessly blends comfort, style, and award-winning design to create a memorable experience.

The Cumberland is home to 900 modern rooms, each thoughtfully designed to provide a restful retreat amidst the bustling city. Guests can enhance their stay by opting for the Breakfast and Dinner rates, which include access to SOUND London, the hotel's premier live entertainment and dining venue.

  • Prime location at Marble Arch, near Hyde Park
  • SOUND London offering live music and American-inspired cuisine
  • Nightly live performances from top London talents
  • Renowned breakfast buffet to start your day
  • Immediate access to shopping, museums, and landmarks

Guests can savor American-inspired cuisine at SOUND London, where a 2-course set menu is paired with a signature cocktail and live music performances each night. The hotel's much-praised breakfast buffet is the perfect way to energize before exploring the capital's attractions.

The Cumberland's strategic location provides easy access to top shopping destinations on Oxford Street, world-renowned museums, and iconic landmarks. With a London Underground station right at the doorstep, navigating the city is effortless.

Experience the best of London at The Cumberland Hotel, where comfort, live entertainment, and prime location come together to create an unforgettable stay.
