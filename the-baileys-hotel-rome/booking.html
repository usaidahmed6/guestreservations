About The Bailey's Hotel

Expect quiet, well-appointed accommodations and friendly, personal service at BB Hotels Smarthotel Bailey's. Housed in a charming 18th-century building, BB Hotels Smarthotel Bailey's is well-located for exploring Rome on foot or using public transport. Via Veneto and the nearest Metro are less than 10 minutes' walk away. BB Hotels Smarthotel Bailey's prides itself on offering expert hospitality. The Bailey's rooms are spacious, with satellite TV, mini-bars and Wi-Fi access available.
