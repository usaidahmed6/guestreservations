About Suites Del Ángel

Suites del Ángel is located in the heart of Mexico City, 787 feet from the Angel of Independence Monument. It offers modern suites with kitchenettes, air conditioning and cable TV. This hotel offers free WiFi in all rooms. Featuring modern décor with parquet floors and warm colors, each suite features a seating area with sofa bed. The kitchens include hob, microwave and fridge. Paseo de la Reforma Avenue can be reached in less than 5 minutes’ walk away, as well as the lively area of Zona Rosa. Bosque de Chapultepec and all its museums is 0.8 mi away. The historic center is 15 minutes’ drive away. Mexico City International Airport is 9.3 mi away.
