About Burton House, Beverly Hills, A Tribute Portfolio Hotel

Located less than a mile from Rodeo Drive, The Burton House Hotel Beverly Hills offers all studio suite accommodations. It features a well-equipped gym. Rooms at the The Burton House Hotel hotel are furnished in a contemporary style with a 55inch LG flat-screen TV and free WiFi. The bedrooms are equipped with crisp linens, thick mattresses and fluffy pillows, while the kitchens feature a microwave, a coffee machine and an refrigerator Guests can cook their own food or discover many fine dining options along Wilshire and Beverly Boulevards. Join us in Emerald Lounge , in the evening featuring signature cocktails and lite bites, or for a great breakfast to start you day. Popular nearby attractions include Rancho Park and Golf Course and the Los Angeles Country Club, both within a 5-minute drive from the Burton House Beverly Hills hotel.
