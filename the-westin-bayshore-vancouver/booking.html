About The Westin Bayshore, Vancouver

The Westin Bayshore, Vancouver: Urban Serenity by the Waterfront

Discover the perfect blend of urban excitement and natural beauty at The Westin Bayshore, Vancouver. Situated on the waterfront, this resort-style hotel provides direct access to the Vancouver Seawall and the iconic Stanley Park, making it an ideal base for exploring the city's stunning surroundings. The Westin Bayshore offers newly renovated guestrooms and suites with floor-to-ceiling windows, showcasing magnificent views of the harbor, mountains, and city skyline.

Guests can indulge in a variety of dining experiences at the hotel's revitalized restaurant venues. Enjoy handcrafted cocktails and local delicacies at H2 Kitchen & Bar, H Tasting Lounge, and T & Co Cafe, all featuring picturesque outdoor dining options. The on-site spa and wellness programs, including the WestinWORKOUT fitness center, Just Add Water Yoga Studio, and Liquid Yoga classes, ensure a rejuvenating stay.

The hotel also features both outdoor (heated) and indoor pools, providing relaxation and recreation year-round. Enhanced cleaning protocols and safety measures are in place to prioritize the welfare of guests and associates, offering peace of mind during your stay. Complimentary BikeWESTIN rentals allow guests to explore the city on two wheels, adding an active element to the visit.

  • Direct access to Vancouver Seawall and Stanley Park
  • Newly renovated guestrooms with floor-to-ceiling windows
  • Revitalized dining venues: H2 Kitchen & Bar, H Tasting Lounge, and T & Co Cafe
  • Outdoor (heated) and indoor pools
  • Comprehensive wellness programs, including yoga and fitness classes

Experience the unique blend of urban life and natural beauty at The Westin Bayshore, Vancouver, where unforgettable moments and serene surroundings await.
