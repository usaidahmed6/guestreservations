About Hotel Mercure Parramatta

Discover Elegance and Convenience at Hotel Mercure Parramatta

Nestled opposite the picturesque Rosehill Gardens Racecourse and a stone's throw from one of Australia's largest shopping centers, Hotel Mercure Parramatta presents a blend of luxury, leisure, and location. Offering sophisticated accommodations with a suite of premium facilities, the hotel is a sanctuary for travelers seeking a vibrant stay in Parramatta. Whether you're here for business or leisure, enjoy the seamless blend of comfort and convenience, with the city's prime attractions and transport links just moments away.

Key highlights of Hotel Mercure Parramatta include:

  • Elegantly designed rooms furnished to high standards, featuring LCD TVs for your entertainment.
  • A large outdoor pool with a surrounding terrace, perfect for a refreshing dip or sunbathing.
  • On-site tennis court, offering guests the chance to enjoy a game in the beautiful outdoors.
  • Proximity to Parramatta Westfield for an unrivaled shopping experience, just minutes away from the hotel.
  • Convenient location near Parramatta city center, Parramatta Train Station, and the University of Western Sydney, facilitating easy access to explore and commute.

At Hotel Mercure Parramatta, every aspect of your stay is thoughtfully curated to ensure a memorable experience. From the luxurious comfort of the guest rooms to the array of leisure facilities available, guests are invited to relax, unwind, and discover the vibrant city of Parramatta. Whether you're visiting for the races, shopping, or exploring the local culture and education, the hotel serves as the perfect base for all your adventures.

Experience the ultimate in comfort and convenience at Hotel Mercure Parramatta, where every stay is a journey to remember.
