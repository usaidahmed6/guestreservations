About The Madison Hotel

Located in Washington, D.C. and built in 1963, this pet-friendly hotel is a 10-minute walk from the White House. All rooms include a flat-screen cable TV and bathrooms with bathrobes. Free WiFi is available in public area. The Madison Hotel offers a 24-hour fitness center. The business center is also available 24-hours a day. Embassy Row is 3 mi away while Georgetown University is 1.2 mi from the hotel. The Madison is also 1.2 mi from the Washington Monument and 5 minutes' walk from the McPherson Square subway station.
