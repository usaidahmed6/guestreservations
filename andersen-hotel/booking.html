About Andersen Boutique Hotel

Welcome to Andersen Boutique Hotel: A Touch of Modern Design in Copenhagen's Heart

Andersen Boutique Hotel, nestled in the vibrant Vesterbro district, stands out as a beacon of modern design and personalized hospitality, just 656 feet from Copenhagen Central Station. Embracing the essence of the trendy surrounding area, this boutique hotel offers an immersive experience with its meticulously designed rooms and thoughtful amenities, setting the stage for an unforgettable stay in Copenhagen.

Guests at Andersen Boutique Hotel enjoy a range of unique features:

  • Stylish rooms adorned with décor by the award-winning Designers Guild, providing a vibrant and inviting atmosphere.
  • Exclusive Molton Brown toiletries and soundproofed windows in every room, ensuring a luxurious and undisturbed experience.
  • A welcoming new lobby area, where guests can indulge in complimentary Nespresso coffee or organic Emeyu tea.
  • A charming terrace for relaxation and bicycle rentals for those who wish to explore the city in a unique way.
  • Proximity to the galleries, restaurants, and bars of the popular Kødbyen (Meatpacking District) and a short 7-minute walk to the Tivoli Gardens Amusement Park.

Each room at the Andersen Boutique Hotel is a haven of comfort, featuring a mini-bar, desk, and safe, alongside a modern LED TV and free Wi-Fi, ensuring that every stay is as convenient as it is comfortable. Whether in town for business or leisure, guests are placed in the heart of Copenhagen's creativity and culture.

The Andersen Boutique Hotel is more than just a place to stay; it's a gateway to the full Copenhagen experience. Within minutes, guests can transition from the peaceful ambiance of their design-inspired room to the bustling streets of Vesterbro, filled with artistic expression and culinary delights. Discover the perfect blend of contemporary design and authentic local culture at Andersen Boutique Hotel.
