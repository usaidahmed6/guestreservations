About Cambria Hotel Fort Lauderdale Beach

The newly opened Cambria Hotel Ft. Lauderdale Beach offers a relaxed, contemporary design with all the modern day amenities expected by today's sophisticated traveler. Cambria Ft. Lauderdale Beach is the perfect destination for both leisure and business travel while in the greater Ft. Lauderdale area. Located directly across from the Atlantic Ocean, Cambria Ft. Lauderdale Beach provides easy access to local attractions, beaches, area businesses, hospitals and specilaty medical facilities.

Cambria Ft. Lauderdale Beach offers 97 comfortably appointed guest rooms featuring spacious baths, outdoor swimming pool with sunning deck, the Melina Ballroom with 2500 sq. ft. of event space, lobby bar, rooftop bar, fitness center, on-site valet parking and direct access to Ft. Lauderdale Beach.
