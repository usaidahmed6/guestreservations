About DoubleTree by Hilton Edinburgh Airport

This DoubleTree by Hilton is set a 7-minute walk from Edinburgh Airport. It features a sauna, a gym and a fine restaurant. Rooms at the DoubleTree by Hilton Edinburgh Airport are light and airy and have large opening windows. They include a flat-screen TV, internet access and a work desk. There is also a 24-hour room service menu. Combining a lounge, bar and elegant restaurant, The Space offers all-day dining with free WiFi. It serves international and Scottish cuisine, including locally sourced steaks. The bar has a wide selection of whiskeys. The health and fitness center has a beauty salon and offers a variety of aerobic classes every day. Edinburgh Zoo and Murrayfield Stadium are both within a 10-minute drive. The city center is 10 miles away and has a wealth of attractions including Edinburgh Castle and the Royal Mile.
