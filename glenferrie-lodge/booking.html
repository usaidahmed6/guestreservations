About Glenferrie Lodge

Welcome to Glenferrie Lodge: A Serene Harbor Retreat

Glenferrie Lodge offers a peaceful escape in a picturesque setting on Sydney Harbor, combining comfortable accommodations with the beauty of lush gardens and stunning water views. Just a minutes walk from Kirribilli Wharf, guests have quick ferry access to Circular Quay and the iconic Sydney Opera House.

Rooms at Glenferrie Lodge are well-equipped with amenities such as a ceiling fan, refrigerator, and TV. Select rooms also feature balconies with water views, ironing facilities, and DVD players, enhancing your stay with additional comfort and convenience. The communal lounge area with cable TV and the charming garden area provide perfect spots for relaxation and socializing.

Every morning, guests have the option to enjoy a cooked breakfast in the dining area for an additional charge. The proximity to Sydney Harbor Bridge and Milsons Point Railway Station, along with easy access to Sydney International Airport, 9.3 miles away, makes Glenferrie Lodge an ideal choice for both domestic and international travelers.

  • Tranquil garden settings and balconies with water views
  • One-minute walk to Kirribilli Wharf for easy ferry access
  • Close proximity to Sydney Harbor Bridge and public transport links
  • Communal lounge with cable TV for guest relaxation
  • Cooked breakfast available in the dining area

Experience the charm and convenience of Glenferrie Lodge, where you can unwind in comfort and enjoy easy access to the best of Sydneys attractions. Discover a home away from home at this delightful harbor lodge.
