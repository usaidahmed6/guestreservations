About The Royal Sonesta Washington DC Capitol Hill

Located in Washington, D.C., a 5-minute walk from Washington Union Station, The Royal Sonesta Washington DC Capitol Hill has accommodations with a fitness center, private parking, a restaurant and a bar. With free WiFi, this 5-star hotel offers room service and a 24-hour front desk. The property provides a concierge service, and luggage storage for guests. Popular points of interest near the hotel include National Gallery of Art, National Museum of the American Indian and Supreme Court. The nearest airport is Ronald Reagan Washington National Airport, 6.8 miles from The Royal Sonesta Washington DC Capitol Hill.
