About Ibis Styles Nice Centre Gare

Just a 5-minute walk from Nice-Ville Train Station and the Avenue Jean Medecin high street, Ibis Styles Nice Center Gare offers accommodations in central Nice. It has free WiFi access in each air-conditioned room and guests can relax on the hotel's sunny terrace. Rooms at the Ibis Styles Nice Center Gare are decorated in a beach motif with pastel colors and include a flat-screen TV. All rooms have a private bathroom equipped with a shower. For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hair dryer. Opened in 2015, this Ibis Styles hotel has a 24-hour front desk and a computer for guests. Your luggage can be stored on site while you explore Nice and secured bicycle storage is available free of charge on site. Breakfast is included. The Promenade des Anglais and the beach are a 10-minute walk from the property. Nice Old Town is a 15-minute walk away. Nice Cte d'Azur Airport is 4.3 mi away and can be reached directly by Line 2 of the Nice tramway. Public parking is available 164 feet from the hotel.
