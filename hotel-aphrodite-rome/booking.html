About Hotel Aphrodite

Boasting a rooftop terrace, Hotel Aphrodite offers rooms with attached bathroom with free WiFi. It is next to Roma Termini Station, offering quick metro links to the Spanish Steps and Vatican Museums. Each room at the Aphrodite features pastel colors and wooden floors, and the private bathrooms come with mosaic tiles and marble floors. Breakfast is buffet style. When booking an half-board option, dinner from a partner restaurant is delivered to your room. The hotel is located on the busy Via Marsala, offering traditional and ethnic restaurants, and cafs. Both the Sapienza University and Opera House are a 10-minute walk away. Guests can enjoy discounts for tours and excursions at the hotel's tour agency, located next door.
