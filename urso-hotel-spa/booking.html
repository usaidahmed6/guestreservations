About URSO Hotel & Spa, a Small Luxury Hotel of the World

Set in a restored palace, URSO Hotel & Spa, a Small Luxury Hotel of the World is 5 minutes walk from Alonso Martnez Metro Station. It offers elegant rooms, free WiFi, a spa and gym. Decorated with stylish, designer details, the rooms come with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV and a mini-bar. Each one has a private bathroom equipped with a bath and shower, as well as slippers, bathrobes, toiletries and a hairdryer. The hotel spa includes a water area, hamman, massage service and a gym. Media Racin is the hotel restaurant , a traditional brasserie where traditional Madrid dishes are served. It also has a cellar of more than 650 references. Many dining options are also available in the surrounding streets. Serrano, a popular shopping area, is just 15 minutes walk from the URSO Hotel & Spa, a Small Luxury Hotel of the World. Staff at the 24-hour reception can provide tourist information and a ticket service. Madrids Puerta del Sol and the Retiro Park can be reached in under 20 minutes on foot. Private parking is available at the property for an extra cost.
