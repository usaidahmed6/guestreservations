About Residenza Talenti Superior Rooms

The family-run Residenza Talenti Superior Rooms features a new and modern design and free Wi-Fi throughout. Located in Rome’s Talenti neighborhood, it offers a shared terrace and air-conditioned rooms with a private balcony. Rooms are stylish and come with a 32” LED TV, ceramic floors, and a spacious bathroom with hairdryer and toiletries. Each morning, a sweet buffet breakfast is served in the breakfast room. Savory foods are available on request. A bus stopping in front of Residenza Talenti links with Termini Train Station and Piazza Venezia square. The Spanish Steps, Trevi Fountain and Coliseum are within 6.2 mi of the B&B.
