About Air China Boyue Beijing Hotel

The Boyue Beijing Hotel is in a great location in the Third Embassy District, a 10-minute walk from Liangmaqiao subway station and a 20-minute drive from Beijing Capital International Airport and China International Exhibition Centre. Guests can enjoy soothing massage services on site. Boyue Beijing Hotel is a 15-minute drive from Sanlitun Bar Street, National Agriculture Centre and Temple of Sun Park. It takes 20 minutes by car from the hotel to Tiananmen Square in the center of the city. All its rooms are elegantly decorated and have LCD TV, minibar and high speed wired and wireless internet. Ironing facilities and a safe are also included. The private bathrooms have both a bathtub and shower facilities. At the Boyue Beijing Hotel, there is a fitness center. Other facilities include meeting facilities, a ticket service and a tour desk. The property provides free parking. Guests can enjoy Cantonese dishes at Yihe Chinese Restaurant, or enjoy delightful refreshments at Ting Café.
