About London Olympus Hotel Nirvana

In the heart of Stratford, this hotel is a 2 minute walk from the University of East London and just 10 minutes from Stratford International Rail and Tube stations. There is free Wi-Fi throughout the building. Stratford station has train, Underground, and DLR services across London, so guests can easily explore all of the citys main attractions. Guests at London Olympus Hotel Nirvana can also make use of a bus service to Stansted Airport. Rooms at London Olympus Hotel Nirvana have simple, traditional dcor, and include a tile private bathroom. Each also has a TV, hairdryer and tea and coffee-making facilities. The Theatre Royal is a 10 minute walk away, and the rest of the towns shops and restaurants are also within easy walking distance.

  • 24 hour front desk
