About Rancho Bernardo Inn

Relax and Recharge at Rancho Bernardo Inn

Nestled in the tranquil setting of Rancho Bernardo, just 23 miles from San Diego, the Rancho Bernardo Inn offers a peaceful retreat with a host of premium amenities. This resort is an oasis of relaxation, featuring a year-round outdoor pool, spa center, hot tub, and sauna, perfect for guests seeking a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of daily life.

Each guest room at the Rancho Bernardo Inn is air-conditioned and comes equipped with a flat-screen cable TV for your entertainment. Select rooms boast additional comforts such as a seating area or a private balcony or patio, where guests can unwind in their own personal space. All accommodations include private bathrooms with baths, ensuring privacy and convenience.

The resort caters to a variety of tastes and preferences with a restaurant and bar on-site, where guests can savor delicious meals and refreshing drinks. A 24-hour front desk is available to meet all your needs at any time, and business travelers can take advantage of the conference center for professional engagements.

  • Year-round outdoor pool and full-service spa center
  • On-site dining options and a welcoming bar
  • Luxurious rooms with optional balconies or patios
  • Conference center for business and events
  • Complimentary courtesy vehicle use within an 8-km radius

Located within easy reach of golfing and hiking opportunities, and close to the vibrant cities of Coronado and La Jolla, Rancho Bernardo Inn is the perfect destination for those looking to relax, rejuvenate, and enjoy the natural beauty and activities the area has to offer. Whether you're visiting for leisure or business, the Rancho Bernardo Inn ensures an unforgettable stay with its thoughtful amenities and exceptional service.
