About Apollinaire Nice

Located in Nice city center, 0.6 mi from the beach and the Promenade des Anglais, Hotel Apollinaire offers free WiFi access and a 24-hour front desk. This property is 2133 feet from Place Massena and from Promenade du Paillon Park. All the air-conditioned guest rooms offer a contemporary décor and are equipped with a flat-screen TV, electric kettle, safety deposit box, mini-bar. The private bathroom has a bath or a shower A buffet breakfast is available every morning in the dining room, and can be taken on the terrace upon request. Hotel Apollinaire is 0.6 mi from Nice's Old Town and 0.9 mi from Acropolis Congress Center. Nice Etoile Shopping Center is 820 feet away and Nice-Ville Train Station is 0.6 mi away.
