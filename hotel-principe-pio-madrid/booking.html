About Principe Pio

Facing Madrids Royal Palace and the picturesque Sabatini Gardens, Principe Po is located just off Madrids Plaza de Espaa Square. It offers air-conditioned elegant rooms and free WiFi throughout. Each room comes with a large work desk and a private bathroom with free toiletries. Some rooms also feature views over the Royal Palace and Sabatini Gardens. The hotel offers a 24-hour reception, currency exchange and tour desk. From the hotel you can easily walk to Gran Va, Plaza Mayor and Puerta del Sol full of cultural sights and a range of shopping.
