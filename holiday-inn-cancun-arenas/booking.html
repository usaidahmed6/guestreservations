About Ocean View Cancun Arenas

Steps from the beach, in Cancun's Hotel Zone, surrounded by gardens, nightclubs and restaurants, this hotel offers enjoyable facilities including 3 on-site restaurants and free wireless internet access. Ocean View Cancun Arenas features an outdoor pool with daily activities and nightly entertainment. Guests can relax with a drink from the poolside snack bar or work out in the fitness center. After a day spent at the beach, visitors can relax on their private balcony or terrace. Guests at the Cancun Arenas Holiday Inn will appreciate a variety of restaurant options ranging from casual to fine dining. Arenas buffet-style restaurant is open for breakfast, lunch and dinner. Visitors can choose from beachfront dinning in Ocean Grill Tequila or upscale Italian cuisine in Diletto.
