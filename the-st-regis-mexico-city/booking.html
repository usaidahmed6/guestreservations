About The St. Regis Mexico City

Experience Unmatched Sophistication at The St. Regis Mexico City

Located on the iconic Paseo de la Reforma Boulevard, The St. Regis Mexico City offers a blend of luxury and convenience in the heart of the city. With first-class accommodations, top-tier amenities, and easy access to major attractions, this hotel provides a perfect setting for both leisure and business travelers.

  • Three on-site gourmet restaurants serving a variety of cuisines
  • Indoor swimming pool for year-round enjoyment
  • State-of-the-art in-room amenities, including wireless internet access
  • Exclusive St. Regis butler service for personalized attention
  • Prime location near landmarks such as the Independence Angel and Chapultepec Castle

Guests at The St. Regis Mexico City can indulge in culinary delights at any of the three on-site gourmet restaurants or relax in the indoor swimming pool. The hotel offers state-of-the-art in-room amenities, ensuring a comfortable stay. The iconic St. Regis butler service provides personalized attention, enhancing the overall experience. Located near notable landmarks like the Independence Angel and Chapultepec Castle, and close to international embassies and Bosque de Chapultepec, the hotel is perfectly situated for exploring Mexico City. Whether visiting for business or pleasure, The St. Regis Mexico City offers an unforgettable stay.
