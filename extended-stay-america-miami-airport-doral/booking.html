About Extended Stay America Premier Suites - Miami - Airport - Doral - 87th Avenue South

Miami International Airport is 6.8 mi from this hotel in Florida. Kitchenette facilities are provided in all studios at this hotel. An on-site launderette is also available. Spacious studios are decorated in neutral colors with bright accents at the Extended Stay America - Miami - Airport - Doral - 87th Ave S. Each has a seating area, cable TV and is air conditioned. Free grab-and-go breakfast including breakfast bars, muffins, hot cereal, coffee and tea is served every morning. Weekly housekeeping services are provided for free and daily provided for a fee. Towels and linen can be exchanged at the front desk. The Museum of the Americas is 2 mi from the hotel. Extended Stay America - Miami - Airport - Doral - 87th Ave S is 2 miles from Carlos Albizu University.
