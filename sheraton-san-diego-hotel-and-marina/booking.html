About Sheraton San Diego Hotel And Marina

Offering a free 24-hour transfer service to San Diego International Airport, this eco-friendly hotel features 5 restaurants, 3 outdoor heated pools and an on-site spa. All rooms have flat-screen HDTVs. A work desk and a private patio or balcony are offered in each 100% nonsmoking room at Sheraton San Diego Hotel & Marina. The air-conditioned nautical-themed rooms include ironing facilities, a coffee machine with Starbucks coffee and an private bathroom with a hairdryer and boutique toiletries. Guests of Sheraton San Diego Hotel & Marina can dine at Harbor and Vine where we serve 90 point wines and artisanal signature flat breads made fresh to order. The restaurant offers a patio overlooking the marina and serves tapas-style dishes, creative martinis and Mojitos. Quinn’s offers meals in a sports bar atmosphere, while Shoreline offers poolside drinks and snacks during the summer season. Enjoy casual American cuisine at Harbor’s Edge or stop by the Marina Market featuring free WiFi, Starbucks coffee and fresh sandwiches and salads. San Diego’s vibrant Gaslamp Quarter is 3.2 mi away from the hotel and Little Italy is 2.1 mi away. Balboa Park and the San Diego Zoo are 2.4 mi away. Seaport Village and entertainment venue Humphreys Concerts By The Bay are within 3.1 mi away.
