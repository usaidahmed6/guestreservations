About Woraburi Phuket Resort & Spa

The beachfront Woraburi Phuket Resort & Spa features stunning ocean and landscaped views. The resort features a Thai-architectures throughout and provides free parking and WiFi. Bright and spacious, rooms feature ample seating area and large windows that let in much fresh air. They come with satellite TV, a personal safe and a mini-bar. Featuring sea, pool or garden views, selected room types also comes with direct pool access. Woraburi Phuket is located a 15-minute drive from Patong Beach, 20 minutes from Chalong Temple and 30 minutes from Phuket Town. Phuket International Airport is located approximately a 1-hour drive away. The large outdoor pool stays open all year and has its own swim-up bar. The resort’s tour desk can also organize excursions for those who wish to explore the area and there is also a free shuttle service to Patong beach. International dishes, Asian specialties and a daily buffet breakfast are served at Smile Coffee Shop. Refreshing beverages are served at Silk Bar, the Pool Bar and the Lobby Lounge. Room rates on 31 December include a gala dinner.
