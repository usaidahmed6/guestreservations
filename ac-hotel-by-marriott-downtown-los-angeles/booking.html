About AC Hotel by Marriott Downtown Los Angeles

Located in Los Angeles, a 5-minute walk from Microsoft Theater, AC Hotel by Marriott Downtown Los Angeles has accommodations with free bikes, private parking, a fitness center and a terrace. This 4-star hotel offers a 24-hour front desk, an ATM and free WiFi. Guests can enjoy American and Japanese dishes at the restaurant or have a drink at the bar. Guests at the hotel can enjoy a continental or an American breakfast. A business center and vending machines with snacks and drinks are available on site at AC Hotel by Marriott Downtown Los Angeles. Staples Center is a 5-minute walk from the accommodation, while California Science Center is 2.2 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Hawthorne Municipal Airport, 11 miles from AC Hotel by Marriott Downtown Los Angeles.
