About Casa Luna

Located 1.4 miles from Puerto Juarez Beach, Casa Luna offers an outdoor swimming pool, a shared lounge and air-conditioned accommodations with a patio and free WiFi. Units come with a kitchen with a dining area, and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and shower. A microwave and coffee machine are also available. Popular points of interest near the bed and breakfast include Cancun Bus Station, Cancun Government Palace and Cristo Rey Church. The nearest airport is Cancn International Airport, 11 miles from Casa Luna.
