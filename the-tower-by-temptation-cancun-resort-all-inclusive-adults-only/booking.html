About The Tower by Temptation Cancun Resort - All Inclusive - Adults Only

Boasting a private beach area, Temptation at The Tower Cancún Resort - All Inclusive - Adults Only is located in Cancún and is 3.1 mi from Cancun Government Palace. In a prime location in the Zona Hotelera district, the property is located 3.7 mi from Cancun Bus Station and 4.3 mi from Beto Avila Stadium. The resort has a fitness center and a 24-hour front desk. At the resort, every room has a balcony. Rooms are fitted with a flat-screen TV, and certain rooms at The Tower by Temptation Cancun Reso have a terrace. At the accommodations all rooms have a wardrobe and a private bathroom. A buffet breakfast is served each morning at the property. Guests can grab a bite to eat in the in-house restaurant, which serves International cuisine. Temptation at The Tower Cancún Resort - All Inclusive - Adults Only offers an outdoor pool. Coco Bongo is 5.6 mi from the resort, while La Isla Shopping Mall is 6.8 mi from the property. The nearest airport is Cancún International Airport, 14 mi from The Tower by Temptation Cancun Reso. From 3 or more rooms under the same name, the first night will be charged to ensure that they are not duplicated.
