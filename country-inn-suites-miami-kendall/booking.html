About Country Inn & Suites by Radisson, Miami (Kendall), FL

Conveniently located adjacent to Town & Country Mall in Kendall, this hotel offers comfortable guestrooms and suites southwest of Miami just minutes from shopping, dining and entertainment venues. The Country Inn features a daily complimentary hot breakfast, which provides Belgian waffles, fresh fruit and more. In the afternoon, go for a swim in the outdoor swimming pool or work out in the fitness center. Cable TV and free WiFi are provided in all the rooms at Country Inn & Suites by Radisson, Miami (Kendall), FL. Rooms also come equipped with a microwave, a small refrigerator and tea- and coffee-making facilities. Visitors can explore the Florida wilderness at the Everglades National Park, which is just a short drive from the Miami (Kendall), FL Country Inn and Suites by Radisson. The Miami Metro Zoo and the Gold Coast Railroad Museum are also located nearby.
