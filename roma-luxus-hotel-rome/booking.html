About Roma Luxus Hotel

Roma Luxus Hotel offers free WiFi and stylish rooms in Rome's Monti district. Set 984 feet from the Roman Forum, the property also features a bar and an à la carte restaurant. Rooms at the Luxus Hotel Roma are all spacious and come with a flat-screen TV, electric kettle and air conditioning. Each room includes a private bathroom with bathrobes and slippers. An extensive sweet and savory international buffet breakfast is available daily. It includes eggs, pancakes, cheese and pastries. The hotel is 1312 feet from both Piazza Venezia and Quirinale Palace, while Via Nazionale shopping street can be reached in 1 minute on foot. Rome Ciampino Airport is 9.3 mi away.
