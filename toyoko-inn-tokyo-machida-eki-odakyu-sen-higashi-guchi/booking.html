About Toyoko Inn Tokyo Machida-Eki Odakyu-Sen Higashi-Guchi

Ideally located in the prime touristic area of Hachioji, Toyoko Inn Tokyo Machida-eki Odakyu-sen Higashi-gu promises a relaxing and wonderful visit. The property features a wide range of facilities to make your stay a pleasant experience. Facilities like free Wi-Fi in all rooms, facilities for disabled guests, Wi-Fi in public areas, newspapers, laundry service are readily available for you to enjoy. Designed for comfort, selected guestrooms offer television LCD/plasma screen, internet access – wireless, internet access – wireless (complimentary), non smoking rooms, air conditioning to ensure a restful night. Entertain the hotel's recreational facilities, including massage. No matter what your reasons are for visiting Tokyo, Toyoko Inn Tokyo Machida-eki Odakyu-sen Higashi-gu will make you feel instantly at home.
