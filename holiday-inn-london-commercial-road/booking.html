About Holiday Inn London - Whitechapel

Located in East London. This Holiday Inn hotel is only a 5-minute walk from both Whitechapel and Shadwell Underground Stations. It has a restaurant, a lounge bar and a fitness center with mini gym. All rooms include free Wi-Fi, air-conditioned and come equipped with a flat-screen TV. London’s financial district, central London and London Heathrow Airport can also be reached within 10 minutes using the multiple transport options including the Elizabeth line, Tube, Overground and DLR tube lines. For shopping and leisure, guests can visit the West End and the new Westfield Stratford, both only 10 minutes away. London City Airport is 5 miles away.
