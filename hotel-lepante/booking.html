About Hotel Lepante

Located in Nice City Center, a 15-minute walk from the beach and the Promenade des Anglais, this Belle Ãpoque-style hotel features a shaded rooftop terrace and a bar. Lepante offers air-conditioned guest rooms with free Wi-Fi access. Each of the individually-decorated rooms is equipped with a cable flat-screen TV and a desk. The private bathroom has a bath or shower and a hairdryer. Some rooms also feature a mezzanine level or views of the city. A buffet breakfast is available every morning at an extra charge. Restaurants, bars and shops can be found in the immediate area. Noter-Dame Cathedral of Nice is 1640 feet from Lepante and the historic district is a 10-minute walk away. An airport shuttle can be arranged upon request at an extra charge.
