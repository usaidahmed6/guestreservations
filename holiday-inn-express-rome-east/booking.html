About GP Hotel

A 15-minute drive from Romes center, GP Hotel has free parking and a 24-hour bar. It offers modern air-conditioned rooms with a satellite LED TV and Wi-Fi is free throughout. Rooms at GP Hotel come with carpeted floors and soundproofing. The private bathroom includes a hairdryer and free toiletries. A safe is available at reception. A buffet breakfast of pastries, ham, cheese and boiled eggs is provided daily. Guests can enjoy drinks or snacks at the Caf Bar, which is open daily. The area has a number of restaurants serving local cuisine and Roman thin-crust pizza. Palmiro Togliatti Station is 2953 feet from the GP Hotel. Ciampino Airport is less than 9.3 mi from the property.
