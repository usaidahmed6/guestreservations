About Residence Inn by Marriott Calgary Airport

This Calgary hotel is 7.5 miles from downtown Calgary and the Calgary International Airport. The hotel features an indoor pool and accommodations with a kitchenette. The suites and studios at the Residence Inn by Marriott Calgary Airport feature flat-screen cable TVs. The accommodations also include a sitting area with a sofa bed and work desk. Guests are served a daily breakfast with hot coffee. The hotel also has a fitness center and business center. The Residence Inn by Marriott is a 15-minute drive from the Fort Calgary Historic Park and the Scotiabank Saddledome. The Nose Hill Park is 5.5 miles from the hotel.
