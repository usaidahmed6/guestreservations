About Ibis Budget Madrid Calle 30

Located in Madrid, 1.8 miles from Reina Sofia Museum, Ibis Budget Madrid Calle 30 provides accommodations with a garden, private parking, a shared lounge and a terrace. With free WiFi, this 1-star hotel offers a 24-hour front desk and a tour desk. Plaza Mayor is 2.6 miles from the hotel and Thyssen-Bornemisza Museum is 2.6 miles away. At the hotel, each room comes with a desk, a flat-screen TV, a private bathroom, bed linen and towels. Guests at Ibis Budget Madrid Calle 30 can enjoy a buffet breakfast. Atocha Train Station is 1.8 miles from the accommodation, while Retiro Park is 2 miles away. The nearest airport is Adolfo Suarez Madrid-Barajas Airport, 7.5 miles from Ibis Budget Madrid Calle 30.
