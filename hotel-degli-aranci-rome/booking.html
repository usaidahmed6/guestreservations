About Hotel Degli Aranci

Overlooking Villa Borghese, Hotel Degli Aranci is in Rome's exclusive Parioli neighborhood. It offers elegant rooms plus a restaurant with outdoor dining area. This 19th-century building is surrounded by a garden with orange trees, where it gets its name. It is a 5-minute walk from Euclide Train Station, with links to central Piazza del Popolo. All air-conditioned, rooms come with free Wi-Fi, a TV with pay-per-view channels, a mini-bar, and a private bathroom with hydromassage shower or bath. Some have a balcony. Il Fiore d'Arancio restaurant also serves snacks, drinks and light meals throughout the day. This 4-star hotel is a 15-minute walk from the popular music venue of Auditorium Parco della Musica. Termini Train Station is easily reached by the 223 bus. The Olympic Stadium is 10 minutes away by car.
