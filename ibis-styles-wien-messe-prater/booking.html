About ibis Styles Wien Messe Prater

The ibis Styles Wien Messe Prater hotel lies just a few hundred yards from the Prater park, exhibition center and the city center. Right on the doorstep is the Danube Canal. The hotel is 1312 feet from the Prater and the Giant Ferris Wheel, it is close to the Messe Wien as well as to the Ernst Happel Stadion. Donauinsel can be reached within a few-minute metro ride. The city center is reachable within a 10-minutes walk. The rooms in the hotel are fitted with a flat-screen TV. The private bathroom is equipped with a hair dryer. All rooms at ibis Styles Wien Messe Prater feature air conditioning and a desk. A continental or buffet breakfast is available every morning at the property. The area is popular for cycling, and car hire is available at the accommodations. When guests need guidance on where to visit, the reception will be happy to provide advice. The nearest airport is Vienna International Airport, 9.9 mi from the property.
