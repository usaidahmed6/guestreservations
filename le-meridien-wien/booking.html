About Le Meridien Vienna

Le Méridien Vienna: A Gateway to Cultural Discovery

Discover the vibrant heart of Vienna with a stay at Le Méridien Vienna, perfectly positioned on the iconic Ringstraße. This prime location offers guests immediate access to the illustrious State Opera House and the bustling Kärntner Strasse shopping district, both just steps away. Experience the seamless blend of traditional architecture and contemporary comfort that defines the rooms, each featuring a large flat-screen TV with a multimedia system, and most boasting spacious bathrooms with a Victorian-style bath and separate massage shower.

Indulge in the diverse culinary delights at YOU Restaurant & Bar, where every dish promises an adventure and every drink a new melody. Beyond gourmet dining, embrace the comprehensive wellness facilities, including a sauna, steam bath, and an inviting hot tub. With a commitment to wellness, guests are encouraged to utilize the fully equipped 24-hour fitness center or dive into the rejuvenating indoor pool.

Le Méridien Vienna serves as your cultured companion in the city, placing you within a mere 5-minute walk of the Hofburg Imperial Palace, while the nearby Karlsplatz Underground Station ensures every corner of the city is within reach. Enjoy complimentary admission to selected cultural attractions and immerse yourself in the rich tapestry of Viennese history and art.

  • Indoor swimming pool for year-round enjoyment
  • Comprehensive spa with sauna, steam room, and hot tub
  • 24-hour fitness center equipped for personal training sessions
  • YOU Restaurant & Bar offering a unique dining and musical experience
  • Complimentary access to top Vienna cultural attractions
