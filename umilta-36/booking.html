About Umiltà 36

Located in Rome and with Piazza Venezia reachable within a 5-minute walk, Umiltà 36 provides concierge services, allergy-free rooms, a fitness center, free WiFi throughout the property and a bar. This 5-star hotel offers room service and a 24-hour front desk. The property is a 3-minute walk from Trevi Fountain, and within 200 yards of the city center. The hotel will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms with a closet, a coffee machine, a minibar, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a bidet. Every room has an electric tea pot, while some rooms will provide you with a terrace and others also provide guests with city views. Breakfast is available daily, and includes buffet, continental and Italian options. At Umiltà 36 you'll find a restaurant serving Italian, Mediterranean and Seafood cuisine. Vegetarian, dairy-free and vegan options can also be requested. Popular points of interest near the accommodation include Torre Argentina, Barberini Metro Station and Palazzo Venezia. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino Airport, 10 miles from Umiltà 36.
