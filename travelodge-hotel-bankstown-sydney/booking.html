About Mercure Sydney Bankstown

Newly refurbished Mercure Sydney Bankstown is an ideal base for working and relaxing in Western Sydney. Discover spacious, comfortable rooms with a kitchenette, TV, ironing equipment, air conditioning, an in-room safe, luggage storage, a 24-hour front desk, a self-check-in desk and a guest laundry. Enjoy a breakfast hosted in the Greenfields Bistro seven days per week and 14 on-site restaurants and cafes, including an impressive Lady Banks rooftop bar, eight conference spaces, a gym, a convenience store, and a kids center, all a part of the Bankstown Sports Club. Guests can be charged to their rooms from most restaurants and can connect to complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi across all public areas. The hotel requires a valid ID to be presented upon check-in (rules by Bankstown Sports Club).
