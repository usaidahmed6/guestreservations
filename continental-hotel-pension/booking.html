About Continental Hotel-Pension

Just around the corner from the Mariahilfer Strae shopping street, this family-run hotel is only 100 ft from the Neubaugasse subway station. Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas. Continental Hotel-Pension is on the upper floors of an office and apartment building. Rooms offer city views and feature cable TV, minibars, safes, and bathrooms with a hairdryer. Air conditioning is available in most of the rooms. A varied breakfast buffet is served each morning at Hotel Continental. The Museum Quarter and the Hofburg Imperial Palace are only a 10 minute walk away. St. Stephens Cathedral can be reached in a 20 minute walk from Hotel-Pension Continental. A public parking lot is 65 ft away.
