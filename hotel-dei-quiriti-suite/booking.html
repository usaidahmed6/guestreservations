About Hotel dei Quiriti Suite

Located 2297 feet from Castel Sant'Angelo, Hotel dei Quiriti Suite features modern-style rooms with air conditioning in Rome. This 3-star hotel offers a 24-hour front desk, small shared gym and free WiFi throughout. This nonsmoking only property is 2625 feet from the Vatican Museums. At the hotel every room includes a desk, a Smart flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with toiletries and hairdryer. Popular points of interest near Hotel dei Quiriti Suite include St Peter's Square and St. Peter's Basilica, both 10 minutes away on foot. Lepanto Metro Station is a 5-minute walk from the hotel.
