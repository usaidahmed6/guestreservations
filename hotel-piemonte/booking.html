About Hotel Piemonte

Hotel Piemonte is set just 984 feet from Rome Termini Train and Metro Station, offering direct links to the Vatican Museums and Fiumicino Airport. It offers air-conditioned rooms, a bar, and free WiFi throughout. All rooms at the Piemonte Hotel include a satellite TV, mini-bar and parquet floors. Each has an private bathroom with a shower. Guests can enjoy a sweet and savory buffet breakfast daily, including boiled eggs and pastries. Gluten-free options are also available. Staff that can suggest nearby restaurants and attractions. It is 1.2 mi from both the Spanish Steps and the Coliseum.
