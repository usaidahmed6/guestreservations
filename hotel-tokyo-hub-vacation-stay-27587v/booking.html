About Hotel Tokyo Hub - Vacation STAY 27587v

Situated within 1.1 km of Hirai Asama Shrine and 700 metres of Hirai Hakuto Shrine, Hotel Tokyo Hub - Vacation STAY 27587v features rooms with air conditioning and a private bathroom in Tokyo. Popular points of interest nearby include Gokinimono Museum, Kameidosuijin-gu and Kameido Chuo Park. Free WiFi is available throughout the property and Komatsugawa Shrine is 1.5 km away. The private bathroom is fitted with a shower, free toiletries and a hairdryer. At the hotel rooms are equipped with a desk and a TV. Popular points of interest near Hotel Tokyo Hub - Vacation STAY 27587v include Kameido Fujizuka Monument, Kameido Sengen Shrine and Kameido Ishii Shrine. The nearest airport is Tokyo Haneda Airport, 23 km from the accommodation.
