About Meriton Suites Kent Street

Offering modern luxury apartments in the heart of Sydney CBD (Central Business District), Meriton Suites Kent Street, Sydney boasts an indoor swimming pool, a fitness centre, sauna and hot tub. Many suites offer excellent views of the city. Guests enjoy unlimited free WiFi at the property. The air-conditioned suites feature laundry facilities. Each suite has a seating area with flat-screen cable TV. Studios have a kitchenette, and all other suites have a full kitchen. The apartment complex is located opposite the trendy World Square shopping centre, 2 minutes’ walk from Town Hall Station. Queen Victoria Building and Darling Harbour are each within a 10-minute walk.
