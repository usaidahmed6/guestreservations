About B&B Claudia a Monteverde

Offering quiet street views, B&B Claudia a Monteverde is set in the Gianicolense district of Rome, 2.6 km from Roma Trastevere Train Station and 4.1 km from St. Peter's Basilica. The property is around 4.2 km from Piazza di Santa Maria in Trastevere, 4.5 km from Basilica San Paolo Metro Station and 5.2 km from Campo de' Fiori. St Peter's Square is 5.5 km from the bed and breakfast and The Vatican is 5.6 km away. The units come with a microwave, toaster, a coffee machine, a bidet, bathrobes and a wardrobe. Free WiFi is available to all guests, while some rooms come with a balcony. At the bed and breakfast, the units come with bed linen and towels. Guests at the bed and breakfast can enjoy an Italian breakfast. Piazza Navona is 5.6 km from B&B Claudia a Monteverde, while Largo di Torre Argentina is 5.7 km away. The nearest airport is Fiumicino Airport, 21 km from the accommodation.
