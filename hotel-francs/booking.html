About Hotel Francs

Only a 3-minute walk from JR Kaihin Makuhari Train Station, Hotel Francs offers modern accommodations with free Wi-Fi. Guests can enjoy drinks at the BBB Lounge. Makuhari Messe is a 5-minute walk away. An airport limousine bus to and from Narita International Airport is available, and the ride takes approximately 50-minutes. The air-conditioned rooms feature carpeted floors, a satellite TV and a desk. Each includes sleepwear, a fridge and an electric kettle with green tea bags. The private bathroom comes with a hairdryer and toothbrush sets. Francs Hotel is a 30-minute drive from Tokyo Disney Resort and JR Makuhari Train Station is a 10-minute drive away. Mitsui Outlet Park Makuhari is within a 3-minute walk away. Guests can also enjoy shopping at the nearby Eon Mall Makuhari New City, which is a 5-minute drive from the hotel. Also, busses bound for Haneda International Airport depart from the north exit of Kaihin Makuhari Station. The bus ride takes approximately 50-minutes. The luggage storage is available at the 24-hour front desk. Drink and snack vending machines are on-site. A breakfast buffet is available at the lounge.
