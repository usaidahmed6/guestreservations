About Copthorne Tara Hotel London Kensington

Discover the Charm of Copthorne Tara Hotel in Kensington

Welcome to Copthorne Tara Hotel London Kensington, a prime destination nestled within the vibrant heart of Kensington. This hotel boasts an exceptional location, just a stone's throw away from Kensington High Street and within a comfortable stroll from the eclectic shops and gourmet restaurants of Knightsbridge and Notting Hill. Experience the ultimate convenience and comfort, complemented by a host of thoughtfully curated amenities designed to make your stay unforgettable.

  • Indulge in culinary delights at the Artist Corner, where modern British and European cuisine takes center stage.
  • Savor the unique flavors of Asia at the hotel's brasserie, offering a menu brimming with Asian-inspired dishes.
  • Enjoy the convenience of Cafexpress cafe for quick bites and refreshing beverages.
  • Stay active or start new fitness routines at the on-site fitness room, tailored to suit all levels of fitness.
  • Attend or host events at the comprehensive conference center, ideal for both business and social gatherings.

Rooms at Copthorne Tara Hotel London Kensington are designed with your comfort in mind, featuring Hypnos beds for a restful night's sleep, flat-screen TVs for your entertainment, and in-room safes for your peace of mind. Each room is en-suite and equipped with coffee and tea making facilities, ensuring a cozy and convenient stay. Guests can also enjoy complimentary WiFi access in all areas, keeping you connected throughout your visit.

With Olympia exhibition center, Hyde Park, the Royal Albert Hall, and the world-renowned museums on Cromwell Road all within walking distance, Copthorne Tara Hotel London Kensington serves as the perfect base to explore the wonders of London. Whether you're here for business or leisure, discover the perfect blend of comfort and convenience at Copthorne Tara Hotel London Kensington.
