About Hotel Brenta Milano

Hotel Brenta Milano is set in Milan's Bocconi University district, just 55 yards away from Brenta Metro Station. Guestrooms come with LCD satellite TVs and free Wi-Fi access. This 2-star hotel is just 5 metro stops away from the Duomo. Milan Central Station is also on the same metro line (Line 3). Reception is open 24-hours a day at the Brenta Hotel. There is a free internet point in the lobby. All rooms are air conditioned and have private bathrooms with hairdryers.

Amenities

  • Room Service
  • Masks are available to guests
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • Lounge/Bar
