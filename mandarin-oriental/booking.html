About Mandarin Oriental, Miami

With stunning views of the Miami skyline and Biscayne Bay, this hotel on Brickell Key Island offers luxurious spa services. The Shops at Mary Brickell Village are located just 3117 feet away from the Mandarin Oriental, Miami. A balcony, flat-screen cable TV with a DVD player, and iPod docking station are included in all guest rooms. Guests can enjoy a well-stocked mini-bar, evening turndown service, and marble bathroom with walk-in shower and separate bathtub. Mandarin Oriental, Miami offers an infinity-edge outdoor pool with butler service as well as a state-of-the-art fitness center. After a yoga class or personal training session, guests can relax with a massage in the full-service spa. La Mar by Gaston Acurio prepares Peruvian dishes while MO Bar + Lounge paires classic cocktails with a light fare menu. Miami city center is only 3 minutes' drive away from the Mandarin Oriental hotel, and South Beach district is located 6.3 mi away. The Wynwood Arts District is 3.4 mi away, and Coconut Grove is 13 minutes' drive from the property.
