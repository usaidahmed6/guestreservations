About Manson Place

Located in Londons museum district of South Kensington, Manson Place offers modern air-conditioned apartments just a 5-minute walk from South Kensington Tube Station. Each Manson Place apartment features a fully equipped kitchen, a satellite TV and free Wi-Fi. A separate seating area with comfortable sofa is also provided. Apartments are serviced daily and towels are changed every second day. The Royal Albert Hall, Hyde Park and museums of Science & Natural History are all within a 10-minute of the apartments. Central London and West End theaters can be reached in a 10-minute Tube ride from South Kensington station.

Amenities

  • 24 hour front desk
  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • Non-Smoking Facility
