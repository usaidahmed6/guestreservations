About NH Wien Belvedere

Located in Viennas diplomatic quarter very close to Belvedere Palace, the NH Wien Belvedere offers air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi. The 492 ft spa area features a sauna, a steam bath, a gym, and a relaxation room. Combining Art Nouveau charm with modern design, the rooms are equipped with a work desk and feature high ceilings and large windows. Some rooms offer views of the palace and the Botanic Gardens. The NH Belvedere occupies the historic former building of the State Printing Offices. A varied breakfast buffet is served in the bright breakfast room every morning. There is an open bar next to the reception offering drinks and snacks 24 hours a day. A tram stop and the Rennweg Train Station, which provides direct access to the airport, are only a few steps away. Please noted that pets are allowed,Charges be applicable with 25 per stay,maximum 2 pets per room,availability is limited,please contact the property to reserve.
