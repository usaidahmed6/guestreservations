About Check-Inn Rooms

Located 1.2 mi from the Vatican Museums, Check-Inn Rooms is a 2-minute walk from the Baldo degli Ubaldi Metro stop. Termini Train Station in 9 metro stops away, and free Wi-Fi is available. With a balcony, the air-conditioned rooms have a private bathroom with a shower and bidet. They are equipped with a flat-screen TV and mini-bar. Guests of Check-Inn Rooms can enjoy an Italian breakfast with savory options. Several restaurants are within a 5-minute walk of the property. Fiumicino Airport is 19 mi away.
