About Hotel Pyramid

Hotel Pyramid is 1640 feet from Piramide Metro and Ostiense Train Station, where trains depart for Fiumicino Airport. It offers basic rooms with free WiFi, air conditioning and a private bathroom. Named after the ancient Pyramid of Cestius, which is 5 minutes’ walk away, this hotel is in the popular Testaccio district. There are many traditional Roman restaurants and bars around. The rooms include satellite TV and a fridge, and some also have a small balcony. All come with a shower, and some have a spa bath with chromotherapy. Breakfast is served in the dining room. It features sweet products together with cold cuts, cheese and fresh fruit. The Pyramid Hotel is set on three floors of a historic building with no elevator. Friendly staff is available 24 hours a day and can provide tourist information.
