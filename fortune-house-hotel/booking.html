About Fortune House Hotel Suites

On Brickell Avenue, and less than 3 miles from the Port of Miami, this apartment-style Florida hotel provides an outdoor pool, hot tub, and on-site restaurant. The spacious, fully-furnished suites at Fortune House Hotel feature a full kitchen, private laundromat, and a balcony. Separate bedrooms and a dining areas are also included. A state-of-the-art fitness center and sauna are available to all guests at the Hotel Fortune House. Guests will have access to free property-wide Wi-Fi and a 24-hour front desk for added convenience. Madison's Restaurant and Lounge serves unique fusion of contemporary and traditional Latin fare. Breakfast, lunch, and dinner are made-to-order from the menu. Fortune House is less than 2 miles from American Airlines Arena and a 15-minute drive from Miami Beach. Miami International Airport is less than 10 miles away.
