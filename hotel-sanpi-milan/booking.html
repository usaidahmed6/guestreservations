About Hotel Sanpi Milano

Equipped with a wonderful and colorful internal garden, Hotel Sanpi is located a few steps from the historic Public Gardens, the Central Station and its connections with the airports, the famous Fashion District, the "Corso Buenos Aires" shopping street, Piazza of the Duomo and not far from the most modern part of the city. Hotel Sanpi is a Boutique Hotel with 82 rooms, many of the rooms different from each others. At Sanpi, the Italian style is clearly recognizable in all the rooms and common areas, in which period furnishings and paintings coexist in harmony with "touches" of more contemporary design. The buffet breakfast is served from 7 am until 10.30 am, offering a wide choice of both sweet and savory products to help you start your day in the best possible way. The Bar is also open every day from 4 pm to midnight, so you can enjoy an aperitif accompanied by an excellent selection of international cocktails. The Azzurra meeting room offers its guests everything they need for a successful corporate meeting. Hotel Sanpi Milano also includes a free gym, which houses a collection of original paintings and sculptures. Within a 5-minute walk you will reach the Repubblica and Porta Venezia train and metro stations, which provide direct connections to Rho Fiera Milano.
