About Norfolk Plaza Hotel

Located in London (London City Centre), Norfolk Plaza Hotel is within a 10-minute walk of Hyde Park and Kensington Gardens. This hotel is 0.7 mi (1.1 km) from Oxford Street and 0.9 mi (1.4 km) from Marble Arch. Make yourself at home in one of the 87 guestrooms. Bathrooms have showers and hair dryers. Conveniences include phones and coffee/tea makers, and housekeeping is provided daily. Wrap up your day with a drink at the bar/lounge. Buffet breakfasts are available daily from 7:00 AM to 10:00 AM for a fee. Featured amenities include complimentary wired Internet access, dry cleaning/laundry services, and a 24-hour front desk.
