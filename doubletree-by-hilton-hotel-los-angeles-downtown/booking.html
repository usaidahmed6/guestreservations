About DoubleTree By Hilton Los Angeles Downtown

Experience DoubleTree By Hilton Los Angeles Downtown

Located within walking distance of the Financial District and just a 10-minute drive from Nokia Theater LA LIVE, DoubleTree By Hilton Los Angeles Downtown offers a convenient and comfortable stay in the heart of the city. This modern hotel provides easy access to key attractions, making it an ideal choice for both business and leisure travelers.

  • Rooftop garden with sweeping city views
  • On-site restaurant, Justice Urban Tavern, serving local California cuisine and craft beer
  • 24-hour fitness center to maintain your workout routine
  • 24-hour business center and concierge desk
  • Complimentary freshly baked chocolate chip cookie upon arrival

Each room at DoubleTree By Hilton Los Angeles Downtown features modern amenities, including a flat-screen cable TV, small fridge, and work desk. Guests can enjoy the convenience of free toiletries, with some rooms offering whirlpool baths and additional refrigerators. The hotel's rooftop garden provides a serene spot to relax and take in the stunning views of the city.

Justice Urban Tavern, the on-site restaurant, offers a delightful selection of local California cuisine and craft beer, while the Rendezvous Lounge is perfect for drinks and appetizers. For those needing to stay productive, the hotel provides a 24-hour business center, currency exchange, and concierge desk, ensuring all needs are met with ease.

The hotel's prime location places guests just 4 miles from the LA Convention Center and within a 30-minute drive of UCLA. With an on-site car rental service, exploring the vibrant city of Los Angeles is convenient and straightforward. Enjoy a warm welcome with a freshly baked chocolate chip cookie and experience the best of downtown LA at DoubleTree By Hilton Los Angeles Downtown.
