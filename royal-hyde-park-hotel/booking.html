About Royal Hyde Park Hotel

In the lively Queensway area, near Hyde Park and London Paddington Station, this newly refurbished budget hotel offers rooms with attached bathroom with air conditioning. The NOX Hyde Park has a good location, close to a wide range of bars, restaurants and cafes, as well as a leisure center. The hotel is just 328 feet from Bayswater London Underground Station, which is on the District and Circle lines. Queensway, on the Central line, is just 984 feet away. Paddington Station offers fast, direct access to London Heathrow Airport via the Heathrow Express train service. Reception is open 24 hours a day and staff can help you organize trips, tours and tickets for all the many attractions that London has to offer.
