About Suite Prado

This hotel enjoys a very central location in the city, next to the Teatro Espaol and Madrids golden triangle of art: the Prado, Thyssen and Reina Sofia museums. Puerta del Sol is just 5 minutes walk away. Suite Prado has spacious and luxurious apartments, ach of which comes with a bedroom area with living room and kitchen. There you can prepare your own evening meals before heading out into the lively city. All apartments at Suite Prado come with free WiFi. A continental breakfast is served in the room. From Suite Prado you can easily stroll to many of the top city sights. The 24-hour reception allows you more flexibility during your stay.
