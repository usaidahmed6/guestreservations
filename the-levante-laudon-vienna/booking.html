About The Levante Rathaus Apartments

Just steps away from the Burg Theater, the Rathaus (town hall) and the Rathaus underground station, The Levante Rathaus Apartments is housed in a building from the Biedermeier period with a picturesque inner courtyard. Free Wi-Fi is available. The apartments include a bedroom, a separate living room with a sofa bed, and a kitchen or kitchenette with all necessary cutlery and crockery. Bed linen and towels are provided. A shared laundry room with a washing machine and a dryer is at guests' disposal free of charge. Sights such as St. Stephens Cathedral, the Museum of Natural History, the Art History Museum, the Albertina Museum and the Hofburg Imperial Palace can be easily reached on foot.
