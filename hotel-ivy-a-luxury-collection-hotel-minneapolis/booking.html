About Hotel Ivy, A Luxury Collection Hotel, Minneapolis

Refined Elegance at Hotel Ivy, A Luxury Collection Hotel, Minneapolis

Nestled in the heart of Minneapolis, Hotel Ivy intertwines historic charm with modern luxury. Connected to the city's extensive 7-mile skyway, this hotel stands as a beacon of sophistication and comfort, offering guests an exceptional urban retreat.

Key Features:

  • Exclusive Ivy Spa Club offering massages and relaxation in sumptuous hot tubs.
  • Luxuriously appointed rooms with deep soaking tubs, 42-inch flat-screen TVs, and a well-stocked mini-bar.
  • Direct access to Minneapoliss heated skyway, linking guests to premier dining and shopping destinations.
  • On-site dining experiences featuring exquisite meals crafted from local ingredients.
  • Personalized concierge service to assist guests in planning an ideal Minneapolis itinerary.

Hotel Ivy, A Luxury Collection Hotel, Minneapolis, invites you to experience an oasis of luxury amidst the vibrant backdrop of the city. Each room showcases original artworks and warm, inviting colors that complement the hotel's historic elegance. Whether indulging in a soothing spa day, exploring the linked skyway system, or enjoying a gourmet meal at the restaurant, Hotel Ivy promises a stay marked by distinction and refinement. Located just a short walk from Block E and 1.5 miles from Guthrie Theater, this hotel is an ideal choice for those seeking a blend of cultural activity and serene luxury.
