About Craigievar Guest House

Located in Edinburgh, Craigievar Guest House provides accommodations with access to a garden. Complimentary WiFi is offered. The guest house offers a seating area with a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with free toiletries, a hair dryer and shower. Kettle are also provided. Craigievar Guest House offers a terrace. Edinburgh Zoo is 1.6 mi from the accommodations, while Murrayfield Stadium is 3.1 mi away. The nearest airport is Edinburgh Airport, 3.7 mi from the property.

Amenities

  • Balcony
  • Non-Smoking Facility
