About Peking Station Hostel

Just 15 minutes' drive away from the bustling shopping areas as Wangfujing Pedestrian Street, Peking Station Hostel is yet a tranquil home base in an old-fashioned courtyard of Hutong. With well reserved green roof tiles, quadrangle yard and ancient wall dating back to Ming Dynasty, the hostel intermingles the old charm with chic guestrooms and floral romance. Dongdan Subway Station (Line 1&5) is only 1148 feet away. Free WiFi access is available. The hostel is an easy 10-minute drive from Beijing Railway Station. Forbidden City and Tian'anmen Square are 15 minutes' drive away. You may reach Beijing Capital International Airport in 1 hour's car journey. Individual rooms and dormitory beds are available for your choice. Offering a refreshing, nostalgic feel, rooms are air-conditioned, fitted with snowy bedding, heating facility and decorative items. Extras include a safety deposit box and lockers. Most of rooms are nonsmoking type while hotel offers a designated smoking area for convenience use. You may join in lively conversation and share travel tips with a drink and light bites at the snack bar. Or chill out with fresh air and flower scent in the yard. The surrounding area may be a perfect spot for a rickshaw or bike tour. Quite a few local restaurants can be easily accessed for sampling. You can also find a mini market on site to meet the shopping desire.
