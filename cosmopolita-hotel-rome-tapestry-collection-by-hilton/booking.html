About Cosmopolita Hotel Rome, Tapestry Collection by Hilton

Just 328 feet from Rome's Piazza Venezia and 984 feet from the Roman Forum, Hotel Cosmopolita offers a furnished terrace and air-conditioned rooms. Wi-Fi is free throughout. Our elegant rooms have a bed, HDTV, desk, air conditioning, free Wi-Fi for your devices. Special touches include a mini-bar, tea and coffee making facilities. Staff at Cosmopolita Hotel are available 24 hours a day and can organize city tours. You can reach the Coliseum on foot in around 10 minutes, while the shopping street Via Del Corso is just 656 feet away. The bus stop next door links with St Peter’s Basilica in 15 minutes.
