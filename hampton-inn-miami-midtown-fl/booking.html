About Hampton Inn By Hilton & Suites Miami Midtown, FL

Set in Miami's Design District, Hampton Inn & Suites Miami Midtown, FL is located 4.3 mi drive from Miami's city center and 5 mi drive from the Miami Cruise Port. A 40-inch flat-screen TV, as well as a work desk with an ergonomic chair, is provided. A small refrigerator and coffee-making facilities are located en suite. Enjoy a daily free hot breakfast at Hampton Inn & Suites Miami Midtown, FL, which offers kid-friendly dining options. Guests looking for a quick meal can grab a weekday breakfast bag. Sunbathe at the outdoor pool or experience a cardio workout in the on-site fitness center. Local artwork dots this Miami Midtown accommodations. Vibrant restaurants and high-end shops are within 12 minutes' walk. Wynwood Walls offer carefully crafted artist murals and is only 6 minutes' drive from the accommodations.
