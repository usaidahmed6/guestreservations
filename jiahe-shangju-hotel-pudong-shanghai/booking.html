About Country Inn&Suites by Radisson, Shanghai PVG

Country Inn&Suites By Radisson, Shanghai Pudong Airport provides flawless service and all the necessary facilities for visitors. Remain linked during your visit by utilizing the complimentary internet access available.Prior to your check-in date, you can arrange airport transportation services, guaranteeing a seamless and efficient experience for both arrival and departure. The hotel offers complimentary parking for guests who arrive with their own mode of transport.The hotel is completely smoke-free. In limited designated zones, smoking is exclusively permitted. Each morning at Country Inn&Suites By Radisson, Shanghai Pudong Airport, a scrumptious, homemade breakfast kick-starts the day. Begin your holiday mornings right with your essential cup of coffee, offered daily at the cafe on-site.During your visit, indulge in a range of delightful culinary choices at hotel to enhance your experience.
