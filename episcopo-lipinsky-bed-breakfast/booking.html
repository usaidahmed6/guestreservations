About Episcopo Lipinsky Luxury Suites

Only 656 feet from Piazza di Spagna Metro in central Rome, Episcopo Lipinsky Luxury Suites, once academy of artist Sigmund Lipinsky, offers a shared terrace with tables and chairs, and air-conditioned rooms with a balcony and free Wi-Fi. Guest rooms at the Episcopo Lipinsky overlook the city, and each has a private bathroom complete with hairdryer. The famous Piazza Del Popolo square and the Spanish Steps are a few minutes' walk from the property, while Termini Train Station is 3 metro stops on line A. The Villa Borghese Park is 1804 feet away.
