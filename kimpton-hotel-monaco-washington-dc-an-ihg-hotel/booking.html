About Kimpton Hotel Monaco Washington DC

Discover the Elegance of Kimpton Hotel Monaco Washington DC

Experience the distinctive charm of Kimpton Hotel Monaco Washington DC, ideally located just 2461 feet from the Walter E. Washington Convention Center. This boutique hotel offers a unique blend of historic architecture and modern luxury, making it an ideal choice for both business and leisure travelers in the heart of Washington, DC.

Each guest room at Kimpton Hotel Monaco Washington DC is designed with sophistication and comfort in mind, featuring flat-screen TVs with cable and plush bathrobes. Selected rooms also boast spa baths, enhancing your stay with a touch of indulgence. Complimentary WiFi is provided throughout the hotel, ensuring guests stay connected.

Kimpton Hotel Monaco does not just provide a place to stay; it offers a lifestyle. The 24-hour fitness center caters to health-conscious guests, while the business center is equipped with all necessary services for corporate needs. Dirty Habit, the hotels on-site restaurant and lounge, serves a delectable array of small plates paired with creative cocktails in an edgy, urban setting with a dramatic patio.

  • Strategic location near the Walter E. Washington Convention Center and National Portrait Gallery
  • Luxurious rooms with spa baths and flat-screen TVs
  • Complimentary high-speed WiFi throughout the hotel
  • 24-hour fitness center and fully equipped business center
  • Dirty Habit restaurant offering innovative cuisine and cocktails

Kimpton Hotel Monaco Washington DC invites guests to immerse themselves in a setting that is as rich in history as it is in contemporary style. Whether you are visiting for a conference, sightseeing, or simply to relax, this hotel offers an unparalleled experience in the capital city. Discover the perfect blend of style, comfort, and convenience at Kimpton Hotel Monaco Washington DC.
