About Pelican Grand Beach Resort, A Noble House Resort

Relax in Style at Pelican Grand Beach Resort, A Noble House Resort

Set against the backdrop of the Atlantic Ocean, Pelican Grand Beach Resort, A Noble House Resort offers a serene beachfront experience with a blend of leisure and luxury. Located in Fort Lauderdale, this resort is perfectly poised for guests who appreciate sophisticated oceanfront living.

Guestrooms at Pelican Grand Beach Resort are thoughtfully decorated to reflect the calm and comfort of the beach. Each room includes a 42-inch flat-screen TV, in-room movies, games, music, and a coffee maker. Small refrigerators in the rooms add an extra level of convenience, making it easy to store refreshments and snacks.

Enhance your stay with a visit to the on-site Pure Spa, where you can indulge in Swedish massages and cleansing facials while enjoying unobstructed ocean views. Stay active with access to a state-of-the-art fitness center, also with views of the Atlantic. Dining at Ocean 2000 allows you to savor oceanfront meals, and poolside cocktails offer the perfect end to a relaxing day.

  • Oceanfront pool and private beach access
  • Lazy river tubing for unique on-site recreation
  • Spa and fitness center with stunning ocean views
  • Oceanfront dining at Ocean 2000 and poolside beverages
  • Conveniently located near The Galleria and Broward County Convention Center

Pelican Grand Beach Resort, A Noble House Resort, provides a distinct blend of relaxation and adventure, all situated on the pristine shores of Fort Lauderdale. Whether you're in town for a conference or looking for a tranquil retreat, this resort ensures a memorable stay with impeccable service and exceptional amenities. Discover the charm of beachfront luxury at Pelican Grand Beach Resort, where every moment is infused with coastal elegance.
