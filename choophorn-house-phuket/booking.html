About Choophorn House SHA

Located next to the peaceful stretch of Kata Beach, Choophorn House SHA offers rooms with private balconies and free Wi-Fi in public areas. It features tropical gardens and a restaurant. House Choophorn is within walking distance from Kata’s shopping areas. All rooms at the guest house come with a private bathroom featuring hot and cold water facilities. They feature a mini-bar and a TV with satellite channels. Choophorn Hotel provides a tour desk where day trips to local attractions can be arranged. Guests can make use of the car rental services. Other services include dry cleaning. There is a restaurant on-site for the convenience of guests.
