About Hotel Regina

Stay in the Heart of Madrid at Hotel Regina

Hotel Regina boasts a superb location in the heart of Madrid, just steps away from the famous Puerta del Sol and only feet from Sevilla metro station. With modern amenities and elegant accommodations, this hotel offers a comfortable and convenient stay for all travelers. Guests can enjoy a rich breakfast and free Wi-Fi access, making it an ideal base for exploring the vibrant city of Madrid.

  • Prime location near Puerta del Sol and Sevilla metro station
  • Modern, air-conditioned rooms with satellite TV
  • Restaurant Castizo serving Mediterranean cuisine with local ingredients
  • Wine cellar with a selection of fine wines
  • Proximity to major attractions like Prado and Thyssen museums

Each room at Hotel Regina is designed for comfort, featuring air conditioning, satellite and pay TV, a spacious work desk, and a private bathroom with free toiletries. Guests can savor Mediterranean dishes at the on-site Restaurant Castizo, which uses seasonal, local ingredients. The restaurant also offers a wine cellar with various wines to complement your meal. With its central location, Hotel Regina is ideally situated for visiting Madrid's most popular sights, including the Prado and Thyssen museums. Experience the best of Madrid with a stay at Hotel Regina.
