About MiniMax Premier Hotel Shanghai Hongqiao

Located in the Changning district in Shanghai, MiniMax Premier Hotel Shanghai Hongqiao features free WiFi access and free private parking. Hongqiao Airport and Hongqiao Railway Station are 2.5 mi from the property. Xintiandi is 6.2 mi from MiniMax Premier Hotel, while Taobao City Fengxiang Clothing and Gift Plaza is 6.8 mi away. Featuring stylish decoration and artistic furnishing, air-conditioned guestrooms are included a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a seating area for your convenience. Besides, electric kettle and desk are also provided. Every room includes a spacious private bathroom with shower. For your comfort, you will find slippers and free toiletries. You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property offers car rental, concierge as well as ticketing service. For the sake of safety, private lockers and safety boxes are also available. Guests who have work need may avail the onsite business center.
