About Holiday Inn Vancouver-Centre Broadway, an IHG Hotel

Discover Comfort and Convenience at Holiday Inn Vancouver-Centre Broadway

Experience a blend of modern amenities and a prime location at the Holiday Inn Vancouver-Centre Broadway. Just a short 7-minute drive from the bustling city center and 7.5 miles from Vancouver International Airport, this hotel offers a welcoming haven for both business and leisure travelers. Enjoy the convenience of being only 3 minutes' walk from Vancouver General Hospital and a quick 1312 ft stroll to the Canada Line Skytrain station, providing easy access to the entire city.

  • Indoor pool and sun deck for relaxation and leisure.
  • Fully-equipped gym to maintain your fitness routine.
  • Business center offering comprehensive services for professionals.
  • On-site laundry and dry cleaning facilities for added convenience.
  • Balconies with views available in select rooms, enhancing your stay with a touch of nature.

Each room at the Holiday Inn Vancouver-Centre Broadway is designed with guest comfort in mind, featuring a cable flat-screen TV, seating area, and work desk. Complimentary WiFi ensures you stay connected throughout your visit. For a touch of added luxury, select rooms offer private balconies with stunning views. The University of British Columbia is a short 6.2-mile drive away, making this hotel an excellent choice for visiting academics and families alike.

Whether you are in Vancouver for business or pleasure, the Holiday Inn Vancouver-Centre Broadway provides the perfect base for exploring the city. Book your stay today and experience exceptional comfort and unmatched convenience.
