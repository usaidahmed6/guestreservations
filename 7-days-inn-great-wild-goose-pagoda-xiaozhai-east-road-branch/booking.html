About 7Days Inn Xi'an Big Wild Goose Pagoda East Xiaozhao Road

Accepting only Mainland Chinese citizens, the 7Days Inn Xi'an Big Wild Goose Pagoda East Xiaozhao Road offers accommodations with free WiFi. Children up to 12 can be accommodated in the existing beds. Big Wild Goose Pagoda is 0.7 mi from 7Days Inn Xian Dayuan Tower East Xiaozhao Road. Xi'an City Wall is 2.1 mi away. The nearest airport is Xianyang Airport, 19 mi from 7Days Inn Xian Dayuan Tower East Xiaozhao Road. All rooms at the 7Days Inn Xi'an Big Wild Goose Pagoda East Xiaozhao Road are fitted with a cable TV and a private bathroom. A hairdryer, towels and a shower are included.
