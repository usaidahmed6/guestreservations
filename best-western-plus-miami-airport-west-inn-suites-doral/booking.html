About Best Western Plus Miami-Doral Dolphin Mall

Located 5 miles from Miami International Airport, this hotel offers comfortable accommodations and convenient services. The property is 1 mile from Costa Del Sol Golf Course and 15 miles from Miami Beach. Best Western features a complimentary hot breakfast buffet for a great start to the day. In the afternoon, go for a refreshing swim in the outdoor pool or work out in the exercise room. Guestrooms include a cable TV and private bathroom. A microwave and refrigerator are also available. Suites include a seating area. The beaches, night life and shopping of South Beach are just a short drive away from the Best Western.
