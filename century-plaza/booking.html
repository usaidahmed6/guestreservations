About Century Plaza Hotel

This all-suite hotel is located in downtown Vancouver. Each suite includes a kitchen or kitchenette. CPrime - Modern Italian Steak & Wine Restaurant is located onsite. A microwave and a refrigerator are provided in each suite at the Century Plaza Hotel. A coffee maker and 40" flat-screen cable TV are available in the suites as well. The hotel also includes a hair salon. Beyond Coffee features Italian coffee and a variety of snacks and desserts. Shopping on Robson Street is within a 10 minute walk from the Century Plaza Hotel. The stylish boutiques of Yaletown are a 15 minute walk from the hotel. English Bay’s beaches are also nearby.
