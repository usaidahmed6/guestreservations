About Trevi Collection

Boasting a panoramic rooftop terrace, Trevi Collection Hotel offers free WiFi throughout and modern rooms with a hydromassage shower. The Spanish Steps are 656 feet away. Rooms here include a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. The sweet and savory breakfast buffet is available daily, served on the covered terrace. The Trevi Fountain is 1640 feet from the property, while Villa Borghese park is a 5-minute walk away.
