About Marble Arch Inn

Ideally located for exploring central London, the modern Marble Arch Inn offers budget accommodations with free Wi-Fi throughout the property. This budget hotel is within a 5-minute walk of the famous Oxford Street and Marble Arch Underground Station. Each room at the Marble Arch Inn includes a TV with satellite channels, a direct-dial telephone, alarm clock radio, and tea and coffee-making facilities. The rooms also have a convenient travel adapter for electrical items. Most rooms have a private in-room shower, while basic rooms share facilities. Featuring a 24-hour front desk, the Marble Arch Inn is just 1 mile from London's popular Regent's Park. Guests can explore the nearby historic sights and museums or catch shows at the West End theaters. Madame Tussauds London is just a 7-minute walk away and Kensington Palace a 30-minute walk through leafy Hyde Park. London Heathrow Airport is just a 30-minute express ride from Paddington Underground Station, within a 15-minute walk of the Marble Arch Inn.
