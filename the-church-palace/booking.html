About TH Roma - Carpegna Palace

Surrounded by a lush garden with centuries-old trees, TH Roma - Carpegna Palace is 650 feet from Cornelia Metro Station, on Rome's line A. It offers free parking, and rooms with a flat-screen satellite TV. Rooms are decorated in a modern style, and include a mini-bar, and pay-per-view TV channels. All rooms offers free WiFi, while some come with tea/coffee facilities. Set in a historic building next to Villa Carpegna Park, the hotel is 4 metro stops from St. Peters Basilica. The A90 orbital road is a 5-minute drive away.
