About Sanctum International Serviced Apartments

Just 10 minutes drive from London's West End, these deluxe 5-star apartments offer fully equipped kitchens and spacious living areas. Kilburn Park Underground Station is less than a 5-minute walk away and Oxford Circus can be reached after a 15-minute tube journey. Each stylish apartment benefits from a plasma-screen TV with satellite channels, free WiFi, air conditioning and dark wood furnishings. Most apartments also have balconies. Sanctum Serviced Apartments offer a 24-hour concierge service, as well as laundry and maid services. Guests can also enjoy access to a health club, which includes a swimming pool, sauna, steam room, and beauty treatments. Gym, Health Club access and Swimming pool are available to guests for stays over 7 nights, number of vouchers issued are based on the apartment type you book.
