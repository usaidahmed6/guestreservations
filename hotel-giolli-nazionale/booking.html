About Hotel Giolli Nazionale

Hotel Giolli offers large rooms with Wi-Fi access, friendly service, and a 24-hour front desk. It is located on Via Nazionale, a short walk from Roma Termini Train Station. Rome's Opera House and the Trevi Fountain are both within walking distance of the Giolli. Repubblica Metro station is 220 yards away. Rooms at Giolli Hotel are quiet and equipped with air conditioning, satellite TV, a hairdryer and minibar. Floors are made of eco-friendly wood parquet, and some rooms feature balconies with views of the main street. Buffet breakfast is served in the panoramic dining hall. The hotel also features a bar and a comfortable lounge. The staff can arrange airport shuttle service and book museum tickets.
