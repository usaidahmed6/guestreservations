About NH Madrid Atocha

NH Madrid Atocha is opposite Atocha Railway Station and is 5 minutes from both the Caixa Forum and Reina Sofa museums. Free WiFi is offered. The hotel's bright, soundproof rooms include air conditioning, a 40-inch TV, pillow menu, safe and mini-bar. Each one has a private bathroom with a rain-effect shower, toiletries and a hairdryer. Complimentary newspapers are offered in reception. The NH Madrid Atocha also offers a laundry service. Retiro Park is a 5-minute walk from the hotel. Atocha Metro Station is 164 feet away. From there it is only 3 stops to Puerta del Sol.
