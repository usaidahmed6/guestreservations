About Mercure Wien Zentrum

Located right in the heart of Viennas historic center, the Mercure Wien Zentrum is only a 5-minute walk away from Saint Stephen's Cathedral. Free Wi-Fi is available in the entire hotel. Each nonsmoking room at the Mercure Wien Zentrum has cable TV, a mini-bar and a private bathroom with toiletries. Characteristic Viennese specialties can be enjoyed in the on-site restaurant. The bar features a terrace on the street and also serves some hot and cold dishes. The State Opera, the recently re-opened Albertina, the Hofburg, the Museum Quarter and the exclusive shopping areas of the center are all within easy walking distance. The Schwedenplatz metro stop of lines U1 and U4 is only a 2-minute walk away.
