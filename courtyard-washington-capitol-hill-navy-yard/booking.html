About Courtyard by Marriott Washington Capitol Hill/Navy Yard

This Courtyard is 1.1 mi away from the National Mall. It features an indoor pool, a fitness center and free WiFi throughout the hotel. All guest rooms at Courtyard Washington Capitol Hill/Navy Yard feature 42" Smart TVs with satellite channels. Every room has a mini refrigerator, and microwave, air conditioning and a well-lit work desk. The Bistro serves an a la carte breakfast every morning. The Bistro Lounge serves a wide selection of drinks and features HDTVs for showing the big game. Guests can relax in the indoor pool, whirlpool or work out in the on-site fitness room. The Smithsonian Institute is just 1.4 mi away, and the hotel is only 0.8 mi away from Capitol Hill. The nearest metro stop, Navy Yard, is just 984 feet from the Courtyard hotel. Ronald Reagan Washington National Airport is 5.2 mi away.
