About Saint B Boutique Hotel

Saint B boutique hotel is located in Rome, 328 feet from Piazza Barberini and 1969 feet from Piazza di Spagna. Every room has a flat-screen TV. You will find a kettle in the room. Every room is equipped with a private bathroom. For your comfort, you will find bathrobes, free toiletries and a hairdryer. Quirinale is 1969 feet from Saint B boutique hotel, while Spanish Steps is 1969 feet away. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino Airport, 8.7 mi from Saint B boutique hotel.
