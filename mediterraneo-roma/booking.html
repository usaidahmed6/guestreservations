About Bettoja Mediterraneo

Bettoja Hotel Mediterraneo features a panoramic roof terrace with views of Rome. Located in the lively Esquilino area, it is only 656 feet from Roma Termini Train Station. The elegant, classically styled rooms of the Bettoja offer free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, large windows and high ceilings. Suites come with a private terrace and some also boast a spa bath. Bettoja Hotel's generous breakfast is complete with freshly baked pastries and seasonal fruit. The bar is open all the day and provides drinks and hot and cold snacks. The hotel is a 5-minute walk from the impressive Basilica of Santa Maria Maggiore. The Roman Forum and the Coliseum are a little further along the road.
