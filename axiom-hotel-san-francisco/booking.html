About Axiom Hotel San Francisco

Sensor locks and online check-in make staying at the Axiom Hotel a technology forward experience. Lounge areas , Century-old architecture fuses with an electric modern design in this boutique hotel. A 48-inch Samsung flat-screen Smart TV with wireless streaming capabilities is featured in each room. A Kube Bluetooth clock radio and an electronic laptop safe are provided for convenience. Premium linens, signature Axiom robes and LATHER brand toiletries are also provided. The entire property includes fiber optic free WiFi, including the lounge common areas and guest rooms. A 24-hour reception is available to assist guests. Shopping and dining opportunities in San Franciscos Union Square are just 984 feet from Axiom Hotel. The Dragons Gate entrance to Chinatown is 0.6 mi away. The Coit Tower and North Beach are 1.9 mi away from this hotel.
