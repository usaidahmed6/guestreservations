About magdas HOTEL

The magdas HOTEL, Austrias first social business hotel operated by refugees under the guidance of tourism professionals is located in Viennas 2nd district, featuring a lush garden, just a 5-minute walk from the Prater. Guests can make use of free WiFi and enjoy snacks served in the on-site bistro. Every room is fitted with upcycling design furniture and tablets are provided on request. Featuring a shower and a hairdryer, each rooms private bathroom also comes with free Lederhaas toiletries including organic body and hair wash. Some rooms feature tea making facilities or TVs. Every day from 7 to 11 am our guest can enjoy an la carte breakfast - ideally in our wonderful garden. magdas HOTEL features a lounge, where gregarious guests can meet. A tablet can be requested at the reception. In good weather conditions, the hotels garden and its terrace are the ideal place to relax. Guests can also rent bicycles for exploring the surrounding area or access a jogging track right at the doorstep. Around the hotel is a short-term parking zone. The hotel has some private parking spaces underground available at extra cost ad based on availability. Viennas Prater Amusement Park with the famous Giant Ferris Wheel is a 5-minute walk away. The Praterstern Transportation Hub is 2297 feet away and the KunstHausWien Museum can be reached within 8 minutes on foot.
