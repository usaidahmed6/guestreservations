About My Trevi Charming & Luxury Rooms

Located in the Trevi district in Rome, 328 feet from Trevi Fountain, The property is located on the first floor in a building with no elevator. My Trevi features air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi throughout the property. The rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. You will find a coffee machine in the room. Rooms are equipped with a private bathroom equipped with a bidet. Extras include bathrobes and free toiletries. Quirinale is 984 feet from My Trevi, while Via Veneto is 1969 feet from the property. Fiumicino International Airport is 21 mi from My Trevi.

Amenities

  • Room Service
  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • Non-Smoking Facility
