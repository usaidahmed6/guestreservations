About Mezcal Hostel

Welcome to Nomads Experience, aside from offering a beautifully-boho designed accommodations with comfortable spaces, we are located in the heart of downtown Cancun, walking distance from convenience stores, authentic Mexican restaurants, shops and 10 minutes away from the beautiful white sand Caribbean beaches. You can also find in-site an art, flower & tattoo shop, all surrounded by a quiet and safe neighborhood. Bicycle rentals are available! We believe by the idea of sharing and experiencing our destination beyond the place. We are a medium sized hotel where our guests can enjoy hanging out and having the best of times sunbathing in our outdoor pool, relaxing in a hammock or just chilling at our Restaurant-Bar, with great food and drink promotions all year round. If you are in the mood, our common areas invite you to socialize and meet awesome people from all over the world. Check out our Instagram @enigmatichotels and @nomadsexperience and be tuned about the activities and experiences provided. Nomads Enigmatic Hotel, Hostel & Restaurant-Bar like to keep it social and fun by making you feel at home, all accommodations are air conditioned, we have Free Wi-Fi available in common areas and running smoothly throughout the entire building and free use of the guest kitchen, luggage storage, a book exchange and a lively hotel bar. Also, we are happy to help you plan trips and book tickets for onward travel and daily tours (Xcaret, Xplor, Xel-Ha, Chichen Itzá, Isla Mujeres and many others) and night life outings to the famous Cancun Party Zone (ask for more information at Front desk, we’ll be more than happy to assist you!). We are happy to welcome you soon!!
