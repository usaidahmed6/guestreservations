About Hilton Vienna Park

Experience Elegance at Hilton Vienna Park

Strategically located in the heart of Vienna, Hilton Vienna Park offers a luxurious and serene stay right next to the City Park, with unparalleled access to major transportation hubs like the Wien-Mitte/LandstraÃŸe Underground Station and the City Airport Train (CAT). This prime location ensures a seamless travel experience to and from Vienna Airport in just 16 minutes.

The hotel's rooms, newly refurbished and soundproof, are adorned in warm colors and offer stunning panoramic views of the park and city. Each room is equipped with a 40-inch LED flat-screen TV and a cozy seating area, perfect for unwinding after a day in the city. Bathrobes and high-speed internet access further enhance the comfort provided in each room.

  • Direct access to the City Airport Train (CAT) for easy airport transfers
  • Panoramic views of the city park and Vienna's skyline
  • Contemporary dining at the a la carte restaurant LENZ and drinks at SELLENY's bar
  • Exclusive Executive Lounge access for guests in executive rooms and suites
  • State-of-the-art gym facilities with modern cardiovascular equipment and in-room gym gear

Whether visiting for business or leisure, Hilton Vienna Park is designed to offer a sophisticated retreat with its extensive facilities, including a steam bath, an executive lounge with additional privileges, and a connectivity station for staying in touch.
