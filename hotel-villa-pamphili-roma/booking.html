About Hotel Villa Pamphili Roma

Located in Rome, 2.5 miles from Roma Trastevere Train Station, Hotel Villa Pamphili Roma provides accommodations with a seasonal outdoor swimming pool, private parking, a fitness center and a garden. This 4-star hotel offers room service and an ATM. The property has a 24-hour front desk, airport transportation, a shared lounge and free WiFi throughout the property. The hotel will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms with a desk, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV, a balcony and a private bathroom with a bidet. All guest rooms include a closet. A buffet, continental or Italian breakfast is served at the property. At Hotel Villa Pamphili Roma you'll find a restaurant serving Italian, Mediterranean and Seafood cuisine. Vegetarian, dairy-free and vegan options can also be requested. The accommodation has a terrace. Bike rental and car rental are available at this hotel and the area is popular for cycling. Battistini Metro Station is 2.5 miles from Hotel Villa Pamphili Roma, while St. Peter's Basilica is 2.8 miles away. The nearest airport is Fiumicino Airport, 14 miles from the hotel.
