About Hotel Central Station

Hotel Central Station is just 2 minutes' walk from Milano Centrale Train and Metro Station. The simple rooms have a TV, and some have private bathrooms. Services at the hotel include WiFi and a drinks' fridge. Located 1.6 mi from Milan's historic center, the Central Station Hotel is 4 Metro stops from Milan Cathedral and Milan Royal Palace.

Amenities

  • 24 hour front desk
  • Masks are available to guests
  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • Non-Smoking Facility
  • Lounge/Bar
