About World Hotels Elite Georgian Court Hotel

Georgian Court Hotel, WorldHotels Elite: Your Stylish Vancouver Stay

Immerse yourself in the heart of Vancouver's vibrant city center with a stay at the Georgian Court Hotel, WorldHotels Elite. This boutique hotel, situated directly across from BC Place, stands out with its unique combination of modern amenities, cozy accommodations, and prime location, making it an ideal choice for both leisure and business travelers. Embrace the convenience and charm of this hotel, where the city's culture and elegance meet.

Key Features:

  • Elegant Accommodations: Each guest room is equipped with a 37-inch flat-screen TV and a coffee maker, ensuring a comfortable and relaxing stay. Beekman 1802 bath products add an extra touch of luxury to the experience.
  • Wellness Amenities: Stay active and rejuvenated with the hotel's fully equipped fitness center, unwind in the soothing hot tub, or detox in the infrared sauna.
  • Dining Delights: The hotel boasts on-site casual dining options, including Frankie’s Italian Kitchen for sumptuous Italian cuisine and Frankie’s Bar for relaxing drinks in a laid-back atmosphere.
  • Central Location: Just a 4-minute walk from Queen Elizabeth Theater and 12 minutes from the bustling shopping on Robson Street, the hotel's location is perfect for exploring Vancouver's top attractions.
  • Complimentary WiFi: Stay connected with the world and share your Vancouver experiences with complimentary WiFi available throughout the hotel.

The Georgian Court Hotel, WorldHotels Elite, offers more than just a place to stay; it’s a gateway to the city's best. Whether you're in town for a show, shopping spree, or simply soaking in the city's vibrant culture, this boutique hotel ensures an elegant, convenient, and memorable visit to Vancouver.
