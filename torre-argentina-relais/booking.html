About Torre Argentina Relais - Residenze di Charme

Located in the heart of Rome, Torre Argentina Relais - Residenze di Charme is 5 minutes walk from the Pantheon. With free Wi-Fi, it offers modern-style rooms featuring a spa bath, and air conditioning. Each room has a flat-screen TV, and a complete bathroom with a sensory rain shower, bathrobes and slippers. Breakfast is served daily in the guests rooms, and consists of cereals, homemade cakes and yogurt. Located 1312 feet from Piazza Navona square, Torre Argentina Relais is a 10-minute walk from the Imperial Fora. A bus stopping 66 feet away has links to Termini Train Station.

Amenities

  • Babysitting Services
  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
