About Hampton by Hilton Rome East

Set a 20-minute drive from Rome city center, Hampton By Hilton Rome Easts features a restaurant specializing in international cuisine and a bar. At the hotel guests can also enjoy free private parking, free WiFi throughout and a free fitness center. All rooms have a classic décor, air conditioning, and a flat-screen TV. The bathroom has a walk-in shower and free toiletries, as well as a hairdryer. A buffet-style American breakfast is available. The hotel also has meeting facilities. Roma Fiumicino Airport is a 30-minute drive from the Hilton Rome East, while Roma Est Shopping Center is a 10-minute drive away.
