About Hotel Alexandra

The stylish Hotel Alexandra is located 656 ft from Copenhagen’s Town Hall Square and Tivoli Gardens. Its soundproofed rooms have air condition, flat-screen TV, tea/coffee maker and classic Danish designer furniture. Free Wi-Fi is available in all of Alexandra’s rooms, along with the lobby. Hotel Alexandra is an environmentally friendly hotel.

Amenities

  • Room Service
  • 24 hour front desk
  • Restaurant
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • Currency Exchange
  • Balcony
  • Non-Smoking Facility
  • Lounge/Bar
