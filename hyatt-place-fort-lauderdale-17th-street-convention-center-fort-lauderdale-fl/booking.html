About Hyatt Place Fort Lauderdale Cruise Port

This Hyatt Place Fort Lauderdale Cruise Port uses an outside shuttle service to and from the Fort Lauderdale/Hollywood International Airport for a fee. The hotel is approximately 2.4 miles from the airport. Free WiFi is also provided. Hyatt Place Fort Lauderdale Cruise Port features a 42-inch flat-screen TV in every guest room. Rooms are also equipped with an alarm clock and a mini-bar. A 24-hour reception is available at the Hyatt Place Fort Lauderdale Cruise Port as well. Facilities at the Hyatt Place Fort Lauderdale Cruise Port include an outdoor pool. A business center with computers and a printer is available.
