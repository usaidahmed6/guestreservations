About The Glamore Milano Duomo

Opening directly on Piazza Duomo square, The Glamore Milano Duomo is set in a historical building overlooking the great Cathedral of Milan. It is a few steps from La Scala Theater and Duomo Metro. With free WiFi throughout, all rooms here feature air conditioning and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. The private bathrooms are complete with a hairdryer, free toiletries and slippers. Linate International Airport is 5.6 mi away.

Amenities

  • Room Service
  • 24 hour front desk
  • Restaurant
  • Concierge
  • Babysitting Services
  • Balcony
  • Non-Smoking Facility
  • Lounge/Bar
