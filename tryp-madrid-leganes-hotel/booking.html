About Hotel Madrid Leganés Affiliated by Meliá

Hotel Madrid Leganés Affiliated by Meliá is located 7.5 mi from the center of Madrid and close to Carlos III University. This hotel features free Wi-Fi access, a café and 24-hour reception. Rooms at the Hotel Madrid Leganés Affiliated by Meliá are bright and have simple décor. Each room has a TV, desk and air conditioning. The Hotel Madrid Leganés Affiliated by Meliá serves a buffet breakfast in its dining room, which features a glass, domed ceiling. Guests can also enjoy a drink or snack in the Coffee Bar. The hotel is approximately 1.2 mi from the M-45 and M-50 Motorways. Leganes Metro Station is 10 minutes’ walk away and connects guests to the center of Madrid within 40 minutes. The Xanadu Snow Park and Shopping Center is 9.3 mi from the hotel. Warner Bros Theme Park is 20 minutes' drive of the hotel.
