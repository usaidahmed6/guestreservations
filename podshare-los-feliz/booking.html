About PodShare Los Feliz

In a prime location in the Hollywood district of Los Angeles, PodShare East Hollywood is located 2.7 miles from Capitol Records Building, 3.1 miles from Griffith Observatory and 3.6 miles from Dolby Theater. The property is around 3.8 miles from Hollywood Bowl, 4 miles from Dodger Stadium and 4.4 miles from Hollywood Sign. The property provides a shared kitchen and free WiFi throughout the property. All guest rooms in the hostel are equipped with a coffee machine. All rooms are equipped with a shared bathroom, a shower and a hairdryer, and some units at PodShare East Hollywood have a safety deposit box. At the accommodation the rooms come with bed linen and towels. Staples Center is 4.6 miles from PodShare East Hollywood, while Microsoft Theater is 4.6 miles away. The nearest airport is Hollywood Burbank Airport, 10 miles from the hostel.
