About Le Fate Apartments

Offering rooms with attached bathroom with free Wi-Fi, Le Fate Apartments is in a quiet residential area of Rome just 15 minutes' walk from the lush Villa Pamphilj Park and Roma Trastevere Train Station. Guests at Le Fate B&B enjoy an Italian-style breakfast daily, featuring yogurt, homemade jams, cakes and more. Guest rooms come with a flat-screen TV, mini-bar, and air conditioning. The property is only 1804 feet from tram number 8, offering direct links to Rome's historical center and shopping area. Guests can find all services in the immediate surroundings.

Amenities

  • Hot Tub or Spa
  • Balcony
  • Non-Smoking Facility
