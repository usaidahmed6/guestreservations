About Hotel Major Aventinus

Located 1969 feet from the Testaccio area and a short walk across the Tiber River to the Trastevere area, Hotel Major Aventinus offers rooms with views of the garden or street. Wi-Fi throughout is free. The simple rooms come with a fan and desk and have private or shared bathroom. Major Aventinus Hotel features a 24-hour front desk and common space, which can also serve as a dining room. A snack and drink machine is available. Circo Massimo Metro Station is a 10-minute walk away, while the Colosseum is 1.2 mi from the hotel.
