About Vatican Style

Vatican Style Luxury Rooms is set in the Vatican City - Prati district in Rome, just 492 feet from St. Peter's Square. Every room at this guest house is air conditioned and comes with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. You will find a kettle in the room. The rooms have a private bathroom. For your comfort, you will find bath robes and slippers. Vatican Style Luxury Rooms features free WiFi throughout the property. There is concierge service at the property. Ottaviano Metro is 8 minutes' walk from the property. Fiumicino Airport is 19 mi away.

Amenities

  • Room Service

  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
