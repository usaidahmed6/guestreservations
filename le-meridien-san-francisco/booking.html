About The Jay, Autograph Collection

Centrally located in San Francisco's Financial District and steps away from Chinatown and the Ferry Building, this luxurious hotel offers unrivaled accommodations and state-of-the-art amenities, including a state-of-the-art fitness center. This completely nonsmoking hotel offers spacious guest rooms furnished with 65-inch flat-screen TVs, goose-down duvets and wireless internet access. In addition to stunning views of the city's skyline, The Jay, Autograph Collection offers easy access to some of the area's most popular attractions. The legendary cable car lines are also easily accessible and provide access to many areas of the city. Fisherman's Wharf is 13 minutes' drive away from The Jay, Autograph Collection, while Chinatown and Union Square are less than 15 minutes' walk away. A number of major corporate offices, including Merrill Lynch, JPMorgan and Wells Fargo can also be found nearby.
