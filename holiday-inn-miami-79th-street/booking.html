About DoubleTree by Hilton Miami North I-95, FL

Located in Miami, 5.2 miles from Adrienne Arsht Center for the Performing Arts, DoubleTree by Hilton Miami North I-95 has accommodations with a fitness center, free private parking, a restaurant and a bar. With free WiFi, this 4-star hotel offers a 24-hour front desk and a concierge service. Guests can have a drink at the snack bar. All rooms at the hotel feature air conditioning and a desk. A buffet breakfast is available every morning at DoubleTree by Hilton Miami North I-95. American Airlines Arena is 5.5 miles from the accommodation, while Marlins Park is 5.7 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Miami International Airport, 6.8 miles from DoubleTree by Hilton Miami North I-95.
