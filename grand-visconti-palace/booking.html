About Grand Hotel Visconti Palace

Just 656 feet from Lodi Metro Stop, Grand Visconti Palace offers luxury accommodations with free WiFi, just 4 metro stops from Milan Cathedral. It features a refined wellness area with swimming pool, and a gourmet restaurant located on the 5th floor, overlooking the citys rooftops. Each spacious room at the 4-star Visconti Palace features an elegant dcor, including beautiful rugs and drapes. They all have soundproofed walls, a flat-screen TV, and a mini-bar. The Al V Piano restaurant specializes in creative dishes of international and Mediterranean cuisine, served with a variety of Italian and foreign wines. Breakfast is a varied buffet. The wellness area includes a fitness area, an indoor pool, a sauna, a Turkish bath and a hot tub. The Grand Visconti is surrounded by a 8202 ft garden, equipped with deck chairs and sun loungers. Fondazione Prada Milano is a 14-minute walk from the property, while Linate Airport is 4.7 mi away.
