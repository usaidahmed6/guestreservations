About GH Colosseum

Just up the road from the Colosseum, GH Colosseum is set on the fourth floor of an early 19th-century building. It offers air-conditioned rooms with free Wi-Fi, each offering a different design. All rooms come with terracotta floors and an LCD TV. Each one has a private bathroom with a hairdryer. . Upon check in, you will receive a free map of the city. GH Colosseum is just around the corner from the Basilica of San Giovanni in Laterano in Rome's most historic district. It has excellent bus links to Piazza Venezia and the shopping street of Via del Corso.
