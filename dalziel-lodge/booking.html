About Dalziel Lodge

Set in North Sydney, a short walk from the new metro station, Dalziel Lodge North Sydney offers express check-in and check-out and free WiFi throughout the property. The property is located between the bustling North Sydney CBD and the relaxed cafe culture of Crows Nest, making it the perfect choice for traveler's seeking economical and clean lodging surrounded by a vibrant array of cafes, bars, and restaurants, ensuring you'll never run out of dining options. We genuinely embrace environmental sustainability. Our power includes solar panels and our hot water uses energy efficient generation, and no single use plastics are on site. The rooms come with air conditioning, a 32-inch flat-screen TV, and a fridge. At Dalziel Lodge the rooms are equipped with bed linen and towels. Our heritage lodge provides guests with a homely atmosphere and a unique, memorable experience. There is a communal kitchen and gorgous dining room in a beautiful heritage setting. For added convenience, we offer coin operated laundry. When the weather is pleasant, you can relax in our garden, complete with outdoor furniture. Located just 15 minutes from North Sydney train station, and 5 minutes from the new Metro Station (Opening 2024) our lodge offers easy access to Sydney's attractions. We also have a bus stop just a two-minute walk away. Additionally, we are proud to be the closest accommodations to the private Mater Hospital, located just 1312 feet away. We are also 1148 feet away from North Sydney Oval which hosts many Australian Sporting events including cricket, rugby league, rugby union, and AFL.
