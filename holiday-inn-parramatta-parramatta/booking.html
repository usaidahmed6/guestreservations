About Holiday Inn Parramatta

Holiday Inn Parramatta is within walking distance from Westfield Parramatta Shopping Center, BankWest Stadium, Eat Street and the Parramatta Railway Station. It is a 30-minute drive from the Sydney city center and Sydney Airport. Rooms at the Holiday Inn Parramatta are spacious and all feature air conditioning, a flat-screen TV and a desk. The One-Bedroom Suites offer a spa bath plus a separate lounge area with a sofa bed and a coffee machine. Guests have access to an outdoor heated swimming pool and hot tub, plus complimentary WiFi throughout the entire hotel. Secure car parking is offered on site for additional charge of AU $20 per night. The on-site restaurant Twenty One Fifty is open on weekdays for a la carte lunch and dinner, and 7 days a week for both a la carte and full buffet breakfast.
