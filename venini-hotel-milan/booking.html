About Hotel Venini

Hotel Venini is 2625 feet from Milano Centrale Railway Station and near the Pasteur Metro Station, with direct links to the Duomo. All rooms offer air conditioning and free Wi-Fi. Venini Hotel includes a TV lounge with bar, a nice breakfast room, and a 24-hour front desk with fax service, safety box and luggage room. Pasteur Metro Station is 1476 feet from the property., From here you can reach the Expo 2015 Exhibition Center in a 45-minute ride.
