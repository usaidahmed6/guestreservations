About The Gig Hotel

A short walk from both Jungceylon Shopping Center and Bangla Road, The Gig Hotel features a rooftop pool and a restaurant. Free WiFi is available. Rooms include air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, a mini-bar and a balcony. The private bathroom has a shower and free toiletries. There is a 24-hour front desk at the property and staff can assist with tour information. The beach is less than a 10-minute walk away and many shops are in the surrounding area. Phuket International Airport is a 1-hour drive from The Gig Hotel.
