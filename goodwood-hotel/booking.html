About Goodwood Hotel

The Goodwood Hotel has good service and budget accommodations in the center of London. It is within easy reach of major public transportation services including St Pancras International and has free Wi-Fi. Goodwood Hotel is in Russell Square. Oxford Circus, the British Museum, Holborn and many other London attractions are very close by. Each budget room has tea/coffee facilities and a TV. Some rooms have private bathrooms.

Amenities

  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • Non-Smoking Facility
