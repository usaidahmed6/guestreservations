About Selina Cancun Laguna Hotel Zone

Located in Cancn, a 5-minute walk from Playa Forum, Selina Cancun Laguna Hotel Zone has accommodations with a garden, free private parking, a shared lounge and a terrace. The property has a bar, as well as a restaurant serving Mexican cuisine. The property provides a 24-hour front desk, airport transportation, a shared kitchen and free WiFi throughout the property. A continental, vegetarian or vegan breakfast is served at the property. The area is popular for cycling, and bike rental is available at this 3-star hotel. Popular points of interest near the hotel include Playa Gaviota Azul, Playa Chac Mool and Playa Punta Cancun. The nearest airport is Cancn International Airport, 13 miles from Selina Cancun Laguna Hotel Zone.
