About The Craigmyle

Located just 3 minutes walk from Craigdarroch Castle, this Victoria heritage property offers free laundry facilities located on-site and free WiFi available in all rooms and common areas. A set of stairs leads to all rooms. An private bathroom is offered in each individually decorated room at The Craigmyle. Select rooms feature kitchenettes, cable TV and castle views. Heritage features are intact including elegant staircases (no elevators) leading up to guestrooms. A common lounge and guest kitchen add extra convenience. A business center offers two guest computers, printers and free US and Canada calls. Guests can enjoy the garden surroundings, terrace, and BBQ facilities. Victoria city center is just under 1.2 mi from The Craigmyle. Jubilee Hospital is 1.2 mi and the University of Victoria is 4.3 mi away.
