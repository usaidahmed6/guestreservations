About Hotel NYX Cancun

Located on a 14-mile stretch of pristine beachfront in Cancuns hotel zone, NYX Hotel Cancun. has an infinity pool overlooking the Caribbean Sea. Some of its rooms have views of the sea or Nichupt Lagoon. All air-conditioned rooms at the NYX Hotel Cancun feature a flat-screen cable TV and a private bathroom with a hairdryer. A coffee maker and free WiFi access are also included. Bellavista Restaurant offers international options. The Chianti Restaurant offers Italian cuisine, open for dinner with prior reservation required. There is also a poolside bar, as well as a lobby bar. The surrounding beaches are popular for water sports. You can go scuba diving at the 2 coral reefs or visit ancient Mayan sites nearby. Cancun Airport is a 20-minute drive away.
