About 16 Pilrig Guest House

Just a 12-minute walk from Edinburgh Playhouse and 1.1 miles from Edinburgh Waverley Train Station, 16 Pilrig Guest House features accommodations in Edinburgh, with a shared lounge. The property is around 1.3 miles from Royal Mile, 1.5 miles from Royal Yacht Britannia, and 1.7 miles from Camera Obscura and World of Illusions. Free Wifi, a 24-hour front desk, and daily room service are provided. At the bed and breakfast, all units come with bed linen and towels. Buffet and continental breakfast options with warm dishes, fresh pastries, and fruit are available daily at the bed and breakfast. National Museum of Scotland is 1.7 miles from 16 Pilrig Guest House, while The Real Mary King's Close is 1.7 miles away. The nearest airport is Edinburgh Airport, 7.5 miles from the accommodation.
