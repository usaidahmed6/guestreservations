About Roma Scout Center

Roma Scout Center offers air-conditioned rooms. It is located near Bologna Metro Station and is 1640 feet from Tiburtina Station, with links to Fiumicino Airport. Accommodations at the Scout Center is colorful and simply furnished. The dormitories feature private lockable cupboards and reading lights, while the bathroom can be shared and external. WiFi is free. An on-site restaurant is available serving classic Italian cuisine, and there are vending machines for drinks and snacks. The Sapienza University is a 10-minute walk away.
