About Hotel Secession an der Oper

The Hotel Secession an der Oper is located in the historical center of Vienna, in the Mariahilf district. Vienna's main tourist attractions (such as the Vienna State Opera House, St. Stephan's Cathedral and various museums) are just a short walk away. The hotel offers spacious, air-conditioned rooms and an indoor car park to make your stay as comfortable as possible. The family-run hotel has a fantastic view of the golden cupola of the Vienna Secession. Traditional Viennese hospitality, cozy atmosphere, spacious, optimally appointed rooms and an attentive team who care about your wellbeing look forward to your visit! The Karlsplatz Underground Station (lines U1, U2 and U4) can be reached in a 5-minute walk. The State Opera, the Ringstraße boulevard and the Museum Quarter are also only a 5-minute walk away. St. Stephen’s Cathedral is reachable within a 15-minute walk from the Hotel Secession an der Oper.
