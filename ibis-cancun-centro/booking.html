About Ibis Cancun Centro Hotel

This hotel is located in Cancún’s commercial district, 10 minutes’ drive from the airport. It offers a 24-hour snack bar, early breakfast and smart rooms with free Wi-Fi and satellite TV. The air-conditioned rooms at the Ibis Cancún Centro feature parquet floors and modern décor. Each one has a work desk and a private bathroom with a hairdryer. The restaurant at the Ibis serves traditional Mexican food. The breakfast combines continental and regional elements. Las Américas Shopping Center is 820 feet from the hotel, and Cancún’s beaches are just 5 minutes’ drive away. Car rental can be arranged at reception.
