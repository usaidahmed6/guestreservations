About Hospes Puerta de Alcalá, a Member of Design Hotels

The elegant Hospes Puerta de Alcalá, a Member of Design Hotels is set next to Puerta de Alcalá, in Madrid’s Salamanca district. It offers free WiFi, great views of the Retiro Park and free access to the Bodyna Spa. The stylish rooms at Hospes Puerta de Alcalá, a Member of Design Hotels come with a flat-screen TV, a bathrobe and special amenities. The Bodyna Spa area has 4 treatment rooms, a soundproofed relaxation room, a hammam, gym and a small relaxation pool. All guests have access to the spa. The Malvar Restaurant offers meals, tapas and a large terrace with views of the Puerta de Alcalá. Guests can also enjoy a drink or relax in the Patio Chill Out. Private parking is available onsite for an additional cost. An elegant 5-star hotel, Hospes Puerta de Alcalá, a Member of Design Hotels is set in a historic building dating from 1883. The Prado, the Reina Sofía Museum and Cibeles Fountain are within a short walk.
