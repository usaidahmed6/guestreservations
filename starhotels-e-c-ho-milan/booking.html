About Starhotels Echo

This eco-friendly hotel has free WiFi throughout and is just 656 feet from Milano Centrale Train Station. Its modern rooms are decorated with soothing tones of beige and floral wall art, and provide a 32" LCD TV. Rooms at Starhotels Echo have elegant parquet floors and contemporary furnishings. All air conditioned, they have been designed to use energy and water in a sustainable way. Offering Milanese and classic Italian cuisine, Orto Green Food & Mood restaurant features large windows and back-lit murals inspired by nature. Breakfast is American-style and includes eggs, bacon and a selection of hot and cold drinks. The shopping street of Corso Buenos Aires is 2297 feet from Echo Starhotels. Milano Centrale Metro and Train Station links you with Rho Fiera Exhibition Center, 9.5 mi away, and Malpensa Airport.
