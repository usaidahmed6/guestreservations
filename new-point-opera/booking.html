About New Point Opera

Located in Madrid, 656 feet from Mercado San Miguel and 1969 feet from Royal Palace of Madrid, New Point Opera provides accommodations with free WiFi, air conditioning, a shared lounge and a terrace. The units come with tiled floors and feature a fully equipped kitchen with a microwave, a dining area, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom with shower and a hairdryer. A fridge, an oven and stovetop are also featured, as well as a kettle and a coffee machine. At New Point Opera Apartments guests will find a 24-hour front desk, which offers tourist information. Extras include ironing facilities and guests can use the apartment land-line phone free of charge. The terrace, located on the 4th floor and including sun loungers and chilling area, is open from 10:00 to 00:00 to all guests. Gran Via is less than 0.6 mi from the aparthotel, while Thyssen-Bornemisza Museum is a 16-minute walk away. The nearest airport is Adolfo Suarez Madrid-Barajas, 16 mi from New Point Opera, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
