About Hi! Ostello Milano

Located in Milan, a 10-minute walk from Fiera Milano, Hi! Ostello Milano has accommodations with a garden, private parking, a shared lounge and a terrace. Offering a bar, the property is located within a 18-minute walk of CityLife. The property provides a concierge service and free WiFi. Languages spoken at the 24-hour front desk include German, English, Spanish and French. San Siro Stadium is 1.6 miles from the hostel, while Santa Maria delle Grazie is 2.5 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Milan Linate Airport, 8.7 miles from Hi! Ostello Milano.
