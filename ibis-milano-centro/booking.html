About Ibis Milano Centro

Comfort and Convenience at Ibis Milano Centro

Located just 1148 feet from Repubblica Train and Metro Station, Ibis Milano Centro offers a prime location for exploring the vibrant city of Milan. This hotel provides a comfortable stay with modern amenities and easy access to Milan Central Train Station, only a 15-minute walk away.

Guests can enjoy air-conditioned and soundproofed rooms, each equipped with a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. The Lineauno Bistro' restaurant serves a variety of international dishes, while the 24-hour lounge-bar area offers hot drinks and snacks with a view of the gardens. Breakfast is served from 04:00 until 12:00, catering to early risers and late starters alike.

The hotel also features a 24-hour front desk and a business center, providing essential services for both leisure and business travelers. Free WiFi is available throughout the property, ensuring guests stay connected during their visit.

  • Proximity to Repubblica Train and Metro Station
  • Air-conditioned and soundproofed rooms
  • 24-hour lounge-bar overlooking the gardens
  • Breakfast available from 04:00 until 12:00
  • Easy access to Milan Central Train Station and major airports

For a well-rounded stay in Milan, Ibis Milano Centro offers a combination of comfort, convenience, and excellent service. Experience the best of Milan from a location that puts the city's attractions within easy reach.
