About DAO by Dorsett West London

Dao by Dorsett West London features a fitness room and air-conditioned accommodations in London, a 18-minute walk from Eventim Apollo. This 4-star condo hotel offers a 24-hour front desk and an elevator. The condo hotel also offers facilities for disabled guests. Featuring a fully equipped kitchen with a microwave and a toaster, each unit also comes with a satellite flat-screen TV, ironing facilities, desk, and a seating area with a sofa. Every room is equipped with a coffee machine, a private bathroom, and free Wifi, while certain rooms come with a terrace and some have garden views. At the condo hotel, every unit is equipped with bed linen and towels. The condo hotel offers a buffet or continental breakfast. A restaurant can be found at the property, offering a child-friendly buffet. Sightseeing tours are available close to the property. Olympia Exhibition Centre is one mile from Dao by Dorsett West London, while Portobello Road Market is 1.8 miles from the property. The nearest airport is London Heathrow Airport, 13 miles from the accommodation.
