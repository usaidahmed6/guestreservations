About IU Hotel Shanghai Dongchuan Road Metro Station

Attractively set in the Minhang District district of Shanghai, IU Hotel Shanghai Dongchuan Road Metro Station is located 12 miles from Shanghai South Train Station, 12 miles from Shanghai South Train Station and 14 miles from Longhua Temple. The property is around 17 miles from Jing'an Temple, 18 miles from People's Square and 18 miles from Jade Buddha Temple. The property has a 24-hour front desk and free WiFi is available throughout the property. The rooms come with air conditioning, a TV with cable channels, an electric tea pot, a shower, free toiletries and a desk. All guest rooms have a private bathroom, a hairdryer and bed linen. Pedestrian Street of East Nanjing Rd. Is 19 miles from the hotel, while Yu Garden is 19 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Shanghai Hongqiao International Airport, 14 miles from IU Hotel Shanghai Dongchuan Road Metro Station.
