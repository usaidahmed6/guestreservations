About Moxy Edinburgh Fountainbridge

Moxy Edinburgh Fountainbridge is set in Edinburgh in the Lothian region, 1312 feet from EICC and less than 0.6 mi from Edinburgh Castle. Boasting family rooms, this property also provides guests with a terrace. The accommodations offers a fitness center, a 24-hour front desk and free WiFi throughout the property. At the hotel, every room has a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. Moxy Edinburgh Fountainbridge offers a continental breakfast. Popular points of interest near the accommodations include Camera Obscura and World of Illusions, Royal Mile and University of Edinburgh. The nearest airport is Edinburgh Airport, 6.2 mi from Moxy Edinburgh Fountainbridge.
