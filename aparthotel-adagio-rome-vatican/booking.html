About Aparthotel Adagio Rome Vatican

Aparthotel Adagio Rome Vatican is located in a green area of Rome, less than 1.2 mi from Monte Mario Park. It's very close to Policlinico Gemelli and Università Cattollica and Villa Stuart. It offers modern rooms and apartments with air conditioning and free WiFi. All of the spacious accommodations features a private balcony, a flat-screen TV with international channels, and an iPod dock. Apartments and studios have an individual safe and a private bathroom with a shower. The fully equipped kitchens come with a microwave, stove and fridge. A daily morning buffet is available in the breakfast room. With a 24-hour reception, the aparthotel provides laundry and dry cleaning facilities. Private parking is possible at Adagio Rome Vatican. The nearby Balduina Station, on the FM3 line, is just 2 stops from Valle Aurelia Metro Station, on Rome's Line A. Saint-Pierre Basilica is a 10- minute drive away and the Vatican City is 1.2 mi from the property.
