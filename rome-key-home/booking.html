About Rome Key Home

Just 492 feet from Rome's La Sapienza University and 656 feet from Policlino Hospital, Rome Key Home offers free Wi-Fi. Rooms here are elegantly furnished, and include air conditioning and a flat-screen satellite TV. Rooms at Rome Key Home include a kettle and a modern private bathroom with hairdryer. This guest house is 2461 feet from Rome's Termini Central Station. The world-famous Coliseum is 2 metro stops away.

Amenities

  • Room Service
  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • Non-Smoking Facility
