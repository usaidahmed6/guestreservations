About Corso Boutique Luxury Rooms

Corso Boutique Luxury Rooms offers free WiFi throughout and elegantly furnished rooms along Via del Corso shopping street in the heart of Rome. The property's interiors combine design touches with classic-style furnishings and frescoed ceilings. Each room at this guest house is air conditioned and features a flat-screen TV with Sky Vision Gold channels and a fully fitted private bathroom. Certain units include a seating area and suites include a spa bath. Via Margutta is 656 feet from Corso Boutique Luxury Rooms, while Piazza del Popolo is 984 feet away. The nearest airport is Ciampino Airport, 9.9 mi from the property.
