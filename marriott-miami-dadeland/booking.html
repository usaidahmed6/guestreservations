About Miami Marriott Dadeland

This Miami, Florida hotel is 9.9 mi from Miami International Airport. An outdoor pool and 3 dining options are on site. A satellite TV, coffee-making facilities, and desk are featured in all rooms at this Dadeland Miami Marriott. Select rooms include access to complimentary food and beverages in the concierge lounge. Nonsmoking rooms can be requested based on availability. Guests can work out with cardiovascular equipment and free weights at the fitness center. The Miami Marriott also has a business center with fax and photocopying facilities. Whitney’s Restaurant serves continental cuisine and is open for breakfast and lunch. The hotel also offers the Datran Deli, which serves delicious dishes in casual surroundings. The Miami Marriott Dadeland is 5 mi from Coral Gables. Guests will also be 8.2 mi from historical Vizcaya Museum and Gardens and 13 minutes' drive from Matheson Hammock Park and Marina.
