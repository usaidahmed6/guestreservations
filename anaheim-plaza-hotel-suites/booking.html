About Anaheim Hotel

Experience Family Fun at Anaheim Hotel

Located just a 4-minute walk from Disneyland, Anaheim Hotel is the perfect destination for families seeking convenience and comfort. This family-friendly hotel features a large pool, a hot tub, and an array of amenities to ensure an enjoyable stay. Guests can easily explore local attractions while enjoying the welcoming atmosphere of the hotel.

Each air-conditioned room at Anaheim Hotel includes a cable TV with extended film channels, a work desk, a coffee maker, and a small refrigerator. Some rooms also offer a balcony or patio for added relaxation. The on-site restaurant, Pizza Press, provides delicious dining options, while room service and grab-and-go coffee are also available for guests' convenience.

  • 4-Minute Walk to Disneyland
  • Large Pool and Hot Tub
  • On-Site Restaurant: Pizza Press
  • 24-Hour Reception and Business Center
  • Fitness Center and Valet Parking

The Anaheim Hotel features a 24-hour reception, a business center with fax and photocopying services, and a fitness center for all guests. Valet parking is available to ensure a hassle-free stay. Nearby attractions include Knott's Berry Farm, 8 miles away, and the Anaheim Convention Center, just a 10-minute walk. Los Angeles International Airport is 35 miles from the hotel, making it a convenient choice for travelers. Book your stay at Anaheim Hotel for a memorable family adventure in the heart of Anaheim.
