About Ritorno a casa

Located in Rome, one mile from Anagnina Metro Station and 2.9 miles from Università Tor Vergata, Ritorno a casa provides accommodations with free WiFi, air conditioning, a shared lounge and a terrace. There's a fully equipped shared bathroom with bidet and a hairdryer. A buffet, Italian or vegetarian breakfast is available every morning at the property. Ponte Lungo Metro Station is 5.9 miles from the bed and breakfast, while San Giovanni Metro Station is 6.5 miles away. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino Airport, 5 miles from Ritorno a casa.
