About Intercontinental Madrid

InterContinental Madrid: A Gateway to Splendor on Paseo de la Castellana

Embrace the magnificence of Madrid with a stay at InterContinental Madrid, an exquisite hotel that blends the grandeur of an 18th-century palace with the vibrancy of modern Madrid. Situated on the prestigious Paseo de la Castellana Avenue and a mere stone's throw from Gregorio Marañón Metro Station, this hotel serves as the perfect base for exploring the city's rich culture, history, and shopping avenues.

Each spacious, air-conditioned room is a testament to classic elegance, outfitted with flat-screen satellite TV, a mini-bar, and a sleek private bathroom. With free WiFi throughout, staying connected is effortlessly simple. Dining at the hotel is a culinary adventure, from the daily buffet breakfast at El Jardín Restaurant to the gourmet tapas and sophisticated beverages of Bar 49.

  • El Jardín Restaurant and Bar 49 offering exquisite dining and drink options
  • 24-hour gym and available massage services for relaxation and rejuvenation
  • Exclusive InterContinental Club access for suites and club room guests
  • Strategically located near Calle Serrano's shopping paradise and Santiago Bernabéu Stadium
  • Easy metro access to Madrid's key attractions and direct link to Barajas Airport

InterContinental Madrid not only promises a stay that is steeped in luxury but also ensures a rich tapestry of experiences with its array of services. Guests of the suites and club rooms enjoy the privilege of the InterContinental Club, which includes a lounge for an all-day buffet, computer access, complimentary ironing service, and the convenience of late check-out. Just a brief walk from Calle Serrano, the hotel is a haven for those seeking to indulge in Madrid's premier shopping experiences, while the proximity to Santiago Bernabéu Stadium appeals to sports enthusiasts. Discover the essence of Madrid's elegance and hospitality at InterContinental Madrid.
