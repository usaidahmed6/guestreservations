About Chilworth Court

In central Londons exclusive Bayswater area, Chilworth Court has independent accommodations with free Wi-Fi. The apartments are within a quarter mile of both Paddington Station and Hyde Parks Lancaster Gate. Each spacious apartment has a sitting area with a TV and a DVD player. The air-conditioned apartments also feature a full kitchen with a dishwasher and a washing machine. Home to a range of restaurants, cafes, and nightclubs, the multi-cultural district of Edgware Road is just a 15-minute walk from Chilworth Court. Londons famous Oxford Street is within 2 miles of the property. Featuring Portobello Market, Notting Hill can be reached within 20 minutes by Underground or bus. London Heathrow Airport is 15 miles from the property.
