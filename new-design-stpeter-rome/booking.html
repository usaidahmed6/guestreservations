About New Design St.Peter

Featuring free WiFi throughout the property, New Design St.Peter offers modern and air-conditioned accommodations in Rome, 1640 feet from the Vatican. All rooms and suites feature a flat-screen TV, parquet floors and kettle. A mini-bar and a safe are also available. The private bathroom is fitted with free toiletries and a chromotherapy shower. The bed and breakfast with vaulted ceilings is 2953 feet from St. Peter's Square. The Vatican Museums are 1640 feet away.
