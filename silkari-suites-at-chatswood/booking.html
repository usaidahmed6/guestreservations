About Silkari Suites at Chatswood

Experience Comfort and Convenience at Silkari Suites at Chatswood

Opening in October 2016, Silkari Suites at Chatswood offers modern accommodations close to Chatswood Train Station. This prime location provides easy access to major department stores, shops, restaurants, cafes, and bars, all within a 5-minute walk. The Sydney Central Business District (CBD) is less than a 20-minute drive away, making it ideal for both business and leisure travelers.

  • Modern, air-conditioned suites with full kitchens
  • Private parking available
  • Outdoor terrace for relaxation
  • 24-hour reception and concierge service
  • Close proximity to Chatswood Train Station and shopping areas

Each suite at Silkari Suites at Chatswood features contemporary furnishings and is equipped with a flat-screen TV with cable channels, full laundry facilities, and a fully-equipped kitchen. The kitchen includes a dishwasher, oven, microwave, and kettle, providing all the conveniences of home. Private bathrooms are stocked with free toiletries and a hairdryer. Select suites offer a balcony with an outdoor dining area.

Guests can relax on the outdoor terrace or take advantage of the 24-hour reception and concierge services available on-site. Free WiFi is provided throughout the property, ensuring guests stay connected during their stay. Private parking is also available for added convenience.

Silkari Suites at Chatswood is ideally located for exploring Sydney, with the Sydney Opera House just 4.3 miles away. The nearest airport, Kingsford Smith Airport, is 9.9 miles from the property, providing easy access for travelers. Experience the perfect blend of comfort and convenience at Silkari Suites at Chatswood.
