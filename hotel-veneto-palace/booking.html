About room007 Select Via Veneto

The 4-star room Select Via Veneto is in central Rome, 656 feet from Via Veneto and 1640 feet from Villa Borghese park. It offers air-conditioned rooms, a 10-minute walk from the Spanish Steps and Trevi Fountain. Wi-Fi throughout is free. Tours, museum tickets, restaurant reservations, car rentals and transfers can be arranged by the concierge service. room Select Via Veneto serves a breakfast buffet daily.
