About Melrose Georgetown Hotel

Discover Elegance at Melrose Georgetown Hotel

Located just steps away from the historic neighborhood of Georgetown and less than one mile from the White House, the Melrose Georgetown Hotel offers a refined stay in the heart of Washington D.C. This boutique hotel combines spacious, well-appointed rooms and dedicated guest services to create a memorable experience for every visitor.

Guests can unwind in stylish rooms featuring upholstered seating and all the essential amenities for a comfortable stay, including ironing facilities and free cable TV. Complimentary bathrobes and daily newspapers add an extra touch of comfort to your routine. Whether you're in town for business or leisure, Melrose Georgetown Hotel caters to all your needs with its array of facilities.

Stay active with a state-of-the-art fitness center, or catch up on work in the fully equipped business center. Dining at the hotel is a delight at Jardenea restaurant, offering meals crafted from fresh, local ingredients. For a more relaxed setting, The Lounge provides a chic backdrop for sipping on cocktails and enjoying a selection of appetizers.

  • Prime location near Georgetown and major D.C. landmarks
  • Spacious rooms with upscale furnishings and daily amenities
  • Fitness center to keep up with your workout routine
  • On-site dining at Jardenea restaurant and cocktails at The Lounge
  • Easy access to Washington Metro for exploring the city

The Melrose Georgetown Hotel is the perfect choice for travelers seeking a blend of comfort and convenience in Washington D.C. Located within easy walking distance to the Lincoln Memorial and Kennedy Center for the Performing Arts, and just blocks away from public transportation, this hotel offers easy access to the best the capital has to offer. Enjoy your stay at Melrose Georgetown Hotel, where comfort meets sophistication.
