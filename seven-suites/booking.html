About Seven Suites

Located 1.2 mi from Romes Colosseum, Seven Suites offers contemporary rooms with free Wi-Fi and air conditioning. A sweet and savory Italian breakfast is included daily at a caf in front of the property. The rooms have a flat-screen TV, electric kettle, and mini-bar. Free toiletries come with the private bathroom. They are furnished in a modern style and some come with a balcony. The Testaccio neighborhood is full of shops, clubs, and restaurants. Porta Portese market place is 1312 feet away. Piramide Metro Stop is a 10-minute walk from Seven Suites. There is also a bus stop 49 feet away with connections to Termini Train Station and the center of Rome.

Amenities

  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
  • Social distancing measures are in place
