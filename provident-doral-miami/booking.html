About Provident Doral At The Blue

Relax and Enjoy at Provident Doral At The Blue

Provident Doral At The Blue offers spacious holiday villas with full kitchens, providing a perfect home-away-from-home experience. Located just a 15-minute drive from Miami International Airport, this resort is ideal for both short and extended stays. Guests can enjoy a resort-style pool and a variety of amenities designed for relaxation and convenience.

Each villa includes free WiFi and flat-screen TVs, ensuring guests stay connected and entertained. Many units feature a spa bath, with some offering a private balcony or an additional bathroom. For those looking to stay active, the on-site fitness center is available, along with a hot tub and sauna for unwinding after a long day.

The resort also boasts an on-site spa and wellness center, where guests can schedule massages and other treatments. Dining options include The Chophouse 5300 restaurant and the seasonal Poolside Bar and Grill, offering delicious meals and refreshing beverages.

Provident Doral At The Blue is conveniently located 16 miles from the shores of Miami Beach, making it easy to enjoy the area's beautiful beaches. The Lincoln Road Mall, known for its extensive shopping options, is just a 30-minute drive away.

  • Spacious villas with full kitchens
  • Resort-style pool, hot tub, and sauna
  • On-site fitness center and spa
  • Dining at The Chophouse 5300 and Poolside Bar and Grill
  • Close to Miami Beach and Lincoln Road Mall

Experience comfort and convenience at Provident Doral At The Blue, where spacious accommodations and a range of amenities make for a memorable stay in Miami.
