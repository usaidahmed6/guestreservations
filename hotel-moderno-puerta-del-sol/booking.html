About Moderno

This hotel is centrally located beside Madrids Puerta del Sol Square. Plaza Mayor is 165 yards away. All air-conditioned rooms have satellite TV and free Wi-Fi . Rooms at the Hotel Moderno Puerta del Sol have a safe and telephone. The modern bathrooms are equipped with a bathtub and a hairdryer. Some rooms have a hydromassage bathtub. The Hotel Moderno Puerta del Sol Hotel offers a buffet breakfast, including homemade fruit salads and cake. Room service is available and there is also a comfortable lounge. An airport shuttle service is available upon request. Public parking is available nearby. You can rent a car at the hotel, and the concierge service can arrange events tickets and tours.

Amenities

  • 24 hour front desk
  • Concierge
  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
