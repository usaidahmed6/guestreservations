About YWCA Hotel Vancouver

Welcome to YWCA Hotel Vancouver

Located adjacent to BC Place Stadium and Rogers Arena, YWCA Hotel Vancouver offers a prime location for both business and leisure travelers. With the Stadium-Chinatown Skytrain station just a five-minute walk away, exploring the city is convenient and effortless. This not-for-profit hotel ensures a comfortable and enjoyable stay, providing excellent amenities and services that cater to all guests.

  • Access to 3 fully equipped guest kitchens with cooking utensils and dishes
  • Daily complimentary coffee in the lobby and fruit-infused water available throughout the day
  • On-site launderette for guests' convenience
  • Proximity to popular attractions like Robson Street shopping and the Queen Elizabeth Theatre
  • Vending machines with a variety of snacks and drinks

Each air-conditioned room at YWCA Hotel Vancouver is thoughtfully designed to provide a comfortable workspace with a desk, along with entertainment options such as cable TV and a telephone. Whether choosing a room with a private or shared bathroom, guests will find all the essentials they need, including a refrigerator. The hotel's central location ensures easy access to a vibrant array of dining and shopping options, making it an ideal choice for travelers looking to experience the best of Vancouver.

For guests looking to maintain their routines while traveling, the hotel’s on-site laundry facilities are a convenient feature. The welcoming lobby, with its daily offerings of coffee and fruit-infused water, adds a touch of hospitality that sets the tone for a pleasant stay. Moreover, the vending machines located within the hotel provide quick and easy access to snacks and beverages at any hour.

Discover the perfect balance of comfort and convenience at YWCA Hotel Vancouver, where thoughtful amenities and a prime location come together to offer a memorable stay.
