About Hotel Villa Rivoli

Villa Rivoli is a privately-run hotel, located in the historic center of Nice, just 328 feet from the beach and the Promenade des Anglais. It offers accommodations in a 19th century Belle Epoque building with a garden and a concierge service. All of the guest rooms feature flat-screen TVs and free WiFi. Some have a balcony and a view of the gardens and the surrounding area. Guests can request a buffet breakfast for an extra charge, which can be enjoyed in the breakfast room or in the guest rooms. Bars and restaurants can be found within walking distance For guests coming by car, parking is available upon reservation.

Amenities

  • Room Service
  • Car Rental
  • 24 hour front desk
  • Concierge
  • Babysitting Services
  • Lounge/Bar
