About Airport Traveller's Inn

This Calgary hotel is located off the Trans-Canada Highway and 10 minutes drive from Calgary city center. Free WiFi is offered in all rooms. A fitness center is located on-site. Rooms are equipped with a TV and private bathroom at Airport Traveler's Inn. The rooms also have bright bedding and large windows. A Thai restaurant and a bar are featured at this property. A 24-hour Denny's restaurant is located just 1-minutes' walk from the hotel entrance. Airport Traveler's Inn is 3.7 mi from Calgary Tower. Heritage Park is 20 minutes' drive away.
