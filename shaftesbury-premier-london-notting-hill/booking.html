About The Premier Notting Hill

Just 10 minutes' walk from Hyde Park and Notting Hill Gate, The Premier Notting Hill is located in a quiet square in Bayswater, and close to Queensway, Whiteleys Shopping Center and the London Underground. Each air-conditioned room features free WiFi, a plasma screen TV and an private bathroom. Bathrobes and slippers can also be provided on request. A buffet-style breakfast is served in the bright and modern restaurant. Oxford Street, Leicester Square and Piccadilly Circus can all be reached in 20 minutes via underground train from Bayswater station, which is a 5-minute walk from the hotel. London Heathrow Airport can be reached in 20 minutes from London Paddington rail station, which is a 20-minute walk away.
