About Courtyard by Marriott Newark Silicon Valley

In the Silicon Valley, this hotel is 18 miles from Oakland International Airport. The hotel offers an outdoor pool and rooms with free Wi-Fi. Courtyard by Marriott Newark Silicon Valley rooms include a desk and coffee maker. They are also equipped with a sitting area. The Courtyard guests can workout in the gym or relax in the hot tub. The Bistro is open for breakfast and dinner. An on-site 24-hour market offers food and drinks. Shopping at the Newpark Mall is just a 10 minute drive from Courtyard Newark. Great America Theme Park is 18 miles from the hotel.
