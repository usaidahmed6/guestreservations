About Hostal Tokio

Hostal Tokio is centrally located, just 1312 feet from Madridโs Gran Via Metro Station. It offers air-conditioned rooms with a laptop safe and free Wi-Fi. The simple rooms at the Tokio guest house have a private bathroom with a bath or shower and amenities. Rooms are also heated, and there is a TV and a work desk. The Tokio has vending machines for drinks and snacks. Its central location means there are many bars and restaurants nearby. There is a tour desk, and many tourist sites, such as the Prado, Reina Sofia and Thyssen Bornemisza museums, are within a 15-minute walk. Parking is available near the guest house for an additional charge.
