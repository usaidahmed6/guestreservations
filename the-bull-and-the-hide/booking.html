About The Bull and The Hide

The Bull and The Hide offers accommodations in Shoreditch. Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant. The hotel is across the road from London Liverpool Street Station. Each room at this hotel is air conditioned and comes with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Some rooms have a seating area for your convenience. You will find a coffee machine in the room. Every room includes a private bathroom. Extras include free toiletries and a hair dryer. A TV is offered. London City Airport is 5.6 mi from the property.
