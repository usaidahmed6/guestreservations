About Bbrothershouse

Located in Rome’s Nomentana area, 984 feet from Villa Torlonia gardens, Bbrothershouse features air-conditioned rooms. Free WiFi is available. Each room features a flat-screen TV, some have a balcony. The private bathroom includes a hairdryer and free toiletries. An Italian-style breakfast is provided daily. Bbrothershouse is 2953 feet from Bologna Metro Stop.

Amenities

  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • Balcony
  • Non-Smoking Facility
