About Embassy Suites By Hilton Hotel Miami - International Airport

Embassy Suites By Hilton Hotel Miami - International Airport: Your Gateway to Miami

Embassy Suites By Hilton Hotel Miami - International Airport serves as a prime location for travelers. Situated a short distance from Miami International Airport, this establishment provides complimentary shuttle services, ensuring smooth transitions to and from your flights. It stands as an ideal starting point for exploring the dynamic city of Miami, offering guests a blend of accessibility and enjoyment.

Guests can relax in the serene ambiance of the outdoor pool, enhanced by a soothing hot tub. Begin each day with a complimentary breakfast, crafted to energize guests for their adventures. The evening reception features snacks and two complimentary drinks per person, creating a relaxed atmosphere for socializing.

  • Complimentary airport shuttle service, facilitating stress-free travel
  • Outdoor pool and hot tub, providing spaces for relaxation and leisure
  • Daily complimentary breakfast and evening reception with snacks and beverages
  • Equipped fitness center catering to health and wellness
  • Convenient on-site tour desk, laundry facilities, and business center

Guest accommodations are designed with contemporary amenities, including microwaves, refrigerators, and cable TV with pay-per-view options, ensuring a pleasant and connected stay. Whether visiting for business or leisure, the hotel's range of facilities and services aims to make every visit smooth and enjoyable.

Adventure awaits at the Miami Seaquarium, only 13.5 miles away, or at the Jungle Island zoological park, 7.5 miles from the hotel. The Embassy Suites By Hilton Hotel Miami - International Airport offers a portal to the excitement and allure of Miami, ensuring a memorable stay.
