About B&B RELAX

Located in Rome, a 8-minute walk from Rome Termini Metro Station and 1.1 miles from the center, B&B RELAX has air-conditioned accommodations with free WiFi, and a terrace. The bed and breakfast offers a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a hairdryer, free toiletries and bidet. Popular points of interest near B&B RELAX include Roma Termini, Repubblica - Teatro dell'Opera Metro Station and Piazza Barberini. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino Airport, 9.3 miles from the accommodation.
