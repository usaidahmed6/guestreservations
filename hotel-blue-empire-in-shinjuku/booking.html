About Blue Empire Hotel

Well situated in the Shinjuku Ward district of Tokyo, Blue Empire Hotel is located a 6-minute walk from Nihon Christ Kyokai Kashiwagi Church, 300 yards from Kaichu Inari Shrine and a 8-minute walk from Meotogi Shrine. With free WiFi, this 2-star hotel offers a 24-hour front desk. The property is a 2-minute walk from Yodobashi Church, and within 0.7 miles of the city center. All guest rooms comes with air conditioning, a fridge, a microwave, an electric tea pot, a bidet, a hairdryer and a desk. All rooms come with a private bathroom with a shower, slippers and bathrobes. Popular points of interest near the hotel include Zenryu-ji Temple, Choko-ji Temple and Koizumi Yakumo Memorial Park. The nearest airport is Tokyo Haneda Airport, 14 miles from Blue Empire Hotel.
