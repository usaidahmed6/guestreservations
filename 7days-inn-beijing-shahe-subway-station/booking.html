About 7Days Inn Beijing Shahe Subway Station

Accepting only Mainland Chinese citizens, 7Days Inn Beijing Shahe Subway Station offers affordable accommodations in Changping District. 7Days Inn Beijing Shahe Subway Station is 67 minutes' drive from Beijing Railway Station and 48 minutes' drive from Beijing Capital International Airport. Every room at this hotel is air conditioned and features a TV. All rooms have a private bathroom fitted with a shower.

Amenities

  • 24 hour front desk
