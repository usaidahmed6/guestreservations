About Qualys Hotel Nasco

Hotel Nasco is just 1640 feet from Allianz MICO - Milanocity Trade Fair and a 5-minute walk from Domodossola Metro stop, and near Parco Sempione. Rooms come with air conditioning and some also have a balcony. Free WiFi is available in all areas. The rooms feature a digital TV with Sky channels, and WiFi access. They come with parquet floors, and a luxury set of toiletries. A generous buffet breakfast is served between 07:00 and 10:00. The lounge bar is available for tasty snacks and drinks throughout the day. Milan historic center is a 15-minute tram ride away. Allianz Mico is 10 min walking (500 mt); the Rho Fiera Milano and Expo exhibition centers are both within 7.5 mi of the Nasco hotel.
