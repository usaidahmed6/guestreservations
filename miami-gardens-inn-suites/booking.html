About Miami Gardens Inn & Suites

Located in Miami, 4.5 miles from Hard Rock Stadium, Miami Gardens Inn & Suites has accommodations with a garden, free private parking and a terrace. With free WiFi, this 2-star hotel offers a 24-hour front desk and an ATM. Seminole Hard Rock Hotel & Casino is 11 miles away and Adrienne Arsht Center for the Performing Arts is 11 miles from the hotel. The rooms come with air conditioning, and some rooms at the hotel have a balcony. American Airlines Arena is 12 miles from Miami Gardens Inn & Suites, while Marlins Park is 12 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport, 12 miles from the accommodation.
