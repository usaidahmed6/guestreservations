About Relais Arco Della Pace

Set in the heart of Rome, Relais Arco Della Pace features air-conditioned accommodations equipped with free WiFi. Guests can enjoy views of the historic center from the furnished rooftop terrace. Rooms and studios are decorated with parquet floors and come with a satellite LCD TV. Each has a private bathroom equipped with free toiletries and a hairdryer. Local specialties and characteristic Italian dishes can be tasted at the several restaurants in the immediate surroundings. Piazza Navona Square is 656 feet from Relais Arco Della Pace. Campo de' Fiori Square is a 7-minute walk away.

Amenities

  • Concierge
  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
