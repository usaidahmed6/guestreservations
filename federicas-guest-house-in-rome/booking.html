About B&B Federica's House In Rome

Located 984 feet from Termini Train Station, B&B Federica's House In Rome features free WiFi throughout. Santa Maria Maggiore is 1969 feet away. All rooms have a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Certain units feature a seating area to relax in after a busy day. Each room is fitted with a private bathroom. Free toiletries and a hairdryer are available. A continental buffet is provided daily near the property. You will find a shared lounge at the property. Domus Aurea is 2953 feet from B&B Federica's House In Rome. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino Airport, 8.1 mi from the property.
