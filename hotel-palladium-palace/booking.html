About Palladium Palace

At 656 feet from Termini Train and Metro Station, Palladium Palace enjoys great transport links. This hotel features a panoramic rooftop bar, free WiFi throughout and rooms with classic-style decor. Palace Palladium is 328 feet from Basilica of Santa Maria Maggiore. The Colosseum is a 15-minute walk away. Rome's Opera House is 2297 feet away. Rooms feature classic wood furniture, satellite TV and air conditioning and grs porcelain or parquet floors. The bathroom includes hairdryer and toiletries. Some rooms have frescoes from the early 1900s and a hydromassage shower or bath. A generous American buffet breakfast with pancakes and omelets is available daily. Set in a 19th-century building, the Palladium hotel features a 24-hour reception. The lobby is decorated with marble and noble wood furniture. Staff can book tours and tickets.
