About Cinema House

Cinema House offers modern-style en suite rooms and free WiFi throughout, all a 3-minute walk from Rome's Termini Central Station. A sweet Italian breakfast is included. A hairdryer and a shower, as well as air conditioning are provided in each room at the Cinema House. Restaurants and cafés are in the property's surrounding area. Scheduled trains to/from Fiumicino International Airport leave from Termini Central Station.
