About Roma Aurelia Antica

The Hotel Roma Aurelia Antica is located just a few minutes from Via Aurelia and the ring-road (exit Aurelia-Citta del Vaticano), 7 kms far from the vatican area.
Roma Aurelia Antica provides 270 rooms; a fully equipped gym with terrace; an outdoor pool surrounded by gardens; a shuttle service to the city centre; different dining spaces a magnificent convention centre with 16 meeting rooms and a capacity for up to 700 people;is the perfect option for your trip to Rome thanks to its excellent communication with the city centre, its complete and comfortable facilities and the quality of its services.
