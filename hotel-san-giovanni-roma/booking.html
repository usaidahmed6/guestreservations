About hotel san giovanni roma

Featuring a bar, Hotel San Giovanni Roma is located in Rome in the Lazio region, a 12-minute walk from San Giovanni Metro Station and 0.9 miles from Ponte Lungo Metro Station. With a shared lounge, the 4-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom. Private parking is available on site. Guest rooms at the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a safety deposit box. All rooms include a desk. Breakfast is available daily, and includes buffet, continental and Italian options. Languages spoken at the 24-hour front desk include English and French. Domus Aurea is 1.2 miles from Hotel San Giovanni Roma, while Vittorio Emanuele Metro Station is 1.6 miles away. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino Airport, 7.5 miles from the accommodation.
