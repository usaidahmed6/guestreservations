About Hotel Everest Inn Rome

Hotel Everest Inn is set in a historic building on Rome's Via Nazionale, 492 feet from Repubblica Metro Station. Rooms come with free WiFi and parquet floors, and breakfast includes fresh fruit. The rooms of the Everest Inn are all air-conditioned and are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, and a private bathroom. Some rooms feature a spa bath. The hotel's staff can provide tourist and travel info. Roma Termini Train Station is a 5-minute walk away.

Amenities

  • Room Service
  • 24 hour front desk
  • Concierge
  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • ATM Machine
  • Currency Exchange
  • Non-Smoking Facility
