About Approdo Ripetta

Featuring free WiFi, Approdo Ripetta offers accommodations in Rome, 984 feet from Sant'Agostino. Every room at this property is air conditioned and features a flat-screen TV with cable channels. Every room is fitted with a private bathroom with a bidet and shower, with free toiletries and a hairdryer provided. Via Condotti is 1640 feet from Approdo Ripetta, while Piazza Navona is 1640 feet away. Fiumicino and Ciampino airports are both within 45 minutes' drive of the property.

Amenities

  • Room Service
  • Masks are available to guests
