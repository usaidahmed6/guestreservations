About Residence Inn by Marriott Newark Silicon Valley

Ardenwood Historic Farm Regional Preserve is 2.9 miles from this Newark, California hotel. Each studio provides a kitchenette with kitchenware. The hotel includes an outdoor pool with hot tub. A free hot breakfast is served daily. Offering free WiFi, studios and suites at Residence Inn by Marriott Newark Silicon Valley include a cable satellite TV and a work desk. A coffee machine and ironing facilities are also available in each accommodation. A gym and a business center are available at this hotel. For guests convenience laundry facilities are also on site. An evening social is offered by the Residence Inn by Marriott Newark Silicon Valley. The 24-hour Market features a wide variety of food and beverage options. Guests can enjoy breakfast or dinner options at The Bistro. Californias Great America Theme Park is 18.4 miles from the hotel. Oakland International Airport is a 25 minute drive away.
