About Agape Hotel

The Agape Hotel AA Hotels is set in a quiet area of Milan, just 66 feet away from the metro. Relax in the hotels comfortable rooms with free Wi-Fi connection. The friendly team of staff will be delighted to welcome you to the Hotel Agape. Its guest rooms are comfortable and decorated in a classic, modern style and feature marble bathrooms. Start your day with a rich buffet breakfast and get into the center quickly and easily by metro from the Crescenzago station just a few steps away. Expo 2015 Exhibition Center is 14 mi away.
