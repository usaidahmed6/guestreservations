About Roma 2B

Located in the Via Veneto district in Rome, 2625 feet from Piazza Barberini, Roma 2B features free WiFi access. The rooms come with a private bathroom. For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hair dryer. A flat-screen TV with cable channels is provided. You will find luggage storage space at the property. The bed and breakfast also offers bike hire and car hire. Quirinale is 0.6 mi from Roma 2B, while Villa Borghese is 0.7 mi away. Rome Ciampino Airport is 9.3 mi from the property.
