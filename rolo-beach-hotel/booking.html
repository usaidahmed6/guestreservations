About Rolo Beach Hotel

Situated in Fort Lauderdale, 600 metres from Fort Lauderdale Beach, Rolo Beach Hotel features a garden and views of the garden. The property is set 2.1 km from International Swimming Hall of Fame, 2.6 km from Fort Lauderdale Beach Park and 4.3 km from Las Olas Boulevard. Free WiFi is available and private parking can be arranged at an extra charge. The hotel will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms offering a wardrobe, a coffee machine, a fridge, a microwave, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a shower. Certain rooms come with a kitchen with a dishwasher, an oven and a stovetop. At Rolo Beach Hotel rooms are fitted with bed linen and towels. Popular points of interest near the accommodation include Bonnet House Museum and Gardens, City of Fort Lauderdale Las Olas Marina and The Galleria at Fort Lauderdale Shopping Center. The nearest airport is Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport, 17 km from Rolo Beach Hotel.
