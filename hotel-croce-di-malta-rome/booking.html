About Hotel Croce Di Malta

A few steps from the Spanish Steps, Hotel Croce di Malta is located in the historic center of Rome. It offers a quiet atmosphere and air-conditioned rooms. WiFi is free throughout. All rooms at the Croce Di Malta Hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV, a safe, and a mini fridge. The private bathroom has a bath or shower and hairdryer. Breakfast is Italian style, with croissants, bread and jam, and juice. The Croce Di Malta is a 2-star hotel housed in a building of the 15th century. Trevi Fountain is a short stroll away, through the narrow streets of the Campo Marzio neighborhood.
