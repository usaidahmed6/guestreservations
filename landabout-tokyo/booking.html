About LANDABOUT TOKYO

Boasting a restaurant, bar, shared lounge and free WiFi, LANDABOUT TOKYO is located in Tokyo, 1312 feet from Kemmyo-in Temple and 1969 feet from Shunsho-in Temple. At the hotel, the rooms are fitted with a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. All rooms will provide guests with a fridge. Guests at LANDABOUT TOKYO can enjoy a continental breakfast. Popular points of interest near the accommodations include Tozen-in Temple, Tokugawa Ietsuna's Mausoleum Gate Plaque and Chosho-ji Temple. The nearest airport is Tokyo Haneda International Airport, 19 mi from LANDABOUT TOKYO.
