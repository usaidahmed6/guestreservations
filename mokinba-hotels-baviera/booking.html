About Mokinba Hotels Baviera

A 10-minute walk from Milan Centrale Train Station, the Baviera Mokinba offers a central location close to the exclusive Via Montenapoleone shopping district. Repubblica Metro is 984 feet away. Buses to Linate, Malpensa and Orio Al Serio Airports stop 1312 feet from this hotel. Air conditioning and satellite TV are standard in all rooms. Each room includes a private bathroom with a hairdryer. Wi-Fi is free throughout the hotel. Breakfast at Baviera Mokinba Hotels is buffet style, with cereals, pastries and cheeses. A choice of restaurants and cafs are located within a 10-minute walk of the hotel.
