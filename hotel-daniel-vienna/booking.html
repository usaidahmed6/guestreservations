About Hotel Daniel Vienna

Hotel Daniel Vienna - Smart Luxury Near City Center combines individual design with a location right next to Belvedere Palace and the Main Train Station. The in-house garage guarantees a pleasant arrival if you travel by car. International cuisine as well as homemade cakes and pastries can be enjoyed at The Bakery. The perfect place to rest your legs after exploring the city. Sustainability is a big topic that shows itself in various ways. Urban gardening, photovaltaic panels on the roof, in-house beehives and E-Parking spaces are some of them. Bikes and Vespa scooters can be rented on site - the perfect means of transport for pleasant sightseeing. The famous Ringstrae boulevard in the center of Vienna can also be reached on foot in 20 minutes or by tram line D in 10 minutes. Art lovers will discover a work of art by the famous Austrian artist Erwin Wurm on the hotel roof upon arrival.
