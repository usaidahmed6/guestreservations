About Cason Del Tormes

The Hotel Casón del Tormes by HappyCulture is located in a tranquil area in the heart of Madrid, between Plaza de España and the Royal Palace. Its spacious rooms enjoy plenty of natural light. Rooms at the Hotel Casón del Tormes by HappyCulture feature a private bathroom, satellite TV and a fridge. They also come with heating and air conditioning. A buffet breakfast is served in the Los Madroños lounge. The central location of the hotel also means guests can easily walk to bars and restaurants in the surrounding area. Plaza de España Metro Station is just 328 feet away, offering direct access to the Art Triangle, including the Prado Museum. Tour busses leave from the famous Gran Via, a 1-minute walk from the hotel. The Hotel Casón del Tormes by HappyCulture offers practical services such as a tour desk and 24-hour front desk. Currency exchange is available at reception. Free WiFi is available.
