About Ajoupa Aparthotel AMMI Massena

This aparthotel is located in in Nice, 656 feet from Place Masséna, and only a 3-minute walk from the beach and the famous Promenade des Anglais. The nonsmoking studios and apartments are air-conditioned and soundproofed. They have windows, a fully equipped kitchenette, TV and a private bathroom. Guests have WiFi access in the studio free of charge. Bed linen, towels and weekly cleaning are included. A Breakfast buffet is available every morning. Just 1640 feet from the property, guests will find the tramway 2 that runs to the airport. The hotel is a 15-minute walk from Nice Ville Train Station and the nearest tramway stop is just 328 feet away. The old town is a 5-minute walk from Ajoupa. The nearest public car park is located 328 feet from the property.
