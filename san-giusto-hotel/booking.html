About Hotel San Giusto

GH Hotel San Giusto is located right next to Piazza Bologna Metro Station, 3 stops from Termini Train Station and 5 from the Coliseum. Rooms feature A mini-bar and air conditioning. The are several bus stops outside the San Giusto Hotel, providing direct connection to Villa Ada park, and the Quartiere Africano shopping district. The catering and food and beverage services in this facility will follow the standards required by the sanitation provisions to combat the Coronavirus (Covid19), therefore they may be subject to limitations in the provision of the service. The San Giusto has a bar, a 24-hour reception and a TV room. Staff can help organize tours and a shuttle service to the airport.
