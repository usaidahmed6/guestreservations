About Arroyo Vista Inn

Located 4 miles from Pasadena city center, this hotel is 25 miles from Los Angeles International Airport. Free Wi-Fi is provided in all rooms. Featuring a view of San Gabriel Mountains, all rooms include an iPod docking station, cable TV and an private bathroom at Arroyo Vista Inn. Styled in elegant dark wood furnishings, all rooms provide plush white linens and cream colored walls. Guests can sip complimentary wine and afternoon tea on the sun terrace onsite. Vista Arroyo Inn features an on-site wine cellar as well. Disneyland is 40 minutes drive from Arroyo Vista Inn. Huntington Library, Art Collections, and Botanical Gardens and the Rose Bowl are 5 miles away.

Amenities

  • 24 hour front desk
  • Airport Transportation
