About Hotel Amico

Located in Milan, 2.1 miles from Fiera Milano, Hotel Amico has accommodations with a shared lounge, private parking and a bar. With a garden, the 1-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom. The property provides room service, and luggage storage for guests. At the hotel, all rooms have a desk. Rooms have a flat-screen TV, and certain accommodations at Hotel Amico have a city view. The rooms have a closet. Speaking English, Spanish, Italian and Russian, staff will be happy to provide guests with practical advice on the area at the reception. CityLife is 2.6 miles from the accommodation, while Arena Civica is 3.2 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Milan Linate Airport, 9.3 miles from Hotel Amico.
