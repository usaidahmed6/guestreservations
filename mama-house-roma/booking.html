About Mama House Roma

Mama House Roma features a balcony and is located in Rome, within just a 5-minute walk of Piazza di Santa Maria in Trastevere and 0.8 miles of Campo de' Fiori. The property is 1.5 miles from Roma Trastevere Train Station and 1.5 miles from Roman Forum. The air-conditioned bed and breakfast also provides a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a bathroom with bidet. A terrace is available for guests at the bed and breakfast to use. Popular points of interest near Mama House Roma include Largo di Torre Argentina, Palazzo Venezia and Great Synagogue of Rome. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino Airport, 10 miles from the accommodation.
