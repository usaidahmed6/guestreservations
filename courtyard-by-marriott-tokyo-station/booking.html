About Courtyard By Marriott Tokyo Station

Open from April 2014, Courtyard by Marriott Tokyo Station offers modern, non-smoking accommodations with free Wi-Fi in all areas. It is conveniently located just a 4-minute walking distance from Tokyo Station. Air conditioned rooms feature contemporary designs and are fitted with a flat-screen TV and refrigerator. En suite bathrooms are fitted with a toilet, bath and shower and come with free toiletries. Free bottled water is provided. At Tokyo Station Courtyard by Marriott, guests can work out at the fitness centre, use the laundry facilities at a surcharge or smoke at the designated smoking area. 24-hour front desk and a luggage storage service are also offered. Complimentary coffee is served at the lobby lounge, if guests bring their own cups from their guestrooms. A buffet-style breakfast is available at the restaurant, while coffee and light meals are available at the caf. The Imperial Palace is a 17-minute walk away, while the upscale shopping district, Ginza is located within a 7-minute walk from the hotel. The Tsukiji Market is accessible by a 12-minute walk and subway ride.
