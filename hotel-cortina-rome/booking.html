About Hotel Cortina

Hotel Cortina offers affordable rooms and is on one of Rome's shopping streets, just a 2 minute walk from the Repubblica Metro Station. The staff is multilingual and provides friendly, helpful service. Termini Train Station is just a short walk away. You can also walk to the Colosseum in 15 minutes from the Cortina. Hotel Cortina's rooms are comfortable and have a minibar, air conditioning and satellite TV. A large free breakfast is provided.
