About TRYP by Wyndham Newark Downtown

Attractively located in the Downtown Newark district of Newark, TRYP by Wyndham Newark Downtown is located 2297 feet from Prudential Center, less than 0.6 mi from New Jersey Performing Arts Center and a 3-minute walk from The New Jersey Historical Society. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, a 24-hour front desk and free shuttle service, along with free WiFi. Private parking can be arranged at an extra charge. Popular points of interest near TRYP by Wyndham Newark Downtown include Alumni Field, Newark Public Library and Newark City Hall. The nearest airport is Newark Liberty International Airport, 3.1 mi from the accommodations.
