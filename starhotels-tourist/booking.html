About Starhotels Tourist

Offering a free fitness center and free WiFi, Starhotels Tourist features modern air-conditioned rooms. It is a few steps from Bignami Metro Stop, with direct links to San Siro Stadium. Milan Bicocca University is 0.6 mi away. Rooms at the Tourist include a mini-bar and a TV with free Sky and Mediaset Premium channels. Each bathroom has a hairdryer and toiletries. A buffet breakfast including fresh fruit and cereals is served daily, while La Cuccagnina restaurant serves Italian and international dishes. You can relax in the L'Agora bar, which serves cocktails and wines in an elegant setting. Starhotels Tourist is 20 minutes' drive from Rho Fiera Milano Exhibition Center, also reachable by public transport.
