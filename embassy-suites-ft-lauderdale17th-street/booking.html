About Embassy Suites By Hilton Hotel Ft. Lauderdale-17th Street

Discover Comfort and Convenience at Embassy Suites By Hilton Hotel Ft. Lauderdale-17th Street

Located in the heart of Fort Lauderdale, Embassy Suites By Hilton Hotel Ft. Lauderdale-17th Street offers a perfect blend of comfort and convenience. This pet-friendly hotel features an outdoor pool, on-site restaurant, and bar, ensuring a delightful stay for all guests. Just an 8-minute walk from the Fort Lauderdale Port Everglades Cruise Port, the hotel is also close to the vibrant nightlife, charming shops, and gourmet restaurants of Las Olas Boulevard.

Each stay at Embassy Suites By Hilton Hotel Ft. Lauderdale-17th Street begins with a complimentary daily cooked-to-order breakfast and ends with a complimentary evening reception. Guests can maintain their fitness routine at the fully equipped fitness center and enjoy many in-room amenities, including flat-screen TVs, microwaves, and refrigerators.

The hotel's prime location makes it an ideal choice for travelers looking to explore Fort Lauderdale's attractions while enjoying the comfort and services offered by Embassy Suites. Whether you're here for business or leisure, you will find everything you need for a pleasant stay.

  • Prime location near Fort Lauderdale Port Everglades Cruise Port
  • Outdoor pool and on-site restaurant and bar
  • Complimentary daily cooked-to-order breakfast and evening reception
  • Fully equipped fitness center
  • Pet-friendly accommodations with in-room amenities like microwaves and refrigerators

Experience the best of Fort Lauderdale at Embassy Suites By Hilton Hotel Ft. Lauderdale-17th Street, where comfort and convenience meet in a vibrant setting.
