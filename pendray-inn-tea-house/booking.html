About Pendray Inn and Tea House

Built in 1897, this historic Victoria mansion provides free WiFi in all rooms. The Pendray restaurant features traditional afternoon tea service Monday and Tuesday from 11:00 to 14:00. Victoria’s Inner Habour is just 984 ft away. A flat-screen TV, a radio, a telephone, and a French press with local organic coffee are included in each uniquely decorated room at Pendray Inn and Tea House. An in suite bathroom offers free toiletries. Select rooms feature harbor views. Guests can enjoy a complimentary breakfast in Pendray's dining room. A free shuttle service to the surrounding area is available by request. The University of Victoria is 20 minutes’ drive away. Beacon Hill Park is 2625 ft from Pendray Inn and Tea House.
