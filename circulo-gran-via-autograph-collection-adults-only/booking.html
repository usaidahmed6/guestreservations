About Círculo Gran Vía, Autograph Collection by Marriott (Adults Only)

Hotel Círculo Gran Vía, Autograph Collection, - Adults only Hotel features a restaurant and a bar. Chueca is 1640 feet away. Every stylish room at this hotel features air conditioned, has a flat-screen TV and coffee facilities. Certain units include a seating area where you can relax. All rooms come with a private bathroom equipped with a bath or shower and free toiletries. Extras include bathrobes and slippers. You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property. Business facilities are also available. Puerta del Sol is 1312 feet from Hotel Círculo Gran Vía - Adults Only, while The royal Theathre is only 0.6 mile away. The nearest airport is Adolfo Suarez Madrid-Barajas Airport, 11 mi from Hotel Círculo Gran Vía, Autograph Collection, - Adults only.
