About A Casa Boschi near Vatican

A Casa Boschi Guest House is located in a central yet quiet neighborhood. At 2297 feet from the main entrance of the Vatican Museums, and the Metro Line A - Ottaviano, Vatican Museums. The structure has 3 independent suites, each with private bathroom inside. Air conditioning in each room. The structure offers bed linen and bath soap hands. Wifi fiber connection. Kitchenette available. Check until 13:00 is assisted by the staff. After 1.30 pm it's remote. You will find plenty of shops and restaurants in the area, plus the traditional Via Doria market. Saint Peter's Square is 20 minutes by walk and stadio Olimpico is far 1.6 mi.
