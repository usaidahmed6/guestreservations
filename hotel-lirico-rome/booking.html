About Hotel Lirico

The Lirico is just around the corner from Rome's Opera House and a 5-minute walk from Termini Train Station. The hotel offers rooms with free Wi-Fi and flat-screen TV with Sky channels, and continental breakfast served in a bar nearby. Hotel Lirico is in the parallel street of via Nazionale, famous for its convenient boutiques. The Viminale Palace is 164 feet away. The staff of Lirico Hotel are available for recommendations on the best places to eat and drink in the area. Reception is up a flight of stairs, but the porter can help with luggage. The hotel is located in a restricted-traffic area. Special permits can be issued for guests traveling by car, upon request and specification of the license plate number.
