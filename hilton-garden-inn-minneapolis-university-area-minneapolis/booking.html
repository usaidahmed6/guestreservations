About Hilton Garden Inn Minneapolis University Area

Located in Minneapolis, 1969 feet from TCF Bank Stadium, Hilton Garden Inn Minneapolis - University Area features accommodations with a restaurant, free private parking, a fitness center and a bar. This 4-star hotel offers an ATM and a concierge service. The hotel has an indoor pool, hot tub and room service. The rooms at the hotel are fitted with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and a shower. All guest rooms at Hilton Garden Inn Minneapolis - University Area have air conditioning and a desk. Guests at the accommodations can enjoy a buffet breakfast. Hilton Garden Inn Minneapolis - University Area offers a terrace. Guests will find a 24-hour front desk, a shared lounge and a business center at the property. U.S. Bank Stadium is 2.7 mi from the hotel, while Target Field is 3.7 mi from the property. The nearest airport is Minneapolis-Saint Paul International Airport, 11 mi from Hilton Garden Inn Minneapolis - University Area.
