About Hotel Alimandi Vaticano

Discover the Timeless Charm of Hotel Alimandi Vaticano

Nestled in the heart of Rome, facing the historic Vatican walls and the entrance to the Vatican Museums, Hotel Alimandi Vaticano offers an exquisite blend of classical elegance and modern convenience. This Art Nouveau-style hotel invites guests to experience its meticulously furnished rooms, each equipped with flat-screen satellite TVs, mini-bars, and private bathrooms. The majority of the rooms offer stunning views of the Vatican Museums or the ancient walls, enhancing the enchanting atmosphere of your stay.

  • Stunning Views: Most rooms overlook the Vatican Museums or the historic walls, providing a unique and picturesque backdrop.
  • Vatican-View Terrace: Relax on the terrace with tables and chairs, perfect for enjoying appetizers and dinner while taking in the breathtaking views.
  • Generous Continental Breakfast: Start your day with a hearty buffet-style breakfast served in a bright and welcoming dining room.
  • Tmark Lounge Bar: Unwind with a drink at the elegant lounge bar, ideal for relaxing after a day of exploration.
  • Private Supervised Parking: Benefit from private and supervised parking, a rare convenience in the bustling area surrounding the Vatican.

Located just 1640 feet from Ottaviano Metro Station and a short nine-minute walk from St. Peters Square, Hotel Alimandi Vaticano places guests at the center of Romes vibrant culture and nightlife. Explore the nearby restaurants, shops, and bars, or simply enjoy the tranquil ambiance of the hotel's Art Nouveau decor. Whether visiting for leisure or business, Hotel Alimandi Vaticano promises an unforgettable stay with its blend of historic charm and modern amenities.
