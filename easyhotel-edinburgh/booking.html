About easyHotel Edinburgh

With scenic views of Edinburgh Castle and Princes Gardens, EasyHotel Edinburgh is located in the New Town district in Edinburgh, 1312 feet from Edinburgh Castle and 1969 feet from EICC. The compact rooms are equipped with a private shower cabin containing a toilet, towels, toilet paper and shower gel. Additional amenities include under-bed luggage storage, air-conditioning and heating. There is a 24-hour front desk at the property. Royal Mile is 2297 feet from easyHotel Edinburgh, while Edinburgh Military Tattoo is 2297 feet away. The nearest airport is Edinburgh Airport, 6.2 mi from the property.
