About Macdonald Hotel

Just 492 feet from Kings Cross St. Pancras, this accommodations is 5 minutes walk from St. Pancras International Rail Station, which offers Eurostar services to Europe. Euston Rail Station is within a 15-minute walk. Oxford Circus, with Oxfords Streets shops, is a 5-minute tube journey away. Regents Park is just over a 10-minute journey from Kings Cross. Buckingham Palace and its gardens are less than 3 miles away. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the Macdonald Hotel. A TV, tea and coffee facilities and a hairdryer are included in each simply decorated room. Some rooms also have a private bathroom.
