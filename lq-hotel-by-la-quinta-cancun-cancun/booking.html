About Wyndham Garden Cancun Downtown

This hotel is located in Cancun, 8 minutes’ drive from the beach. It offers an outdoor pool, fitness center and elegant, air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi and flat-screen cable TV. The rooms at the Wyndham Garden Cancun Downtown feature tiled floors and modern décor. Each one comes with a coffee-maker, microwave and a private bathroom with hairdryer. Wyndham Garden Cancun Downtown offers a daily breakfast of waffles, cereal, fruit juice and coffee. Free coffee is available 24 hours a day. There is also a lobby bar with a big screen. You can drive to the Mayan ruins of Tulum in 90 minutes. Cancun Airport is a 15-minute drive away. Free parking is available at the hotel. The hotel offers a discount for the Cabana Beach Club.
