About LH Hotel Lloyd Roma

On the doorstep of Porta Pia Square, this recently refurbished 3-star hotel provides comfortable, high-quality accommodations in the heart of Romes historic city center. Just a short walk from the most beautiful cultural and artistic treasures of the city, the Lloyd Hotel greets you with its unique style and elegant setting. The charming ambiance and the services quality will make your stay pleasant and extremely comfortable. The classically furnished rooms with attached bathroom offer air conditioning, satellite TV and a mini-bar. Wi-Fi is free throughout the hotel and in the rooms. Set within a tranquil area, the hotel is not far from public transport and a market. Please note that when booking 4 rooms or more, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

Amenities

  • 24 hour front desk
  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • Non-Smoking Facility
