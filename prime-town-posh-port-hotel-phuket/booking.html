About PRIME TOWN - Posh & Port Hotel PHUKET

Located in Phuket, 1.5 miles from Chinpracha House, PRIME TOWN - Posh & Port Hotel PHUKET has accommodations with an outdoor swimming pool, free private parking, a fitness center and a garden. With free WiFi, this 4-star hotel offers a shared lounge and a 24-hour front desk. The hotel has a sauna and a tour desk. At the hotel, rooms come with a balcony. Complete with a private bathroom equipped with a shower and a hairdryer, all rooms at PRIME TOWN - Posh & Port Hotel PHUKET have a flat-screen TV and air conditioning, and selected rooms also offer a terrace. At the accommodation all rooms include bed linen and towels. Thai Hua Museum is 1.6 miles from PRIME TOWN - Posh & Port Hotel PHUKET, while Prince of Songkla University is 2 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Phuket International Airport, 19 miles from the hotel.
