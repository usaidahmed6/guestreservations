About Homewood Suites By Hilton Newark/Fremont, Ca

Conveniently located north of Silicon Valley between San Jose and Oakland, Homewood Suites by Hilton Newark Fremont is adjacent to Newarks Newpark Mall. The hotel provides a free full hot breakfast daily. The Tesla Factory is 5 mi away from the property. Offering free WiFi, the spacious suites at this hotel feature a separate living area with a sofa bed and a fully equipped kitchen with an oven, a stove-top and a refrigerator. Select suites are available with an office work space or a fireplace. Standard room types are also available. Monday through Thursday guests of Homewood Suites by Hilton Newark Fremont can enjoy a complimentary evening social. For recreation, the hotel features a sports court, a fitness center and an outdoor pool. San Jose International Airport is 15 mi away from the property, while Oakland International Airport is 19 mi away. Central San Francisco is 36 mi away from Homewood Suites by Hilton Newark Fremont.
