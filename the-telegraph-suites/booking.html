About The Telegraph Suites

PROPERTY

Essential and elegant design for 16 suites in an ancient building just a few meters from the Vittoriana ‘Altare della Patria’ Monument and the Imperial Forum. At just a stone throw you can savour the majestic Piazza Venezia and the Altar of the Homeland. A few steps ahead and you can admire the splendour of the Trevi Fountain or walk along Via del Corso and Via Condotti, the centre of the most important international fashion houses. 
The surrounding picturesque narrow streets are full of charming restaurants, trattorias and local glamor, where regulars and visitors mingle in a vibrant swirl of voices and colours. Whether it is the art and fashion, design and history, everything is just a pleasant walk. The strategic centrality of The Telegraph Suites is just as important as its enveloping intimacy of the exclusive suites, offering its guests an unforgettable stay. The hotel is located in the limited traffic zone (ZTL). Please ask to our staff for more information.   

LOCATION

Located in the historic centre of Rome, a few steps from Via del Corso, The Telegraph Suites is the perfect mix of sophisticated design and a prestigious location such as Via del Mancino in Rome. The Piazza Venezia and the Altare della Patria are a few minute walk, a few more steps and you can admire the splendour of the Fontana di Trevi, or to walk along Via del Corso and Via Condotti, the centre of the most important international fashion houses.

ROOMS

Nestled nicely in the attractive Via del Mancino a few meters from Via del Corso, The Telegraph Suites welcomes guests in distinct suites exhibiting 60’s design laced with contemporary lines where attention to detail and care provides The Telegraph Suites and its guests a distinct and friendly atmosphere. No stone has been left unturned in the quest to envelop our guests in an atmosphere of beauty. The furnishing, lighting of the suite and the softness of the fabric have specifically thought through for the very purpose. The suites feature an elegant bedroom area, a cosy living room with fitted kitchen, an en suite bathroom with tub (incl shower) or with a comfortable shower box and bathroom amenities, satellite LCD TV, free Wi-Fi and a safe.

TERRACE

The Terrace og f Hotel  is a place you do not expect in the heart of Rome. The terrace at The Telegraph Suites is ready to welcome you for a moment of relax after a day exploring the wonders of the old town. A meeting place for a chat with friends, to relax with a good book or to enjoy a moment just for you nestled among the rooftops of the Eternal City.
