About Beverly Hills Plaza Hotel

Experience Luxury at Beverly Hills Plaza Hotel

Located on Wilshire Boulevard in Los Angeles, Beverly Hills Plaza Hotel offers a unique blend of European dÃ©cor and modern amenities. Just a 5-minute drive from the University of California, this boutique hotel features an outdoor pool, full-service spa, and on-site dining, ensuring a comfortable and memorable stay.

  • Outdoor pool with poolside cabanas
  • Full-service spa with exclusive guest discounts
  • On-site dining at Le Petit Cafe and Bar
  • Private courtyard with hot tub
  • Suites with courtyard or pool views

Each suite at Beverly Hills Plaza Hotel includes a cable TV with in-room movies, a comfortable seating area, and a mini-bar. Guests can enjoy views of the courtyard or pool from their suites, providing a tranquil atmosphere during their stay.

Relax and unwind at the hotel's outdoor pool, complete with poolside cabanas, or indulge in a variety of treatments at the full-service spa, where guests receive special discounts on select services. The private courtyard with a hot tub offers an additional space for relaxation.

Dining is a delight at Le Petit Cafe and Bar, which serves a variety of American dishes all day. Guests can enjoy their meals in the cafe, the garden, or by the pool area, providing a range of dining experiences.

Conveniently located, Beverly Hills Plaza Hotel is within a 15-minute drive of popular destinations such as Santa Monica Pier and Venice Beach. The Los Angeles Convention Center is just 20 minutes away, and the renowned Rodeo Drive is only 1.5 miles from the property.

Book your stay at Beverly Hills Plaza Hotel and enjoy a luxurious experience with modern amenities in the heart of Los Angeles.
