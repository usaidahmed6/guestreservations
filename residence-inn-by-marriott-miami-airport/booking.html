About Residence Inn by Marriott Miami Airport

This hotel is just under 1 mile from Miami International Airport and 8 miles from Crandon Park Golf Course. It has a free shuttle service, a gym and free public Wi-Fi. The rooms at the Residence Inn Miami Airport South have a full kitchen including a stove and a dishwasher. The rooms also include a spacious living area with a work desk and free wired internet connection. In the mornings, guests can enjoy a large buffet breakfast consisting of a wide range of hot and cold dishes. Cane Fire Grille serves Nuevo Latino Cuisine, and there is an on-site Sports Bar. The Residence Inn also has a well-equipped gym featuring the latest equipment. There is also a heated outdoor pool and a whirlpool. Shopping at Dolphin Mall is just a 15-minute drive from this hotel. Guests are 3 miles from the Shoppes at Miracle Mile.
