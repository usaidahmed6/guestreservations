About Hotel Skt. Ann√¶

This boutique hotel is housed in buildings dating back to the 17th century and is located in a central yet quiet area. Nyhavn Harbor is just around the corner and Kongens Nytorv Metro Station is 1640 feet away. The rooftop terrace offers views of Copenhagen. The modern rooms feature a stylish Copenhagen design, a TV, mini-bar and a private bathroom, including a hairdryer and toiletries. The on-site restaurant The Shrimp offers Coastal American cuisine with a selection of steaks, fish dishes and shellfish. Guests can also unwind in the lounge with a cocktail and snacks from the adjoining bar, The Club. Guests can rent bicycles at the reception to explore Copenhagen. Within 5 minutes‚Äô walk from the hotel is Amalienborg Castle and the Royal Danish Theater. Central bus routes are right by the property.
