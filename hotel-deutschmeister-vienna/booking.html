About Hotel Deutschmeister

Located 1.1 miles from Vienna Volksgarten, Hotel Deutschmeister offers 3-star accommodations in Vienna and features a garden. The property is close to several well-known attractions, 1.2 miles from Imperial Treasury Vienna, a 19-minute walk from St. Peter's Catholic Church and 1.1 miles from Parliament of Austria. Free WiFi is available and private parking can be arranged at an extra charge. All guest rooms at the hotel come with air conditioning, a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a safety deposit box and a private bathroom with a shower, free toiletries and a hairdryer. At Hotel Deutschmeister all rooms have bed linen and towels. The daily breakfast offers buffet, continental or Italian options. Popular points of interest near the accommodation include Vienna City Hall, Hofburg and St. Stephen's Cathedral. The nearest airport is Vienna International Airport, 12 miles from Hotel Deutschmeister.
