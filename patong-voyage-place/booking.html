About 99 Voyage Patong

99 Voyage Patong is located in Amphur Kathu, a 8-minute walk to Patong Beach and a 10-minute walk to the Bangla Road Walking Street. The hotel provides easy access to nightlife and shopping options. Free Wi-Fi and parking are available.

Rooms at 99 Voyage Patong feature a private balcony with city views. Each room has a cable TV, minibar and safety deposit box.

The hotel has a 24-hour reception, and provides laundry and tour desk services.

99 Voyage Patong is a 20-minute drive to Phuket Town.
