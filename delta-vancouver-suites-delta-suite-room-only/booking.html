About Delta Hotels by Marriott Vancouver Downtown Suites

This Vancouver all-suite hotel offers high-speed WiFi and floor-to-ceiling windows. The Vancouver Convention Center is only 10 minutes’ walk away. A flat-screen TV feature in the separate living room in the Delta Hotels by Marriott Vancouver Downtown Suites. A work desk, coffeemaker, and mini-refrigerator are provided. Select rooms offer a view. Guests can enjoy a fitness center. Business facilities are also provided. English Bay Beach is just over 1.9 mi from the property. Vancouver Aquarium is 15 minutes’ drive from Delta Hotels by Marriott Vancouver Downtown Suites.
