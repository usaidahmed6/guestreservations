About Pier Sixty-Six & Marina

Stretching along the Atlantic Ocean is the Gulf Intracoastal Waterway, beside which this hotel is located, in close proximity to a multitude of leisure options. Guests are offered a lobby with a lift, a cloakroom, a safe, currency exchange desk and 24-hour reception, a newsstand and a hairdresser, six restaurants and lounges. Guests may also take advantage of the hotel's room service, laundry service (for an additional fee) and onsite parking. Further amenities include 2325 m² of flexible function space, including the 279 m² Pier Top, offering panoramic views.
