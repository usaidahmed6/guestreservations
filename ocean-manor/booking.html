About Ocean Manor Beach Resort

Surrounded by a variety of recreational activities and offering direct, private beach access, this Fort Lauderdale hotel features on-site dining options, spacious accommodations and easy access to area attractions. The Ocean Manor Beach Resort places guests moments from snorkeling, scenic boat cruises, deep-sea fishing and much more. Top-rated golf courses, shopping centers and delicious restaurants are also nearby. While staying at the Ocean Manor, guests can enjoy relaxing with a tropical beverage at the beachfront Tiki Bar, or lounging in the outdoor pool. The hotel also offers free wireless internet access. The Ocean Manor Resort offers guests a variety of accommodations, including cozy hotel-style rooms and spacious suites furnished with full kitchens.
