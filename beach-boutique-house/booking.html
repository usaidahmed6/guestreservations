About The Beach Boutique House SHA Extra Plus

A 10-minute walk from Kata Beach, Beach Boutique offers value-for-money accommodations with private balconies. Only a stroll from entertainment and dining spots, it provides 2 swimming pools and a restaurant. The hotel is 7.5 mi from The Big Buddha, 1.9 mi from Kata View Point and 1312 feet from Kata Center. Phuket International Airport is 20 mi away. The hotel provides a tour desk for guests convenience. Air-conditioned rooms at The Beach Boutique House SHA Extra Plus SHA Plus are equipped with high quality bedclothes and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. They also come with a hairdryer, a mini-bar and a tea/coffee maker. Guests can enjoy massages and body treatments at the spa, or enjoy a swim at one of the outdoor pools. Boutique Beach House also offers sun loungers where guests can relax. The Boutique House's Yanas Restaurant serves international food. Meals can be enjoyed in the privacy of guests room. Other services provided by the hotel include laundry and dry cleaning facilities.
