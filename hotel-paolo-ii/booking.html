About Hotel Paolo II

Hosted in a building from the early 1900s, Hotel Paolo II offers accommodations in a quiet courtyard in Rome, a 15-minute walk from the Vatican Museums. The hotel is 1 mi from Ottaviano Metro Station. The rooms come with a flat-screen TV and air conditioning. Every room is equipped with a safe, mini-bar and private bathroom with a shower. Free toiletries and a hairdryer are provided. Hotel Paolo II features free WiFi throughout the property. You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property and a lounge bar for your convenience. St. Peter's Basilica is a 10-minute walk from Hotel Paolo II, while Roma San Pietro Train Station is 1148 feet away, with links to the Trastevere neighborhood and the city of Viterbo. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino Airport, 9.9 mi from the property.
