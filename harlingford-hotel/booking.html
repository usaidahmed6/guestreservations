About Harlingford Hotel

Located in Bloomsbury, London, the family-run Harlingford Hotel features free WiFi and is just 2297 feet from British Museum. This Georgian styled hotel is also just a 6-minute stroll to Euston Train Station and Russell Square Underground Station. Each of the rooms at the property includes a flat-screen TV, tea and coffee making facilities and a hairdryer. Some also benefit from a private shower or bathtub. Full English and Continental Breakfast is served each morning. After a busy day exploring the city, guests can relax in the communal lounge which features a cozy open fireplace. The Dominion Theater is less than a mile from Harlingford Hotel, while Prince Edward Theater is 1.2 miles from the property. London City Airport is 9 miles away.
