About Alt Hotel Calgary East Village

Discover Vibrant Urban Living at Alt Hotel Calgary East Village

Located in the heart of Calgary, Alt Hotel Calgary East Village offers a contemporary stay with modern amenities and a lively atmosphere. This centrally located hotel is perfect for both business and leisure travelers, providing easy access to the city's top attractions.

Each air-conditioned room at Alt Hotel features a flat-screen TV, a coffee machine, a desk, and a private bathroom with complimentary linens and towels. Some rooms offer stunning city views, enhancing the urban experience. The hotel's allergy-free environment ensures a comfortable stay for all guests.

Guests can enjoy a variety of dining options at the on-site restaurant, which serves American, Japanese, and local cuisine with vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options available upon request. The hotel bar provides a relaxed setting for socializing and unwinding after a day of exploring Calgary.

For recreation, the hotel offers a fitness center and a pool table, while business travelers can utilize the on-site business center. The multilingual staff at the 24-hour reception are always available to assist guests, making every stay seamless and enjoyable.

Alt Hotel Calgary East Village is conveniently located near popular points of interest such as Calgary Tower, Calgary Stampede, and Stampede Park. The Calgary Telus Convention Center is just a 12-minute walk away, and the Calgary International Airport is 6.8 miles from the hotel.

  • Central location in Calgary's East Village
  • On-site restaurant offering diverse cuisine options
  • Fitness center and recreational activities like pool
  • Allergy-free rooms with city views available
  • Close proximity to major attractions and convention center

Experience the dynamic energy of Calgary at Alt Hotel Calgary East Village, where modern comfort meets urban convenience.
