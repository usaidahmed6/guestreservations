About Avani Alonso Martinez Madrid hotel -previously NH Alonso Martinez

The property features a gym open 7 days a week. Air-conditioned, soundproof rooms at Avani Alonso Martinez Madrid hotel -previously NH Alonso Martinez include a 32-inch TV, a mini-bar, safe and desk. Avani Alonso Martinez Madrid hotel -previously NH Alonso Martinez is a 20-minute walk from Madrid's historic center and a 5-minute walk from the famous Chueca neighborhood.
