About Radisson Blu Hotel, London Canary Wharf East

Located in London, a 16-minute walk from Canary Wharf Tube Station, Radisson Blu Hotel, London Canary Wharf East has accommodations with a fitness center, private parking, a terrace and a restaurant. Featuring a bar, the 4-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom. The property provides room service, a 24-hour front desk and currency exchange for guests. The rooms include a flat-screen TV, and some accommodations at the hotel have a river view. The rooms will provide guests with a desk and a coffee machine. A buffet, la carte or continental breakfast is available each morning at the property. At Radisson Blu Hotel, London Canary Wharf East guests are welcome to use a sauna. Guests can enjoy playing pool, or take advantage of the business center. Tube Station is 2.2 miles from the accommodation, while Olympic Stadium is 2.8 miles from the property. The nearest airport is London City Airport, 3.1 miles from Radisson Blu Hotel, London Canary Wharf East.
