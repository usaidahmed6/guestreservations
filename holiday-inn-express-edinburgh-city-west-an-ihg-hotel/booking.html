About Holiday Inn Express Edinburgh City West

This modern hotel has a quiet location with easy access by bus to the city center, 2 mi away. It is 5 mi from Edinburgh Airport and offering parking and air-conditioned rooms. Each room overlooks the city or the Pentland Hills. All rooms are having Walk In Power Showers and Free Wifi throughout the hotel. City West is less than 2.5 mi from Edinburgh Waverley Rail Station. The Scottish National Gallery of Modern Art and the Dean Gallery are less than 1 mi away. Order food from the hotel's snack menu. The bar/lounge serves meals and drinks all day and the large-screen TV.
