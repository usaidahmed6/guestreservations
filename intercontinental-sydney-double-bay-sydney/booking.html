About Intercontinental Sydney Double Bay

InterContinental Sydney Double Bay offers luxurious seaside accommodations, 10 minutes drive outside Sydney City Center. The outdoor swimming pool is crafted with limestone rock walls and surrounded by striped day beds. Guests can enjoy a cocktail at sunset at the roof top bar, before dining at Destination Restaurant. InterContinental Double Bay is 10 minutes drive from Sydney Opera House. A range of shopping and dining options are within 10 minutes walk, and Sydney International Airport is 20 minutes drive away. With sophisticated dcor, all rooms are air-conditioned and offer a flat-screen TV. There is a mini-bar and a tea and coffee making facilities. Private bathroom includes a bath tub and free toiletries. Room service is available from 07:00 - 21:00, as well as a concierge service and a tour desk. Valet parking is available.
