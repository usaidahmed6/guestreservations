About Hyatt Regency San Francisco Downtown SOMA

Located in San Francisco, Hyatt Regency San Francisco Downtown SOMA offers air-conditioned rooms, a fitness center, free WiFi and a shared lounge. This 4-star hotel offers a 24-hour front desk and an ATM. The property is nonsmoking and is set 1969 feet from Union Square. At the hotel, all rooms are fitted with a desk, a flat-screen TV, a private bathroom, bed linen and towels. Each room has a safety deposit box and selected rooms will provide you with city views. The units in Hyatt Regency San Francisco Downtown SOMA are equipped with free toiletries and wireless phone charging stations. Guests can experience the critically-acclaimed French restaurant, La Societe Bar & Café, now open for breakfast, cocktails, and dinner. Named as one of the best restaurants in San Francisco by Eater, Forbes, Thrillist, and more, La Societe is the one of city’s top destinations for modern French cuisine from Chef Alexandre Viriot. There is an on-site bar and guests can also make use of the business area. Popular points of interest near the hotel include Moscone Center, Oracle Park and San Francisco City Hall. The nearest airport is San Francisco International Airport, 13 mi from Hyatt Regency San Francisco Downtown SOMA
