About The Royal Sonesta Washington DC Dupont Circle

Experience Comfort and Convenience at The Royal Sonesta Washington DC Dupont Circle

Nestled in the vibrant heart of Washington, D.C., The Royal Sonesta Washington DC Dupont Circle is a prime destination for travelers seeking both comfort and convenience. Located just a five-minute walk from the iconic Dupont Circle and a mile from the White House, this hotel offers modern accommodations and an array of amenities designed to enhance every stay.

Guests can unwind in rooms equipped with plush bedding and flat-screen TVs, with select rooms featuring deep soaking spa baths for added relaxation. The hotels heated pool and sun deck provide a serene environment for relaxation, while the 24-hour fitness center caters to those maintaining their workout routines.

Culinary delights await at Certo, the on-site restaurant that serves seasonal Italian dishes, complemented by an extensive wine and cocktail menu. The Royal Sonesta Washington DC Dupont Circle is also conveniently located near popular attractions like the Washington National Monument and offers easy access to Ronald Reagan Washington National Airport, just five miles away.

  • Prime location near Dupont Circle and the White House
  • On-site dining at Certo with seasonal Italian cuisine
  • Heated pool and sun deck for relaxation
  • 24-hour fitness and business centers
  • Modern rooms with plush bedding and flat-screen TVs

Whether visiting for business or leisure, The Royal Sonesta Washington DC Dupont Circle promises a memorable stay in the nations capital. Book a stay today to experience the best of Washington, D.C.
