About Hotel De Berne

Offering free WiFi, Hotel de Berne is located in Nices city center. Gare Thiers Tramway Station is just 164 feet away and Nice Etoile Shopping Center is 1640 feet from the hotel. Each room here will provide you with a satellite TV and air conditioning. Featuring a shower, the private bathroom also comes with a hairdryer. Extras include a desk and a safety deposit box. At Hotel de Berne you will find a 24-hour front desk. Other facilities offered at the property include a tour desk, luggage storage and a vending machine. The hotel is a 2-minute walk from Nice-Ville Train Station and 0.6 mi from both Cours Saleya and Nices Old Town. Nice Cte d'Azur Airport is 4 mi away.
