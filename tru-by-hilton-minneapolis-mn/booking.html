About Tru By Hilton Minneapolis, Mn

Ideally located in the center of Minneapolis, Tru By Hilton Minneapolis, Mn has American breakfast and free WiFi. The property is located 3 miles from TCF Bank Stadium, 5.4 miles from Lake Harriet and 11 miles from Mall of America. The hotel provides a fitness center and a 24-hour front desk. At the hotel, the rooms have a desk. All rooms in Tru By Hilton Minneapolis, Mn are equipped with a flat-screen TV and free toiletries. You can play pool at this 3-star hotel. Popular points of interest near the accommodation include Target Field, U.S. Bank Stadium and Minnesota Orchestra. The nearest airport is Minneapolis-Saint Paul International Airport, 9.3 miles from Tru By Hilton Minneapolis, Mn.
