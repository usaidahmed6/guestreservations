About Hamilton Hotel Washington DC

Experience the Heart of Washington D.C. at Hamilton Hotel

Located just steps from the McPherson Square Metro Station and a short stroll from the White House, Hamilton Hotel Washington DC offers a prime city center location for guests looking to explore the nation's capital. This historic hotel combines modern amenities with accessible luxury, nestled amidst the vibrant energy of Washington D.C.

Each guest room at Hamilton Hotel is thoughtfully equipped with a flat-screen cable TV, a coffee maker, and a refrigerator. Desks and comfortable seating areas make it easy to relax or catch up on work. Bathrooms are outfitted with plush bathrobes, complimentary toiletries, and a hairdryer, ensuring a comfortable stay.

The on-site restaurant and bar provide a convenient dining option, offering a range of delicious meals and beverages in a sophisticated setting. Guests looking to maintain their fitness routine will appreciate the 24-hour fitness center equipped with a variety of exercise equipment.

  • Steps away from McPherson Square Metro Station and close to the White House
  • On-site dining and bar facilities for convenient meal options
  • 24-hour fitness center to cater to all workout schedules
  • Near major attractions such as the Smithsonian and Washington Monument
  • Easy metro access to the United States Capitol Building

Hamilton Hotel Washington DC is not just a place to stay—it's a part of your Washington D.C. experience. Whether you're visiting for business or leisure, the hotel provides an elegant base from which to explore the rich history and vibrant culture of the city. Enjoy unparalleled access to famous landmarks, museums, and shopping all within minutes of this prestigious location.
