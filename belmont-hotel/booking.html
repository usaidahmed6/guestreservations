About Belmont Hotel

Near Kensington Gardens, the Belmont Hotel is a 5-minute walk from Paddington Rail Station and the Heathrow Express terminal. Free WiFi is also available. Each room is individually decorated with wooden furnishings, and has a TV for guests to use. There are tea and coffee-making facilities in each room, as well as a telephone. Several Tube stations are within easy walking distance and Buckingham Palace is nearby. The famous shops of Oxford Street can be reached in 15 minutes on foot.
