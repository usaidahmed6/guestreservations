About Augusta Lucilla Palace

Augusta Lucilla Palace offers elegant and air-conditioned rooms with flat-screen satellite TVs and free WiFi throughout. It is set in central Rome, 5 minutes' walk from both Repubblica Metro Station and Termini Train Station. The rooms are en suite and come with smart beds with massaging function and both memory-foam mattresses and pillows. Some feature unique tapestries, paintings, and a sofa for 2 people. Located in an 18th-century building, the Augusta Lucilla has a rooftop terrace, 24-hour front desk and a bar. A buffet breakfast is served daily. Just a few steps from the hotel you will find Rome's Opera House. From Termini Metro Station, there are direct connections to the Spanish Steps, the Vatican and the Colosseum.
