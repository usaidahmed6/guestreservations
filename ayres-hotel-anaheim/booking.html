About Ayres Hotel Anaheim

Offering an outdoor pool and a hot tub, Ayres Hotel Anaheim is 15 minutes’ drive from Disneyland Resort. Free WiFi access is available. Honda Center is 984 ft away. A free full breakfast is served daily. Each room features a flat-screen satellite TV, a work desk, a microwave, and a refrigerator at Ayres Hotel Anaheim. Featuring a bath or shower, private bathrooms also include a hairdryer. At Anaheim Ayres Hotel a 24-hour front desk assists guests. Other facilities offered at the property include a fitness center and a business center. Anaheim Convention Center is 10 minutes’ drive from the hotel. Los Angeles International Airport is 37 mi from Ayres Hotel Anaheim. Angels Stadium of Anaheim is 0.6 mi away.
