About 7Days Inn Changshou Road Subway station

Accepting only Mainland Chinese citizens, 7Days Inn Changshou Road Subway station is a 6-minute drive from Changshou Road Commercial Area. Free WiFi is available in all areas. The Bund is a 20-minute drive from the property. Yu Garden is a 30-minute drive away. Shanghai Train Station is just a 7-minute car journey away. Hongqiao Train Station is 40 minutes' ride away. It takes 45 minutes to reach Shanghai Hongqiao International Airport and 85 minutes to Shanghai Pudong International Airport by car from the property. Each room here will provide you with a TV, air conditioning and cable channels. There is also an electric kettle. Featuring a shower, private bathroom also comes with free toiletries and slippers. Extras include bed linen. At 7Days Inn Changshou Road Subway station you will find a 24-hour front desk. Other facilities offered at the property include luggage storage.
