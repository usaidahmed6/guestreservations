About 7Days Inn Beijing Huawei

Accepting only Mainland Chinese citizens, 7Days Inn Beijing Huawei offers simply decorated guestrooms. Free WiFi is available throughout the property.

Located in Chaoyang District, 7Days Inn Beijing Huawei is within a 2-minute walk of Beijing University of Technology and a 20-minute walk of Panjiayuan Metro Station (Line 10). It is about a 30-minute drive from Beijing Railway Station. Beijing Capital International Airport is 40 minutes away by car.

All rooms are equipped with air conditioning and a TV. Free towels are provided in the private bathroom with a shower.

Staff at the 24-hour front desk can help with luggage storage. The hotel also provides a public computer in the lobby.

Guests can have breakfast at the hotel. Meanwhile, many dining options are located within walking distance.
