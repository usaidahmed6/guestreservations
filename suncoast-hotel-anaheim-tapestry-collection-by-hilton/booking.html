About SunCoast Park Hotel Anaheim, Tapestry Collection by Hilton

SunCoast Park Hotel Anaheim, Tapestry Collection by Hilton: Your Gateway to Disneyland Adventures

Discover the perfect blend of comfort and convenience at SunCoast Park Hotel Anaheim, Tapestry Collection by Hilton. Located just a 12-minute walk from the enchanting Disneyland Resort, this family-friendly hotel offers an ideal stay for both leisure and business travelers. With easy access to Anaheim Convention Center and Angel Stadium of Anaheim, guests are perfectly situated to explore the best of what Anaheim has to offer.

  • Heated outdoor pool and hot tub for ultimate relaxation
  • Spacious rooms equipped with 55-inch flat-screen TVs, microwaves, and refrigerators
  • 24-hour front desk service for seamless guest assistance
  • Convenient Disneyland ticket purchase available at the front desk
  • Proximity to John Wayne Airport, only 9.3 miles away

SunCoast Park Hotel Anaheim, Tapestry Collection by Hilton ensures a memorable stay with its welcoming atmosphere and thoughtful amenities. Each air-conditioned room is designed to provide a comfortable and enjoyable experience, featuring extended cable channels, coffee machines, and complimentary toiletries. Whether visiting for a family vacation or attending a conference, guests will appreciate the hotel's convenient location and friendly service.

Book your stay today and immerse yourself in the magic of Anaheim, where unforgettable experiences await just steps from the hotel doors.
