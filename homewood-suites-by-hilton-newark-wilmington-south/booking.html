About Homewood Suites By Hilton Newark/Wilmington South

Only moments from the University of Delaware campus and close to many Newark attractions, this all-suite hotel features residential-style accommodations, complete with modern amenities and fully equipped kitchens. The Homewood Suites Newark-Wilmington South Area offers spacious guestrooms featuring separate sleeping and living areas along with free high-speed internet access. The hotel also provides a free daily breakfast along with modern fitness and business centers. Outdoor barbecue and patio areas are also available. A number of area points of interest, including Delaware Parks Race Track can be found close to the Newark-Wilmington South Area Homewood Suites. Daimler Chrysler Production Plant, Dover Downs Casino and tax-free shopping at Christiana Mall are also easily accessible.
