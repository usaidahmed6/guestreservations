About BBCinecitta4YOU

BBCinecitta4YOU offers accommodations in Rome, 1.7 miles from Anagnina Metro Station and 3.6 miles from Ponte Lungo Metro Station. This bed and breakfast offers air-conditioned accommodations with a balcony. The bed and breakfast provides mountain views, a sun terrace, and free Wifi is available throughout the property. At the bed and breakfast, every unit includes a desk. There's a private bathroom with bidet in every unit, along with bathrobes, a hair dryer, and free toiletries. At the bed and breakfast, every unit has bed linen and towels. Buffet and Italian breakfast options with local specialities, fresh pastries, and pancakes are available daily at the bed and breakfast. A mini-market is available at the bed and breakfast. Sightseeing tours are available nearby. San Giovanni Metro Station is 4.2 miles from BBCinecitta4YOU, while Università Tor Vergata is 4.3 miles away. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino Airport, 5 miles from the accommodation.
