About London House Hotel

A short walk from Hyde Park, this modern hotel has rooms with air conditioning, a mini fridge and flat-screen TV. Bayswater and Queensway Tube stations are 2 minutes away. The 3-star London House Hotel offers free Wi-Fi and a private bathroom in all rooms. Kensington Palace, Oxford Street and Buckingham Palace are within walking distance. Notting Hill and Portobello Market are also close by.

Amenities

  • 24 hour front desk
  • Concierge
  • Masks are available to guests
