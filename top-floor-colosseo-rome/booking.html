About Top Floor Colosseo Guesthouse

Top Floor Colosseo is located in Rome, 656 feet from Domus Aurea. The rooms include a flat-screen TV. You will find a kettle in the room. All rooms come with a private bathroom fitted with a bidet and shower. For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer. Top Floor Colosseo features free WiFi . The guest house also offers bike hire. Coliseum is 2297 feet from Top Floor Colosseo, while Santa Maria Maggiore is 2953 feet away. The nearest airport is Ciampino Airport, 8.1 mi from the property.
