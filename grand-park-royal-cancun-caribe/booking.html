About Grand Park Royal Cancun - All Inclusive

Experience the Caribbean at Grand Park Royal Cancun - All Inclusive

Grand Park Royal Cancun - All Inclusive is located on a private 500-yard stretch of beach along the Caribbean Sea, offering breathtaking sea views and a variety of amenities for a memorable stay. This all-inclusive resort features two outdoor pools and four restaurants and lounges, providing guests with ample options for relaxation and dining.

  • Private 500-yard beachfront on the Caribbean Sea
  • Two outdoor pools with stunning views
  • Four dining options offering international, Italian, seafood, and oriental cuisines
  • Outdoor massages and spa treatments available
  • Fitness center access for an additional cost

Each room at Grand Park Royal Cancun - All Inclusive offers a sea view, balcony, mini-bar, and flat-screen satellite TV, along with conveniences like an iron and desk. Villas provide additional luxury with butler service and 24-hour room service. Dining options include El Mirador, offering Italian dishes in a fine-dining setting, Cocay with an international buffet, Flor de Sal serving seafood and Mexican cuisine, and Koi Restaurant featuring oriental dishes. Guests can also enjoy outdoor massages and spa treatments or stay active at the fitness center. Cancun International Airport is just a 30-minute drive away, making Grand Park Royal Cancun - All Inclusive a convenient and enjoyable destination for a Caribbean getaway.
