About Deluxe Rooms

Only 984 feet from the Vatican City, White Vatican is a 3-minute walk from Ottaviano Metro Station. It offers air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi and a flat-screen TV, and self-catering units with a kitchen or kitchenette. Soundproofed rooms at the Deluxe come with a small fridge and a private bathroom complete with a hairdryer. Some rooms are located in a separate building nearby and some overlook the street. The staff can give you tips on the area's best restaurants and shops. Fiumicino Airport can be reached by car in 30 minutes, or via a bus stopping in nearby Via Crescenzio. The property is set in an area with no traffic restrictions. Both public and private parking is available nearby.
