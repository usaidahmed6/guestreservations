About LE'DA Hotel SHA

Located within a 3-minute walk of Chalong Beach and 1.6 miles of Mu Ban Tan Khu Beach, LE'DA Hotel SHA has rooms in Rawai Beach. The property is around 1.2 miles from Chalong Pier, 4 miles from Chalong Temple and 7.7 miles from Chinpracha House. The property provides room service and free WiFi throughout the property. The hotel will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms with a desk, an electric tea pot, a fridge, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV, a balcony and a private bathroom with a shower. All guest rooms include a closet. Thai Hua Museum is 7.8 miles from LE'DA Hotel SHA, while Prince of Songkla University is 8.7 miles away. The nearest airport is Phuket International Airport, 27 miles from the accommodation.
