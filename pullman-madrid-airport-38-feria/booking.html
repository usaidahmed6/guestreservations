About Pullman Madrid Airport & Feria

This hotel, next to the IFEMA and Palacio de Congresos business centers, offers rooms with free WiFi and a 32-inch plasma TV. Pullman Madrid Airport & Feria features a seasonal outdoor pool and sun terrace. Guests have free access to the on-site gym. Olivar De La Hinojosa Golf Club is just 0.6 mi away. All soundproofed rooms at the Madrid Airport & Feria have a mini-bar and some rooms come with a bathrobe and slippers. The private bathrooms include a hairdryer and a bathtub or hydromassage shower. Some rooms include free accommodations and breakfast for one child under 12. The hotels restaurant serves Mediterranean and international food. The Cosmopolitan Bar offers an extensive menu. There is also room service. Pullman Madrid Airport & Feria is 328 feet from Campo de las Naciones Metro Station. You can reach Madrid city center in 15 minutes from the hotel.
