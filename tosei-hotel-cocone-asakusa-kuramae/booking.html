About Tosei Hotel Cocone Asakusa Kuramae

Attractively set in the Taito Ward district of Tokyo, Tosei Hotel Cocone Asakusa Kuramae is located a 2-minute walk from Kuramae Jinja Shrine, 500 yards from World Bags and Luggage Museum and a 6-minute walk from Komagatado. With a terrace, the 3-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom. Private parking is available on site. At the hotel, rooms have a desk. The rooms in Tosei Hotel Cocone Asakusa Kuramae are equipped with a flat-screen TV and free toiletries. The restaurant at the accommodation specializes in European cuisine. With staff speaking English and Japanese, advice is available at the reception. Popular points of interest near Tosei Hotel Cocone Asakusa Kuramae include Tokyo Origami Museum, Asakusa Station and Kuramae Mizu no Yakata. The nearest airport is Tokyo Haneda Airport, 13 miles from the hotel.
