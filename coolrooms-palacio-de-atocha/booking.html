About CoolRooms Palacio de Atocha

Located in Madrid, 2625 feet from Puerta del Sol, CoolRooms Palacio de Atocha features accommodations with a restaurant, private parking, a bar and a garden. Boasting family rooms, this property also provides guests with a terrace. The accommodations offers a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, room service and free WiFi. The rooms are fitted with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a kettle, a shower, a hairdryer and a wardrobe. At the hotel rooms come with a private bathroom with free toiletries. Guests at CoolRooms Palacio de Atocha can enjoy an la carte breakfast prepared upon request. Popular points of interest near the accommodations include Mercado San Miguel, Prado Museum and Plaza Mayor. The nearest airport is Adolfo Suarez Madrid-Barajas Airport, 8.1 mi from CoolRooms Palacio de Atocha.
