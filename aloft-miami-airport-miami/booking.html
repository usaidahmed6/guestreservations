About Aloft Miami Airport

Located in Miami, Aloft Miami Airport features a fitness center and guestrooms with free WiFi. 4.3 mi from Miracle Mile Miami, the hotel offers a garden and a bar. The hotel provides an outdoor pool and a 24-hour front desk. All guestrooms in the hotel are fitted with a flat-screen TV, desk and small refrigerator. Each room has a private bathroom, while some boast a pool view. An American breakfast is available every morning at the Aloft Miami Airport. Enjoy the sun terrace or play billiards at this 3-star hotel. For guests' convenience, the hotel has a business center. Marlins Park is 5 mi from Aloft Miami Airport. The nearest airport is Miami International Airport, 1.2 mi from the accommodations.
