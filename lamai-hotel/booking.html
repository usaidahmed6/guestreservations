About Lamai Hotel

Lamai Hotel is conveniently located in Phuket along Pee Road, just a 10-minute stroll to Patong Beach and Bangla Road. Lamai hotel offers a variety of room categories to suit your needs. Starting with our cozy double rooms, perfect for couples or friends traveling together. These rooms feature a comfortable bed, modern amenities, a TV, kettle, coffee and tea station, and two water bottles daily. For those seeking a little more space, our twin bed rooms are ideal. Equipped with the same amenities as the double rooms, they provide a comfortable stay for friends or family members traveling together. Our top-of-the-line suite room, located on the top floor, is the epitome of luxury. Not only does it offer all the amenities of our other rooms, but it also grants you exclusive access to the rooftop. Relax in the bathing chairs while taking in the stunning view. No matter which room category you choose, our hotel promises a memorable stay with exceptional service and attention to detail. Lamai Hotel is located 25 mi from Phuket Airport and 9.9 mi from Phuket Bus Station. It is about 656 feet to Jungceylon Shopping Center.
