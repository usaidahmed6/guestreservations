About Omni San Francisco Hotel

Centrally located at the intersection of California Street and Montgomery Street, Omni San Francisco features a restaurant and elegant rooms. Offering service to San Francisco International Airport, Montgomery Street BART Station is 6 minutes’ walk from the hotel. Blending luxurious 1920s décor with state-of-the-art amenities, each room at Omni San Francisco is equipped with a flat-screen cable TV. Plush bathrobes and free spa toiletries are offered in the private bathrooms. Located on the historic California cable car line, a short walk to Union Square, the Embarcadero, Chinatown, the Ferry Building, and Fisherman’s Wharf, the Omni San Francisco is at the center of the city and provides a true respite for travelers with luxury accommodations and modern comforts. Located in the heart of downtown, Omni San Francisco Hotel is near world-class shopping, restaurants, arts districts and the cable car lines that make the city famous. Whether you are visiting for family fun, a staycation, business or romance, make your trip extra special with one of our packages. At Omni San Francisco you will find a fitness center. Other facilities offered include meeting facilities, a tour desk and luggage storage. Chinatown is 5 minutes’ walk from the hotel. Shops and attractions in Union Square are 10 minutes’ walk from the property. The Ferry Building and the Embarcadero are just 1 mi away.
