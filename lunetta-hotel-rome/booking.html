About Hotel Lunetta

Offering a free spa center and stylish rooms with free Wi-Fi and a 32” LCD TV, the 4-star Hotel Lunetta is in central Rome, just 656 feet from Piazza Navona. The top-floor terrace overlooks Rome’s rooftops. Rooms are air conditioned and soundproofed and have an elegant combination of black and white colors. Each one also comes with refined parquet floors and modern furniture. Some feature wood-beamed ceilings. A varied breakfast buffet is served in a small but colorful breakfast room until 10:30. It includes homemade cakes and croissants, along with cold cuts, cheese, scrambled eggs and bacon. The hotel bar is perfect for a drink or snack and is open until 14:00. Less than 328 feet from Campo De’ Fiori square, Lunetta Hotel is a 15-minute walk from St Peter’s Basilica. The Pantheon is 2297 feet away.
