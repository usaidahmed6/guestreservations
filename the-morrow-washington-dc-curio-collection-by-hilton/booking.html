About The Morrow Washington Dc, Curio Collection By Hilton

Located a 16-minute walk from Washington Union Station, The Morrow Washington Dc, Curio Collection By Hilton offers 4-star accommodations in Washington, D.C. And has a terrace, a restaurant and a bar. The property is around 1.2 miles from Walter E Washington Convention Center, 1.3 miles from Supreme Court and 1.3 miles from The Capitol. The property has room service and a 24-hour front desk for guests. The rooms have air conditioning, a desk and a flat-screen TV, and some accommodations at the hotel have a balcony. All rooms have a safety deposit box. Guests at The Morrow Washington Dc, Curio Collection By Hilton will be able to enjoy activities in and around Washington, D.C., like cycling. National Gallery of Art is 1.8 miles from the accommodation, while National Museum of the American Indian is 1.9 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Ronald Reagan Washington National Airport, 8.1 miles from The Morrow Washington Dc, Curio Collection By Hilton.
