About Hotel Zara Milano

Featuring a garden and free WiFi access available throughout, Hotel Zara Milano offers rooms in Milan, a 10-minute walk from the Milano Garibaldi Train Station. A 24-hour front desk service is available. With air conditioning, each room is equipped with a flat-screen TV and safety deposit box. The private bathroom comes with a shower, bidet and hairdryer. A characteristic Italian breakfast is served daily in the breakfast room. It includes freshly brewed coffee or cappuccino and sweet pastries. Room service is available. The Zara Metro Station is a 2-minute walk from Zara Hotel Milano, while the Sempione Park is 1.9 mi away. The Expo 2015 exhibition center is 9.3 mi from the property.
