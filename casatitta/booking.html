About CasaTitta

CasaTitta Bed & Breakfast is set in the Bicocca - Zara district in Milan, 984 feet from Maciachini metro station. Each room includes a flat-screen TV. The rooms are equipped with a private bathroom. CasaTitta Bed & Breakfast features free WiFi throughout the property. Brera is 1.8 mi from CasaTitta Bed & Breakfast, while Fiera Milano City is 1.9 mi away. The nearest airport is Milan Linate Airport, 5 mi from CasaTitta Bed & Breakfast.

Amenities

  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Non-Smoking Facility
