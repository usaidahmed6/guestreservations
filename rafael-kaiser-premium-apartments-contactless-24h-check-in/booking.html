About Rafael Kaiser Premium Apartments - Contactless 24h Check-In

Rafael Kaiser Premium Apartments offers units with air conditioning, a shared or private roof terrace, free WiFi and an underfloor heating system. They are decorated with various themes and all come with a kitchen and private bathroom. Laundry facilities are provided in the building, which is also equipped with a elevator. Bed linen and towels are changed once a week; additional cleaning and bed linen change is available at an extra charge. Located in Vienna's 18th district, Rafael Kaiser Apartments is a 3-minute walk from the Vienna General Hospital (AKH). The U6 Michelbeuern/Allgemeines Krankenhaus Metro Stop is a 2-minute walk away. The Kreuzgasse Tram Stop (line 42) and a taxi stand are both 2 minutes' walk away.
