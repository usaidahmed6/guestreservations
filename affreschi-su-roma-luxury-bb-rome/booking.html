About Affreschi Su Roma Luxury B&B

Just 220 yards from Piazza Navona and a 10-minute walk from Castel Sant’Angelo, the Affreschi Roma is located in an Art-Nouveau building. It features free Wi-Fi and rooms with frescoed ceilings. Rooms at the Affreschi Su Roma B&B are decorated with antique furniture and luxurious fabrics. Each has LCD TV and a safe, and most have in-room private bathrooms. The Affreschi Su Roma Luxury B&B’s breakfast can be enjoyed in the dining room or in guests’ rooms. The bus stop in front of the Affreschi Luxury B&B has services to Piazza Venezia, 1 mile away, and Termini Station. St. Peter’s Cathedral is a 15-minute walk from the property.
