About Fiesta Inn Cancun Las Americas

Located next to Plaza Las Americas Shopping Center, this modern hotel offers an outdoor pool, , gym and free Wi-Fi zone. Communal areas have beautiful views over Nichupt Lagoon and the Caribbean Sea. Each stylish air-conditioned room at Fiesta Inn Cancn Las Americas offers flat-screen cable TV, a coffee maker and a private bathroom with free toiletries. The hotels spacious open-plan lounge includes a restaurant. Drinks and snacks are available 24 hours a day from room service, the self-service caf or the small shop. Fiesta Inn Cancun Las Americas is a 5-minute drive from the beaches of Mujeres Bay and Puerto Juarez Harbor. Cancun Airport is a 20-minute drive away.
