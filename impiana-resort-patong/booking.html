The resort is completely smoke-free.In limited designated zones, smoking is exclusively permitted. Each morning at Impiana Resort Patong, a scrumptious, homemade breakfast kick-starts the day. Begin your holiday mornings right with your essential cup of coffee, offered daily at the cafe on-site.During your visit, indulge in a range of delightful culinary choices at resort to enhance your experience. Experience unparalleled comfort as groceries can be brought right to your room at Impiana Resort Patong through their distinctive delivery assistance.Impiana Resort Patong provides a superb assortment of leisure amenities for guests to enjoy. During your stay, the resort provides direct access to a beach, ensuring you remain near the sea throughout your visit.Begin your holiday perfectly by taking a plunge into the swimming pool. At the resort, enjoy a laid-back beverage experience by the poolside bar, sipping on a soothing cocktail.
