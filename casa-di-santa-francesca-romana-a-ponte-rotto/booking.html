About Casa Di Santa Francesca Romana a Ponte Rotto

Set in a historical building in Rome’s lively Trastevere district, Casa Di Santa Francesca Romana a Ponte Rotto offers air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi. The Porta Portese Flea Market is 1969 feet away. Classically furnished rooms at Casa Di Santa Francesca come with satellite TV and tiled floors. The private bathroom includes a hairdryer and free toiletries. A sweet and savory breakfast is provided daily and can be enjoyed in the courtyard in fine weather. The surrounding area is filled with traditional restaurant and cafès. The property is 10 minutes’ walk from the Great Synagogue of Rome, while the Bocca della Verità is 1640 feet away on foot.

Amenities

  • 24 hour front desk
