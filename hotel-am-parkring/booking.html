About Hotel Am Parkring

Offering panoramic views over Vienna, the Hotel Am Parkring is located opposite the City Park (Stadtpark) on the Ringstrae boulevard. Guests benefit from air-conditioned rooms and a tablet PC with free WiFi in every room. Hotel Am Parkrings tastefully furnished rooms feature a flat-screen TV with cable and SKY channels, a mini-bar, and a seating area. Tea- and coffee-making facilities are provided in each room, and the suites additionally feature a Nespresso coffee machine with free tabs. Hotel Am Parkring occupies the 11th to 13th floors of the highest building on the Ringstrae. The reception and the lobby bar are located on the ground floor. The rooms can be reached with express elevators. The Weihburggasse Tram Stop is right outside, and the Stadtpark and Stubentor Underground Stations are within 656 feet. An underground car park is available on site.
