About DoubleTree by Hilton Hotel Newark Airport

DoubleTree by Hilton Hotel Newark Airport: A Convenient Stay with Exceptional Amenities

Experience seamless travel and comfortable accommodation at the DoubleTree by Hilton Hotel Newark Airport. Conveniently located adjacent to Newark Liberty International Airport and just 3 miles from Newark, this hotel ensures easy access to the city and beyond. Guests can enjoy a range of top-notch amenities designed to enhance their stay, from dining options to leisure facilities.

  • Complimentary airport shuttle service for hassle-free transfers
  • 24-hour fitness center equipped with modern exercise equipment
  • Glass-enclosed atrium with a refreshing indoor pool
  • On-site restaurant offering American cuisine and a delightful breakfast buffet
  • Hotel bar with a light menu, cocktails, and flat-screen TVs for entertainment

Each room at DoubleTree by Hilton Hotel Newark Airport is thoughtfully designed with comfort and convenience in mind. Guests can enjoy in-room features such as a coffee maker, a work desk, and cable TV, making it easy to relax or stay productive during their stay. The hotel's strategic location also provides easy access to popular destinations such as Manhattan, the Meadowlands Sports Complex, and the Jersey Gardens Outlet Mall.

Whether traveling for business or leisure, DoubleTree by Hilton Hotel Newark Airport offers a welcoming atmosphere and a variety of amenities to make every stay memorable. Book now and enjoy a stay that combines convenience, comfort, and exceptional service.
