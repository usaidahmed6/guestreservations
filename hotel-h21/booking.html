About HOTEL H21

Located a 5-minute walk from Turro Metro Station in Milan, Hotel H21 offers free WiFi. Every unit features a private bathroom and bidet, air conditioning, a flat-screen TV and a fridge. The bed and breakfast offers a continental or buffet breakfast. Gallery of Modern Art is 2.5 mi from Hotel H21, while Villa Necchi Campiglio is 3.1 mi awa. The nearest airport is Milan Linate Airport, 5.6 mi from the property.

Amenities

  • Room Service
  • 24 hour front desk
  • Wheelchair Access
  • Non-Smoking Facility
  • Lounge/Bar
