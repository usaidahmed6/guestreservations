About Austria Trend Hotel Europa Wien

Right on the famous Kärntnerstraße Shopping Street, this 4-star hotel is only a 5-minute walk from Saint Stephen's Cathedral and the State Opera. It offers free WiFi and a rich buffet breakfast at an additional charge. All rooms of the Austria Trend Hotel Europa are air-conditioned and have a work desk, a mini-bar and satellite TV. Guests can enjoy an ample Power Breakfast Buffet with fresh and healthy products. It offers a range of warm pastries, different kinds of bread and a large selection of fresh fruit. The Europa Bar with its relaxed ambiance and elegant style is also a meeting point for trendy locals. The Hofburg Congress and Event Center is a 10-minute walk away. Please note that there are no parking spaces directly at the hotel.
