About Deerfoot Inn And Casino

Welcome to Deerfoot Inn And Casino: Calgary's Premier Entertainment Destination

Perched atop a picturesque hill in Calgary, Deerfoot Inn And Casino offers a unique blend of excitement and relaxation, making it the perfect getaway for every traveler. With proximity to Mckenzie Meadows Golf Club and an array of recreational activities, this 4-star hotel merges comfort with thrill seamlessly. Guests enjoy an unforgettable stay with access to diverse dining experiences, exceptional spa services, and vibrant casino life, all under one roof.

  • Indoor water slide and pools for all ages, including a specific children’s pool, ensuring fun for the entire family.
  • Five on-site restaurants and bars, catering to a plethora of tastes and culinary preferences.
  • Exclusive spa services offering rejuvenating body and facial treatments for a relaxing escape.
  • State-of-the-art fitness center and business facilities, catering to both leisure and business needs.
  • Strategic location offering easy access to local attractions like Techno Sport, Southcentre Mall, and Fish Creek Provincial Park.

Each guest room at Deerfoot Inn And Casino is designed with comfort and convenience in mind, featuring 32-inch flat-screen TVs, in-room movies, and thoughtful amenities such as coffee makers and refrigerators. The added luxury of bathrobes and the stunning views of the city and mountains elevate the stay experience.

Whether looking to hit the slopes at Techno Sport, shop till you drop at Southcentre Mall, or immerse in the natural beauty of Fish Creek Provincial Park, Deerfoot Inn And Casino serves as the perfect base. With its unparalleled services, diverse amenities, and a warm, welcoming atmosphere, a memorable Calgary adventure awaits.
