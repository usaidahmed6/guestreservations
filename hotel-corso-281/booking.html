About Corso 281 Luxury Suites

Corso 281 Luxury Suites offers modern accommodations located in Roma, 656 feet from Piazza Venezia. Free Wi-Fi access is available in all areas. Each air-conditioned suite will provide you with a flat-screen TV, a Nespresso machine and a mini-bar. Featuring free Bulgari toiletries, the private bathroom also comes with a hairdryer and bathrobes. Some studios feature an equipped kitchenette and a furnished balcony. Breakfast features a sweet or a savory option and it is served directly in your room. The Coliseum is a 15-minute walk from Corso 281 Luxury Suites. Trevi Fountain is 1804 feet away.
