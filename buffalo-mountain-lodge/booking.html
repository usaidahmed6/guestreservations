About Buffalo Mountain Lodge

This Banff hotel is located on the slopes of Tunnel Mountain, within a 20 minute walk of downtown. Each room offers a wood fireplace and private bathroom. A cable TV, a coffee maker and ironing facilities are also included in each room at the Buffalo Mountain Guests can enjoy a workout in the gym or relax in front of the fireplace in the lounge. Meeting rooms are available on site for guest use. Mount Norquay ski area is only 5.6 mi away. Banff Gondola is 13 minutes' drive from Buffalo Mountain Lodge.
