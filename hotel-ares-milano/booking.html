About Hotel Ares Milano

Featuring Wi-Fi, Ares Hotel is in the Isola district in Milan and features excellent public transport links. Set just 3 stops from the Fiera Milano exhibition center in Rho, It offers rooms with air conditioning and a LCD TV. All guest rooms at the hotel come with a safe and mini-bar. The private bathroom includes free toiletries and a hairdryer. Most rooms overlook an internal courtyard. A continental buffet breakfast is available daily at the property. Gluten-free products can be provided. Lancetti train statio is just a 5-minute walk away while, CityLife is 1.7 mi from the accommodations. Milan Linate Airport is 0.6 mi from Hotel Ares Milano.

Amenities

  • Room Service
  • 24 hour front desk
  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • Lounge/Bar
