About Mayflower Hotel

Well located for Londons West End, this Townhouse hotel is close to Earl's Court and a 2-minute walk from the Tube. The rooms are Individually decorated and come with a flat-screen TV and complimentary WiFi. The bathrooms have walk-in showers. Kensington High Streets famous shops are a 10-minute walk away, with the renowned Natural History and Victoria & Albert Museums, Harrods and King's Road just 15 minutes' journey by Tube from nearby Earls Court Station.
