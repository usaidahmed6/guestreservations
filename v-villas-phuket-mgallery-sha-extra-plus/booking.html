About V Villas Phuket, MGallery

Located in Panwa Beach, a 4-minute walk from Ao Yon Beach, V Villas Phuket, MGallery provides accommodations with an outdoor swimming pool, free private parking, a fitness center and a garden. Among the various facilities of this property are a terrace, a restaurant and a bar. The property has a 24-hour front desk, airport transportation, room service and free WiFi throughout the property. A buffet, continental or Full English/Irish breakfast is available daily at the property. Panwa Beach is 1.5 miles from the hotel, while Phuket Aquarium is 1.5 miles away. The nearest airport is Phuket International Airport, 25 miles from V Villas Phuket, MGallery.
