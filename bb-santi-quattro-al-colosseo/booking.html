About B&B Santi Quattro Al Colosseo

Located a 5-minute walk from the Coliseum, Imperial Fora and Colosseo Metro, B&B Santi Quattro Al Colosseo is in Rome city center. It features free WiFi . Housed in a historical building, guest rooms come with elegant dcor and tiled floors. Each includes a flat-screen TV and electric kettle. The private bathroom is either en suite or outside the room. Breakfast is a sweet and savory including croissants, homemade cakes, cold cuts and cheese. Under the same management, Il Bocconcino Restaurant serves local specialties , reserves a special discount for guests of the facility and is only 164 feet away.

Amenities

  • Non-Smoking Facility
