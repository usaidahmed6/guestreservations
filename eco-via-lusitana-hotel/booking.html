About Eco Via Lusitana

This smart, modern hotel is set in the residential area of Carabanchel Alto, 3.7 mi from Madrids historic city center. It offers a terrace and free Wi-Fi, 492 feet from San Francisco Metro Station. Rooms at Eco Via Lusitana feature wooden floors and contemporary dcor. All include a mini-bar, and a work desk. The private bathroom includes a hairdryer. A buffet breakfast is served daily and the hotel also has a cafeteria serving drinks and snacks. There are bars and restaurants within 10 minutes walk and the Islazul Shopping Center is 15 minutes' walk away. The Via Lusitana is set just next to the Aguacate Industrial Estate and 10 minutes drive from the Legans Technology Park. You can reach the center via the nearby metro station in around 30 minutes. Eco Via Lusitana has private parking for a fee and is 0.9 mi from the M-40 Motorway. Madrid-Barajas Airport is 20 minutes drive away.
