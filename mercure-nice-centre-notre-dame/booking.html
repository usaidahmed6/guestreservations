About Mercure Nice Centre Notre Dame

Featuring a rooftop pool with panoramic views over Nice's city center, Mercure Nice Center Noter Dame offers a 2000-m interior garden and a shaded terrace, 1640 feet from Nice-Ville Train Station. Free WiFi is available throughout. Each air-conditioned room features a flat-screen TV, desk, wardrobe and mini-bar. Rooms have modern dcor and the private bathrooms include a bath or shower and a hairdryer. Breakfast is served every morning in the spacious breakfast room. Cocktails can be enjoyed on the rooftop bar, Bella Ciela. Snacks are also available throughout the day. The hotel offers a fitness center, 2 whirlpool baths, a Skylounge Bar and 2 terraces with panoramic views over Nice. With a 24-hour reception, Mercure Nice Center Noter Dame also offers room service and luggage storage. The Promenade des Anglais is a 15-minute walk away. Jean Mdecin Tram Stop is 853 feet from the hotel, and Acropolis Congress Center is 5 tram stops away. Parking is available on site for a surcharge.
