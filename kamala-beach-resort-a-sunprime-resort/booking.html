About Sunprime Kamala Beach - SHA Plus

Boasting 4 outdoor pools and easy beach access, this large beachfront property offers spacious rooms set in landscaped grounds. It is a 10-minute walk from Kamala Village’s dining and shopping options. A large flat-screen TV, private balcony and wooden flooring are standard in all air-conditioned rooms. Offering modern interiors, in-room comforts include a bathtub and bathrobes. A 10-minute walk from Phuket Fantasea, Kamala Beach Resort, A Sunprime Resort is a 15-minute drive from lively Patong. It is a 35-minute drive from Phuket International Airport. Guests can work out at the fitness center or enjoy a Thai traditional massage. Providing tour bookings and airport transfers, the hotel also offers water sports by the beach. Hearty buffet breakfasts and Italian specialties are served at our seasonal Restaurant by the pool. Other dining options include a swim-up bar and a beachfront year-round Restaurant by the sea offering Thai and Western dishes.
