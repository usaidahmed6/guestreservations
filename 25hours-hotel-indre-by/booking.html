About 25hours Hotel Indre By

Discover Copenhagen's Charm at 25hours Hotel Indre By

Located in the heart of Copenhagen, 25hours Hotel Indre By invites guests to experience a vibrant stay marked by creative design and a casual atmosphere. Just a short walk from The David Collection and other cultural landmarks, this hotel is perfectly placed for exploring the city's rich history and modern attractions.

All rooms at 25hours Hotel Indre By are air-conditioned and thoughtfully equipped with a desk, a flat-screen TV, and an iPod docking station, ensuring comfort and connectivity. Guests can enjoy panoramic city views from select rooms, enhancing the stay with a picturesque backdrop.

The hotel goes beyond standard amenities to offer unique experiences. From a morning buffet breakfast to relaxing evenings in the sauna, each moment is designed for guest enjoyment. The property’s commitment to an allergy-free environment further underscores its dedication to guest comfort and wellness.

  • Fitness center and sauna for relaxation and wellness
  • Bike rental available, ideal for exploring Copenhagen like a local
  • Terrace with views of the vibrant city surroundings
  • On-site restaurant and bar offering a variety of dining options
  • Business center equipped to meet the needs of professional travelers

With its dynamic blend of modern amenities and a prime location close to landmarks like Rosenborg Palace and Christiansborg Palace, 25hours Hotel Indre By offers an authentic Copenhagen experience. Whether visiting for business or leisure, guests will find everything needed for a memorable and engaging stay.
