About NH Milano Palazzo Moscova

Set in a historical palazzo that used to host Milan's first train station, Avani Palazzo Moscova Milan Hotel -Previously NH Palazzo Moscova- features free WiFi, a restaurant and a wellness center. It is located less than a 5-minute walk from the lively Corso Como and Corso Garibaldi streets in Milan. Featuring air conditioning, the rooms with attached bathroom provide elegant dcor, a mini-bar and flat-screen TV. Some rooms are also set on 2 levels. The property also feature a fitness center and a wellness center. The spa includes a sauna, Hammam and a hot tub. Access to the spa is subject to availability. Avani Palazzo Moscova Milan Hotel -Previously NH Palazzo Moscova- is 1476 feet from Milan Garibaldi Train Station which provides links to Milan Malpensa Airport. A covered parking is available next door, at extra charge.
