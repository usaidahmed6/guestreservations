About GreenTree Inn Beijing Changping District Beiqijia Future Science and Technology City Business Hotel

Located in Changping, GreenTree Inn Beijing Changping District Beiqijia Future Science & Technology City Business Hotel provides air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi. The property is around 14 miles from Beijing National Stadium - Bird Nest, 16 miles from Yonghegong Temple and 17 miles from Shichahai Area. Tiananmen Square is 19 miles away and Qianmen Street is 20 miles from the hotel. Guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a TV. Wangfujing Street is 18 miles from GreenTree Inn Beijing Changping District Beiqijia Future Science & Technology City Business Hotel, while Forbidden City is 19 miles away. The nearest airport is Beijing Capital International Airport, 8.7 miles from the accommodation.
