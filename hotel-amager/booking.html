About Hotel Amager

This family owned hotel is a 5-minute drive from Copenhagen Central Station, Tivoli Gardens and Den Blå Planet aquarium. It features 24-hour room service and bright, fresh accommodations with cable TV channels and private bathrooms. Hotel Amager’s in-house café bar serves a daily breakfast buffet. For dinner, the front desk staff is happy to help recommend local restaurants. Recreation options include a children’s playground and bike rentals. Copenhagen’s bohemian quarter, Christianshavn, is a 10-minute walk from Amager Hotel. Parks and jogging paths can be found along the route. Amagerbro Metro Station is just under a quarter of a mile away and a 10-minute ride from Copenhagen International Airport.
