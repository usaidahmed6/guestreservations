About Holiday Inn Resort Nice Port St Laurent

Facing the beachfront, Holiday Inn Nice - Port St Laurent, an IHG Hotel offers 4-star accommodations in Saint-Laurent-du-Var and has a fitness center, garden and shared lounge. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, free shuttle service and room service, along with free WiFi throughout the property. Private parking can be arranged at an extra charge. At the hotel, rooms come with a desk. Complete with a private bathroom equipped with free toiletries, the rooms at Holiday Inn Nice - Port St Laurent, an IHG Hotel have a flat-screen TV and air conditioning, and some rooms contain a balcony. At the accommodation rooms are equipped with bed linen and towels. Holiday Inn Nice - Port St Laurent, an IHG Hotel has a terrace. Languages spoken at the reception include English, Spanish, French and Italian. Popular points of interest near the hotel include Plage des Flots Bleus, Plage Landsberg and Plage Cousteau. The nearest airport is Nice Cte d'Azur Airport, 1.9 miles from Holiday Inn Nice - Port St Laurent, an IHG Hotel.
