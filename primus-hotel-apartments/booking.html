About Primus Hotel & Apartments

Primus Hotel & Apartments in Vienna, 164 feet from the Kurpark Oberlaa, offers air-conditioned rooms and apartments with free WiFi access. A bar is also featured. The rooms and apartments are fitted with a TV with both satellite and cable channels, and a bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer. Breakfast is served to guests every morning, and there is a snack bar on site to buy small food necessities, as well as a vending machine with drinks and snacks. Packed lunches can be prepared. Alternatively, meals can be taken in a restaurant 492 feet from the property, and more groceries can be purchased in a supermarket 656 feet away. Guests can take a rest in a garden, relax on a terrace or in a shared lounge and sunbathe on a sun deck. The Oberlaa Metro Stop (line U1) is an 8- to 10-minute walk away from the hotel. Vienna International Airport is a 16-minute drive from the hotel.
