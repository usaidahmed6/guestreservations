About Hilton Minneapolis - St. Paul Airport

This Bloomington hotel offers free shuttle services to Minneapolis/St. Paul International Airport, the Mall of America and the light rail station. The hotel features an indoor pool and hot tub. Every room at the Hilton Minneapolis-St Paul Airport provides cable TV with pay-per-view movies. Guests can have a cup of coffee from the in-room coffee makers. Ironing facilities and a hairdryer are also included. Minneapolis Hilton contains a modern gym and a full-service 24-hour business center. This hotel is pet friendly. The Blue Water Grill & Lounge are on-site at the Hilton Minneapolis-St Paul Airport. It serves a buffet breakfast, lunch and dinner offering seafood and steak dishes. The Minnesota Children’s Museum is 9.9 mi from the Hilton Minneapolis-St Paul Airport. The Minnesota Zoo is 11 mi away.
