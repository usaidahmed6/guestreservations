About Hotel Madrid Plaza España Affiliated by Meliá

This modern hotel is set right on Madrid’s Gran Vía, 328 feet from Plaza de España and 10 minutes' walk from Puerta del Sol and Plaza del Callao. Plaza Mayor is a 12-minute walk away. Hotel Madrid Plaza España, managed by Meliá offers free access to a well-equipped gym. The air-conditioned//heating (depending on the season) rooms at the Hotel Madrid Plaza España, managed by Meliá feature free internet access and a TV. Each one comes with a private bathroom with toiletries upon request. Hotel Madrid Plaza España offers modern design in a central location, surrounded by shops, bars, and parks. La Almudena is a 5-minute walk away, while Teatro Real and Templo de Debod are a 10-minute walk from the hotel. Triangulo del Arte is 4.2 mi from the property. The hotel has a stylish dining room with views over La Gran Vía, where a varied buffet breakfast is served. Public parking is available behind the hotel, and guests have free access to a well-equipped gym. The hotel has a nearby parking lot with a 30% discount.
