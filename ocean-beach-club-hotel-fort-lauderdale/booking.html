About Ocean Beach Club

Ocean Beach Club is located 7 minutes' walk from the beach and features a heated, outdoor pool. The property provides free WiFi and is 3.1 mi drive from Bonnet House Museum and Gardens. Guestrooms come standard with a 42-inch flat-screen TV and a private bathroom equipped with a bath and free toiletries. A microwave, mini-fridge, and coffee maker are included in every room. A 24-hour front desk is available. The Galleria at Fort Lauderdale Shopping Center is 3.1 mi drive from Ocean Beach Club, while City of Fort Lauderdale Las Olas Marina is 4.3 mi from the property. The nearest airport is Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport, 8.1 mi from the property.
