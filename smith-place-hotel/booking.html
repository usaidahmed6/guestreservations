About SMITH PLACE HOTEL

Located in Edinburgh, within a 18-minute walk of Edinburgh Playhouse and 1.3 miles of Edinburgh Waverley Train Station, SMITH PLACE HOTEL has accommodations with a shared lounge and a garden, and free WiFi throughout the property. The property is around 1.6 miles from Royal Mile, 1.7 miles from Royal Yacht Britannia and 2 miles from National Museum of Scotland. University of Edinburgh is 2.2 miles from the guesthouse and Edinburgh Castle is 2.4 miles away. Rooms come complete with a private bathroom equipped with a shower and a hairdryer, while certain accommodations at the guesthouse also feature a seating area. At SMITH PLACE HOTEL rooms include bed linen and towels. Camera Obscura and World of Illusions is 2 miles from the accommodation, while The Real Mary King's Close is 2.1 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Edinburgh Airport, 11 miles from SMITH PLACE HOTEL.
