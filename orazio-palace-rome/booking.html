About Orazio Palace Hotel

Offering modern interiors and a relaxing garden, the Orazio Palace Hotel is located in Rome's Prati quarter. It offers air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi and soft bathrobes and slippers. Each room at the Orazio Palace is fitted with a satellite flat-screen TV, and a mini-bar. The private bathroom includes a shower and hairdryer. There is a 24-hour front desk at the property, together with a concierge. Trips and excursions can also be booked on site. Via Cola di Rienzo shopping street is just 394 feet from the property, while Castel Sant'Angelo is a 10-minute walk away. Lepanto Metro is 1969 feet from Hotel Orazio Palace.
