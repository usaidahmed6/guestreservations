About ibis London Earls Court

Explore London with Comfort: Ibis London Earls Court Hotel

Nestled a short walk from West Brompton Tube Station, Ibis London Earls Court Hotel positions guests perfectly to explore the enchanting city of London. With Harrods just four Tube stops away and Fulham within a leisurely 16-minute walk, the hotel serves as an ideal base for shoppers, explorers, and business travelers alike.

Boasting spacious and comfortable rooms, each equipped with a work area, satellite TV, and a private bathroom that includes both a bath and shower, guests are guaranteed a restful stay. Free WiFi throughout the property ensures that everyone, from the leisure traveler to the business guest, stays connected.

  • Conveniently located minutes from West Brompton Tube Station, offering easy access to London's top attractions
  • Spacious rooms with work areas, ideal for both leisure and business guests
  • On-site La table restaurant and daily carvery buffet with seasonal menus
  • Full English and continental breakfast options to start the day right
  • On-site pub for light snacks, live sports, and a selection of drinks

Ibis London Earls Court Hotel is more than just a place to stay; it's a gateway to the vast experiences London has to offer. From the vibrant shops of Oxford Circus to the historic sights of Hyde Park and Kensington, all are accessible within minutes via public transport. Whether visiting for the lively streets of the West End or in town for business, Ibis London Earls Court provides a comfortable and convenient stay, ensuring your London adventure is both enjoyable and memorable.
