About Ocean View Treasure Residence

Offering an outdoor pool and a restaurant, Oceanview Treasure Hotel is located just 1312 feet from Patong Boxing Stadium, 0.6 mi from Jungceylon Shopping Center and 0.7 mi from Bangla Road. All air-conditioned rooms come with free WiFi and views of the city. Staff at the 24-hour front desk can assist guests with luggage storage, laundry and other queries. The hotel also arranges shuttle service to and from Phuket International Airport (15 mi) as well as local attractions. Each spacious room is fitted with tile floorings, a flat-screen cable TV and a seating area. A safety deposit box is standard in all units. The mini-bar area comes with an electric kettle and a refrigerator. Private bathrooms are supplied with slippers and free toiletries. Under a journey of 0.4 mi, guests can pay a visit to Phuket Simon Cabaret, Go-Kart Speed Way and Paradise Beach. The in-house restaurant and bar serves delectable Thai and international cuisines as well as special cocktails and drinks.
