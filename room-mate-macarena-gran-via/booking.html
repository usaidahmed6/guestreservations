About Room Mate Macarena – Gran Vía

Well situated in the Madrid City Center district of Madrid, Room Mate Macarena – Gran Vía is located a 6-minute walk from Gran Via Station Metro Station, 500 yards from Plaza de España Metro Station and a 16-minute walk from Thyssen-Bornemisza Museum. With a terrace, the 3-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom. The property is a 3-minute walk from Gran Via, and within 400 yards of the city center. All guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. All rooms are equipped with a safety deposit box and some feature city views. At Room Mate Macarena – Gran Vía all rooms come with bed linen and towels. Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a buffet breakfast. Staff at the 24-hour front desk speak English and Spanish. Popular points of interest near Room Mate Macarena – Gran Vía include Temple of Debod, Royal Palace of Madrid and Mercado San Miguel. The nearest airport is Adolfo Suarez Madrid-Barajas Airport, 8.1 miles from the hotel.
