About Cable Car Hotel

Located in the famous Nob Hill District of San Francisco, this centrally located 1907 hotel is within 20 minutes' walk to major attractions such as Union square, Fisherman's Wharf, Chinatown and Lombard Street. A cable car stop is across the street from the building. Cable Car Hotel offers guest rooms with both private and shared bathrooms. A microwave, a refrigerator, a work desk, a ceiling fan, and a flat-screen TV are available in each room. Free WiFi is available in all rooms. A vending machine and laundry facilities are offered on site. An Internet workstation is available free of charge in the lobby. Cable Car Hotel is 1.1 mi from Moscone Center, 0.7 mi from Lombard Street and 2.2 mi from Golden Gate Bridge. San Francisco International Airport is 12 mi away.
