About Hotel Bristol

Hotel Bristol features rooms with free Wi-Fi access, just a few steps from Milan Central Station and metro stop that offer links to the city center. All the rooms have a mini-bar, air conditioning, and a TV. Some of the Bristol's rooms feature a spa bath and a balcony. Breakfast is buffet style. Guests can enjoy a drink from the bar and relax in the lounge area. There is also an internet point at reception. The hotel is 3.7 mi from the center of Milan. The bus terminal with connections to the airports is a 5-minute walk away. Garage parking is available nearby with discounted rates for the hotels guests.
