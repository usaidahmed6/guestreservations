About Re di Luna B&B

Offering air-conditioned rooms in the San Giovanni district of Rome, Re di Luna B&B is a 6-minute walk from San Giovanni Metro Station. Among the facilities at this property are a 24-hour front desk and an elevator, along with free Wifi throughout the property. The accommodation features a shared kitchen, full-day security, and luggage storage for guests. At the bed and breakfast, each unit includes a desk, a flat-screen TV, a private bathroom, bed linen, and towels. The bed and breakfast offers some units with city views, and the units are fitted with a kettle. Additional in-room amenities include fruit and chocolates or cookies. Sightseeing tours are available close by. Popular points of interest near the bed and breakfast include Ponte Lungo Metro Station, Porta Maggiore, and Vittorio Emanuele Metro Station. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino, 6.8 miles from Re di Luna B&B, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
