About 7Days Inn Beijing Xikezhan Maliandao Wanzi Subway Station

Accepting only Mainland Chinese citizens, 7Days Inn Beijing Xikezhan Maliandao Wanzi Subway Station offers simply-furnished accommodations for guests to rest in. Free WiFi is accessible in all areas. The hotel is just about a 6-minute walk from Wanzi Subway Station (Line 7), and Beijing West Railway Station is a 12-minute drive away. It takes 24 minutes by car to the famous Tiananmen Square and 36 minutes by car to the Temple of Heaven. Beijing Nanyuan Airport can be reached in a 33-minute drive from the property. Each room at 7Days Inn Beijing Xikezhan Maliandaowanzi Subway Station has air conditioning, a cable TV and a desk. The private bathroom includes a shower, a hairdryer, slippers and free toiletries. The area is popular for sightseeing as well as shopping. A wide selection of restaurants and local street food can be found within less than 492 feet.
