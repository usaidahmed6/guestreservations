About Radisson Blu Plaza Hotel Sydney

Stay at Radisson Blu Plaza Hotel Sydney: Where Convenience Meets Comfort

Located in the heart of Sydney's Central Business District, the Radisson Blu Plaza Hotel Sydney offers a perfect blend of modern amenities and historic charm. Just a 15-minute walk from Circular Quay, guests can easily explore iconic attractions such as the Sydney Opera House, the Museum of Contemporary Art, and the Sydney Harbor Bridge.

The hotel's rooms are designed with comfort in mind, featuring marble bathrooms with walk-in showers, bathrobes, and premium guest toiletries. Some rooms include a separate bath tub, adding an extra touch of relaxation. Each room is equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and elegant décor to ensure a pleasant stay.

  • Proximity to major Sydney attractions like the Sydney Opera House and Circular Quay
  • Award-winning Bentley Restaurant and Bar located in the lobby
  • Convenient access to public transport with Wynyard Train Station just a 2-minute walk away
  • Delicious buffet breakfast served daily at The Lady Fairfax Room
  • Historic building with early 1900s architecture

Guests can start their day with a delicious buffet breakfast in The Lady Fairfax Room or unwind with a coffee at The Fax Bar. The award-winning Bentley Restaurant and Bar, situated on the lobby level, is perfect for dinner and drinks with friends.

The Radisson Blu Plaza Hotel Sydney is ideally positioned for shopping enthusiasts, with Pitt Street Mall and the Queen Victoria Building just a 15-minute walk away. The historic Rocks district is a 20-minute walk from the hotel, offering a glimpse into Sydney's past.

With its prime location, excellent dining options, and well-appointed rooms, Radisson Blu Plaza Hotel Sydney provides a comfortable and convenient base for exploring all that this vibrant city has to offer. Book your stay today and experience the best of Sydney.
