About Riu Palace las Americas - Adults Only - All Inclusive

Located in Cancún, a 2-minute walk from Caracol, Riu Palace Las Americas - All Inclusive - Adults Only has accommodations with an outdoor swimming pool, free private parking, a fitness center and a garden. Offering a restaurant, the property also has a bar, as well as a sauna and a hot tub. The property provides nightclub and room service. The resort will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms offering a closet, a coffee machine, a minibar, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV, a terrace and a private bathroom with a shower. Free WiFi is accessible to all guests, while some rooms contain a balcony. At Riu Palace Las Americas - All Inclusive - Adults Only rooms are equipped with bed linen and towels. Buffet and American breakfast options are available each morning at the accommodation. Guests at Riu Palace Las Americas - All Inclusive - Adults Only will be able to enjoy activities in and around Cancún, like snorkeling and cycling. Speaking German, English and Spanish at the reception, staff will be happy to provide guests with practical advice on the area. Popular points of interest near the resort include Ancha Beach, Playa Forum and Cancun Convention Center. The nearest airport is Cancún International Airport, 14 miles from Riu Palace Las Americas - All Inclusive - Adults Only.
