  1. Click on the link in the email to make a booking.

Additional Details:

  • You can’t apply a promo code after you book: Even if you contact Guestreservations.com support, we can’t apply a current or expired promo code after you’ve booked a reservation (future or past). The promo code also can’t be used if you change or cancel your original booking.

  • Limit one promo code per reservation: There’s no way to apply multiple promo codes for a single reservation.
  • Discount amounts vary: Your final discount will vary and be displayed in your booking summary at checkout. The promo amount may depend on, for example, the total booking amount.
