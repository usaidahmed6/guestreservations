Branson, MO: Top Hotels, Hotels On The Strip And Popular Lodges

July 11, 2022

Branson is nestled in the Ozark Mountains of southwest Missouri. It is known as the “Live Entertainment Capital of the Midwest.” The area combines natural beauty with live entertainment, theme parks, golf, watersports and more.

What is “The Strip” in Branson, MO?

The Strip is the heart and soul of Branson, one of America’s more popular vacation destinations. It is a stretch of Highway 76 running east to west through the city. Visitors enjoy shows, restaurants, shopping and accommodations, all within blocks of one another. The Strip is also known as 76 Country Boulevard.

What are the Best Hotels in Branson, MO?

Whether you prefer a quaint inn, a modern hotel or a luxurious resort, Branson has it all when it comes to lodging. Here are some of the best hotels in Branson, MO.

Chateau on the Lake

415 North State Highway 265

Chateau on the Lake is Branson's only AAA Four Diamond resort hotel. It is high on a hill overlooking majestic Table Rock Lake. Relax and rewind in an amenity-rich environment. There's a full-service spa as well as pools indoors and out. Play a set or two on one of the lighted tennis courts. Take an invigorating hike on the year-round nature trail. This upscale resort offers multiple dining and beverage options onsite.

Cliffs Resort Table Rock Lake

15 Stone Cliff Circle

The 4-star Cliffs Resort offers 30 condominium-style accommodations with furnished balconies or patios. Select from five different floor plans. They range from one-bedroom kitchenette units to two-bedroom penthouse units. Some condos include a view of Table Rock Lake.

Guests relax on a well-furnished sundeck and swim in the infinity pool. Thinking of a BBQ? Head to the big stone patio at the base of the rock bluff. Grab one of the two gas grills and prepare a memorable meal. On a cool evening, gather with family and/or friends around the bonfire fire pit.

Stone Castle Hotel & Conference Center

3050 Green Mountain Drive

Guests at the Stone Castle Hotel & Conference Center enjoy pillowtop beds and a complimentary hot/cold breakfast. Accommodations include standard rooms, jacuzzi suites, kitchenette rooms and two-bedroom family suites. For an unforgettable experience, select from one of the themed suites. Outdoors you’ll find a gazebo complemented by a drawbridge over a pond.

Lodges at Timber Ridge by Welk Resorts

1984 State Highway 165

The Lodges at Timber Ridge offers one, two and three-bedroom villas. Some units include a fireplace, jacuzzi and/or full kitchen. Guests enjoy amenities like indoor and outdoor pools, hot tub and fitness center. There’s also mini-golf and an arcade. The 2,300-seat Welk Resort Theatre is on the premises. It's hosted world-class performers like Trace Adkins and Huey Lewis. The Splash-a-torium is an indoor/outdoor waterpark with a 117-foot waterslide.

Savannah House Hotel

165 Expressway Lane

Guests at the Savannah House enjoy a focus on Southern hospitality. The peaceful country setting affords guests great views of the Ozark Mountains. Take advantage of the concierge services for show tickets and attractions. Select from standard rooms or those with a jacuzzi. The romantic Victorian Suite includes a marble whirlpool and private balcony. Savannah House also offers pet-friendly rooms. Amenities include two outdoor swimming pools, fitness center and complimentary hot/cold breakfast. In the evening, you'll find fresh-baked cookies, hot cobblers and soft-serve ice cream.

Branson, MO Hotels on the Strip

Want to be in the heart of the action? Here are five places to consider.

Gazebo Inn

2424 Highway 76 West

The Gazebo Inn is centrally located on The Strip. Enjoy easy access to the most popular shows, shopping and dining options. The hotel has a distinctive wrap-around porch and an outdoor pool.

Grand Country Waterpark Resort

1945 West 76 Country Boulevard

Guests at the 319-room Grand Country Waterpark Resort enjoy various recreational amenities. There’s a waterpark, two outdoor pools and two indoor pools.

Radisson Hotel Branson

120 South Wildwood Drive

At the Radisson Hotel, each of the 472 guestrooms comes with a pillowtop bed and down comforter. The Radisson also offers concierge services, indoor and outdoor pools and a spa tub.

Grand Plaza Hotel & Conference Center

245 North Wildwood Drive

The Grand Plaza's amenities include an indoor pool, spa tub and 24-hour fitness center. There's also an arcade/game room.

Grand Oaks Hotel

2315 Green Mountain Drive

At the Grand Oaks Hotel, amenities include a fitness center and two spa tubs. Enlist the services of the concierge to get show tickets. Begin your day with a free hot/cold breakfast.

Best Lodging in Branson MO

The lodging options in Branson include condominium resorts, independent hotels and national chains.

Marriott Willow Ridge Lodge

2929 Green Mountain Drive

Willow Ridge is an amenity-rich condominium resort. Guests enjoy an outdoor pool, indoor pool, spa tub and tennis courts. Call on the concierge to arrange for tickets to popular shows.

Lodges at Timber Ridge

1984 Highway 165

The Lodges at Timber Ridge is a four-star condominium resort offering one, two and three-bedroom, units. Guests relax on private furnished balconies or patios. Amenities include a waterpark and fitness center.

Lodge of the Ozarks

3431 West 76 Country Boulevard

Lodge of the Ozarks is in the heart of Branson’s theater district. Amenities include an indoor pool, fitness center and free buffet breakfast.

Econo Lodge at Thousand Hills

230 South Wildwood Drive

Econo Lodge at Thousand Hills is known for its friendly staff and value-priced lodging. Guests enjoy an outdoor pool and free breakfast.

Lodge at the Falls

3245 Falls Parkway

Lodge at the Falls has 62 guestrooms and an outdoor pool. The complimentary breakfast includes biscuits and gravy, eggs, sausage, bagels and more.
