About Amazing Mexican Airport Inn

Amazing Mexican Airport Inn has a restaurant, outdoor swimming pool, a fitness center and garden in Cancún. Featuring family rooms, this property also provides guests with a grill. The property has a 24-hour front desk, room service and currency exchange for guests. At the hotel, the rooms come with a patio with a garden view. Complete with a private bathroom, the rooms at Amazing Mexican Airport Inn come with air conditioning, and certain rooms are equipped with a seating area. At the accommodation rooms contain bed linen and towels. Amazing Mexican Airport Inn has a sun terrace. Cancun Underwater Museum is 3.3 miles from the hotel, while Beto Avila Stadium is 4.5 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Cancún International, 6.8 miles from Amazing Mexican Airport Inn, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
