About Homewood Suites By Hilton Washington DC Convention Ctr Area

Featuring free WiFi throughout the property, Homewood Suites by Hilton Washington DC Convention Center offers pet-friendly accommodations in Washington, 1312 feet from Washington Convention Center. Private parking is available on site. All suites have a seating area; some suites have a separate living room which includes a sofa bed and a flat-screen TV. Guests can enjoy a complimentary hot buffet breakfast every morning and an evening social with snacks and nonalcoholic beverages on weekday evenings. Newseum is 0.8 mi from Homewood Suites By Hilton, while National Gallery of Art is 0.9 mi away. The nearest airport is Ronald Reagan Washington National Airport, 3.7 mi from the property. Property offers Evening Reception on Wednesdays only.
