About Hotel Strand Continental

Hotel Strand Continental is conveniently located in the popular The City area. Both business travelers and tourists can enjoy the hotel's facilities and services. 24-hour room service, 24-hour front desk, luggage storage, Wi-Fi in public areas, family room are on the list of things guests can enjoy. Internet access wireless, non smoking rooms, heating, shower, coffee/tea maker can be found in selected guestrooms. The hotel offers various recreational opportunities. Convenience and comfort makes Hotel Strand Continental the perfect choice for your stay in London.
