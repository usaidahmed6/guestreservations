About Travelodge Hotel Hurstville Sydney

Located in Sydney and with Sydney Central Station reachable within 10 miles, Travelodge Hotel Hurstville Sydney has a restaurant, non-smoking rooms, free WiFi and a bar. The property is around 10 miles from ANZ Stadium, 10 miles from Bicentennial Park and 10 miles from Sydney Showground. Private parking is available on site. The daily breakfast offers la carte, continental or vegetarian options. The reception at the hotel can provide tips on the area. Sydney International Convention Centre is 10 miles from Travelodge Hotel Hurstville Sydney, while Qudos Bank Arena is 11 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Sydney Kingsford Smith Airport, 8.1 miles from the accommodation.
