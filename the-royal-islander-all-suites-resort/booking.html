About The Royal Islander All Suites Resort

The 3.5-star The Royal Islander An All Suites Resort offers comfort and convenience whether you're on business or holiday in Cancun. The property offers a high standard of service and amenities to suit the individual needs of all travelers. Free Wi-Fi in all rooms, convenience store, gift/souvenir shop, luggage storage, car park are there for guest's enjoyment. All rooms are designed and decorated to make guests feel right at home, and some rooms come with flat screen television, additional bathroom, mirror, sofa, closet. To enhance guests' stay, the property offers recreational facilities such as outdoor pool, table tennis. Discover all Cancun has to offer by making The Royal Islander An All Suites Resort your base.
