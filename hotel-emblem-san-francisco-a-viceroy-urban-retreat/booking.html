About Hotel Emblem San Francisco

This Beat Generation-inspired boutique centrally located hotel is located in San Francisco's Theater District, just 4 minutes' walk from Union Square. Hotel Emblem San Francisco, offers eclectic guest rooms with Wi-Fi and room service. Hotel Emblem San Francisco, offers a unique hotel atmosphere, complete with literary-themed furnishings, original art and live music. Cozy guestrooms at the hotel are furnished with state-of-the-art amenities, including smart TVs, bathrobes and Nespresso coffee machines. Plan the day's activities with help from the concierge staff or take advantage of wireless internet access. Relax in the evening with cocktails at the Obscenity Bar & Lounge. Discover many of central San Francisco's most popular attractions, including the historic cable car system, within walking distance of Hotel Emblem San Francisco. San Francisco's Chinatown is 12 minutes' walk from Hotel Emblem San Francisco. North Beach and the iconic Coit Tower are 2 mi away. The hotel features bicycles for touring San Francisco.
