About A+ Bed and Breakfast

Offering air-conditioned rooms in the Central Station district of Rome, A+ Bed and Breakfast is a 4-minute walk from Porta Maggiore. Among the facilities at this property are luggage storage space and private check-in and check-out, along with free Wifi throughout the property. On-site staff can arrange a shuttle service. All units come with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, and a private bathroom with a hair dryer, bidet, and shower. A dishwasher, an oven, and microwave are also featured, as well as a coffee machine and a kettle. Additional in-room amenities include wine or champagne, fruit, and chocolates or cookies. There is a coffee shop, and a mini-market is also available. Sightseeing tours are available within a reachable distance. There is a shared lounge at this property and guests can go pub crawls nearby. Popular points of interest near the bed and breakfast include Vittorio Emanuele Metro Station, San Giovanni Metro Station, and Ponte Lungo Metro Station. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino, 7.5 miles from A+ Bed and Breakfast, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
