About Wyndham Canterbury At San Francisco

Located within 2.5 miles of Fishermans Wharf and Ghirardelli Square, this hotel offers spacious suites with kitchenettes and free Wi-Fi. A gym and business center are also on site. A living room with a sofa bed and cable TV is furnished in all suites at Club Wyndham Canterbury. The modern rooms feature bright color accents and include a large bathroom with bathtub amenities. The San Francisco Wyndham Canterbury has a game room with billiards for guest use. Meeting rooms are also available on site. Alcatraz Cruises and the Aquarium of the Bay are each less than 3 miles from this hotel. Golden Gate Bridge is about 4 miles away.
