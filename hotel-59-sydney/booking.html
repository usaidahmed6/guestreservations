About Hotel 59 Sydney

Hotel 59 Sydney offers accommodations in a convenient location, a 5-minute walk from Rushcutters Park, Elizabeth Bay and Kings Cross Station. Guests enjoy free Wi-Fi. Each room includes air conditioning, heating and a private bathroom. The restaurants and art galleries of Kings Cross are at the doorstep. All rooms feature a TV, ironing facilities, a refrigerator and tea/coffee making supplies. Each private bathroom includes a hairdryer and toiletries. Some rooms feature a spacious lounge area with a sofa. The Sydney Central Business District is 1.2 mi away, while the Sydney Opera House is only 1.9 mi away. Sydney Airport is 6.8 mi away.

Amenities

  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
