About Hotel Rio

The Rio is a charming, small hotel set in Via Mazzini, near Milan's Duomo Cathedral. The Duomo Metro Station, on the red line, is just 328 feet away. From here you have access to direct links to Expo 2015 Exhibition Center. All air conditioned, the rooms come with modern furnishings, a TV and a private bathroom. You can enjoy drinks and snacks at the on-site bar. The Sforzesco Castle and many renowned boutiques, such as Ippogriffo and La Compagnia dell’Oriente, are also near Hotel Rio. Milano Centrale Train Station is 5 metro stops of the yellow line from the property.

Amenities

  • Room Service
  • 24 hour front desk
  • Concierge
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • Non-Smoking Facility
  • Lounge/Bar
