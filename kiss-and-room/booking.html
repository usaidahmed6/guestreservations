About Caos Calmo 2

Caos Calmo 2 is located in Rome, just 2625 feet from the Tiburtina Train and Metro Station. It offers air-conditioned rooms with a balcony and free WiFi. With contemporary décor, these soundproofed rooms feature satellite flat-screen TV, a mini-bar and a dining table. Each has a private bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries. A sweet, Italian-style breakfast is served daily at Caos Calmo 2. A shared kitchen is also available. Villa Torlonia is a 5-minute drive away. check-in from 10.30am
