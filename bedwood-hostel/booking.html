About Capsule Hotel Nyhavn63

Nyhavn63 welcomes guests to experience Nyhavn in a truly unique way through their double luxury sleep capsule. The establishment offers luxurious accommodations with all the amenities one could wish for. Each capsule provides room for two people, ensuring complete privacy. Access to the capsule is granted through a personal code, providing a sense of safety and security. It is worth noting that the pricing is specified for two individuals, allowing guests to share both the experience and costs. The family-run capsule hotel, Nyhavn63, takes pride in its exceptional location in Copenhagen. Located in the most captivating tourist area alongside the canals, where historic wooden ships dock, visitors can enjoy breathtaking views and immerse themselves in a charming atmosphere. Guests can indulge in an authentic Danish experience by staying in a historic property dating back to 1756. The outdoor courtyard offers a tranquil space to unwind and enjoy serenity, while the bar provides a diverse selection of high-quality products that will tantalize taste buds. As the only and first capsule hotel in Copenhagen, Nyhavn63 is housed within a unique historic Danish building at the heart of Nyhavn. Guests can relish the perfect blend of traditional charm and modern comfort and convenience during their stay.
