About The Sanctuary Urban Retreat

Featuring a garden, The Sanctuary Urban Retreat is located in the center of Rome, a 19-minute walk from Roman Forum. The property is around 1.2 miles from Piazza Venezia, a 19-minute walk from Palatine Hill and 0.9 miles from Great Synagogue of Rome. The property provides a 24-hour front desk, airport transportation, room service and free WiFi throughout the property. The hotel will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms offering a desk, an electric tea pot, a minibar, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a bidet. Guest rooms include a closet. A buffet, continental or Italian breakfast is available daily at the property. Popular points of interest near The Sanctuary Urban Retreat include Domus Aurea, The Colosseum and Palazzo Venezia. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino Airport, 8.7 miles from the accommodation.
