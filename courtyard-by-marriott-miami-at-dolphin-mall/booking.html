About Courtyard by Marriott Miami At Dolphin Mall

Convenience and Comfort at Courtyard by Marriott Miami At Dolphin Mall

Located at the bustling Dolphin Mall, Courtyard by Marriott Miami At Dolphin Mall offers a comfortable stay with a variety of amenities. This hotel is perfect for both business and leisure travelers, providing easy access to shopping, dining, and local attractions.

Each nonsmoking room at Courtyard Miami features a 37 flat-screen TV, a small refrigerator, and modern media devices including cable and Pay TV. The private bathrooms are equipped with granite bathtubs and essential bathroom amenities, ensuring a pleasant and convenient stay.

  • Proximity to Dolphin Mall and Miami International Mall
  • Outdoor swimming pool and hot tub for relaxation
  • On-site dining at The Bistro, serving breakfast
  • Well-equipped fitness center
  • Free parking for all guests

Guests can start their day with a delicious breakfast at The Bistro before exploring the nearby Miami International Mall, just 1148 feet away. The hotel also features an outdoor swimming pool and hot tub, providing the perfect spots to unwind after a busy day. For those looking to maintain their fitness routine, the on-site fitness center is well-equipped with modern facilities.

Courtyard by Marriott Miami At Dolphin Mall is conveniently located just a 10-minute drive from Florida International University, making it an excellent choice for visitors to the campus. With free parking available, this hotel offers both convenience and comfort for a memorable stay in Miami.
