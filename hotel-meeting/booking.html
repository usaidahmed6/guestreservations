About Hotel Meeting

Just 2.5 mi from Ciampino Airport, Hotel Meeting is set close to the Rome's GRA ring road. It offers free parking and easy connections to Rome's metro system. Rooms at the Meeting Hotel come with satellite TV, air conditioning and a fully equipped private bathroom. On site you will find meeting rooms, a TV lounge, and a 24-hour reception. On request, a shuttle service to/from Ciampino Airport and Train Station can be organized. The Meeting Hotel is close to a bus stop with frequent local buses taking you to Ciampino Airport and to Anagnina Metro Station on line A, which is less than a 5-minute ride away.
