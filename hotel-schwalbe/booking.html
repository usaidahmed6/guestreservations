About Hotel Schwalbe

A charming Viennese hotel in delightful surroundings, this hotel offers friendly, spacious, excellently equipped and comfortable rooms. Hotel Schwalbe is informal, centrally located, yet quiet. Whether you are in town on vacation or business, this hotel endeavors to make your stay in Vienna something special, something to remember. Traditional Viennese charm, personal attention and a historic building are the hallmarks of the establishment, whose motto is "warm - original - informal".
