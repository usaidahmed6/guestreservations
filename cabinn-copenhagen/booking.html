About Cabinn Copenhagen

Discover Smart, Eco-Friendly Living at Cabinn Copenhagen

Nestled in the heart of the city, just a stone's throw away from Copenhagen Central Station and the enchanting Tivoli Gardens, Cabinn Copenhagen offers a unique blend of eco-conscious accommodation and modern convenience. This hotel stands out for its commitment to sustainability without compromising on comfort, providing guests with a cozy base to explore the vibrant capital of Denmark.

Each guest room is designed with efficiency and comfort in mind, featuring a flat-screen TV, work desk, and a private bathroom equipped with a shower. Complementing these essentials are tea and coffee making facilities, ensuring a pleasant and relaxing stay. Despite the compact size, the clever use of space ensures that all rooms are both functional and inviting.

  • Free high-speed WiFi across the hotel, keeping you connected 24/7
  • Functional and private rooms with flat-screen TVs and private bathrooms
  • Daily breakfast buffet served in a caf with stunning panoramic views of Copenhagen
  • 24-hour front desk for your convenience, ensuring a worry-free stay
  • Extensive parking facility, catering to guests with vehicles

Located directly opposite Denmark's largest shopping mall, Fisketovet, and within easy reach of Copenhagen Airport, Cabinn Copenhagen not only offers the convenience of location but also ensures a sustainable stay with its eco-friendly practices. Whether you're visiting for business or leisure, Cabinn Copenhagen provides everything you need for a comfortable, environmentally conscious visit to Denmark's capital. Experience the charm of Copenhagen from a fresh, eco-friendly perspective at Cabinn Copenhagen.
