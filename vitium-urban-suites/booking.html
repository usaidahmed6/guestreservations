About Vitium Urban Suites

Located in central Madrid, right on Gran Vía Street with its trendy boutiques, Vitium Urban Suites offers soundproofed rooms with plenty of natural light, free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, and heating. All rooms are elegantly decorated and include a flat-screen TV, mini-bar, and safety deposit box. The private bathroom comes with a shower and free toiletries. The property offers continental breakfast. You can also have breakfast served directly in your room. A shared lounge with TV is at guests’ disposal. Airport shuttle services are offered for a surcharge and car rental is also available. Every morning, you will find free newspapers in the lobby. Madrid Principe-Pio Train Station is 0.6 mi from the property and Plaza de España Metro Station is 623 feet away. Prado and Thyssen Bornemisza museums can be reached in 25 minutes on foot.
