About THE REPUBLIC

Set 1312 feet from termini Station, The Republic offers a shared lounge and air-conditioned rooms. Repubblica Metro and square are 5 minutes' walk away. Rooms are all en suite. A continental breakfast buffet is available daily, from 7:00, including ham, cheese, pastries, and hot drinks. Santa Maria Maggiore Basilica can be reached on foot in 15 minutes.
