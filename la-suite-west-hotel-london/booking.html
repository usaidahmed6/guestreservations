About La Suite West - Hyde Park

Located on a quiet street on the edge of London's magnificent Hyde Park, La Suite West is set in an elegant Victorian building and has rooms with free Wi-Fi, a restaurant and a lounge. Bayswater and Queensway tube stations are a few minutes' walk away. An en suite marble bathroom, 32-inch flat-screen TV and blackout curtains are featured in each room, and most feature a four-poster bed. La Suite West is near vibrant Queensway with its shopping, colorful cafs and traditional English pubs. Hyde Park is a leisurely 3-minute walk away. Shopping in nearby Oxford Street and Knightsbridge is within easy reach, and Notting Hill is a 10-minute walk from the building. The famous West End is only 2.5 miles away and Paddington tube station is 15 minutes' walk from the property.
