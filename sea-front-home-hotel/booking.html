About Sea Front Home Hotel - Patong Beach

Sea Front Home Hotel - Patong Beach is just 5 minutes’ walk from Patong Beach and 15 minutes’ walk from Jungceylon Shopping Mall. It offers air-conditioned rooms, a tour desk, and a 24-hour reception. The rooms at Sea Front come with satellite TV, a safety deposit box, and a fridge with mini-bar. Each room has a private bathroom with a hot shower. Free Wi-Fi is available in the lobby. Car rental, luggage storage and laundry services are also provided. Sea Front Home Hotel - Patong Beach is a 45-minute drive from Phuket International Airport.

Amenities

  • Car Rental
  • Balcony
  • Non-Smoking Facility
  • Lounge/Bar
