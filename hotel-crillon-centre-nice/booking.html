About Hotel Nap By HappyCulture

Hotel Nap By HappyCulture is located in the center of Nice, just a 10-minute walk from the Promenade des Anglais and Nice's Old Town, and 853 feet from Place Massena. Acropolis Congress Center is 15 minutes' walk away. Free Wi-Fi access is available throughout . Hotel Nap also offers long stay accommodations with a fortnightly cleaning service The guest rooms at Hotel Nap By HappyCulture feature a Mediterranean décor and are all accessible by elevator. Some rooms have a hairdryer. A continental breakfast box is available daily and can be enjoyed onsite or taken away. Nice-Ville Train Station is a 10-minute walk from the property. The hotel is directly accessible from Nice Côte d'Azur Airport via the Tramway Line 2, which stops at Jean Médecin Station located a 3-minute walk away.
