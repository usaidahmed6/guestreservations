About Hotel Wing International Premium Shibuya

In a prime location in the Shibuya Ward district of Tokyo, Hotel Wing International Premium Shibuya is located a 4-minute walk from Shidax Culture Hall, 500 yards from Shibuya Scramble Crossing and a 4-minute walk from Shibuya Center-gai Shopping Street. Featuring a restaurant, the 3-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom. The property is a 2-minute walk from Ancient Egyptian Art Museum, and within 2.2 miles of the city center. All guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. At Hotel Wing International Premium Shibuya all rooms are equipped with bed linen and towels. Staff at the accommodation are available to give information at the 24-hour front desk. Popular points of interest near Hotel Wing International Premium Shibuya include Shibuya Center Town, Hachiko Statue and Moyai Statue. The nearest airport is Tokyo Haneda Airport, 12 miles from the hotel.
