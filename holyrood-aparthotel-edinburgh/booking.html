About Holyrood Aparthotel

Just off Edinburgh’s Royal Mile in the city center, Holyrood Aparthotel features studio and 2-bedroom apartments, as well as an on-site gym. Each spacious lounge features a large-screen LCD TV, stereo and DVD player. Each self-catering apartment at Holyrood Aparthotel offers modern bedrooms, Italian tiled bathrooms and a dining area. For an extra charge, guests can book breakfast hampers which are available for an additional charge which include a variety of continental items including Bread, Milk, Orange Juice, A selection of Jams, A selection of Cereals and muesli, Butter, Oatcakes, Cheese & Scottish Shortbread. Holyrood Aparthotel features fully-equipped kitchens with washer/dryers and free tea, coffee and milk. There is free internet access in the reception area, and there is also maid service upon request. Restaurants and bars are within a 5-minute walk and Waverley Rail Station is 10 minutes’ walk away. Holyrood Park and the picturesque Arthur’s Seat can be reached in 5 minutes on foot.
