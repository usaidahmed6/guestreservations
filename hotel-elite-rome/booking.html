About Elite Hotel

Well situated in the Spagna district of Rome, Hotel Elite is located a 4-minute walk from Barberini Metro Station, 400 yards from Piazza Barberini and a 11-minute walk from Quirinale Palace. The 3-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with a private bathroom and free WiFi. The property is a 5-minute walk from Spanish Steps, and within half a mile of the city center. The rooms in the hotel are equipped with an electric tea pot. All guest rooms at Hotel Elite are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a safety deposit box. Popular points of interest near the accommodation include Piazza di Spagna Metro Station, Via Condotti and Repubblica - Teatro dell'Opera Metro Station. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino Airport, 9.3 miles from Hotel Elite.
