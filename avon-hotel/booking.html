About Avon Hotel

The Avon Hotel is in Londons West End, just 215 yards from Paddington Train and Underground Station. In a registered historic Victorian building, the hotel features affordable rooms with free Wi-Fi and provides cooked English breakfasts. Rooms at the Avon all have TVs, sitting areas and bathrooms with showers. Hairdryers, irons and tea/coffee-making facilities are also provided. Some rooms have picturesque park views. A full English breakfast is cooked to order and included in the room rate. Guests can relax on the large leather sofas in the lobby. The Avon provides 24-hour access to its guests. The friendly staff can assist guests with sightseeing recommendations and travel arrangements. Hyde Park and Oxford Street, famous for its fantastic shopping, are both within a 10-minute walk of the property. There is a direct connection from the nearby Underground to Heathrow Airport.
