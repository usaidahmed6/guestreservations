About Yve Hotel Miami

Discover Chic Comfort at YVE Hotel Miami

YVE Hotel Miami, a gem located just 1 mile from the bustling Port of Miami, stands adjacent to the vibrant Bayside Marketplace and scenic Bayfront Park. This hotel, mere steps away from the American Airlines Arena, provides the perfect blend of location and amenities, ensuring a memorable stay for guests seeking to explore Miami's iconic attractions.

Key Features:

  • Prime location adjacent to Bayside Marketplace and Bayfront Park, with easy access to the Port of Miami and American Airlines Arena.
  • Complimentary transfer services to the Port of Miami, making it convenient for cruise passengers.
  • 24-hour fitness center and modern meeting facilities cater to both leisure and business travelers.
  • Rooms equipped with a flat-screen TV, work desk, en suite safe, coffee maker, and ironing facilities, ensuring a comfortable and productive stay.
  • On-site Colada Cuban Grill and Cafe offers a taste of local and Cuban cuisine, artisan cocktails, and seasonal craft beers.

YVE Hotel Miami invites guests to experience the heart of Miami from a location that offers easy access to the city's most sought-after shopping, dining, and entertainment options. Whether you're in town for a cruise, a concert, or to soak up the vibrant Miami culture, YVE Hotel Miami is the perfect urban retreat.
