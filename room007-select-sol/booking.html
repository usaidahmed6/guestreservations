About Room007 Select Sol

room Select Sol is set in Madrid, 656 feet from Plaza Mayor and 1312 feet from The Royal Palace. Every room in this guest house is equipped with a private bathroom. For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer. room Select Sol features free WiFi throughout the property. A flat-screen TV is provided. There is a 24-hour front desk at the property. Puerta del Sol is 1640 feet from room Select Sol, while Calle Princesa is 2625 feet from the guest house. The nearest airport is Adolfo Suarez Madrid-Barajas Airport, 8.7 mi from room Select Sol.
