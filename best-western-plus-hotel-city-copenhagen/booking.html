About The Huxley Copenhagen, BW Premier Collection

The Huxley Copenhagen, BW Premier Collection is just around the corner from Copenhagen’s 17th-century Nyhavn waterfront district. It offers free Wi-Fi and rooms with flat-screen TVs and Danish designer furniture. The Huxley Copenhagen's newly renovated, bright rooms all feature Nichba bedframes, Montana shelves, and are all furnished with Arne Jacobsen chairs. A hearty breakfast buffet is provided. Guests can explore the city, and relax in the lobby bar. Staff can recommend or book local restaurants. Kongens Nytorv Metro Station is 5 minutes’ walk from The Huxley Copenhagen, BW Premier Collection. Tivoli Gardens and Amalienborg Palace are also within easy walking distance.
