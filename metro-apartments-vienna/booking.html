About Metro Apartments Vienna

Gartl Apartments is located in the 12th district in Vienna, offering apartments and rooms facing the quiet inner courtyard. The Längenfeldgasste Metro Stop (lines U4 and U6) is just across the street. The city center with all major attractions is a 10-minute drive away. The units include a seating area where you can relax. You will find a kettle in the room. Each room includes a private bathroom, while apartments also feature a kitchen. Gartl Apartments features bed linen, towels, free WiFi and a city map. Raimund Theater is 2953 feet from Gartl Apartments, while Schönbrunn Palace is 1.1 mi from the property. The nearest airport is Vienna International Airport, 11 mi from Gartl Apartments.
