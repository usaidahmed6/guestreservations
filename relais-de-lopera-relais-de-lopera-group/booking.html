About Relais de l'Opera - Relais de l'Opera Group

Just 15 minutes' walk from Romes Trevi Fountain, Relais de l'Opera - Relais de l'Opera Group Group is a renovated 19th-century building offering free WiFi throughout and air-conditioned rooms with flat-screen satellite TVs. The rooms feature a private bathroom complete with free toiletries and a hairdryer. They include a tea/coffee making facilities with a selection of teas. Termini Train Station is a 10-minute walk from the property, while the Spanish Steps are 0.6 mi away.
