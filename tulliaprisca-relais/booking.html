About Tullia&Prisca Relais

Just 984 feet from the Romes Coliseum, Tullia & Prisca Relais offers modern-style rooms set in a historic building. It features free WiFi throughout. The bright rooms come with air conditioning, a mini-bar and a flat-screen TV. Each has a private bathroom equipped with free toiletries and a hairdryer. The area around the property is filled with traditional restaurants and cafs. The Domus Aurea is a 2-minute walk from the Tullia & Prisca. Colosseo Metro Station is 1312 feet away.

Amenities

  • Room Service
  • Concierge
  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • Non-Smoking Facility
