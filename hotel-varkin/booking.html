About Hotel VARKIN

Conveniently set in Tokyo, Hotel VARKIN provides air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, free private parking and room service. This 3-star hotel offers a 24-hour front desk and luggage storage space. Metropolitan Plaza is a 9-minute walk away and Ikebukuro Mitake Shrine is 0.6 miles from the hotel. Guest rooms at the hotel include a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, DVD player, and a private bathroom with a bidet, free toiletries and a hairdryer. Hotel VARKIN has certain units with city views, and every room comes with an electric tea pot. At the accommodation every room is equipped with bed linen and towels. Popular points of interest near Hotel VARKIN include Ikebukuro Nishi-guchi Park, Tobu Department Store Ikebukuro and Ikebukuro Station. The nearest airport is Tokyo Haneda Airport, 17 miles from the hotel.
