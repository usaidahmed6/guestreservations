About ibis Budget - Casula Liverpool

Ibis Budget - Casula Liverpool is on the Hume Highway, Casula, and just off Highway M5 to Sydney. The hotel has free parking and daily breakfast. All rooms are air conditioned and include a writing desk, TV and private bathroom. Ibis Budget - Casula Liverpool (formerly Formule 1) is located close to a range of food outlets and local attractions, and just 5 minutes from Liverpool Train Station and bus services.
