About St Mark Hotel

St. Mark Hotel is a 5-minute walk from Earl's Court Tube Station. Each room has a microwave and fridge. The comfortable rooms have private bathrooms, tea/coffee and a TV. Wi-Fi internet access is available throughout the building. A continental breakfast is available to guests each morning, and St. Mark also features a relaxing lounge and a 24-hour front desk. The hotel is located in Earl's Court, close to many restaurants and shops. The Olympia Exhibition Center is within walking distance of the hotel.
