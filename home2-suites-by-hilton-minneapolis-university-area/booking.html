About Home2 Suites by Hilton Minneapolis University Area

Located within a 9-minute walk of TCF Bank Stadium and 2.8 miles of U.S. Bank Stadium, Home2 Suites By Hilton Minneapolis University Area has rooms in Minneapolis. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, a 24-hour front desk and an ATM, along with free WiFi. Private parking is available on site. All guest rooms at the hotel come with a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a kitchenette. All rooms at Home2 Suites By Hilton Minneapolis University Area feature air conditioning and a desk. The accommodation offers a buffet or continental breakfast. Home2 Suites By Hilton Minneapolis University Area offers 3-star accommodations with an indoor pool. Target Field is 4 miles from the hotel, while Lake Harriet is 7.3 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Minneapolis-Saint Paul International Airport, 6.2 miles from Home2 Suites By Hilton Minneapolis University Area.
