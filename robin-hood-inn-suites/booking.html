About Robin Hood Motel

E-Bikes and Kayaks: Explore Victoria like never before with our complimentary e-bikes, kayaks, and SUP adventures. Unleash your inner adventurer and immerse yourself in the natural beauty of our surroundings. Continental Buffet Breakfast: Start your day on a high note with our delectable continental buffet breakfast. Savor a wide selection of freshly baked pastries, yogurt, fruit, and much more. Impeccable Cleanliness: We take pride in our spotlessly clean rooms and facilities. Rest easy knowing that we maintain the highest standards of hygiene to ensure your comfort and well-being. Located 5 minutes' drive from Victoria city center, this hotel offers free Wi-Fi in all areas. An on-site launderette is provided. A flat-screen cable TV is offered in each guest room at Robin Hood Inn and Suites. Swartz Bay Ferry Terminal is 19 mi from Robin Hood Inn and Suites. Gorge Vale Golf Club is 5 minutes' drive from this hotel.
