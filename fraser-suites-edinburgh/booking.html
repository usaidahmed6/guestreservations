About Fraser Suites Edinburgh

Located in the city center, Fraser Suites Edinburgh serviced apartments offer spacious and furnished rooms and suites that are perfect for both short and long stays. Equipped with convenient laundry services, a gym, and various rooms options, the apartments are an ideal alternative to a hotel stay in Edinburgh city center for both business and leisure travelers. The Edinburgh aparthotel’s sandstone Victorian facade is a landmark in the famous Old Town district, just a stone’s throw from Edinburgh Castle and located just off the Royal Mile on St Giles’ Street. History comes alive at Fraser Suites Edinburgh, with its comfortable boutique serviced apartments in the city center, housed in a beautifully conserved 130-year-old building that epitomizes the rich heritage of the Scottish capital. The comfortable apartments at Fraser Suites Edinburgh combine Victorian charm with contemporary interiors and modern conveniences. Each of its 75 elegant fully serviced apartments in Edinburgh offers exclusivity and the comforts of home right in the Scottish capital
