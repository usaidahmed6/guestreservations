About Mercure Roma West

Mercure Roma West is in the residential EUR district, 9.3 mi from Rome's historical center. It offers free WiFi and air-conditioned rooms with classic or modern dcor. Its Mediterranean restaurant overlooks the surrounding greenery. Featuring a flat-screen TV, all rooms include a mini-bar with free mineral water. They come with carpeted floors, and most are decorated with earth tones. Their marble bathroom is complete with toiletries and a hairdryer. A sweet and savory breakfast buffet is available daily. The De Gustibus Restaurant features large windows and vaulted ceilings. It serves both Italian and international dishes, and a selection of fine wines. Snacks and cocktails can be enjoyed at the Bar Divinus. The wellness area includes a hydromassage corner, a gym with Technogym equipment, a heated swimming pool, a hot tub, a sauna and hammam. Entrance upon payment. Wellness area and pool are temporarily closed. Guests can use 2 free internet points available in the lobby.
