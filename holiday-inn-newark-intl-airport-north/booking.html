About Holiday Inn Newark International Airport

Renovated and newly opened April 2022. The hotel is located just 2 easy miles away from Newark International Airport (EWR). Holiday Inn Newark International Airport hotel features a variety of dining options and an on-site fitness center opening July 2022. New York City is just 11.9 miles away or 20 minutes on the train. Each guest room at the Holiday Inn Newark International Airport hotel has a cable TV, free WiFi access, mini-refrigerator and Keurig coffee-maker. They are warmly decorated in earth tones and have large windows. Guests can use the on-site business center, as well as the laundry facilities. The front desk is available 24-hours. The Cafe is open 7am-10pm and serves breakfast, lunch, and dinner, while Teds restaurant offers American cuisine for lunch and dinner starting at 3pm. The Prudential Center is 2.9 miles away from the Holiday Inn Newark International Airport. The Meadowlands Sports Complex is 12.4 miles away. Newark Penn Station is 2.9 miles away.
