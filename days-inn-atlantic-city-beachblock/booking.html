About Days Inn by Wyndham Atlantic City Beachblock

Located just a 1-minute walk from the Atlantic City beach and boardwalk, Days Inn by Wyndham Atlantic City Beachblock offers convenient services including free WiFi access and Light Breakfast Included which contains Coffee, a packed pastry and a small bottle of juice. A 32-inch flat-screen TV is provided in each air-conditioned guest room. Free daily newspapers are available to guests at the Atlantic City Days Inn by Wyndham. Ripley's Believe It or Not Museum is 3 minutes' walk and Steel Pier is 13 minutes' walk from the property. Free parking for motel guests
