About ibis Nice Centre Notre-Dame

Located 820 feet from Nice-Ville Train Station, and a 3-minute walk from Gare Thiers Tram Stop on Jean-Mdecin Avenue, ibis Nice Center Noter Dame offers a 24-hour front desk, free WiFi and air-conditioned rooms. All soundproofed rooms feature a flat-screen TV with cable channels. The private bathroom is complete with a hairdryer, free toiletries and a shower. A buffet breakfast composed of sweet and savory dishes such as eggs, fruit salad, yogurts and juices is served every day. Pastries baked on site and fresh French Madeleine cakes are also on offer, as well as a hot beverage and a piece of fruit to take away. Outside of regular breakfast hours guests can also enjoy a lighter option, available from 04:00. The property has a bar where snacks are served 24 hours a day. Restaurants can be found within 2133 feet of the ibis Nice Center Noter Dame. This hotel is 5.6 mi from the A8 motorway and 3.7 mi from Nice Cte d'Azur Airport. Cannes is 21 mi away. A paid public parking can be found 656 feet away.
