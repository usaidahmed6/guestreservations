About Quest at Sydney Olympic Park

Experience Unmatched Convenience at Quest at Sydney Olympic Park

Nestled in the heart of Sydney's dynamic Olympic Park precinct, Quest at Sydney Olympic Park offers an exceptional stay for both business and leisure travelers. Just a short stroll from iconic venues like Qudos Bank Arena and Accor Stadium, this hotel provides the perfect blend of comfort and convenience. Whether attending a major event, exploring the vibrant surroundings, or conducting business, Quest at Sydney Olympic Park is your ideal accommodation choice.

The hotel features a range of well-appointed studio, one, two, and three-bedroom apartments. Each apartment is designed to cater to the needs of modern travelers, offering fully equipped kitchens or kitchenettes, living and dining areas, and in-unit laundry facilities. With a reception that operates from 7:00 am to 11:00 pm, guests enjoy seamless check-ins and support throughout their stay. A well-equipped fitness center is available to keep up with your workout routine even while traveling.

  • Prime location within walking distance to Sydney Olympic Park attractions
  • Spacious apartments with fully equipped kitchens and laundry facilities
  • Fitness center to maintain your exercise regimen
  • Reception services available from 7:00 am to 11:00 pm
  • Close proximity to Sydney Showground and Olympic Park Station

Quest at Sydney Olympic Park is perfectly positioned for guests to explore all that Sydney has to offer. The Sydney Showground is a mere 0.6 miles away, while Olympic Park Station, offering easy access to the city's extensive public transport network, is just a seven-minute walk. The drive to Circular Quay, Sydney's bustling harbor area, takes about 35 minutes, while Central Sydney is a quick 25-minute drive. For those arriving by air, Sydney International Airport is conveniently located 35 minutes away by car.

Discover unparalleled convenience and comfort at Quest at Sydney Olympic Park, your home away from home in the vibrant city of Sydney.
