About Hilton London Angel Islington

Experience Urban Chic at Hilton London Islington

Located in the vibrant heart of fashionable Islington, Hilton London Angel Islington offers a sophisticated and convenient base for exploring London's eclectic mix of new and old. Positioned just a 5-minute walk from Angel Tube station, this sleek hotel merges prime location with impeccable service and modern comfort.

Each guest room and suite at Hilton London Angel Islington features contemporary decor, a work desk, and a flat-screen TV, with select rooms boasting striking city views. Guests will appreciate the convenience of 24-hour room service and free limited WiFi in public areas.

The hotel's dining options cater to a variety of tastes, serving both locally and internationally inspired cuisine. Open for breakfast, lunch, and dinner, the onsite bar and restaurant provide a perfect setting for a casual meal or a relaxing evening drink.

  • Central location near Angel Tube station and St Pancras International
  • Contemporary guest rooms with optional city views
  • Onsite dining featuring local and international dishes
  • Adjacent to the Business Design Center
  • Easy access to Emirates Stadium and Camden Market

Hilton London Angel Islington is ideally situated next to the Business Design Center and within a short drive of both the Emirates Stadium and the lively scenes of Camden Market. Whether in town for business or leisure, guests will find Hilton London Angel Islington a perfect blend of convenience and style, all within the pulsating energy of London’s Islington district.
