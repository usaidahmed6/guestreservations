About Hotel Fiori

Located in the heart of Rome, Hotel Fiori is within a 15-minute walk from the Coliseum, the Pantheon and Trevi Fountain. It offers air-conditioned rooms with a TV and private bathroom. WiFi is free throughout. Each morning you will be served a sweet and savory continental breakfast in the bright breakfast room, which overlooks the ancient ruins of Trajans Market. The Fiori is 2461 feet from Cavour Metro Station and 0.9 mi from Roma Termini Train Station. Hotel staff are available 24 hours a day, and are happy to recommend restaurants, museums and excursions.
