About Qt Bondi

Opened in December 2015, QT Bondi offers a glamorous beachside escape. Located just 5 minutes’ walk from the sandy shores and blue waters of world-famous Bondi Beach, you can enjoy living like a local, with an array of restaurants, cafes, and stores at your fingertips. Guests can relax with free WiFi and in-room movies. You can take in the spectacular ocean views from the Bondi to Bronte Coastal walk, which begins 5 minutes’ walk from the hotel. Westfield Bondi Junction is 10 minutes’ drive away. QT Bondi is 20 minutes’ drive from Sydney City Center and 25 minutes’ drive from Sydney Airport. Accommodations include guest rooms, and spacious apartments. Each stylish room is air-conditioned and offers a balcony, a flat-screen TV with Foxtel channels, and laundry facilities. The modern bathroom features a bathtub, Kevin Murphy toiletries, and a hairdryer. Kitchen facilities include a coffee machine, microwave, toaster, and a mini-bar. Guests have access to the services and amenities at Pacific Bondi, including Speedo’s fitness center. QT Bondi is styled with quirky décor and bespoke amenities. Original artwork by artist Shaun Gladwell adorn the lobby. There is a 24-hour reception desk, and self-parking is available for an additional charge.
