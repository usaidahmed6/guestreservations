About NH Collection Milano CityLife

NH Collection Milano CityLife is located only 328 feet from Portello metro station, and in the heart of Milan CityLife district. The hotel has a restaurant with an outdoor area and features a seasonal pool located on the hotel's rooftop on the 13th floor. The bar is located at the border of the pool. All guest rooms at NH Collection Milano CityLife have a flat-screen TV, air conditioning and free Wi-Fi and selected rooms also offer a terrace. All rooms have a private bathroom fitted with a shower and a hairdryer. Fiera Milano City is 984 feet from the accommodations. The nearest airport is Milan Linate Airport, 6.2 mi from NH Collection Milano CityLife.
