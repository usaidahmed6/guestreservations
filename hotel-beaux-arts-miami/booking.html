About Hotel Beaux Arts, Autograph Collection by Marriott

Located in Miami city center, 5.5 mi from South Beach, this luxury hotel features rooms with an Apple iPad and espresso machine. Its amenities include Enliven Spa and Salon, 3 restaurants, and a café. Hotel Beaux Arts Miami is locared inside of the JW Marriott Marquis and offers spacious rooms with an iPod docking station. Each room has a flat-screen TV, sofa, and stunning views. Dining options at Beaux Arts include Boulud Sud, offering Mediterranean cuisine, and L'Atelier . Intermezzo coffee house and ThreeFortyFive are both open for breakfast. Enjoy 24-hour room service. Guests of Miami Beaux Arts can enjoy state-of-the-art facilities such as an NBA-approved basketball court, Jim McLean Indoor Golf School and virtual bowling. An outdoor pool, hot tub, and sauna are available for relaxation. American Airlines Arena, home of the professional basketball team, the Miami Heat, is 3 minutes' drive from the hotel. Miami International Airport is 7.1 mi away.
