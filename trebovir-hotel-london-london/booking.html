About Trebovir Hotel London

Just 492 feet from Earls Court Underground Station,Trebovir Hotel offers air-conditioned rooms with a flat-screen TV. Earls Court Exhibition Center is a 3-minute walk away and free WiFi is available throughout the building. The shops and boutiques of South Kensington and Chelsea can be reached after 20 minutes’ walk. Stamford Bridge, home of Chelsea Football Club, is just over a mile away, and Heathrow Airport is a 30-minute journey by car. An LCD flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a selection of toiletries are included in each compact room.
