About Hostal Goyal Pizarro

Hostal Goyal Pizarro is housed in a modernist building located in central Madrids Chueca district famed throughout the world for its lively gay and lesbian nightlife. As well as its central setting, this gay-friendly property offers a lounge with Wi-Fi access and satellite TV, which also serves as a venue to meet other travelers. In the surrounding area you will find shops, bars and clubs to suit any taste, as well as cultural offerings including some of the worlds most prestigious galleries (Reina Sofia, Prado, Thyssen-Bornemisza).
