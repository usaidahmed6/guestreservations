About Milan Suite Hotel

Milan Suite Hotel is a stylish property located 10 minutes' drive from San Siro Stadium and the Fiera Milano Exhibition Center. The spacious, design accommodations offers free WiFi in all rooms and a flat-screen TV. Modern junior suites and suites have air conditioning. The private bathroom comes with bath tub, slippers, free toiletries, and a hairdryer. A microwave and kettle are available on request. A breakfast buffet is provided daily in the modern dining hall with large windows. Guests of the Milan Suite have access to a games room and a small fitness room. The property is 1969 feet from Milano Villapizzone Train Station. Public busses provide links to the city center. The property is a 15-minute drive from the Area Expo Exhibition Center.
