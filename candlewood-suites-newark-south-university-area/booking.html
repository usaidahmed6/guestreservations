About Candlewood Suites NEWARK SOUTH - UNIVERSITY AREA

Located 12 miles from Old Dutch House, Candlewood Suites - Newark South - University Area, an IHG Hotel offers 3-star accommodations in Newark and features a fitness center, a shared lounge and barbecue facilities. The property is around 18 miles from Delaware Museum of Natural History, a 17-minute walk from Tennis Court and 1.3 miles from University of Delaware. Wilmington and Western Railroad is 11 miles from the hotel and New Castle Court House is 12 miles away. Certain rooms will provide you with a kitchen with a fridge, a dishwasher and a microwave. A business center and vending machines with snacks and drinks are available on site at the hotel. Candlewood Suites - Newark South - University Area, an IHG Hotel can conveniently provide information at the reception to help guests to get around the area. Tennis Courts is 2.5 miles from the accommodation, while Dayett Station is 3.2 miles away. The nearest airport is New Castle Airport, 9.3 miles from Candlewood Suites - Newark South - University Area, an IHG Hotel.
