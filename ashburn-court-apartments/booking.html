About Ashburn Court Apartments

Just 14 minutes' walk from the Victoria and Albert and the Natural History museums, Ashburn Court Apartments feature self-catering accommodations in London, 3 minutes' walk from Gloucester Road. Free Wi-Fi access is available throughout. The apartments come with a lounge with flat-screen TV and DVD player, a fully equipped kitchen with oven, microwave and dishwasher and a bathroom with free toiletries, hairdryer and bathtub or shower. Extras include a washing machine and air conditioning. The shops on Gloucester Road, as well as Knightsbridge, Sloane Street and The King's Road are within walking distance. The property is within 1 mile of the world-famous Harrods, Hyde Park and the Royal Albert Hall. Marble Arch can be reached in less than 30 minutes by bus and King's Cross St Pancras is 24 minutes' away by Tube.
