About Courtyard by Marriott Calgary South

Courtyard by Marriott Calgary South is set in the Seton urban district in south Calgary. The hotel has an indoor pool and hot tub, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant. Several coffee shops and brewpubs are within walking distance The rooms come with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Some units include a seating area for your convenience. Rooms are fitted with a private bathroom fitted with a shower, free toiletries and a hair dryer. Guests can enjoy the on-site spa and fitness center. The front desk is available 24 hours a day. South Calgary Health Campus Hospital is only 10 minutes' walk away. Calgary Tower is 12 mi from Courtyard by Marriott Calgary South, while TELUS Convention Center is 0.7 mi away. Calgary International Airport is 25 mi from the property.
