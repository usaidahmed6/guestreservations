About Grand Oasis Palm All-Inclusive

The all-inclusive Grand Oasis Palm offers a private beach and is set 3.1 mi from central Cancún, within the Hotel Zone. It features an outdoor pool, squash and tennis courts and a spa. All rooms offer Caribbean Sea views. This Mexican-style hotel has a kid's club with cooking lessons, a disco and a games area with a Wii and Xbox. There is also a pool with a waterslide. Air-conditioned rooms have flat-screen cable TV, a mini-bar and coffee maker. There is a safe for hire. The private bathroom comes with a hairdryer, shower and toiletries. The hotel complex features 9 bars and 16 specialty restaurants, including one for children. Restaurants include sushi, Tex-Mex and international buffets. There are also mezcal, tequila and wine tasting sessions. Many shops, bars and restaurants can be found nearby. There is a 24-hour reception, and you can hire a car from the tour desk. Free parking is available and Cancun Airport is 12 mi away. The hotel is next to a pier, where you can take a 20-minute boat trip to Mujeres Island.
