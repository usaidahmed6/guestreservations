About The Andrews Hotel

Located in San Francisco’s Theater District, this European-style hotel is 6 minutes’ walk from Union Square. The Andrews Hotel offers an on-site restaurant and guest rooms with free WiFi. Offering service to San Francisco International Airport, Powell Street BART station is 10 minutes’ walk away. The warm colored rooms at The Andrews Hotel feature a flat-screen satellite TV. A leather armchair and a work desk are also provided. Guests can dine at Fino Ristorante & Bar, which offers Italian cuisine, daily specials and a fireplace. Hotel Andrews Hotel is less than 0.6 mi away from Chinatown, Nob Hill and the San Francisco Museum of Modern Art. Fisherman’s Wharf is 1.2 mi away.
