About Kempinski Hotel Cancun

Experience Kempinski Hotel Cancun

Set against the vibrant backdrop of Cancun, Kempinski Hotel Cancun offers a serene escape with its pristine private beach and an array of tailored amenities to ensure a memorable stay. Nestled just 20 minutes from Cancun International Airport and close to the bustling city center, guests can enjoy the perfect blend of convenience and tranquility.

The hotel features well-appointed modern rooms, each boasting a private balcony with breathtaking ocean views. In-room amenities include an iPod docking station, CD player, and cable TV, complemented by a mini bar, desk, and cozy bathrobes and slippers. Kempinski Hotel Cancun stands out with its personalized service and attention to detail.

Indulge in a variety of dining experiences on-site, from the fresh Asian delights at The Sushi Bar to Mediterranean flavors at Fantino, offering both indoor and outdoor seating options. The Lobby Lounge provides a relaxed atmosphere for enjoying innovative cocktails. Enhance the relaxation with a visit to Kempinski Spa and Salon, offering a broad selection of massage therapies, body treatments, and facials, or maintain your fitness routine at the gym and on the tennis court.

  • Two outdoor swimming pools with cabana service
  • Private beach for sunbathing and water activities
  • Specialty dining options with diverse cuisine
  • Full-service spa and wellness center
  • Tennis court and state-of-the-art gym facilities

Kempinski Hotel Cancun provides an immersive experience where guests can explore the natural beauty and lively culture of Cancun, or simply relax and rejuvenate under the sun. Whether visiting for leisure or business, the hotel ensures an exceptional stay with a variety of comfort and convenience.
