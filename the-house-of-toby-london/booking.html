About The House of Toby

Located in London, 2297 feet from King's Cross Theater, The House of Toby features air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi. All rooms are fitted with a flat-screen TV. Each room has a private bathroom. For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer. There is a 24-hour front desk at the property. British Museum is 0.7 mi from The House of Toby, while Dominion Theater is 1 mi away. London City Airport is 7.5 mi from the property.
