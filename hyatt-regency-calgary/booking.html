About Hyatt Regency Calgary

Offering the largest guestrooms in the city, an indoor pool and spa center, Hyatt Regency Calgary is centrally located in Calgary. Guests can dine in the on-site restaurant and lounge. Free WiFi is available. A flat-screen Smart TV and Google Chromecast streaming are featured in the rooms at Hyatt Regency Calgary. All units have a seating area for your convenience and a work desk. A Keurig coffee machine and refrigerator are provided in each room. A private bathroom offers free toiletries and a hair dryer. Serving Canadian dishes with a modern twist, Thomsons Kitchen + Bar offers casual all-day dining and evening drinks. Guests looking for a lighter fare can enjoy craft coffee, gourmet sandwiches and pastries at Baker's Coffee Shop. Both outlets are offering takeaways and are available for dine-in on select days. Guests can enjoy exercising in the fitness center located on site. The front desk is available 24 hours a day. Calgary Telus Convention Center is 656 feet from Hyatt Regency Calgary, while Calgary Tower is 656 feet from the property. The nearest airport is Calgary International Airport, 6.2 mi from Hyatt Regency Calgary.
