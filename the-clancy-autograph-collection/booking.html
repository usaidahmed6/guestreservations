About The Clancy, Autograph Collection

Discover Urban Elegance at The Clancy, Autograph Collection

Welcome to The Clancy, Autograph Collection, downtown San Francisco's beacon of contemporary luxury and a hub for exploration and discovery. Nestled in the vibrant SoMa district, this hotel stands at the crossroads of San Francisco's celebrated views and its bold, intellectual spirit. Whether you're a tech enthusiast, a culture aficionado, or an urban nomad, The Clancy invites you to experience a stay that's as enriching as it is comfortable.

Embrace the unique blend of modern sophistication and warm hospitality that defines The Clancy. The pet-friendly, family-friendly, and business-friendly policies ensure that every guest feels at home, whether visiting for work, leisure, or a little bit of both. With its prime location, the hotel offers unrivaled access to some of downtown San Francisco's most iconic attractions, including Oracle Park and the Chase Center.

  • Prime location in SoMa, near museums, tech hubs, and cultural attractions
  • Unique, pet-friendly and family-friendly accommodations
  • State-of-the-art fitness center to keep you active and energized
  • Seven Square Taproom for exquisite dining and unwinding experiences
  • Easy access to notable attractions like Oracle Park and Chase Center

The Clancy, Autograph Collection is more than just a hotel; it's a celebration of San Francisco's dynamic spirit. Start your day with a vigorous workout in the fitness center, and as the sun sets, relax and reminisce about the day's adventures at Seven Square Taproom, the on-site dining gem. Whether you're here to soak in the sights, attend a business meeting, or simply escape the ordinary, The Clancy ensures your San Francisco journey is filled with memorable moments. Embark on an unforgettable urban adventure at The Clancy, where every stay is a story waiting to be told.
