About Red Roof PLUS+ Miami Airport

This all nonsmoking hotel is only 5 minutes' drive from Miami International Airport. The hotel offers free airport transfers, and free Wi-Fi. Laundry facilities are also available at this pet-friendly hotel. Guest rooms at the Miami Airport Red Roof Plus are equipped with flat-screen cable TVs and ironing facilities. Some rooms feature microwaves and mini-refrigerators. Miami city center is 10 minutes' drive from the Red Roof Plus, and South Beach is 15 minutes' drive away. Flagler Dog Racing is only 1.9 mi away.
