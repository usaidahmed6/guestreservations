About Hilton Washington DC National Mall The Wharf

Discover the Heart of D.C. at Hilton Washington DC National Mall The Wharf

Experience the pulse of America's capital from the doorstep of Hilton Washington DC National Mall The Wharf, a LEED Silver-certified haven nestled in downtown Washington, D.C. This hotel stands as a beacon of modernity and convenience, directly connected to a Metro subway station and a stone's throw from the Smithsonian Museums. With the National Mall, Washington Monument, and a myriad of iconic landmarks within a mile, the essence of D.C.'s rich history and vibrant culture is right at your fingertips.

Each elegantly appointed room is a testament to comfort and style, equipped with high thread-count linens and designer bath amenities, alongside spacious work desks for those on business or leisure. Beyond the rooms, guests are invited to explore a world of amenities designed for the discerning traveler.

Key features include:

  • An outdoor, rooftop pool offering panoramic views of the city skyline.
  • Around-the-clock modern fitness center catering to all workout routines.
  • L'Enfant Grill, a contemporary bistro serving breakfast, lunch, and dinner with a focus on local and seasonal ingredients.
  • L'Enfant Bar, where hand-crafted cocktails and local beers meet in a sophisticated setting.
  • Direct access to a Metro subway station, placing the entirety of D.C. within easy reach.

From the moment of arrival, guests at Hilton Washington DC National Mall The Wharf are enveloped in the energetic spirit of D.C. Whether it's for business or pleasure, a stay here promises not just a room, but an experience that bridges the gap between the historical and contemporary, all while offering the hospitality and comfort synonymous with the Hilton name.
