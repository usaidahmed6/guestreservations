About 196 Bishopsgate Serviced Apartments

Located in the heart of London's financial district, 196 Bishopsgate offers fully serviced 4-star hotel apartments, opposite Liverpool Street train and underground stations. 196 Bishopsgate has a range of accommodations - from Studios to Executive One-Bedroom and Two-Bedroom Apartments. All apartments are uniquely spacious, have free Wi-Fi and luxurious marble-clad private bathrooms. Each apartment offers a separate living space, dining area and a fully equipped kitchen with dishwasher, fridge and microwave. Features also include a plasma-screen TV. Londons South Bank, home to the Tate Modern, National Theater and London Eye, is just a few stops away on the Tube. There is a 24-hour reception.
