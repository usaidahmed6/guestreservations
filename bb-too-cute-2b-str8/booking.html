About B&b Too Cute 2b Str8

The Too Cute 2b Str8 is an elegant B&B in Milan, 300 metres from Lambrate Station. It offers free WiFi, private parking, and a large living room with sofas and tables. An Italian-style breakfast is offered daily.

With wooden furnishings and parquet floors, each room includes air conditioning, a flat-screen TV and work desk. Some rooms are en suite and boast a spa bath, while others share a bathroom.

B&B Too Cute 2b Str8 is a 10-minute walk from Milan's Politecnico University. You can reach the historical centre and Milan Cathedral by Tram 23, stopping right by the property.
