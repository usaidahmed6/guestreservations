About Best Western Hotel Astrid

This property offers a Special Protection Program, a strict program of precise safeguards dedicated to our guests and our staff. Best Western Hotel Astrid offers free Wi-Fi and stylish rooms with wood floors, an LCD TV and a mini-bar. In summer, breakfast is served on the 5th-floor terrace overlooking St. Peters Dome and the Tiber River. Some rooms include a tea/coffee maker, bathrobes, and slippers. Breakfast is continental style, and includes gluten-free food on request. Hotel Astrid is near the Flaminio and Olimpico stadiums, the Foro Italico, and the Auditorium. Tram number 2 stops just 492 feet away and provides connection with Flaminio Metro Station and Piazza del Popolo.
