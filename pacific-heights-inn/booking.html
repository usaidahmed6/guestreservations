About Pacific Heights Inn By Magnuson Worldwide

This San Francisco hotel on Union Street is 1 mi from Fisherman’s Wharf. Pacific Heights Inn offers guests a free parking and free WiFi in every room. The traditional-style guest rooms at Pacific Heights Inn are equipped with cable TV. The hotel has a tour desk and offers assistance in planning tours of the area, such as to Napa Valley Wine Country and a walking tour in Chinatown. Pacific Heights Inn is located 3.7 mi from the Golden Gate Bridge. The hotel is within 3 minutes' walk of the Octagon House, a historical landmark.

Amenities

  • 24 hour front desk
  • Refrigerator
  • Hair Dryer
