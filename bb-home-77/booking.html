About B&B Home 77

B&B Home 77 offers stylish rooms with free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, and a plasma-screen satellite TV. The bus stop to the historic center stops outside the building, and Castro Pretorio Metro Station is a 5-minute walk away. The rooms at Home 77 have a modern design and are equipped with a private bathroom with hairdryer and toiletries. A sweet Italian-style breakfast is provided daily. Some snacks and drinks are available for free in a shared lounge. Located in the embassies' district, the B&B is a 15-minute walk from Villa Borghese park. Roma Termini Train Station is just 1 metro stop away.
