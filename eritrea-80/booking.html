About Eritrea 80

Set in a quiet residential part of Rome, Eritrea 80 offers air-conditioned rooms with an LCD TV and free Wi-Fi. Rome's Termini Central Station is 4 metro stops away. Rooms at Alloggio Turistico Eritrea 80 are set in a building with elevator. Each room has large windows and colorful or classic furnishings. The surrounding streets offer a choice of shops, restaurants and bars. The new metro station Annibaliano is a 3-minute walk away. Alloggio Turistico Eritrea 80 is a 10-minute walk to Villa Ada, one of the city's biggest parks.
