About Vincci Soho

Located in Madrid’s historic city center, the Vincci Soho is just 656 feet from the Thyssen-Bornemisza Art Gallery. The modern design hotel has rooms with basic WiFi and a flat-screen TV. Vincci Soho has distinctive rooms, decorated in red and black. Rooms all include air conditioning and a safe. The Vincci Soho offers a breakfast buffet, starting at 05:00 daily. The hotel's breakfast includes products for celiacs. The restaurant Nomad offers creative traditional cuisine or tapas. In summer months, lunch and dinner are served on the patio. The lively bars and restaurants of Plaza Santa Ana are just 328 feet away, while the Puerta del Sol is about 1312 feet away. Anton Martin Metro Station is 984 feet away.
