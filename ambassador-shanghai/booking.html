About Shanghai Metropark Jichen Hotel

The 26-story Ambassador Hotel is in Jing’an District of Shanghai, a 5-minute drive from Longde Road subway station (Line 11). It houses a fitness center, 3 dining options and accommodations with free internet access. Ambassador Hotel is a 15-minute drive from the hotel to Shanghai Train Station, a 20-minute drive from Hongqiao Airport. The Bund is 4 miles away. Pudong International Airport is a 40-minute car ride away. The air-conditioned guest rooms feature warm lighting and modern interiors. Each unit comes equipped with a flat-screen satellite TV, minibar and has a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries. Guests can enjoy soothing massage treatment, or rent a car to explore the surroundings. For convenience, fax, printing and photocopying facilities are provided. The Grand Palace Restaurant serves a fine selection of local and Cantonese dishes, and international dishes are available at Clivia Coffee Shop. Alternatively, the Japanese Restaurant & Teppanyaki YAWARA provides a variety of the Japanese cuisine.
