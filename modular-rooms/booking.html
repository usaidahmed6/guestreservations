About Modular Rooms

Boasting a shared lounge, Modular Rooms Hotels is located in Madrid in the Community of Madrid region, 1640 feet from Plaza Mayor and 1640 feet from Mercado San Miguel. Featuring a shared kitchen, this property also provides guests with a terrace. The accommodations provides evening entertainment and a 24-hour front desk. All units are equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a microwave, a coffee machine, a shower, a hairdryer and a desk. All guest rooms at the hostel are fitted with a seating area. Popular points of interest near the accommodations include Puerta del Sol, Puerta de Toledo and Gran Via. The nearest airport is Adolfo Suarez Madrid-Barajas, 8.7 mi from Modular Rooms Hotels, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
