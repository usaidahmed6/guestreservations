About Los Angeles Inn & Suites LAX

Located in Los Angeles, 8.7 miles from LA Memorial Coliseum, Los Angeles Inn & Suites - LAX provides accommodations with free WiFi and free private parking. The property is around 8.8 miles from California Science Center, 9.2 miles from Natural History Museum of Los Angeles County and 11 miles from Staples Center. Los Angeles County Museum Of Art (LACMA) is 13 miles from the motel and LA Union Station is 13 miles away. Complete with a private bathroom equipped with a bath, all guest rooms at the motel have a flat-screen TV and air conditioning, and some rooms come with a balcony. Staff at Los Angeles Inn & Suites - LAX are always available to provide information at the reception. Microsoft Theater is 11 miles from the accommodation, while Petersen Automotive Museum is 13 miles away. The nearest airport is Hawthorne Municipal Airport, 2.5 miles from Los Angeles Inn & Suites - LAX.
