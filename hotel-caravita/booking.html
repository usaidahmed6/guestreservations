About Hotel Caravita

Featuring a shared lounge, a terrace as well as a bar, Hotel Caravita is located in the center of Rome, a 10-minute walk from Torre Argentina. This 5-star hotel offers a concierge service and a tour desk. The property has a 24-hour front desk, airport transportation, room service and free WiFi throughout the property. The hotel will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms offering a desk, an electric tea pot, a fridge, a minibar, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a bidet. At Hotel Caravita all rooms include bed linen and towels. Breakfast is available every morning, and includes à la carte, continental and Italian options. At the accommodation you'll find a restaurant serving Italian, Mediterranean and International cuisine. Vegetarian, vegan and gluten-free options can also be requested. Popular points of interest near Hotel Caravita include Piazza Navona, Palazzo Venezia and Pantheon. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino Airport, 10 miles from the hotel.
