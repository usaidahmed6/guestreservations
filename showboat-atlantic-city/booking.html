About Showboat Hotel Atlantic City

Showboat Hotel Atlantic City: Fun and Comfort on the Boardwalk

Showboat Hotel Atlantic City, located directly on the famous boardwalk, offers a blend of entertainment and comfort in this iconic seaside city. Known for its spacious accommodations and exciting on-site activities, this beachfront property provides a unique and lively stay for travelers looking for fun in the sun and the vibrant nightlife of Atlantic City.

Key Features:

  1. Boardwalk Location: Experience direct access to the Atlantic City Boardwalk, with miles of shops, restaurants, and entertainment.
  2. Pet-Friendly Rooms: Enjoy a stay with your furry friends in the hotel's contemporary, pet-friendly accommodations.
  3. Largest Arcade on the East Coast: Have fun in the largest arcade on the East Coast, right within the hotel.
  4. Exquisite Suites: Relax in suites featuring double vanities, spa baths, and waterfall shower heads for a luxurious experience.
  5. Proximity to Attractions: Located near the Steel Pier Amusements and just 1.6 mi from the Atlantic City Convention Center and the Pier Shops at Caesars.

Accommodations and Services:

The guest rooms at Showboat Hotel Atlantic City are designed with modern furnishings and equipped with a flat-screen TV featuring premium channels. The hotel offers a variety of dining options on-site, ensuring that guests have a wide range of culinary experiences to choose from without leaving the comfort of the hotel.

Location and Exploration:

Showboat Hotel's prime location on the boardwalk places guests in the heart of Atlantic City's entertainment and shopping district. Whether you're here to enjoy the beach, the casinos, or the amusement rides, everything is just a short walk or drive away.

Showboat Hotel Atlantic City is more than just a place to stay; it's an entertainment hub where guests can enjoy the best of Atlantic City's leisure and nightlife. With its unique offerings and prime location, it promises a memorable stay filled with fun and relaxation.
