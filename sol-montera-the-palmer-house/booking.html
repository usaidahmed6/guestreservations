About SOL Montera The Palmer House

SOL Montera The Palmer House offers accommodations in Madrid, a 13-minute walk from Plaza de Espaa Metro Station and 1.1 miles from Temple of Debod. This bed and breakfast features air-conditioned accommodations with a patio and free Wifi. The property is 700 yards from the city center and a 3-minute walk from Gran Via Station Metro Station. The bed and breakfast features 3 bedrooms, a fully equipped kitchen with an oven and a microwave, a washing machine, and 2 bathrooms with a hair dryer. Towels and bed linen are offered in the bed and breakfast. This bed and breakfast is allergy-free and non-smoking. Popular points of interest near SOL Montera The Palmer House include Thyssen-Bornemisza Museum, Gran Via, and Retiro Park. The nearest airport is Adolfo Suarez Madrid-Barajas Airport, 7.5 miles from the accommodation.
