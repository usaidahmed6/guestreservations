About Solymar Beach Resort

This hotel offers an excellent location in Cancun, boasting the proximity to the beach and optimum personalized service is the perfect place for families and couples. The establishment takes pride of a great variety, quality and flavor on food and beverages affording travelers an unforgettable experience in a relaxed atmosphere and economic prices. The property was constructed in 1985 and partly refurbished in 2005. The hotel consists of a main building with 3 annexes and 70 rooms, 1 acuatic pool park for kids , 1 hidrojet hot tub, 1 adults pool, 1 pool bar , 1 beach bar and 1 snack bar
