About Vincci The Mint

Revolutionary Style at Vincci The Mint, Madrid

Step into the innovative world of Vincci The Mint, a unique hotel experience set in the heart of Madrid's bustling Gran Via. Designed by the renowned Jaime Beriestain, this hotel offers a fresh take on hospitality with its engaging blend of comfort and modern style, perfect for the savvy traveler.

Vincci The Mint redefines the traditional check-in process by integrating it with a welcome drink at the reception-bar, ensuring your experience is smooth and enjoyable from the moment you arrive. Each room and suite exudes a contemporary charm with parquet floors, combining green and white walls for a vibrant yet soothing ambiance. Modern amenities include air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, work desk, mini-bar, and a coffee machine, all designed to facilitate a comfortable stay.

The hotel's terrace offers stunning views of Madrid's iconic architecture, creating the perfect backdrop for relaxation or evening cocktails. Indulge in gourmet breakfasts available à la carte daily at the on-site gastrobar, which serves a variety of delightful dishes from morning until late.

  • Stylish rooms with modern amenities and vibrant decor
  • Unique bar-reception for an innovative check-in experience
  • Exquisite terrace with panoramic views of Madrid
  • Gastrobar serving à-la-carte gourmet breakfasts and meals
  • Prime location on Gran Via, close to Puerta del Sol and other attractions

Located just a short walk from Puerta del Sol and within easy reach of many of Madrid's most famous sites, Vincci The Mint provides an ideal base for exploring the city. Whether you're visiting for business or leisure, Vincci The Mint offers a memorable stay with its bold design, exceptional service, and unbeatable location. Discover Madrid's charm with a touch of minty freshness at this avant-garde hotel.
