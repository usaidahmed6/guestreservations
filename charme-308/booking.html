About Charme 308

Just a 13-minute walk from Pantheon and 0.4 miles from Piazza Navona, Charme 308 features accommodations in Rome, with a shared lounge. Featuring city and inner courtyard views, this guest house also features free Wifi. The property is 0.8 miles from the city center and a 15-minute walk from Torre Argentina. The guest house will provide guests with air-conditioned units with a desk, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom with a walk-in shower. At the guest house, all units are soundproof. The rooms are equipped with heating facilities. Popular points of interest near the guest house include Campo de' Fiori, St Peter's Square, and The Vatican. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino Airport, 10 miles from Charme 308.
