About Ratana Apart Hotel Kamala

Located a 10-minute walk from Kamala Beach, Ratana Apart Hotel offers free WiFi throughout the property. Boasting a stunning rooftop outdoor pool, this marine-style apart hotel provides a 24-hour front desk and fitness centre. Free public parking is available.

Phuket FantaSea is a 3-km drive away, while Phuket International Airport is 37 km from the hotel.

Each non-smoking room is equipped with an air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, a fridge and a telephone. Shower facilities are included in an en suite bathroom.

A wide range of International and Thai cuisine can be sampled at The Anchor Bistro and Bar.

Amenities

  • Room Service
  • 24 hour front desk
  • Fitness Center
  • Restaurant
  • Refrigerator
  • Microwave
  • In room safe
