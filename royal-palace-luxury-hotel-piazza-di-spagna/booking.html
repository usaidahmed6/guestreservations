About Royal Palace Luxury Hotel

Located 328 feet from the Spanish Steps, the Royal Palace is set in a 15th-century building with original period tapestries. The property offers a restaurant with terrace, stylish rooms, and free WiFi throughout. All air conditioned, the spacious rooms here feature a satellite LCD TV and a mini-bar. The private bathroom has free toiletries and a hairdryer. Le Bistrot restaurant serves an Italian and à la carte breakfast and is open until 01:00 every day. Royal Palace Luxury Hotel is 656 feet from Spagna Metro Station. It is surrounded by Rome's most popular designer stores, restaurants and cafés.
