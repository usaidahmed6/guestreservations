About Hilton London Olympia

Stay in Style at Hilton London Olympia

Experience the sophistication of Hilton London Olympia, ideally situated on the vibrant Kensington High Street, just 656 feet from the Olympia Exhibition Centre. This prime location offers guests easy access to the heart of central London, making it a perfect choice for both business travelers and tourists seeking a stylish stay in one of Londons most fashionable areas.

The spacious, air-conditioned rooms are designed with modernity and comfort in mind, featuring stylish bathrooms with granite surfaces and all the necessary amenities. Each room is equipped with satellite TV and a large workspace, catering to the needs of guests looking for a productive and relaxing environment.

Dining at Hilton London Olympia is a delightful experience. The open-plan Society Restaurant offers a contemporary setting where guests can enjoy British cuisine made from fresh, local ingredients. For those looking to unwind, the trendy Society Bar provides a relaxed atmosphere to enjoy a drink or small plates. Additionally, the on-site coffee shop is perfect for guests in need of a quick caffeine fix or a light snack.

  • Convenient location close to Olympia Exhibition Centre and central London
  • Modern, air-conditioned rooms with granite bathroom surfaces
  • Society Restaurant serving British cuisine
  • Society Bar for relaxing drinks and small plates
  • Excellent transport links, with close proximity to Kensington Olympia Overground and Earls Court Underground Stations

Whether you are visiting for a conference, shopping at the nearby Westfield Shopping Centre, or exploring the sights of London, Hilton London Olympia provides a comfortable base with excellent facilities and superb dining options. Discover everything that London has to offer while enjoying the comfort and convenience of this well-placed hotel.
