About W Rome

W Rome has free bikes, fitness center, a garden and terrace in Rome. This 5-star hotel offers room service and a 24-hour front desk. The property is a 10-minute walk from Piazza di Spagna Metro Station, and within 0.7 miles of the city center. The hotel will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms offering a closet, a coffee machine, a fridge, a minibar, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a shower. W Rome has certain accommodations that have a balcony, and each room includes an electric tea pot. At the accommodation rooms contain bed linen and towels. A buffet, continental or Italian breakfast is available each morning at the property. At W Rome you'll find a restaurant serving Italian, Mediterranean and Pizza cuisine. Vegetarian, dairy-free and vegan options can also be requested. Popular points of interest near the hotel include Piazza Barberini, Spanish Steps and Barberini Metro Station. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino Airport, 9.3 miles from W Rome.
