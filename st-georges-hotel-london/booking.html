About St George's Hotel

Located over 2 restored 19th-century Victorian buildings, St George's Pimlico is just a 10-minute walk from Victoria railway and coach station. Most guest rooms have original period features, including high ceilings and Georgian windows. An private bathroom is featured in each room, as well as a flat-screen satellite TV and tea/coffee making facilities. Many bars and restaurants are found in the local area. Guests can also relax in the lounge which features glass walls and comfortable seating. Central London can be reached in less than 10 minutes from Pimlico tube station, which is a 5-minute walk from the property. Westminster Cathedral and the Tate Britain are less than a mile away.
