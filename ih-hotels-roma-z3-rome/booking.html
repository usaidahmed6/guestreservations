About Idea Hotel Roma Z3

IH Hotels Roma Z3 is a design property in east Rome, near the GRA and A24 motorways. The hotel offers free high-speed WiFi throughout, ultra-modern rooms and a state-of-the-art gym with Technogym equipment. Rooms have contemporary furnishings, air conditioning, a work area and a 32-inch flat-screen TV. The American breakfast consists of an extensive sweet and savory buffet, including freshly baked homemade cakes. The à-la-carte restaurant serves classic Italian cuisine for lunch and dinner, and gluten-free and vegetarian options are also available on request. A tram stopping 2625 feet away has services to Termini Station. Tecnopolo Tiburtino is a 15-minute drive from the property.
