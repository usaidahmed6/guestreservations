About B&B EurGardenie

Offering garden views, a shared lounge and free WiFi, B&B EurGardenie is located in Rome, 3.5 miles from PalaLottomatica Arena and 3.8 miles from EUR Fermi Metro Station. Fitted with a balcony, the units offer air conditioning and feature a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with bidet and a hairdryer. A microwave and fridge are also offered, as well as an electric tea pot and a coffee machine. Guests at the bed and breakfast can enjoy a continental breakfast. Biomedical Campus Rome is 4.4 miles from B&B EurGardenie, while Laurentina Metro Station is 4.5 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino Airport, 10 miles from the accommodation.
