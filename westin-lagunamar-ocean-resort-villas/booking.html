Unwind, then settle into the separate bedroom, where a second 37-inch LCD flat panel TV unites with a king-sized Westin Heavenly Bed. Wrapped in crisp cotton linens and sumptuous down duvets, it promises a restorative nights sleep.

About The Westin Lagunamar Ocean Resort Villas & Spa Cancun

Discover the Ultimate Cancun Getaway at The Westin Lagunamar Ocean Resort Villas & Spa

Nestled in the vibrant heart of Cancun's Hotel Zone, The Westin Lagunamar Ocean Resort Villas & Spa offers an unparalleled beachfront experience with breathtaking views of the Caribbean Sea. This resort provides a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure, featuring spacious outdoor pools, a state-of-the-art fitness center, and convenient access to Cancun's bustling attractions. Whether seeking a serene retreat or an exciting exploration, guests will find everything needed for an unforgettable stay.

  • Expansive Beachfront with Direct Caribbean Sea Access
  • Four Outdoor Pools Including an Infinity Pool
  • Heavenly Spa by Westin for Rejuvenating Massages
  • Guided Tours to Explore Cancun and Surrounding Areas
  • Accommodations with Stunning Views of the Caribbean Sea or Nichupte Lagoon

Guests can start their day with a workout at the modern fitness center or a swim in one of the four outdoor pools. The resort's private beach offers a variety of activities, perfect for both relaxation and adventure seekers. After a day of exploration or sunbathing, unwind with a therapeutic massage at the Heavenly Spa by Westin. Each villa provides a sanctuary of comfort with satellite TV, high-speed wireless internet, plush bathrobes, and the signature Westin Heavenly Bed, ensuring a restful night's sleep.

With its prime location, The Westin Lagunamar Ocean Resort Villas & Spa is just minutes away from Cancun's vibrant nightlife, shopping centers, and cultural attractions. Experience the best of Cancun at this exceptional resort, where every moment is designed to create lasting memories.
