About Stanford Court San Francisco

Located adjacent to the Powell and California cable car stop, Stanford Court San Francisco is a technology-friendly boutique hotel that features a lobby and business center equipped with 7 MacBooks, iPads and iPad minis all running high-speed WiFi. Bar and Lounge serves craft brews and whiskey. San Franciscos Union Square,Chinatown, and North Beach are is just 10 minutes walk away. Select rooms feature a view of the San Francisco skyline at Stanford Court San Francisco. All guest rooms offer a pillow top mattress, speedy WiFi, a 49-inch high-definition television with HDMI and USB Ports, USB outlets, Staycast that enables guests stream content from their own devices, and a mini-refrigerator. Breakfast is available on-site at Mixing Room. Serves dinner and craft cocktails, beer, and whiskey in the evening. Guests can workout in the state-of-the-art fitness center at Stanford Court San Francisco. AT&T Park is 10 minutes drive away from Stanford Court San Francisco. Overlooking Grace Cathedral, Huntington Park, atop Nob Hill, is less than 5 minutes walk away. Fisherman's Wharf is just 15 minutes cable car ride away. Chinatown is 7 minutes walk away. The premium early arrival is $50 plus tax before 09:00 to 14:00. Arriving before 09:00 is the rate of the day plus tax.
