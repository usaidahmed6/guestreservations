About Room Mate Oscar

Room Mate Oscar is a stylish hotel located in central Madrid, between Gran Via and the Chueca district. It offers free Wi-Fi, rooms with flat-screen TVs and a rooftop bar. Room Mate Oscar features chic dcor with bright colors throughout. Each of its air conditioned rooms is spacious and comfortable. All have a mini-bar and a fully equipped bathroom. The hotels restaurant serves a buffet breakfast and has a rooftop terrace bar with fantastic views of Madrid. Gluten-free meals are available on prior request. Other facilities include meeting and conference rooms as well as portable WiFi. Room Mate Oscar Madrid is within 10 minutes walk of several bars, cafs and restaurants. Plaza Mayor Square and the Puerta del Sol district are less than 2625 feet away. Gran Via Metro Station is a 5-minute walk from the hotel. This hotel has won an award for the accessibility standards offered to guests with reduced mobility.
