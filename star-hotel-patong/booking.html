About Star Hotel Patong

A 5 minute walk from Patong Beach, Star Hotel Patong offers non-smoking rooms with private balconies. It features an outdoor pool and free Wi-Fi, which is available throughout the hotel. Star Hotel Patong is less than a 10 minute drive from the bustling Bangla Road and Jungceylon Shopping Center. It is a 30 minute drive from Phuket Town and a 45 minute ride from Phuket International Airport. Modern rooms come equipped with air conditioning, a safe and a cable TV. Shower facilities are in the private bathrooms. Offering a 24-hour front desk, the hotel provides a laundry service, luggage storage and safe facilities. Airport shuttle service can be arranged for an additional fee. For meals, guests can enjoy local delicacies at many eateries and restaurants located just a 2 minute walk away.
