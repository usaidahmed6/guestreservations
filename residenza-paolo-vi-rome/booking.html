About Residenza Paolo VI

Offering panoramic views of Saint Peter's Square from its rooftop terrace, Residenza Paolo VI is a converted monastery with free WiFi. Just yards from Saint Peter's, the property is 15 minutes' walk from the Vatican Museums. Classically furnished and all air conditioned, rooms here have an LCD TV with satellite channels, tiled or wooden floors, and elegant rugs. The private bathroom comes with a hairdryer. Breakfast at Paolo VI is an extensive sweet and savory buffet including scrambled eggs, cheese, cereal and yogurt. Drinks and snacks can be enjoyed up on the terrace, with great views of the Apostolic Palace. The Residenza is 2297 feet from both Castel Sant'Angelo castle and the river Tiber. It has excellent bus and metro links around Rome, with the 40 Express bus stopping nearby.
