About Eurostars Zarzuela Park Hotel

The Exe Zarzuela Park enjoys a peaceful setting, surrounded by beautiful gardens and only 3.7 mi from Madrid city center. It features a seasonal outdoor swimming pool and modern rooms with air conditioning. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel and there is free on-site parking. The hotel also operates a 24-hour reception for guests’ convenience. Paddle-tennis courts are available. A 50 square yards meeting room is also available for holding meetings or banquets. The restaurant at Exe Zarzuela Park serves creative fusion cuisine. During summer months guests can enjoy meals on the terrace. Moncloa is just a 10-minute bus ride away from Exe Zarzuela Park, with a bus stop directly in front of the hotel. Madrid Adolfo Suarez Airport is 25 minutes' drive from the property.
