About Hotel Bernina

Just 656 feet from Milano Centrale Train Station, Hotel Bernina features rooms with WiFi access, a flat-screen TV, and a mini-bar. It also offers room service. The Bernina Hotel has excellent public transport links around Milan and to its international airports. Milan Cathedral is just 4 metro stops away. Rooms here are bright and comfortable with parquet floors. They come with a private bathroom with hairdryer. WiFi access is available throughout the hotel and there is an internet point in the lobby. A varied breakfast buffet is served from 07:00 until 10:00. You will also find a bar and a reception open 24 hours a day. The Rho Fiera Milano is 9.3 mi from the property.
