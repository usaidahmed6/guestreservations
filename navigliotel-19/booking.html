About Navigliotel 19 - Rooms & Suites

Featuring free WiFi throughout, Navigliotel 19 - Rooms & Suites offers accommodations in Milan. It is just 1640 feet from the Naviglio Grande canal and 0.6 mi from Romolo Station, offering direct connections to Milan's historical center. Spacious and air conditioned, rooms at this B&B include a flat-screen TV, electric kettle and private bathroom. Free toiletries and a hairdryer are also provided. The accommodations offers the check in online. Set 0.9 mi from MUDEC, Navigliotel 19 - Rooms & Suites is a 30-minute drive from Milan Linate Airport.
