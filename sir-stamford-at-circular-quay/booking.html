About Sir Stamford Circular Quay

Discover Elegance and History at Sir Stamford Circular Quay

Located in the heart of Sydney's Central Business District, Sir Stamford Circular Quay provides a blend of historical charm and modern comfort. Just a 5-minute stroll from the iconic Sydney Opera House, this hotel offers stunning views of Sydney Harbor and Circular Quay, making it an ideal choice for both business and leisure travelers.

  • Sundeck Overlooking Sydney Harbor and Circular Quay
  • Award-Winning Restaurant with Views of the Royal Botanic Gardens
  • Rooms with Executive Work Desks and Marble Bathrooms
  • One of Australia's Largest Private Collections of Fine Art
  • 18th-Century Antiques and Open Fireplaces

Each room at Sir Stamford Circular Quay features an executive work desk with broadband internet access, a marble bathroom with a double vanity, bathrobe, and slippers. Some rooms also offer intimate Juliet balconies with stunning views, enhancing the overall experience. Guests can enjoy a modern Australian cuisine at The Dining Room, which provides exceptional service in a relaxed atmosphere.

The hotel's bar is perfect for high tea, fine wines, or cocktails, offering a stylish gourmet menu in a sophisticated setting. Sir Stamford Circular Quay also boasts one of Australia's largest private collections of fine art, alongside 18th-century antiques, open fireplaces, and crystal chandeliers that add to its unique charm.

Experience the best of Sydney with a stay at Sir Stamford Circular Quay, where historical elegance meets contemporary comfort, all in a prime location.
