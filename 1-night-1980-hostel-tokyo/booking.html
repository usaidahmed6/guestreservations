About 1 Night 1980 Yen Tokyo Hotel

At 1 Night 1980 Yen Tokyo Hotel, exceptional service and top-notch amenities create a memorable experience for guests. Remain linked during your visit by utilizing the complimentary internet access available.Parking is accessible, supplied by the capsule hotel for guests who arrive by car. The capsule hotel is completely smoke-free.In limited designated zones, smoking is exclusively permitted.
