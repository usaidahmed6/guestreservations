About Acacia Hotel

Acacia Hotel is Newly Renovated Redecorated Budget Family hotel in South Kensington London. provides comfortable rooms, all with En suite private bathrooms and TV. Guests can also make use of tea and coffee-making facilities. There is free Wi-Fi internet access in the building. The hotel has the Best Location as it is really convenient to reach and enjoy the life of London in the nearby pubs and Restaurant as well as at walkable distance to Natural History Museum and Science Museum. People enjoy their stay at Acacia Hotel during the BBC Proms and other Music events as it also near to Royal Albert Hall. Walkable to Hyde park , Winter wonderland. Acacia Hotel is very near to South Kensington tube station. The Royal Albert Hall is 2297 feet from Acacia Hotel, while Harrods is also less than a mile from the property. The nearest airport is London City Airport, 10 miles from the property. Very good Connectivity to Heathrow Airport (Piccadilly line).
