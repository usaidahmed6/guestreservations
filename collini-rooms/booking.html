About Collini Rooms

Located in Milan, less than 10 minutes' drive from Milan Linate Airport, Collini Rooms provides accommodations with an Italian restaurant and a panoramic terrace. Free WiFi is available throughout the property. All rooms at the hotel come with air conditioning and a flat-screen TV. The private bathroom includes a hairdryer, free toiletries and slippers. A buffet breakfast including sweet and savory options is available every morning at Collini Rooms. Milan Fashion District is 3.1 mi from the property. The tram that stops 656 feet away will take you to Milan's Cathedral in approximately 25 minutes.
