About Crowne Plaza Sydney Burwood

Located in Sydney, 3.5 miles from ANZ Stadium, Crowne Plaza Sydney Burwood, an IHG Hotel provides accommodations with an outdoor swimming pool, private parking, a fitness center and a shared lounge. Providing a bar, the property is located within 3.5 miles of Bicentennial Park. The hotel has a terrace, a 24-hour front desk, and free WiFi is available throughout the property. The hotel will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms offering a desk, a coffee machine, a minibar, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV, a balcony and a private bathroom with a shower. At Crowne Plaza Sydney Burwood, an IHG Hotel all rooms come with bed linen and towels. A buffet, continental or Full English/Irish breakfast is available every morning at the property. At the accommodation you'll find a restaurant serving American, Mediterranean and Australian cuisine. Vegetarian, halal and kosher options can also be requested. Sydney Showground is 3.9 miles from Crowne Plaza Sydney Burwood, an IHG Hotel, while Qudos Bank Arena is 4.2 miles away. The nearest airport is Sydney Kingsford Smith Airport, 8.7 miles from the hotel.
