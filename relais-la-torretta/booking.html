About Relais La Torretta

Relais La Torretta is located in the Rome, less than 1.2 mi from the Basilica of Saint John in Lateran and a 5-minute walk from the Ponte Lungo Metro Station. The rooms are fitted with a flat-screen TV. Each has a private bathroom equipped with a shower. Extras include free toiletries and a hair dryer. Relais La Torretta features free WiFi throughout the property. A sweet breakfast of a croissant, hot beverage, bread and jam is served in a dedicated shared area. Cold cuts can for breakfast can be prepared on request and purchased on site. Relais La Torretta is a 15-minute walk from the Via Sannio street market. Rome's Ciampino and Fiumicino international airports are respectively 15 and 19 mi away.

Amenities

  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • Non-Smoking Facility
