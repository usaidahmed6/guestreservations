About Highfield Guest House

With a stay at Highfield Guest House in Edinburgh (Newington), you'll be within a 10-minute drive of University of Edinburgh and Princes Street. This 4-star property is 1.9 mi (3 km) from Royal Mile and 2 mi (3.2 km) from Edinburgh Castle. Make yourself at home in one of the 5 guestrooms. Breakfast is available for a fee. Featured amenities include wired Internet access (surcharge) and a computer station.
