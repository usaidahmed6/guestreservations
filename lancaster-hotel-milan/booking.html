About Lancaster Hotel

Hotel Lancaster is set in a central but tranquil area of Milan, just a 15-minute walk from the city center. Each room is air conditioned and has a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Services at the Lancaster include a 24-hour front desk where you can purchase bus and metro tickets. A buffet breakfast is served daily and later, guests can relax with a drink from the bar. Located in the Sempione district, the hotel is just a few steps from the Arch of Peace and Sempione Park. A 5-minute walk will take you to the FieraMilanoCity Exhibition Center.
