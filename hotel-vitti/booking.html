About Hotel Vitti

Hotel Vitti offers free parking and is easily reached from the A1 motorway and Rome's ring road, the GRA. It features air-conditioned rooms, each with an LCD TV, balcony and free wired internet access. Rooms here are bright and comfortable with a fridge, satellite TV channels and a private bathroom. An Italian breakfast of coffee, cappuccino and croissants is served each morning at the Vitti Hotel. Bolognetta Metro on Line C is 2461 feet from the property. A bus stop with links to Anagnina Metro is just 164 feet away. Rainbow MagicLand theme park and Valmontone Shopping Outlet are 30 minutes away by car. Ciampino Airport is 20 minutes' drive away.
