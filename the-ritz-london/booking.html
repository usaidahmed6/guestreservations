About The Ritz London

The Ritz London: Timeless Grandeur in the Heart of London

Overlooking the lush expanse of Green Park, The Ritz London stands as an iconic testament to timeless grandeur and sophisticated charm. Housed in a stunning Neoclassical building, The Ritz seamlessly blends historic elegance with contemporary comforts. Each room is a sanctuary of style, featuring high ceilings, ornate fireplaces, and chandeliers that illuminate the exquisite antique furnishings. The air-conditioned rooms offer a serene retreat with modern amenities, including designer toiletries and complimentary WiFi throughout the hotel.

Guests can indulge in culinary delights at the Michelin-starred Ritz Restaurant, where classic British cuisine is reimagined with a modern twist, using the finest ingredients. The dining experience is further elevated by the restaurant's picturesque views of the gardens. For a more relaxed yet equally refined atmosphere, The Rivoli Bar captivates with its Art Deco style and gold leaf decoration, offering an impressive array of cocktails and beverages.

  • Exquisite British cuisine at the Michelin-starred Ritz Restaurant
  • Stunning period architecture with antique furnishings
  • Prime location overlooking Green Park
  • Art Deco Rivoli Bar with gold leaf decorations
  • Classic afternoon teas in a lavish setting

Experience the epitome of British hospitality at The Ritz London, where every detail is crafted to ensure an unforgettable stay in one of the world's most celebrated hotels.
