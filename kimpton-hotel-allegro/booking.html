About Hotel Allegro Wien

This eco-friendly 3-star hotel is directly opposite the Matzleinsdorfer Platz Train Station and Tram Stop, just a 10-minute tram ride from the State Opera. Free WiFi is available in the entire Hotel Allegro Wien. The elegant and soundproofed rooms offer a bright décor and come with a flat-screen cable TV, a work desk, and a bathroom with shower or bath and a hairdryer. Guests can enjoy drinks and snacks at the bar in the lobby. A number of underground tram lines leaving from Matzleinsdorfer Platz directly opposite the hotel take you straight to the center in only 8 minutes. The hotel uses green electricity. Guests also have direct access to the train network, offering connections to sights such as the Prater Amusement Park, while a taxi rank is right next to the hotel.
