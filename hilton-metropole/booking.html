About Hilton London Metropole

Experience Central London at Hilton London Metropole

Located close to Paddington Station and a short 15-minute walk from Oxford Street, Hilton London Metropole offers a convenient base for exploring the heart of London. With easy access to major attractions and transport links, this hotel is perfect for both business and leisure travelers.

Each room at Hilton London Metropole features an LCD TV with wireless internet access, tea/coffee making facilities, air conditioning, and a private bathroom with toiletries. Guests can also take advantage of the executive lounge, providing a comfortable space to relax and unwind. The hotel is just a 2-minute walk from Edgware Road Underground Station, offering excellent connectivity to the rest of the city.

  • Prime location near Paddington Station and Oxford Street
  • Executive lounge for added comfort
  • Rooms with LCD TVs and wireless internet access
  • Convenient access to Edgware Road Underground Station
  • Proximity to Madame Tussauds and Hyde Park

The hotels central location places guests within walking distance of popular attractions such as Madame Tussauds and Hyde Park. Whether visiting for shopping, sightseeing, or business, Hilton London Metropole provides a comfortable and convenient stay in the heart of London.

Discover the best of London with a stay at Hilton London Metropole, where top amenities and a prime location ensure an unforgettable visit.
