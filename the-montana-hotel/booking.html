About The Montana Hotel

The modern and clean 3-star Montana is very close to museums, the Royal Albert Hall and Hyde Park. It has a restaurant, bar and air-conditioned rooms. Free Wi-Fi is available in the lobby. Gloucester Road London Underground Station is a 3-minute walk away and has a link to Heathrow Airport. Each room has a fridge and an interactive flat-screen TV. The hotel offers a discount at its Light of India restaurant where it offers continental breakfast each morning and an à la carte menu for lunch and dinner. Guests can also relax and enjoy a signature cocktail or choose from a collection of wine and beers at the Lion's Bar. Montana Hotel is a 10-minute walk from the shops of Kensington, Knightsbridge, including Harrods department store. Earls Court and Olympia are easy to access.
