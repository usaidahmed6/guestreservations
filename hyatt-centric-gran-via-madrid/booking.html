About Hyatt Centric Gran Via Madrid

Hyatt Centric Gran Via Madrid is located in the heart of Madrid and features a 24-hour gym, a bar and on-site dining. Gran Via Metro Station is 492 feet away. Free WiFi is offered throughout the property. Each room is stylishly decorated featuring custom designed furniture, city views, functional work areas, 55-inch TV, air conditioning, and heating. The private bathroom comes with free toiletries, bath or shower, and hairdryer. A buffet breakfast is served daily at the property and in the summer months. Hyatt Centric Gran Via Madrid also features a music studio, Ondas, where guests can enjoy a vermouth or gourmet coffee while listening to some foot-tapping tunes or enjoy a light snack. On the first floor you will find Hielo y Carbn, an exclusive grill restaurant, with a wide variety of local meats, as well as an extensive wine list in its wine cellar and different cocktails served in the Gintonera. Finally, Ondas, a bar inspired by a music studio, where guests can enjoy a vermouth or a gourmet coffee, small snacks or a slice of cake to the rhythm of the music. In addition, the hotel offers an extensive room service 24 hours a day, which can be served directly in the comfort of your room. The property has a 24-hour front desk and a concierge service and books and magazines about Madrid are available in the lobby. A 7-minute walk will take you to Puerta del Sol while El Retiro Park is 0.9 mi away. Madrid-Barajas Airport is a 45-minute metro ride away.
