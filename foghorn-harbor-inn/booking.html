About Foghorn Harbor Inn

Situated in Los Angeles, a few steps from Marina Del Rey Beach, Foghorn Harbor Inn features accommodation with a garden, free private parking and a shared lounge. The 3-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with a private bathroom and free WiFi. The hotel provides a sun terrace and a 24-hour front desk. At the hotel, rooms include a desk. Venice Beach is 1.5 km from Foghorn Harbor Inn, while Venice Beach Boardwalk is 2 km from the property. The nearest airport is Los Angeles International Airport, 7 km from the accommodation.
