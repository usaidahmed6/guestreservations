About Music City Hotel - Home of the San Francisco Music Hall of Fame

Music-themed, boutique lifestyle hotel & hostel, now with onsite bar & restaurant and newly renovated common areas. Stay comfortably in a private or shared room and take advantage of our capsule sleeping pods. Sleep with the stars in the home of the San Francisco Music Hall of Fame! Guests get access to free live entertainment six nights a week in our multiple music venues. Free guided tours of the SF Hall of Fame offered daily by SF Flower Child Blandina Farley. Discount to local cannabis dispensary. Music City, a bold entertainment company, brings artists and music lovers together to ignite the soul of local music culture. Bookings help support local musicians. Free WIFI is offered. Free coffee/tea and fresh fruit are offered daily as well as free luggage storage. Airport drop off services can be arranged by Music City Hotel. Daily maid service is included. The Mission District is 2.2 mi away. North Beach is 2.9 mi away from this accommodations. San Francisco’s Haight Ashbury District is 2.9 mi away, while Japantown is a 0.9 mi away and walking distance to Union Square.
