About Residenza Flaminio Gaio

Residenza Flaminio Gaio offers luxury accommodations in the heart of Rome, just a stone's throw from Piazza del Popolo. Some units include a seating area for your convenience. The rooms come with a private bathroom with a bidet and shower, with slippers, free toiletries and a hair dryer provided. Residenza Flaminio Gaio features free WiFi. You will find concierge service at the property. Via Margutta is 1969 feet from Residenza Flaminio Gaio. Ciampino Airport is 12 mi from the property.

Amenities

  • Room Service
  • Concierge
  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • ATM Machine
  • Non-Smoking Facility
