About Aristos Express CDMX

Located in Mexico City and with National Palace reachable within 4 miles, Aristos Express CDMX has express check-in and check-out, non-smoking rooms, a garden, free WiFi throughout the property and a terrace. The property is around 4.2 miles from Zocalo Square, 4.4 miles from Metropolitan Cathedral of Mexico City and 4.7 miles from Tenochtitlan Ceremonial Center. Private parking is available on site. The rooms at the hotel come with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a safety deposit box and a private bathroom with a shower, free toiletries and a hairdryer. Aristos Express CDMX provides certain accommodations with garden views, and every room includes a balcony. At the accommodation rooms include bed linen and towels. Speaking English and Spanish, staff will be happy to provide guests with practical advice on the area at the reception. The Museum of Fine Arts is 4.9 miles from Aristos Express CDMX, while Palacio de Correos is 5.1 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Benito Juarez International Airport, 1.9 miles from the hotel.
