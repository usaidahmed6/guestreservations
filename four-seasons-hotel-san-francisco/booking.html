About Four Seasons Hotel San Francisco

Located five minutes’ walk to the shops and restaurants at Union Square and Yerba Buena Gardens, this San Francisco hotel provides a full-service spa, an adult indoor lap pool and a restaurant. Select suites feature a view of San Francisco at the Four Seasons San Francisco. Each elegant room is equipped with a seating area, a large soaking bath tub, a flat-screen TV and an iPod docking station. Bathrobes, slippers and large deposit boxes to store valuables are also provided. San Francisco Four Seasons Hotel provides free access to the adjacent Equinox Sports Club fitness center. Guests can enjoy a group exercise class and relax with a massage in the spa. MKT Restaurant - Bar features an urban-chic atmosphere and offers handcrafted cocktails. Small plates for sharing, steaks from local ranches, and other market-to-table options are provided. The Ferry Building gourmet markets, shops and ferry transport to Sausalito is less than 20 minutes’ walk away from Four Seasons Hotel San Francisco. Pier 39 and Aquarium on the Bay at Fisherman’s Wharf is just 1.5 miles away.
