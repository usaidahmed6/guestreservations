About Agora Juan de Austria

This elegant hotel is located in the historic Chamberi neighborhood of Madrid and offers free Wi-Fi. The design of the hotel is based on the figure of Don Juan of Austria. It offers spacious and soundproofed rooms, decorated in a colonial style with patterned fabrics. Iglesia and Bilbao Metro Stations are both within 1476 feet of Agora Juan de Austria. These offer direct access to the main Atocha Train Station. The Agora Juan de Austria has a caf where guests can enjoy drinks and light meals. Private parking is offered at an extra cost.
