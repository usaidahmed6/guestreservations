About Park Plaza County Hall London

Stay at Park Plaza County Hall London for Unmatched Convenience

Located on London's vibrant South Bank, Park Plaza County Hall London offers modern and family-friendly accommodations just minutes from iconic landmarks like the River Thames and the London Eye. This hotel features a range of amenities designed to enhance the stay of every guest, ensuring both comfort and convenience in the heart of the city.

  • Bar and restaurant serving Mediterranean cuisine
  • Well-equipped gym with a sauna and steam room
  • Spacious rooms with Smart TVs and large work desks
  • 24-hour room service for round-the-clock convenience
  • Proximity to top attractions including Big Ben and the Houses of Parliament

Park Plaza County Hall London combines a central location with thoughtful amenities. Guests can enjoy Mediterranean dishes at the Atrio Restaurant & Bar, or start the day with a full English breakfast. The well-equipped gym includes a sauna and steam room, perfect for relaxation after a busy day. Spacious rooms come with Smart TVs, large work desks, and some even feature microwaves. The hotel's prime location offers easy access to major attractions like the London Eye, Big Ben, and the National Theater, making it an ideal choice for both leisure and business travelers.
