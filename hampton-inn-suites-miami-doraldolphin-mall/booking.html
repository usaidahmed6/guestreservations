About Hampton Inn By Hilton & Suites Miami-Doral/Dolphin Mall

Hampton Inn & Suites Miami-Doral Dolphin Mall features an outdoor pool and on-site restaurant. A free hot breakfast is provided daily. Costa Green Golf Club is 4 minutes drive away. Free WiFi, a flat-screen cable TV, and a desk are featured in each room. A sofa and kitchenette are included in all suites at this Miami-Doral Dolphin Mall Hampton Inn and Suites. A fitness center is available to all guests at Hampton Inn and Suites Miami-Doral as well. Local favorites such as Las Vegas Cuban Cuisine and Spizzigo Italian restaurant are within 2 minutes walk of the hotel. Miami International Airport is 9.9 mi away.
