About Auberge Vancouver Hotel

Located in the Vancouver city center, Auberge Vancouver Hotel features an indoor pool and a sauna. Free WiFi, a refrigerator, and a microwave are provided in all rooms. Gastown is 2625 ft away. A flat-screen cable TV and an iPod docking station are offered in rooms at Auberge Vancouver Hotel. A coffee maker is included. An private bathroom includes bathrobes. Select rooms feature harbor or mountain views. Lion's Pub offers a British menu including fish and chips, meatloaf and burgers. After visiting the fitness center, guests of Vancouver Auberge Hotel can relax in the hot tub. A Turkish bath is also featured. Table tennis and a squash court offer indoor recreation and a tour desk is on site. The Vancouver Convention Center is 10 minutes walk away. The Canada Place Cruise Ship Terminal is 1640 ft from Auberge Vancouver Hotel.
