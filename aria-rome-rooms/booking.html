About Aria Rome Rooms

Aria srls B&B is located in Rome. It offers free Wi-Fi and modern-style rooms. San Giovanni Metro Stop is 1312 feet away. A sweet breakfast is served daily. The air-conditioned rooms here will provide you with a balcony and parquet floors. Featuring a shower, private bathrooms also come with a hairdryer and free toiletries. A bus with links to Termini Train Station stops 328 feet from Aria B&B. The Coliseum is a 10-minute walk away.
