About The Royal Horseguards

Experience Historic Elegance at The Royal Horseguards

Located between the River Thames and Trafalgar Square, The Royal Horseguards offers guests a luxurious stay in a Grade I listed building that combines historical grandeur with modern elegance. Overlooking iconic landmarks such as the London Eye, and just a short walk from the National Gallery, this 4-star hotel serves as a gateway to the best of London, including Covent Garden and Theatreland.

Each bedroom at The Royal Horseguards reflects Victorian elegance paired with contemporary amenities. Guests can enjoy modern comforts such as free Wi-Fi, Elemis toiletries, a 49-inch LCD TV, and complimentary mineral water, ensuring a stay that is both lavish and comfortable.

Key features of The Royal Horseguards include:

  • One Twenty One Two Restaurant, offering exquisite dining with fine food and wine
  • Outdoor terrace for al fresco dining with views of the Thames
  • Access to a fully equipped gym to keep up with fitness routines
  • Strategic location near major London attractions like the National Gallery and London Eye
  • Luxurious rooms combining Victorian décor with high-end modern amenities

Whether you are visiting London for leisure or business, The Royal Horseguards provides an impeccable base with its prestigious location and superior facilities. Guests can indulge in the rich history of the building while enjoying the comforts and conveniences of a modern luxury hotel.

Choose The Royal Horseguards for a truly memorable stay in the heart of London, where history and contemporary luxury meet to deliver an unparalleled experience.
