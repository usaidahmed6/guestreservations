About Vatican Relais Rome

Offering free WiFi throughout the property, Vatican Relais Rome is located in Rome, a 5-minute walk from St. Peter's Basilica. All rooms in the bed and breakfast are fitted with a satellite flat-screen TV and air conditioning. The bathrooms come with free toiletries and a hairdryer. Some also have a spa bath. A buffet breakfast can be enjoyed at the property. Piazza del Popolo is 1.2 mi from Vatican Relais Rome, while Ottaviano metro stop is a 7-minute walk away.
