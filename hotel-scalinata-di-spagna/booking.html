About Hotel Scalinata Di Spagna

Set at the top of the Spanish Steps leading to Trinità dei Monti, Hotel Scalinata Di Spagna offers a covered rooftop terrace, free WiFi, and rooms with a free mini-bar. The elevator to Spagna Metro Station is 164 feet away. Classically furnished rooms here are equipped with an LCD satellite TV, and a free mini-bar containing water. Turndown service is provided daily. Served on the partially covered terrace, breakfast is an extensive sweet and savory buffet including scrambled eggs, bacon, fresh fruit, cold cuts and cheese. A coffee maker with grinder is available all day for free. Let the concierge provide the best of an attentive and personalized service and offer information or suggestions, for you to spend a truly pleasant stay.
