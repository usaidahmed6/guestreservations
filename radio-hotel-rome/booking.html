About Radio Hotel

Set 656 feet from Rome’s Trastevere Train Station, Radio Hotel offers rooms and self-catering accommodations with free WiFi. The property is located on the 7th floor of a building offering panoramic views of the city. Piramide Metro Station is a 15-minute walk away. Rooms at the Radio Hotel all come with a flat-screen TV, desk and fan. Some upper-floor rooms feature views of Rome and some have air conditioning. Bathrooms are either private or shared. A sweet and savory buffet breakfast is served daily in the breakfast room. There is a bar serving drinks and snacks. A bus stop in front of the property links to the city center and Termini Train Station. A shuttle service to Fiumicino Airport is available on request, and can be reached in 30 minutes.

Amenities

  • Room Service
  • 24 hour front desk
  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
