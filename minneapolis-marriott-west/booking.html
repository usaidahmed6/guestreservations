About Minneapolis Marriott West

Marriott West offers modern accommodations a 15-minute drive from central Minneapolis and 4 miles from Brookview Golf Course. It features an indoor pool, a 24-hour fitness center. Our spacious rooms at Minneapolis Marriott West are equipped with free Wi-Fi, TV's with Netflix capabilities, ample desk space and comfortable beds. Guests can enjoy Irish entertainment and food at the Kip's Irish Pub and Restaurant, open for breakfast, lunch and dinner. On site snacks 24/7. Popular nearby attractions include US Bank Stadium, 9 miles away and Mka Ska Park, 7 miles from the Minneapolis Marriott West hotel. Free parking is available at the property. A free shuttle is provided within 5 miles of the property based on availability.
