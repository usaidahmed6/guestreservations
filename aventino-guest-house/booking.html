About Aventino Guest House

Aventino Guest House is 984 feet from the Circo Massimo ancient stadium in Rome. Guests can stroll to the Imperial Forum and Coliseum in about 15 minutes. Offering free Wi-Fi and air-conditioned rooms with an private bathroom, the Aventino features a furnished patio. Surrounded by its own garden, the property is 10 minutes' walk from the famous Mouth of Truth and Caracalla Roman Baths. Roma Ostiense Train Station is 0.6 mi away.
