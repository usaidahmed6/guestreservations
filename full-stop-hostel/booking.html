About Full Stop. Hostel

Full stop. Hostel is a popular choice amongst travelers in Phuket, whether exploring or just passing through. Both business travelers and tourists can enjoy the hotel's facilities and services. All the necessary facilities, including free Wi-Fi in all rooms, daily housekeeping, taxi service, ticket service, luggage storage, are at hand. Designed for comfort, selected guestrooms offer television LCD/plasma screen, linens, locker, mirror, towels to ensure a restful night. The hotel offers various recreational opportunities. Full stop. Hostel is an excellent choice from which to explore Phuket or to simply relax and rejuvenate.
