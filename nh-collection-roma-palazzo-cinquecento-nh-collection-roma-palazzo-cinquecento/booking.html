About NH Collection Palazzo Cinquecento

Set in Piazza del Cinquecento Square in Rome, NH Collection Palazzo Cinquecento offers luxurious accommodations a few steps from Termini Metro and Train Station. A free fitness center and a rooftop terrace are featured. Each room and suite come with air conditioning, a mini bar and free WiFi. Some have a terrace and views of the surrounding extensive gardens with Roman ruins. Suites feature a seating area with flat-screen TV. Breakfast is served daily. Guests enjoy a bar and a restaurant that serves local dishes. Room service is also available. The Spanish Steps are a 25-minute walk from NH Collection Palazzo Cinquecento, while the Coliseum is 0.9 mi away. You can find links to all main attractions in Rome through the nearby metro.
