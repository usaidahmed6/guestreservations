About Casa Prim Hotel Boutique

Casa Prim Hotel Boutique features a fitness center, shared lounge, a terrace and restaurant in Mexico City. This 4-star hotel offers a bar. The property provides a 24-hour front desk, airport transportation, room service and free WiFi throughout the property. The hotel will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms with a closet, a coffee machine, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a shower. An American breakfast is available daily at Casa Prim Hotel Boutique. Popular points of interest near the accommodation include Museo de Memoria y Tolerancia, Museo de Arte Popular and United States Embassy. The nearest airport is Benito Juarez International Airport, 6.8 miles from Casa Prim Hotel Boutique.
