About Kellogg Conference Hotel

Welcome to the Kellogg Conference Hotel: A Gateway to History and Culture

Nestled within the historic grounds of Gallaudet University, the Kellogg Conference Hotel offers a unique glimpse into the architectural beauty and serene ambiance of an area recognized by the National Register of Historic Places. With structures dating back to the early 19th century and set amidst picturesque 99-acre campus grounds, guests are invited to explore and immerse themselves in a blend of history, education, and nature.

Strategically positioned to provide easy access to the heart of Washington, DC, this distinguished hotel is not only minutes away from Union Station but also within close proximity to some of the nations most iconic landmarks. Whether the day calls for a leisurely stroll on Capitol Hill, a shopping spree in DuPont Circle, or a night out in the vibrant neighborhood of Georgetown, the Kellogg Conference Hotel serves as the perfect starting point for all types of adventures.

  • Direct access to the historic and educational tours of Gallaudet Universitys campus
  • Proximity to Washington, DCs major attractions, including the Capitol, Smithsonian Institute, and Lincoln Memorial
  • Unique setting in a National Register of Historic Places-listed area
  • Perfect base for exploring the shopping districts, restaurants, and nightlife of Georgetown and DuPont Circle
  • Exceptional convenience for travelers, being just minutes from Union Station

Whether visiting for business or leisure, the Kellogg Conference Hotel promises an experience that transcends the ordinary. Discover the charm of historical Washington, DC, while enjoying the thoughtful amenities and comfort that make every stay memorable. Embark on a journey of discovery and indulge in the rich tapestry of culture and history that surrounds the Kellogg Conference Hotel.
