About Shinjuku Urban Hotel

In a prime location in the Shinjuku Ward district of Tokyo, Shinjuku Urban Hotel is located a 3-minute walk from Samurai Museum, a few steps from Full Gospel Tokyo Church and a 6-minute walk from Shinjuku Subnade Shopping Mall. This 2-star hotel offers a 24-hour front desk. The property is 2.2 miles from the city center and a 4-minute walk from Inari Kio Shrine. Selected rooms here will provide you with a kitchen with a fridge, a microwave and a stovetop. Popular points of interest near the hotel include Korea Museum, Shinjuku SanPark Shopping Mall and Studio Alta. The nearest airport is Tokyo Haneda Airport, 14 miles from Shinjuku Urban Hotel.

Amenities

  • 24 hour front desk
  • Masks are available to guests
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
