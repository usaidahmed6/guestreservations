About Hyatt Ziva Cancun

Experience the Enchantment of Hyatt Ziva Cancun

Discover the captivating beauty of Hyatt Ziva Cancun, a stunning beachfront resort situated on the picturesque Punta Cancun strip, surrounded by the crystal-clear waters of the Caribbean Sea. Immerse yourself in the serene ambiance of white sand beaches and azure waves, while enjoying top-tier amenities and a wealth of activities designed to create unforgettable memories.

  • Theater Shows and Live Entertainment: Enjoy captivating performances and lively shows that showcase local culture and talent.
  • Exclusive Microbrewery: Savor unique, locally brewed beers in an intimate setting, perfect for enthusiasts and casual drinkers alike.
  • Water Sports Adventures: Dive into an array of activities including snorkeling, sailing, and diving, all in the crystal-clear Caribbean waters.
  • Cascading Pools with Pool Bar: Relax in multi-level pools overlooking the sea, complete with a pool bar for refreshing drinks.
  • Oceanfront Spa: Rejuvenate with massages, holistic treatments, and hydrotherapy while enjoying breathtaking ocean views.

All rooms at Hyatt Ziva Cancun are thoughtfully appointed with flat-screen TVs, minibars, and coffee machines, alongside private bathrooms equipped with showers, bath robes, and slippers. Whether lounging in a beach cabana or partaking in tequila tastings, every moment at Hyatt Ziva Cancun is designed to provide an exceptional experience.

Conveniently located, Hyatt Ziva Cancun is just 984 feet from the lively Coco Bongo and 2.1 miles from La Isla Shopping Mall. Cancun International Airport is a short 11 miles away, ensuring easy access for travelers. Indulge in the vibrant spirit of Cancun at Hyatt Ziva Cancun, where adventure and relaxation seamlessly blend.
