About Domina Romae B&B

Domina Romae B&B enjoys a location in Rome, 1.2 miles from Battistini Metro Station and 1.7 miles from Vatican Museums. Complimentary Wifi is provided throughout the property and private parking is available on site. When staying at the bed and breakfast, guests can use private entrance. The bed and breakfast will provide guests with air-conditioned units with a desk, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom with a bidet. At the bed and breakfast, all units are allergy-free. The rooms are equipped with heating facilities. There is a coffee shop, and a mini-market is also available. St. Peter's Basilica is 1.8 miles from Domina Romae B&B, while Ottaviano Metro Station is 1.8 miles away. The nearest airport is Fiumicino, 16 miles from the accommodation, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
