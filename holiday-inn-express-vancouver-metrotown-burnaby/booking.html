About Holiday Inn Express Vancouver-Metrotown (Burnaby)

Welcome to Holiday Inn Express Vancouver-Metrotown (Burnaby)

Discover the newly renovated Holiday Inn Express Vancouver-Metrotown (Burnaby), ideally situated just 7.5 miles from Vancouver city center and a short 4-minute walk from Metrotown Station. Whether visiting for business or pleasure, enjoy a stay that combines comfort and convenience in a refreshed setting.

Each guest room is equipped with modern amenities such as a microwave, refrigerator, and flat-screen cable TV, ensuring a comfortable and hassle-free stay. Air-conditioning and dedicated workspaces cater to both leisure and business travelers. Start each day with a complimentary buffet breakfast, a perfect way to fuel up before exploring the city or attending meetings.

Enhance your experience with the hotels comprehensive facilities. Stay active in the fully-equipped fitness center, or relax by the outdoor pool, perfect for unwinding after a busy day. For those on business, take advantage of the on-site business facilities. Additionally, enjoy the convenience of complimentary underground parking with a height restriction of 6'8", making it easy for guests to come and go as they please.

  • Renovated guest rooms with modern appliances
  • Complimentary buffet breakfast
  • Proximity to Metrotown Station and major attractions
  • On-site fitness center and outdoor pool
  • Complimentary underground parking with height restrictions

Located just minutes away from Deer Lake Park and 23 minutes from Vancouver International Airport, the Holiday Inn Express Vancouver-Metrotown is an ideal choice for travelers seeking a convenient and comfortable experience in Vancouver. Whether it's for business or exploring the local area, this hotel ensures a memorable stay with a host of thoughtful amenities and a prime location.
