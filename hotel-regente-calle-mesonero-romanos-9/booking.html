About Regente Hotel

Hotel Regente is located on Madrid’s Gran Vía Avenue and 440 yards from Puerta del Sol Square. It offers smart, simple rooms with satellite TV and Wi-Fi access. The air conditioned rooms at the Regente feature parquet floors and light, elegant décor. They come with a private bathroom with a hairdryer. Facilities at the Regente Hotel include a fitness center and car rental service. The staff at the 24-hour front desk can help booking tickets for guided tours and live shows. The Regente is located on Gran Vía, which is full of shops, restaurants and theaters. You can walk to the Royal Palace in 10 minutes. Callao Metro Station, located just 110 yards away, provides easy public transportation connections with the rest of the city.
