About Calgary Airport Marriott In-Terminal Hotel

Located right next to Calgary International Airport, Calgary Airport Marriott In-Terminal Hotel features a fitness center and indoor pool. Free WiFi is provided. A 49" flat-screen TV is provided in the rooms at this Calgary hotel. A mini-refrigerator, sitting area, work desk, and an in-room safe are also featured. A private bathroom is available. Enjoy an unique culinary experience at YAKIMA Social Kitchen + Bar with a blend of local flavors. A spacious conference center is available for business travelers. Guests can relax with a full range of spa services at OraOxygen Spa which provides first class treatments. Calgary Zoo is and Calgary Telus Science Center are both 11 mi from the property. Cross Iron Mills shopping center is 14 minutes' drive away.
