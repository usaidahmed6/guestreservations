About Hotel La Villa Nice Promenade

Located in Nice City Center, this elegant hotel is just 164 feet from the Promenade des Anglais and a 15-minute walk from Nice-Ville Train Station. The hotel features a furnished terrace shaded by an orange tree. Free WiFi access is provided. Each air-conditioned guest room is equipped with a flat-screen TV and some of the stylish rooms have a balcony. The private bathrooms are decorated in light shades of gray. A buffet breakfast is served every morning at Hotel La Villa Nice Promenade. Guests can enjoy a drink on the terrace or in the stylish lounge areas. Nice's Old Town is 0.9 mi from Hotel La Villa Nice Promenade and the Fine Arts Museum is a 10-minute walk away. Gambetta Promenade bus stop is 2 minutes' walk away and Nice Cote d'Azur Airport is 3.7 mi from the property. Private parking is available on site, upon reservation only and at an extra cost.
