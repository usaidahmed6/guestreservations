About The Darling at The Star

Experience Unmatched Luxury at The Darling at The Star

Located just 2133 feet from Cockle Bay, The Darling at The Star offers a vibrant stay with a variety of amenities and attractions. This modern hotel features rooms with stunning views of the city skyline or Pyrmont and its surrounding suburbs. With a range of on-site facilities, including a casino, a day spa, and 20 bars and restaurants, guests are assured an exciting and comfortable stay.

Each room is equipped with a flat-screen satellite TV, iPod docking station, mini-bar, tea-and-coffee maker, and a safety deposit box. Guests can enjoy the comfort of air-conditioned rooms with luxurious bathrobes and Egyptian cotton sheets.

  • Casino and day spa for relaxation and entertainment
  • 20 diverse bars and restaurants, including Luke Nguyen’s Fat Noodle
  • Scented outdoor pool with a dedicated bar
  • Prime location near Sydney Opera House and The Rocks
  • Live music and extensive tequila selection at Rock Lily Bar

The Darling Sydney is conveniently located, with Sydney Opera House and The Rocks historic district just an 8-minute drive away. Sydney International Airport is a 20-minute drive, and the Sydney International Convention Center is a 10-minute walk from the hotel. Guests can unwind in the Turkish bath or enjoy the outdoor pool, making The Darling at The Star a perfect choice for an unforgettable stay in Sydney.
