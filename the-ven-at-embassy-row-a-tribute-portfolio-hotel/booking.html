About The Ven at Embassy Row, Washington, D.C., a Tribute Portfolio Hotel

Nestled between historic embassies and enchanting row homes, The Ven at Embassy Row, Washington, DC is located in the Dupont Circle district of Washington D.C. Located just 755 feet from the Dupont Circle Metro station, the hotel is only one metro stop from the National Zoo. A 47-inch LCD flat-screen TV is featured in each room at The Ven at Embassy Row, Washington, DC. Offering a view of the city, and also includes a large work desk. Guests staying at The Ven at Embassy Row, Washington, DC can enjoy the hotel's rooftop terrace and outdoor pool and a covered lounge. Seasonal rooftop dining is also provided, and guests can benefit from the fitness center and 24-hour front desk. WiFi is available in all areas. The Foggy Bottom - GWU Metro station is 0.7 mi from Embassy Row Hotel. The Capitol Building is 0.9 mi from The Ven at Embassy Row, Washington, DC.
