About Erase un Hotel

Featuring free high-speed WiFi and an on-site café, Erase un Hotel is located in Madrid's financial district, 0.9 mi from the Santiago Bernabeu Stadium. Private parking is also available at the property. The modern and stylish rooms at this hotel include air conditioning, wooden floors, and a private bathroom with a bath or shower and a hairdryer. A TV, mini-bar, and a work desk are also available. Valdeacederas Metro Station is 656 feet from Erase un Hotel. The famous Puerta del Sol Square is 25 minutes away by metro, and Retiro Park is 8 stops away. There is easy access to the M-40 and M-30 roads, allowing access to the IFEMA Exhibition Center and Barajas Airport within 15 minutes. Plaza de Castilla Square and Chamartin Train Station are both within 10 minutes' walk of the property.
