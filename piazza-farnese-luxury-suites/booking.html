About Piazza Farnese Luxury Suites

In a 15th-century building and offering views of Piazza Farnese, the Luxury Suite has spacious and elegant rooms in the heart of Rome. The property features free Wi-Fi access throughout. With air conditioning and a satellite flat-screen TV, each room includes an iPod docking station, coffee machine and electric kettle. The private bathroom has a bathtub or shower, bidet and hairdryer. Some rooms have a spa tub. A continental breakfast buffet is served daily in the breakfast room. It includes local products, freshly brewed coffee or cappuccino, sweet pastries, cold cuts and cheeses. Campo dé Fiori Market is 165 ft from Piazza Farnese Luxury Suite, and Piazza Navona is a 4-minute walk away. The Pantheon is just under half a mile from the property.

Amenities

  • Room Service
  • Car Rental
  • Balcony
  • Non-Smoking Facility
