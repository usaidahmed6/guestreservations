About Shanghai Chi Chen Boutique Hotel

Only 98 feet from Xiaonanmen Subway Station (line 9), Shanghai Chi Chen Hotel is located in Shanghai. Free Wi-Fi access is available. Shanghai Chi Chen Hotel is a 15-minute walk from Yu Garden, a 20-minute walk from Nanjing Road pedestrian street, and a 10-minute drive from People's Square or Nanjing Road Pedestrian Street. It is a 15-minute drive from Xin Tian Di. Pudong International Airport is about 24 mi away. Shanghai Disneyland is a 33-minute drive away. Each room here will provide you with a cable TV and air conditioning. There is also an electric kettle. Featuring a shower, private bathroom also comes with a hairdryer and free toiletries. Extras include a sofa and a desk. At Shanghai Chi Chen Hotel you will find a 24-hour front desk. Others offered at the property include a shared lounge with TV, free luggage storage, a games room and ticketing service. The property offers free parking. Guests can use the computer at the lobby for free.
