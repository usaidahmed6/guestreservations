About Motto by Hilton Washington DC City Center

Stay Smart at Motto by Hilton Washington DC City Center

Located in the vibrant Penn Quarter, just across the street from Gallery Place/Chinatown Metro, Motto by Hilton Washington DC City Center offers a modern urban experience. This micro-hotel is designed for efficiency and comfort, making it an ideal choice for travelers looking to explore Washington DC. The hotel is within walking distance of top attractions such as the Washington Convention Center, National Mall, and the White House.

Each guest room at Motto by Hilton Washington DC City Center is smartly designed with a 50-inch flat-screen TV with streaming capabilities, private bathrooms featuring customized bath products, in-room safes, hairdryers, and compact workspaces. Guests can enjoy the convenience of a 24-hour front desk and the urban vibe of the hotel's restaurant and bar.

  • Prime Location in Penn Quarter
  • Smart and Efficient Guest Rooms
  • On-Site Restaurant and Bar
  • 24-Hour Front Desk
  • Walking Distance to Top DC Attractions

Motto by Hilton Washington DC City Center offers a unique urban experience with its micro-hotel concept, providing all the essentials for a comfortable stay. Whether visiting for business or leisure, guests can enjoy the proximity to Union Station and Ronald Reagan Washington National Airport, making travel convenient and hassle-free. Book your stay today and discover the smart way to experience Washington DC.
