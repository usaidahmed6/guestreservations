About Hyatt Place Minneapolis Downtown

Discover Downtown Comfort at Hyatt Place Minneapolis Downtown

Experience the heart of Minneapolis with a stay at Hyatt Place Minneapolis Downtown, seamlessly connected to the city's indoor skyway system. Ideal for business and leisure travelers, this centrally located hotel puts you within walking distance of the Minneapolis Convention Center and Target Field. Enjoy the blend of convenience and comfort, all set against the vibrant backdrop of downtown Minneapolis.

Each guest room is equipped with modern essentials, including a 42-inch flat-screen HDTV, small refrigerator, and coffee maker, designed to make your stay as comfortable as possible. A dedicated work area with an ergonomic chair ensures productivity for those visiting on business.

Hyatt Place Minneapolis Downtown is not just about a good night's sleep. Dive into the indoor pool or stay active in the 24-hour fitness center. For those who need to stay connected and productive, the 24-hour business center and meeting facilities provide all the necessary resources. Dining options are conveniently available around the clock at the 24/7 Gallery Market, which serves a variety of flatbreads, hot appetizers, and to-go sandwiches.

  • Direct access to Minneapolis skyway system
  • 24-hour indoor pool
  • Round-the-clock fitness center
  • 24/7 dining at Gallery Market
  • Immediate proximity to major Minneapolis landmarks

Whether you're here to explore the University of Minnesota or attend an event at the nearby venues, Hyatt Place Minneapolis Downtown offers a prime location and thoughtful amenities to enhance your stay in the city. Enjoy everything from premium coffee at the Coffee to Cocktails Bar to seamless connectivity and convenience throughout your visit.
