About Shakespeare Hotel

Just 656 feet from Paddington Tube Station, Shakespeare Hotel is located in charming, tree-lined Norfolk Square. It has a 24-hour front desk and luggage storage. The rooms at Shakespeare Hotel come with a private bathroom, tea and coffee facilities and a fridge. Please note there is no elevator at the hotel. A generous breakfast (included in some rates) is served daily in the bright and airy breakfast room. Hot beverages and soft drinks are available 24-hours, and there are a number of restaurants within a few minutes’ walk. Located just north of Hyde Park, the Shakespeare Hotel is a 15-minute walk from Marble Arch and lively Oxford Street. Knightsbridge can be reached with a 20-minute walk through Hyde Park.
