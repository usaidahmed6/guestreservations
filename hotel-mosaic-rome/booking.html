About Hotel Mosaic

Hotel Mosaic is in a historic building behind Rome's Santa Maria Degli Angeli Basilica and Piazza della Repubblica, 0.3 miles from Termini Train Station. Rooms offer air conditioning and free Wi-Fi. All rooms at Mosaic Hotel have marble floors and some have a private bathroom. Breakfast is served in the breakfast room. The Mosaic is a short walk from the shops of Via Nazionale. The staff is available 24-hours and can provide travel and tourist info.

Amenities

  • 24 hour front desk
  • Airport Transportation
