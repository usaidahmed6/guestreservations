About Savoy Hotel

This hotel is set in a 1905, Art Deco building just 8 minutes walk from Tivoli Gardens and Copenhagen Central Station. All rooms face the quiet inner courtyard and include a TV and tea/coffee facilities. SAVOY Hotel have 66 rooms on 6 floors. All rooms have a flat screen with built-in Chromecast, and All rooms face a quiet courtyard. If You want to experience the calmness feel of a seaside style hotel, but still be in the vibrant city - This is it. Free Wi-Fi internet is available throughout the building, and bicycles can be rented on site. Hotel Savoys staff can give directions or tips on local attractions. The hotel is in the localhood of Vesterbro with numourous restaurants and only 5 minutes walk to the popular Meat Packing district (Kodbyen). The Tycho Brahe Planetarium is only 656 feet away and offers a 3-D IMAX theater.
