About Orchard Hotel

Located in Downtown San Francisco, this boutique hotel is just 2 blocks from Union Square. It features an on-site restaurant, a cardio room, and spacious rooms with free WiFi. Rooms feature traditional decor. The Orchard Hotel rooms offer a desk and laptop safe. Decorated with black and white photography of San Francisco scenery, the rooms also have mini-bars. Smart TVs are also included with the ability to connect to guests' phones or tablets. Guests can enjoy on-site dining at Daffodil Restaurant and Bar. The hotel also offers discount passes to the full-service Active Sports Club. The Orchard Hotel is within walking distance of a BART subway station, Chinatown and the Cable Car Museum.
