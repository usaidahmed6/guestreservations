About Hotel Indigo Rome - St. George

Offering a free spa, an à la carte restaurant and a mini-gym, Hotel Indigo Rome - St. George is located on the exclusive Via Giulia, just 1969 feet from Campo De’ Fiori in Rome. Free WiFi is available. Elegant, air-conditioned rooms come with a flat-screen TV with Sky channels, an electric kettle and mini-bar. The bathrooms include a hairdryer and free toiletries. The spa and wellness center includes a sauna and Turkish bath. The hotel is 2461 feet from Piazza Navona, while Vatican City is 0.9 mi away. The lively area of Trastevere is a 15-minute walk from the hotel.
