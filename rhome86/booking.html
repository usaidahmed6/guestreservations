About Rhome86

Rhome86 is located in a central area of Rome, a few steps from Vittorio Emanuele Metro and 3 metro stops from the Spanish Steps. This B&B offers free Wi-Fi and air-conditioned rooms with private bathrooms. All rooms are colorful and individually decorated. Each offers a TV with satellite channels. Guests enjoy free use of a computer with webcam and printer, in the communal lounge. A tea corner with herbal teas and teas is available 24 hours a day. The property is a 10-minute walk from Roma Termini Station, departing point of shuttles to/from the 2 airports of Rome. Transfer services from/to Ciampino and Fiumicino Airports can also be booked at reception. The Coliseum is a 15-minute walk away.

Amenities

  • Room Service
  • Masks are available to guests
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
