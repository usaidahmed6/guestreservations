About Novotel Sydney International Airport Hotel

Just 0.6 mi from Sydney International Airport, Novotel Sydney International Airport features a bar, 24-hour room service and an airport shuttle. All of the renovated and refurbished rooms have views over a golf course or the airport. Guests can enjoy 30 minutes of free WiFi in the lobby. Located next to Cahill Park, Novotel Sydney International Airport is 5 minutes walk from Kogarah Golf Course and the Cooks River. Wolli Creek Train Station is a 15-minute walk away. The air-conditioned rooms feature blackout curtains, individual climate control, satellite TV, a tea and coffee maker and a work desk. The bathroom features a hairdryer and guest toiletries. The Marke Kitchen & Bar serves modern Australian dishes and an international breakfast buffet. The Bar offers a snack menu and a large screen showing live sports.
