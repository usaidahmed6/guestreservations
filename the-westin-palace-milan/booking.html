About The Westin Palace Milan

The Westin Palace Milan is an iconic meeting point for Milanese and travelers, recently renovated after a full restyling of common areas and meeting rooms. Set in the Porta Nuova district, the hotel features a red marble faade and a rooftop terrace with city views. The rooms combine classical architecture with contemporary design. Each room features LCD TV and the brands Heavenly Bed and Heavenly Bath. WiFi is free throughout. The hotel is complete with a wellness center and the Westin Workout gym. Breakfast is an extensive sweet and savory buffet, including hot dishes. The Westin's PanEVO restaurant serves traditional and regional cuisine for dinner. The Lounge Bar is decorated with precious woods and fabrics. From Repubblica Train and Metro Station, links to Rho Fiera Exhibition Center are available. Linate Airport is a 20-minute drive away.
