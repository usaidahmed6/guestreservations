About Mokinba Hotels King

Discover Milanese Charm at Mokinba Hotels King

Located in the heart of Milan, Mokinba Hotels King offers an enchanting stay amidst the city's rich history and vibrant culture. Just a 10-minute walk from the magnificent Duomo and the iconic Last Supper by Leonardo da Vinci, this hotel is perfectly positioned for guests to explore Milan's artistic heritage. Close to Cadorna Station, Mokinba Hotels King boasts excellent metro and tram links, making it an ideal base for discovering all that Milan has to offer.

The guest rooms are designed for comfort and convenience, featuring air conditioning, a radio, a well-stocked mini-bar, and complimentary WiFi throughout the hotel. Start your day with a generous buffet breakfast, a delightful spread that promises to energize you for your adventures ahead.

Key features include:

  • Prime location near Milan's Duomo, Leonardos Last Supper, and Sforzesco Castle.
  • Easy access to public transport with close proximity to Cadorna Station.
  • Comfortable, air-conditioned rooms with en suite bathrooms and free WiFi.
  • Generous buffet breakfast to kickstart your morning.
  • 12-hour reception and a large garage nearby for your convenience.

At Mokinba Hotels King, we are dedicated to making your Milanese getaway as memorable as it is comfortable. Whether you're here to delve into the city's illustrious art scene, marvel at architectural wonders, or simply soak in the local culture, this hotel serves as a tranquil retreat in the bustling city center. Experience Milan in style and comfort at Mokinba Hotels King, where every guest is treated to the royal treatment.
