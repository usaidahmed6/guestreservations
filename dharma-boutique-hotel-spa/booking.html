About Dharma Boutique Hotel & SPA

Dharma Boutique Hotel & SPA is located in Monti, one of Rome's most popular districts. Cavour Metro Station is nearby, and Termini Train Station and the Coliseum are about 10 minutes' walk away. The rooms offer air conditioning and free Wi-Fi. All rooms come with private bathroom, a flat-screen TV, and wooden furniture. Guests enjoy a continental breakfast daily. Dharma Boutique Hotel & SPA is a 19th-century building with an elegant breakfast room, and a bar open 24-hour. You can enjoy views over the rooftops of Rome from the beautiful terrace, where breakfast is served during summer.
