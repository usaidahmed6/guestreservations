About Hotel Berlino

Set a 5-minute walk from the lively Corso Sempione street, Hotel Berlino is located in a residential area of Milan. It is 0.6 mi from Domodossola Metro and 0.9 mi from the Fiera Milano City exhibition center. With free WiFi throughout, the Berlino hotel offers rooms with parquet floors, a Smart TV and air conditioning. A sweet and savory buffet breakfast is served daily in a breakfast room. Gluten-free options are also available. At the property guests can also enjoy a street art exhibition by local artists. The hotel is opposite the stop of several tram lines, linking to Milan Cathedral and the city center. Bus number 90 takes you directly to Milano Centrale Train Station. Portello shopping center is just 0.6 mi from the property, while San Siro Stadium is a 5-minute drive away. The Rho Fiera Exhibition Center is 6.8 mi from the property.
