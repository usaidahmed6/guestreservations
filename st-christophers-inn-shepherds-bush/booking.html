About St Christopher's Inn Shepherd's Bush

Located 4 minutes walk from Shepherds Bush tube station, Westfield shopping center and various pubs and bars, St Christopher's Shepherds Bush is located above Belushis bar which features live sports on a big-screen TV, drink deals and homemade burgers. Shared bathroom facilities are provided with each room at St Christophers Shepherd's Bush. A luggage room, free Wi-Fi throughout and a 24-hour reception are also provided. Guests can relax in the chill-out room which offers a 52-inch plasma-screen satellite TV, 24-hour bar and table tennis. A continental breakfast is available daily at St Christopher's Shepherd's Bush and the Belushis Bar offers guests a 25% discount on food and drinks offers. Piccadilly Circus, Oxford Street and Leicester Square can all be reached in under 30 minutes from Shepherds Bush tube station, and 24-hour night busses run between Shepherds Bush and central London. The O2 Shepherds Bush Empire is a 5-minute walk away and hosts regular concerts.
