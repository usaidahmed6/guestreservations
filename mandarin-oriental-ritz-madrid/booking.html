About Mandarin Oriental Ritz, Madrid

Opulent and exclusive Mandarin Oriental Ritz, Madrid is located in a Belle Époque palace and offers an experience unlike any other. It features a luxurious Wellness Centre, free parking and in suite reservations free Wi-Fi. One-way airport transportation from Palm Court Suite bookings is also included.. The hotel’s 100 rooms and 53 spacious suites are individually decorated with rich materials and a classic-contemporary design, which blends seamlessly with the hotel’s iconic structure. Mandarin Oriental Ritz, Madrid has a wellness centre with indoor swimming pool, a vitality pool, experience showers, a steam room and a fully-equipped fitness centre that offers dedicated treatments. Overseen by acclaimed Chef Quique Dacosta, the hotel is home to five restaurants and bars. The hotel is set in the heart of the Retiro district between the Retiro Park and Madrid’s most important museums. Prado and Thyssen Bornemisza museums are just 200 metres from the hotel while the Reina Sofia museum is 10 minutes walk away.
