About Hyton Leelavadee Hotel - SHA Plus

A short 3-minute walk from Patong Beach, Hyton Leelavadee offers air-conditioned rooms surrounded by tropical gardens. The hotel has an outdoor pool with jet stream, traditional Thai massage, a 24-hour front desk, and free WiFi. Air-conditioned and spacious, rooms each come with a private balcony and a cable/satellite TV. Modern amenities include a mini-bar, bathrobes and slippers. Hyton Leelavadee Hotel - SHA Plus is a 15-minute walk from key attractions like Jungceylon Shopping Center, Soi Bangla and Tri Trang Beach. It is a 30-minute drive to Phuket International Airport. For relaxation and fun, Hotel Hyton has a fitness center equipped with cardio equipment, a spa bath and aromatherapy massage therapies. There are also barbecue facilities for casual outdoor dining. The on-site restaurant serves a daily American breakfast. Local Thai dishes are also served there.
