About Budget Inn - Washington

Located 6 miles from Country Oaks Golf Club, this Washington, Indiana motel offers free Wi-Fi internet access throughout the property. Each guest room includes cable TV. A microwave and fridge are provided in every air-conditioned rooms at Budget Inn – Washington. Guests can make use of the coffee-making facilities and ironing facilities during their stay. Free parking is available on site at Washington Budget Inn. Fax and photocopying services are offered for additional guest convenience. Dogwood Lake is 13 miles away. Gasthof Amish Village can be reached in 20 minutes’ drive.
