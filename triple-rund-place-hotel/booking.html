About Triple Rund Place Hotel

Set in a prime location of Phuket, Maelarn Hotel puts everything the city has to offer just outside your doorstep. Featuring a complete list of amenities, guests will find their stay at the property a comfortable one. 24-hour room service, free Wi-Fi in all rooms, 24-hour front desk, Wi-Fi in public areas, car park are just some of the facilities on offer. Comfortable guestrooms ensure a good night's sleep with some rooms featuring facilities such as internet access wireless, internet access wireless (complimentary), non smoking rooms, air conditioning, wake-up service. Enjoy the hotel's recreational facilities, including massage, karaoke, before retiring to your room for a well-deserved rest. No matter what your reasons are for visiting Phuket, Maelarn Hotel will make you feel instantly at home.
