About Nob Hill Inn

This hotel is located in the Nob Hill district of San Francisco and is within a 10-minute walk of Union Square. The boutique hotel offers rooms with a flat-screen TV. Nob Hill Inn rooms include a private bathroom. The rooms have unique décor in the Edwardian style. The suites have a separate seating area. Guests at Nob Hill can enjoy the historic hotel, built in 1907 as a private residence or explore the surrounding area. Inn Nob Hill is within a 15-minute walk of Chinatown, which offers shopping and dining. The famous Fisherman’s Wharf is one mile from the hotel.
