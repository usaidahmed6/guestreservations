About Hotel Grand Pacific

Welcome to Hotel Grand Pacific: Elegance by Victoria’s Waterfront

Immerse yourself in the understated elegance of Hotel Grand Pacific, located just a stone's throw from the Victoria Clipper and Coho Ferry Terminals. The Hotel Grand Pacific hotel offers guests a seamless blend of comfort and convenience, with every room designed to be a peaceful retreat after a day of exploration.

  • Prime Location: Only 3 minutes’ walk from the ferry terminals, offering easy access to and from Victoria.
  • Comfortable Accommodations: Each guest room features a private balcony, flat-screen cable TV, and complimentary WiFi, ensuring a relaxing stay.
  • Indoor Pool and Hot Tub: Unwind in the indoor pool or relax sore muscles in the hot tub, perfect after a day of sightseeing.
  • Diverse Dining Options: From the upscale FATHOM Restaurant & Terrace to the casual ambiance of The Courtyard Café, each dining option caters to all tastes and occasions.
  • Convenient Amenities: Guests enjoy access to s fitness center and rooms come equipped with tea and coffee-making facilities, desks, and cozy seating areas.

Located just moments from Beacon Hill Park and the BC Parliament Buildings, Hotel Grand Pacific stands as a gateway to Victoria's charm and natural beauty. Experience the perfect blend of sophistication and warmth at Hotel Grand Pacific, where every stay is memorable.
