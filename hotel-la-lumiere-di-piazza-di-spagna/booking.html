About Hotel La Lumiere Di Piazza Di Spagna

Set in the historical center of Rome, 656 feet from the Spanish Steps, Hotel La Lumière di Piazza di Spagna, offers elegant rooms and a rooftop terrace. Hotel La Lumière di Piazza di Spagna provides a stylish atmosphere where you can admire precious marble work and antique furniture. Relax in elegant rooms furnished with modern comforts such as satellite TV. You can even request a room with a view. Your day will begin with a buffet breakfast in the bright restaurant, featuring views of the Eternal City. From the roof terrace, guests can have a special panorama of Rome including the famous attractions of Trinità dei Monti and Villa Medici. Hotel La Lumière di Piazza di Spagna is set in a renovated medieval building next to the most exclusive shopping districts in Rome. You will be within walking distance of the Trevi Fountain, Pantheon, Via Veneto and Piazza Navona.
