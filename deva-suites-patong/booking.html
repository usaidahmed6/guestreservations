About Deva Suites Patong Hotel

Set in a prime location of Phuket, Deva Suites Patong Hotel puts everything the city has to offer just outside your doorstep. The hotel has everything you need for a comfortable stay. To be found at the hotel are free Wi-Fi in all rooms, 24-hour security, daily housekeeping, convenience store, taxi service. Guestrooms are fitted with all the amenities you need for a good night's sleep. In some of the rooms, guests can find linens, mirror, towels, umbrella, closet. Enjoy the hotel's recreational facilities, including golf course (within 3 km), diving, massage, billiards, before retiring to your room for a well-deserved rest. For reliable service and professional staff, Deva Suites Patong Hotel caters to your needs.
