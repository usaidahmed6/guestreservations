About Far Home Bernabeu

Located in Madrid and with Santiago Bernabéu Stadium reachable within a 15-minute walk, Far Home Bernabeu has a tour desk, non-smoking rooms, a shared lounge, free WiFi throughout the property and a terrace. The property is around 2.1 miles from Chamartin Station, 2.8 miles from Gran Via Station Metro Station and 2.8 miles from Temple of Debod. The property provides a shared kitchen and a 24-hour front desk for guests. The rooms include air conditioning, a desk and a TV, and some accommodations at the hostel have a balcony. All rooms have bed linen. Buffet and continental breakfast options are available each morning at Far Home Bernabeu. Plaza de España Metro Station is 2.8 miles from the accommodation, while Gran Via is 2.8 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Adolfo Suarez Madrid-Barajas Airport, 9.3 miles from Far Home Bernabeu.
