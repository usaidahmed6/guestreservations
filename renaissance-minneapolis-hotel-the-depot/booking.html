About Renaissance by Marriott Minneapolis Hotel, The Depot

The Renaissance Minneapolis Hotel, The Depot is located 11 miles from Mall of America and features an indoor pool. Spacious rooms offer 43-inch and 50-inch flat-screen TVs with HBO. Guest rooms at this historic hotel provide WiFi, seating areas and desks. They also have ironing facilities and coffee makers. Renaissance Minneapolis Hotel, The Depot offers guests a fitness center. It has a restaurant and bar and is equipped with meeting rooms. The hotel provides laundry facilities and valet dry cleaning. Shoe shines are available for guests. The Depot Hotel is less than one mile from the River Front District and Mill City Museum. It is 2 miles from University of Minnesota and 13 miles from Minneapolis-Saint Paul International Airport.
