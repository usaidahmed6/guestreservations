About San Francisco Marriott Fisherman's Wharf

This San Francisco hotel is within 10 minutes' walk of Fishermans Wharf. The hotel offers a refrigerator in every room. Marriott San Francisco Fishermans Wharf rooms include a coffee maker and hairdryer. The rooms are equipped with cable TV and feature pay-per-view movies. The San Francisco Marriott is a pet-friendly hotel. The hotel features a business center and a gym. Free coffee is offered in the lobby each morning. The Marriott San Francisco is within 5 minutes' walk of Ghirardelli Square, which features dining and shopping. The Golden Gate Bridge is 3 miles from the hotel. Pier 39 is within 15 minutes' walk of the hotel.
