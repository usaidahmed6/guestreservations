About 1 Hotel Mayfair

Nestled in the heart of London's iconic Mayfair district surrounded by the vibrant natural beauty of numerous Royal Parks and the unparalleled history of world-renowned museums, galleries, and theaters. 1 Hotel Mayfair echoes the tale of a stunning cityscape uniquely graced by nature's boundless wonder. Thoughtful touches bring nature into your space with flourishing greenery, sustainably-sourced British oak flooring, hand-chiseled stones and filtered water taps in every room. 1 Hotel Mayfair's restaurant and terrace, Dovetale by two Michelin starred chef Tom Sellers, is grounded in a deep respect for seasonal, organic and locally sourced ingredients. The hotel also houses a signature cocktail bar, Dover Yard as well as a welcoming lobby bar and cafe, Neighbors. The gym is fully equipped with the state-of-the-art equipment. Cardio on a resistance treadmill with a Dover Street view or recharge by stretching on a yoga mat on a reclaimed wooden floor. Alternatively, ease into wellness at Bamford Wellness Spa through their individualized treatments and natural & organic skincare products.
