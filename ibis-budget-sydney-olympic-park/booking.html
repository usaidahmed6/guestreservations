About Ibis Budget Sydney Olympic Park

Located in the heart of Sydney Olympic Park, this hotel offers air-conditioned rooms with flat-screen TV and a desk. Qudos Bank Arena (Allphones Arena) and ANZ Stadium are less than 10 minutes walk away. Free WiFi is provided throughout the hotel. Olympic Park Train Station and Sydney International Aquatic Center are each a 2-minute walk from ibis Budget Sydney Olympic Park. Paddys Market Flemington is a 5-minute drive away. There are also a number of restaurants and bars within a 10-minute walk. Sydneys city center is 30 minutes away by car or train.
