About Siray House

Siray House offers self-catering accommodations with free WiFi and free private parking. The property is a 10-minute driver from Rassada Pier and a 15-minute drive from the heart of Phuket Town. The apartment comes with a cable TV, air conditioning and a seating area. Featuring a shower, private bathroom also comes with free toiletries. At Siray House guests can find a garden, a shared lounge and TV area. Car and bicycle rentals as well as shuttle services can be arranged by the property staff. Guests can enjoy local dishes featuring seafood at the local restaurant located within a few minutes' walk from the property. Siray House is located a 5.6 mi drive from Central Festival Phuket. Phuket International Airport is a 1-hour drive away.
