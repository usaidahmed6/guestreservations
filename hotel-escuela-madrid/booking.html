About Hotel Escuela Madrid

Hotel Escuela Madrid is set 25 minutes drive outside central Madrid, close to the M40 Motorway and just 2.5 mi from the Universidad Autonoma de Madrid. It has a caf and free Wi-Fi. The simply decorated and air-conditioned rooms at Hotel Escuela Madrid have a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. The surrounding countryside is ideal for hiking and cycling. The Sierra de Guadarrama Mountains are approximately 19 mi away. The IFEMA Exhibition Center is a 20-minute drive from the property. Barajas Airport can be reached in 30 minutes by car.
