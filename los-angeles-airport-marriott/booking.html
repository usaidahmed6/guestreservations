About Los Angeles Airport Marriott

Boasting a resort pool, this Los Angeles, California hotel offers several upscale dining options. The hotel offers a free airport shuttle to Los Angeles International Airport, which is 1.5 mi away. At the Los Angeles Airport Marriott, guest rooms are equipped with a coffee maker and TV internet access. Select rooms offer concierge lounge access with free breakfast, open on the weekdays. Guests can unwind at the fitness center or utilize the meeting event space. The Manhattan Beach Pier is a short drive from the Marriott Los Angeles Airport. The Santa Monica Pier and Pacific Park are also within driving distance of the hotel.
