About Hotel Viminale

Offering a convenient central location just 2297 feet from Termini Station, Hotel Viminale is a 10-minute walk from the Coliseum. It offers a roof garden with panoramic views of Rome. Housed in a historical building and all air conditioned, rooms at Viminale feature classic dcor, parquet or carpeted floors, a mini-bar, and satellite TV. Breakfast is a continental-style buffet including ham, cheese, eggs, cakes, pastries, fresh fruit and hot drinks. It can be enjoyed out on the courtyard and is available from 7:00. Guests will have excellent transport links from Termini Station, where they can get both lines of the Metro and reach every area in Rome.

Amenities

  • 24 hour front desk
  • Laundry
