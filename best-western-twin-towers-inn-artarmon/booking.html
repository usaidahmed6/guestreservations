About Twin Towers Inn

Welcome to Twin Towers Inn, Sydney Twin Towers Inn is located only 15 minutes from the Sydney CBD and all its major attractions. The commercial centers stretching from North Sydney, St Leonards, to Chatswood are also positioned conveniently nearby. Onsite facilities such as complimentary parking, seasonal swimming pool and guest kitchen make it an ideal place for your next family gateway, also for the corporate and leisure guests. Restaurant and Bar are currently closed for dinner. Breakfast is available from 7-9:30am daily, dine in only. Shuttle Service is available Monday to Friday from 9:30am to 1pm. Not available on Public Holidays.
