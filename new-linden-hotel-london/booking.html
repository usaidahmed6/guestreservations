About New Linden Hotel

Located in the heart of the Notting Hill in the West End of London, this boutique, stylish and luxury hotel is located just a short stroll from Notting Hill Gate and Bayswater Tube Stations. Famous Portobello Road Market with its stalls, cafes and restaurants is also a short walk away. Individually designed and themed, the bedrooms with attached bathroom come with smart flat-screen TV, iPod docking station, luxury Egyptian cotton sheets, along with marble, limestone and slate bathrooms with walk-in power showers and bathtub. The suites feature floor-to-ceiling windows and and high ceilings. Every room comes with a mobile including mobile data and calls to several countries that guests can use. Local attractions include Madame Tussauds, Hyde Park, Marble Arch, Oxford Street and Marylebone.
