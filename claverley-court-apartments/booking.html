About Claverley Court Apartment Knightsbridge

These elegant modern apartments have smart wooden floors and free Wi-Fi, and are located in Knightsbridge, one of London’s most wealthiest and trendy areas. Harrods and Hyde Park are both a 5-minute walk away from Claverley Court Apartments Knightsbridge. Claverley Court Apartments Knightsbridge offers 5-star accommodations in a picturesque tree-lined square. There is a comfortable lounge area featuring a flat-screen TV with Sky channels, a DVD player, and an iPod docking station. The fully equipped kitchen has a dishwasher and washer/dryer, as well as an oven, microwave, and fridge. Buckingham Palace can be reached in around 25 minutes’ walk, or is just a short Tube ride away. Knightsbridge Underground Station is less than a 10-minute walk from the building.
