About Sino Imperial Phuket - SHA Plus

Sino Imperial Phuket offers nonsmoking and modern rooms with private bathrooms and free mini-bar. It is a 5-minute ride from Robinson Shopping Mall and local market. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel. The classic Chino-Portuguese style buildings of Old Phuket Town and Thalang Road are a 15-minute drive from Sino Imperial Phuket. Phuket International Airport is a 35-minute ride away. This hotel's uniquely designed rooms have air conditioning, a flat-screen cable TV and a refrigerator. Hot and cold shower facilities are in the private bathrooms. Luggage storage and airport shuttle can be arranged at the 24-hour front desk. Fax and photocopying service is available. Staff at the tour desk can help guests with travel arrangements. A range of local and international restaurants are located within a 5-minute drive from the hotel.
