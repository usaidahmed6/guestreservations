About Hostal Castilla II Puerta del Sol

Only 82 feet from Puerta del Sol and Plaza Mayor, this guest house offers rooms with flat-screen TVs and free WiFi. The Prado Museum and Madrid’s botanic gardens are a 10-minute walk away. Hostal Castilla II provides accommodations with individually controlled air conditioning and work desks, as well as a private bathroom. Some rooms include a small balcony. Vending machines with drinks and snacks are available to all guests. In the evening, visitors can discover many authentic tapas bars around the Castilla II area. Hostal Castilla II Puerta del Sol is located 2297 feet from the Royal Palace and 2625 feet from the Reina Sofia Museum.

Amenities
