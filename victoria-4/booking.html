About Victoria 4 Puerta del Sol

This historic hotel is in the heart of Madrid, by the Puerta del Sol. Facilities include free Wi-Fi and a café-bar on the large outdoor terrace. The Hotel Victoria 4 Puerta del Sol dates back to 1850 and offers elegant, renovated interiors. Rooms at Victoria 4 Puerta del Sol are soundproof, with a free safe. They all have a spacious bathroom with a bathtub and hairdryer. The hotel has a traditional restaurant, La Carmela, where guests can enjoy breakfast, lunch or dinner. The restaurant also features a range of tapas. Sol Metro Station is only yards away and offers easy transport around the city. Victoria 4 Puerta del Sol is in a lively area, within walking distance of Gran Vía and the Art Triangle.
