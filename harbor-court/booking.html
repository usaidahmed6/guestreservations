About Harbor Court Hotel

Nestled along the edge of the San Francisco Bay, between the Financial District and Oracle Park, Harbor Court Hotel is a completely nonsmoking, boutique hotel. It is eco-friendly. Harbor Court Hotel features guestrooms with flat-screen TVs and iHome sound systems. A yoga mat is also available, as well as premium bath products. Accessible rooms are available upon request. Guests at the Harbor Court will have access to the adjacent YMCA's state-of-the-art workout equipment and indoor pool. Guests can enjoy a meal from the Ozumo Sushi Bar and Robata Grill, also located next door, for delicious Japanese cuisine.
