About Hotel North Beach

Located in the heart of San Franciscos North Beach Little Italy District, Hotel North Beach offers free WiFi in the public areas. Each guest room includes a cable TV. Chinatown is just 5 minutes walk from this hotel. Guest rooms at Hotel North Beach feature a refrigerator, a microwave and a shared bathroom. Rooms also include a work desk. The hotel is within walking distance from a variety of restaurants, bars and clubs. City Lights Bookstore is 2 minutes walk away from this hotel. The Beat Museum is 4 minutes walk away. Fishermans Wharf is 1 mi away and the Golden Gate Bridge is 5 mi from Hotel North Beach.
