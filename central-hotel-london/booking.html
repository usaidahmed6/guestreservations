About Central Hotel

In bustling Camden, Central Hotel offers cozy rooms with free Wi-Fi and is just 5 minutes walk from Kings Cross and St Pancras Railway Stations. The traditional bedrooms feature a TV and hairdryer, and tea/coffee facilities are provided. All rooms have fresh towels and linens, and most have a private modern bathroom. Just a 15-minute walk from The British Museum, Central Hotel is just over a mile from lively Oxford Street. Covent Garden is a 25-minute walk, and Camden High Street is a 20-minute walk away.
