About New Road Hotel

New Road Hotel offers stylish accommodations in a vibrant and trendy area of London, perfectly located 45 minutes from Heathrow Airport on the Elizabeth line. Ideally located, the hotel is close to the Excel Exhibition Center, the City of London, and only three stops from central London. Major venues like Wembley Stadium, London Stadium, the O2 Arena, and the Abba Voyage Arena are easily accessible. Immerse yourself in modern design while dining at Mr. White's Chophouse Restaurant by Marco Pierre White, or unwind at the on-site bar. Guests can enjoy fantastic social areas including a games room, co-working spaces, and a yoga and fitness terrace. Benefit from high-speed WiFi throughout, a 24/7 front desk, and one of the best hotel breakfasts in East London. Plus, Brick Lane, Tower Bridge, and Shoreditch are all within walking distance, making New Road Hotel the perfect base for exploring the city.
