About Big Hostel

Featuring free WiFi and free luggage storage, Big Backpackers Hostel is located in a convenient location within walking distance from the city's attractions in Sydney. The hostel features a large terrace with a BBQ and sun deck, for relaxing on a sunny afternoon or enjoying in the evening. It is a 10-minute walk from Darling Harbor. Big Backpackers Hostel is designed with the convenience of the traveler in mind and offers a shared fully equipped kitchen, lounge area with unlimited free WiFi and laundry facilities, which are all open 24- hours for guest use. The hostel staff are available to assist guests with advice on the city. The rooms at Big Backpackers Hostel are a selection of mixed and female-only 8, 6 and 4-bed share dormitories, plus a large range of private rooms most with private bathrooms. The building is fully air-conditioned throughout and each floor is serviced by both an elevator and stairs. CC TV cameras are throughout the common areas of the building. The hostel also promotes eco-friendly practices, including recycling facilities, a service for donating unwanted clothes to community charities. Central Station is within a 5-minute walk from Big Backpackers Hostel, so guests can arrive from Sydney Airport within 20 minutes'. The rooms at Big Backpackers Hostel are a selection of mixed, male-only and female-only 8, 6 and 2-bed share dormitories
