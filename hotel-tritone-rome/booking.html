About Tritone Hotel

Hotel Tritone is in the heart of historic Rome, 820 feet from Trevi Fountain and 1640 feet from Barberini Metro Station. WiFi throughout is free. An American breakfast buffet is served in the panoramic breakfast room, which overlooks ancient Rome. Rooms at the Tritone Rome Hotel offer a flat-screen satellite TV, air conditioning and a mini-bar. Some feature classic decor, while others a minimalist design. They have carpeted or parquet floors. The Spanish Steps and the Pantheon are less than a 10-minute walk away. The hotel enjoys good public transport links.
