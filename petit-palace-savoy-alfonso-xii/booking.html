About Petit Palace Savoy Alfonso XII

This stylish boutique hotel is located next to Madrids Retiro Park, 820 feet from Puerta de Alcal. Decorated in brown and white, the air-conditioned rooms at Petit Palace Savoy Alfonso XII feature a flat-screen TV, laptop safe and mini-bar. The bathroom comes with a hairdryer and amenities. Delicious breakfast buffet in the breakfast room. There is a 24-hour reception and a tour desk. Free bicycles can be borrowed, and parking is available nearby for an extra charge. The Prado and Reina Sofia Museums are 5 minutes walk away.
