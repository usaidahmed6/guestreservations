About Argentina Residenza Style Hotel

Located in the heart of Rome, Argentina Residenza Style Hotel is just a 5-minute walk from the Pantheon and Piazza Navona square. Set on the first floor of a 17th-century building, this property offers air-conditioned rooms and free WiFi. These elegant, nonsmoking only rooms feature an LCD TV with Sky channels, and a mini-bar. Each has a private bathroom with a hairdryer, bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries. Some rooms have a hot tub while others have a sensory shower. A generous sweet and savory breakfast is served daily, buffet style. Gluten-free option for breakfast as well as Kosher option is available on request. Argentina Style Hotel is 328 feet from Largo di Torre Argentina square. Buses stopping 33 feet away link with the Vatican, Piazza Venezia square and Termini Train Station.
