About A by Adina Sydney

Just a 13-minute walk from The Royal Botanic Gardens and 0.6 miles from Art Gallery of New South Wales, A by Adina Sydney features accommodations in Sydney, with pool with a view, a fitness center, and an indoor pool. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, a 24-hour front desk, and an elevator, along with free Wifi throughout the property. The property is 500 yards from the city center and a 11-minute walk from Hyde Park Barracks Museum. The condo hotel will provide guests with air-conditioned units offering a desk, a coffee machine, a microwave, a toaster, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom with a walk-in shower. The rooms are fitted with a kettle, while selected rooms will provide you with a fully equipped kitchen with a dishwasher and a stovetop. At the condo hotel, the units are fitted with bed linen and towels. Guests at the condo hotel can enjoy an American breakfast, and breakfast in the room is also available. There is an on-site bar. Popular points of interest near A by Adina Sydney include Harbour Bridge, Sydney International Convention Centre, and Circular Quay. The nearest airport is Sydney Kingsford Smith Airport, 8.1 miles from the accommodation.
