About The Hoxton, Shepherds Bush

"

Discover The Hoxton, Shepherds Bush: A Smart Choice for Savvy Travelers

Set in the vibrant heart of London, The Hoxton, Shepherds Bush offers a refreshingly straightforward hotel experience that caters to those who value practicality and convenient location. Whether in town for business or leisure, this 4-star establishment ensures a comfortable stay without the unnecessary frills.

Located just a stone's throw from notable attractions like the Eventim Apollo and Olympia Exhibition Centre, and a quick journey to the Natural History Museum and the bustling Portobello Road Market, The Hoxton, Shepherds Bush positions guests perfectly to explore the rich tapestry of London. Plus, with easy access to public transport and major tourist sites, navigating the city is a breeze.

  • Proximity to major attractions and public transport hubs
  • Diverse dining options with a restaurant and bar on-site
  • Comfortable, air-conditioned rooms equipped with essential modern amenities
  • Helpful, multilingual staff available 24/7 at the front desk
  • Terrace area to relax after a bustling day in the city

Each room at The Hoxton, Shepherds Bush is designed with the guest's comfort in mind, featuring minimalistic yet functional furnishings, ensuring a restful stay. From solo travelers needing a snug spot to rest, to families requiring a bit more space, the hotel offers a variety of room types to suit all needs.

Experience a blend of convenience and comfort at The Hoxton, Shepherds Bush, where the focus is on delivering a seamless stay, letting you concentrate on what really matters making the most of your London adventure.
