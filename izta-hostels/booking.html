About Izta Hostels

Located within 1.5 miles of The Angel of Independence and 1.5 miles of Chapultepec Castle, Izta Hostels has rooms in Mexico City. The property is around 2 miles from United States Embassy, 2.3 miles from National Museum of Anthropology and 3 miles from Chapultepec Forest. The hotel also provides free WiFi as well as a paid airport shuttle service. At the hotel, each room includes a closet. At Izta Hostels all rooms have bed linen and towels. Around-the-clock assistance is available at the reception, where staff speak English and Spanish. Museo de Arte Popular is 3.1 miles from the accommodation, while Museo de Memoria y Tolerancia is 3.2 miles away. The nearest airport is Benito Juarez International Airport, 8.7 miles from Izta Hostels.
