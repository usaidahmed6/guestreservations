About Preciados

The Hotel Preciados is located in the heart of Madrid, 5 minutes’ walk from the Puerta del Sol. It offers modern services, including free Wi-Fi access and free mini-bar in rooms. The hotel is housed in a restored 19th-century building. One floor is completely nonsmoking. It also offers well-equipped meeting rooms, a restaurant and a cocktail lounge. Bedrooms at the Preciados are tastefully decorated, and most have a balcony. There is also satellite TV and a pillow menu. The hotel is a 5-minute walk from the Plaza Mayor and the Royal Palace. It also has excellent public transport connections, with 2 metro stations within 246 feet.
