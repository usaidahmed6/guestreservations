About City Express Junior by Marriott Cancun

Located opposite Plaza Las Amricas, this modern hotel has air-conditioned rooms. It offers a free continental breakfast, free Wi-Fi and free private parking. All rooms at City Express Junior Cancn feature a modern dcor. Each includes cable TV and a private bathroom with shower. There is a snack bar and guests will find several restaurants and shops in Plaza Las Amricas mall across the road. Staff are happy to help arrange excursions and tours to nearby sites and attractions, including Xcaret Ecological Park, Isla Mujeres and the Tulum archaeological site. Playa del Carmen is less than 1 hours drive away. Cancn International Airport is 9.9 mi from City Express Junior Cancn.
