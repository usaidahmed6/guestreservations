About Hotel Franklin Feel The Sound

Set in Rome's elegant Prati district, between the Vatican walls and Piazza Mazzini, Hotel Franklin Feel The Sound is a charming hotel. The rooms come with free Wi-Fi, Bang & Olufsen sound systems, and LCD TVs. A sweet and savory breakfast buffet is available daily. Weather permitting, you can enjoy it in the garden with furnished patio. Free bicycle rentals are available, and the staff can assist you with tourist and travel info, and reservations at restaurants and events. The Franklin Feel the Sound hotel is a 10-minute walk from Ottaviano Metro Station on line A, the same of the Spanish Steps and Termini Train Station.
