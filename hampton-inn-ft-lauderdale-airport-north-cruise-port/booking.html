About Hampton Inn By Hilton Fort Lauderdale Airport North

Located a 6-minute drive from Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport and an 11-minute drive from the beach, this hotel provides airport and cruise port transportation services for an additional charge. Free Wi-Fi is available. A microwave, a refrigerator, and coffee-making facilities are included in each room at the Hampton Inn Fort Lauderdale Airport North. A work desk and ironing facilities are provided. A daily continental breakfast is included at the Fort Lauderdale Airport Hampton Inn. A fitness center and a business center are available on site for added guest convenience. Port Everglades is 4 miles away. Guests are a 14-minute drive from Las Olas Marina.
