About The First Musica

Located in Rome and with Castel Sant'Angelo reachable within a 13-minute walk, The First Musica has concierge services, non-smoking rooms, a terrace, free WiFi throughout the property and a bar. This 5-star hotel offers room service and luggage storage space. Private parking can be arranged at an extra charge. The rooms at the hotel come with air conditioning, a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a safety deposit box and a private bathroom with a bidet, free toiletries and a hairdryer. The First Musica provides certain accommodations with city views, and rooms include a coffee machine. At the accommodation the rooms include bed linen and towels. The daily breakfast offers à la carte, continental or American options. At The First Musica you'll find a restaurant serving Italian, Pizza and Grill/Bbq cuisine. Vegetarian, dairy-free and gluten-free options can also be requested. Speaking Arabic, English, Spanish and French, staff will be happy to provide guests with practical advice on the area at the reception. Popular points of interest near the hotel include Piazza Navona, Via Condotti and Piazza di Spagna. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino, 10 miles from The First Musica, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
