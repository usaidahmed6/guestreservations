About Donna Laura Palace

Overlooking the Tiber River, Donna Laura Palace by OMNIA hotels is a 10-minute walk from Lepanto Metro Station. It features a fitness center, a sauna and hydromassage tub area, and a bar. Wi-Fi is free throughout. Rooms are decorated in a classic style. Each is air conditioned and offers a flat-screen TV, mini-bar and private bathroom with hairdryer. Some also have a balcony with views of the Tiber River. The restaurant specializes in traditional local and Italian cuisine. During summer, meals are served on the rooftop terrace with city views. Donna Laura is located in Rome’s elegant Prati district. It is 0.6 mi from Piazza del Popolo and 1.2 mi from the Vatican.
