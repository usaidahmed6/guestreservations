About The Wesley

Located next to Londons Euston Station, within a 15-minute walk from The British Museum, Regents Park and Kings Cross Station, The Wesley Euston features modern rooms with free WiFi access and a sustainable international fusion bar and restaurant, The Savannah, opened in summer 2016. Each room is decorated in warm colors and features private bathroom facilities, a flat-screen TV and air conditioning. Room service is provided, as well as a concierge service. The Wesley Euston features 100 guest rooms and 15 fully-equipped conference rooms. The reception is staffed 24 hours. The ethical, eco-friendly Savannah bar and restaurant combines European food with the flavors of Africa and Asia, alongside popular classic dishes. The bar has its own menu of snacks, with a wine list including biodynamic and organic wines, and several artisanal beers. The hotel is within a 2-minute walk from the nearest tube and bus connections, providing easy access to any part of the city. Regents Place is just a 4-minute walk from the hotel while the Oxford Street Shopping Area is 1 mile away.
