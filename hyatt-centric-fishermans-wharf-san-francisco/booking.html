About Hyatt Centric Fishermans Wharf

Just 3 minutes walk from Fisherman's Wharf, the Hyatt features an on-site restaurant and lounge. A heated outdoor pool and hot tub are offered. Free WiFi and a 47 HDTV are featured in all rooms. The Wardroom features an extensive breakfast menu with a la carte options. Modern, yet rustic decor is featured at Brick & Beam; which serves up cocktails in lounge and bar. Snacks, drinks and toiletries are available at the 24-hour Marketplace daily. Styled in modern dcor, a Bluetooth capable media hub and an iPod docking station are featured in all rooms. Spa toiletries are included. A mini refrigerator, a coffee maker and laptop sized safe are also provided in each room at Hyatt Fisherman's Wharf. A 24-hour front desk is just one of the many convenient amenities offered at Hyatt Fisherman's Wharf San Francisco. Other services include dry cleaning, currency exchange and concierge. A cash machine is located on site. Historic street car trollies and the famed cable cars are within 2 minutes walk from Hyatt . Sweet treats are available at Ghirardelli Square 5 minutes walk. PIER 39 is 10 minutes walk away. Golden Gate Bridge is 4 miles away. Other nearby attractions include Alcatraz, North Beach and Union Square.
