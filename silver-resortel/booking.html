About Silver Resortel

Just a 2-minute walk from Patong Beach and just a few minutes away from Jungceylon Shopping Center it offers air-conditioned rooms with free Wi-Fi and Free Beach towel every day. All nonsmoking rooms are simply furnished and come with Free Wi-Fi, cable TV, mini-bar and safety box. The private bathroom comes with a shower. We have a pharmacy shop and currency exchange shop service in front of the hotel every day.

Amenities

  • Room Service
  • Beachfront
  • 24 hour front desk
  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • ATM Machine
  • Currency Exchange
