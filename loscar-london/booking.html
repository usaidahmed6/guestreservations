About L'oscar London

L’oscar London is a 39-bedroom hotel set within the former Headquarters of the Baptist Church. Set near Covent Garden, L'oscar London is in an location for experiencing the city. Guests can enjoy access to a range of boutiques, restaurants and cultural hot spots including Theatreland, the British Museum and the Royal Opera House. The rooms come with a flat-screen TV, a desk, a seating area and a private bathroom. All rooms have air conditioning and complimentary WiFi access. A la carte and Full English breakfast options are available each morning at L'oscar London. Royal Opera House is 1969 feet from the accommodations, while Lyceum Theater is 2297 feet away. The nearest airport is London City Airport, 7.5 mi from the property.
