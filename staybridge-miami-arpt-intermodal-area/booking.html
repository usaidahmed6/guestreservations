About Staybridge Suites Miami International Airport

Comfort and Convenience at Staybridge Suites Miami International Airport

Staybridge Suites Miami International Airport offers a welcoming and convenient stay just 1.2 miles from Miami International Airport. This hotel features air-conditioned rooms with modern amenities, including a full kitchen, dining area, and seating area with a flat-screen TV. Guests can relax and enjoy the outdoor swimming pool, fitness center, and barbecue facilities.

Start your day with a continental breakfast, and unwind at the evening reception with complimentary light fare, beer, and wine, available Monday through Wednesday. The hotel also offers an onsite bar and juice bar for added convenience. With a 24-hour front desk, business center, and laundry services, guests have everything they need for a comfortable stay.

  • Outdoor swimming pool
  • Fitness center and barbecue facilities
  • Complimentary airport shuttle service
  • Evening reception with complimentary refreshments
  • Full kitchens in all rooms

Staybridge Suites Miami International Airport provides easy access to nearby attractions, with the Miami Intermodal Center just 1476 feet away. The hotel is located 2.7 miles from Marlins Park and 3.7 miles from Miracle Mile Miami. Experience comfort and convenience at Staybridge Suites Miami International Airport.
