About The Orchid Hotel Kalim Bay

Orchid Hotel and Spa is located on Phuket’s Kalim Bay, a 5-minute walk from central Patong. Overlooking the Andaman Sea, this hotel features a restaurant, outdoor swimming pool and free internet. Orchid Hotel is 1700 ft from Patong Beach and half a mile from the lively Bang-La Road. The hotel offers free parking and is 20 miles from Phuket Airport. Spacious rooms at Orchid come with modern interiors and a private balcony that offer plenty of natural light. All rooms come with a minibar and private bathroom with shower facilities. Guests can enjoy a massage at the spa, or have a relaxing barbecue by the pool. With a 24-hour front desk, the hotel provides currency exchange and airport shuttle services. The restaurant serves a variety of local and international dishes. Refreshing beverages are also available at the bar.
