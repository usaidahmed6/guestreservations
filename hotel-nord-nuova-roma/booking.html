About Hotel Nord Nuova Roma

Nord Nuova Roma is a classic 1930s-style hotel offering soundproofed rooms with satellite TV and free WiFi. It is a 5-minute walk from Roma Termini Train Station. Guests at Hotel Nord can have an aperitif on the hotel's spacious sun terrace with views of central Rome, which is open all summer. There is a fully equipped gym on the 6th floor, and a meeting room is also available. Rooms are air conditioned and feature a fully equipped marble bathroom with bath or shower. Breakfast can be enjoyed in the room or enjoyed in the dining room. The buffet includes eggs, cold meats, yogurt and fruit. Snacks and light meals are on offer at the bar every afternoon and evening, and dinner is available at a partner restaurant located 328 feet away. The hotel is just 656 feet from Rome's Opera House, in an area hosting numerous restaurants and bars. The Trevi Fountain and the Spanish Steps are both a 15-minute walk away.
