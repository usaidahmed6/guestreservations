About Hotel Mayfair

Hotel Mayfair is located in Vesterbro, a 5 minute walk from Tivoli Gardens and Copenhagen Central Station. All rooms have free wifi and Chromecast. Our rooms are bright, individually decorated and have a private bathroom with shower. General facilities include a reception bar, a lounge, a business center and a 24/7 fitness room. Our breakfast buffet can be enjoyed in the courtyard during the summer. When booking more than 5 rooms or 5 room nights, different policies and additional supplements may apply. Guests are recommended to inform about late check-in if arriving around midnight or later.
