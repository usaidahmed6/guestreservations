About Aparthotel Adagio Nice Promenade des Anglais

Aparthotel Adagio Nice Promenade des Anglais is located on the Promenade des Anglais in Nice. This beach-front aparthotel offers air-conditioned and eco-friendly accommodations with sea or city views and free WiFi access. The soundproof accommodations has a satellite TV, a private bathroom, a private balcony or terrace, and a kitchenette equipped with a microwave, stovetop and dishwasher. The apartments also have a seating area. A buffet breakfast is served everyday. Aparthotel Adagio Nice Promenade des Anglais also provides a 24-hour reception with a tour desk and a currency exchange service. Aparthotel Adagio Nice Promenade des Anglais is a 10-minute drive from Nice-Ville Train Station and the Old Town. Nice-Cote d’Azur Airport is a just 3.1 mi away.
