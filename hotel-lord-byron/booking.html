About Hotel Lord Byron - Small Luxury Hotels Of The World

Hotel Lord Byron is in Rome’s classy and elegant Parioli district, just behind the Borghese Gardens. This exclusive hotel offers calm, quiet surroundings, excellent service and beautiful interiors. One of the Small Luxury Hotels of the World, the hotel is in one of the charming town houses common to this part of Rome. Admire stylish, Art Deco furnishings and enjoy the luxurious rooms and lounges. Via Veneto and the Spanish Steps are a 15 minute walk from Hotel Lord Byron. Villa Borghese’s attractions, from the zoo to the famous art gallery are even closer. After a day out discovering Rome, spend an evening at peaceful Hotel Lord Byron. Enjoy a few drinks at Il Salotto, the hotel’s lounge bar before moving on to the hotel restaurant. Enjoy award-winning regional cuisine accompanied by excellent Italian and foreign wines.
