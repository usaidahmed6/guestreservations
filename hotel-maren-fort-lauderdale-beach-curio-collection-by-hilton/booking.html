About Hotel Maren Fort Lauderdale Beach, Curio Collection By Hilton

Hotel Maren Fort Lauderdale Beach, Curio Collection By Hilton: Seaside Splendor

Hotel Maren Fort Lauderdale Beach, part of the esteemed Curio Collection by Hilton, offers a breathtaking seaside retreat in the heart of Fort Lauderdale. Located just steps from the International Swimming Hall of Fame, this hotel blends modern luxury with a prime coastal location.

Key Features:

  • Stunning Sea Views: Each room provides a picturesque view of the sea, immersing guests in the beauty of Fort Lauderdale's beaches.
  • Convenient Location: Situated 1.4 miles from Bonnet House Museum and Gardens, and close to The Galleria and Broward Convention Center. The hotel's prime location allows easy access to the city's top attractions.
  • Dining and Bar: Boasts an on-site restaurant and bar, offering a range of culinary experiences with splendid sea views.
  • 24-Hour Front Desk and Room Service: Round-the-clock assistance and in-room dining options ensure a comfortable and convenient stay.
  • Proximity to Key Landmarks: Near City of Fort Lauderdale Las Olas Marina, Bahia Mar Shopping Center, and the Jungle Queen Riverboat, perfect for exploring local culture and entertainment.

Additional Information:

  • The hotel is an ideal choice for those seeking a blend of luxury and convenience, with easy access to Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport, located just 4.3 miles away.
  • Designed for both leisure and business travelers, Hotel Maren Fort Lauderdale Beach stands out with its elegant architecture, exceptional service, and prime beachfront location, making it a distinguished choice in the Curio Collection by Hilton.
