About Queensway Hotel, Sure Hotel Collection by Best Western

Located in elegant Paddington, Queensway Hotel, Sure Hotel Collection by Best Western is just 984 feet from Paddington Railway Station. The traditional hotel has a 24-hour front desk and spacious rooms with free Wi-Fi. The rooms each have a private bathroom with a hairdryer. All rooms have fresh, modern decor, and each features a work desk, personal safe and tea/coffee facilities. A generous continental breakfast buffet is served daily in the bright and airy dining room. Breakfast options include fresh coffee and a selection of cereals and fruit juices. Just 1640 feet from Hyde Park, Queensway Hotel, Sure Hotel Collection by Best Western is a 15-minute walk from lively Oxford Street. The glamorous shops of Knightsbridge are a 25-minute walk, and Buckingham Place is 2 miles away.
