About Madison Hotel

Madison Hotel is directly opposite Rome Termini Station for metro and bus services across the city. There is generous daily buffet breakfast of sweet and savory items, and staff are available 24 hours a day. All air conditioned, rooms here have a flat-screen TV and a mini-bar, which is available for free in summer months. All have private bathrooms and some include satellite channels. Hotel Madison is well located for visiting Rome's historic sites, with the Spanish Steps and Trevi Fountain around 15 minutes' walk away. Trains and shuttle busses to Rome Fiumicino Airport depart from Termini.

Amenities

  • Room Service
  • 24 hour front desk
  • Masks are available to guests
  • Contactless check-in and check-out are available
