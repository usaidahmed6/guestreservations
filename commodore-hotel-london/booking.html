About Commodore Hotel

Newly Refurbished Victorian townhouse hotel located in quiet Victorian Square, just steps from Hyde Park and ten minutes by bus to Oxford Street and Marble Arch. WiFi is available in the hotel and is free of charge. Notting Hill and Portobello Market are 5 minutes away. Lancaster Gate Station and the Heathrow Express at Paddington are all within walking distance of Commodore Hotel.

Amenities

  • Room Service
  • 24 hour front desk
  • Fitness Center
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • Non-Smoking Facility
