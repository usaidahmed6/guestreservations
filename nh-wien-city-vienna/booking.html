About NH Wien City

The NH Wien City offers air-conditioned rooms directly on Mariahilfer Straße, Vienna’s biggest shopping street. An underground station is in the same building complex. Free WiFi is available throughout the hotel. The spacious rooms offer a work desk and a flat-screen TV. Most have a separate living and sleeping area (sliding door) and some come with a kitchenette. A rich breakfast buffet is served at the hotel in the morning. There is an open bar next to the reception offering drinks and snacks 24 hours a day. A sauna, exercise equipment, and a relaxation room can be found in the spa center. The NH Wien City also offers a private parking garage (at a charge). The Spittelberg, a scenic quarter with many restaurants and bars, is very close to the NH Wien City. The Hofburg Imperial Palace and the Museum Quarter are only a short walk away. The Neubaugasse Underground Station (line U3) in the same building complex offers direct access to the public transport network. St. Stephen’s Cathedral in the city center can be reached within a 5-minute underground ride.
