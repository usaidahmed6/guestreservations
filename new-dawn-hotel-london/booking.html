About New Dawn Hotel

This small, family-run hotel in Queensway is next to Whiteleys Shopping Center and just 5 minutes' walk from Hyde Park. There are 2 tube stations within 1640 feet. The hotel also offers up to 50% off theater tickets, and can book tours for Madam Tussads, London Eye, Big Bus and the London Zoo. All rooms at The New Dawn offer bright, comfortable accommodations and have a TV, telephone and a private bathroom. Guests can prepare for a day exploring central London with a continental breakfast, served every morning on the dining room. Kensington Palace is just 10 minutes' walk from the New Dawn, and guests are less than a mile from the shops and cafes of Notting Hill. The West End is only a few stops away on the Tube, while the Heathrow Express Service at Paddington Station is 0.5 miles away.
