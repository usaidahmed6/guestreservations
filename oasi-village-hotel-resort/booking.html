About Oasi Village Hotel

Just 656 feet from Milan’s Piola Metro on the Green Line, the Oasi Village is a modern hotel with a bar and free Wi-Fi throughout. It offers air-conditioned rooms with a satellite TV. Rooms at the Oasi Village Hotel & Resort are decorated in a minimalist, contemporary style. Each comes with a private bathroom with a hairdryer and toiletry set. A daily buffet breakfast is provided. The area has many cafés and restaurants serving Lombard cuisine and pizza. The shopping street of Corso Buenos Aires is 2625 feet from the property. Loreto M1 metro stop offers fast links to Rho Fiera Exhibition Center and is just 2953 feet away. Milan Central Station is a 5-minute metro ride away.
