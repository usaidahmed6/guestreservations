About Hotel Romana Residence

Conveniently located in the center of Milan, Palazzo Porta Romana Hotel provides air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, private parking and room service. This 4-star hotel offers luggage storage space. The property is non-smoking throughout and is located a 9-minute walk from Palazzo Reale. Guest rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, minibar, an electric tea pot, a bidet, a hairdryer and a desk. The hotel has certain rooms that include city views, and each room is equipped with a private bathroom with a bath. All rooms have a safety deposit box. Guests at Palazzo Porta Romana Hotel can enjoy a continental or an Italian breakfast. Staff at the 24-hour front desk speak German, English, Spanish and French. Popular points of interest near the accommodation include Museo Del Novecento, Porta Romana Metro Station and San Babila Station. The nearest airport is Milan Linate Airport, 4.3 miles from Palazzo Porta Romana Hotel.
