About Hotel Domidea

The Domidea is a modern 4-star hotel featuring design rooms with free WiFi and a flat-screen TV. The bus to Rebibbia Metro Station stops 164 feet away. Rooms at Hotel Domidea have minimalist dcor and free cinema, sport, and satellite channels. Some rooms include a shuttle service to the metro station. A continental buffet breakfast is available each morning. A small unguarded free car park is available on site. Tor Sapienza Station, with services to Termini, is 1476 feet away.
