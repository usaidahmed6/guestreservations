About Quality Inn Sunshine Haberfield

Located in Sydney, 4.7 miles from Sydney Central Station, Quality Inn Sunshine Haberfield provides accommodations with free WiFi and free private parking. The property is around 4.8 miles from Australian National Maritime Museum, 4.9 miles from The Star Event Centre and 4.9 miles from Sydney International Convention Centre. The hotel features family rooms. The hotel will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms offering a desk, an electric tea pot, a fridge, a minibar, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a shower. At Quality Inn Sunshine Haberfield every room comes with bed linen and towels. Hyde Park Barracks Museum is 5.5 miles from the accommodation, while Bicentennial Park is 5.6 miles away. The nearest airport is Sydney Kingsford Smith Airport, 6.8 miles from Quality Inn Sunshine Haberfield.
