About Hotel Marc Aurel

Located 984 feet from the Schwedenplatz Underground Station, Hotel Marc Aurel - Vienna City Center is decorated in a Mediterranean style and offers free WiFi access. Garage parking is provided, and St. Stephen's Cathedral is a 7-minute walk away. All rooms at Hotel Marc Aurel - Vienna City Center feature air conditioning, a TV, and a hairdryer. A stop of the airport bus is just a few yards away. Sights such as the State Opera can be reached in a short walk.
