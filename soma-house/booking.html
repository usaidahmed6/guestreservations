About SoMa House

Located in San Franciscos vibrant SoMa district, renowned for its museums, shops, and gourmet restaurants, SoMa House seamlessly blends retro-style furnishings with state-of-the-art amenities and services. Civic Center Plaza is just a 9-minute walk away. Guests can enjoy complimentary coffee and tea each morning in the hotels trendy lobby. Guests can unwind at the outdoor swimming pool or stay active in the fitness center. Each room includes complimentary Wi-Fi, a smart TV, and an iPod docking station. Guest robes, a coffee machine, and an in-room safe are also provided for your convenience. Valet Parking is available on site with a surcharge, based on availability on a first-come, first-served basis. Please note that an additional fee applies for oversize vehicles. For more information, kindly contact the Front Desk. Explore nearby attractions with easevisit the War Memorial Opera House, just a 9-minute drive away, or enjoy a show at the SHN Orpheum Theater, only a 6-minute walk away. The Civic Center/UN Plaza BART Station, offering service to Union Square and San Francisco International Airport, is a mere 4-minute walk from the hotel.
