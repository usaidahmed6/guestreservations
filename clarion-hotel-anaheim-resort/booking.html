About Clarion Hotel Anaheim Resort

Welcome to Clarion Hotel Anaheim Resort: A Gateway to Disneyland Adventures

Step into the heart of Anaheim with a stay at Clarion Hotel Anaheim Resort, a beacon for travelers seeking comfort and convenience just a stone's throw from Disneyland. This well-positioned hotel serves as the perfect base for exploring the wonders of California, offering more than just a place to rest.

  • Heated outdoor pool for year-round enjoyment
  • On-site Palm Tree Caf and Cantina Lounge for diverse dining experiences
  • Studios equipped with microwaves and sofa beds, perfect for families
  • Seasonal cabana bar offering refreshing drinks by the pool
  • Convenient access to Anaheim Convention Center, just a 4-minute walk away

Each guest room at Clarion Hotel Anaheim Resort comes equipped with essential amenities to enhance your stay, including free Wi-Fi for staying connected, cable TV with HBO for entertainment, and refrigerators and coffee makers for in-room convenience. The addition of sitting areas in each room provides that extra space to relax and unwind.

For those looking to mix leisure with pleasure, the hotel boasts a fitness center, game room, and heated outdoor pool, ensuring fun and relaxation are always within reach. Business travelers will appreciate the on-site business center and meeting facilities, making it easy to stay productive.

Located just one block from the magical world of Disneyland and a short walk from the Anaheim Convention Center, Clarion Hotel Anaheim Resort offers an unbeatable location. Whether visiting for the enchanting theme parks, attending a conference, or exploring the local attractions like the Angel Stadium of Anaheim, guests are in for a memorable stay.

With a focus on convenience, comfort, and accessibility, Clarion Hotel Anaheim Resort invites guests to experience the best of Anaheim in a welcoming and relaxed atmosphere.
