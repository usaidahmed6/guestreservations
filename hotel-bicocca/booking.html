About Hotel Bicocca

Just 820 feet from Istria Station on Milan's M5 metro line, Hotel Bicocca is in the new Bicocca district. It has free parking, which is subject to availability and free Wi-Fi. The hotel has a garden and features air conditioned rooms with private bathrooms. The property is located in a quiet and residential area which is not far from Milan city center.
