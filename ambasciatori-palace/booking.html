About Intercontinental Rome Ambasciatori Palace

A 10-minute walk from the Trevi Fountain, the Ambasciatori is located on Rome's chic Via Veneto. The property is set in an early 20th-century building with luxurious rooms and free Wi-Fi. Elegant and all air-conditioned, rooms at the Ambasciatori Palace Hotel come with precious fabrics and either carpeted or parquet floors. Overlooking Via Veneto, the hotel offers an array of amenities for guests to enjoy. From the panoramic rooftop bar Charlies to the restaurant Scarpetta NYC, where the culinary traditions of Italy and New York City converge, and the inviting lounge & bar, Anita, there's something for every palateoffers drinks. Homemade cakes, pastries and Italian coffee are included in the buffet breakfast every morning. Only 1148 feet from the hotel, the Barberini-Fontana di Trevi Metro offers direct links to the Vatican Museums, Spanish Steps and Termini Station. The Villa Borghese park is a 5-minute walk away.
