About Grosvenor House Suites

Grosvenor House Suites offers 5-star accommodations in the heart of Mayfair, with a 24-hour concierge service, a daily maid service, and a 24-hour fitness room. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout. The ultramodern sophistication of clean lines and slate gray dcor accentuate its superb location on Park Lane. Overlooking Hyde Park, Grosvenor House Suites by are within minutes walk of boutique shops and exclusive galleries. Grosvenor House Suites have a residents-only dining area in its impressive atrium, but also offers a 24-hour room service to guest rooms, in-room spa and beauty treatments and private dining options. The property offers a children's menu in the Atrium and this is also available as room service. Selfridges on Oxford Street is within a 10-minute walk, as are Hyde Park Corner, Marble Arch and Bond Street Tube Stations. The famous Hyde Park provides guests with a chance to escape from the hustle and bustle of the city streets. The exclusive boutiques of Mount Street are less than 5 minutes' walk away. Heathrow Airport is 16 miles from the suites, with connections to the Center via the Underground.
