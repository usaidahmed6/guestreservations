About Holiday Inn San Francisco - Golden Gateway

Welcome to Holiday Inn San Francisco - Golden Gateway: A Gateway to the City's Charms

Discover the heart of San Francisco from the conveniently located Holiday Inn San Francisco - Golden Gateway. Nestled in the serene Nob Hill neighborhood, this hotel presents an ideal starting point for exploring the vibrant city. With proximity to iconic landmarks such as the Golden Gate Bridge and Union Square, guests find themselves perfectly placed for both leisure and adventure. Embrace the unique combination of comfort and convenience, alongside the captivating views of the city or bay that each guest room affords.

Here, every stay is enhanced by thoughtfully curated amenities designed to fulfill the needs of modern travelers. From on-site dining options that cater to a variety of tastes to a 24-hour fitness center ensuring health routines remain uninterrupted, the Holiday Inn San Francisco - Golden Gateway ensures a memorable visit. The outdoor swimming pool offers a refreshing respite, while the legendary San Francisco cable car system, just a short stroll away, unlocks the rest of the city's treasures.

  • Strategic location in Nob Hill with easy access to the Golden Gate Bridge and Union Square
  • Guest rooms boasting captivating city or bay views
  • On-site dining with diverse culinary options
  • 24-hour fitness center for round-the-clock workouts
  • Outdoor swimming pool for a rejuvenating dip

Whether it's the allure of Fisherman's Wharf, the shopping spree at Union Square, or the cultural journey through Chinatown, the Holiday Inn San Francisco - Golden Gateway positions you just minutes away from the city's most sought-after destinations. Discover San Francisco from a place where convenience meets comfort, and every stay promises a new adventure.
