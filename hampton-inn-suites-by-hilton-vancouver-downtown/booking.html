About Hampton Inn And Suites By Hilton Downtown Vancouver

Featuring a hot tub, his Vancouver hotel is located on Robson Street across from the BC Place Stadium. An on-site restaurant is available. Free WiFi is provided throughout the property. A flat-screen TV and pay-per-view movies are included in every room at the Hampton Inn & Suites Vancouver Downtown. A coffee maker, a refrigerator and a microwave are available as well. The rooms also include a safety deposit box. Guests can also enjoy the convenience of laundry facilities and a 24-hour front desk. Rogers Arena is 9 minutes' walk and the Queen Elizabeth Theater is 5 minutes' walk from Hampton Inn & Suites Robson Street.
