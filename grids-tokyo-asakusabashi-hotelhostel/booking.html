About Grids Tokyo Asakusa-bashi Hotel＆Hostel

Offering air-conditioned rooms in the Taito district of Tokyo, Grids Tokyo Asakusabashi Hotel＆Hostel is a 14-minute walk from Edo Tokyo Museum. This 2-star hostel offers a shared kitchen and a shared lounge. At the hostel, rooms at Grids Tokyo Asakusabashi also have free WiFi. Private guest rooms in the accommodations are fitted with a TV, hairdryer, shower and toilet. Dormitory rooms have shared bathrooms. Towels and bath amenities are available for an additional fee. A Continental breakfast is served daily at the property. At Grids Tokyo Asakusabashi Hotel＆Hostel you will find a restaurant serving cuisine. Sensoji Temple is 1.4 mi from the hostel.
