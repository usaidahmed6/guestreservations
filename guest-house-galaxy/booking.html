About Guest House Galaxy

The Galaxy Guest House is located a few yards from the Roma Termini railway station. The property offers large air-conditioned rooms with free Wi-Fi. The multilingual staff is available 24 hours a day, to help you make the most of your Roman experience, with information and bookings for trips, rentals or transfers. All rooms are nonsmoking and soundproofed. The free continental breakfast is served in the elegant breakfast room. On request available buffet breakfast, vegan or gluten free. Being so close to Termini, you will have bus and metro links to the city, as well as connections to the 2 airports of Rome, Fiumicino and Ciampino.
