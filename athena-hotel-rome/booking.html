About Albergo Athena

A short walk away from Rome La Sapienza University and 10 minutes on foot from Policlinico Umberto I hospital, Albergo Athena features functional rooms with attached bathroom with air conditioning and a fridge. It offers free Wi-Fi and a daily buffet breakfast. The family-run Athena hotel is right by Viale Ippocrate, 10 minutes' walk from Bologna and Policlinico metro stops, and from Roma Tiburtina Train Station. Buses to Roma Termini Station run regularly. Upon check-out you can leave your luggage in the Athena's storage room and pick it up later in the day. Parking is available at a garage in the same building, at extra charge.
