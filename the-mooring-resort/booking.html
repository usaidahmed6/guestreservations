About The Mooring Resort

Located in Panwa Beach, a 4-minute walk from Ao Yon Beach, The Mooring Resort has accommodations with an outdoor swimming pool, free private parking, a garden and a terrace. Offering water sports facilities, the property is located within 1.3 miles of Panwa Beach. The resort also provides free WiFi as well as a paid airport shuttle service. The resort will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms offering a desk, an electric tea pot, a fridge, a minibar, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a shower. At The Mooring Resort all rooms include bed linen and towels. The area is popular for snorkeling and canoeing, and bike rental is available at this 4-star resort. Phuket Aquarium is 1.3 miles from the accommodation, while Chinpracha House is 6.5 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Phuket International Airport, 25 miles from The Mooring Resort.
