About Lombard Plaza Motel

This motel is in San Francisco's Marina District, minutes from Fisherman's Wharf and other attractions. The motel has free on-site parking. Lombard Plaza Motel is near the Golden Gate Bridge and Alcatraz Island. The shops and restaurants of Chestnut Street are also close by. The spacious guest rooms at the Lombard Plaza Motel include satellite TV. In-room mini-refrigerators and microwaves are also included.
