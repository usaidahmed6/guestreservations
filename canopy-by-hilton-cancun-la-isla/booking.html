About Canopy by Hilton Cancun La Isla

Located in Cancún, a 7-minute walk from Playa Marlin, Canopy By Hilton Cancun La Isla has accommodations with free bikes, free private parking, an outdoor swimming pool and a fitness center. Featuring a terrace, the 4-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi. The property provides room service, a 24-hour front desk and organizing tours for guests. At the hotel, the rooms have a desk. All rooms at Canopy By Hilton Cancun La Isla come with a seating area and a TV with cable channels. A buffet, à la carte or continental breakfast is served at the property. At the accommodation you'll find a restaurant serving American, Caribbean and Mexican cuisine. Vegetarian, vegan and gluten-free options can also be requested. Canopy By Hilton Cancun La Isla features amenities such as an on-site business center and hot tub. Popular points of interest near the hotel include El Rey Beach, La Isla Shopping Mall and Backstage Cancun. The nearest airport is Cancún International Airport, 11 miles from Canopy By Hilton Cancun La Isla.
