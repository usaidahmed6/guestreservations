About Best Western Ars Hotel

This property offers a Special Protection Program, a strict program of precise safeguards dedicated to our guests and our staff. Housed in a beautiful historic villa, Ars Hotel is located in Rome's Monte Sacro district. In the surroundings there are many shops, restaurants and public parks. Nearby, you can catch bus 90 Express and be at Termini Railway Station in approximately 20 minutes. The Conca D'Oro Metro Station of the new B1 line is about 984 feet away. Ars Hotel offers a gym, a lounge and a panoramic terrace overlooking the quiet surroundings. Guest rooms are bright and spacious. Immersed in green, they are perfect for relaxation.
