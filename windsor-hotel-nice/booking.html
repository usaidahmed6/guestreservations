About Hotel Windsor

This hotel is set in a 19th-century building in central Nice and features a tropical garden with a swimming pool. The hotels fitness and wellness center offers a hammam, a sauna and massage treatments. Each air-conditioned and soundproofed room has been uniquely designed and decorated by an artist. Rooms are equipped with free WiFi, a satellite TV and an private bathroom. The larger rooms have a balcony or a spa bath. Hotel Windsor offers a menu of traditional meals made from organic ingredients. In the morning a buffet breakfast is served in the dining area or in the garden. Guests can enjoy a drink at the lounge bar, and during the winter are able to relax near the fireplace. Hotel Windsor is 1640 feet from the beach and the Promenade des Anglais, and 3117 feet from the Old Town of Nice. Gare de Nice-Ville Train Station is 2625 feet away. Limited parking spaces are available upon request and for an extra charge.
