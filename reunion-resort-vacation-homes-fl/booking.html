About Reunion Resort and Golf Club

Reunion Resort & Club is within 6.2 mi of the Walt Disney World Resort. Features include 11 outdoor pools with a childrens water park. A balcony and free WiFi are provided in all villas. A cable TV, full kitchen, and seating area with a sofa are included in all villas at Reunion Resort & Club. Extras include a washing machine and a tumble dryer. Guests can expect 3 on-site golf courses and a golf academy at this Kissimmee Reunion Resort & Club. A tennis court, fitness center, and concierge are available as well. The 7 on-site restaurants include Eleven steakhouse, Traditions at Nicklaus Clubhouse, and Grand Lobby and Sushi Bar. Full-service dining is also available poolside at The Grill at Water Park. Located 5 minutes drive from Interstate 4 Exit 60, guests will be 20 minutes drive from Aquatica, SeaWolds water park, and the Orlando Premium Outlets. The Universal Studios Resort is 21 mi away.
