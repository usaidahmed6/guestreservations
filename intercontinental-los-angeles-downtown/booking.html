About InterContinental - Los Angeles Downtown

Soar Above the City at InterContinental - Los Angeles Downtown

Elevate your Los Angeles experience with a stay at InterContinental - Los Angeles Downtown, strategically situated in the heart of the city's vibrant downtown district. This premier destination not only offers luxurious accommodations but also boasts an outdoor pool, an on-site restaurant, and complimentary WiFi throughout the property. Start each day with an American breakfast and enjoy the convenience of guest rooms equipped with a TV, coffee machine, and complimentary toiletries. Select rooms enhance your stay with separate showers and bathtubs, along with breathtaking views of Los Angeles.

Ascend to Spire 73, the hotel's rooftop bar, acclaimed as the tallest open-air bar in the Western Hemisphere. Here, guests can sip on expertly crafted cocktails while taking in panoramic views of the Los Angeles skyline. Dining options include Dekkadance for an interactive farm-to-fork experience and La Boucherie, an elegant steakhouse and seafood restaurant with stunning city vistas.

  • Outdoor pool for a refreshing escape amidst the cityscape
  • Spire 73, offering unparalleled views from the tallest open-air bar in the Western Hemisphere
  • On-site dining options including Dekkadance and La Boucherie for exquisite culinary experiences
  • Rooms equipped with modern amenities and select rooms featuring separate showers, bathtubs, and city views
  • Prime location near Microsoft Theater and Staples Center, with Los Angeles International Airport just 11 miles away

Whether in town for business or leisure, InterContinental - Los Angeles Downtown provides an unmatched starting point for exploring all that Los Angeles has to offer. From the highest heights at Spire 73 to the comfort of your well-appointed room, every aspect of your stay is designed with your enjoyment in mind. Discover a blend of luxury and convenience at InterContinental - Los Angeles Downtown, where every visit is a journey to remember.
