About NH Breton

The NH Madrid Chamberí is located in Madrid’s Chamberi district, just 984 feet from Paseo de la Castellana and 1312 feet from the Sorolla Gallery. It offers free WiFi throughout the hotel. All modern, air-conditioned rooms feature a 48-inch TV, a desk, mini-bar and safe. The private bathroom includes a bath with rain-effect shower and a professional hairdryer. The NH Madrid Chamberí is within 984 feet of 3 different Metro stops, giving guests easy access to the airport (30 minutes), the city center (12 minutes) and the IFEMA convention center. The commercial area of Nuevos Ministerios with its shops and restaurants is a 15-minute walk away.
