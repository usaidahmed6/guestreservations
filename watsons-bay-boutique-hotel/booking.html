About Watsons Bay Boutique Hotel

Experience Waterfront Bliss at Watsons Bay Boutique Hotel

Nestled along the pristine shores of Sydney Harbor, Watsons Bay Boutique Hotel offers an unforgettable stay with breathtaking views and top-tier amenities. Just a short walk from iconic landmarks such as Watsons Bay Baths, Camp Cove, and Hornby Lighthouse, this hotel combines convenience with stunning natural beauty. The air-conditioned guest rooms provide a cozy haven, equipped with flat-screen cable TV, tea and coffee making facilities, and private bathrooms complete with complimentary toiletries, hairdryers, and bathtubs. Enjoy a picturesque view of the city skyline and Sydney Harbor, right from the comfort of the hotel.

  • Waterfront location with stunning harbor views
  • Proximity to Watsons Bay Baths, Camp Cove, and Hornby Lighthouse
  • Complimentary parking for all guests
  • Air-conditioned rooms with flat-screen cable TV
  • Easy access to Sydney's central attractions and Circular Quay

Located just 7.5 miles from central Sydney and a 20-minute drive from Circular Quay, Watsons Bay Boutique Hotel is perfectly positioned for exploring the vibrant city. Whether you're looking to unwind by the waterfront or explore Sydney's bustling heart, this hotel offers the best of both worlds. Book your stay today and indulge in the unique charm of Watsons Bay.
