About Austria Trend Hotel Ananas Wien

Located within a 1-minute walk from Pilgramgasse U4-Underground Station, Austria Trend Hotel Ananas Wien is just a 5-minute ride from the State Opera in the center of Vienna. Free WiFi is available, and a private underground car park can be used against a surcharge. The Johann Strauss Café offers characteristic Viennese coffee house culture. The restaurant serves traditional Viennese specialties, national and international dishes, as well as fresh pastries. Rooms at Hotel Ananas Wien combine rococo decorative elements with modern amenities. They are all air-conditioned and offer cable TV, a mini-bar, and a bathroom with hairdryer and make-up mirror. Guests of Trend Hotel Ananas Wien benefit from a souvenir shop and a hair salon. Thanks to its central location, guests can access the Wientalradweg (bicycle trail), right outside the hotel, and the Mariahilfer Straße shopping street, a 10-minute walk away. The popular Naschmarkt, with its many food stalls, can be reached in a 5-minute walk.
