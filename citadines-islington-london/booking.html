About Citadines Islington

1.4 miles from King's Cross St Pancras Tube Station in London, Citadines Islington London offers accommodations with access to a fitness center. This 4-star condo hotel offers full-day security and a business center. The accommodation provides a 24-hour front desk, a shuttle service, an elevator, and free Wifi. The units at the condo hotel come with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a kitchen, a dining area, a safety deposit box, and a private bathroom with a walk-in shower, a hair dryer, and free toiletries. A dishwasher, a microwave, and toaster are also featured, as well as a kettle. At the condo hotel, all units include bed linen and towels. Buffet and continental breakfast options with fresh pastries, fruit, and juice are available daily at the condo hotel. Emirates Stadium is 1.5 miles from Citadines Islington London, while King's Cross Theatre is 1.6 miles away. The nearest airport is London City Airport, 8.7 miles from the accommodation.
