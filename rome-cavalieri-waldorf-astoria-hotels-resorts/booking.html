About Rome Cavalieri, Waldorf Astoria By Hilton Hotels & Resorts

Rome Cavalieri, Waldorf Astoria By Hilton Hotels & Resorts

Nestled within extensive Mediterranean gardens, Rome Cavalieri, Waldorf Astoria By Hilton Hotels & Resorts offers panoramic views of Rome and the Vatican from its scenic hilltop location in Montemario. The hotel features spacious rooms with balconies, each providing breathtaking vistas of the gardens or Rome's historic center. Guests can indulge in the 8202 ft² wellness center, which boasts luxurious amenities designed for relaxation and rejuvenation.

Experience unparalleled dining at the 3-Michelin star rooftop restaurant La Pergola, known for its gourmet cuisine, or savor classic Italian and international dishes at L'Uliveto on the poolside terrace. With two restaurants and four bars, culinary delights await at every turn. The hotel also offers unique recreational facilities, including two red-clay tennis courts and an 800-yard fitness trail equipped with Technogym facilities.

  • 3-Michelin star rooftop restaurant La Pergola
  • Extensive Mediterranean gardens with panoramic views
  • 8202 ft² wellness center with Turkish bath and saunas
  • Two red-clay tennis courts and fitness trail
  • Exclusive access to the Imperial Club Lounge

The Grand Spa at Rome Cavalieri is a haven of tranquility, featuring a stylish room with an indoor swimming pool and a fireplace. Art enthusiasts will appreciate the hotel's extensive art collection, which includes antique furniture, magnificent paintings, and precious tapestries displayed throughout the suites and public spaces.

Conveniently located just 1.9 miles from the Vatican and a 25-minute drive from the Coliseum, the hotel provides a complimentary hourly shuttle service to/from Via Veneto, ensuring easy access to Rome's iconic attractions.
