About Residence Share Inn

Auberge Share Inn is located in the Nice City Center district in Nice, only 1148 feet away from the nearest beach. Jean Médecin shopping street is 886 feet away. Free WiFi is available throughout the property. Accessed by a elevator, each air-conditioned room features a flat-screen satellite TV and a safe. The rooms have a private bathroom with a bath or shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries. The shared kitchen offers free access to a coffee machine, individual refrigerators and a microwave. An ice machine is also at guests' disposal. The Ancient Fort of Mont Boron is 3.4 mi from Auberge Share Inn, while Allianz Riviera Stadium is 7.5 mi from the property. Nice Côte d'Azur Airport is 4.8 mi away.

Amenities

  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Non-Smoking Facility
