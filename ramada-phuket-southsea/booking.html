About Ramada by Wyndham Phuket Southsea

Ramada by Wyndham Phuket Southsea, a beachfront resort located on Karon Beach, is a 10-minute drive from Patong Beach and a 20-minute drive from central Phuket. It features a restaurant and spa complete with a spa bath and pool. A complimentary scheduled shuttle service from the property to Patong is available 3 days per week. Free WiFi access is available. All air-conditioned guest rooms offer a private terrace and bathrooms with rain shower. Some rooms come with flat-screen TV. Guests can enjoy meals with a sea view at Morng Talay Restaurant. Room service is available at the resort. Guests can enjoy luxurious treatments at the spa, followed by a dip in the pool. Ramada by Wyndham Phuket Southsea also features a library and souvenir shops. The property also has a fitness center and an internet cafe. Services provided include babysitting, laundry and dry cleaning. Travel arrangements can also be made at the tour desk.
