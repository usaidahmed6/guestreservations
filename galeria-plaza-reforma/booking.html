About Galeria Plaza Mexico City

Located in Zona Rosa one block from Paseo de la Reforma's office buildings, the Galeria Plaza Reforma attracts both business travelers and vacationers. This distinguished 434 room hotel is surrounded by the capital citys financial and commercial district where one can find exclusive restaurants, shops and boutiques. Award-winning service has positioned Galeria Plaza as a modern yet comfortable 5-star hotel considered a leader among Mexico Citys numerous lodging alternatives.
