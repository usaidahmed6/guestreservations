About Cairn Hotel

The Cairn Hotel is 2625 feet from Edinburgh Waverley Rail Station and the shops, theaters and nightlife of Princes Street. It offers free WiFi and has a breakfast room. The Cairn Hotel is in Edinburgh city center, close to Calton Hill. The hotel is in a quiet location 984 feet from the Playhouse Theater and has easy access to public transport. All of the rooms have showers, TVs and tea/coffee. Hair dryers, irons and ironing boards are available on request. There is a bar area with a large flat-screen TV.

Amenities

  • 24 hour front desk
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • Non-Smoking Facility
  • Lounge/Bar
