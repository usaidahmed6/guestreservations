About Best Western Plus Miami Executive Airport Hotel & Suites

Located opposite Kendall-Tamiami Executive Airport in Miami, Florida, this hotel features an outdoor pool and a daily continental breakfast. Free WiFi access is available. You can visit the cocktail lounge, a full-service bar offering daily beverages, light dining, and coffee, during your stay. A microwave, refrigerator, and coffee machine are available in each room at Best Western Plus Kendall Airport Hotel & Suites. A flat-screen cable TV and a private bathroom stocked with a hairdryer and free toiletries are provided. At Best Western Plus Kendall Airport Hotel & Suites you will find a fitness center. Other facilities offered include luggage storage and a vending machine. The property offers free parking. Zoo Miami is just 3.5 mi away. Guests will be 30 minutes’ drive from Miami city center.
