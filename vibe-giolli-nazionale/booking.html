About Vibe Nazionale

Overlooking Romes Palazzo delle Esposizioni and San Vitale Basilica, Vibe Giolli Nazionale offers elegant rooms with parquet floors, free WiFi, and marble bathrooms. Repubblica Square and metro station are 1640 feet away. All air conditioned, rooms at the Vibe Giolli come with satellite flat-screen TV, extra long beds and a mini-bar. The bathroom has free toiletries and a hairdryer. Some rooms feature frescoed ceilings. Piazza Venezia can be reached on foot in 15 minutes.
