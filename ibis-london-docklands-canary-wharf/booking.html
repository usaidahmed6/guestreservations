About Ibis London Docklands Canary Wharf

Just yards from lively Canary Wharf, the Ibis London Docklands Canary Wharf features free Wi-Fi, a modern restaurant and 24-hour front desk. Canary Wharf London Underground Station is a 10-minute walk away. The spacious, sound-proofed bedrooms at the hotel have air-cooling systems and flat-screen TVs. All rooms were refurbished in 2015 and feature private bathrooms and work desks. The Ibis London Docklands Canary Wharf has secure on-site parking, and is just across the road from Blackwall Station on the Docklands Light Railway (DLR). The O2 Arena is a 15-minute tube-ride away. The bright and airy cafe-style restaurant serves a varied evening menu and a wide selection of snacks. The stylish bar offers a range of drinks. Guests may wish to indulge in the Ibis all-you-can-eat breakfast buffet available each morning.
