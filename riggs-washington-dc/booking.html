About Riggs Washington DC

The Riggs Washington DC is located in the former home of Riggs National Bank, and offers modern accommodations 5-minutes away from Gallery Place - Chinatown Metro Station. Rooms at the Riggs Washington DC include a city views of Penn Quarter. Riggs Washington DC rooms offer Smart TV and WiFi access is free throughout. Guests can enjoy all-American cuisine at Cafe Riggs and Lounge. Silver Lyan is a cocktail mixology bar that can be found in the original basement vault. A rooftop event space is available as well. The National Portrait Gallery is 1 mi from the property.
