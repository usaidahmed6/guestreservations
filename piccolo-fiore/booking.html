About Piccolo Fiore

Set in the popular area of Rione Monti, Hotel Piccolo Fiore offers rooms with parquet floors in Rome. The Trevi Fountain is an 8-minute walk from the property. All air-conditioned, rooms at the hotel feature parquet floors, a flat-screen TV, a mini-bar and a safe. The bathroom includes a shower and a hairdryer. Free WiFi is available in some rooms. Piazza Venezia is 1640 feet from Piccolo Fiore, while Villa Aldobrandini is a 3-minute walk away.

Amenities

  • Room Service
  • 24 hour front desk
  • Guests are provided with free hand sanitizer
  • Social distancing measures are in place
  • Lounge/Bar
