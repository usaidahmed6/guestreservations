About Hotel Princesa Plaza Madrid

Princesa Plaza is located in the west of Madrid, in the Argelles District. Some of the elegant rooms at the hotel feature a large reproduction of a famous painting from the Museo del Prado. The hotel comes with the Holiday Gym fitness center, featuring a small indoor pool where aquagym classes are organized. The pool is available for guests over 16. Complimentary fitness classes are offerred, as well as cardiovascular equipment. Rooms at the Princesa are spacious with large windows and come with a seating area. They have satellite TV and air conditioning. Princesa Plaza is close to Plaza Espaa Square, Gran Via Avenue and the Royal Palace. It offers car rental and currency exchange services. The tour desk can provide useful information on the surrounding area. The hotel also gives information about a company that organizes shuttle service at an extra cost.
