About Hotel Indipendenza

Just a 3-minute walk from Roma Termini Train Station, Hotel Indipendenza offers free WiFi throughout and rooms with an LCD TV and private bathroom. Free city maps, a book-exchange library and a rooftop terrace are available. The Indipendenza Hotel is on the 5th and 6th floors of a building with an original Otis elevator. Rooms have wooden floors and include a work desk and radio. Some have a terrace. The reception of the main property is available 24 hours a day, and there is a free luggage storage room. You can catch both metro lines from Termini, plus the bus to Ciampino Airport and the train to Fiumicino Airport. The surrounding area has plenty of shops and restaurants.
