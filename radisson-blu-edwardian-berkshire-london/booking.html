About Radisson Blu Edwardian Bond Street Hotel

Radisson Blu Bond Street - a boutique hotel in central Londons best location. From the exclusive boutiques on New Bond Street to the art galleries of Marylebone Village, the citys top attractions are right on your doorstep. Explore famous department stores such as Selfridges, Hamleys and Liberty, stroll through nearby Hyde Park or marvel at the art and architecture of the Royal Academy of Arts, Spencer House and Wellington Arch. The newly reopened Bond Street station is located directly opposite the hotel, providing easy access to Canary Wharf and Heathrow Airport on the Elizabeth Line. Choose from 149 contemporary bedrooms and suites complete with handcrafted Vispring beds and luxury bathroom amenities, while further opportunities to relax are offered on the refurbished ground and first floors. Enter through the refurbished lobby and take a seat in the elegant Bond St. Lounge Bar. Furnished with comfortable sofas, armchairs and social seating, the Lounge Bar serves an Italian menu of cicchetti, antipasti, pasta and secondi, as well as a refreshing selection of aperitivi and wines. Alternatively, if you are looking for a moment of indulgence, we invite you upstairs to enjoy a British afternoon tea with a twist in the wood-paneled Bond St. Tea Rooms.
