About Hanover 71 Suites

Attractively located in the center of Edinburgh, Hanover 71 Suites has Full English/Irish breakfast and free WiFi. Featuring a restaurant, the hotel is close to several noted attractions, around a 8-minute walk from Camera Obscura and World of Illusions, half a mile from The Real Mary King's Close and a 9-minute walk from Edinburgh Waverley Train Station. The hotel has family rooms. At the hotel, rooms contain a desk, a flat-screen TV, a private bathroom, bed linen and towels. Each room is equipped with an electric tea pot, while certain rooms are equipped with a patio and others also feature garden views. The rooms have a closet. Popular points of interest near Hanover 71 Suites include Edinburgh Playhouse, Edinburgh Castle and National Museum of Scotland. The nearest airport is Edinburgh Airport, 5.6 miles from the accommodation.
