About Hotel Madera

Located in the heart of Washington, D.C. the modern Hotel Madera features an on-site restaurant and bar services. Dupont Circle is just 2 minutes walk away. Each room at Hotel Madera features a flat-screen TV, and a work desk. They are decorated in cool tones. Coffee makers are available upon request. Select rooms have a balcony. The Firefly Restaurant serves contemporary American comfort food and a variety of cocktails. Hotel Madera is Wi-Fi accessible and offers valet parking. The White House is 1 mile from the hotel. The Washington National Monument is 2 miles away.
