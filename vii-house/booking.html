About Vii House

Ideally located in the prime touristic area of Naiharn, Vii House by V.Hemtanon MUAY THAI promises a relaxing and wonderful visit. Featuring a complete list of amenities, guests will find their stay at the property a comfortable one. Take advantage of the hotel's free Wi-Fi in all rooms, 24-hour security, daily housekeeping, Wi-Fi in public areas, car park. Clothes rack, mirror, towels, internet access wireless (complimentary), non smoking rooms can be found in selected guestrooms. Recuperate from a full day of sightseeing in the comfort of your room or take advantage of the hotel's recreational facilities, including fitness center. A welcoming atmosphere and excellent service are what you can expect during your stay at Vii House by V.Hemtanon MUAY THAI.
