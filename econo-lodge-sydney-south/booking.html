About Hotel Harry, an Ascend Hotel Collection Member

The renovated Hotel Harry, Ascend Hotel Collection is located in the vibrant Surry Hills, a hub for food, fashion, and the arts. Originally built in 1912 this building has been artfully restored to highlight the historical architecture, together with stylish contemporary dcor. Reopened in mid-2015, Hotel Harry, Ascend Hotel Collection offers comfortable suites with plush carpet and luxury bed linen. Each of the air-conditioned rooms includes a digital TV, a small refrigerator and an private bathroom. Private parking is available at a nearby location. Guests can enjoy a 5-minute stroll to Sydney CBD (Central Business District). Hotel Harry is a 10-minute walk from Central Station, and a 15-minute drive from Bondi Beach. Hotel Harry is a 10-minute walk from Hyde Park.
