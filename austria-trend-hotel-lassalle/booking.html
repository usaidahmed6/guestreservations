About voco Vienna Prater

Boasting a spectacular location, this hotel resides in a peaceful setting in the 2nd district of Vienna. There are numerous opportunities for a wide range of excursions, such as to the Prater Garden and Vienna Exhibition Centre, ensuring that guests have a mesmerising and thoroughly enjoyable stay. Access to public transport is readily available, a mere 50 metres from the hotel itself, with Vienna airport just 17 km away. In a short walk, guests will find themselves amidst a sea of entertainment, shopping and dining opportunities. This hotel scores with comfort and modernity. The guest rooms are spacious, including a sitting and reading area, allowing for guests to engage in ultimate relaxation, or to utilise the space for work purposes. A large selection of sport and entertainment options are available nearby, ensuring that guests have a memorable stay at this hotel.
