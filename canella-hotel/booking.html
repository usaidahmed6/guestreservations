About CANELLA HOTEL

Located in Rome, 4.1 miles from Bologna Metro Station, CANELLA HOTEL has accommodations with a garden, free private parking, a shared lounge and a terrace. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, room service and a 24-hour front desk, along with free WiFi throughout the property. Guests can use a bar. The hotel will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms with a desk, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a bidet. Auditorium Parco della Musica is 4.3 miles from CANELLA HOTEL, while Piazza di Spagna Metro Station is 4.6 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Rome Ciampino Airport, 19 miles from the accommodation.
