About Oleo Cancun Playa All Inclusive Resort

ÓLEO Cancún Playa is a 4.5-star resort, with an all-inclusive plan that includes a handmade gastronomic experience offering two specialty restaurants, an international buffet and a snack bar, two bars and premium drinks. In addition, it has a Spa with a recreational pool with hydromassage, steam room and sauna On the property dogs up to 9 kilos are accepted (fees and restrictions apply) with Pet - It amenities. Its 168 minimalist rooms and the Artist Service® OLEO concept personalized service for a memorable vacation, including: VIP Concierge, Room Service, Pool Concierge, Tanning and Sunscreen Menu,Free Wi-Fi, and welcome drink during check in, among others. The resort has a privileged location within the Hotel Zone, just 7.5 mi from the Cancun International Airport, 5 mi from Plaza la Isla. A tax of MXN 76 (approximately USD 5) will be charged per room per night. The mandatory tax is not included in the daily rate and must be paid directly at the hotel during check-in.
