About MSK Premium

MSK Premium offers accommodations within 2.2 miles of the center of London, with free Wifi and a kitchenette with a microwave, a toaster, and a fridge. This 4-star apartment offers a concierge service. Royal Albert Hall is a 19-minute walk away, and Olympia Exhibition Centre is 2 miles from the apartment. At the apartment complex, the units are equipped with a wardrobe, a TV, a private bathroom, bed linen, and towels. The rooms are equipped with a kettle, while some have a balcony. Popular points of interest near the apartment include Paddington Station, Portobello Road Market, and The Serpentine. The nearest airport is London City Airport, 12 miles from MSK Premium.
