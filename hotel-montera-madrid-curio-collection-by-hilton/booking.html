About Hotel Montera Madrid, Curio Collection by Hilton

The hotel, conveniently located in the city center on Gran Via, is within walking distance of attractions and shopping areas. The Art Triangle or Golden Triangle, an area made up of three art museums, is just a kilometer away. Enjoy the incredible view from our rooms and from the rooftop pool and Skybar. Take a 10-minute walk to explore Plaza Mayor and Plaza del Sol.
