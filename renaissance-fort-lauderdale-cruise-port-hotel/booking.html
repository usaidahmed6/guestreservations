About Renaissance by Marriott Fort Lauderdale Cruise Port Hotel

Located 3 minute-drive from Fort Lauderdale Beach, this hotel with contemporary décor features an outdoor pool in the middle of landscaped tropical gardens. A restaurant is on site. The Fort Lauderdale Cruise Port is 2 miles away. A 37-inch flat-screen cable TV and a laptop safe are featured in all sound-proofed rooms. Select rooms include club-level lounge access with snacks and beverages at this Fort Lauderdale Cruise Port Renaissance Hotel. Renaissance Fort Lauderdale Cruise Port Hotel offers a well-equipped fitness center as well as a hot tub. Guests can have their newspaper delivered to the room upon request. Facing the pool, R Lounge offers American cuisine and indoor sitting. Bistro 17 serves American cuisine with local flavors in a tropical-inspired setting. The Broward County Convention Center is adjacent to the hotel, and a Fort Lauderdale Water Taxi stop is a short walk from the property. Fort Lauderdale International Airport is 3 miles away.
