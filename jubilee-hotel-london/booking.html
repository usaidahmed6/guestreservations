About Jubilee Hotel Victoria

Set in a Georgian townhouse in Victoria, Jubilee Hotel Victoria is 5 minutes’ walk from Victoria Rail Station. It offers continental breakfasts and fast Wi-Fi, and the shops of Knightsbridge are a 15-minute walk away. The bright and airy rooms at the Jubilee feature colorful linens and fresh towels. All rooms have an private bathroom and tea and coffee facilities, and each also has a TV for guests to enjoy. The family-run Jubilee Hotel Victoria is located in a quiet area, within walking distance of many of London’s main attractions. Buckingham Palace is a 10-minute stroll away, and fashionable Pimlico can also be reached in 10 minutes.
