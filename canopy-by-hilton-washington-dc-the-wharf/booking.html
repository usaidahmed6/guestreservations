About Canopy By Hilton Washington DC The Wharf

Located in Washington, D.C., a 14-minute walk from Smithsonian National Air and Space Museum, Canopy By Hilton Washington DC The Wharf provides accommodations with free bikes, private parking, a fitness center and a shared lounge. Featuring a 24-hour front desk, this property also provides guests with a terrace. Guests can enjoy African and American dishes at the restaurant or have a drink at the bar. Popular points of interest near the hotel include National Museum of the American Indian, US Holocaust Memorial Museum and Washington Monument. The nearest airport is Ronald Reagan Washington National Airport, 5.6 miles from Canopy By Hilton Washington DC The Wharf.
