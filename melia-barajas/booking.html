About Meliá Barajas

Conveniently located near Madrid Barajas Airport, this hotel has a garden with a swimming pool. The hotel offers a free shuttle service Airport/Hotel/Airport. Rooms at the Meliá Barajas are elegantly decorated with rich colors, luxurious fabrics and polished wooden furniture. Easily access the center of the Spanish capital and other surrounding attractions including Olivar de la Hinojosa Golf Course and the business districts. In the hotel restaurant you can try Spanish specialties and there are barbecue facilities in the garden beside the pool. You can also relax with a drink in the café-bar.
