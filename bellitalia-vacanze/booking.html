About Bellitalia Vacanze

Relais Vacanze Romane is housed in a historic building of the residential Prati district, in Rome. It offers rooms with air conditioning and free WiFi. The Vatican is a 10-minute walk away. Every room at this bed and breakfast is fitted with a flat-screen TV. Rooms are fitted with a private bathroom equipped with a bath or shower and bidet. For your comfort, you will find slippers, free toiletries and a hair dryer. Relais Vacanze Romane features free WiFi . There is concierge service at the property. The bed and breakfast also offers bike hire. Castel Sant'Angelo is 984 feet from Relais Vacanze Romane, while Sant'Agostino is 2625 feet from the property. A bus stop with direct links to Fiumicino Airport is just opposite the property.
