About STRAF | a Member of Design Hotels

The ultramodern STRAF Hotel is just 164 feet from Milan Cathedral. It offers a 24-hour gym, an international restaurant, and its rooms have large bathrooms with luxurious bathrobes and slippers. Rooms at the STRAF feature modern, artistic and design furnishings. Each comes with free Wi-Fi, an interactive LCD TV and an electric kettle with a selection of natural teas. Some rooms also feature a Lavazza coffee machine and, upon request, an ironing set. Guests can start the day with a rich Continental and American buffet breakfast. Open for lunch, the restaurant specializes in Mediterranean and International cuisine, along with vegetarian options. Located in a renovated 19th-century building, the Hotel offers concierge and ticket service and staff is available 24-hour a day. Duomo Metro Station is just 656 feet away. The fashion district is a 5-minute walk from the hotel and Linate Airport is 5.6 mi away.
